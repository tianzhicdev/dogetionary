Tenacity

Pronunciation:tɪˈnæs.ɪ.ti
ZH→EN

Quick Translations

  • 韧性
  • 坚持
  • 顽强

Definitions

noun

Definition

韧性

In EN

The quality of being determined and persistent; the ability to hold firm to a course of action despite difficulties or obstacles.

Examples

  • 他以非凡的韧性克服了生活的困难。
  • 在比赛中，运动员展示了令人钦佩的坚持和韧性。
  • 成功者往往具备超强的韧性和耐心。

Cultural Notes

Tenacity is a highly valued trait in many cultures, especially in Chinese culture, where perseverance in the face of hardship is often admired. The phrase '不怕困难' (not afraid of difficulties) is frequently associated with tenacity.

noun

Definition

坚持

In EN

The quality of continuing to do something despite challenges; persistence in maintaining a course of action.

Examples

  • 他的坚持让他最终达成了目标。
  • 科学研究需要长时间的坚持。
  • 即使遇到挫折，她的坚持也没有动摇。

Cultural Notes

In Chinese culture, '坚持' (persistence) is often linked to traditional values such as hard work and determination, illustrated in idioms like '锲而不舍' (perseverance brings success).

noun

Definition

顽强

In EN

The quality of being strong and not easily discouraged; resilience in the face of difficulties.

Examples

  • 她在艰苦的环境中表现出了顽强的精神。
  • 面对挑战，他展现了顽强的决心。
  • 这支队伍在比赛中顽强拼搏，最终赢得了胜利。

Cultural Notes

顽强 (resilience) embodies a spirit of toughness and endurance, and is often celebrated in stories of heroism and overcoming adversity in Chinese literature and film.
