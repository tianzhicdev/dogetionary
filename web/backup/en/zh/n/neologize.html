Neologize

Pronunciation:/niˈɒlədʒaɪz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 创造新词
  • 新词的形成
  • 新词语

Definitions

verb

Definition

To create new words or phrases.

In ZH

创造新词或短语。

Examples

  • The author tends to neologize throughout her novels.
  • As technology advances, many people neologize to describe new phenomena.
  • In order to express modern concepts, we often need to neologize.
  • Linguists study how languages neologize over time.
  • Children frequently neologize as they learn to speak.

Cultural Notes

Neologism is often seen in literature and social media, reflecting changing cultural dynamics.
