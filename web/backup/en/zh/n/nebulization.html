Nebulization

EN→ZH

What Does Nebulization Mean?

  • 雾化
  • 气雾剂

Nebulization Pronunciation

/ˌnɛb.jə.laɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/

Nebulization Definition

noun

Definition

The process of turning a liquid into a fine mist or vapor, typically for inhalation or medical purposes.

In ZH

将液体转化为细雾或蒸汽的过程，通常用于吸入或医疗目的。

How to Use Nebulization in a Sentence

  • Nebulization is commonly used for asthma treatment.
  • The doctor recommended nebulization for the patient's respiratory issues.
  • During nebulization, the medication is aerosolized for easier inhalation.
  • Nebulization can help deliver medicine directly to the lungs.
  • Many patients find nebulization more effective than oral medications.

Synonyms for Nebulization

  • Nebula
  • Nebulous
