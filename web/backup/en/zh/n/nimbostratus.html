Nimbostratus

EN→ZH

What Does Nimbostratus Mean?

  • 雨层云
  • 层积雨云

Nimbostratus Pronunciation

/ˌnɪm.boʊˈstræ.təs/

Nimbostratus Definition

noun

Definition

A type of cloud characterized by a thick, gray layer that produces continuous, steady precipitation.

In ZH

一种以厚重灰色层为特征的云，产生持续稳定的降水。

How to Use Nimbostratus in a Sentence

  • The sky was completely covered by nimbostratus clouds.
  • Nimbostratus clouds often signal a long day of rain ahead.
  • When nimbostratus forms, it can obscure the sun completely.
  • Hikers should prepare for wet conditions when nimbostratus clouds appear.
  • Meteorologists use satellite images to track nimbostratus formations.

Synonyms for Nimbostratus
