Noxious

Pronunciation:/ˈnɒk.ʃəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 有毒的
  • 有害的
  • 令人厌恶的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Harmful, poisonous, or very unpleasant.

In ZH

有害的，毒性的，或非常令人厌恶的。

Examples

  • The noxious fumes from the factory caused health problems in the surrounding community.
  • Certain plants produce noxious chemicals that can harm animals.
  • The noxious attitude of the leader demoralized the team.
  • We must take action to eliminate noxious weeds from our garden.
  • His noxious behavior at the party made everyone uncomfortable.
