Non Verbal

Pronunciation:nɒn ˈvɜːr.bəl
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 非语言的
  • 非言语的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Not involving or using words; communicated without speech.

In ZH

不涉及或使用词语；通过非言语交流。

Examples

  • Her non-verbal gestures showed that she was excited.
  • Children often communicate non-verbally before they learn to speak.
  • The presentation included many non-verbal cues to help convey the message.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, non-verbal communication can be just as impactful as verbal communication. Body language, facial expressions, and gestures all play a significant role in how messages are interpreted.

noun

Definition

A non-verbal communication method; an act or instance of communicating without words.

In ZH

一种非言语的交流方法；通过没有词汇的方式进行交流的行为或实例。

Examples

  • Non-verbals can sometimes speak louder than words.
  • In therapy, non-verbals may reveal a lot about a person's feelings.
  • Observing non-verbals is crucial in negotiations.

Cultural Notes

Understanding non-verbal communication is essential in multicultural settings, where gestures may have different meanings.
