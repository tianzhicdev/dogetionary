Navigate

EN→ZH

What Does Navigate Mean?

  • 导航
  • 航行
  • 操纵

Navigate Pronunciation

ˈnæv.ɪ.ɡeɪt

Navigate Definition

verb

Definition

To plan and direct the route or course of a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.

In ZH

规划和指挥船只、飞机或其他交通工具的航线或路线。

How to Use Navigate in a Sentence

  • She learned how to navigate using the stars.
  • The captain navigated the ship through the storm.
  • They used GPS to navigate through the city.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, navigation is not only related to physical travel but also often used metaphorically to describe figuring out complex situations.

verb

Definition

To find one's way or to move through a place with skill; to manage or control a process or system.

In ZH

灵巧地找到方向或在某个地方移动；管理或控制一个过程或系统。

How to Use Navigate in a Sentence

  • He navigated the conversation back to the main topic.
  • She has to navigate through many challenges in her new job.
  • They navigated the interface of the new software easily.

Cultural Notes

This usage reflects the broader concept of 'navigating life' or 'navigating challenges', emphasizing problem-solving and adaptability.
