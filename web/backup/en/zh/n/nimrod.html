Nimrod

EN→ZH

What Does Nimrod Mean?

  • 尼姆罗德

Nimrod Pronunciation

/ˈnɪm.rɒd/

Nimrod Definition

noun

Definition

A foolish or inept person.

In ZH

一个愚蠢或无能的人。

How to Use Nimrod in a Sentence

  • He acted like a complete nimrod during the meeting.
  • Don't be such a nimrod and forget the plan.
  • That nimrod dropped the ball on the project.
  • The movie was full of nimrod characters doing silly things.
  • Sometimes I feel like a nimrod when I can't solve a simple problem.

Cultural Notes

Originally, 'Nimrod' refers to a biblical figure known for being a mighty hunter, but in modern slang, it has taken on a more derogatory meaning.

Synonyms for Nimrod
