Neologism

Pronunciation:/niˈɒlədʒɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 新词
  • 新词汇
  • 新造词

Definitions

noun

Definition

A newly coined word or expression.

In ZH

新创造的单词或表达方式。

Examples

  • The term 'selfie' is a neologism that became popular in the 2010s.
  • Linguists study neologisms to understand how language evolves.
  • Many neologisms arise from technological advancements and cultural shifts.
  • The rise of social media has led to the creation of numerous neologisms.
  • Neologisms often reflect the changes in society and human behavior.

Cultural Notes

Neologisms are often used in pop culture, especially in relation to technology and social media.
