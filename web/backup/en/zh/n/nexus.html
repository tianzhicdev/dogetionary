Nexus

EN→ZH

What Does Nexus Mean?

  • 连接
  • 联系
  • 纽带

Nexus Pronunciation

/ˈnɛk.səs/

Nexus Definition

noun

Definition

a connection or series of connections linking two or more things.

In ZH

连接或一系列连接两个或更多事物的关系。

How to Use Nexus in a Sentence

  • The internet serves as a nexus for global communication.
  • There is a strong nexus between poverty and crime.
  • The book explores the nexus of politics and economics.
  • They formed a nexus of support for local artists.
  • The nexus of science and technology continues to drive innovation.
