Nuisance

Pronunciation:/ˈnjuː.səns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 麻烦
  • 讨厌的人或事
  • 滋扰

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person, thing, or circumstance causing inconvenience or annoyance.

In ZH

造成不便或烦恼的人、事或情况。

Examples

  • The noisy construction outside is such a nuisance.
  • I find it a nuisance when people talk loudly on the phone in public places.
  • The stray cats in the neighborhood have become a nuisance to the residents.
  • He is considered a nuisance for always lingering around the playground without any purpose.
  • The constant advertisements on the website are a real nuisance for users.
