Nonplused

EN→ZH

What Does Nonplused Mean?

  • 困惑的
  • 茫然的
  • 不知所措的

Nonplused Pronunciation

/nɒnˈplʌst/

Nonplused Definition

adjective

Definition

baffled; bewildered; at a loss for what to say, think, or do.

In ZH

困惑的；茫然的；不知所措的。

How to Use Nonplused in a Sentence

  • She was nonplused by the unexpected question.
  • He looked nonplused when he realized he forgot his keys.
  • The sudden change in plans left everyone nonplused.
  • Nonplused by the news, he took a moment to gather his thoughts.
  • They found themselves nonplused by the complex instructions.

Synonyms for Nonplused

  • Non Sequitur
  • Non Verbal
  • Nonce
  • Nonchalance
  • Nonchalant
