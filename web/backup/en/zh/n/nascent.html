Nascent

Pronunciation:/ˈneɪ.sənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 初生的
  • 新兴的
  • 萌芽的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

just coming into existence and beginning to display signs of future potential

In ZH

刚刚开始出现，并开始显示未来潜力的迹象

Examples

  • The nascent technology has the potential to revolutionize communication.
  • She was part of a nascent movement advocating for environmental sustainability.
  • The nascent stages of the project were marked by excitement and uncertainty.
  • He is working on a nascent startup that focuses on renewable energy.
  • The artist's nascent talent was evident in her first few paintings.
