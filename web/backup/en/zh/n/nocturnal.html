Nocturnal

Pronunciation:/nɒkˈtɜrnəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 夜间的
  • 夜行的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Active at night; occurring at night.

In ZH

晚上活跃的；发生在晚上的。

Examples

  • Owls are known for their nocturnal hunting habits.
  • Some species of flowers bloom at night, making them nocturnal attractions.
  • Many animals are nocturnal, preferring the cover of darkness to forage for food.
  • Nocturnal creatures can often be seen more easily under the light of a full moon.
  • People who work night shifts often adapt to a nocturnal lifestyle.
