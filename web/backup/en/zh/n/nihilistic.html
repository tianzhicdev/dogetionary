Nihilistic

Pronunciation:/naɪəˈlɪstɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 虚无主义的
  • 无意义的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

rejecting all religious and moral principles, in the belief that life is meaningless.

In ZH

拒绝一切宗教和道德原则，认为生活没有意义。

Examples

  • He had a nihilistic view of the universe.
  • Her writing often reflects a nihilistic philosophy.
  • The movie presented a nihilistic perspective on human existence.
  • Many artists in the 20th century explored nihilistic themes.
  • In his nihilistic outlook, nothing held any intrinsic value.
