Numismatic

EN→ZH

What Does Numismatic Mean?

  • 钱币学的
  • 币制学的

Numismatic Pronunciation

/ˌnjuː.mɪzˈmæt.ɪk/

Numismatic Definition

adjective

Definition

relating to the study or collection of currency, including coins, tokens, paper money, and related objects

In ZH

与货币的研究或收藏有关，包括硬币、代币、纸币及相关物品

How to Use Numismatic in a Sentence

  • He has a numismatic collection that dates back to ancient Rome.
  • The museum hosts a numismatic exhibit featuring rare coins.
  • Numismatic studies can reveal much about historical economies.
  • She became interested in numismatic activities through her grandfather.
  • Many people find numismatic pursuits to be a rewarding hobby.

Synonyms for Numismatic

  • Numinous
