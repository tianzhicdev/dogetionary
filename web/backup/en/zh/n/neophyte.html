Neophyte

Pronunciation:/ˈniːəfaɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 初学者
  • 新手
  • 菜鸟

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who is new to a subject, skill, or belief.

In ZH

对某一主题、技能或信仰感到陌生的人。

Examples

  • As a neophyte in the world of coding, I found the tutorials very helpful.
  • The neophyte gardener was excited to see her first flowers bloom.
  • Being a neophyte in the art of negotiation, he felt overwhelmed during the meeting.
  • The organization welcomed neophytes into their training program.
  • Many neophytes struggle to understand the complex jargon used in the field.
