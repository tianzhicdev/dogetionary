Nourishment

EN→ZH

What Does Nourishment Mean?

  • 营养
  • 滋养
  • 养分

Nourishment Pronunciation

ˈnɜrɪʃmənt

Nourishment Definition

noun

Definition

The food or other substances necessary for growth, health, and good condition.

In ZH

生长、健康和良好状态所需的食物或其他物质。

How to Use Nourishment in a Sentence

  • Proper nourishment is essential for children's development.
  • She believes that a balanced diet provides the best nourishment for the body.
  • The doctor emphasized the importance of nourishment for recovery after illness.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, nourishment is not just about physical food but also includes emotional support and relationships. In traditional Chinese culture, nourishment can also refer to the concept of '养生' (yangsheng), which emphasizes health maintenance.

noun

Definition

The process of providing or obtaining the food necessary for health and growth.

In ZH

提供或获取必要食物以保持健康和生长的过程。

How to Use Nourishment in a Sentence

  • Nourishment involves both eating and absorbing nutrients effectively.
  • The community program focuses on the nourishment of underprivileged children.
  • Understanding the science of nourishment can help improve overall health.

Cultural Notes

The process of nourishment is often linked to food traditions and values. In Chinese culture, there is a strong emphasis on the balance of ingredients for optimal nourishment and health benefits.
