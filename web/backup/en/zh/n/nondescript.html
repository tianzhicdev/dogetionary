Nondescript

Pronunciation:/ˌnɒn.dɪˈskrɪpt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无特征的
  • 平凡的
  • 不引人注目的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Lacking distinctive or interesting features or characteristics.

In ZH

缺乏独特或有趣的特征或特点。

Examples

  • The building was completely nondescript, blending in with the other structures.
  • His nondescript appearance made it easy for him to go unnoticed in the crowd.
  • She wore a nondescript outfit that didn’t attract any attention.
  • The town was filled with nondescript houses that looked the same.
  • Despite its nondescript exterior, the restaurant served exceptional food.
