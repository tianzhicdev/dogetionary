Narcissism

EN→ZH

What Does Narcissism Mean?

  • 自恋
  • 自我陶醉
  • 自恋主义

Narcissism Pronunciation

/ˈnɑːr.sɪ.sɪ.zəm/

Narcissism Definition

noun

Definition

Excessive interest in or admiration of oneself and one's physical appearance.

In ZH

对自己及自己外貌的过度关注或钦佩。

How to Use Narcissism in a Sentence

  • Her narcissism made it difficult for her to maintain close friendships.
  • He was accused of narcissism after constantly seeking validation from others.
  • Narcissism can often lead to problems in personal relationships.
  • The rise of social media has been linked to increased narcissism among young people.
  • Therapists often encounter clients with traits of narcissism in their practice.

Synonyms for Narcissism

  • Narcissistic
  • Narration
  • Narrative
