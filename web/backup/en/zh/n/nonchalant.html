Nonchalant

Pronunciation:/ˌnɒnʃəˈlɒnt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 冷静的
  • 无所谓的
  • 漠不关心的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Feeling or appearing casually calm and relaxed; not displaying anxiety, interest, or enthusiasm.

In ZH

感到或显得随意、冷静而放松；没有表现出焦虑、兴趣或热情。

Examples

  • She walked into the meeting with a nonchalant attitude.
  • His nonchalant demeanor made everyone feel at ease.
  • Despite the chaos around him, he remained nonchalant and collected.
  • She gave a nonchalant shrug when asked if she was worried.
  • He was nonchalant about the test results, claiming he hadn't studied.
