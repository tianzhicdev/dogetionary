Neologistic

Pronunciation:/niːˌɒləˈdʒɪstɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 新词的
  • 新造词的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to or characteristic of a neologism; having newly coined words or expressions.

In ZH

与新词或新表达有关的；具有新造的词或表达的特征。

Examples

  • The poet's work is known for its neologistic qualities.
  • In linguistics, a neologistic approach can reveal how language evolves.
  • Some writers employ neologistic language to create a unique style.
  • The committee discussed the impact of neologistic terms on modern communication.
  • Her neologistic phrases often puzzled her audience.
