Nihilism

EN→ZH

What Does Nihilism Mean?

  • 虚无主义
  • 无意义主义

Nihilism Pronunciation

/ˈnaɪɪˌlɪzəm/

Nihilism Definition

noun

Definition

The belief that life is without objective meaning, purpose, or intrinsic value.

In ZH

生命是没有客观意义、目的或内在价值的信念。

How to Use Nihilism in a Sentence

  • Nihilism can lead individuals to feel disillusioned with traditional values.
  • The philosopher embraced nihilism as a way to challenge existing moral frameworks.
  • During periods of crisis, some people may turn to nihilism to make sense of their suffering.
  • Nihilism is often depicted in literature as a response to the chaos of life.
  • Some argue that nihilism can promote personal freedom, while others see it as destructive.

Synonyms for Nihilism

  • Nihilistic
  • Nihility
