What Does Nihility Mean?

Nihility Pronunciation

/naɪˈhɪlɪti/

Nihility Definition

noun

Definition

the state of being nothing; nonexistence

In ZH

无存在的状态；虚无

How to Use Nihility in a Sentence

  • Philosophers often debate the concept of nihility.
  • In a moment of despair, she felt engulfed by nihility.
  • The artist captured the essence of nihility in her paintings.
  • Nihility can be a source of fear for many individuals.
  • He contemplated the nihility of existence during his meditation.
