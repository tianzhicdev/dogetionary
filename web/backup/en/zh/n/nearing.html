Nearing

EN→ZH

What Does Nearing Mean?

  • 接近
  • 快到
  • 临近

Nearing Pronunciation

ˈnɪərɪŋ

Nearing Definition

verb

Definition

Approaching or getting closer to a particular point or situation.

In ZH

接近或靠近某个特定的点或情况。

How to Use Nearing in a Sentence

  • We are nearing the end of the semester, so it's time for final exams.
  • As the storm neared, everyone began to take shelter.
  • She could feel time nearing its end as she raced to finish her project.

Cultural Notes

The word 'nearing' is often used in both literal and figurative contexts, such as approaching a physical destination or heading towards a deadline.

adjective

Definition

Close in space, time, or relationship.

In ZH

在空间、时间或关系上接近。

How to Use Nearing in a Sentence

  • The nearing deadline prompted everyone to work harder.
  • With the nearing holidays, the streets filled with shoppers.
  • His nearing birthday made him excited for the upcoming celebration.

Cultural Notes

In English, using 'nearing' as an adjective can convey urgency or importance, especially in contexts involving deadlines or significant events.
