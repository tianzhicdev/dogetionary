Nonchalance

Pronunciation:/nɒnˈʃæl.əns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 冷漠
  • 漠不关心
  • 无所谓

Definitions

noun

Definition

the state of being casually calm and relaxed; a lack of concern or enthusiasm.

In ZH

一种随意冷静和放松的状态；缺乏关注或热情。

Examples

  • Her nonchalance in the face of danger surprised everyone.
  • He showed a remarkable nonchalance about the deadline.
  • Despite the chaos around her, she maintained her nonchalance.
  • His nonchalance made it hard to take the situation seriously.
  • The actor's nonchalance during the interview captivated the audience.
