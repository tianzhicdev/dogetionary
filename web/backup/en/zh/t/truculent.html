Truculent

Pronunciation:/ˈtrʌkjʊlənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 好斗的
  • 凶猛的
  • 挑衅的
  • 残暴的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

eager or quick to argue or fight; aggressively defiant.

In ZH

渴望或迅速争论或打斗；具有攻击性挑战的。

Examples

  • His truculent behavior during the debate put off many viewers.
  • The truculent dog barked aggressively at strangers.
  • She had a truculent attitude that made it hard to work with her.
  • The soldier's truculent nature led him to confrontations with others.
  • In the meeting, his truculent remarks caused tension among the team.
