Transience

EN→ZH

What Does Transience Mean?

  • 短暂性
  • 无常
  • 瞬息

Transience Pronunciation

/ˈtræn.si.əns/

Transience Definition

noun

Definition

the state or quality of being transient; brief existence or impermanence.

In ZH

短暂性；临时存在或不持久的状态。

How to Use Transience in a Sentence

  • The transience of life makes every moment precious.
  • We should cherish the beauty of transience in nature.
  • His art reflects the transience of human emotions.
  • Transience is a theme often explored in poetry.
  • The transience of youth is something everyone experiences.

Synonyms for Transience

  • Tractable
  • Trading
  • Tranquil
  • Tranquility
  • Tranquillity
