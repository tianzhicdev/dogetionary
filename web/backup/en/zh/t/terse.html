Terse

Pronunciation:/tɜːrs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 简洁的
  • 简练的
  • 简要的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

brief and to the point; effectively concise

In ZH

简明扼要的；有效地简洁

Examples

  • The report was terse and focused on the main issues.
  • She gave a terse response to the lengthy question.
  • His terse message lacked any warmth or detail.
  • I prefer terse communication over lengthy explanations.
  • The author's terse writing style made the book easier to read.
