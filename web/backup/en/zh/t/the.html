The

EN→ZH

What Does The Mean?

  • 这个
  • 那
  • 这些
  • 那些

The Definition

Definition

Used to specify a particular noun that is known to the reader or listener.

In ZH

用于指定读者或听者已知的特定名词。

How to Use The in a Sentence

  • The cat that stole my sandwich is now a local celebrity.
  • I finally found the remote control, but it was in the fridge next to the milk!
  • The weather today is so strange that I’m starting to think the sun is on vacation.

Definition

Used before a noun to indicate that it is unique or one of a kind.

In ZH

在名词前使用，以表明它是独特的或独一无二的。

How to Use The in a Sentence

  • The moon is looking particularly bright tonight, like it just got a new haircut.
  • I went to the store to buy the best chocolate, but they were out of stock because of a chocolate shortage!
  • The internet is a strange place where cats are more famous than most politicians.
