Tempestuous

EN→ZH

What Does Tempestuous Mean?

  • 狂暴的
  • 激烈的
  • 暴风雨般的

Tempestuous Pronunciation

/tɛmˈpɛstʃuəs/

Tempestuous Definition

adjective

Definition

characterized by strong and turbulent or conflicting emotion; stormy

In ZH

特征是强烈和动荡或冲突情感；风暴的

How to Use Tempestuous in a Sentence

  • The tempestuous relationship between the two characters is central to the novel.
  • Her tempestuous personality often led to dramatic confrontations.
  • The weather was tempestuous, with strong winds and heavy rain.
  • They had a tempestuous romance that ended in heartbreak.
  • The tempestuous sea made sailing impossible that day.
