Trickster

Pronunciation:/ˈtrɪk.stər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 骗子
  • 恶作剧者
  • 诡计者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who tricks or deceives others, often for their own amusement.

In ZH

一个欺骗或愚弄他人的人，通常是为了自己的娱乐。

Examples

  • The trickster played a prank on his friends.
  • In many cultures, the trickster is a popular character in folklore.
  • She acted like a trickster, constantly fooling everyone around her.
  • The story features a clever trickster who outsmarts his enemies.
  • As a trickster, he thrived on chaos and confusion.
