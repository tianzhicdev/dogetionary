Tweet

EN→ZH

What Does Tweet Mean?

  • 推文
  • 鸟叫声

Tweet Pronunciation

twiːt

Tweet Definition

verb

Definition

To post a message or media on the social media platform Twitter.

In ZH

在社交媒体平台推特上发布消息或媒体。

How to Use Tweet in a Sentence

  • I tweeted about my pet parrot learning to dance; now he's more popular than I am!
  • Just tweeted about my failed attempt at baking; the smoke alarm is the real chef here!
  • I tweeted a selfie with my plant, but it got more likes than my ex!

Cultural Notes

In modern culture, 'tweeting' has become a common way to share thoughts and updates quickly. It is often used humorously to convey both trivial and significant events.

noun

Definition

A post made on Twitter, typically limited to 280 characters.

In ZH

在推特上发布的帖子，通常限制为280个字符。

How to Use Tweet in a Sentence

  • My last tweet was so clever, I think it deserves a spot in the Twitter Hall of Fame!
  • Did you see the tweet that went viral? It was just a picture of a cat in a hat!
  • I wrote a tweet about my lost sock, and suddenly it sparked a national debate!

Cultural Notes

Tweets are known for their brevity and can lead to significant discussions or trends, often in a humorous or satirical context.

noun

Definition

The sound that a bird makes, often used to denote a short, sharp sound.

In ZH

鸟类发出的声音，通常用于表示短促、尖锐的声音。

How to Use Tweet in a Sentence

  • The tweet of the sparrow outside my window woke me up, but I wasn't ready for the day!
  • When I open my window, the birds start tweeting, as if they’re giving me morning gossip!
  • After eating pizza, I tweeted like a bird, hoping to fly away from my diet!

Cultural Notes

The term 'tweet' as a sound can be used in various contexts, often to evoke a sense of nature or light-heartedness.
