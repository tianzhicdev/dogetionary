Temporality

Pronunciation:/tɛmpəˈræl.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 时间性
  • 暂时性
  • 临时性

Definitions

noun

Definition

The state of existing within or having some relation to time.

In ZH

与时间有关的状态或存在的状态。

Examples

  • The temporality of human life is often reflected in art.
  • He reflected on the temporality of his experiences and how they shaped him.
  • In philosophy, temporality is a central theme in understanding existence.
  • The temporality of trends in fashion can be quite fleeting.
  • She wrote about the temporality of youth and its inevitable passage.
