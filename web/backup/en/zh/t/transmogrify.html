Transmogrify

Pronunciation:/trænˈsmɒɡrəfaɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 变形
  • 转化
  • 突变

Definitions

verb

Definition

To change in appearance or form, especially in a surprising or magical manner.

In ZH

以惊人的或魔法般的方式改变外观或形式。

Examples

  • The magician promised to transmogrify the ordinary rabbit into a giant dragon.
  • In the story, the prince was transmogrified into a frog by a witch's curse.
  • She felt her mood transmogrify from sadness to joy after hearing the good news.
  • The software allows users to transmogrify their photos into artistic renditions.
  • Watching the old building transmogrify into a modern office was astonishing.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used humorously to describe a bizarre or whimsical transformation.
