Trite

EN→ZH

What Does Trite Mean?

  • 陈腐的
  • 平庸的
  • 老生常谈的

Trite Pronunciation

/traɪt/

Trite Definition

adjective

Definition

Lacking in originality or freshness; dull on account of overuse.

In ZH

缺乏独创性或新鲜感；因为过度使用而显得乏味。

How to Use Trite in a Sentence

  • The movie was criticized for its trite storyline.
  • Her speech was filled with trite phrases that didn't resonate with the audience.
  • He avoided using trite expressions in his writing.
  • Despite its trite theme, the book was quite enjoyable.
  • I grew tired of the trite comments on social media.
