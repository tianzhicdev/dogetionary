Transient

Pronunciation:/ˈtræn.zi.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 短暂的
  • 临时的
  • 瞬间的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

lasting only for a short time; temporary.

In ZH

仅持续短时间的；临时的。

Examples

  • The transient beauty of the sunset amazed everyone.
  • She took a transient job to earn some extra money.
  • His stay in the city was transient, as he moved soon after.
  • Transient populations often struggle to find permanent housing.
  • The transient nature of youth can be bittersweet.
noun

Definition

a person who is staying or working in a place temporarily.

In ZH

在某个地方临时居住或工作的人。

Examples

  • The shelter provides services for transients in need.
  • Many transients pass through the city during the summer.
  • She met a transient traveler who shared his stories.
  • Transients often find it challenging to settle down.
  • The city has a large population of transients during the festival.
