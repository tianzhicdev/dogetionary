What Does Trapezium Mean?

  • 梯形
  • 四边形

Trapezium Pronunciation

/trəˈpiː.zi.əm/

Trapezium Definition

noun

Definition

A four-sided figure with at least one pair of parallel sides.

In ZH

一种至少有一对平行边的四边形。

How to Use Trapezium in a Sentence

  • The artist used a trapezium shape in her painting.
  • You can find trapezium shapes in architecture.
  • The trapezium is often used in various engineering designs.
