Tremolo

EN→ZH

What Does Tremolo Mean?

  • 颤音
  • 颤抖
  • 颤音效果

Tremolo Pronunciation

/ˈtrɛm.ə.loʊ/

Tremolo Definition

noun

Definition

A musical effect consisting of a slight, rapid variation in pitch or volume.

In ZH

一种音乐效果，由音高或音量的轻微快速变化组成。

How to Use Tremolo in a Sentence

  • The musician used a tremolo to add emotion to the piece.
  • She played the guitar with a beautiful tremolo effect.
  • In classical music, tremolo is often used to create a sense of urgency.
  • The singer added a tremolo at the end of the note.
  • He practiced the tremolo technique for hours before the performance.

Synonyms for Tremolo

  • Trembling
  • Tremors
  • Tremulous
  • Tremulousness
  • Trenchant
