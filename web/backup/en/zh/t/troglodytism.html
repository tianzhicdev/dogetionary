Troglodytism

EN→ZH

What Does Troglodytism Mean?

  • 穴居人主义
  • 隐居生活
  • 原始人生活

Troglodytism Pronunciation

/ˈtrɒɡləˌdaɪtɪzəm/

Troglodytism Definition

noun

Definition

The condition or behavior of living in a cave or similar dwelling, often associated with primitive or reclusive lifestyles.

In ZH

居住在洞穴或类似住所的状态或行为，通常与原始或隐居的生活方式相关联。

How to Use Troglodytism in a Sentence

  • The artist embraced troglodytism, retreating to a cave to find inspiration.
  • His troglodytism allowed him to escape modern society's pressures.
  • Troglodytism has been romanticized in literature as a return to simplicity.
  • Many ancient cultures practiced forms of troglodytism for shelter and safety.
  • The concept of troglodytism can be seen in various mythologies around the world.

Cultural Notes

Troglodytism is often viewed through a romantic lens in literature and art, representing a desire to return to nature or simpler times.

Synonyms for Troglodytism

  • Trompe-L'œil
  • Trope
  • Tropism
