Transmogrification

Pronunciation:/trænzˌmɒɡrɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 变形
  • 转变
  • 变换

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of transforming into a different form, especially in a surprising or magical manner.

In ZH

以惊人或魔幻的方式转变成不同形态的行为。

Examples

  • The transmogrification of the caterpillar into a butterfly is a magical process.
  • In the story, the wizard's spell caused the transmogrification of the prince into a frog.
  • Her transmogrification from a shy girl to a confident performer amazed everyone.
  • The film featured the transmogrification of ordinary objects into fantastical creatures.
  • Transmogrification is a central theme in many fairy tales.
