Traverse

Pronunciation:/trəˈvɜrs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 穿越
  • 横贯
  • 横跨

Definitions

verb

Definition

to travel across or through something

In ZH

穿越或通过某物旅行

Examples

  • We will traverse the mountains on our hike.
  • The explorers traversed the dense forest.
  • She traverses the city by bike every day.
  • The river traverses the entire valley.
  • They had to traverse difficult terrain to reach the summit.
noun

Definition

an act of traveling across or through something

In ZH

穿越或通过某物的行为

Examples

  • His traverse across the desert took several days.
  • The traverse of the canyon was challenging for the team.
  • She completed the traverse in record time.
  • The hike includes a traverse of the rocky cliffs.
  • A rare traverse of the region brings in tourists.
