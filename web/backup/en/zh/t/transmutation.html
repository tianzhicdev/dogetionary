Transmutation

EN→ZH

What Does Transmutation Mean?

  • 转化
  • 变形
  • 易位

Transmutation Pronunciation

/trænzmjuːˈteɪʃən/

Transmutation Definition

noun

Definition

The act or process of changing from one form, nature, or substance into another.

In ZH

从一种形态、性质或物质转变为另一种形态的行为或过程。

How to Use Transmutation in a Sentence

  • The transmutation of lead into gold was a common goal for alchemists.
  • In modern physics, transmutation refers to the conversion of one chemical element into another.
  • The artist's work is often seen as a transmutation of emotions into visual form.
  • Transmutation can occur in chemical reactions where elements change into different substances.
  • Some myths and legends speak of transmutation as a magical transformation.
noun

Definition

A fundamental change in form or nature, especially in a philosophical or spiritual context.

In ZH

一种形式或性质的根本变化，尤其是在哲学或精神层面。

How to Use Transmutation in a Sentence

  • The transmutation of the soul is a theme found in many spiritual traditions.
  • In literature, transmutation often symbolizes personal growth or enlightenment.
  • The concept of transmutation is crucial in discussions of metamorphosis and transformation.
  • Philosophers have debated the implications of transmutation in understanding existence.
  • Transmutation in this context often reflects deeper existential questions.

Synonyms for Transmutation

  • Tractable
  • Trading
  • Tranquil
  • Tranquility
  • Tranquillity
