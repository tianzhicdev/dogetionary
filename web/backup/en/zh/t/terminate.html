Terminate

EN→ZH

What Does Terminate Mean?

  • 终止
  • 停止
  • 结束

Terminate Pronunciation

ˈtɜːrmɪneɪt

Terminate Definition

verb

Definition

To bring something to an end or halt; to stop something from continuing.

In ZH

使某事物结束或停止；让某事物不再继续。

How to Use Terminate in a Sentence

  • The company decided to terminate the project due to lack of funding.
  • If you violate the terms of your contract, we reserve the right to terminate your employment.
  • The software will terminate the program if it detects a virus.

Cultural Notes

The term 'terminate' is often used in formal contexts, such as business, legal matters, and technology. It can carry a strong connotation, especially in employment, where 'termination' refers to the end of employment.

verb

Definition

To end or conclude a relationship or agreement.

In ZH

结束或终止一种关系或协议。

How to Use Terminate in a Sentence

  • After several discussions, they agreed to terminate the partnership amicably.
  • The landlord decided to terminate the lease agreement due to multiple violations.
  • She felt it was time to terminate her friendship after the betrayal.

Cultural Notes

In personal relationships, 'to terminate' may sound harsh. People might choose softer terms like 'end' or 'conclude' in casual conversation.

noun

Definition

The act of terminating or ending something; a conclusion.

In ZH

终止或结束某事的行为；结论。

How to Use Terminate in a Sentence

  • The termination of the agreement was mutual and agreed upon by both parties.
  • He received a termination notice from his employer last week.
  • The termination of the service will take effect immediately.

Cultural Notes

As a noun, 'termination' is frequently used in legal documents and business contracts, emphasizing the finality of an agreement or service.

Synonyms for Terminate

  • Teratoid
  • Tergiversate
  • Tergiversation
  • Terminus
  • Ternion
