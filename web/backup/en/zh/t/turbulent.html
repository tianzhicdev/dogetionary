Turbulent

EN→ZH

What Does Turbulent Mean?

  • 动荡的
  • 剧烈变化的
  • 不稳定的

Turbulent Pronunciation

/ˈtɜːrbjələnt/

Turbulent Definition

adjective

Definition

characterized by conflict, disorder, or confusion; not stable or calm.

In ZH

以冲突、混乱或困扰为特征；不稳定或平静。

How to Use Turbulent in a Sentence

  • The turbulent waters of the river made it dangerous to navigate.
  • Their relationship became turbulent after the argument.
  • The company's turbulent history included several financial crises.
  • Turbulent weather often leads to flight delays.
  • The turbulent political climate resulted in protests.

Synonyms for Turbulent

  • Turbid
  • Turbulence
  • Turgid
