Tremors

Pronunciation:/ˈtrɛm.ərz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 震动
  • 颤动
  • 地震

Definitions

noun

Definition

Involuntary quaking or shaking.

In ZH

不自主的颤动或摇晃。

Examples

  • The tremors from the earthquake were felt miles away.
  • She experienced tremors in her hands as a side effect of the medication.
  • The building shook, and tremors rattled the windows.
  • He felt the tremors of anxiety before his speech.
  • The scientist measured the tremors to assess the seismic activity.
