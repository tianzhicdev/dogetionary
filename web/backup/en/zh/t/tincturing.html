Tincturing

EN→ZH

What Does Tincturing Mean?

  • 酊剂制作
  • 提取药物
  • 浸泡

Tincturing Pronunciation

/ˈtɪŋk.tʃər.ɪŋ/

Tincturing Definition

verb

Definition

The process of extracting the essence of a plant or herb by soaking it in alcohol or another solvent.

In ZH

通过将植物或草药浸泡在酒精或其他溶剂中提取其精华的过程。

How to Use Tincturing in a Sentence

  • Tincturing herbs is a common method in herbal medicine.
  • She is tincturing several different plants for her remedies.
  • The tincturing process can take several weeks to complete.
  • Many herbalists prefer tincturing over other extraction methods.
  • He learned tincturing techniques to create his own herbal remedies.

Synonyms for Tincturing

  • Tincture
  • Tinctured
  • Tintinnabulation
