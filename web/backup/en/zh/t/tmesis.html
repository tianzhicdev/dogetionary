Tmesis

EN→ZH

What Does Tmesis Mean?

  • 分开语素
  • 间隔句法
  • 分割法

Tmesis Pronunciation

/ˈtmēsɪs/

Tmesis Definition

noun

Definition

The separation of parts of a compound word by an intervening word or words.

In ZH

复合词的部分被中间的一个或多个词分隔开。

How to Use Tmesis in a Sentence

  • The phrase 'un-freaking-believable' is an example of tmesis.
  • In the word 'abso-bloomin-lutely', the insertion creates a dramatic effect.
  • She said, 'You are my best friend' using tmesis in her expression.
  • Tmesis often adds emphasis to a phrase.
  • Many poets utilize tmesis to enhance their lyrical compositions.
