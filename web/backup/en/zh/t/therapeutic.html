Therapeutic

Pronunciation:/ˌθɛrəˈpjuːtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 治疗的
  • 有疗效的
  • 康复的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to the healing of diseases or disorders; therapeutic.

In ZH

与疾病或疾病的治疗相关的；治疗性的。

Examples

  • The therapist recommended a therapeutic exercise routine.
  • Many patients find that therapeutic music helps with their stress.
  • She enrolled in a therapeutic program to help with her recovery.
  • The therapeutic properties of this herb have been well documented.
  • They are exploring therapeutic options for chronic pain management.
