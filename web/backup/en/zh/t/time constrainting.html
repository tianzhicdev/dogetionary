Time Constrainting

EN→ZH

What Does Time Constrainting Mean?

  • 时间约束
  • 时间限制

Time Constrainting Pronunciation

taɪm kənˈstreɪntɪŋ

Time Constrainting Definition

noun

Definition

The practice or state of limiting the time available for a task or activity.

In ZH

限制进行某项任务或活动所用时间的做法或状态。

How to Use Time Constrainting in a Sentence

  • We are facing some serious time constraints on this project.
  • Due to time constraints, we have to prioritize our tasks.
  • Time constrainting often leads to increased stress among team members.

Cultural Notes

In many professional environments, time constrainting is a common challenge that can affect productivity and quality of work.

verb

Definition

To impose limits on the duration of a particular task or activity.

In ZH

对某项特定任务或活动施加时间限制。

How to Use Time Constrainting in a Sentence

  • We need to time constraint the discussions to stay on schedule.
  • He decided to time constraint his study sessions for better focus.
  • Time constrainting the meeting helped us cover all important topics efficiently.

Cultural Notes

Time constrainting can also affect decision-making, as people might rush to complete tasks without thorough consideration.
