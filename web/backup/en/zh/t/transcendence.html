Transcendence

EN→ZH

What Does Transcendence Mean?

  • 超越
  • 超脱
  • 超凡

Transcendence Pronunciation

/trænˈsɛndəns/

Transcendence Definition

noun

Definition

The state of going beyond ordinary limits; surpassing, exceeding.

In ZH

超越普通界限的状态；超越、超出。

How to Use Transcendence in a Sentence

  • His transcendence was evident in his ability to overcome adversity.
  • The artist sought transcendence through his work.
  • In philosophy, transcendence refers to the condition of being beyond the limits of ordinary experience.
  • Many believe that love is a form of transcendence.
  • The concept of transcendence can be found in various religious texts.

Synonyms for Transcendence

  • Tractable
  • Trading
  • Tranquil
  • Tranquility
  • Tranquillity
