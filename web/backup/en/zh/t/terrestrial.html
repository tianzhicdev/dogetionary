Terrestrial

Pronunciation:/təˈrɛstrɪəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 陆生的
  • 地球的
  • 地面的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

relating to the earth or land; occurring on or inhabiting the earth

In ZH

与地球或陆地相关的；发生在地球上或栖息在地球上的

Examples

  • The terrestrial ecosystem is vital for maintaining biodiversity.
  • Scientists study terrestrial habitats to understand environmental changes.
  • Humans are primarily terrestrial beings rather than aquatic.
  • Terrestrial planets, like Earth, have solid surfaces.
  • The book explores the differences between terrestrial and aquatic life.
noun

Definition

a terrestrial being or organism; especially, an animal or plant that lives on land

In ZH

陆生生物，特别是生活在陆地上的动物或植物

Examples

  • The terrestrial is a key component of the food chain.
  • Many terrestrial species are threatened by habitat loss.
  • We need to protect terrestrial life from pollution.
  • Terrestrial organisms have adapted to survive in various climates.
  • The exhibit features both aquatic and terrestrial animals.
