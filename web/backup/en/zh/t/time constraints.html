Time Constraints

EN→ZH

What Does Time Constraints Mean?

  • 时间限制
  • 时间约束
  • 时间限制条件

Time Constraints Pronunciation

taɪm kənˈstreɪnts

Time Constraints Definition

noun

Definition

Limits or restrictions on the duration available to complete a task or project.

In ZH

完成任务或项目所需时间的限制或约束。

How to Use Time Constraints in a Sentence

  • Due to time constraints, we had to expedite our decision-making process.
  • The project was delayed because of time constraints imposed by the client.
  • She expressed her concerns about meeting the deadline given the time constraints.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, meeting deadlines is often seen as a measure of professional competence. Understanding how to manage time constraints effectively is critical in business and academic environments.

noun

Definition

A condition where available time is insufficient to undertake all desired activities or responsibilities.

In ZH

可用时间不足以承担所有想要的活动或责任的条件。

How to Use Time Constraints in a Sentence

  • The team had to prioritize tasks because of the time constraints they faced.
  • With time constraints, it’s important to focus on the most critical objectives first.
  • Students often experience time constraints during exam weeks.

Cultural Notes

Students in educational systems often face time constraints due to heavy workloads and tight schedules, which can affect their performance and stress levels.
