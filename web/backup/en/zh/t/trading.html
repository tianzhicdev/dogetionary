Trading

EN→ZH

What Does Trading Mean?

  • 交易
  • 贸易

Trading Pronunciation

ˈtreɪdɪŋ

Trading Definition

noun

Definition

The action of buying and selling goods and services.

In ZH

买卖商品和服务的行为。

How to Use Trading in a Sentence

  • The trading of stocks is a common practice in financial markets.
  • She is involved in trading various commodities like oil and gold.
  • Online trading has made it easier for individuals to invest in the stock market.

Cultural Notes

In modern economies, trading can occur at various levels, from local markets to international exchanges. It plays a crucial role in commerce and can involve complex financial instruments.

verb

Definition

To buy and sell goods or services; to exchange one thing for another.

In ZH

买卖商品或服务；将一种东西交换为另一种。

How to Use Trading in a Sentence

  • They have been trading their old furniture for new appliances.
  • He enjoys trading football cards with his friends.
  • The shop trades in rare books and collectibles.

Cultural Notes

Trading is not limited to physical goods; it also applies to services, currencies, and other assets. It can involve negotiation and an understanding of market value.

Synonyms for Trading

  • Tranquil
  • Tranquility
  • Tranquillity
  • Transcendence
