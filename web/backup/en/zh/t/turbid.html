Turbid

EN→ZH

What Does Turbid Mean?

  • 浑浊的
  • 不清晰的

Turbid Pronunciation

/ˈtɜːrbɪd/

Turbid Definition

adjective

Definition

not clear or transparent because of stirred-up sediment or the presence of particles; muddy or cloudy

In ZH

由于搅动沉淀物或颗粒的存在而不清晰或透明; 混浊或模糊

How to Use Turbid in a Sentence

  • The river was turbid after the heavy rain.
  • She filtered the turbid water before drinking.
  • His explanation was turbid and difficult to comprehend.
  • The artist used turbid colors to create a more dramatic effect.
  • The turbid atmosphere in the room made it hard to breathe.

Synonyms for Turbid

  • Turbulence
  • Turbulent
  • Turgid
