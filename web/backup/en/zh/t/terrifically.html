Terrifically

EN→ZH

What Does Terrifically Mean?

  • 非常
  • 极好
  • 极其

Terrifically Pronunciation

/təˈrɪfɪkli/

Terrifically Definition

adverb

Definition

In a terrific manner; very well or greatly.

In ZH

以极好的方式；非常好或极大地。

How to Use Terrifically in a Sentence

  • The performance was terrifically entertaining.
  • She did terrifically on her exam.
  • He is terrifically talented at playing the piano.
  • The team played terrifically in the championship game.
  • I felt terrifically happy after hearing the good news.

Cultural Notes

The word 'terrifically' is often used to express enthusiasm and positivity in spoken and written English.

Synonyms for Terrifically

  • Terminate
  • Terminus
