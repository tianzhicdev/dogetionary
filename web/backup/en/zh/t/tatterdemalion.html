Tatterdemalion

Pronunciation:/ˌtætərdɪˈmeɪliən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 衣衫褴褛的人
  • 蓬头垢面的人

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person in ragged clothing; a ragamuffin.

In ZH

衣衫褴褛的人；流浪者。

Examples

  • The children appeared like little tatterdemalions after playing outside in the mud.
  • He was a tatterdemalion, his clothes hanging off him in rags.
  • The street was filled with tatterdemalions begging for change.
  • In the fairytale, the prince fell in love with a tatterdemalion who turned out to be a princess.
  • The artist often painted scenes of tatterdemalions living in the slums.
