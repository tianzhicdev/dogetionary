Tedious

EN→ZH

What Does Tedious Mean?

  • 冗长的
  • 乏味的
  • 单调的

Tedious Pronunciation

/ˈtiːdiəs/

Tedious Definition

adjective

Definition

too long, slow, or dull; tiresome or monotonous

In ZH

过长、缓慢或单调；令人厌倦或单调的

How to Use Tedious in a Sentence

  • The lecture was so tedious that many students fell asleep.
  • He found the process of data entry to be exceedingly tedious.
  • I had to finish a tedious report for work last night.
  • Reading through the tedious instructions made me lose interest quickly.
  • Some tasks can be tedious, but they are also important for the overall project.
