Tryst

Pronunciation:/trɪst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幽会
  • 约会
  • 密会

Definitions

noun

Definition

A private romantic rendezvous between lovers.

In ZH

恋人之间私密的约会。

Examples

  • They planned a secret tryst at the old cabin.
  • She wrote him a love letter to arrange their next tryst.
  • The couple enjoyed their tryst under the stars.
  • His heart raced at the thought of their clandestine tryst.
  • They had a tryst every Friday evening.

Cultural Notes

The term 'tryst' often carries a connotation of secrecy or illicit love.
