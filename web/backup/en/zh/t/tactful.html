Tactful

EN→ZH

What Does Tactful Mean?

  • 机智的
  • 得体的
  • 圆滑的

Tactful Pronunciation

ˈtæktfəl

Tactful Definition

adjective

Definition

Having or showing a sense of what is fitting and considerate in dealing with others.

In ZH

拥有或表现出处理他人事务时的恰当和体贴的感觉。

How to Use Tactful in a Sentence

  • She was tactful in her response, making sure not to offend anyone.
  • His tactful remarks helped ease the tension in the room.
  • Being tactful is important when giving constructive criticism.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, being tactful is considered a sign of emotional intelligence and respect for others' feelings. It is especially important in professional settings where sensitive topics may arise.

noun

Definition

The quality of being tactful; sensitivity in dealing with others or with difficult issues.

In ZH

机智的品质；在处理他人或困难问题时的敏感度。

How to Use Tactful in a Sentence

  • Tactfulness is essential when managing a team.
  • Her tactfulness made her a great mediator in conflicts.
  • Developing tactfulness can improve interpersonal relationships.

Cultural Notes

In negotiations or discussions, tactfulness can lead to better outcomes by avoiding conflicts and fostering collaboration.

Synonyms for Tactful

  • Tacit
  • Taciturn
  • Taciturnity
