Trenchant

EN→ZH

What Does Trenchant Mean?

  • 尖锐的
  • 犀利的
  • 深刻的

Trenchant Pronunciation

/ˈtrɛn.tʃənt/

Trenchant Definition

adjective

Definition

Incisive or sharp in expression or style; having a keen intellect or insight.

In ZH

表达或风格上尖锐或锋利；具有敏锐的智力或洞察力。

How to Use Trenchant in a Sentence

  • Her trenchant remarks cut through the nonsense of the debate.
  • He has a trenchant wit that often leaves people speechless.
  • The article provided a trenchant analysis of the current political climate.
  • His trenchant observations on society were published in leading journals.
  • She delivered a trenchant critique of the government's policies.

Synonyms for Trenchant

  • Trembling
  • Tremolo
  • Tremors
  • Tremulous
  • Tremulousness
