Trapeze

EN→ZH

What Does Trapeze Mean?

  • 秋千
  • 空中秋千

Trapeze Pronunciation

/trəˈpiːz/

Trapeze Definition

noun

Definition

a swing used by acrobats, suspended by two ropes from a frame or from the ceiling.

In ZH

一种由两根绳子从框架或天花板悬挂而来的，供杂技演员使用的秋千。

How to Use Trapeze in a Sentence

  • The acrobat flew gracefully through the air on the trapeze.
  • She practiced her routine on the trapeze every day.
  • Trapeze artists often train for years to perfect their skills.
  • The circus performance featured several amazing trapeze acts.
  • He was thrilled to take a trapeze class for the first time.

Synonyms for Trapeze

  • Tractable
  • Trading
  • Tranquil
  • Tranquility
  • Tranquillity
