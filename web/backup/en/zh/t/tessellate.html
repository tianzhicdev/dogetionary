Tessellate

EN→ZH

What Does Tessellate Mean?

  • 镶嵌
  • 拼接

Tessellate Pronunciation

/ˈtɛs.ə.leɪt/

Tessellate Definition

verb

Definition

To create a pattern of shapes that fit together without any gaps or overlaps.

In ZH

创建一个形状的图案，这些形状紧密贴合，没有任何缝隙或重叠。

How to Use Tessellate in a Sentence

  • The artist decided to tessellate the floor with colorful tiles.
  • Mathematicians study how certain polygons can tessellate a plane.
  • In her design, she chose to tessellate various geometric shapes.
  • The ancient mosaics tessellated beautifully across the courtyard.
  • Using a computer program, he was able to tessellate the image efficiently.
