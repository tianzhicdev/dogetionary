Trip

Pronunciation:trɪp
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 旅程
  • 旅行
  • 绊倒

Definitions

noun

Definition

A journey or excursion, especially for pleasure.

In ZH

一次旅行，尤其是为了娱乐或游玩而进行的短途旅程。

Examples

  • I went on a trip to Hawaii and came back with a sunburn that was more colorful than my souvenirs!
  • My last trip to the grocery store was so long, I thought I should start sending postcards from the cheese aisle.
  • I planned a trip to the moon, but they told me they were fully booked until 3023!

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking countries, a 'trip' often refers to shorter journeys compared to 'voyage' or 'expedition', which imply longer travels.

verb

Definition

To stumble or fall; to cause someone to fall by catching their foot on something.

In ZH

绊倒；使某人因脚被某物绊倒而摔倒。

Examples

  • I tripped over my cat while trying to show off my dance moves – I think it's safe to say I will not be going viral!
  • Every time I try to impress someone with my balance, I end up tripping and making a fool of myself – my friends call it 'performance art'.
  • I tripped on a rock while hiking, and I’m now the unofficial spokesperson for hiking shoes!

Cultural Notes

In casual conversation, 'trip' as a verb is often used to describe funny or embarrassing moments.
