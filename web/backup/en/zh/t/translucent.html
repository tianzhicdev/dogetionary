Translucent

Pronunciation:/trænsˈluːsənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 半透明的
  • 透光的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Allowing light to pass through but not detailed shapes.

In ZH

允许光通过但不能透视细节的形状。

Examples

  • The translucent curtains let in the soft morning light.
  • She chose a translucent dress for the summer party.
  • The paper was translucent enough to see the text underneath.
  • Translucent jellyfish drifted gracefully in the water.
  • They installed translucent panels in the roof to brighten the room.
