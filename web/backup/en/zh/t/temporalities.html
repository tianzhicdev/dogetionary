Temporalities

Pronunciation:/tɛmpəˈrælətiːz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 时间性
  • 暂时性
  • 时间范畴

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality or state of being temporal; a concept that refers to time or temporal existence.

In ZH

时间的特质或状态；指时间或时间存在的概念。

Examples

  • The temporalities of human life are often overlooked in philosophical discussions.
  • In our modern world, we frequently neglect the importance of temporalities in our decision-making.
  • Different cultures have various understandings of temporalities and their influence on daily life.
  • Temporalities can greatly affect how individuals perceive their lifespan and achievements.
  • The study of temporalities helps us understand the relationship between time and consciousness.
