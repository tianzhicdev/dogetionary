Taxidermy

EN→ZH

What Does Taxidermy Mean?

  • 标本制作
  • 动物标本
  • 剥制

Taxidermy Pronunciation

/ˈtæksɪˌdɜrmi/

Taxidermy Definition

noun

Definition

The art or practice of preparing, stuffing, and mounting the skins of animals for display or study.

In ZH

将动物皮肤进行准备、填充和装配以供展示或研究的艺术或实践。

How to Use Taxidermy in a Sentence

  • The museum has an impressive collection of taxidermy animals.
  • He studied taxidermy to learn how to preserve specimens.
  • Taxidermy allows us to appreciate the beauty of wildlife even after death.
  • Many hunters learn taxidermy to preserve their trophies.
  • Taxidermy has been a part of natural history museums for centuries.
