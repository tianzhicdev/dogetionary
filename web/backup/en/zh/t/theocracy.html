Theocracy

Pronunciation:/θiˈɒkrəsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 神权政治
  • 神治国
  • 政教合一

Definitions

noun

Definition

A system of government in which priests rule in the name of God or a god.

In ZH

一种政府体系，在这种体系中，祭司以上帝或神的名义统治。

Examples

  • In a theocracy, the laws are often based on religious doctrines.
  • Iran is an example of a theocracy where the Supreme Leader holds significant power.
  • Many critics argue that theocracy limits personal freedoms and secular governance.
  • The ancient Egyptians practiced a form of theocracy where the Pharaoh was considered a divine figure.
  • The transition from a democracy to a theocracy can create significant societal tensions.
