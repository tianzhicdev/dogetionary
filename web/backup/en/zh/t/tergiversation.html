Tergiversation

Pronunciation:/ˌtɜːr.dʒɪ.vɚˈseɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 推诿
  • 含糊其辞

Definitions

noun

Definition

the act of deliberately evasive or ambiguous behavior; the act of avoiding a straightforward answer or action

In ZH

故意模棱两可或模糊不清的行为；避免明确回答或行动的行为
