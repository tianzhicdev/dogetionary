Theosophy

EN→ZH

What Does Theosophy Mean?

  • 神智学
  • 神秘学

Theosophy Pronunciation

/θiˈɒsəfi/

Theosophy Definition

noun

Definition

A religious philosophy that seeks to understand the nature of divinity and the universe through the study of spiritual truths and metaphysical knowledge.

In ZH

一种宗教哲学，通过研究精神真理和形而上学知识，试图理解神性和宇宙的本质。

How to Use Theosophy in a Sentence

  • Theosophy teaches that all religions hold some truth.
  • Many practitioners of theosophy believe in the unity of all life.
  • Her interest in theosophy led her to explore various spiritual systems.
  • Theosophy often emphasizes personal spiritual experience over dogma.
  • He wrote a book explaining the principles of theosophy.

Cultural Notes

Theosophy has influenced various spiritual movements and is often associated with figures like Helena Blavatsky.
