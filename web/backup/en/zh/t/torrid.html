Torrid

EN→ZH

What Does Torrid Mean?

  • 炎热的
  • 酷热的
  • 热情的

Torrid Pronunciation

/ˈtɒr.ɪd/

Torrid Definition

adjective

Definition

very hot and dry; characterized by intense heat or passion

In ZH

非常炎热和干燥；以强烈的热量或激情为特征

How to Use Torrid in a Sentence

  • The torrid sun beat down on the desert landscape.
  • They had a torrid love affair that lasted for years.
  • The torrid summer heat made everyone seek shelter indoors.
  • He described his experience in the torrid climate of the tropics.
  • The book describes the torrid romance between the two main characters.

Synonyms for Torrid

  • Torpid
  • Torpor
