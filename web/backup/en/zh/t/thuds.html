Thuds

EN→ZH

What Does Thuds Mean?

  • 重击声
  • 沉闷声
  • 碰撞声

Thuds Pronunciation

/θʌdz/

Thuds Definition

noun

Definition

A dull sound made when something heavy falls or hits a surface.

In ZH

当某物重物落下或撞击表面时发出的沉闷声音。

How to Use Thuds in a Sentence

  • I heard the thuds of heavy boxes being dropped.
  • The thuds of the drums echoed through the hall.
  • She winced at the thuds coming from the construction site.
  • The thuds of his footsteps could be heard in the quiet hallway.
  • He felt the thuds in his chest after running up the stairs.
verb

Definition

To make a thud sound; to fall or hit heavily.

In ZH

发出沉闷声；重重落下或撞击。

How to Use Thuds in a Sentence

  • He thuds the book onto the table.
  • The large stone thudded to the ground.
  • The child thudded down the stairs in a hurry.
  • The bag thudded against the door as he rushed out.
  • The tree thudded when it fell in the storm.
