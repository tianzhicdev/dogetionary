Titular

EN→ZH

What Does Titular Mean?

  • 名义上的
  • 名义的
  • 标题的

Titular Pronunciation

/ˈtɪtʃələr/

Titular Definition

adjective

Definition

describing something that has a title but is not actively involved or significant; often used in reference to a character or position that exists primarily in name only.

In ZH

描述某物有标题但并不积极参与或重要; 通常用于指主要仅在名称上存在的角色或职位。

How to Use Titular in a Sentence

  • The titular character in the movie had very little screen time.
  • She held the titular role in the play, but there were far more important characters.
  • In the organization, he was merely the titular head without real power.
  • The book was about the titular city, which plays a major role in the story.
  • His position as the titular leader was mostly ceremonial.
