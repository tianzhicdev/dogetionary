Time Allocation

EN→ZH

What Does Time Allocation Mean?

  • 时间分配
  • 时间配置
  • 时间安排

Time Allocation Pronunciation

taɪm ˌæl.əˈkeɪ.ʃən

Time Allocation Definition

noun

Definition

The process of distributing available time for various tasks or activities.

In ZH

将可用时间分配给各种任务或活动的过程。

How to Use Time Allocation in a Sentence

  • Effective time allocation can lead to increased productivity.
  • She struggles with time allocation when managing multiple projects.
  • Time allocation is crucial for balancing work and personal life.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, especially in business environments, effective time allocation is considered a key factor in achieving success and maintaining balance. It reflects on one's organizational skills.

verb

Definition

To assign or designate specific amounts of time to particular tasks or activities.

In ZH

将特定的时间量分配给特定的任务或活动。

How to Use Time Allocation in a Sentence

  • We must allocate our time wisely to meet the project deadline.
  • He decided to allocate more time to studying for his exams.
  • It’s important to allocate time for rest in a busy schedule.

Cultural Notes

In cultures that value time management, such as in many Western countries, the ability to allocate time efficiently is often viewed as a sign of professionalism and personal responsibility.

Synonyms for Time Allocation

  • Time Constrainting
  • Time Constraints
  • Timorous
