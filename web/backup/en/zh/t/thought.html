Thought

EN→ZH

What Does Thought Mean?

  • 思想
  • 想法
  • 思考

Thought Pronunciation

θɔt

Thought Definition

noun

Definition

The process of thinking, especially as a cognitive activity.

In ZH

思考的过程，尤其是作为一种认知活动。

How to Use Thought in a Sentence

  • She had a wonderful thought about how to solve the problem.
  • His thoughts were filled with ideas for the new project.
  • After a long thought, she decided to take the job.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'thought' can also imply a deep consideration on issues, which could vary in context from casual reflections to serious deliberations.

noun

Definition

An idea or opinion produced by thinking, or occurring suddenly in the mind.

In ZH

由思考产生的想法或意见，或突然出现在心中的想法。

How to Use Thought in a Sentence

  • That was a brilliant thought that nobody had considered before.
  • She expressed her thought on the matter clearly and confidently.
  • His initial thought was to decline the offer, but he reconsidered.

Cultural Notes

In conversational English, expressing your 'thoughts' can be a way of engaging in discussions, showing respect for others' opinions and encouraging dialogue.

verb

Definition

To think or to have a thought; to reason mentally.

In ZH

思考或有思想；在心里推理。

How to Use Thought in a Sentence

  • I often thought about moving to a different city.
  • She was thinking of a way to improve her skills.
  • They thought carefully before making a decision.

Cultural Notes

The verb form 'to think' is commonly used to suggest contemplation or consideration, often regarded as an important part of decision-making in English-speaking cultures.
