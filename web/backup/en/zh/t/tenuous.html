Tenuous

Pronunciation:/ˈtɛnjuəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 微弱的
  • 稀薄的
  • 脆弱的
  • 不稳固的
  • 空洞的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Very weak or slight; having little substance or strength.

In ZH

非常微弱或轻微；没有多少实质或力量。

Examples

  • The connection between the two theories is rather tenuous.
  • Her grasp of the subject was tenuous at best.
  • They had only a tenuous friendship based on past experiences.
  • The evidence for his claims was found to be quite tenuous.
  • Their tenuous hold on the lead made the final moments of the game very tense.
