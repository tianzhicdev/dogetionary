Tesseract

Pronunciation:/ˈtɛsəˌrækt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 超立方体
  • 四维立方体

Definitions

noun

Definition

A four-dimensional geometric shape that is an extension of a cube into the fourth dimension.

In ZH

一个四维几何形状，是立方体在第四维的扩展。

Examples

  • A tesseract can be thought of as a cube within a cube.
  • In science fiction, a tesseract can be a portal to other dimensions.
  • Mathematicians often study the properties of a tesseract in higher-dimensional geometry.
  • In the film, the tesseract serves as a powerful energy source.
  • The concept of a tesseract challenges our understanding of space and dimensions.
