Tweets

Pronunciation:twiːts
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 推文
  • 微博
  • 鸟叫声

Definitions

noun

Definition

Posts made on the social media platform Twitter, typically containing short messages limited to 280 characters.

In ZH

在社交媒体平台Twitter上发布的帖子，通常包含限制在280个字符以内的短消息。

Examples

  • Did you see the tweet where a cat has more followers than a famous politician?
  • My friend tweeted about his breakfast, and now I feel like I've just gained 5 pounds by looking at it!
  • Breaking news: A local squirrel tweeted about stealing a peanut from a rival, and now it's trending!

Cultural Notes

Tweets are often used for sharing opinions, news, or funny observations. The term 'tweet' has become a significant part of internet culture.

verb

Definition

To post a message on Twitter.

In ZH

在Twitter上发布消息。

Examples

  • I just tweeted a picture of my dog wearing sunglasses; I think he might be the next big influencer!
  • After tweeting about my terrible cooking, I gained followers who just want to see my kitchen disasters.
  • I tweeted my opinion on the latest superhero movie, and now I'm getting arguments from fans of both sides!

Cultural Notes

Tweeting is a quick way to communicate thoughts or updates. Many celebrities and public figures use it for direct engagement with fans.
