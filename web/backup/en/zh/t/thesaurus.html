Thesaurus

Pronunciation:/θɪˈsɔːrəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 同义词词典
  • 词库

Definitions

noun

Definition

A book or database that provides synonyms and sometimes antonyms for words.

In ZH

一种提供单词同义词，有时也提供反义词的书籍或数据库。

Examples

  • I consulted a thesaurus to find synonyms for 'happy'.
  • The writer used a thesaurus to enhance her vocabulary.
  • A thesaurus can help you avoid repetition in your writing.
  • He found the word he needed in the thesaurus.
  • For better expression, she refers to a thesaurus frequently.
