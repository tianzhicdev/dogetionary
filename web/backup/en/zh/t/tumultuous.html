Tumultuous

Pronunciation:/tjuːˈmʌl.tʃu.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 动荡的
  • 喧闹的
  • 骚动的
  • 剧烈的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by disorder, chaos, or commotion; noisy and turbulent

In ZH

以混乱、混沌或骚动为特征；嘈杂而动荡的

Examples

  • The tumultuous waves crashed against the shore.
  • Her tumultuous emotions made it difficult for her to focus.
  • The meeting was tumultuous, with everyone shouting their opinions at once.
  • Their tumultuous relationship often led to dramatic arguments.
  • The tumultuous political climate caused widespread unrest.
