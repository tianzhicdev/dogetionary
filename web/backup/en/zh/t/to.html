To

EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 到
  • 向
  • 对于
  • 为了

Definitions

Definition

Used to indicate direction, place, or position.

In ZH

用于表示方向、地点或位置。

Examples

  • I walked to the store, but it was closed because the owner was busy trying to teach his cat to fetch.
  • She sent a letter to her friend, but it got lost because the postman was too busy watching cat videos.
  • He traveled to the mountains, only to find out that the only thing higher than the peaks was his WiFi signal!

Definition

Used before the base form of a verb to indicate the infinitive form.

In ZH

用于动词的基本形式之前，表示不定式形式。

Examples

  • I want to learn how to juggle, but my only talent is dropping things.
  • She hopes to become a chef, but her specialty is burning toast.
  • He plans to start a podcast, but his only experience is talking to his plants.

Definition

Used to indicate a limit or extent.

In ZH

用于表示限制或程度。

Examples

  • I can run to the end of the street, but only if there's a pizza waiting for me.
  • She can sing to the top of her lungs, especially when her favorite song comes on, even if the neighbors don't appreciate it.
  • He can stay awake to the crack of dawn, but only if there's a marathon of his favorite show on TV.
