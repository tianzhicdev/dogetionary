Tender-Hearted

EN→ZH

What Does Tender-Hearted Mean?

  • 温柔的心
  • 善良的心
  • 心肠柔软的

Tender-Hearted Pronunciation

/ˈtɛndər ˈhɑːrtɪd/

Tender-Hearted Definition

adjective

Definition

Having a kind, gentle, and sympathetic nature; compassionate.

In ZH

具有善良、温柔和富有同情心的特点；富有同情心。

How to Use Tender-Hearted in a Sentence

  • She is a tender-hearted person who always helps others in need.
  • Despite his tough exterior, he has a tender-hearted soul.
  • The story touched the tender-hearted among us.
  • Her tender-hearted nature makes her a great friend.
  • He can't bear to watch sad movies because he is so tender-hearted.

Synonyms for Tender-Hearted

  • Tenebrous
  • Tentative
  • Tenuous
