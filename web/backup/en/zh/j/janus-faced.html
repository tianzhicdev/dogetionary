Janus-Faced

EN→ZH

What Does Janus-Faced Mean?

  • 双面人
  • 狡诈的人
  • 虚伪的人

Janus-Faced Pronunciation

/ˈdʒeɪnəs feɪst/

Janus-Faced Definition

adjective

Definition

Having two contrasting aspects or phases, often used to describe someone who is deceitful or hypocritical.

In ZH

具有两种对比的方面或阶段，常用于描述一个狡诈或虚伪的人。

How to Use Janus-Faced in a Sentence

  • The politician was often described as janus-faced for his contradictory statements.
  • In literature, the janus-faced character adds complexity to the narrative.
  • Her janus-faced nature made it difficult for people to trust her fully.
  • The company's janus-faced approach to business led to both success and criticism.
  • Some critics argue that his janus-faced ideology confuses his followers.
