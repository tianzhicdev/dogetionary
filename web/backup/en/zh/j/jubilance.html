Jubilance

EN→ZH

What Does Jubilance Mean?

  • 欢腾
  • 喜悦
  • 庆祝

Jubilance Pronunciation

/ˈdʒuː.bɪ.ləns/

Jubilance Definition

noun

Definition

A feeling of great joy and celebration.

In ZH

一种极大的快乐和庆祝的感觉。

How to Use Jubilance in a Sentence

  • The jubilance of the crowd was infectious.
  • Her eyes sparkled with jubilance as she received the award.
  • After the victory, the team took to the streets in jubilance.
  • The jubilance at the festival brought everyone together.
  • His jubilance was evident in his laughter and demeanor.

Synonyms for Jubilance

  • Jubilant
  • Jubilation
