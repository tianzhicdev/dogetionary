Journal

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒɜːrnəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 期刊
  • 日志
  • 日记

Definitions

noun

Definition

A daily record of subjective experiences, observations, and feelings.

In ZH

个人经历、观察和感受的日常记录。

Examples

  • I decided to keep a journal to document my travels.
  • She writes in her journal every night before bed.
  • His journal entries reflect his thoughts on current events.
  • They published a journal on environmental science.
  • The students were asked to write a journal about their learning experiences.
noun

Definition

A periodical publication containing articles and information on a particular subject.

In ZH

包含特定主题的文章和信息的定期出版物。

Examples

  • He subscribes to a scientific journal for his research.
  • The journal of psychology releases new studies every month.
  • She wrote an article for a literary journal.
  • The journal of history is well-respected in academic circles.
  • They are editing the latest issue of the medical journal.
