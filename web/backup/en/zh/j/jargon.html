Jargon

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒɑːrɡən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 行话
  • 术语
  • 行业术语

Definitions

noun

Definition

Special words or expressions used by a profession or group that are difficult for others to understand.

In ZH

由某个职业或群体使用的专门词汇或表达，难以为他人理解。

Examples

  • Doctors often use medical jargon that can confuse their patients.
  • The software engineers communicated using programming jargon that was incomprehensible to outsiders.
  • During the meeting, the lawyer's jargon made the contract difficult to follow.
  • Understanding the jargon of the tech industry is essential for new employees.
  • Many people find legal jargon to be overly complex and confusing.
