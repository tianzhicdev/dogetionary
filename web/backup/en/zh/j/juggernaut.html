Juggernaut

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒʌɡərnɔːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 巨头
  • 强大力量
  • 压倒性的力量

Definitions

noun

Definition

A massive inexorable force, campaign, or a large vehicle that crushes everything in its path.

In ZH

一种巨大的不可阻挡的力量、运动，或一种在其路径上摧毁一切的大型车辆。

Examples

  • The advertising juggernaut of the new blockbuster is overwhelming.
  • The company's growth has become a juggernaut in the market.
  • He was a juggernaut in the world of athletics, breaking numerous records.
  • The new policy became a juggernaut, shaping the future of the industry.
  • The political campaign turned into a juggernaut as more supporters joined.
