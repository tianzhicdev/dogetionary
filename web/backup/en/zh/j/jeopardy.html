Jeopardy

EN→ZH

What Does Jeopardy Mean?

  • 危险
  • 劫难
  • 危机

Jeopardy Pronunciation

/ˈdʒɛpərdi/

Jeopardy Definition

noun

Definition

Danger of loss, harm, or failure.

In ZH

丧失、伤害或失败的危险。

How to Use Jeopardy in a Sentence

  • He put his career in jeopardy by making that decision.
  • The hikers were in jeopardy when the storm hit.
  • His health is in jeopardy due to the lack of proper care.
  • The project was in jeopardy because of funding issues.
  • They faced jeopardy during the dangerous expedition.

Synonyms for Jeopardy

  • Jeopardize
