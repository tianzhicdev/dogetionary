Jubilant

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒuː.bɪ.lənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 喜庆的
  • 欢欣的
  • 兴高采烈的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

feeling or expressing great happiness and triumph

In ZH

感到或表达极大的快乐和胜利

Examples

  • The crowd was jubilant after the team won the championship.
  • She felt jubilant when she received the news of her promotion.
  • Their jubilant celebration lasted well into the night.
  • He had a jubilant smile on his face when he saw his friends.
  • The jubilant atmosphere at the festival was contagious.
