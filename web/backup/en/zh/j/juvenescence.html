Juvenescence

EN→ZH

What Does Juvenescence Mean?

  • 青春期
  • 青年期
  • 年轻的状态

Juvenescence Pronunciation

/ˌdʒuː.vəˈnɛs.əns/

Juvenescence Definition

noun

Definition

the state of being youthful or juvenile; the process of growing or being young.

In ZH

年轻或青少年状态；成长或年轻的过程。

How to Use Juvenescence in a Sentence

  • The juvenescence of the plants in spring is a beautiful sight.
  • He felt a sense of juvenescence when he returned to his childhood home.
  • Art often reflects the spirit of juvenescence in its themes and subjects.
  • Juvenescence can be a time of great exploration and self-discovery.
  • In literature, juvenescence is often depicted as a time of innocence and adventure.
