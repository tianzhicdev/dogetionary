Jocular

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒɒkjʊlər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 滑稽的
  • 爱开玩笑的
  • 幽默的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by joking or jesting; playful.

In ZH

以开玩笑或玩乐为特征；顽皮的。

Examples

  • His jocular personality makes him a favorite at parties.
  • She often tells jocular stories that leave everyone laughing.
  • The jocular tone of the conversation lightened the mood.
  • Although he was serious at times, he maintained a jocular attitude.
  • The comedian's jocular style captivated the audience.
