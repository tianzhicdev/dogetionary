Jamboree

Pronunciation:/ˌdʒæm.bəˈriː/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 欢乐聚会
  • 大会
  • 狂欢

Definitions

noun

Definition

A large celebration or party, often featuring a variety of activities and entertainment.

In ZH

一种大型庆祝活动或聚会，通常有多种活动和娱乐节目。

Examples

  • The annual jamboree attracted thousands of visitors from all over the country.
  • Our town is hosting a jamboree this summer with music, food, and games.
  • She was excited to attend the jamboree and meet new friends.
  • The jamboree was a huge success, bringing together families and communities.
  • At the jamboree, participants shared stories and experiences around the campfire.

Cultural Notes

Jamborees are often associated with scouting organizations and can include outdoor activities, competitions, and community service.
