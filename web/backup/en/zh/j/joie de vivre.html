Joie De Vivre

EN→ZH

What Does Joie De Vivre Mean?

  • 生活的乐趣
  • 生活的喜悦
  • 活力

Joie De Vivre Pronunciation

/ˌʒwɑː.dəˈvi.vʁ/

Joie De Vivre Definition

noun

Definition

A cheerful enjoyment of life; an exultation of spirit.

In ZH

对生活的愉悦；精神的欢愉。

How to Use Joie De Vivre in a Sentence

  • She approached her work with such joie de vivre that everyone around her couldn't help but be inspired.
  • His joie de vivre was infectious, and soon everyone at the party was laughing and dancing.
  • In the midst of winter, she found joy in the simple pleasures of life, embodying a true joie de vivre.
  • Traveling to new places always fills him with a sense of joie de vivre.
  • Even in tough times, he maintained his joie de vivre, reminding others to appreciate the small things.

Cultural Notes

The term 'joie de vivre' is often used in French culture to express a particular zest for life and an attitude of enjoying the present moment.
