Jeremiad

Pronunciation:/ˌdʒɛrəˈmaɪæd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 悲惨的故事
  • 哀叹
  • 悲伤的长篇大论

Definitions

noun

Definition

a long, mournful complaint or lamentation; a list of woes.

In ZH

一篇漫长而悲伤的诉说或哀叹；一系列的忧虑。

Examples

  • The article was a jeremiad about the decline of moral values in society.
  • In his jeremiad, the politician outlined the nation's many problems.
  • She often writes jeremiads about environmental degradation.
  • He delivered a jeremiad that resonated with the audience's fears.
  • The novel is a jeremiad against the injustices of the modern world.
