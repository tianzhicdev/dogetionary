Jeopardize

EN→ZH

What Does Jeopardize Mean?

  • 危害
  • 损害
  • 危及

Jeopardize Pronunciation

/ˈdʒɛpərdaɪz/

Jeopardize Definition

verb

Definition

to put something at risk or in danger

In ZH

使某物处于风险或危险之中

How to Use Jeopardize in a Sentence

  • If you don't study, you jeopardize your chances of passing the exam.
  • They decided to jeopardize their safety for the sake of adventure.
  • His careless driving could jeopardize the lives of others on the road.
  • She wouldn't want to jeopardize her career by making a bad decision.
  • Failing to follow safety protocols could jeopardize the entire project.

Synonyms for Jeopardize

  • Jeopardy
