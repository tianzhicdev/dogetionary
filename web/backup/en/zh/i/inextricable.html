Inextricable

Pronunciation:/ˌɪnˈɛkstrɪkəbl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不可分割的
  • 密不可分的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

impossible to disentangle or separate; closely involved.

In ZH

无法解开的；密切相关的。

Examples

  • Their fates were inextricable from one another.
  • The inextricable link between poverty and education cannot be ignored.
  • The story weaves an inextricable narrative that captivates the audience.
  • Her emotions were inextricable from her memories of the place.
  • The problems are inextricable, making a solution difficult.
