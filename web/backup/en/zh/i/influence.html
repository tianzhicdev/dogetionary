Influence

Pronunciation:/ˈɪn.flu.əns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 影响
  • 感化
  • 作用

Definitions

noun

Definition

The capacity or power to have an effect on the character, development, or behavior of someone or something.

In ZH

影响某人或某事的性格、发展或行为的能力或权力。

Examples

  • Her influence on his career was significant.
  • The influence of social media is undeniable.
  • He has a strong influence in the community.
  • Music can have a profound influence on emotions.
  • Parents have a major influence on their children's values.
verb

Definition

To have an effect on the character, development, or behavior of someone or something.

In ZH

对某人或某事的性格、发展或行为产生影响。

Examples

  • The teacher influenced her students positively.
  • Advertising influences consumer choices.
  • He tries to influence public opinion through his writings.
  • Personal experiences can influence our decisions.
  • Friends can greatly influence our actions.
