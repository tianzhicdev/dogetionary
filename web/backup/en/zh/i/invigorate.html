What Does Invigorate Mean?

Invigorate Pronunciation

/ɪnˈvɪɡəreɪt/

Invigorate Definition

verb

Definition

to give strength or energy to

In ZH

给予力量或能量

How to Use Invigorate in a Sentence

  • The morning coffee helped to invigorate me for the day ahead.
  • She took a brisk walk in the park to invigorate her mind.
  • The new policies are designed to invigorate the economy.
  • We need to invigorate our approach to customer service.
  • A good night's sleep can invigorate your body and mind.
