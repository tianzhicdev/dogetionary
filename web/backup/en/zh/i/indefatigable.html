Indefatigable

Pronunciation:/ˌɪndɪˈfætɪɡəbəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不知疲倦的
  • 不屈不挠的
  • 坚持不懈的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Persisting tirelessly; incapable of being fatigued.

In ZH

不知疲倦的；无法感到疲倦的。

Examples

  • Her indefatigable spirit inspired everyone around her.
  • The volunteers worked indefatigably to help the community rebuild after the disaster.
  • He has shown an indefatigable commitment to improving education.
  • Despite the challenges, she remained indefatigable in her efforts to complete the project.
  • The team's indefatigable pursuit of excellence has earned them numerous awards.
