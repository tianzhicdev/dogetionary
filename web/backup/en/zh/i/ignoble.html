Ignoble

EN→ZH

What Does Ignoble Mean?

  • 卑鄙的
  • 不光彩的
  • 可耻的

Ignoble Pronunciation

/ɪɡˈnoʊ.bəl/

Ignoble Definition

adjective

Definition

Not honorable in character or purpose; of low moral quality.

In ZH

在性格或目的上不光彩；道德品质低下。

How to Use Ignoble in a Sentence

  • His ignoble actions left a stain on his reputation.
  • The ignoble motives behind his charity were revealed.
  • She was disgusted by his ignoble behavior at the party.
  • The ignoble act of cheating shocked everyone.
  • They were criticized for their ignoble pursuit of wealth.

Synonyms for Ignoble

  • Ignis Fatuus
  • Ignominious
  • Ignominy
