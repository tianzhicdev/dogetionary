I

What Does I Mean?

  • 我
  • 自我
  • 我的

I Pronunciation

/aɪ/

I Definition

pronoun

Definition

The pronoun used by a speaker to refer to themselves.

In ZH

说话者自指的代词。

How to Use I in a Sentence

  • I am learning English.
  • I love reading books.
  • I went to the park yesterday.

Cultural Notes

The pronoun 'I' is always capitalized in English, which indicates the importance of the self in English-speaking cultures.

Definition

The speaker's sense of self or identity.

In ZH

  • Finding your i can help you understand who you are.

Cultural Notes

In discussions of identity and self-awareness, the concept of 'I' is central in many Western philosophical contexts.
