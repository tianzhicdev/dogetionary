Internship

Pronunciation:ˈɪntɜrnˌʃɪp
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 实习
  • 实习期
  • 实习生的职位

Definitions

noun

Definition

A period of work experience offered by an organization for a limited period of time, especially to students or recent graduates.

In ZH

一个组织在有限时间内提供的工作经验，以学生或应届毕业生为主。

Examples

  • I told my parents I got an internship at a tech company, and they replied, 'What's an internship? Is that like getting a job with training wheels?'
  • During my internship, I learned that coffee runs can actually be a full-time job if you’re not careful!
  • My internship ended up being a quest to find the best coffee in the office, which turns out was just in my boss's hidden stash!

Cultural Notes

Internships are common in many countries, especially in Western cultures, as a way to gain practical experience in a specific field. They can be paid or unpaid, and often serve as a critical stepping stone for employment.
