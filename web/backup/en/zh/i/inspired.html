Inspired

Pronunciation:ɪnˈspaɪərd
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 受到启发的
  • 激发的
  • 灵感的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

filled with a strong desire or motivation to create or do something meaningful.

In ZH

充满强烈的欲望或动机去创造或做一些有意义的事情。

Examples

  • She was inspired to write a novel after reading a powerful book.
  • The artist was inspired by nature to create stunning landscapes.
  • His speech inspired the audience to take action.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, being 'inspired' is often associated with creativity, passion, and motivation. It can refer to anything from art and literature to personal goals and achievements.

verb

Definition

to fill someone with the urge or ability to do or feel something, particularly something creative or beneficial.

In ZH

使某人充满做某事或感受到某种情绪的冲动或能力，尤其是创造性或有益的事情。

Examples

  • The teacher inspired her students to pursue their dreams.
  • He inspired his friends to start a community service project.
  • The film inspired many viewers to reflect on their own lives.

Cultural Notes

The act of inspiring others is highly valued in many cultures, as it promotes positive change and growth. Often, leaders or influential figures are celebrated for their ability to inspire others.
