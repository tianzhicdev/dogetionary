Inviolate

EN→ZH

What Does Inviolate Mean?

  • 不受侵犯的
  • 神圣的
  • 不可侵犯的

Inviolate Pronunciation

/ɪnˈvaɪələt/

Inviolate Definition

adjective

Definition

free from violation or infringement; intact; unbroken.

In ZH

不受侵犯的；完整的；未被打破的。

How to Use Inviolate in a Sentence

  • The rights of the individual must remain inviolate.
  • The contract was designed to keep the terms inviolate for both parties.
  • In a democracy, the freedom of expression is considered inviolate.
  • Her personal space is considered inviolate by everyone who knows her.
  • The sacredness of the site must be kept inviolate during the ceremony.

Synonyms for Inviolate

  • Invective
  • Inveigle
  • Inveterate
  • Invigorate
  • Invincible
