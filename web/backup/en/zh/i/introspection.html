Introspection

EN→ZH

What Does Introspection Mean?

  • 内省
  • 反思

Introspection Pronunciation

/ˌɪn.trəˈspɛk.ʃən/

Introspection Definition

noun

Definition

the examination of one's own conscious thoughts and feelings

In ZH

对自己意识中的思想和感情的审视

How to Use Introspection in a Sentence

  • After a long week, she took some time for introspection.
  • His introspection led him to a deeper understanding of his goals.
  • Through introspection, he realized the importance of his relationships.
  • Introspection can be a valuable tool for personal growth.
  • She enjoyed the quiet moments of introspection while hiking in nature.
