Impervious

EN→ZH

What Does Impervious Mean?

  • 不透水的
  • 无动于衷的

Impervious Pronunciation

/ɪmˈpɜːrviəs/

Impervious Definition

adjective

Definition

Not allowing fluid to pass through; unable to be affected by something.

In ZH

不允许液体通过；无法受某事物影响的。

How to Use Impervious in a Sentence

  • The raincoat is impervious to water.
  • She remained impervious to the criticism.
  • The concrete is impervious to moisture.
  • His impervious nature made him an excellent leader.
  • They designed the wall to be impervious to wind and rain.

Synonyms for Impervious

  • Impact
  • Impasse
  • Impeccable
  • Impecuniosity
  • Impecunious
