Insouciant

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈsuː.sɪ.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 漫不经心的
  • 无忧无虑的
  • 漠不关心的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Showing a casual lack of concern; indifferent.

In ZH

表现出漫不经心的态度；漠不关心。

Examples

  • He walked into the meeting, insouciant and carefree.
  • Her insouciant attitude towards the project surprised everyone.
  • The insouciant smile on his face made it hard to take him seriously.
  • Despite the chaos around her, she remained insouciant and calm.
  • His insouciant remarks about the situation angered his friends.
