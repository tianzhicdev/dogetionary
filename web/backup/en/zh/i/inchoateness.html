Inchoateness

EN→ZH

What Does Inchoateness Mean?

  • 未完成性
  • 初步状态
  • 不完全性

Inchoateness Pronunciation

/ɪnˈkoʊət.nəs/

Inchoateness Definition

noun

Definition

The state of being incomplete or not fully developed.

In ZH

未完成或未完全发展的状态。

How to Use Inchoateness in a Sentence

  • The inchoateness of the project led to many unforeseen challenges.
  • Her inchoateness as a writer was evident in her early drafts.
  • The inchoateness of his ideas required further refinement.
  • Inchoateness can often lead to creativity amidst chaos.
  • The theory remained in a state of inchoateness for several years before it was fully articulated.

Synonyms for Inchoateness

  • Incantation
  • Incendiary
  • Incentevize
  • Incertitude
