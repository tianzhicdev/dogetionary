Itinerant

Pronunciation:/aɪˈtɪn.ə.rənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 流动的
  • 巡游的
  • itinerant的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

traveling from place to place, especially on a regular basis

In ZH

在不同地方旅行，特别是定期地

Examples

  • The itinerant musician played at various venues around the city.
  • She leads an itinerant lifestyle, moving between countries every few months.
  • Itinerant workers often face challenges in finding stable employment.
  • The festival features itinerant artists showcasing their work.
  • Itinerant salespeople can often be found in rural areas.
noun

Definition

a person who travels from place to place; a wanderer

In ZH

从一个地方旅行到另一个地方的人；游荡者

Examples

  • The itinerant was welcomed at the village, where he shared stories of his travels.
  • Many itinerants struggle to find a home and stable community.
  • As an itinerant, he never stayed in one place for too long.
  • The book tells the stories of various itinerants throughout history.
  • An itinerant lifestyle can be both liberating and challenging.
