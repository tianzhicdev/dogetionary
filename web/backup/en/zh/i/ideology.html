Ideology

Pronunciation:/aɪˈdiːələdʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 意识形态
  • 思想体系
  • 理想

Definitions

noun

Definition

A system of ideas and ideals, especially one that forms the basis of economic or political theory and policy.

In ZH

一套思想和理念的体系，尤其是构成经济或政治理论和政策基础的体系。

Examples

  • The ideology of socialism emphasizes social ownership.
  • Many political parties operate under a specific ideology.
  • We studied various ideologies during our political science class.
  • Her ideology influences her decisions on social issues.
  • Understanding different ideologies is crucial for political discourse.
