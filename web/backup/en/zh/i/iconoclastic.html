Iconoclastic

Pronunciation:/ˌaɪ.kənˈɒk.læst.ɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 偶像破坏的
  • 反传统的
  • 革新主义的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by attacking or ignoring cherished beliefs and institutions

In ZH

以攻击或忽视珍视的信仰和制度为特征

Examples

  • The iconoclastic artist challenged traditional notions of beauty.
  • His iconoclastic views often alienated him from his peers.
  • She wrote an iconoclastic book that stirred controversy in literary circles.
  • The movement was founded by iconoclastic thinkers who questioned all authority.
  • Iconoclastic ideas can lead to significant societal changes.
