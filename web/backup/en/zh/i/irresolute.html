Irresolute

EN→ZH

What Does Irresolute Mean?

  • 优柔寡断的
  • 犹豫不决的

Irresolute Pronunciation

/ˌɪrɪˈzɒljuːt/

Irresolute Definition

adjective

Definition

uncertain or indecisive; not having or showing determination.

In ZH

不确定或犹豫不决；没有或不表现出决心。

How to Use Irresolute in a Sentence

  • She remained irresolute about which career path to choose.
  • His irresolute nature often led to missed opportunities.
  • Despite the evidence, he felt irresolute in making a decision.
  • The committee was irresolute, unable to finalize the report.
  • Her tone was irresolute as she spoke about her plans.

Synonyms for Irresolute

  • Irrefragable
  • Irrefutable
  • Irreverence
  • Irreverent
  • Irrevocable
