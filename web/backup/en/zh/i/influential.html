Influential

EN→ZH

What Does Influential Mean?

  • 有影响力的
  • 有影响的
  • 权威的

Influential Pronunciation

ɪnˈfluːənʃəl

Influential Definition

adjective

Definition

Having a great influence on someone or something.

In ZH

对某人或某事有很大影响的。

How to Use Influential in a Sentence

  • The influential leader changed the course of history.
  • Her influential speech inspired many young activists.
  • He is considered one of the most influential artists of the 20th century.

Cultural Notes

The term 'influential' is often used to describe figures in politics, art, and social movements who have the power to shape public opinion or drive change.

noun

Definition

A person or thing that has a significant influence on others.

In ZH

对其他人有重大影响的人或事物。

How to Use Influential in a Sentence

  • As an influential, she uses her platform to advocate for equality.
  • Many influencers today are found on social media, shaping trends and opinions.
  • The professor is an influential in the field of environmental science.

Cultural Notes

In contemporary usage, 'influential' is frequently associated with social media influencers, who can impact consumer behavior and trends.

Synonyms for Influential

  • Infinitesimal
  • Inflammation
  • Influence
