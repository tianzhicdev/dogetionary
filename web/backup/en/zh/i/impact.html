Impact

Pronunciation:/ˈɪmpækt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 影响
  • 冲击
  • 效果
  • 印象

Definitions

noun

Definition

the effect or influence of one person, thing, or action on another.

In ZH

一个人、物体或行为对另一个人、物体或行为的影响或作用。

Examples

  • The impact of the new law was immediately felt.
  • Her speech had a significant impact on the audience.
  • The company is studying the environmental impact of its operations.
  • There was a positive impact on the community after the project was completed.
  • The impact of technology on education cannot be overstated.
verb

Definition

to have a strong effect on someone or something.

In ZH

对某人或某事产生强烈影响。

Examples

  • The changes will impact our workflow dramatically.
  • Their decision will impact future generations.
  • The economy is expected to impact the job market.
  • Pollution can negatively impact health.
  • How will this policy impact the local population?
