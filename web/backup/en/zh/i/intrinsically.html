Intrinsically

Pronunciation:ɪnˈtrɪnzɪkli
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 本质上
  • 内在地
  • 固有地

Definitions

adverb

Definition

In an essential or fundamental way; inherently.

In ZH

以本质或基本的方式；固有地。

Examples

  • Love is intrinsically valuable.
  • She is intrinsically motivated to learn.
  • The beauty of nature is intrinsically connected to our wellbeing.
  • Time is intrinsically linked to change.
  • The concept of fairness is intrinsically important in justice.
