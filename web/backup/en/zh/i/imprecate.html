Imprecate

Pronunciation:/ˈɪmprɪkeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 咒骂
  • 诅咒

Definitions

verb

Definition

To invoke evil upon; to curse.

In ZH

施加魔咒；咒骂。

Examples

  • He imprecates his enemies when they wrong him.
  • The villagers often imprecate against the drought that plagues their crops.
  • In ancient times, people would imprecate the spirits they believed were causing misfortune.
  • She whispered a prayer to imprecate good fortune in her life.
  • The poet chose to imprecate the injustices he saw in society.
