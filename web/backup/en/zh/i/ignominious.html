Ignominious

EN→ZH

What Does Ignominious Mean?

  • 不光彩的
  • 耻辱的
  • 丢脸的

Ignominious Pronunciation

/ˌɪɡ.nəˈmɪn.i.əs/

Ignominious Definition

adjective

Definition

deserving or causing public disgrace or shame

In ZH

值得或造成公众耻辱或羞耻的

How to Use Ignominious in a Sentence

  • His ignominious defeat in the election led to his resignation.
  • The ignominious end of the once-great empire shocked historians.
  • She found herself in an ignominious position after the scandal broke.
  • His actions resulted in an ignominious reputation that never faded.
  • The team's ignominious loss in the championship was hard for fans to accept.

Synonyms for Ignominious

  • Ignis Fatuus
  • Ignoble
  • Ignominy
