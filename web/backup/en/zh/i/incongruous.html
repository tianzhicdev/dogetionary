Incongruous

EN→ZH

What Does Incongruous Mean?

  • 不协调的
  • 不一致的
  • 不合时宜的

Incongruous Pronunciation

/ɪnˈkɒŋɡruəs/

Incongruous Definition

adjective

Definition

not in harmony or keeping with the surroundings or other aspects of something

In ZH

与周围环境或其他方面不和谐或不一致

How to Use Incongruous in a Sentence

  • His cheerful demeanor was incongruous in the somber setting of the funeral.
  • The bright colors of the building seemed incongruous against the gray skyline.
  • It was incongruous to see a sports car parked beside a rusty old truck.
  • Her dress was incongruous with the formal atmosphere of the event.
  • The comic relief felt incongruous in such a serious film.

Synonyms for Incongruous

  • Incandescent
  • Incantation
  • Incendiary
  • Incentevize
  • Incertitude
