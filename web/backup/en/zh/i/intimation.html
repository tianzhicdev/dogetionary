Intimation

EN→ZH

What Does Intimation Mean?

  • 暗示
  • 提示
  • 通告

Intimation Pronunciation

/ˌɪn.tɪˈmeɪ.ʃən/

Intimation Definition

noun

Definition

An indication or hint about something that is to come or that may be true.

In ZH

对即将到来或可能真实的事情的指示或暗示。

How to Use Intimation in a Sentence

  • She gave an intimation of her displeasure.
  • The report contained an intimation of future changes.
  • His silence was an intimation that he disagreed with the plan.
  • An intimation came from the committee about new regulations.
  • There was an intimation that the event might be canceled.

Synonyms for Intimation

  • Integrity
  • Interact
  • Interlocution
  • Interlocutor
  • Interloper
