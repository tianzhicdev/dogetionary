Invocation

Pronunciation:/ˌɪnvəˈkeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 祈求
  • 呼唤
  • 召唤

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of invoking something or someone, often in a formal manner.

In ZH

以正式方式召唤某人或某事的行为。

Examples

  • The invocation of the spirits was a central part of their ritual.
  • During the ceremony, an invocation was offered to bless the gathering.
  • The lawyer spoke an invocation before presenting the evidence.
  • An invocation of the law was necessary to proceed with the case.
  • The religious leader began the service with an invocation for peace.
