Insurrection

Pronunciation:/ˌɪnsəˈrɛkʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 叛乱
  • 起义
  • 暴动

Definitions

noun

Definition

a violent uprising against an authority or government.

In ZH

对权威或政府的暴力起义。

Examples

  • The insurrection was quickly suppressed by the military.
  • Many citizens participated in the insurrection, seeking political reform.
  • The insurrection led to a change in leadership.
  • Historians debate the causes of the insurrection.
  • The insurrection was marked by significant violence and unrest.
