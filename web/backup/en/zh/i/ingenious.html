Ingenious

EN→ZH

What Does Ingenious Mean?

  • 灵巧的
  • 聪明的
  • 机灵的

Ingenious Pronunciation

/ɪnˈdʒiːniəs/

Ingenious Definition

adjective

Definition

clever, original, and inventive

In ZH

聪明的，独特的，富有创造力的

How to Use Ingenious in a Sentence

  • Her ingenious solution to the problem impressed everyone.
  • He is known for his ingenious designs in architecture.
  • The gadget was simple yet ingenious in its functionality.
  • They came up with an ingenious plan to save money.
  • His ingenious mind always finds creative solutions.

Synonyms for Ingenious
