Imperative

EN→ZH

What Does Imperative Mean?

  • 命令
  • 必要的
  • 强制的

Imperative Pronunciation

/ɪmˈpɛrətɪv/

Imperative Definition

noun

Definition

a command or instruction that must be followed

In ZH

必须遵循的命令或指示

How to Use Imperative in a Sentence

  • The teacher gave an imperative to finish the assignment.
  • In programming, the imperative mode instructs the computer to perform specific tasks.
  • When traveling, it is imperative to have your passport ready.
  • He spoke in an imperative tone during the meeting.
  • The imperative of this situation is to act quickly.
adjective

Definition

of vital importance; crucial

In ZH

至关重要的；关键的

How to Use Imperative in a Sentence

  • It is imperative that we address climate change immediately.
  • For your health, a balanced diet is imperative.
  • The success of the project is imperative for the company's future.
  • In a crisis, clear communication is imperative.
  • Knowing the rules is imperative for participation in the game.

Synonyms for Imperative

  • Impact
  • Impasse
  • Impeccable
  • Impecuniosity
  • Impecunious
