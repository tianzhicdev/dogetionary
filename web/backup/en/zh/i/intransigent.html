Intransigent

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈtræn.zɪ.dʒənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 固执的
  • 不妥协的
  • 强硬的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Refusing to change one's views or to agree about something.

In ZH

拒绝改变自己的观点或同意某事。

Examples

  • The intransigent attitude of the negotiators led to a stalemate.
  • Despite the evidence presented, he remained intransigent in his beliefs.
  • Her intransigent stance on the issue frustrated the committee members.
  • The company took an intransigent position regarding the new policies.
  • His intransigent nature often caused conflict in discussions.
