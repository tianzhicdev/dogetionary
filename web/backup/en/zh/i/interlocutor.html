Interlocutor

EN→ZH

What Does Interlocutor Mean?

  • 对话者
  • 交谈者
  • 谈话人

Interlocutor Pronunciation

/ˌɪntərˈlɒkjʊtə/

Interlocutor Definition

noun

Definition

A person who takes part in a conversation or dialogue.

In ZH

参与对话或交谈的人。

How to Use Interlocutor in a Sentence

  • The interviewer was a skilled interlocutor, making the candidates feel at ease.
  • In a lively discussion, each interlocutor brought a unique perspective to the topic.
  • During the debate, the interlocutors challenged each other's viewpoints.
  • The book presents various interlocutors in a philosophical dialogue.
  • As an interlocutor, she ensured that everyone had a chance to voice their opinions.

Synonyms for Interlocutor

  • Integrity
  • Interact
  • Interlocution
  • Interloper
  • Interminable
