Inexorable

Pronunciation:/ɪˈnɛksərəbl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不可阻挡的
  • 无情的
  • 坚定不移的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

impossible to stop or prevent

In ZH

无法停止或阻止的

Examples

  • The inexorable march of time cannot be halted.
  • She faced the inexorable facts of her situation with courage.
  • His inexorable determination led him to success despite the challenges.
  • The weather is inexorable; it won't change no matter how much we wish for it.
  • Inexorable fate often comes when least expected.
