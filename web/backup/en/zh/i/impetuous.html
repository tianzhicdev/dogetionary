Impetuous

Pronunciation:/ɪmˈpɛtʃʊəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 冲动的
  • 猛烈的
  • impulsive

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Acting or done quickly and without thought or care; impulsive.

In ZH

迅速且不加思考或考虑地行动或完成；冲动的。

Examples

  • Her impetuous decision to quit her job surprised everyone.
  • He is known for his impetuous actions, often leading to regrets later.
  • The impetuous young man jumped into the river without a second thought.
  • Many people have impetuous reactions when faced with unexpected challenges.
  • She made an impetuous promise to travel the world without a plan.
