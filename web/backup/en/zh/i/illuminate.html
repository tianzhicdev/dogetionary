Illuminate

Pronunciation:/ɪˈluː.mɪ.neɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 照亮
  • 阐明
  • 启发

Definitions

verb

Definition

To light up; to make something visible or bright by shining light on it.

In ZH

照亮；通过照射光线使某物可见或明亮。

Examples

  • The streetlights illuminate the road at night.
  • The scientist's discoveries illuminate new possibilities for treatment.
  • Her speech helped to illuminate complex issues surrounding the topic.
  • The painting is designed to illuminate the room with its bright colors.
  • In the dark, the stars illuminate the night sky.
