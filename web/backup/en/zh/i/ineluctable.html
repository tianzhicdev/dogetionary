Ineluctable

EN→ZH

What Does Ineluctable Mean?

  • 不可避免的
  • 必然的
  • 逃避不了的

Ineluctable Pronunciation

/ɪˈnɛlʌktəbl/

Ineluctable Definition

adjective

Definition

inevitable; unavoidable

In ZH

不可避免的；必然的

How to Use Ineluctable in a Sentence

  • The consequences of climate change are ineluctable.
  • Death is an ineluctable part of life.
  • She felt an ineluctable pull towards the ocean.
  • He realized that change was ineluctable and decided to embrace it.
  • The ethical dilemmas presented in the novel seemed ineluctable.
