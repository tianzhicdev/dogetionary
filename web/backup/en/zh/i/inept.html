Inept

EN→ZH

What Does Inept Mean?

  • 无能的
  • 不称职的
  • 拙劣的

Inept Pronunciation

/ɪˈnɛpt/

Inept Definition

adjective

Definition

having or showing no skill; clumsy

In ZH

没有或显示出没有技能；笨拙的

How to Use Inept in a Sentence

  • He was inept at negotiating a deal.
  • The inept handling of the situation made it worse.
  • Her inept remarks only added to the confusion.
  • He is known for his inept management style.
  • Inept attempts to fix the car led to more problems.

Synonyms for Inept
