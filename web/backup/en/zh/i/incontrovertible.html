Incontrovertible

Pronunciation:/ɪnˌkɒntrəˈvɜːtɪbəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不可争辩的
  • 毫无疑问的
  • 无可辩驳的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Not able to be denied or disputed; indisputable.

In ZH

无法否认或争辩的；不容置疑的。

Examples

  • The evidence presented at the trial was incontrovertible.
  • Her logic was so sound that it made his arguments seem inconsequential and incontrovertible.
  • He had incontrovertible proof of his claims.
  • The scientist provided incontrovertible data supporting the theory.
  • Incontrovertible facts are essential in a court of law.
