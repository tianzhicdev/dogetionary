Iconolatry

EN→ZH

What Does Iconolatry Mean?

  • 偶像崇拜
  • 图像崇拜

Iconolatry Pronunciation

/ˌaɪ.kəˈnɒl.ə.tri/

Iconolatry Definition

noun

Definition

The worship of icons or images, particularly in a religious context.

In ZH

对偶像或图像的崇拜，尤其是在宗教背景下。

How to Use Iconolatry in a Sentence

  • Many religious movements have been criticized for their iconolatry.
  • The church was accused of iconolatry due to its use of statues in worship.
  • Iconolatry can provoke debates about the nature of faith and idolatry.
  • In some cultures, iconolatry is seen as a form of artistic expression.
  • The debate over iconolatry continues to influence modern religious practices.

Cultural Notes

Iconolatry has been particularly significant in the history of Eastern Orthodox Christianity, where the veneration of icons is a central practice.
