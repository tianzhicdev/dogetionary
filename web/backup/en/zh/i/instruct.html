Instruct

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈstrʌkt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 指示
  • 教授
  • 命令

Definitions

verb

Definition

To direct or command someone to do something; to provide knowledge or skills to someone.

In ZH

指示或命令某人做某事；向某人提供知识或技能。

Examples

  • The teacher will instruct the students on how to solve the problem.
  • He was instructed to report to the manager immediately.
  • They instructed the participants to wear safety gear during the experiment.
  • Please instruct me on the steps I need to follow.
  • The course will instruct you in basic computer programming.
