Interstitial

EN→ZH

What Does Interstitial Mean?

  • 间质的
  • 中间的
  • 插缝的

Interstitial Pronunciation

/ˌɪntərˈstɪʃəl/

Interstitial Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or situated in the interstices; occupying a space between things.

In ZH

与空隙有关的；位于空隙中；占据事物之间的空间。

How to Use Interstitial in a Sentence

  • The interstitial spaces in the tissue are critical for cellular communication.
  • Interstitial fluid helps transport nutrients and waste products in the body.
  • In architecture, interstitial areas can be used for utilities and maintenance.
  • The interstitial regions of the forest are home to many unique species.
  • Interstitial growth contributes to the overall size of the tissue.
noun

Definition

A space or gap between things, especially in a physical structure or system.

In ZH

事物之间的空间或空隙，特别是在物理结构或系统中。

How to Use Interstitial in a Sentence

  • The interstitial between the two buildings allows for better airflow.
  • Researchers studied the interstitials within the grid system for efficiency.
  • In biology, the interstitial areas are crucial for the development of organisms.
  • The engineer designed interstitial channels to enhance drainage.
  • The interstitial in the fabric helped it remain breathable.

Synonyms for Interstitial

  • Integrity
  • Interact
  • Interlocution
  • Interlocutor
  • Interloper
