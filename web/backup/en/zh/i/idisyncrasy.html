Idisyncrasy

Pronunciation:/ɪdiəˈsɪŋkrəsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 特质
  • 习性
  • 气质

Definitions

noun

Definition

A characteristic, habit, or mannerism that is peculiar to an individual.

In ZH

一个人特有的特征、习惯或举止。

Examples

  • His idiosyncrasy of always wearing mismatched socks made him stand out.
  • Many artists have their own idiosyncrasies that influence their work.
  • She had a strange idiosyncrasy of talking to her plants.
  • His idiosyncrasy included a love for collecting unusual artifacts.
  • The professor's idiosyncrasies often amused his students.
