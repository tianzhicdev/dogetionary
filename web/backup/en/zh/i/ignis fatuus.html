Ignis Fatuus

EN→ZH

What Does Ignis Fatuus Mean?

  • 鬼火
  • 萤火虫
  • 幻影火

Ignis Fatuus Pronunciation

/ˈɪɡ.nɪs ˈfæ.tjuː.əs/

Ignis Fatuus Definition

noun

Definition

A phosphorescent light seen at night, especially over marshy ground, caused by the combustion of gases emanating from decaying organic matter.

In ZH

一种在夜间看到的磷光，在沼泽地区尤其常见，由于腐烂有机物释放的气体燃烧引起。
