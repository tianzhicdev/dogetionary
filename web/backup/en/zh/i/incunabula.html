Incunabula

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈkjuːnəˌbjuːlə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 初印本
  • 早期印刷书籍

Definitions

noun

Definition

Books printed before 1501, during the infancy of printing.

In ZH

在印刷初期（1501年之前）印刷的书籍。

Examples

  • The library has a remarkable collection of incunabula.
  • Incunabula offer a glimpse into the early days of printing.
  • Many incunabula are now considered rare artifacts.
  • The university's incunabula are kept in a special climate-controlled room.
  • Scholars study incunabula to understand the evolution of printed texts.

Cultural Notes

Incunabula are often highly valued by collectors and historians for their historical significance.
