Ineffability

EN→ZH

What Does Ineffability Mean?

  • 不可言喻
  • 难以言喻
  • 无以言表

Ineffability Pronunciation

/ɪˈnɛf.ə.bəl.ɪ.ti/

Ineffability Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being too great or extreme to be expressed or described in words.

In ZH

难以用言语表达或描述的特性。

How to Use Ineffability in a Sentence

  • The ineffability of her emotions left her speechless.
  • He pondered the ineffability of divine mysteries.
  • The artwork conveyed an ineffability that words could not capture.
  • In moments of awe, we often encounter the ineffability of nature.
  • The ineffability of the experience made it unforgettable.

Synonyms for Ineffability

  • Ineffable
  • Inefficacy
  • Inefficiency
  • Ineluctable
  • Inept
