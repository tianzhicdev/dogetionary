Itinerary

EN→ZH

What Does Itinerary Mean?

  • 行程表
  • 旅行计划
  • 日程安排

Itinerary Pronunciation

/aɪˈtɪnəˌrɛri/

Itinerary Definition

noun

Definition

A detailed plan or route of a journey.

In ZH

详细的旅行计划或路线。

How to Use Itinerary in a Sentence

  • I reviewed the itinerary before our trip to ensure we wouldn't miss anything.
  • The travel agent provided a printed itinerary that included all our flight details.
  • Make sure to share your itinerary with your friends so they know where you'll be.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, especially in travel and tourism, having a well-prepared itinerary is seen as a key to a successful trip. It allows travelers to make the most of their time and avoid missing important sites or activities.

noun

Definition

A list of places to visit on a journey, often including the dates and times of travel.

In ZH

旅行时要访问的地方列表，通常包括旅行的日期和时间。

How to Use Itinerary in a Sentence

  • The itinerary included stops at famous landmarks and scenic spots.
  • We changed our itinerary at the last minute to visit a new exhibition.
  • He kept his itinerary flexible so that he could explore unexpected locations.

Cultural Notes

An itinerary can be very detailed or quite flexible, depending on the travel style and preferences of the traveler. In some family-oriented cultures, itineraries are often created collectively to involve everyone's preferences.

Synonyms for Itinerary
