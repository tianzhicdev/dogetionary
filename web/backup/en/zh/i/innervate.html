Innervate

EN→ZH

What Does Innervate Mean?

  • 神经支配
  • 神经促动

Innervate Pronunciation

/ˈɪn.ər.veɪt/

Innervate Definition

verb

Definition

To supply nerve fibers to an organ or a body part.

In ZH

向器官或身体部位供应神经纤维。

How to Use Innervate in a Sentence

  • The spinal cord innervates various muscles in the body.
  • Doctors study how different nerves innervate the limbs.
  • Injuries can affect how nerves innervate certain areas.
  • The autonomic nervous system innervates the heart and digestive organs.
  • Understanding how to innervate the muscles can help improve motor function.

Synonyms for Innervate

  • Innocuous
  • Innovate
