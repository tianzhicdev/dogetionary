Iterative

Pronunciation:/ˈɪtərətɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 迭代的
  • 反复的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to or involving repetition, especially of a process or procedure.

In ZH

与重复相关或涉及重复，尤其是一个过程或程序。

Examples

  • The iterative process allowed us to refine our design over multiple cycles.
  • In software development, an iterative approach can lead to more robust results.
  • He used an iterative method to solve the equation step by step.
  • Each iteration of the algorithm brought us closer to the solution.
  • An iterative approach is essential in agile project management.
