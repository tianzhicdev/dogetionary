Immigrate

EN→ZH

What Does Immigrate Mean?

  • 移民
  • 移入
  • 迁移

Immigrate Pronunciation

/ˈɪm.ɪ.ɡreɪt/

Immigrate Definition

verb

Definition

To come to live permanently in a foreign country.

In ZH

永久居住在外国。

How to Use Immigrate in a Sentence

  • Many people choose to immigrate to the United States for better opportunities.
  • She decided to immigrate to Canada in search of a better life.
  • After years of planning, they were finally able to immigrate to Australia.
  • Immigrating can be a challenging process but it often leads to new beginnings.
  • He immigrated from Mexico to find work and support his family.

Synonyms for Immigrate

  • Immolate
  • Immure
  • Immutability
  • Immutable
