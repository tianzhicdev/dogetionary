Impute

EN→ZH

What Does Impute Mean?

  • 归因
  • 归咎
  • imput
  • 推断

Impute Pronunciation

/ɪmˈpjuːt/

Impute Definition

verb

Definition

To attribute or assign a value or quality to something, often used in the context of assigning blame or responsibility.

In ZH

将值或特征归因于某事，通常用在指责或责任的上下文中。

How to Use Impute in a Sentence

  • He tried to impute the failure to external factors.
  • The report imputed the rise in crime to socioeconomic issues.
  • They imputed her lack of experience as the reason for her mistakes.
  • The lawyer imputed negligence on the part of the company.
  • It is wrong to impute malice to someone without evidence.
