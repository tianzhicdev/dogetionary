Inure

Pronunciation:/ɪˈnjʊər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 使适应
  • 使习惯
  • 练习

Definitions

verb

Definition

To accustom someone to something, especially something unpleasant.

In ZH

使某人习惯于某事，尤其是不愉快的事情。

Examples

  • He inured himself to the harsh conditions of the desert.
  • The soldiers were inured to the hardships of war.
  • Days of practice inured her to the pressure of competition.
  • Over time, they inured themselves to the noise of the city.
  • The constant exposure inured him to criticism.
