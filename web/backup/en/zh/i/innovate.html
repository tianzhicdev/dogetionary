Innovate

Pronunciation:/ˈɪn.ə.veɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 创新
  • 革新
  • 引入新方法

Definitions

verb

Definition

to introduce new ideas, methods, or products

In ZH

引入新思想、新方法或新产品

Examples

  • Companies need to innovate to stay competitive.
  • Innovating is essential for growth in technology sectors.
  • She aims to innovate the educational system with her new approach.
  • The team worked hard to innovate their product line this year.
  • Innovate or fall behind is the motto of many startups.
