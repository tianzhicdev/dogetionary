Inspire

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈspaɪər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 启发
  • 激励
  • 鼓舞

Definitions

verb

Definition

to fill someone with the urge or ability to do or feel something, especially to do something creative

In ZH

使某人充满做某事的冲动或能力，特别是做某种创造性的事情

Examples

  • Her speech inspired many people to take action.
  • The music inspired him to write his own songs.
  • Nature has the power to inspire great art.
  • Teachers can inspire students to achieve their dreams.
  • A good book can inspire readers to think differently.
