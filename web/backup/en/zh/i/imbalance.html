Imbalance

EN→ZH

What Does Imbalance Mean?

  • 不平衡
  • 失衡
  • 不对称

Imbalance Pronunciation

/ɪmˈbæl.əns/

Imbalance Definition

noun

Definition

a lack of proportion or an unequal distribution between two or more elements.

In ZH

缺乏比例或两个或多个元素之间的分配不均衡。

How to Use Imbalance in a Sentence

  • There is an imbalance in the distribution of resources.
  • An imbalance between supply and demand can lead to price fluctuations.
  • She noticed an imbalance in her work-life schedule.
  • The imbalance in power among the team members caused tension.
  • Researchers are studying the hormonal imbalance that affects mood.

Synonyms for Imbalance

  • Imbecile
  • Imbricate
  • Imbrication
  • Imbrogate
  • Imbroglio
