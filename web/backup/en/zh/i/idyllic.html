Idyllic

EN→ZH

What Does Idyllic Mean?

  • 田园的
  • 田园诗般的
  • 理想的

Idyllic Pronunciation

/ɪˈdɪlɪk/

Idyllic Definition

adjective

Definition

charmingly simple and serene; picturesque and peaceful, often relating to a rural setting.

In ZH

迷人地简单和宁静；如画且平静，通常与乡村环境相关。

How to Use Idyllic in a Sentence

  • The countryside offered an idyllic setting for their summer retreat.
  • They spent an idyllic afternoon by the lake.
  • Her childhood was spent in an idyllic village surrounded by mountains.
  • The novel describes an idyllic life away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
  • He dreams of an idyllic world where everyone lives in harmony.
