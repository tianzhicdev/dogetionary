Institute

Pronunciation:/ˈɪnstɪtjuːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 学院
  • 机构
  • 研究所

Definitions

noun

Definition

An organization or establishment set up for a specific purpose, especially for education or research.

In ZH

为特定目的而设立的组织或机构，特别是用于教育或研究的机构。

Examples

  • The institute offers a variety of courses.
  • She was accepted into a prestigious art institute.
  • The research institute is located in the city.
  • They founded an institute for advanced studies.
  • He works at a historical preservation institute.
verb

Definition

To establish or set up (something, especially an organization or set of rules).

In ZH

建立或设立（某事，尤其是组织或规则）。

Examples

  • They plan to institute a new policy next year.
  • The government will institute reforms to improve education.
  • She was chosen to institute changes in the curriculum.
  • We need to institute better safety measures.
  • He was tasked to institute a new training program.
