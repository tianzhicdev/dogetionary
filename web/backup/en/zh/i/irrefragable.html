Irrefragable

Pronunciation:/ɪˈrɛfrəɡəbl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不可辩驳的
  • 无可争辩的
  • 不可否认的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Not able to be refuted or disproved; indisputable.

In ZH

不可被驳斥或推翻的；无可争辩的。

Examples

  • The evidence presented was irrefragable, leaving no room for doubt.
  • Her logic was so strong that it seemed irrefragable.
  • They reached an irrefragable conclusion after years of research.
  • The defendant provided irrefragable proof of his innocence.
  • In science, an irrefragable theory is one that stands up to all scrutiny.
