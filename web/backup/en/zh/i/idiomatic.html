Idiomatic

EN→ZH

What Does Idiomatic Mean?

  • 习惯用语的
  • 成语的
  • 俗语的

Idiomatic Pronunciation

/ˌɪdiəˈmætɪk/

Idiomatic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or denoting an expression that is natural to a native speaker.

In ZH

与以母语者自然使用的表达方式相关或指的。

How to Use Idiomatic in a Sentence

  • The phrase 'kick the bucket' is an idiomatic expression meaning to die.
  • She has an idiomatic way of speaking that shows her fluency in English.
  • Idiomatic phrases can be difficult for non-native speakers to understand.
  • In literature, idiomatic language can add depth to character dialogue.
  • Understanding idiomatic expressions is essential for mastering a language.
