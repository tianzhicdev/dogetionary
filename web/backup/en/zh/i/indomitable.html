Indomitable

EN→ZH

What Does Indomitable Mean?

  • 不屈不挠的
  • 坚韧不拔的

Indomitable Pronunciation

/ɪnˈdɒmɪtəbl/

Indomitable Definition

adjective

Definition

Impossible to subdue or defeat.

In ZH

无法驯服或打败的。

How to Use Indomitable in a Sentence

  • Her indomitable spirit inspired everyone around her.
  • Despite the challenges, his indomitable will kept him going.
  • They admired her indomitable courage in the face of danger.
  • The team's indomitable resolve led them to victory against all odds.
  • He faced the storm with an indomitable attitude.

Synonyms for Indomitable

  • Indebted
  • Indefatigable
  • Indelible
  • Indolence
