Incognito

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈkɒɡ.nɪ.təʊ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 隐形
  • 不公开的
  • 化名的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

in disguise or under a false identity

In ZH

伪装或使用假身份的

Examples

  • He traveled incognito to avoid being recognized.
  • She preferred to be incognito during her vacation.
  • The celebrity went incognito to the restaurant.
  • He was incognito at the event, blending in with the crowd.
  • Traveling incognito can sometimes lead to unexpected adventures.
noun

Definition

a person who is in disguise or operating under a false identity

In ZH

伪装者或使用假身份的人

Examples

  • The detective worked incognito to gather information.
  • He assumed an incognito while investigating the case.
  • Celebrities often go incognito to maintain their privacy.
  • The incognito at the party was later revealed to be a famous author.
  • She enjoyed the thrill of being an incognito during her trip.
