Impugn

Pronunciation:/ɪmˈpjuːn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 质疑
  • 抨击
  • 攻击

Definitions

verb

Definition

To call into question or challenge the validity or truth of something.

In ZH

质疑或挑战某事的有效性或真实性。

Examples

  • The lawyer decided to impugn the witness's credibility.
  • She chose to impugn his motives during the debate.
  • It's unfair to impugn someone's character without evidence.
  • He tried to impugn her achievements out of jealousy.
  • They impugned the reliability of the report after discovering the errors.
