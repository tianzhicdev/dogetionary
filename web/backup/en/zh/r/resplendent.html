Resplendent

Pronunciation:/rɪˈsplɛndənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 辉煌的
  • 灿烂的
  • 光辉的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

shining brilliantly; radiant; glowing or gleaming with beauty or splendor.

In ZH

辉煌的；灿烂的；光辉的。

Examples

  • The sunset was resplendent, painting the sky in shades of orange and pink.
  • Her resplendent gown caught everyone's attention at the gala.
  • The garden was resplendent with flowers in full bloom.
  • He spoke with a resplendent enthusiasm that inspired everyone.
  • The king entered the hall in a resplendent robe adorned with jewels.
