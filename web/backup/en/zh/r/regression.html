Regression

EN→ZH

What Does Regression Mean?

  • 回归
  • 退化
  • 逆行

Regression Pronunciation

/rɪˈɡrɛʃən/

Regression Definition

noun

Definition

A return to a previous state, typically a worse one.

In ZH

回归到以前的状态，通常是更糟的状态。

How to Use Regression in a Sentence

  • The regression in his health was alarming.
  • The software experienced a regression after the update.
  • Many learners face regression in their skills after a long break.
  • The regression of species in the environment is concerning.
  • She showed signs of regression in her behavior.
noun

Definition
