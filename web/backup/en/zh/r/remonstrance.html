Remonstrance

EN→ZH

What Does Remonstrance Mean?

  • 抗议
  • 反对
  • 陈述意见

Remonstrance Pronunciation

/rɪˈmɒnstrəns/

Remonstrance Definition

noun

Definition

an expression of protest, complaint, or objection.

In ZH

抗议、投诉或反对的表达方式。

How to Use Remonstrance in a Sentence

  • The citizens submitted a remonstrance to the government regarding the new law.
  • Their remonstrance went unheard in the crowded council meeting.
  • He raised a remonstrance about the unfair treatment of workers.
  • The organization filed a formal remonstrance against the project.
  • She felt that the remonstrance was justified due to the circumstances.
