Reparative

EN→ZH

What Does Reparative Mean?

  • 修复的
  • 补偿的
  • 修复性

Reparative Pronunciation

/rɪˈpærəˌtɪv/

Reparative Definition

adjective

Definition

intended to repair or restore something to a previous state

In ZH

旨在修复或恢复某物到先前状态的

How to Use Reparative in a Sentence

  • The reparative effects of therapy can be profound.
  • These techniques offer reparative solutions for damaged relationships.
  • Her approach was focused on reparative justice rather than punitive measures.
  • Reparative actions are often necessary after environmental disasters.
  • The program aims to provide reparative support for those affected by trauma.

Synonyms for Reparative

  • Repartee
  • Repentance
  • Repercussion
  • Repertory
  • Replete
