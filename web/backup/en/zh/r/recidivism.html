Recidivism

Pronunciation:/rɪˈsɪdɪvɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 累犯
  • 再犯
  • 前科

Definitions

noun

Definition

the tendency of a convicted criminal to reoffend

In ZH

有罪的罪犯再次犯罪的倾向

Examples

  • Recidivism is a significant problem in the criminal justice system.
  • Many programs aim to reduce recidivism among former inmates.
  • Studies show that recidivism rates can be influenced by rehabilitation efforts.
  • Understanding the factors contributing to recidivism is crucial for effective policy making.
  • The community must work together to address issues related to recidivism.
