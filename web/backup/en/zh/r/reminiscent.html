Reminiscent

Pronunciation:ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsənt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 怀旧的
  • 引起回忆的
  • 想起的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Tending to remind one of something.

In ZH

倾向于让人想起某事的。

Examples

  • The song was reminiscent of my childhood summers.
  • Her painting is reminiscent of the works of Van Gogh.
  • The smell of the bakery was reminiscent of my grandmother's kitchen.

Cultural Notes

The word 'reminiscent' is often used in contexts where something evokes memories or makes one think of past experiences. In English, it conveys a sense of nostalgia, which is a common theme in literature and music.

noun

Definition

A word or phrase that describes something that brings to mind a memory or idea.

In ZH

一种描述某物能够唤起记忆或想法的词或短语。

Examples

  • The film's use of sepia tones is a reminiscent of old photographs.
  • His speech was full of reminiscent anecdotes.
  • The garden had a reminiscent quality, reminding me of my childhood home.

Cultural Notes

While the adjective form is more commonly used, the noun form, though less common, can also be applied in creative writing or poetic contexts.
