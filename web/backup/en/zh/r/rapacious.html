Rapacious

EN→ZH

What Does Rapacious Mean?

  • 贪婪的
  • 掠夺的
  • 抢夺的

Rapacious Pronunciation

/rəˈpeɪʃəs/

Rapacious Definition

adjective

Definition

having or showing a strong or excessive desire to acquire money or other possessions; greedy.

In ZH

对获取金钱或其他财物有强烈或过度欲望的；贪婪的。

How to Use Rapacious in a Sentence

  • The rapacious businessman cared only for profits.
  • She was known for her rapacious appetite for luxury.
  • The rapacious predator stalked its prey relentlessly.
  • Their rapacious demands broke the spirit of the negotiations.
  • The rapacious growth of the company led to several ethical concerns.

Synonyms for Rapacious

  • Rapacity
  • Rapper
  • Rapport
  • Rapprochement
  • Rapscallion
