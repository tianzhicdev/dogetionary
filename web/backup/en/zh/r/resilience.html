Resilience

EN→ZH

What Does Resilience Mean?

  • 韧性
  • 适应力
  • 恢复力

Resilience Pronunciation

/rɪˈzɪl.jəns/

Resilience Definition

noun

Definition

The capacity to recover quickly from difficulties; toughness.

In ZH

迅速从困难中恢复的能力；坚韧性。

How to Use Resilience in a Sentence

  • Her resilience helped her overcome the challenges of her new job.
  • The community showed great resilience in the face of the natural disaster.
  • Children often demonstrate remarkable resilience after traumatic experiences.
  • Building resilience can lead to better mental health outcomes.
  • The athlete's resilience was evident in her ability to bounce back from injuries.
