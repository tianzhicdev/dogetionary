Retronym

EN→ZH

What Does Retronym Mean?

  • 回溯词
  • 重命名词
  • 后缀词

Retronym Pronunciation

/ˈrɛtrəˌnɪm/

Retronym Definition

noun

Definition

A term that is created from an existing word to differentiate the original from a new version of it, often in response to technological changes.

In ZH

一种从现有词语中创造的术语，用于区分原始词汇与其新版本，通常是在技术变革后出现的。

How to Use Retronym in a Sentence

  • The term 'acoustic guitar' is a retronym formed after the invention of the electric guitar.
  • In the age of smartphones, 'dumb phone' became a retronym for traditional mobile phones.
  • 'Classic rock' is a retronym that emerged to distinguish older rock music from newer genres.
  • The phrase 'landline phone' is a retronym developed due to the rise of mobile phones.
  • 'Regular coffee' is a retronym that arose with the popularity of specialty coffee drinks.

Synonyms for Retronym

  • Reticent
  • Reticulate
  • Retort
  • Retrench
  • Retribution
