Resurgence

EN→ZH

What Does Resurgence Mean?

  • 复兴
  • 再起
  • 重新崛起

Resurgence Pronunciation

/rɪˈsɜːrdʒəns/

Resurgence Definition

noun

Definition

An increase or revival after a period of little activity, popularity, or occurrence.

In ZH

在一段活动、流行或出现寥寥无几的时期后，增加或复兴。

How to Use Resurgence in a Sentence

  • The resurgence of interest in classical music is evident in recent concert attendance.
  • There has been a resurgence of local businesses in the area due to the new mall.
  • After several years of decline, the city is experiencing a resurgence in tourism.
  • The environmental movement has seen a resurgence in recent years as more people become aware of climate change.
  • The resurgence of the team this season has surprised many fans.

Synonyms for Resurgence

  • Rescind
  • Reside
  • Resilience
  • Resilient
  • Resipiscence
