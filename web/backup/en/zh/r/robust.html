Robust

Pronunciation:/roʊˈbʌst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 强健的
  • 稳健的
  • 强大的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having or exhibiting strength or vigorous health; strong and healthy.

In ZH

具有或表现出力量或充沛健康的；强壮和健康的。

Examples

  • The robust economy is expected to grow even further this year.
  • She has a robust appetite for adventure and travel.
  • They developed a robust plan to improve the city's infrastructure.
  • The software is robust enough to handle large amounts of data.
  • He maintains a robust exercise routine to stay fit.
adjective

Definition

Able to withstand or overcome adverse conditions; resilient.

In ZH

能够抵御或克服不利条件；有韧性的。

Examples

  • The robust design of the product ensures durability in harsh environments.
  • Her robust personality helped her overcome numerous challenges.
  • They need a robust strategy to tackle climate change.
  • The robust architecture of the bridge can withstand heavy loads.
  • His robust health allowed him to recover quickly from the illness.
