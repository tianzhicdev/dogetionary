Regale

Pronunciation:/rɪˈɡeɪl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 宴请
  • 款待
  • 享乐

Definitions

verb

Definition

to entertain or amuse someone with stories or jokes

In ZH

用故事或笑话娱乐或使某人开心

Examples

  • He regaled us with tales of his adventures in the jungle.
  • During the party, she regaled her guests with funny anecdotes.
  • The comedian regaled the audience with clever jokes.
  • He regaled his children with stories of his youth.
  • They regaled their friends with a recount of their recent trip.
