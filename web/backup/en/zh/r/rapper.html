Rapper

Pronunciation:ˈræpər
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 说唱歌手
  • 饶舌歌手

Definitions

noun

Definition

A rapper is a musician who performs rhythmically and with rhyme, often in a spoken style that emphasizes beats.

In ZH

说唱歌手是一种音乐家，以节奏和押韵的方式表演，通常采用一种强调节拍的说话风格。

Examples

  • I saw a rapper wear a gold chain that was bigger than my car!
  • The rapper just released a new album titled, 'I Can't Believe It's Not Butter'.
  • When the rapper tried to bake a cake, it turned into a rap battle with flour everywhere!

Cultural Notes

In hip hop culture, a rapper often tells stories or shares experiences through their lyrics, and they might engage in freestyle sessions, where they create raps on the spot.

verb

Definition

To rapper means to perform rap music, often improvising on a beat. (Note: This usage is less common.)

In ZH

“说唱”一词意味着在节拍上演唱说唱音乐，通常是即兴创作。 (注意：这种用法较少见。)

Examples

  • When I rap in the shower, I sound like a superstar... in my own mind!
  • I tried to rap during my Zoom meeting, but my boss just muted me.
  • Every time my dog howls, I think he's trying to rapper better than I can!

Cultural Notes

The verb form of 'to rap' is often used in casual contexts, especially among young people who enjoy creating music.
