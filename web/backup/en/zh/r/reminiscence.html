Reminiscence

Pronunciation:ˌrɛmɪˈnɪsəns
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 回忆
  • 追忆
  • 怀旧

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of remembering past experiences, often in a nostalgic or sentimental way.

In ZH

回忆过去经历的行为，通常带有怀旧或感伤的色彩。

Examples

  • As she looked through the old photographs, a sense of reminiscence washed over her.
  • He often shared his reminiscences of growing up in a small town.
  • The movie was a beautiful reminiscence of the carefree days of youth.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, reminiscing is a way to connect with family and friends, often during gatherings or celebrations.

noun

Definition

A memory or recollection of something from the past, especially one that is pleasant or significant.

In ZH

对过去事情的记忆或回忆，尤其是令人愉快或重要的事情。

Examples

  • His reminiscences of childhood brought smiles to the family during dinner.
  • I have fond reminiscences of my travels in Europe during college.
  • The book is filled with reminiscences of historical events that shaped the nation.

Cultural Notes

Reminiscence can serve as a storytelling tool that strengthens bonds in families by sharing personal histories.
