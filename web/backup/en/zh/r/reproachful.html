Reproachful

Pronunciation:/rɪˈproʊtʃfəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 指责的
  • 责备的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Expressing disapproval or disappointment; indicating blame or censure.

In ZH

表达不满或失望；指责或谴责。

Examples

  • She gave him a reproachful glance after his careless mistake.
  • He spoke in a reproachful tone, making it clear how he felt about the situation.
  • Her reproachful words left him feeling guilty about his actions.
  • The teacher's reproachful look silenced the student immediately.
  • They had a reproachful conversation about the missed deadlines.
