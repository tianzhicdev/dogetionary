Resourceful

Pronunciation:/rɪˈzɔːrsfəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 足智多谋
  • 资源丰富的
  • 机智的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having the ability to find quick and clever ways to overcome difficulties

In ZH

能够迅速而巧妙地找到克服困难的能力

Examples

  • She is very resourceful in finding solutions to problems.
  • During the crisis, his resourceful nature helped the whole team.
  • Being resourceful is a key skill for entrepreneurs.
  • They came up with resourceful ways to save money during the trip.
  • Her resourceful thinking led to an innovative product design.
