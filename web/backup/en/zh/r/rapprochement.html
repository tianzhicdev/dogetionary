Rapprochement

Pronunciation:/ræˈprɒʃ.mɒ̃/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 接近
  • 和解
  • 改善关系

Definitions

noun

Definition

an establishment or resumption of harmonious relations, particularly between countries or groups

In ZH

建立或恢复和谐关系，特别是国家或团体之间

Examples

  • The rapprochement between the two countries opened up new avenues for trade.
  • After years of tension, the two nations finally achieved a rapprochement.
  • Cultural exchanges have contributed to the rapprochement of the communities.
  • Rapprochement is key to resolving longstanding conflicts.
  • Diplomatic efforts aimed at rapprochement often require compromise from both sides.
