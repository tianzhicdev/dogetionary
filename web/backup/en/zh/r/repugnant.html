Repugnant

Pronunciation:/rɪˈpʌɡnənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 令人厌恶的
  • 反感的
  • 令人作呕的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

extremely distasteful or unacceptable

In ZH

极其令人厌恶或不可接受的

Examples

  • The idea of cheating on a test is repugnant to me.
  • Certain practices in the past were deemed repugnant by society.
  • She found the behavior of the bully utterly repugnant.
  • His repugnant comments outraged the audience.
  • Eating certain insects is repugnant to many cultures.
