Rapscallion

Pronunciation:/ræpˈskæljən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 恶棍
  • 不法之徒
  • 流氓

Definitions

noun

Definition

A mischievous or cheeky person, often charming but also a bit of a troublemaker.

In ZH

一个顽皮或无礼的人，通常很有魅力，但也有点麻烦。

Examples

  • The rapscallion next door always gets into mischief.
  • She smiled at the rapscallion, knowing he was up to no good.
  • In the story, the rapscallion outsmarts the town guard.
  • He was a rapscallion in his youth, always looking for adventure.
  • Despite being a rapscallion, he had a heart of gold.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in a lighthearted manner, suggesting mischief rather than serious wrongdoing.
