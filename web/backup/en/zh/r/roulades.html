Roulades

Pronunciation:/ruːˈlɑːdz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 花腔
  • 旋律
  • 音乐的装饰音

Definitions

noun

Definition

A rapid, melodious ornamentation of a musical phrase, typically involving a series of notes sung to one syllable.

In ZH

快速而旋律优美的音乐短语装饰，通常涉及一系列音符鸣唱于一个音节上。

Examples

  • The singer added roulades to her performance, showcasing her vocal agility.
  • In classical music, roulades are often used to embellish a melody.
  • The pianist played a series of roulades that impressed the audience.
  • Roulades can add dramatic flair to a simple song.
  • Many opera singers are known for their ability to execute elaborate roulades.
