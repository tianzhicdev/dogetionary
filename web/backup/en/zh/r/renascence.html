Renascence

EN→ZH

What Does Renascence Mean?

  • 复兴
  • 再生
  • 文艺复兴

Renascence Pronunciation

/rɪˈneɪsəns/

Renascence Definition

noun

Definition

a revival or renewed interest in something, particularly in arts and culture.

In ZH

复兴，特别是在艺术和文化方面的复兴或重新兴趣。

How to Use Renascence in a Sentence

  • The renascence of classical music has led to many new interpretations.
  • Many artists experienced a renascence during the early 20th century.
  • The city is known for its cultural renascence in the past decade.
  • There has been a renascence of interest in ancient philosophies.
  • This exhibition showcases the renascence of traditional crafts.

Cultural Notes

The term is often associated with the Renaissance period in Europe, which marked a significant cultural revival.

Synonyms for Renascence

  • Renunciation
