Reify

Pronunciation:/ˈrɛɪɪfaɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 使具体化
  • 物化
  • 实体化

Definitions

verb

Definition

To make something abstract more concrete or real.

In ZH

将抽象的事物变得更具体或真实。

Examples

  • Philosophers often criticize theories that reify concepts.
  • In his speech, he tried to reify the idea of freedom into tangible actions.
  • The artist aimed to reify his thoughts through his paintings.
  • The study aims to reify the theoretical model into applicable solutions.
  • Reifying emotions can help us understand them better.
