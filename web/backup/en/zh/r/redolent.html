Redolent

Pronunciation:/ˈrɛdələnt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 芳香的
  • 充满气味的
  • 使人想起的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

strongly reminiscent or suggestive of something; having a strong smell

In ZH

强烈的回忆或暗示某事；有强烈气味的

Examples

  • The garden was redolent of roses.
  • His speech was redolent of the great leaders of the past.
  • This dish is redolent of garlic and herbs.
  • The air was redolent with the scent of fresh coffee.
  • Memories of summer were redolent in the old song.
