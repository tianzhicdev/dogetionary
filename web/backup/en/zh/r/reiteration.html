Reiteration

EN→ZH

What Does Reiteration Mean?

  • 重申
  • 重述
  • 再言

Reiteration Pronunciation

/riːˌɪtəˈreɪʃən/

Reiteration Definition

noun

Definition

The act of saying something again or a repeated statement.

In ZH

重申或再次声明的行为。

How to Use Reiteration in a Sentence

  • The teacher's reiteration of the instructions helped all the students understand the assignment better.
  • His reiteration of the company's goals reinforced their importance to the team.
  • Reiteration of the key points can aid in solidifying one's understanding.
  • The manager's reiteration of the policy ensured that everyone was on the same page.
  • In her speech, she made a reiteration of her commitment to transparency.

Synonyms for Reiteration

  • Reify
