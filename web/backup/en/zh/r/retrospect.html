Retrospect

EN→ZH

What Does Retrospect Mean?

  • 回顾
  • 追溯
  • 反思

Retrospect Pronunciation

/ˈrɛtrəspɛkt/

Retrospect Definition

noun

Definition

a review or contemplation of past events or situations

In ZH

对过去事件或情况的回顾或思考

How to Use Retrospect in a Sentence

  • In retrospect, I should have made a different decision.
  • Looking back in retrospect, the project was a success.
  • She often reflects on her childhood in retrospect.
  • In retrospect, I realize how lucky I was to have that opportunity.
  • He spoke about his career in retrospect, highlighting key moments.
verb

Definition

to think about the past, especially in a way that benefits the present understanding

In ZH

思考过去，尤其是以对现在理解有益的方式

How to Use Retrospect in a Sentence

  • We often retrospect on our past mistakes to avoid repeating them.
  • It's important to retrospect and learn from experiences.
  • They decided to retrospect to gain insights for the future.
  • When we retrospect, we can appreciate our growth over the years.
  • Retrospecting on her past led her to new conclusions about her life.

Synonyms for Retrospect

  • Reticent
  • Reticulate
  • Retort
  • Retrench
  • Retribution
