Resonant

EN→ZH

What Does Resonant Mean?

  • 共鸣的
  • 回声的
  • 响亮的

Resonant Pronunciation

/ˈrɛzənənt/

Resonant Definition

adjective

Definition

Having the ability to evoke resonance, typically used to describe sounds that are deep, clear, and continuing to sound; also implies a capacity to produce a strong emotional response.

In ZH

具有发出共鸣的能力，通常用来描述声音深沉、清晰且持续发声的特性；也暗示能产生强烈情感反应的能力。

How to Use Resonant in a Sentence

  • The resonant voice of the speaker captivated the audience.
  • We could hear the resonant tones of the bell from miles away.
  • Her singing was resonant and filled the entire hall with warmth.
  • The resonant laughter of children brought joy to the park.
  • He played a resonant chord that echoed beautifully in the room.
