Recumbent

Pronunciation:/rɪˈkʌm.bənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 卧姿的
  • 躺下的
  • 斜靠的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

lying down; in a position of rest or repose

In ZH

躺着的；处于休息或安静状态的姿势

Examples

  • He was recumbent on the sofa, watching television.
  • The recumbent bike is designed for comfort during long rides.
  • She preferred to read in a recumbent position on her bed.
  • The statue depicted a recumbent figure of a reclining woman.
  • After a long day, he found solace in his recumbent chair.
noun

Definition

a type of bicycle designed with a reclining seat

In ZH

一种设计为有倾斜座椅的自行车

Examples

  • Many cyclists enjoy the comfort of riding a recumbent.
  • Recumbents are known for their aerodynamic efficiency.
  • He decided to buy a recumbent after testing several models.
  • The recumbent in the shop had a beautiful design and great features.
  • Riding a recumbent can offer a different experience compared to traditional bikes.

Cultural Notes

Recumbent bicycles are often favored for their ergonomic design, making them popular among long-distance cyclists.
