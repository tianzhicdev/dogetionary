Reprehensible

EN→ZH

What Does Reprehensible Mean?

  • 应受谴责的
  • 可指责的
  • 应该谴责的

Reprehensible Pronunciation

/ˌrɛprɪˈhɛnsɪbəl/

Reprehensible Definition

adjective

Definition

deserving condemnation or criticism; morally objectionable.

In ZH

应受谴责的；值得指责的；道德上不可接受的。

How to Use Reprehensible in a Sentence

  • His actions were reprehensible and could not be justified.
  • The company's reprehensible practices were exposed by the media.
  • She made a reprehensible decision that hurt many people.
  • It is reprehensible to cheat in an exam.
  • The reprehensible behavior of the officials shocked the public.

Synonyms for Reprehensible

  • Reparative
  • Repartee
  • Repentance
  • Repercussion
  • Repertory
