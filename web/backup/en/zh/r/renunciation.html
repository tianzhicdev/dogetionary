Renunciation

Pronunciation:/rɪˌnʌn.siˈeɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 放弃
  • 弃权
  • 宣告放弃

Definitions

noun

Definition

the formal rejection of something, typically a belief, claim, or course of action.

In ZH

对某事的正式拒绝，通常是信仰、主张或行动方针。

Examples

  • His renunciation of wealth was surprising to everyone.
  • She made a public renunciation of her previous statements.
  • The monk's renunciation of worldly pleasures inspired many.
  • He declared his renunciation of the title and all its privileges.
  • Her renunciation came after years of reflection on her beliefs.
