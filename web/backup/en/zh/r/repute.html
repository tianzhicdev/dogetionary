Repute

Pronunciation:/rɪˈpjuːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 名声
  • 声誉
  • 名誉

Definitions

noun

Definition

The opinion generally held of someone or something; the state of being regarded in a particular way.

In ZH

人们普遍持有的对某人或某事的看法；在特定方式下被看待的状态。

Examples

  • His repute as a reliable source is well-known.
  • The restaurant has a good repute for its high-quality food.
  • She has a repute for being an excellent teacher.
  • His repute suffered after the scandal.
  • A company’s repute can take years to build.
verb

Definition

To give a specified name or title to.

In ZH

赋予特定的名称或称号。

Examples

  • They are reputed to be the best in the field.
  • She is reputed as one of the foremost experts.
  • He is reputed to have invented the device.
  • The author is reputed to write with great passion.
  • This city is reputed for its vibrant arts scene.

  • Repertory
