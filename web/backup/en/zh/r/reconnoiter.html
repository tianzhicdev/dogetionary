Reconnoiter

Pronunciation:/ˌrɛkəˈnɔɪtə(r)/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 侦察
  • 勘查

Definitions

verb

Definition

To make a preliminary survey to gather information, especially about military enemy positions or operations.

In ZH

进行初步调查以收集信息，尤其是关于敌方位置或军事行动的情报。

Examples

  • The soldiers were sent to reconnoiter the enemy's fortifications.
  • Before the hiking trip, they decided to reconnoiter the trail.
  • The scout was tasked with reconnoitering the area for potential dangers.
  • They planned to reconnoiter the coastline before launching their operation.
  • The general ordered his men to reconnoiter the terrain ahead of the battle.
