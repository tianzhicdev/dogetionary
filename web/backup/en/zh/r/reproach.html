Reproach

Pronunciation:/rɪˈproʊtʃ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 责备
  • 耻辱
  • 指责
  • 谴责

Definitions

noun

Definition

An expression of disapproval or disappointment.

In ZH

对不满或失望的表达。

Examples

  • His actions brought reproach upon the entire family.
  • She looked at him with reproach in her eyes.
  • The child's behavior was met with reproach from the teacher.
  • He faced reproach from his peers after the incident.
  • There was a hint of reproach in her tone.
verb

Definition

To address someone in such a way as to express disapproval.

In ZH

以表达不满的方式对某人说话。

Examples

  • She reproached him for his negligence.
  • He reproached her for not inviting him to the party.
  • They reproached the government for its inaction.
  • I don't want to reproach you, but you missed the deadline.
  • He felt it was necessary to reproach his friend for lying.

Related Words

  • Reparative
  • Repartee
  • Repentance
  • Repercussion
  • Repertory
