Remonstrate

EN→ZH

What Does Remonstrate Mean?

  • 抗议
  • 反对
  • 申诉

Remonstrate Pronunciation

/rɪˈmɒn.streɪt/

Remonstrate Definition

verb

Definition

to make a forcefully reproachful protest.

In ZH

提出强烈的抗议或反对意见。

How to Use Remonstrate in a Sentence

  • She remonstrated with her friend for being late.
  • The workers remonstrated against the unfair working conditions.
  • He remonstrated loudly when he saw the decision was unjust.
  • The teachers remonstrated with the administration about the new policies.
  • They remonstrated in public to draw attention to their cause.

Synonyms for Remonstrate

  • Reminiscence
  • Reminiscent
  • Remonstrance
  • Remonstration
