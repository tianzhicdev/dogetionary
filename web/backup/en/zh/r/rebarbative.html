Rebarbative

Pronunciation:/rɪˈbɑː.bə.tɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 令人厌恶的
  • 生涩的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Causing annoyance or irritation; off-putting.

In ZH

引起烦恼或恼怒的；令人不悦的。

Examples

  • His rebarbative attitude made it difficult for anyone to approach him.
  • The rebarbative style of the film left many viewers unimpressed.
  • Despite her talent, her rebarbative personality kept her from making friends.
  • The rebarbative nature of the topic led to a tense discussion.
  • Many found the professor's rebarbative lecturing style to be discouraging.
