Reticent

EN→ZH

What Does Reticent Mean?

  • 沉默寡言的
  • 缄默的

Reticent Pronunciation

/ˈrɛtɪsənt/

Reticent Definition

adjective

Definition

Not revealing one's thoughts or feelings readily; reserved or silent.

In ZH

不轻易透露思想或感情的；沉默或保留的。

How to Use Reticent in a Sentence

  • She was reticent about her personal life.
  • Despite his wealth, he was reticent to talk about it.
  • The reticent student surprised everyone when he finally spoke up.
  • He is generally reticent, preferring to listen rather than engage in conversation.
  • Her reticent nature made her a great listener.

Synonyms for Reticent

  • Reticulate
  • Retort
  • Retrench
  • Retribution
  • Retrograde
