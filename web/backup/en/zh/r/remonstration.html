Remonstration

Pronunciation:/ˌrɛmənˈstreɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 抗议
  • 申诉
  • 反对
  • 陈述意见

Definitions

noun

Definition

a forcefully reproachful protest.

In ZH

强烈的抗议或反对。

Examples

  • The citizens held a remonstration against the new law.
  • His remonstration was met with indifference by the authorities.
  • After much remonstration, the committee decided to reconsider their decision.
  • A loud remonstration erupted during the town hall meeting.
  • The remonstration of the workers led to a negotiation with management.
