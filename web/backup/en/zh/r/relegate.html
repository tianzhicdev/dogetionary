Relegate

EN→ZH

What Does Relegate Mean?

  • 降级
  • relegation
  • 转移

Relegate Pronunciation

/ˈrɛləˌɡeɪt/

Relegate Definition

verb

Definition

to assign to a lower position or rank

In ZH

将...降级或转移到较低的位置或等级

How to Use Relegate in a Sentence

  • The manager decided to relegate the underperforming employee to a different department.
  • Due to the team's poor performance, they were relegated to a lower league this season.
  • He was relegated to a supporting role after the lead actor's return.
  • The committee will relegate the issue to the next meeting for further discussion.
  • After the scandal, the politician was forced to relegate himself from public life.

Synonyms for Relegate

  • Relent
  • Reluctant
