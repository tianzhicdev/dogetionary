Reciprocal

Pronunciation:/rɪˈsɪprəkl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 互惠的
  • 相互的
  • 互相的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

involving two parties or things that mutually affect each other

In ZH

涉及两个互相影响的当事人或事物

Examples

  • The reciprocal relationship between teacher and student enhances learning.
  • They made a reciprocal agreement to support each other's projects.
  • In mathematics, the reciprocal of a number is one divided by that number.
  • The treaty established reciprocal trade benefits for both countries.
  • Friendship is often based on reciprocal trust and respect.
noun

Definition

something that is reciprocated; in mathematics, the reciprocal of a number is one divided by that number

In ZH

一种被回报的事物；在数学中，一个数的倒数是一除以该数

Examples

  • In mathematics, the reciprocal of 4 is 0.25.
  • The concept of reciprocals is crucial in solving equations.
  • They established a reciprocal of favors in their friendship.
  • Understanding reciprocals helps in grasping division and fractions.
  • The reciprocal of a fraction is obtained by flipping the numerator and denominator.
