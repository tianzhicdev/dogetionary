Rapacity

Pronunciation:/rəˈpæs.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 贪婪
  • 掠夺

Definitions

noun

Definition

aggressive greed; voraciousness

In ZH

侵略性的贪婪；贪得无厌

Examples

  • The rapacity of the corporation led to the destruction of the local environment.
  • Historically, the rapacity of conquerors often led to the downfall of entire civilizations.
  • His rapacity was evident in the way he tried to monopolize all the resources.
  • The film critiques the rapacity of the financial system.
  • In literature, characters exhibiting rapacity are often portrayed as villains.
