Repose

Pronunciation:/rɪˈpoʊz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 安息
  • 静止
  • 休息

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of rest, sleep, or tranquility.

In ZH

一种休息、睡眠或宁静的状态。

Examples

  • She found repose in the quiet of the morning.
  • In repose, the statue looked almost lifelike.
  • He sighed in repose as the sun set behind the mountains.
  • After a long day, she needed some repose to recharge.
  • The garden offered a perfect place for repose and reflection.
verb

Definition

To lie or rest in a particular place.

In ZH

在某个特定地点躺着或休息。

Examples

  • He chose to repose under the shade of the tree.
  • The cat likes to repose on the windowsill during sunny afternoons.
  • They decided to repose themselves by the fireplace after dinner.
  • During the break, she likes to repose in her favorite chair.
  • The ancient philosophers often spoke of the need to repose and meditate.
