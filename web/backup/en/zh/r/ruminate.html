Ruminate

Pronunciation:/ˈruː.mɪ.neɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 反刍
  • 沉思
  • 考虑

Definitions

verb

Definition

To think carefully and for a long period about something.

In ZH

仔细思考并长期考虑某事。

Examples

  • She ruminated over the decision for several days.
  • He has a tendency to ruminate on past mistakes.
  • Before making a choice, it's wise to ruminate on your options.
  • The author ruminates on the meaning of life in her latest book.
  • They sat in silence, ruminating on the events of the day.
