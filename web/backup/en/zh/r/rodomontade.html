Rodomontade

Pronunciation:/ˌrɒdəˈmɒnteɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 大言不惭的行为
  • 自夸
  • 夸张的言辞

Definitions

noun

Definition

boastful or inflated behavior; bragging or boasting in a way that is exaggerated or pretentious.

In ZH

自夸或夸张的行为；以夸大或自负的方式吹嘘或夸耀。

Examples

  • His speech was full of rodomontade, lacking any real substance.
  • She engaged in rodomontade, claiming to have achieved much more than she really had.
  • The politician's rodomontade during the debate turned many voters off.
  • We grew tired of his constant rodomontade at the party.
  • In literature, rodomontade is often used to develop characters who are arrogant or boastful.
