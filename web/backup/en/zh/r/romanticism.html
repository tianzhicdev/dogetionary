Romanticism

EN→ZH

What Does Romanticism Mean?

  • 浪漫主义
  • 浪漫派

Romanticism Pronunciation

/roʊˈmæntɪsɪzəm/

Romanticism Definition

noun

Definition

a movement in literature, art, and music during the late 18th and early 19th centuries that emphasized inspiration, subjectivity, and the primacy of the individual.

In ZH

一种在18世纪末至19世纪初的文学、艺术和音乐运动，强调灵感、主观性和个体的首要性。

How to Use Romanticism in a Sentence

  • Romanticism celebrated nature and the individual spirit.
  • The paintings of the Romantic period often depicted dramatic landscapes.
  • Many poets of Romanticism wrote about their feelings and emotions.
  • Romanticism was a reaction against the Industrial Revolution.
  • The themes of love and beauty were central to Romanticism.
