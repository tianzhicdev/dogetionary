Requiem

Pronunciation:/ˈrɛkwɪəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 安魂曲
  • 追悼弥撒
  • 安息曲

Definitions

noun

Definition

A mass for the repose of the souls of the dead, often characterized by solemnity and musical composition.

In ZH

为死者的灵魂安息而举行的弥撒，通常以庄重和音乐作品为特征。

Examples

  • The composer wrote a beautiful requiem to honor those lost in war.
  • During the service, a requiem was performed to commemorate the deceased.
  • She sang a requiem solo that moved everyone to tears.
  • The requiem was played at the memorial service for the fallen soldiers.
  • In many cultures, a requiem is an important ritual for the deceased.

Cultural Notes

Requiems are often associated with Catholic traditions, but they have been adapted across various cultures and musical styles.
