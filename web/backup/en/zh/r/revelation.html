Revelation

Pronunciation:/ˌrɛvəˈleɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 启示
  • 揭示
  • 启示录

Definitions

noun

Definition

a surprising and previously unknown fact, especially one that is made known in a dramatic way.

In ZH

一个令人惊讶且之前未知的事实，尤其是在戏剧性的方式下被揭示出来。

Examples

  • The scientist had a revelation about the nature of the universe.
  • The book contains revelations about the author's life.
  • Her sudden revelation changed the course of the discussion.
  • The revelation of the secret left everyone in shock.
  • In the meeting, he made a revelation that surprised all of us.
