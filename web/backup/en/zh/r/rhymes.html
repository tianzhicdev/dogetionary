Rhymes

Pronunciation:raɪmz
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 押韵
  • 韵脚

Definitions

noun

Definition

A rhyme is a correspondence of sounds between different words, especially when these are used at the end of lines in poetry.

In ZH

押韵是指不同单词之间在音节上的对应，尤其是在诗歌的行尾使用时。

Examples

  • The poem has a beautiful rhyme scheme that makes it sound melodious.
  • Children often enjoy nursery rhymes because they are simple and fun to recite.
  • In songwriting, using rhymes can help create memorable lyrics.

Cultural Notes

Rhyming is a fundamental aspect of poetry and songwriting in English. It's often used to enhance the musical quality of language.

verb

Definition

To rhyme means to have or create a similar sound at the end of words or lines, especially in poetry or music.

In ZH

押韵的意思是指在单词或诗句的末尾有相似的声音，尤其是在诗歌或音乐中。

Examples

  • The artist decided to rhyme the last two lines of his song for a catchy effect.
  • Can you rhyme 'cat' with any other word?
  • She loves to rhyme different words together when she writes.

Cultural Notes

Rhyme in English is widely used in various forms of literature and art, making the language playful and engaging.
