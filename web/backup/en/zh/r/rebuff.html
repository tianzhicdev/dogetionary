Rebuff

Pronunciation:/rɪˈbʌf/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 拒绝
  • 回绝
  • 抵制

Definitions

verb

Definition

to reject (someone or something) in an abrupt or curt manner

In ZH

以突然或简短的方式拒绝（某人或某事）

Examples

  • He attempted to apologize, but she rebuffed him sharply.
  • The proposal was rebuffed by the committee without any discussion.
  • Despite his attempts to ask her out, she consistently rebuffed his advances.
  • Her offer was rebuffed, leaving her feeling embarrassed.
  • When asked for help, she rebuffed him coldly.
noun

Definition

an abrupt or blunt rejection

In ZH

突然或生硬的拒绝

Examples

  • The rebuff he received from his boss was unexpected.
  • Her rebuff left him speechless and hurt.
  • After his rebuff, he chose to remain silent.
  • The rebuff was a clear sign that he should not try again.
  • Getting a rebuff can be discouraging, but it’s often part of life.
