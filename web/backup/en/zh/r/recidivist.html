Recidivist

EN→ZH

What Does Recidivist Mean?

  • 再犯者
  • 惯犯

Recidivist Pronunciation

/rɪˈsɪdɪvɪst/

Recidivist Definition

noun

Definition

A person who reoffends, especially after having been punished for a previous crime.

In ZH

一个在受过惩罚后再次犯罪的人，尤其是在之前的犯罪之后。

How to Use Recidivist in a Sentence

  • The judge considered the defendant a recidivist.
  • Recidivists often struggle to reintegrate into society after serving their sentences.
  • Programs for recidivists aim to reduce the likelihood of reoffending.
  • The prison system sometimes fails to rehabilitate recidivists effectively.
  • Community-based initiatives support recidivists in finding employment.

Synonyms for Recidivist

  • Recalcitrance
  • Recalcitrant
  • Recant
  • Recantation
  • Recapitulate
