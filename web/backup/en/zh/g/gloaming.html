Gloaming

Pronunciation:/ˈɡloʊ.mɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 黄昏
  • 傍晚
  • 暮色

Definitions

noun

Definition

The time of day when daylight fades, especially just before nightfall.

In ZH

白天逐渐消逝的时刻，尤其是夜幕降临之前的时间。

Examples

  • The gloaming is the perfect time for a walk in the park.
  • As the gloaming set in, the stars began to twinkle in the sky.
  • She loved to watch the gloaming from her window.
  • The photographer captured the beauty of the gloaming in his pictures.
  • During the gloaming, the landscape took on a magical quality.
