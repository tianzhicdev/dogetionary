Grievance

EN→ZH

What Does Grievance Mean?

  • 抱怨
  • 不满
  • 诉苦
  • 冤屈

Grievance Pronunciation

/ˈɡriː.vəns/

Grievance Definition

noun

Definition

A complaint or a reason for dissatisfaction.

In ZH

投诉或不满的原因。

How to Use Grievance in a Sentence

  • She filed a grievance with the human resources department.
  • His main grievance was about the unfair treatment at work.
  • They expressed their grievances during the meeting.
  • The union addressed the grievances of the workers effectively.
  • After much deliberation, they decided to submit a formal grievance.

Synonyms for Grievance

  • Grievous
  • Grit
