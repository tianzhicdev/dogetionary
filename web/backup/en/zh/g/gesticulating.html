Gesticulating

EN→ZH

What Does Gesticulating Mean?

  • 挥手
  • 手势
  • 做手势

Gesticulating Pronunciation

/dʒɛˈstɪkjʊleɪtɪŋ/

Gesticulating Definition

verb

Definition

Using gestures, especially dramatic ones, to emphasize or illustrate what one is saying.

In ZH

用手势，尤其是戏剧性的手势，来强调或说明所说的内容。

How to Use Gesticulating in a Sentence

  • She was gesticulating wildly as she spoke about her travels.
  • During the presentation, he was gesticulating to keep the audience engaged.
  • The angry man was gesticulating to express his frustration.
  • They gesticulated to each other to communicate over the noise.
  • He often gesticulates when he's excited about a topic.

Synonyms for Gesticulating

  • Gestalt
  • Gestaltism
  • Gestate
  • Gestation
  • Gesticulate
