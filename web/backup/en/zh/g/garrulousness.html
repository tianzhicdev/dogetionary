Garrulousness

Pronunciation:/ˈɡɛr.ə.ləs.nəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 喋喋不休
  • 多话
  • 讲个不停

Definitions

noun

Definition

the quality of being excessively talkative, especially on trivial matters

In ZH

过度谈话的特质，尤其是琐事

Examples

  • Her garrulousness made the meeting last longer than necessary.
  • The garrulousness of the tour guide kept us entertained throughout the trip.
  • In contrast to his brother's silence, his garrulousness was often a source of amusement.
  • The garrulousness of the chatty neighbor was both a blessing and a curse.
  • During the dinner party, her garrulousness dominated the conversation.
