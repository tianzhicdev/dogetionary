Glitch

Pronunciation:ɡlɪtʃ
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 故障
  • 瑕疵
  • 小故障

Definitions

noun

Definition

A minor malfunction or error in a system, particularly in technology or software.

In ZH

系统中的小故障，尤其是在技术或软件中。

Examples

  • The video game had a glitch that made the character walk through walls.
  • There was a glitch in the application that caused it to crash unexpectedly.
  • After the software update, I noticed a glitch that affected the performance.

Cultural Notes

In technology and gaming culture, 'glitch' often refers to unexpected occurrences that can be humorous or frustrating.

verb

Definition

To experience a minor malfunction or error.

In ZH

经历小故障或错误。

Examples

  • The system glitched during the presentation, causing a delay.
  • I think my computer glitched again when I tried to save the file.
  • Sometimes the app will glitch and not respond properly.

Cultural Notes

'Glitching' can also be used informally to describe situations where things don’t go as planned, even outside of technology.
