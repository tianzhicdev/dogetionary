Gesticulate

EN→ZH

What Does Gesticulate Mean?

  • 手势表达
  • 挥动手臂
  • 做手势

Gesticulate Pronunciation

/dʒɛˈstɪk.jə.leɪt/

Gesticulate Definition

verb

Definition

To use gestures, especially dramatic ones, instead of speaking or to emphasize one's words.

In ZH

用手势代替说话，尤其是强调某些话时

How to Use Gesticulate in a Sentence

  • She tends to gesticulate wildly when she talks.
  • He gesticulated to express his excitement about the project.
  • The lawyer gesticulated to the jury during his argument.
  • In the meeting, she gesticulated to illustrate her point more clearly.
  • Children often gesticulate when they are animated in conversation.

Synonyms for Gesticulate

  • Gestalt
  • Gestaltism
  • Gestate
  • Gestation
  • Gesticulating
