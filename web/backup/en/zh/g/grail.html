Grail

Pronunciation:/ɡreɪl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 圣杯
  • 杯子
  • 理想目标
  • 极难获得的东西

Definitions

noun

Definition

A cup or chalice that is said to have miraculous powers and to be the object of a quest in Arthurian legend.

In ZH

据说具有奇迹般力量的杯子或圣杯，并且是亚瑟王传说中的一个探求目标。

Examples

  • Many knights sought the Holy Grail during their quests.
  • The legend of the Holy Grail has inspired countless stories.
  • In the movie, the protagonist searches for the grail to restore peace.
  • The grail is often seen as a symbol of perfection.
  • Finding the grail symbolizes the journey for enlightenment.

Cultural Notes

In Arthurian legend, the Holy Grail is often associated with the Last Supper and is considered a symbol of divine grace.
