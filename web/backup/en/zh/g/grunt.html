Grunt

Pronunciation:/ɡrʌnt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 咕哝
  • 低沉的声音
  • 士兵

Definitions

noun

Definition

a low, guttural sound made by a human or animal, often indicating effort or displeasure

In ZH

人或动物发出的低沉的、喉音的声音，通常表示努力或不快。

Examples

  • The old man made a grunt as he lifted the heavy box.
  • The pig let out a grunt after eating its meal.
  • She heard a grunt from the other room, indicating someone was struggling.
  • He responded with a grunt instead of a verbal answer.
  • The athlete gave a grunt of exertion during the competition.
verb

Definition

to make a low, guttural sound

In ZH

发出低沉的喉音。

Examples

  • He grunted in approval as he nodded his head.
  • The dog grunted while rolling over on the carpet.
  • She grunted in frustration when she dropped her keys.
  • The soldier grunted as he carried his equipment up the hill.
  • He grunted softly while working out at the gym.
