Grandiloquence

EN→ZH

What Does Grandiloquence Mean?

  • 夸张
  • 言辞华丽
  • 浮夸的言辞

Grandiloquence Pronunciation

/ɡrænˈdɪləkwəns/

Grandiloquence Definition

noun

Definition

A lofty, pompous, or extravagant manner of speaking; language that is high-flown or bombastic.

In ZH

一种高尚、浮夸或奢华的说话方式；辞藻华丽或夸张的语言。

How to Use Grandiloquence in a Sentence

  • His grandiloquence during the speech impressed the audience.
  • The politician's grandiloquence masked the simplicity of his message.
  • She was known for her grandiloquence, using elaborate words to express simple ideas.
  • In literature, grandiloquence can sometimes confuse the reader.
  • Despite his grandiloquence, the truth was very straightforward.

Synonyms for Grandiloquence

  • Grade
  • Grail
  • Grandeur
  • Grandiloquent
  • Grandiose
