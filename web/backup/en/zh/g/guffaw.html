Guffaw

Pronunciation:/ɡəˈfɔː/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 大笑
  • 呵呵笑
  • 狂笑
  • 哄笑

Definitions

noun

Definition

A loud and boisterous laugh.

In ZH

一种响亮而喧闹的笑声。

Examples

  • His guffaw filled the room and made everyone smile.
  • She couldn't help but let out a guffaw at the joke.
  • The audience erupted in guffaws during the comedy show.
  • I heard his guffaw from across the street.
  • The guffaw of the child was infectious, spreading laughter all around.
verb

Definition

To laugh loudly and boisterously.

In ZH

大声而喧闹地笑。

Examples

  • The comedian's joke caused everyone to guffaw uncontrollably.
  • He guffawed at the absurdity of the situation.
  • We all guffawed when the prank was revealed.
  • She guffawed so hard that she nearly fell off her chair.
  • They guffawed together at the funny movie scene.
