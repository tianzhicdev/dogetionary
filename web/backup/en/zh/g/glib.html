Glib

Pronunciation:/ɡlɪb/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 口齿伶俐的
  • 流畅的
  • 油嘴滑舌的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Smooth-talking or overly persuasive in a way that is not sincere or trustworthy.

In ZH

口齿伶俐的，过于圆滑的，方式不真诚或不可信。

Examples

  • His glib remarks made everyone suspicious of his true intentions.
  • She has a glib way of speaking that can charm her audience.
  • The politician's glib answers failed to address the serious questions.
  • Don't be deceived by his glib words; he doesn't truly care.
  • Her glib responses were a cover for her lack of real knowledge.
