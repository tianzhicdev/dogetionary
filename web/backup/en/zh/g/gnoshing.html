Gnoshing

EN→ZH

What Does Gnoshing Mean?

  • 嘴里嚼的
  • 吃东西
  • 进食

Gnoshing Pronunciation

/ˈnɒʃɪŋ/

Gnoshing Definition

verb

Definition

To eat or chew noisily or with enthusiasm, often casually or in a relaxed manner.

In ZH

大声或热情地吃或咀嚼，通常是随意或放松的方式。

How to Use Gnoshing in a Sentence

  • I was gnoshing on popcorn while watching the movie.
  • He loves gnoshing on chips during the game.
  • She was gnoshing on a sandwich in the park.
  • The kids were gnoshing happily at the picnic.
  • After the hike, we started gnoshing on some trail mix.

Synonyms for Gnoshing

  • Gnosis
  • Gnostic
  • Gnosticism
