Gossamer

EN→ZH

What Does Gossamer Mean?

  • 轻纱
  • 薄纱
  • 蛛丝

Gossamer Pronunciation

/ˈɡɒs.ə.mər/

Gossamer Definition

noun

Definition

A fine, filmy substance consisting of cobwebs spun by small spiders, or a delicate and light fabric.

In ZH

由小蜘蛛吐出的细丝，或一种轻薄的织物。

How to Use Gossamer in a Sentence

  • The morning light caught the gossamer threads of the spider's web.
  • She wore a dress made of gossamer that floated around her as she moved.
  • The gossamer veil added an ethereal quality to the bride's appearance.
  • In autumn, gossamer can often be seen glistening on grass in the early morning.
  • The artist painted the landscape with a gossamer touch, creating a dreamlike effect.
