Gemutlichkeit

Pronunciation:/ɡəˈmʊt.lɪx.kaɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 舒适
  • 温馨
  • 惬意

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of warmth and friendliness, often associated with a sense of belonging and comfort in social settings.

In ZH

一种热情友好、舒适的状态，通常与在社交环境中归属感和舒适感相关联。
