Geopolitics

Pronunciation:/ˌdʒiːəʊˈpɒlɪtɪks/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 地缘政治

Definitions

noun

Definition

The study of the effects of geography (human and physical) on international politics and international relations.

In ZH

地理（人文和自然）对国际政治和国际关系影响的研究。

Examples

  • Geopolitics plays a significant role in shaping global events.
  • Understanding geopolitics is crucial for international business.
  • The country's foreign policy is heavily influenced by geopolitics.
  • Geopolitics can explain conflicts between nations.
  • He studied geopolitics to better understand global trends.
