Gibberish

EN→ZH

What Does Gibberish Mean?

  • 胡言乱语
  • 无意义的话
  • 乱码

Gibberish Pronunciation

/ˈdʒɪbərɪʃ/

Gibberish Definition

noun

Definition

spoken or written language that is meaningless or difficult to understand.

In ZH

无意义或难以理解的口语或书面语言。

How to Use Gibberish in a Sentence

  • The instructions were just gibberish to me.
  • He was speaking in gibberish after staying up all night.
  • Her explanation was so complicated that it sounded like gibberish.
  • When I read the contract, it seemed like gibberish filled with legal jargon.
  • The software produced gibberish when I pressed the wrong button.

Synonyms for Gibberish

  • Gibbous
