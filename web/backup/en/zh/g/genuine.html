Genuine

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒɛnjʊɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 真实的
  • 真诚的
  • 真正的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Something that is real, authentic, or sincere.

In ZH

真实的，真正的，诚恳的。

Examples

  • She gave a genuine smile that lit up the room.
  • This painting is a genuine piece of art from the 18th century.
  • He is a genuine person who always speaks his mind.
  • I appreciate your genuine concern for my well-being.
  • The antique dealer assured me that the vase was genuine.
