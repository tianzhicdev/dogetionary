Glossolalia

EN→ZH

What Does Glossolalia Mean?

  • 说方言
  • 灵语
  • 无意义的语言

Glossolalia Pronunciation

/ˌɡlɒsəˈleɪliə/

Glossolalia Definition

noun

Definition

The phenomenon of speaking in an unknown language, often in a religious context.

In ZH

在宗教环境中说出一种未知语言的现象。

How to Use Glossolalia in a Sentence

  • Many Pentecostals practice glossolalia during their worship services.
  • Glossolalia can serve as a form of spiritual expression.
  • Some psychologists study glossolalia to understand its effects on the brain.
  • In ancient cultures, glossolalia was often seen as a divine gift.
  • The use of glossolalia varies widely among different religious groups.
