Gregarian

Pronunciation:/ɡrɪˈɡɛəriən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 格雷戈里安

Definitions

noun

Definition

A term that appears to refer to communities or individuals that exhibit a strong preference for social gatherings or collective living, but is often confused with 'gregarious'.

In ZH

一个似乎指的是那些表现出强烈社交偏好或集体生活的个体或社区的术语，但经常与'群居的'混淆。

Examples

  • The gregarian society thrived on teamwork and collaboration.
  • In a gregarian lifestyle, social interactions are prioritized.
  • Many found comfort in the gregarian habits of their neighbors.
  • His gregarian nature made him a beloved member of the community.
  • During festivals, the gregarian spirit was evident in everyone's participation.
