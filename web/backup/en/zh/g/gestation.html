Gestation

EN→ZH

What Does Gestation Mean?

  • 怀孕
  • 妊娠
  • 孕期

Gestation Pronunciation

/dʒɛsˈteɪʃən/

Gestation Definition

noun

Definition

The process of carrying or being carried in the womb between conception and birth.

In ZH

从受孕到出生在子宫内怀孕或被怀孕的过程。

How to Use Gestation in a Sentence

  • The gestation period for humans is approximately nine months.
  • Veterinarians can determine the gestation stage of an animal through ultrasound.
  • The duration of gestation varies widely among different species.
  • Gestation is crucial for fetal development.
  • Many factors can affect the health of a pregnancy during gestation.
