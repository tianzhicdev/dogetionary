Gregariously

EN→ZH

What Does Gregariously Mean?

  • 群居地
  • 社交地
  • 社群地

Gregariously Pronunciation

/ɡrɪˈɡɛəriəsli/

Gregariously Definition

adverb

Definition

In a gregarious manner; sociably, enjoying the company of others.

In ZH

以群居的方式；社交地，享受他人的陪伴。

How to Use Gregariously in a Sentence

  • He gregariously mingled with the crowd at the festival.
  • The dog gregariously played with the other pets in the park.
  • She gregariously hosted gatherings every weekend.
  • They gregariously shared their experiences during the meeting.
  • The children gregariously interacted with each other during recess.

Synonyms for Gregariously

  • Gregarian
  • Gregarious
