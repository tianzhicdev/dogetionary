Gerrymandering

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒɛrɪˌmændərɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 选区划分不公
  • 人为划分选区

Definitions

noun

Definition

The manipulation of electoral district boundaries to favor one party or class.

In ZH

人为操控选区边界以使某一政党或阶层受益。

Examples

  • Gerrymandering can significantly influence election outcomes.
  • The state legislature engaged in gerrymandering to secure more seats.
  • Various groups are protesting against gerrymandering practices.
  • Gerrymandering undermines the democratic process by diluting votes.
  • Courts are often tasked with ruling on cases of alleged gerrymandering.
