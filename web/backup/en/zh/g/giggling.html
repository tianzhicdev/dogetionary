Giggling

Pronunciation:/ˈɡɪɡəlɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 咯咯笑
  • 轻声笑
  • 咯咯声

Definitions

verb

Definition

to laugh lightly and spontaneously, often in a silly or nervous manner

In ZH

轻声轻笑，通常带有傻乎乎或紧张的感觉

Examples

  • The children were giggling at the funny clown.
  • She couldn't help giggling when he told that joke.
  • They were giggling quietly in the back of the classroom.
  • Giggling is contagious; soon everyone was laughing along.
  • He heard her giggling from the other room.
noun

Definition

a series of light, silly laughs

In ZH

一连串轻松、傻乎乎的笑声

Examples

  • Her giggling was so infectious that everyone around her started laughing.
  • I heard giggling coming from the playground.
  • The giggling of the girls echoed through the hallway.
  • His giggling made it difficult for him to finish his speech.
  • The sound of giggling filled the air during the party.
