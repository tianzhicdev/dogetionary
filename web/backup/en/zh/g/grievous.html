Grievous

EN→ZH

What Does Grievous Mean?

  • 严重的
  • 悲伤的
  • 痛苦的

Grievous Pronunciation

/ˈɡriːvəs/

Grievous Definition

adjective

Definition

causing grief or sorrow; very serious or severe

In ZH

造成悲伤或痛苦的；非常严重或严重的

How to Use Grievous in a Sentence

  • The grievous loss of her father left her in despair.
  • His grievous error in judgement cost the company millions.
  • The grievous injuries sustained by the victims prompted a public outcry.
  • They faced grievous consequences for their reckless actions.
  • The grievous nature of the crime shocked the entire community.

Synonyms for Grievous

  • Grievance
  • Grit
