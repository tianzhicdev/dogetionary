Gestate

Pronunciation:/ˈdʒɛsteɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 孕育
  • 怀孕
  • 酝酿

Definitions

verb

Definition

to carry a fetus in the womb during the period from conception to birth

In ZH

在怀孕期间将胎儿带在子宫中，从受精到出生的阶段

Examples

  • She gestated her first child for nine months.
  • The animal gestates its young for a long period.
  • Humans have a gestation period of about nine months.
  • The research project is still in its early stages, gestating new ideas.
  • Plants gestate their seeds until conditions are right for germination.
