Gnosis

Pronunciation:/ˈnoʊ.sɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 知识
  • 领悟
  • 智慧

Definitions

noun

Definition

Knowledge of spiritual mysteries, particularly in Gnosticism.

In ZH

关于精神奥秘的知识，尤其是在诺斯替主义中。

Examples

  • Gnosis is often sought through personal spiritual experiences.
  • Many Gnostic texts emphasize the importance of gnosis for salvation.
  • The pursuit of gnosis can lead to profound personal transformations.
  • In Gnosticism, gnosis is considered a way to connect with the divine.
  • She felt that her journey into meditation was leading her towards gnosis.
