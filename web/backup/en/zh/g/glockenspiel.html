Glockenspiel

EN→ZH

What Does Glockenspiel Mean?

  • 钟琴
  • 格洛肯斯皮尔

Glockenspiel Pronunciation

/ˈɡlɒkənʃpiːl/

Glockenspiel Definition

noun

Definition

A musical instrument consisting of a set of tuned metal bars played with mallets.

In ZH

一种由一组调音金属条组成的乐器，用锤子演奏。

How to Use Glockenspiel in a Sentence

  • The glockenspiel is often used in orchestras for its bright, high-pitched sound.
  • Children in music class learned to play the glockenspiel together.
  • During the parade, the band featured a glockenspiel in their performance.
  • She played a beautiful melody on the glockenspiel for the school concert.
  • The music teacher introduced the students to the glockenspiel as an easy instrument to start with.

Cultural Notes

The glockenspiel is commonly used in classical music and is also popular in school music programs.
