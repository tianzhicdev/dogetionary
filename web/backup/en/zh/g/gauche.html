Gauche

EN→ZH

What Does Gauche Mean?

  • 笨拙的
  • 左派的

Gauche Pronunciation

/ɡoʊʃ/

Gauche Definition

adjective

Definition

Lacking social grace or ease; awkward; clumsy.

In ZH

缺乏社交优雅或轻松感；笨拙；笨重。

How to Use Gauche in a Sentence

  • His jokes were so gauche that no one laughed.
  • She felt gauche in her new dress at the fancy party.
  • They made a gauche attempt to impress their guests.
  • His gauche manners made everyone uncomfortable.
  • Avoiding eye contact can seem somewhat gauche in conversations.

Cultural Notes

The term 'gauche' comes from French, where it originally means 'left,' but in English it has taken on a negative connotation regarding awkwardness in social situations.
