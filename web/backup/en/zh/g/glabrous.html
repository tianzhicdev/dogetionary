Glabrous

Pronunciation:/ˈɡleɪbrəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 光滑的
  • 无毛的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Smooth and hairless; specifically refers to skin or other surfaces that are free from hair or fuzz.

In ZH

光滑无毛的；特别指没有毛发或绒毛的皮肤或其他表面。

Examples

  • The glabrous skin of the frog helps it blend into its surroundings.
  • Some plants have glabrous leaves that are easier to clean.
  • After the procedure, her glabrous skin felt smooth and fresh.
  • The fruit has a glabrous surface that is shiny and appealing.
  • He was surprised to find the glabrous areas of the tree were free from moss.
