Grandiose

Pronunciation:/ˈɡræn.di.oʊs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 宏伟的
  • 壮丽的
  • 浮夸的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Impressive or magnificent in appearance or style, especially pretentiously so.

In ZH

外观或风格令人印象深刻或壮丽，尤其是夸张的。

Examples

  • The grandiose architecture of the palace left everyone in awe.
  • She planned a grandiose celebration for her daughter's graduation.
  • His grandiose ideas often exceeded what was realistically possible.
  • The film was criticized for its grandiose visual effects.
  • They made grandiose promises during their campaign, but delivered little.
