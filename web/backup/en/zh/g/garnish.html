Garnish

EN→ZH

What Does Garnish Mean?

  • 装饰
  • 配料
  • 点缀

Garnish Pronunciation

/ˈɡɑːrnɪʃ/

Garnish Definition

noun

Definition

An item or substance used as a decoration or embellishment for food or drink.

In ZH

用于食品或饮料装饰或点缀的项目或物质。

How to Use Garnish in a Sentence

  • The chef added a sprig of parsley as a garnish for the dish.
  • A lemon wedge served as the perfect garnish for the iced tea.
  • The cocktail was topped with a colorful garnish of fruit.
  • She used edible flowers as a garnish for the dessert.
  • The restaurant is known for its creative garnishes that enhance the presentation of each meal.
verb

Definition

To decorate or embellish (food or drink) with a small item or substance.

In ZH

用小物件或物质装饰或点缀（食物或饮料）。

How to Use Garnish in a Sentence

  • You should garnish the steak with some fresh herbs before serving.
  • He garnished the soup with a drizzle of cream.
  • After plating, it is important to garnish the dish for visual appeal.
  • She garnished the cupcakes with colorful sprinkles.
  • The drink was garnished with a cherry and a slice of orange.

Synonyms for Garnish

  • Garamond
  • Gargantuan
  • Gargoyle
  • Garish
  • Garnet
