Garrulity

EN→ZH

What Does Garrulity Mean?

  • 喋喋不休
  • 多话

Garrulity Pronunciation

/ɡəˈruː.lɪ.ti/

Garrulity Definition

noun

Definition

excessive talkativeness, especially on trivial matters

In ZH

过度的谈话，尤其是关于琐碎的事情

How to Use Garrulity in a Sentence

  • Her garrulity made long meetings feel even longer.
  • The garrulity of the host kept the party lively and engaging.
  • I appreciate his insight, but his garrulity can be tiring at times.
  • The garrulity of the children was a source of amusement for the parents.
  • In moments of garrulity, she often forgot the point of the conversation.
