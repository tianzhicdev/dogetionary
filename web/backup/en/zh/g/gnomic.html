Gnomic

Pronunciation:/ˈɡnəʊ.mɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 格言的
  • 警句的
  • 名言的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

expressing a general truth or principle in a concise and clever way

In ZH

以简洁巧妙的方式表达一般真理或原则

Examples

  • The poet's gnomic verses often left readers pondering their deeper meanings.
  • Many cultures have gnomic sayings that capture wisdom in just a few words.
  • Her advice was gnomic, leaving me to reflect on its implications for days.
  • The philosopher's gnomic statements were profound yet cryptic.
  • In his writings, he often used a gnomic style to convey complex ideas simply.
