Gloze

EN→ZH

What Does Gloze Mean?

  • 解释
  • 粉饰
  • 美化

Gloze Pronunciation

/ɡloʊz/

Gloze Definition

verb

Definition

To explain or interpret (something) in a way that tries to make it seem better than it is.

In ZH

以尽量美好的方式解释或解读（某事物）。

How to Use Gloze in a Sentence

  • The politician tried to gloze over the scandal during the debate.
  • She attempted to gloze the mistake as a minor error.
  • They glozed their previous failures by emphasizing their future plans.
  • His performance was glozed by the media as a great success.
  • Sometimes people gloze their true feelings in order to fit in.

Synonyms for Gloze

  • Gloaming
  • Globular
  • Glockenspiel
  • Glossary
  • Glossolalia
