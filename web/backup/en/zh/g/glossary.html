Glossary

Pronunciation:/ˈɡlɒsəri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 词汇表
  • 术语表

Definitions

noun

Definition

A list of terms in a particular domain of knowledge with their definitions.

In ZH

特定知识领域中术语及其定义的列表。

Examples

  • The glossary at the end of the book helps readers understand the technical terms.
  • She referred to the glossary to find the meaning of the complex word.
  • The teacher provided a glossary of vocabulary for the students.
  • In the presentation, he included a glossary for the audience's reference.
  • The glossary includes acronyms that may be unfamiliar to first-time readers.
