Garnet

EN→ZH

What Does Garnet Mean?

  • 石榴石
  • 红宝石

Garnet Pronunciation

/ˈɡɑːrnɪt/

Garnet Definition

noun

Definition

A deep red, translucent gemstone often used in jewelry.

In ZH

一种深红色、半透明的宝石，常用于珠宝制作。

How to Use Garnet in a Sentence

  • She wore a beautiful garnet pendant around her neck.
  • The garnet is the birthstone for January.
  • He gave her a ring set with a large garnet.
  • Garnet is known for its ability to invigorate and energize.
  • Ancient warriors believed garnets to protect them in battle.
