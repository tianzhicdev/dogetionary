Glibness

EN→ZH

What Does Glibness Mean?

  • 圆滑
  • 口齿伶俐
  • 善辩

Glibness Pronunciation

/ˈɡlɪb.nəs/

Glibness Definition

noun

Definition

the quality of being fluent and easy in a way that suggests insincerity or lack of thoughtfulness

In ZH

流利而轻松的特质，暗示不真诚或缺乏深思熟虑

How to Use Glibness in a Sentence

  • His glibness made it hard to trust his words.
  • She spoke with glibness, but her ideas lacked substance.
  • The politician's glibness was evident in his speech.
  • People often confuse glibness with intelligence.
  • Despite his glibness, the audience remained skeptical.

Synonyms for Glibness

  • Glib
  • Glisten
  • Glitch
