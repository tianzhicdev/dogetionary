Garish

EN→ZH

What Does Garish Mean?

  • 华丽的
  • 过于艳丽的
  • 俗丽的

Garish Pronunciation

/ˈɡɛrɪʃ/

Garish Definition

adjective

Definition

Obtrusively bright and showy; lurid.

In ZH

过于鲜艳和华丽；刺眼的。

How to Use Garish in a Sentence

  • The garish colors of the advertisement caught everyone's attention.
  • She wore a garish dress that clashed with everything around her.
  • The restaurant's garish decor made it seem cheap instead of inviting.
  • His garish tie was the talk of the office.
  • They decorated the party with garish balloons and streamers.
