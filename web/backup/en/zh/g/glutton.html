Glutton

EN→ZH

What Does Glutton Mean?

  • 贪吃者
  • 暴食者

Glutton Pronunciation

/ˈɡlʌtən/

Glutton Definition

noun

Definition

A person who is excessively greedy or indulges in something to excess, especially food.

In ZH

一个过分贪婪或沉溺于某事的人，尤其是食物。

How to Use Glutton in a Sentence

  • He was such a glutton that he could eat an entire pizza by himself.
  • During the holidays, she turns into a glutton, unable to resist the endless buffet.
  • Being a glutton for punishment, he kept volunteering for the hardest tasks.
  • The festival was a paradise for gluttons, featuring food from all over the world.
  • They called him a glutton for his ability to eat five burgers in one sitting.

Synonyms for Glutton
