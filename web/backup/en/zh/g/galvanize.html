Galvanize

Pronunciation:/ˈɡælvənaɪz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 刺激
  • 激励
  • 电镀

Definitions

verb

Definition

to stimulate or excite someone into taking action

In ZH

刺激或激励某人采取行动

Examples

  • The coach's speech galvanized the team into action.
  • Social media can galvanize public opinion quickly.
  • Her passion for the cause galvanized many to join.
  • The crisis galvanized the community to volunteer.
  • They hope the initiative will galvanize support for the environment.
