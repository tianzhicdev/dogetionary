Grade

Pronunciation:/ɡreɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 等级
  • 年级
  • 成绩

Definitions

noun

Definition

A rating or assessment of someone's performance or quality, often represented by a letter or number.

In ZH

对某人表现或质量的评级或评估，通常用字母或数字表示。

Examples

  • She received a high grade on her exam.
  • The grade of the building was higher than expected.
  • His final grade in the course was a B+.
  • Grades are released at the end of each semester.
  • They used a grading system to evaluate the projects.
verb

Definition

To assign a score or rating to something, often in an educational context.

In ZH

给某物分配分数或等级，通常在教育环境中。

Examples

  • The teacher will grade the assignments this weekend.
  • She grades papers quickly and efficiently.
  • Students often worry about how their essays will be graded.
  • He decided to grade on a curve this semester.
  • It's important to grade fairly and consistently.
