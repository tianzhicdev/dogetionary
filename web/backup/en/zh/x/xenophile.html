Xenophile

EN→ZH

What Does Xenophile Mean?

  • 好客者
  • 喜欢外国文化的人

Xenophile Pronunciation

/ˈzɛnəˌfaɪl/

Xenophile Definition

noun

Definition

A person who is attracted to or has a strong preference for foreign peoples, cultures, or customs.

In ZH

一个对外国人、文化或习俗有吸引力或强烈偏好的人。

How to Use Xenophile in a Sentence

  • As a true xenophile, she enjoys traveling to different countries.
  • His xenophile nature makes him eager to learn new languages.
  • Many xenophiles actively participate in cultural exchange programs.
  • Being a xenophile, he often invites international students to his home.
  • The xenophile embraced various traditions from around the world.

Synonyms for Xenophile

  • Xenial
  • Xenogenesis
  • Xenogenetic
  • Xenogenic
  • Xenogeny
