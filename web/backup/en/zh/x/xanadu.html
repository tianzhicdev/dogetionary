Xanadu

EN→ZH

What Does Xanadu Mean?

  • 仙境
  • 乌托邦
  • 理想之地

Xanadu Pronunciation

/ˈzænəˌduː/

Xanadu Definition

noun

Definition

A place of great beauty, luxury, and contentment; an idyllic or paradise-like location.

In ZH

一个极其美丽、奢华和令人满足的地方；一个理想的或像天堂一样的地方。

How to Use Xanadu in a Sentence

  • They envisioned their vacation as a trip to Xanadu, filled with relaxation and enjoyment.
  • The artist's studio was a Xanadu of creativity and inspiration.
  • In her dreams, she often visited a Xanadu where all her worries disappeared.
  • The film depicted a modern Xanadu, where technology and nature coexist.
  • He described his childhood home as a Xanadu of joy and laughter.

Cultural Notes

The term 'xanadu' originates from the poem 'Kubla Khan' by Samuel Taylor Coleridge, which depicts an opulent palace.

Synonyms for Xanadu
