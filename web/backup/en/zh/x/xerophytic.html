Xerophytic

Pronunciation:/ˌzɪəroʊˈfɪtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 旱生的
  • 抗旱植物的
  • 干旱植物的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to or denoting plants that thrive in dry conditions.

In ZH

与在干燥条件下生长的植物有关或表示。

Examples

  • Cacti are excellent examples of xerophytic plants.
  • Xerophytic adaptations allow these plants to conserve water.
  • The desert is home to many xerophytic species.
  • Xerophytic vegetation is crucial for surviving arid climates.
  • Studying xerophytic plants can help in developing drought-resistant crops.

Related Words

  • Xerophilous
  • Xerophyte
