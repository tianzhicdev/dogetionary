Xylophonist

Pronunciation:/zaɪˈlɒfəˌnɪst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 木琴演奏家
  • 木琴手

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who plays the xylophone, a musical instrument made of wooden bars that produce sound when struck.

In ZH

演奏木琴的人，一种用木条制成的乐器，敲击时发出声音。

Examples

  • The xylophonist performed a beautiful solo during the concert.
  • She trained for years to become a professional xylophonist.
  • The xylophonist accompanied the orchestra with a vibrant melody.
  • Children in music class learned to play the xylophone from a skilled xylophonist.
  • His passion for music led him to become a renowned xylophonist.
