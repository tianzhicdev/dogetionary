Xenophobe

Pronunciation:/ˈzɛnəˌfoʊb/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 仇外者
  • 排外者
  • 外来恐惧症患者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who has a dislike of or prejudice against people from other countries.

In ZH

一个对来自其他国家的人有厌恶或偏见的人。

Examples

  • He is often labeled as a xenophobe due to his strong anti-immigration views.
  • The xenophobe's comments at the meeting sparked a heated debate.
  • As a xenophobe, she struggled to accept cultural differences.
  • Many believe that xenophobes are often misinformed about other cultures.
  • His xenophobic attitudes affected his ability to make friends in a multicultural city.
