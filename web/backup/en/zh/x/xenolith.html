Xenolith

Pronunciation:/ˈzɛnəˌlɪθ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 外来岩石

Definitions

noun

Definition

A fragment of rock that is different from the surrounding rock in which it is embedded, typically found within igneous rocks.

In ZH

一种与周围岩石不同的岩石碎片，通常发现于火成岩中。

Examples

  • The xenolith was a surprising find in the volcanic rock.
  • Scientists studied the xenolith to learn more about the Earth's crust.
  • During the hike, we discovered a fascinating xenolith embedded in the granite.
  • Geologists often use xenoliths to understand the processes that occurred beneath the Earth's surface.
  • The presence of the xenolith suggested that the magma had mixed with the surrounding rock.
