Yonder

Pronunciation:/ˈjɒn.dər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 那边
  • 远处
  • 某处

Definitions

adverb

Definition

At a distance in the direction indicated; over there.

In ZH

在所示方向的远处；在那里。

Examples

  • The mountains yonder are beautiful.
  • Look at the house yonder, it's so old.
  • Yonder lies the valley where we used to play.
  • She pointed to yonder tree that we climbed as kids.
  • Do you see the stars yonder in the sky?
adjective

Definition

Being at a distance; that or those over there.

In ZH

距离较远的；那或那些在那边。

Examples

  • Yonder hills are where the sunset is most beautiful.
  • His voice came from yonder place, echoing through the valley.
  • Yonder people seem to be having a picnic.
  • The treasure is hidden in yonder cave.
  • Yonder star shines brighter than the others tonight.

  • Yonderly
