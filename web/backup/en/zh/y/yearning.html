Yearning

EN→ZH

What Does Yearning Mean?

  • 渴望
  • 向往
  • 心愿

Yearning Pronunciation

/ˈjɪr.nɪŋ/

Yearning Definition

noun

Definition

a deep longing or desire for something

In ZH

对某物的强烈渴望或愿望

How to Use Yearning in a Sentence

  • She felt a yearning for her hometown.
  • There was a yearning in his voice as he spoke about the past.
  • Her yearning for adventure led her to travel the world.
  • The poem expresses a deep yearning for love and connection.
  • He couldn't shake the feeling of yearning for something greater.

Synonyms for Yearning

  • Yearnful
