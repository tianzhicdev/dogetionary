Yeoman

Pronunciation:/ˈjoʊ.mən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 佃农
  • 自由民
  • 忠诚的助手

Definitions

noun

Definition

a man who cultivates his own land; a freeholder.

In ZH

耕种自己土地的男子；自由持有土地的人。

Examples

  • The yeoman played a crucial role in the feudal system of medieval England.
  • As a yeoman, he took pride in his independent farming.
  • The yeoman was known for his loyalty to the king.
  • In the story, the yeoman assists the knight on his quest.
  • Historically, a yeoman would often serve in the army as a skilled archer.
noun

Definition

a servant in a royal or noble household, especially one who performs menial tasks.

In ZH

在王室或贵族家中工作的仆人，尤其是那些从事琐碎工作的仆人。

Examples

  • The yeoman prepared the table for the banquet.
  • She worked as a yeoman in the lord's castle.
  • The yeoman was responsible for maintaining the estate.
  • In literature, the yeoman often represents the common man.
  • The yeoman's duties included running errands for the noble family.
