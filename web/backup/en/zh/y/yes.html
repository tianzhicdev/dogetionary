Yes

Pronunciation:/jɛs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 是
  • 对
  • 好的

Definitions

interjection

Definition

Used to express affirmation, agreement, or consent.

In ZH

用来表示肯定、同意或许可。

Examples

  • Yes, I would like some coffee.
  • Do you want to join us? Yes, I would love to!
  • Is this the right way? Yes, it is.
  • Yes, I agree with your opinion.
  • Can we leave early? Yes, let's go!
