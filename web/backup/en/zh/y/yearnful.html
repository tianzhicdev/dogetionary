Yearnful

What Does Yearnful Mean?

  • 渴望的
  • 怀念的
  • 充满渴望的

Yearnful Pronunciation

/ˈjɜrnfəl/

Yearnful Definition

adjective

Definition

having or showing a feeling of yearning; longing

In ZH

充满渴望的；怀念的

How to Use Yearnful in a Sentence

  • She had a yearnful expression as she reminisced about her childhood.
  • His poetry was often yearnful, reflecting deep desires.
  • The yearnful gaze of the artist captured the essence of beauty.
  • They spoke in a yearnful tone about their distant homeland.
  • The song's yearnful lyrics resonated with many listeners.
