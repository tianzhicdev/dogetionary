Yoga

Pronunciation:ˈjoʊɡə
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 瑜伽

Definitions

noun

Definition

A physical, mental, and spiritual practice that originated in ancient India, combining physical postures, breathing exercises, and meditation.

In ZH

一种源于古印度的身心灵练习，结合了体位、呼吸练习和冥想。

Examples

  • Many people practice yoga to improve their flexibility and strength.
  • She takes a yoga class every Saturday morning.
  • Yoga not only helps with physical fitness but also enhances mental clarity.

Cultural Notes

Yoga is widely practiced around the world today, often for its health benefits and stress-relief properties.

verb

Definition

To engage in the practice of yoga.

In ZH

Examples

  • I plan to yoga at the beach this weekend.
  • He wants to yoga regularly to maintain his health.
  • They yoga together every evening to unwind after work.

Cultural Notes

In casual contexts, 'to yoga' can refer to participating in a yoga class or session, commonly used among practitioners.
