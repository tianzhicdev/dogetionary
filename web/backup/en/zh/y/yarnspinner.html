Yarnspinner

Pronunciation:/ˈjɑrnˌspɪnər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 讲述者
  • 故事讲述者
  • 编故事的人

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who tells stories, especially in an entertaining or exaggerated manner.

In ZH

一个讲故事的人，特别是以一种娱乐性或夸张的方式讲故事的人。

Examples

  • The old man is a true yarnspinner, captivating everyone with his tales.
  • At the campfire, he became a yarnspinner, weaving stories of adventure.
  • She’s known as a yarnspinner in her circle of friends, always with a new tale.
  • The yarnspinner entertained the children with his whimsical stories.
  • Every family gathering features at least one yarnspinner sharing amusing anecdotes.
