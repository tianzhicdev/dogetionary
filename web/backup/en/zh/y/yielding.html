Yielding

Pronunciation:/ˈjiːldɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 屈服的
  • 易弯的
  • 可产出的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

giving way to pressure; easily influenced or shaped

In ZH

屈服于压力；容易受到影响或塑造的

Examples

  • The yielding nature of the material makes it perfect for this application.
  • In negotiations, a yielding attitude can lead to better outcomes.
  • Her yielding personality makes her well-liked among her peers.
  • The tree was yielding as the wind blew, bending without breaking.
  • The yielding of the floorboards suggested that they were old and in need of repair.
verb

Definition

to produce or provide; to give way under pressure

In ZH

生产或提供；在压力下让步

Examples

  • The farm yields a variety of crops each season.
  • He is yielding to the demands of his boss to get the promotion.
  • The research may yield insights into customer behavior.
  • The heat caused the metal to yield and deform.
  • After much consideration, she decided to yield her position.
