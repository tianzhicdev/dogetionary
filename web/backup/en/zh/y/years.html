Years

Pronunciation:/jɪrz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 年

Definitions

noun

Definition

A period of twelve months, corresponding to the time taken by the Earth to make one revolution around the sun.

In ZH

一段十二个月的时间，对应地球绕太阳转一圈所需的时间。

Examples

  • She has lived in this city for five years.
  • In a few years, they hope to travel the world.
  • His birthday is celebrated every year in January.
  • This year has been particularly challenging for everyone.
  • They plan to start a family in the next couple of years.
