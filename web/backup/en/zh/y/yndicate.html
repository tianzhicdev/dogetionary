Yndicate

Pronunciation:/ˈsɪndɪkeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 辛迪加
  • 联合
  • 集团

Definitions

noun

Definition

A group of individuals or organizations combined to promote a common interest.

In ZH

一个为了促进共同利益而结合在一起的个人或组织的团体。

Examples

  • The film was produced by a syndicate of investors.
  • A syndicate of companies worked together to build the new technology park.
  • The crime syndicate operated in several states.
  • She joined a syndicate to purchase the lottery tickets together.
  • A writing syndicate can help authors gain better visibility.
verb

Definition

To form or join a syndicate.

In ZH

组成或加入一个辛迪加。

Examples

  • Many entrepreneurs syndicate their projects to attract investors.
  • The companies decided to syndicate their resources to maximize efficiency.
  • He plans to syndicate his new article to various newspapers.
  • They syndicate their content across multiple platforms.
  • Investors syndicate their funds to support large projects.
