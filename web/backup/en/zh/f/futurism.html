Futurism

EN→ZH

What Does Futurism Mean?

  • 未来主义
  • 未来派

Futurism Pronunciation

/ˈfjuːtʃərɪzəm/

Futurism Definition

noun

Definition

A movement in art, literature, and society that emphasizes and promotes the dynamic energy and values of modern technology, speed, and youth.

In ZH

一种在艺术、文学和社会中强调并促进现代技术、速度和青春的动能和价值观的运动。

How to Use Futurism in a Sentence

  • The futurism movement celebrated technological innovation.
  • Futurism influenced various art forms and literature in the early 20th century.
  • Many futurist artists depicted speed and modernity in their works.
  • Futurism rejected the past and embraced the new.
  • The influence of futurism can still be seen in contemporary design.

Synonyms for Futurism

  • Futile
  • Futilitarian
  • Futility
