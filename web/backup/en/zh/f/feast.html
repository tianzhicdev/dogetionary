Feast

Pronunciation:fiːst
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 盛宴
  • 宴会
  • 大餐

Definitions

noun

Definition

A large meal, typically one in celebration of something.

In ZH

一种大型餐餐，通常是为了庆祝某事而举行的。

Examples

  • We had a grand feast for the wedding.
  • The annual feast attracted hundreds of guests.
  • During the festival, there was a feast with various traditional dishes.

Cultural Notes

Feasts are often associated with celebrations, holidays, and significant life events such as weddings and religious ceremonies.

verb

Definition

To eat a large amount of food; to enjoy a feast.

In ZH

大量进食；享用盛宴。

Examples

  • On Thanksgiving, we feast on turkey and pie.
  • They feasted on delicious dishes from around the world.
  • After the harvest, the villagers feasted together under the stars.

Cultural Notes

The act of feasting can also imply a sense of community and sharing, often seen in traditional celebrations.
