Furtive

Pronunciation:/ˈfɜːrtɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 偷偷摸摸的
  • 暗中的
  • 隐秘的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

attempting to avoid notice or attention, typically because of guilt or a belief that discovery would lead to trouble; secretive.

In ZH

试图避免被注意或关注，通常因为内心有愧或认为被发现会带来麻烦；秘密的。

Examples

  • He cast a furtive glance at the clock.
  • She made a furtive attempt to escape the meeting.
  • They exchanged furtive whispers in the corner of the room.
  • His furtive behavior made everyone suspicious.
  • The furtive look in her eyes gave away her true intentions.
