Flummoxed

EN→ZH

What Does Flummoxed Mean?

  • 困惑
  • 迷惑
  • 使困扰

Flummoxed Pronunciation

/ˈflʌməksd/

Flummoxed Definition

adjective

Definition

bewildered or perplexed; unable to understand or respond due to confusion.

In ZH

感到困惑或困扰；由于困惑而无法理解或回应。

How to Use Flummoxed in a Sentence

  • She was flummoxed by the complex puzzle.
  • The unexpected question left him flummoxed.
  • He looked flummoxed when he heard the news.
  • The intricate instructions flummoxed the entire team.
  • I was flummoxed by the sudden change in plans.
