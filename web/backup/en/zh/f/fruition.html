Fruition

Pronunciation:/fruːˈɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 成果
  • 实现
  • 完成
  • 结果

Definitions

noun

Definition

the realization or fulfillment of a plan or project

In ZH

计划或项目的实现或完成

Examples

  • Her efforts finally came to fruition with the successful launch of the product.
  • After years of hard work, he saw the fruition of his dreams.
  • The project reached fruition after a long and challenging process.
  • The initiatives taken by the community led to a fruitful fruition of their goals.
  • In the end, their collaboration resulted in the fruition of innovative ideas.
