Frangible

Pronunciation:/ˈfræn.dʒə.bəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 易碎的
  • 脆弱的
  • 可碎的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

capable of being broken or shattered; fragile.

In ZH

能够被打破或破碎的；脆弱的。

Examples

  • The frangible glassware must be handled with care.
  • He carefully packed the frangible items to prevent breakage.
  • The frangible nature of the old manuscript required special preservation techniques.
  • Frangible ammunition is designed to disintegrate upon impact.
  • In art, frangible materials can create delicate and beautiful works.
