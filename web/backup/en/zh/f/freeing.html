Freeing

Pronunciation:ˈfriːɪŋ
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 解放
  • 释放
  • 自由化

Definitions

verb

Definition

The gerund form of 'free', meaning to make or become free; to release from captivity or bondage.

In ZH

动词'free'的动名词形式，意味着使或变得自由；从监禁或束缚中释放出来。

Examples

  • The act of freeing the caged birds brought joy to the children.
  • She found freeing herself from negative thoughts was liberating.
  • They are working on freeing the hostages soon.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, the concept of 'freeing' is closely associated with liberation movements and human rights. It can refer to both physical freedom and mental or emotional liberation.

adjective

Definition

Describing something that provides freedom or is related to the concept of freedom.

In ZH

形容词，描述提供自由或与自由概念相关的事物。

Examples

  • Her freeing spirit led her to travel the world.
  • The freeing atmosphere at the festival encouraged creativity.
  • He felt a freeing sensation when he quit his corporate job.

Cultural Notes

The adjective form highlights not just physical freedom but also emotional and spiritual liberation, which are often celebrated in art and literature.
