Faun

EN→ZH

What Does Faun Mean?

  • 小山神
  • 半人半羊的神

Faun Pronunciation

/fɔːn/

Faun Definition

noun

Definition

A mythical creature that is half human and half goat, often associated with rustic music and nature.

In ZH

一种神话中的生物，半人半羊，通常与田园音乐和自然相联系。

How to Use Faun in a Sentence

  • The faun played his flute under the trees.
  • In mythology, the faun is a symbol of nature and fertility.
  • The story features a faun guiding the main character through the forest.
  • The faun's laughter echoed in the glade.
  • Many artists depict fauns in their works to represent the wild spirit of nature.
