Frabjous

EN→ZH

What Does Frabjous Mean?

  • 极好的
  • 幸運的
  • 精彩的

Frabjous Pronunciation

/ˈfræb.dʒəs/

Frabjous Definition

adjective

Definition

wonderful; delightful; superb

In ZH

极好的；令人愉快的；卓越的

How to Use Frabjous in a Sentence

  • The frabjous day filled everyone with joy.
  • She had a frabjous time at the festival.
  • It was a frabjous evening under the stars.
  • Finding that rare book was a frabjous moment for the collector.
  • His frabjous personality made him very popular.

Cultural Notes

The word 'frabjous' was popularized by Lewis Carroll in his poem 'Jabberwocky' from 'Through the Looking-Glass', where it conveys a sense of joy and wonder.
