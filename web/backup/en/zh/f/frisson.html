Frisson

Pronunciation:/frɪˈsɒn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 颤栗
  • 震颤
  • 激动

Definitions

noun

Definition

A sudden strong feeling of excitement or fear; a thrill.

In ZH

突然强烈的兴奋或恐惧感；一种刺激感。

Examples

  • The frisson of anticipation filled the air before the concert started.
  • She felt a frisson of fear as she stepped into the dark room.
  • The novel was filled with frisson, keeping the readers on the edge of their seats.
  • He experienced a frisson of excitement when he heard the news.
  • The frisson between them was palpable as they locked eyes.

Cultural Notes

The term 'frisson' is often used in contexts involving art and music, highlighting the emotional response evoked by beauty or drama.
