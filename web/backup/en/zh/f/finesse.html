Finesse

Pronunciation:/fɪˈnɛs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 技巧
  • 灵巧
  • 精细

Definitions

noun

Definition

Intricate and refined delicacy; subtlety in handling a situation or in achieving a goal.

In ZH

精巧和细致的优雅；在处理情况或实现目标时的微妙性。

Examples

  • She handled the negotiation with great finesse.
  • His finesse in playing the piano captivated the audience.
  • To succeed in this field, you need a finesse that's hard to teach.
  • They approached the delicate issue with finesse and care.
  • The artist painted the landscape with finesse, capturing every detail.
verb

Definition

To handle or deal with something in a refined or delicate way.

In ZH

以精致或微妙的方式处理或对待某事。

Examples

  • He had to finesse the situation to avoid conflict.
  • She finessed her way through the tough questions during the interview.
  • They finessed the event to ensure everything went smoothly.
  • You need to finesse your argument to make it more persuasive.
  • He finessed the project to meet the tight deadline.
