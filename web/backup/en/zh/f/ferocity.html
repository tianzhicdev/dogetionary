Ferocity

Pronunciation:/fəˈrɒs.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 凶猛
  • 激烈
  • 猛烈

Definitions

noun

Definition

The state or quality of being ferocious; savagery or fierceness.

In ZH

凶猛的状态或特质；野蛮或凶狠。

Examples

  • The ferocity of the storm surprised everyone.
  • The lion is known for its ferocity when hunting.
  • Her ferocity in debate left her opponents speechless.
  • The ferocity of the dog's bark scared the intruder away.
  • He approached the challenge with a ferocity that was unmatched.
