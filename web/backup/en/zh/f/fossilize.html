Fossilize

Pronunciation:/ˈfɒs.ɪ.laɪz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 化石化
  • 使成化石
  • 固定

Definitions

verb

Definition

to convert (organic matter) into a fossil; to preserve in a form resembling a fossil

In ZH

将（有机物）转化为化石；以类似化石的形式进行保存

Examples

  • The scientist was excited to fossilize the ancient plant species.
  • They discovered a way to fossilize the remains of extinct animals.
  • In the museum, we learned how to fossilize samples of wood.
  • The process can take millions of years to fully fossilize a creature.
  • Many materials can fossilize under the right conditions in nature.
