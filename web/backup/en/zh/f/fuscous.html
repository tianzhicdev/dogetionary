Fuscous

EN→ZH

What Does Fuscous Mean?

  • 暗色的
  • 灰褐色的
  • 暗褐色的

Fuscous Pronunciation

/ˈfʌskəs/

Fuscous Definition

adjective

Definition

of a dark or dusky color; specifically, grayish-brown or dark brown.

In ZH

暗色的或阴暗的颜色；特指灰褐色或深褐色。

How to Use Fuscous in a Sentence

  • The fuscous feathers of the bird helped it blend into the forest.
  • He painted the walls a fuscous shade to create a cozy atmosphere.
  • The fuscous landscape was stark against the bright sky.
  • Fuscous hues are often used to depict the changing seasons in art.
  • The scientist noted the fuscous coloration in the specimen under the microscope.

Synonyms for Fuscous

  • Fustian
  • Fustigate
  • Fusty
