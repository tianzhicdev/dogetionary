Fugue

Pronunciation:/fjuːɡ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 赋格
  • fuga

Definitions

noun

Definition

A musical composition in which a theme is introduced by one voice and then imitated by others.

In ZH

一种音乐作品，其中一个声部引入主题，然后其他声部模仿它。

Examples

  • Bach is well-known for his fugues.
  • The fugue had multiple layers and complexity.
  • She played a beautiful fugue on the piano.
  • Composers often use a fugue to showcase their skills.
  • The fugue structure can be quite challenging for musicians.
noun

Definition

A state of loss of awareness of one’s identity, often associated with dissociative disorders.

In ZH

一种失去自我意识的状态，通常与解离性障碍相关。

Examples

  • After the traumatic event, he experienced a fugue.
  • The crime was committed during a fugue state.
  • Doctors diagnosed her with dissociative fugue.
  • He wandered for days without recalling his past.
  • Fugue states can lead to significant memory loss.
