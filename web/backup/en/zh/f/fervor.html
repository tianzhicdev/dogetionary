Fervor

EN→ZH

What Does Fervor Mean?

  • 热情
  • 激情
  • 热烈

Fervor Pronunciation

/ˈfɜːrvər/

Fervor Definition

noun

Definition

Intense and passionate feeling.

In ZH

强烈而热情的感觉。

How to Use Fervor in a Sentence

  • She spoke with great fervor about her beliefs.
  • His fervor for the project inspired everyone around him.
  • The crowd cheered with fervor during the concert.
  • They approached their work with a fervor that was contagious.
  • In times of crisis, fervor often drives people to take action.
