Fenestration

EN→ZH

What Does Fenestration Mean?

  • 窗户设计
  • 开窗
  • 窗户排列

Fenestration Pronunciation

/ˌfɛnɪˈstreɪʃən/

Fenestration Definition

noun

Definition

The arrangement, design, and placement of windows and other openings in a building.

In ZH

建筑物内窗户和其他开口的排列、设计和位置。

How to Use Fenestration in a Sentence

  • The fenestration of the building allows for plenty of natural light.
  • Architects pay special attention to fenestration to enhance energy efficiency.
  • The fenestration can greatly affect the aesthetic appeal of a structure.
  • In modern architecture, innovative fenestration designs are becoming more popular.
  • Effective fenestration can help reduce heating and cooling costs.
