Formula

Pronunciation:ˈfɔːr.mjʊ.lə
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 公式
  • 配方
  • 方程式

Definitions

noun

Definition

A mathematical or chemical rule expressed in symbols.

In ZH

以符号表示的数学或化学规则。

Examples

  • If only my diet plan had a formula to turn pizza into salad!
  • Scientists say the new formula for happiness includes 50% chocolate, but I think they forgot about pizza!
  • I tried to follow the formula for a perfect selfie, but all I got was a picture of my cat.

Cultural Notes

Formulas are widely used in science and mathematics, and knowing how to interpret a formula can be crucial for students.

noun

Definition

A conventional or accepted way of doing something; a method.

In ZH

一种常规或公认的方法；一种做事的方法。

Examples

  • The formula for success in 2023 seems to be: post a cute cat video and watch the likes roll in!
  • I have a secret formula for making friends—just say 'free snacks'!
  • If the formulas for love were only as simple as the ones for baking cookies...

Cultural Notes

In business and writing, using the right formula can often lead to success, but sometimes it can feel repetitive or uninspired.

noun

Definition

A recipe or method for achieving a desired result, often used in cooking or making products.

In ZH

实现所需结果的配方或方法，通常用于烹饪或制造产品。

Examples

  • My grandmother's formula for chocolate cake involves an obscure ingredient that sounds like a spell!
  • The secret formula for my new smoothie is simply a mix of fruits I found lurking in my fridge!
  • Trying to find a formula for the perfect sandwich is harder than rocket science, especially when all you have is peanut butter.

Cultural Notes

In cooking, a good formula can either create a culinary masterpiece or a kitchen disaster.
