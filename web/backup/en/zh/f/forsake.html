Forsake

EN→ZH

What Does Forsake Mean?

  • 抛弃
  • 放弃
  • 离弃

Forsake Pronunciation

/fɔːrˈseɪk/

Forsake Definition

verb

Definition

To abandon someone or something; to renounce or give up.

In ZH

抛弃某人或某事；放弃或拒绝。

How to Use Forsake in a Sentence

  • He decided to forsake his old life and start anew.
  • Many people forsake their beliefs in the face of adversity.
  • She felt she had to forsake her dreams for the sake of her family.
  • They promised never to forsake one another in times of trouble.
  • It is unwise to forsake your principles for temporary gain.

Synonyms for Forsake

  • Forbear
  • Forbearance
  • Foreboding
  • Foresight
  • Forlorn
