Flotsam

Pronunciation:/ˈflɒtsəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 浮木
  • 漂流物

Definitions

noun

Definition

wreckage or cargo that is floating on the water after a shipwreck

In ZH

在船只失事后漂浮在水面上的残骸或货物

Examples

  • After the storm, we found flotsam washed up on the shore.
  • The beach was littered with flotsam from the sunken vessel.
  • Marine life often gets entangled in flotsam and jetsam.
  • The rescue team searched the area for any flotsam from the wreckage.
  • Scientists study flotsam to understand ocean currents.
