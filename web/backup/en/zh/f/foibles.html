Foibles

EN→ZH

What Does Foibles Mean?

  • 缺点
  • 弱点
  • 小缺陷

Foibles Pronunciation

/ˈfɔɪ.bəlz/

Foibles Definition

noun

Definition

A minor weakness or eccentricity in someone's character.

In ZH

一个人在个性上的小缺点或怪癖。

How to Use Foibles in a Sentence

  • Everyone has their foibles, and it's what makes us unique.
  • His foibles often lead to amusing situations.
  • She embraces her foibles as part of her charm.
  • The group accepted each other's foibles without judgment.
  • Understanding each other's foibles can strengthen relationships.
