Fetid

EN→ZH

What Does Fetid Mean?

  • 恶臭的
  • 腐臭的
  • 臭的

Fetid Pronunciation

/ˈfɛtɪd/

Fetid Definition

adjective

Definition

having a foul odor; rotten or decayed

In ZH

有恶臭的；腐烂或腐败的

How to Use Fetid in a Sentence

  • The fetid smell of garbage filled the air.
  • He could hardly breathe due to the fetid odor in the room.
  • After the rain, the fetid waters of the swamp were exposed.
  • The fetid stench of the stagnant water was unbearable.
  • She wrinkled her nose at the fetid cheese.
