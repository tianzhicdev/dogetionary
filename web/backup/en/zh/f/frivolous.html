Frivolous

Pronunciation:/ˈfrɪv.ə.ləs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 轻浮的
  • 无聊的
  • 不重要的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

not having any serious purpose or value

In ZH

没有任何严肃的目的或价值

Examples

  • He made a frivolous comment during the meeting.
  • The lawsuit was dismissed as frivolous.
  • She spends her money on frivolous things.
  • His frivolous behavior annoyed his colleagues.
  • They had a frivolous discussion about reality TV shows.
