Fractals

Pronunciation:/ˈfræk.təlz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 分形
  • 分形几何
  • 分形图形

Definitions

noun

Definition

Geometric shapes that can be split into parts, each of which is a reduced-size copy of the whole, often described by recursive processes.

In ZH

可以分割成部分的几何形状，每个部分都是整体缩小版的拷贝，通常通过递归过程来描述。

Examples

  • Fractals can be found in nature, such as in the branching of trees.
  • The Mandelbrot set is one of the most famous examples of a fractal.
  • Artists often use fractals to create intricate designs and patterns.
  • Fractals are used in computer graphics to produce realistic landscapes.
  • Mathematics has shown that fractals can model complex structures in real life.
