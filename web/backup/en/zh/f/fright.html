Fright

Pronunciation:/fraɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 惊吓
  • 恐慌
  • 害怕

Definitions

noun

Definition

a feeling of fear or panic

In ZH

恐惧或恐慌的感觉

Examples

  • The sudden noise gave me a fright.
  • He was filled with fright when he saw the ghost.
  • She couldn't hide her fright during the horror movie.
  • The fright of losing her job was overwhelming.
  • They experienced fright when they heard the thunder.
verb

Definition

to cause (someone) to feel fear

In ZH

使（某人）感到恐惧

Examples

  • The loud bang frightened the children.
  • She didn't mean to fright him with her sudden appearance.
  • The storm frightened the pets in the house.
  • The story is designed to fright young readers.
  • He had a way of frightening people with his jokes.
