Fallibility

Pronunciation:/ˌfælɪˈbɪlɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 错误性
  • 不可靠性
  • 易错性

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being prone to errors; the likelihood of making mistakes.

In ZH

容易出错的性质；犯错误的可能性。

Examples

  • Everyone has a degree of fallibility in their judgments.
  • The fallibility of human memory is well-documented.
  • He acknowledged his fallibility and sought feedback.
  • The fallibility of the system led to unexpected consequences.
  • Understanding fallibility can foster compassion in conflict.
