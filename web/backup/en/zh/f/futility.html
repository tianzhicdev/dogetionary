Futility

Pronunciation:/fjʊˈtɪləti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无用
  • 徒劳
  • 无效

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being useless or not achieving any result.

In ZH

无用或未能取得任何结果的特性。

Examples

  • The futility of the effort became apparent after several failed attempts.
  • He felt a deep sense of futility in trying to change the situation.
  • Their argument highlighted the futility of arguing with someone who is not listening.
  • She was frustrated by the futility of her daily routine.
  • The futility of war was evident in the countless lives lost.
