Felicity

EN→ZH

What Does Felicity Mean?

  • 幸福
  • 快乐
  • 愉快

Felicity Pronunciation

/fəˈlɪs.ɪ.ti/

Felicity Definition

noun

Definition

Intense happiness; the state of being extremely happy.

In ZH

极度的幸福；极快乐的状态。

How to Use Felicity in a Sentence

  • Her face was beaming with felicity at the news.
  • The couple shared a moment of pure felicity during their wedding.
  • He wrote about his feelings of felicity in his journal.
  • The children played in the park, their laughter a sound of unrestrained felicity.
  • Finding true love is often described as the ultimate felicity.

Synonyms for Felicity

  • Felicitate
  • Felicitous
  • Felicitude
