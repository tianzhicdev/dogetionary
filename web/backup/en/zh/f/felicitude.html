Felicitude

EN→ZH

What Does Felicitude Mean?

  • 幸福
  • 快乐
  • 愉快

Felicitude Pronunciation

/ˌfɛlɪˈsɪtjuːd/

Felicitude Definition

noun

Definition

the state of being happy or joyful; happiness.

In ZH

幸福或快乐的状态；快乐。

How to Use Felicitude in a Sentence

  • Her smile was a reflection of her felicitude.
  • Finding joy in small things is a key to felicitude.
  • He pursued a life filled with felicitude and purpose.
  • The book's theme revolves around the pursuit of felicitude.
  • Their friendship brought them both a sense of felicitude.
