Flabbergast

EN→ZH

What Does Flabbergast Mean?

  • 大吃一惊
  • 使大为震惊
  • 使大为惊讶

Flabbergast Pronunciation

/ˈflæbəɡɑːrɡæst/

Flabbergast Definition

verb

Definition

To shock or greatly surprise someone.

In ZH

使某人感到震惊或大为惊讶。

How to Use Flabbergast in a Sentence

  • I was flabbergasted by the news of her unexpected promotion.
  • The magician's trick flabbergasted everyone in the audience.
  • He was flabbergasted to learn he had won the lottery.
  • Her sudden departure flabbergasted all her friends.
  • The complexity of the puzzle flabbergasted me.

Synonyms for Flabbergast

  • Flabbergasted
  • Flabbergastedness
  • Flabbergasting
  • Flagitious
  • Flagrant
