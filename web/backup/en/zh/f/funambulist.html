Funambulist

EN→ZH

What Does Funambulist Mean?

  • 走钢丝的人
  • 绳索行者

Funambulist Pronunciation

/ˌfjuː.næm.bjʊˈlɪst/

Funambulist Definition

noun

Definition

A performer who balances and walks on a tightrope or a rope.

In ZH

在细绳或绳索上保持平衡并行走的表演者。

How to Use Funambulist in a Sentence

  • The funambulist amazed the crowd with her daring stunts.
  • He trained for years to become a skilled funambulist.
  • The circus featured a famous funambulist known for his incredible balance.
  • As a funambulist, she must maintain focus and precision while performing.
  • The funambulist walked across the high wire with ease and grace.
