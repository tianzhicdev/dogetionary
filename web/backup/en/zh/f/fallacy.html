Fallacy

Pronunciation:/ˈfæləsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 谬论
  • 错误
  • 谬误

Definitions

noun

Definition

A mistaken belief, especially one based on unsound argument.

In ZH

一种错误的信念，尤其是基于不合理的论证。

Examples

  • The idea that all swans are white is a fallacy.
  • He pointed out the fallacy in her reasoning.
  • Believing that money can buy happiness is a common fallacy.
  • The fallacy of the argument was exposed in the debate.
  • It's a fallacy to think that success guarantees happiness.
