Funk

EN→ZH

What Does Funk Mean?

  • 放克
  • 霉味
  • 沮丧
  • 坏情绪

Funk Pronunciation

/fʌŋk/

Funk Definition

noun

Definition

A state of depression or low spirits.

In ZH

一种抑郁或低落的状态。

How to Use Funk in a Sentence

  • He was in a funk after losing his job.
  • I need to find a way out of this funk.
  • She has been in a funk since the breakup.
  • Don't let a bad day put you into a funk.
  • He snapped out of his funk after a few days.
noun

Definition

A style of dance music characterized by strong bass lines and rhythms.

In ZH

一种以强烈的低音线和节奏为特征的舞曲风格。

How to Use Funk in a Sentence

  • Funk music originated in the 1960s and 70s.
  • They danced to the funk music all night long.
  • The band played a funky groove that made everyone move.
  • She loves to listen to funk when she's working out.
  • His guitar skills brought the funk to the performance.

Cultural Notes

Funk music is often associated with African American culture and has influenced a variety of other music genres.

verb

Definition

To be in a state of depression or to sulk.

In ZH

处于沮丧状态或怨怼。

How to Use Funk in a Sentence

  • Don't funk around when you should be serious.
  • He tends to funk when things don't go his way.
  • She is funked about the upcoming exam.
  • It's easy to funk after a setback.
  • They both funked at the thought of losing.

Synonyms for Funk

  • Funambulist
  • Fund
  • Fundamental
