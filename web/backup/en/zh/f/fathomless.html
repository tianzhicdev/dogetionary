Fathomless

Pronunciation:/ˈfæðəm.ləs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 深不可测的
  • 无底的
  • 不可理解的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Incapable of being fully explored or understood; immeasurable.

In ZH

无法完全探索或理解的；不可测量的。

Examples

  • The ocean appeared fathomless in its depth.
  • Her sorrow felt fathomless after the loss of her friend.
  • He had fathomless curiosity about the universe.
  • There are fathomless mysteries in the depths of the forest.
  • The artist created a fathomless sense of emotion in his painting.
