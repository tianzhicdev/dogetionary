Fiasco

Pronunciation:/fiˈæs.koʊ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 大失败
  • 惨败
  • 可悲的结局

Definitions

noun

Definition

A complete failure, especially in a humiliating way.

In ZH

彻底的失败，尤其是以一种羞辱的方式。

Examples

  • The event turned into a fiasco due to poor planning.
  • His attempt to impress her ended in a fiasco.
  • The company's launch was a fiasco that cost them millions.
  • What started as a fun party ended in a complete fiasco.
  • The negotiation was a fiasco from the start.

Cultural Notes

The term 'fiasco' originates from the Italian word meaning 'flask' or 'bottle', and was historically used in theatre to describe a failed performance.
