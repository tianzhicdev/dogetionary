Flimflam

Pronunciation:/ˈflɪmˌflæm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 欺骗
  • 骗局
  • 哄骗

Definitions

noun

Definition

A deception or trick designed to fool someone.

In ZH

一种旨在愚弄某人的欺骗或把戏。

Examples

  • The salesman used a flimflam to sell his questionable product.
  • She was a victim of a flimflam that cost her thousands.
  • His story was nothing more than a flimflam to get attention.
  • They pulled off a flimflam at the carnival to entertain the crowd.
  • Flimflams are often used by con artists to mislead people.
verb

Definition

To deceive or trick someone.

In ZH

欺骗或愚弄某人。

Examples

  • He tried to flimflam me into buying a useless warranty.
  • Don't flimflam your friends; they deserve honesty.
  • The magician flimflammed the audience with his clever tricks.
  • They flimflammed the investors, promising unrealistic returns.
  • Flimflamming is a common tactic in street performances.
