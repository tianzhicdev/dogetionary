Flocculent

EN→ZH

What Does Flocculent Mean?

  • 绒毛的
  • 絮状的
  • 松散的

Flocculent Pronunciation

/ˈflɒkjʊlənt/

Flocculent Definition

adjective

Definition

having a fluffy or woolly appearance; resembling flocculence.

In ZH

有毛茸茸或毛状的外观；类似于絮状物。

How to Use Flocculent in a Sentence

  • The flocculent clouds drifted lazily across the sky.
  • She noticed the flocculent texture of the newly harvested wheat.
  • The stew had a flocculent surface from the herbs floating on top.
  • He described the flocculent feathers of the chickadee.
  • The laboratory observed the flocculent growth of bacteria in the petri dish.

Cultural Notes

In biology, the term flocculent can refer to certain types of microbial colonies that have a fluffy appearance.
