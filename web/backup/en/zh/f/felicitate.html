Felicitate

EN→ZH

What Does Felicitate Mean?

  • 祝贺
  • 庆祝
  • 祝福

Felicitate Pronunciation

/fɪˈlɪsɪˌteɪt/

Felicitate Definition

verb

Definition

To congratulate or express joy to someone on their achievements or happy occasions.

In ZH

祝贺或向某人表示在其成就或快乐时刻的喜悦。

How to Use Felicitate in a Sentence

  • I would like to felicitate you on your recent promotion.
  • She was felicitated for her outstanding performance in the competition.
  • At the ceremony, they felicitated the winners with awards.
  • We should felicitate our friends on their wedding day.
  • It's customary to felicitate someone on their birthday.

Synonyms for Felicitate

  • Felicitous
  • Felicitude
  • Felicity
