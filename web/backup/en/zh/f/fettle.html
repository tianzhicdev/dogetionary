Fettle

EN→ZH

What Does Fettle Mean?

  • 状态
  • 情形
  • 健康

Fettle Pronunciation

/ˈfɛt.əl/

Fettle Definition

noun

Definition

A state of fitness or readiness.

In ZH

健康或准备的状态。

How to Use Fettle in a Sentence

  • He is in good fettle after his holiday.
  • The car is in fine fettle and ready for the road trip.
  • She was in a fettle mood at the party.
  • The team is in excellent fettle for the upcoming match.
  • Make sure to keep the equipment in proper fettle.
verb

Definition

To put into a specified state; to arrange or set in order.

In ZH

将某物置于特定状态；安排或整理。

How to Use Fettle in a Sentence

  • She fettled the garden, making it ready for planting.
  • The carpenter fettled the wood to ensure a perfect fit.
  • He fettled the model airplane before the race.
  • They fettled the documents for the meeting.
  • After a long day, he fettled his desk to clear away the clutter.

Synonyms for Fettle

  • Fetid
  • Fetter
  • Fettered
