Fluctuation

Pronunciation:/ˌflʌk.tʃuˈeɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 波动
  • 起伏
  • 浮动

Definitions

noun

Definition

an irregular rising and falling in number or amount; a variation.

In ZH

数量或金额的波动；变化的规律。

Examples

  • The stock market experienced significant fluctuations last year.
  • There can be fluctuations in temperature throughout the day.
  • Her mood showed frequent fluctuations, making it hard to anticipate her reactions.
  • The fluctuation in prices affected many consumers' purchasing decisions.
  • Economic fluctuations can lead to uncertainty in the market.
