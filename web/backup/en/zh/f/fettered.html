Fettered

Pronunciation:/ˈfɛtərd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 束缚的
  • 受限制的
  • 被限制的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

restricted or bound; unable to act freely.

In ZH

受到限制或束缚；无法自由行动。

Examples

  • She felt fettered by the constraints of her job.
  • His creativity was fettered by strict guidelines.
  • They lived in a fettered society where freedom of speech was not allowed.
  • The artist broke free from her fettered existence to pursue her passion.
  • Fettered by tradition, the organization struggled to innovate.
