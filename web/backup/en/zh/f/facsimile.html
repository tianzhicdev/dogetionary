Facsimile

EN→ZH

What Does Facsimile Mean?

  • 传真
  • 仿本
  • 复制品
  • 副本

Facsimile Pronunciation

/fækˈsɪmɪli/

Facsimile Definition

noun

Definition

An exact copy or reproduction of a document, artwork, or other item.

In ZH

文档、艺术品或其他物品的精确副本或复制品。

How to Use Facsimile in a Sentence

  • She sent a facsimile of the signed contract to the office.
  • The museum displayed a facsimile of the ancient manuscript.
  • For legal purposes, we need a facsimile of the original document.
  • He received a facsimile of the painting instead of the original.
  • The publisher included a facsimile of the author's handwritten notes.

Synonyms for Facsimile

  • Facetious
  • Facilitate
  • Factitious
  • Factotum
