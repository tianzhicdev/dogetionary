Fidelity

Pronunciation:/fɪˈdɛlɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 忠诚
  • 准确性
  • 保真度

Definitions

noun

Definition

Faithfulness to a person, cause, or belief demonstrated by continuing loyalty and support.

In ZH

对一个人、事业或信仰的忠诚，表现为持续的忠心和支持。

Examples

  • He showed great fidelity to his friends throughout the years.
  • The fidelity of her voice was impressive.
  • In marriage, fidelity is often considered a key component.
  • The company prides itself on the fidelity of its product to the original design.
  • His fidelity to duty was recognized by the community.
noun

Definition

The degree to which a sound or image is accurately reproduced.

In ZH

声音或图像被准确再现的程度。

Examples

  • The fidelity of the audio equipment was outstanding.
  • They tested the fidelity of the recordings.
  • High fidelity systems are preferred by audiophiles.
  • The artist aimed for high fidelity in the portraits.
  • Fidelity in video quality affects viewer experience.
