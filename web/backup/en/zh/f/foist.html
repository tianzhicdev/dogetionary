Foist

Pronunciation:/fɔɪst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 强加
  • 欺骗
  • 偷天换日

Definitions

verb

Definition

To impose something unwanted on someone

In ZH

将不想要的东西强加于某人

Examples

  • He tried to foist his old car onto me.
  • Don't let them foist their problems on you.
  • She foisted her responsibility onto her colleague.
  • They foisted the blame onto the new employee.
  • The salesman attempted to foist the defective product on unsuspecting customers.
noun

Definition

An instance of foisting; an act of imposing something

In ZH

强加的情况；施加某物的行为

Examples

  • The foist was clearly evident when he sold me counterfeit goods.
  • Her foist of responsibilities led to confusion in the team.
  • The foist during the negotiation was unexpected.
  • There was a foist when they changed the terms last minute.
  • Avoid any foist that might come from misleading promises.
