Fiduciary

Pronunciation:/fɪˈdjuːʃəri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 信托人
  • 受托人
  • 受托责任

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who holds a legal or ethical relationship of trust with one or more other parties.

In ZH

持有法律或伦理信任关系的人。

Examples

  • The lawyer acted as a fiduciary for the client.
  • In a fiduciary capacity, the trustee must act in the best interest of the beneficiaries.
  • He has a fiduciary duty to manage the funds responsibly.
  • The fiduciary relationship requires complete honesty and transparency.
  • It is essential for a fiduciary to avoid conflicts of interest.
