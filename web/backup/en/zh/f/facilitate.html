Facilitate

Pronunciation:/fəˈsɪlɪteɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 促进
  • 便利
  • 帮助

Definitions

verb

Definition

to make (an action or process) easy or easier.

In ZH

使（行动或过程）变得容易或更容易。

Examples

  • The new software will facilitate project management.
  • We aim to facilitate communication between departments.
  • Teachers facilitate learning by providing resources.
  • The organization works to facilitate disaster relief efforts.
  • Good planning can facilitate a smooth transition.
