Fluctuate

Pronunciation:/ˈflʌk.tʃu.eɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 波动
  • 起伏
  • 浮动

Definitions

verb

Definition

To rise and fall irregularly in number or amount.

In ZH

数量或金额不规则地上升和下降。

Examples

  • The stock prices tend to fluctuate throughout the day.
  • Temperatures can fluctuate widely depending on the season.
  • Her mood seemed to fluctuate with the weather changes.
  • The value of cryptocurrencies can fluctuate dramatically.
  • Sales figures have fluctuated over the past few months.
