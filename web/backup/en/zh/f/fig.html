Fig

EN→ZH

What Does Fig Mean?

  • 无花果
  • 干无花果

Fig Pronunciation

/fɪg/

Fig Definition

noun

Definition

A fruit that is soft, sweet, and usually pear-shaped, with a thin skin, and containing many seeds.

In ZH

一种柔软、甜美、通常呈梨状的水果，外皮薄，含有许多种子。

How to Use Fig in a Sentence

  • I enjoy eating fresh figs during the summer months.
  • The fig tree in my backyard produces the sweetest fruits.
  • She made a delicious fig jam for her breakfast.
  • Figs are rich in dietary fiber and nutrients.
  • In some cultures, figs are associated with prosperity and fertility.
