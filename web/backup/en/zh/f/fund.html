Fund

Pronunciation:/fʌnd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 资金
  • 基金
  • 基金会
  • 财源
  • 经费

Definitions

noun

Definition

A sum of money saved or made available for a particular purpose.

In ZH

一笔为了特定目的而保存或提供的资金。

Examples

  • We need to secure a fund for the new project.
  • The charity has a fund for helping homeless families.
  • She inherited a fund from her grandfather.
  • The fund has grown significantly over the past year.
  • They are looking to create a pension fund for their employees.
verb

Definition

To provide money for something.

In ZH

为某事提供资金。

Examples

  • The government plans to fund education initiatives.
  • Many organizations fund research in various fields.
  • He agreed to fund the startup company.
  • We need to find a way to fund our community program.
  • They are raising money to fund the new library.
