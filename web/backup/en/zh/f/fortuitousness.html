Fortuitousness

EN→ZH

What Does Fortuitousness Mean?

  • 偶然性
  • 意外性
  • 幸运性

Fortuitousness Pronunciation

/fɔːrˈtjuːɪtəsnəs/

Fortuitousness Definition

noun

Definition

The state of being fortuitous; the quality of occurring by chance, often in a positive or beneficial manner.

In ZH

偶然发生的状态；一种以随机方式发生的特性，往往以积极或有益的方式发生。

How to Use Fortuitousness in a Sentence

  • Her success was attributed to the fortuitousness of her meeting the right people at the right time.
  • The fortuitousness of the situation led to unexpected opportunities for collaboration.
  • We often underestimate the fortuitousness of our life events and the role they play in shaping our future.
  • The fortuitousness of the weather allowed us to enjoy a perfect day at the beach.
  • In literature, the fortuitousness of chance encounters can lead to significant plot developments.

Synonyms for Fortuitousness

  • Forbear
  • Forbearance
  • Foreboding
  • Foresight
  • Forlorn
