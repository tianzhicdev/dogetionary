Finespun

EN→ZH

What Does Finespun Mean?

  • 细纺的
  • 精细的
  • 细致的

Finespun Pronunciation

/ˈfaɪn.spʌn/

Finespun Definition

adjective

Definition

Very delicate or finely made; intricate and subtle.

In ZH

非常精致或制作得很细；复杂而微妙。

How to Use Finespun in a Sentence

  • The artist's finespun technique created an ethereal quality in the painting.
  • Her finespun silk scarf draped elegantly around her neck.
  • The writer's finespun prose captivated readers with its depth.
  • They admired the finespun details of the handcrafted jewelry.
  • His argument was finespun, weaving together various perspectives smoothly.

Synonyms for Finespun
