Fluorescent

EN→ZH

What Does Fluorescent Mean?

  • 荧光的
  • 荧光灯

Fluorescent Pronunciation

/flɔːˈrɛsənt/

Fluorescent Definition

adjective

Definition

Having the property of fluorescence; emitting light while absorbing radiation, especially ultraviolet light.

In ZH

具有荧光特性；在吸收辐射（特别是紫外线）时发出光。

How to Use Fluorescent in a Sentence

  • Fluorescent dyes are often used in biological research.
  • The fluorescent lights in the room are very bright.
  • She wore a fluorescent jacket for visibility while biking.
  • Fluorescent minerals can glow under UV light.
  • The signage was made of fluorescent materials to attract attention.
noun

Definition

A type of light bulb that uses fluorescence to produce visible light.

In ZH

一种使用荧光产生可见光的灯泡。

How to Use Fluorescent in a Sentence

  • The fluorescent bulb has become popular in modern lighting.
  • Fluorescent lamps are more energy-efficient than incandescent bulbs.
  • Many office buildings use fluorescent lights for illumination.
  • They replaced the old incandescent bulbs with fluorescent ones.
  • The store installed fluorescent lighting to save on energy costs.
