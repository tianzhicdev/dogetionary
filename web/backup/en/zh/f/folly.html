Folly

EN→ZH

What Does Folly Mean?

  • 愚蠢
  • 荒唐
  • 无知
  • 错误

Folly Pronunciation

/ˈfɒli/

Folly Definition

noun

Definition

Lack of good sense; foolishness.

In ZH

缺乏良好判断；愚蠢。

How to Use Folly in a Sentence

  • His folly led him to make poor decisions.
  • It was sheer folly to invest in that failing company.
  • She realized her folly only after the damage was done.
  • The folly of youth often results in regret later in life.
  • In their folly, they ignored the warnings and went ahead with the plan.

Synonyms for Folly
