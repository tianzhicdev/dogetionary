Fusty

Pronunciation:/ˈfʌsti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 陈腐的
  • 过时的
  • 霉臭的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having a stale, moldy, or damp smell; old-fashioned or outdated.

In ZH

有霉臭味的；过时或过时的。

Examples

  • The old books had a fusty smell that filled the room.
  • He has some fusty ideas about how to run the company.
  • The basement was dark and fusty, filled with forgotten items.
  • Her fusty wardrobe seemed to belong to another decade.
  • The museum's fusty exhibits failed to attract young visitors.
