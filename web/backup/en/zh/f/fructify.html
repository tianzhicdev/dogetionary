Fructify

EN→ZH

What Does Fructify Mean?

  • 使结果实
  • 使丰收
  • 使生效

Fructify Pronunciation

/ˈfrʌk.tɪ.faɪ/

Fructify Definition

verb

Definition

to make fruitful or productive; to bear fruit or yield results

In ZH

使结果实或有成果；结出果实或产生结果

How to Use Fructify in a Sentence

  • The new farming techniques will fructify the land.
  • Hard work often fructifies in the form of success.
  • They hope their investments will fructify over time.
  • The research is designed to fructify innovations in technology.
  • With patience, every effort will fructify eventually.

Synonyms for Fructify

  • Fruition
