Forthcoming

Pronunciation:/fɔːrθˈkʌmɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 即将到来的
  • forthcoming
  • 即将发生的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

About to appear or happen; imminent; available when required.

In ZH

即将出现或发生的；迫近的；需要时可用的。

Examples

  • The forthcoming election is generating a lot of excitement.
  • She is looking forward to the forthcoming holiday.
  • New details about the forthcoming project will be released soon.
  • The forthcoming concert will feature several popular artists.
  • He was known for his forthcoming nature, always ready to help others.
noun

Definition

The act of coming forth; the arrival of something expected.

In ZH

即将到来的事情；预期事件的到来。

Examples

  • The forthcoming of spring brings new life to the garden.
  • The forthcoming of the new policy caused concern among employees.
  • With the forthcoming of new technology, many jobs are changing.
  • The forthcoming of summer is a time for vacations and relaxation.
  • The forthcoming of the new product launch was highly anticipated.
