Feral

Pronunciation:/ˈfɛr.əl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 野生的
  • 狂野的
  • 未驯化的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

In a wild state, especially after escape from captivity or domestication.

In ZH

在野生状态，尤其是从圈养或驯化中逃脱后。

Examples

  • The feral cats in the alley often hunt for food together.
  • She adopted a feral dog and helped it become more social.
  • Feral animals can pose a threat to local wildlife.
  • The feral population of rabbits has increased significantly this year.
  • His behavior was considered feral after spending months in isolation.
noun

Definition

A feral animal, particularly one that has reverted to a wild state.

In ZH

一种野生动物，尤其是指已经恢复到野生状态的动物。

Examples

  • A feral can often be more unpredictable than a domesticated pet.
  • The feral was rescued and taken to a shelter for rehabilitation.
  • They found a feral in the woods, living off the land.
  • The shelter is working to manage the feral population in the city.
  • They launched a campaign to trap and neuter ferals in the area.
