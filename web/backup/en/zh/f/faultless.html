Faultless

EN→ZH

What Does Faultless Mean?

  • 无瑕疵的
  • 完美的
  • 无缺的

Faultless Pronunciation

/ˈfɔːltləs/

Faultless Definition

adjective

Definition

Free from fault or error; perfect.

In ZH

没有错误或缺陷；完美

How to Use Faultless in a Sentence

  • Her performance was faultless and received a standing ovation.
  • He has a faultless reputation in his field.
  • The design of the building was faultless in its execution.
  • I strive for faultless results in my work.
  • The chef prepared a faultless meal that impressed everyone.

Synonyms for Faultless

  • Faun
  • Faux Pas
