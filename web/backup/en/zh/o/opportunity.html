Opportunity

EN→ZH

What Does Opportunity Mean?

  • 机会
  • 机遇
  • 良机

Opportunity Pronunciation

/ˌɒp.əˈtjuː.nɪ.ti/

Opportunity Definition

noun

Definition

A set of circumstances that makes it possible to do something.

In ZH

一组使做某事成为可能的情况。

How to Use Opportunity in a Sentence

  • She took the opportunity to travel abroad during her vacation.
  • This job offers a great opportunity for professional growth.
  • Don't miss this opportunity to learn new skills.
  • He saw an opportunity to invest in the rising tech market.
  • They provided a wonderful opportunity for networking at the conference.

Synonyms for Opportunity

  • Opprobrium
  • Oppugn
