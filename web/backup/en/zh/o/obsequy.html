Obsequy

Pronunciation:/ˈɒbsɪkwɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 葬礼
  • 殡葬仪式
  • 追悼仪式

Definitions

noun

Definition

A funeral rite or ceremony, especially a formal one.

In ZH

葬礼或仪式，特别是正式的仪式。

Examples

  • He was given a proper obsequy attended by family and friends.
  • The ancient culture placed great importance on the obsequy for their deceased.
  • They held an elaborate obsequy that lasted several days.
  • The obsequy was marked by traditional music and rituals.
  • Respectful obsequies were performed for the fallen hero.
