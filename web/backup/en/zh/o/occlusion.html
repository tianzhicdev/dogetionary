Occlusion

Pronunciation:/əˈkluːʒən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 闭塞
  • 阻塞
  • 遮挡

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of occluding or the state of being occluded; blockage or closure of a passage or opening.

In ZH

闭塞的行为或状态；通道或开口的阻塞或关闭。

Examples

  • The occlusion of the artery can lead to serious health issues.
  • In dentistry, occlusion refers to the alignment of the teeth when the jaws are closed.
  • He suffered from a sinus occlusion that caused severe headaches.
  • The occlusion of light by the clouds created a gloomy atmosphere.
  • We need to check for any occlusion in the pipes to ensure proper water flow.
