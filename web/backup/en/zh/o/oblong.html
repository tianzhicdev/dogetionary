Oblong

Pronunciation:/ˈɒblɒŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 长方形
  • 椭圆形

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having an elongated shape; longer than it is wide.

In ZH

具有延长的形状；比宽度大。

Examples

  • The table had an oblong shape, perfect for seating many people.
  • She preferred oblong frames for her pictures.
  • The garden was laid out in an oblong design.
  • His oblong balloon quickly floated away in the wind.
  • They placed an oblong rug in the center of the living room.
noun

Definition

A shape or object that is longer than it is wide, typically rectangular or oval.

In ZH

一种形状或物体，其长度超过宽度，通常是矩形或椭圆形。

Examples

  • The artist drew an oblong to represent the table.
  • They measured the area of the oblong in the park.
  • An oblong of fabric was needed for the quilt.
  • In geometry class, we studied the properties of oblongs.
  • The oblong shape of the cake made it easy to slice.
