Obsequious

Pronunciation:/əbˈsiː.kwi.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 恭维的
  • 阿谀的
  • 谄媚的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Showing too great a willingness to serve or please someone; excessively fawning or flattering.

In ZH

表现出过于渴望为某人服务或取悦某人；过分谄媚或奉承的。

Examples

  • His obsequious behavior towards his boss was insincere.
  • She found his obsequious compliments to be irritating.
  • The obsequious waiter made sure to attend to their every need.
  • They were tired of his obsequious attitude around powerful people.
  • Obsequious followers often fail to provide honest feedback.
