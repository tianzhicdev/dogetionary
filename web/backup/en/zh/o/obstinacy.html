Obstinacy

EN→ZH

What Does Obstinacy Mean?

  • 固执
  • 倔强
  • 执拗

Obstinacy Pronunciation

/ˈɒb.stɪ.nə.si/

Obstinacy Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being obstinate; stubbornness.

In ZH

固执的性质；倔强。

How to Use Obstinacy in a Sentence

  • His obstinacy often leads him to make poor decisions.
  • Despite the evidence presented to him, her obstinacy prevented any change of mind.
  • The child's obstinacy frustrated his parents during the negotiation.
  • Obstinacy in the face of reason can be a barrier to progress.
  • She admired his obstinacy in sticking to his beliefs.

Synonyms for Obstinacy

  • Obscurantist
  • Obsequious
  • Obsequiousness
  • Obsequy
  • Obsolescence
