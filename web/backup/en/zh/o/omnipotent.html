Omnipotent

EN→ZH

What Does Omnipotent Mean?

  • 全能的
  • 无所不能的
  • 万能的

Omnipotent Pronunciation

/ɑːmˈnɪpətənt/

Omnipotent Definition

adjective

Definition

having unlimited power; able to do anything.

In ZH

拥有无穷的力量；能够做任何事情。

How to Use Omnipotent in a Sentence

  • Many religions believe in an omnipotent being that created the universe.
  • She felt omnipotent in her new position of authority.
  • The novel explores the theme of an omnipotent ruler.
  • In mythology, gods are often depicted as omnipotent.
  • The concept of an omnipotent deity raises philosophical questions.

Synonyms for Omnipotent

  • Omnipresent
  • Omniscience
  • Omniscient
  • Omnishambles
