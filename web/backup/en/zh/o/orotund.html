Orotund

EN→ZH

What Does Orotund Mean?

  • 洪亮的
  • 圆润的
  • 响亮的

Orotund Pronunciation

/ˈɔːrəˌtʌnd/

Orotund Definition

adjective

Definition

Characterized by a full, rounded sound; rich and sonorous.

In ZH

以饱满、圆润的声音为特征；丰富而响亮。

How to Use Orotund in a Sentence

  • The orotund voice of the speaker captivated the audience.
  • He delivered his speech in an orotund manner that commanded attention.
  • The orotund tones of the organ filled the grand cathedral.
  • Her orotund laughter echoed through the hall.
  • The actor's orotund delivery added depth to the character.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in literary or formal contexts to describe speech or sounds that are imposing or impressive.
