Odyssey

Pronunciation:/ˈɒd.ɪ.si/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 长途旅行
  • 冒险
  • 史诗

Definitions

noun

Definition

A long and eventful journey or experience.

In ZH

漫长而充满事件的旅程或经历。

Examples

  • His life has been an odyssey of exploration and adventure.
  • The odyssey of her recovery was filled with challenges.
  • We embarked on an odyssey across the country.
  • Their odyssey through the mountains was unforgettable.
  • This book is a modern odyssey about finding oneself.
