Omnishambles

Pronunciation:/ˈɒmnɪˌʃæmbəlz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 全方位混乱
  • 全面失败
  • 杂乱无章

Definitions

noun

Definition

A situation that has been comprehensively mismanaged, characterized by a string of blunders and miscalculations.

In ZH

一个被全面管理不善的情况，其特点是一系列失误和错误的计算。

Examples

  • The project was an omnishambles from start to finish.
  • The political debate turned into an omnishambles with constant interruptions.
  • The event was an omnishambles, leaving attendees frustrated and confused.
  • Their plans for the new product launch ended up in an omnishambles.
  • After the last-minute changes, the presentation became an omnishambles.
