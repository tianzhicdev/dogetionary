Obdurate

Pronunciation:/ˈɒb.dʒʊ.ər.ɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 顽固的
  • 冷酷的
  • 无情的
  • 坚决的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Stubbornly refusing to change one's opinion or course of action.

In ZH

顽固地拒绝改变自己的意见或行动方向。

Examples

  • Despite numerous pleas, he remained obdurate in his decision.
  • The obdurate child refused to apologize for his behavior.
  • Her obdurate stance on the issue frustrated her friends.
  • He was obdurate in his belief that he was right, no matter the evidence.
  • Even the most convincing arguments could not sway her obdurate heart.
