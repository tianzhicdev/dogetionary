Oleaginous

EN→ZH

What Does Oleaginous Mean?

  • 油腻的
  • 油脂状的

Oleaginous Pronunciation

/ˌoʊliˈædʒɪnəs/

Oleaginous Definition

adjective

Definition

rich in, covered with, or resembling oil; having a smooth or greasy texture; excessively flattering in a servile way

In ZH

富含或覆盖着油；有光滑或油腻的质地；过于奉承的，谄媚的

How to Use Oleaginous in a Sentence

  • The oleaginous substance covered the surface of the pan.
  • His oleaginous charm made everyone question his sincerity.
  • The politician's oleaginous speech left many listeners feeling uneasy.
  • She wiped her hands on a towel to remove the oleaginous residue.
  • They served an oleaginous dish that was too rich for my taste.
