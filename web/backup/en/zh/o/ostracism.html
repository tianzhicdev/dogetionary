Ostracism

Pronunciation:/ˈɒstrəsɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 排斥
  • 孤立
  • 放逐

Definitions

noun

Definition

Exclusion from a society or group.

In ZH

被排除在某个社会或群体之外。

Examples

  • Ostracism can lead to severe emotional distress.
  • Many students fear ostracism from their peers.
  • The ancient Greeks practiced ostracism as a form of political control.
  • Ostracism can occur in workplaces as well.
  • Facing ostracism, she struggled to find new friends.
