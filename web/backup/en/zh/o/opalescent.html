Opalescent

Pronunciation:/ˌoʊ.pəˈlɛs.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 乳白色的
  • 闪烁的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Exhibiting a play of colors like that of an opal; having a milky iridescence.

In ZH

呈现像蛋白石一样的颜色变化；具有乳白色的虹彩效果。

Examples

  • The opalescent surface of the stone caught the light beautifully.
  • Her dress had an opalescent sheen that changed colors as she moved.
  • The opalescent sky at sunrise was a breathtaking sight.
  • He admired the opalescent finish on the pottery.
  • The jewelry displayed an opalescent glow that was mesmerizing.
