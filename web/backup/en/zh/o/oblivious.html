Oblivious

EN→ZH

What Does Oblivious Mean?

  • 无知的
  • 遗忘的
  • 忘却的

Oblivious Pronunciation

/əˈblɪviəs/

Oblivious Definition

adjective

Definition

not aware of or concerned about what is happening around one

In ZH

对周围发生的事情毫不在意或没有意识的

How to Use Oblivious in a Sentence

  • She was oblivious to the noise around her.
  • He walked down the street, oblivious to the stares from passersby.
  • Despite the warnings, he remained oblivious to the danger ahead.
  • They were so wrapped up in their conversation that they were oblivious to the time.
  • The dog seemed oblivious to the fact that it was being filmed.

Synonyms for Oblivious

  • Oblique
  • Oblivion
  • Oblong
  • Obloquy
