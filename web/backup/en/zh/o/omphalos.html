Omphalos

Pronunciation:/ˈɒm.fə.lɒs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 肚脐
  • 中心点
  • 枢纽

Definitions

noun

Definition

a central point; a navel or umbilicus; in ancient cultures, a religious center or a symbolic location often identified as the center of the world.

In ZH

一个中心点；肚脐或脐带；在古代文化中，宗教中心或通常被视为世界中心的象征性地点。

Examples

  • The omphalos stone at Delphi was believed to be the center of the world.
  • In ancient Greece, many temples were constructed around the omphalos.
  • The omphalos is a significant symbol in various mythologies.
  • He traced the omphalos to understand the significance of centrality in his studies.
  • The concept of the omphalos is often used in discussions of geography and spirituality.

Cultural Notes

In ancient Greek mythology, the omphalos was a stone that represented the navel of the world, often connected to oracles and divination.
