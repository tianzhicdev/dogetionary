Octogenarian

EN→ZH

What Does Octogenarian Mean?

  • 八十岁的人
  • 八旬
  • 八十岁以上的人

Octogenarian Pronunciation

/ˌɒktəʊdʒɪˈnɛəriən/

Octogenarian Definition

noun

Definition

A person who is between 80 and 89 years old.

In ZH

一个年龄在80到89岁之间的人。

How to Use Octogenarian in a Sentence

  • My grandfather is an octogenarian who still plays tennis.
  • Many octogenarians enjoy sharing stories from their youth.
  • The community center offers special programs for octogenarians.
  • Octogenarians often have a wealth of wisdom to share.
  • Celebrating the birthday of an octogenarian is a special occasion.
