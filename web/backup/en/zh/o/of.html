Of

EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 的
  • 属于
  • 关于

Definitions

Definition

Used to indicate belonging or possession.

In ZH

用于表示属于或拥有关系。

Examples

  • The cat of my neighbor thinks it owns my garden. I guess it’s the ruler of the backyard now!
  • The secret of the universe is that it’s just a giant game of hide and seek, and we’re all hiding.
  • The taste of this pizza is so good, it should be declared a national treasure!

Definition

Used to indicate the material or substance from which something is made.

In ZH

用于表示某物的材料或成分。

Examples

  • This statue is made of chocolate, which is great until it starts to melt in the sun!
  • The dress of my dreams is made of pizza slices, but I’m still waiting for the fashion industry to catch up.
  • The cake of my birthday was so big, it could have been a contestant on a cooking show!

Definition

Used to indicate the topic or subject of something.

In ZH

用于表示某事的主题或内容。

Examples

  • The book of my life is a bestseller, but only in my imagination!
  • The news of the day is that cats have officially taken over the internet, and we are just their loyal subjects.
  • The story of my last vacation involves a lot of sunscreen and a very confused seagull.
