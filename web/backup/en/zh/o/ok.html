Ok

Pronunciation:/oʊˈkeɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 好
  • 可以
  • 行

Definitions

adjective

Definition

satisfactory but not exceptionally good

In ZH

令人满意，但不特别好

Examples

  • The movie was ok, but I expected more.
  • Her grades in school are ok, nothing to worry about.
  • The food at the restaurant was ok, but I wouldn't go back again.
  • It's an ok solution but we can do better.
  • He gave me an ok performance during the competition.
noun

Definition

an acknowledgment or approval

In ZH

一种承认或批准

Examples

  • He gave me the ok to start the project.
  • I need the boss's ok before proceeding.
  • She received the ok from her parents to go out.
  • The teacher gave the ok for the field trip.
  • We are waiting for the ok from the manager.
adverb

Definition

in a satisfactory manner

In ZH

以令人满意的方式

Examples

  • He handled the situation ok.
  • The team performed ok during the game.
  • She adjusted to the new environment pretty ok.
  • The presentation went ok, considering the circumstances.
  • Overall, the project turned out ok.
