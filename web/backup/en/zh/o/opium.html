Opium

EN→ZH

What Does Opium Mean?

  • 鸦片
  • 罂粟
  • 毒品

Opium Pronunciation

ˈoʊ.pi.əm

Opium Definition

noun

Definition

A narcotic drug obtained from the juice of the opium poppy, used for pain relief and as a sedative.

In ZH

从罂粟花的汁液中提取的麻醉药物，用于缓解疼痛和作为镇静剂。

How to Use Opium in a Sentence

  • Opium has been used for centuries for its pain-relieving properties.
  • Many countries have strict laws regulating the use of opium and its derivatives.
  • The historical trade of opium has significantly impacted international relations.

Cultural Notes

Opium has a long history in various cultures, particularly in traditional medicine, but it is also associated with addiction and illegal drug trade.
