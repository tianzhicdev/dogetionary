Omnifarious

EN→ZH

What Does Omnifarious Mean?

  • 各类的
  • 各种各样的
  • 多样的

Omnifarious Pronunciation

/ˌɒmnɪˈfɛərɪəs/

Omnifarious Definition

adjective

Definition

comprising or relating to all varieties

In ZH

包含或涉及所有种类的

How to Use Omnifarious in a Sentence

  • The festival featured omnifarious cultural exhibits.
  • Her interests are omnifarious, ranging from art to technology.
  • The library has an omnifarious collection of books.
  • The restaurant offers an omnifarious menu to cater to all tastes.
  • He has omnifarious talents, including music, painting, and writing.

Synonyms for Omnifarious

  • Omniscience
