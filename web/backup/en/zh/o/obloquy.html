Obloquy

EN→ZH

What Does Obloquy Mean?

  • 恶名
  • 诽谤
  • 辱骂

Obloquy Pronunciation

/ˈɒbləkwɪ/

Obloquy Definition

noun

Definition

Strong public criticism or verbal abuse.

In ZH

强烈的公众批评或口头辱骂。

How to Use Obloquy in a Sentence

  • The politician faced obloquy after the scandal was revealed.
  • She endured public obloquy for her controversial statements.
  • His actions brought obloquy upon his family.
  • The author's obloquy was short-lived once the truth came out.
  • They tried to escape the obloquy associated with their failures.

Synonyms for Obloquy

  • Oblique
  • Oblivion
  • Oblivious
  • Oblong
