Ophidian

Pronunciation:/əˈfɪd.i.ən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 蛇类的
  • 蛇的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

of or relating to snakes

In ZH

Examples

  • The ophidian design of the logo featured a stylized snake.

  • In the documentary, they explored the ophidian adaptations that allow survival in the wild.
