Obstreperous

Pronunciation:/əbˈstrɛp.ər.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 喧闹的
  • 吵闹的
  • 难以控制的
  • 不屈从的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

noisy and difficult to control

In ZH

喧闹且难以控制的

Examples

  • The obstreperous crowd made it hard for the speaker to be heard.
  • His obstreperous behavior got him into trouble with the teachers.
  • She had an obstreperous dog that never followed commands.
  • They tried to shush the obstreperous children at the party.
  • The obstreperous debate continued late into the night.
