Ombudsman

Pronunciation:/ˈɒmbʌdz.mən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 申诉专员
  • 监察员
  • 公诉人

Definitions

noun

Definition

An official appointed to investigate individuals' complaints against a company or organization, especially a public authority.

In ZH

被任命调查个人对公司或组织（特别是公共机构）投诉的官员。

Examples

  • The ombudsman helped resolve the dispute between the employee and the management.
  • Citizens can approach the ombudsman if they feel their rights have been violated.
  • The university's ombudsman provides a confidential service to students.
  • An independent ombudsman ensures accountability in government practices.
  • Many countries have an ombudsman to address social justice issues.
