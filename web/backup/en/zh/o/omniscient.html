Omniscient

Pronunciation:/əmˈnɪʃ.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无所不知的
  • 全知的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having complete or unlimited knowledge, awareness, or understanding; perceiving all things.

In ZH

具有完全或无限的知识、意识或理解；洞察所有事物。

Examples

  • The narrator in the novel is omniscient.
  • In many religions, God is often described as omniscient.
  • The omniscient point of view allows the reader to know what every character is thinking.
  • With an omniscient narrator, the story can unfold with multiple perspectives.
  • An omniscient being is often attributed to possessing infinite knowledge.
