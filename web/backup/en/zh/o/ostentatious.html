Ostentatious

Pronunciation:/ˌɒstənˈteɪʃəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 炫耀的
  • 华丽的
  • 夸耀的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by vulgar or pretentious display; designed to impress or attract notice.

In ZH

以庸俗或炫耀的显示为特征；旨在给人留下深刻印象或吸引注意。

Examples

  • His ostentatious mansion was the talk of the town.
  • She wore an ostentatious dress that drew everyone's attention.
  • The ostentatious display of wealth made him unpopular among his peers.
  • Their ostentatious behavior at the gala was considered inappropriate.
  • He found it hard to blend in with the ostentatious crowd at the party.
