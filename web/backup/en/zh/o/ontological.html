Ontological

EN→ZH

What Does Ontological Mean?

  • 本体论的
  • 存在论的
  • 形而上学的

Ontological Pronunciation

/ˌɒn.təˈlɒɡ.ɪ.kəl/

Ontological Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to the branch of metaphysics dealing with the nature of being.

In ZH

与研究存在本性的形而上学分支相关。

How to Use Ontological in a Sentence

  • Ontological arguments for the existence of God have been debated for centuries.
  • The ontological status of abstract objects raises interesting philosophical questions.
  • He explored ontological questions in his dissertation.
  • In philosophy, ontological inquiries seek to understand what it means to exist.
  • The ontological implications of quantum mechanics are still being studied.

Synonyms for Ontological

  • Ontogeny
