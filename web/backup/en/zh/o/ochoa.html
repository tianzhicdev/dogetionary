Ochoa

EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 奥乔
  • 奥丘

Definitions

noun

Definition

Ochoa is a surname of Spanish origin, often found in Spanish-speaking countries and communities.

In ZH

Ochoa是一个源于西班牙的姓氏，常见于讲西班牙语的国家和社区。

Examples

  • Ochoa is a common last name in Mexico.
  • I met someone named Maria Ochoa at the conference.
  • The famous goalkeeper is named Guillermo Ochoa.

Cultural Notes

The surname Ochoa is often associated with Hispanic heritage. It can be common among families with roots in Spain and Latin America.
