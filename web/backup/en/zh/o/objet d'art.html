Objet D'art

Pronunciation:/əʊbʒɛ ˈdɑːr/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 艺术品
  • 艺术对象

Definitions

noun

Definition

an object made for artistic purposes

In ZH

为艺术目的制作的物品

Examples

  • This gallery features a stunning collection of objets d'art.
  • The antique shop specializes in rare objets d'art from the 18th century.
  • She purchased an objet d'art during her trip to Paris.
  • The auction showcased various objets d'art from renowned artists.
  • He admired the intricacy of the objet d'art displayed on the mantelpiece.
