Unctuous

Pronunciation:/ˈʌn(k)tʃuəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 油腔滑调的
  • 阿谀奉承的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Excessively or ingratiatingly flattering; oily in manner or speech.

In ZH

过分恭维的；油腔滑调的

Examples

  • The salesman had an unctuous tone that made me suspicious of his intentions.
  • His unctuous compliments quickly lost their charm after the first few minutes of conversation.
  • She found his unctuous manner to be off-putting during the interview.
  • The film critic described the actor's performance as unctuous, lacking genuine emotion.
  • In the meeting, his unctuous approach failed to impress the tough crowd.
