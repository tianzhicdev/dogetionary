Unctuousness

Pronunciation:/ˈʌŋk.tʃəs.nəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 油腔滑调
  • 油滑
  • 奉承

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being ingratiatingly flattering or ingratiating.

In ZH

讨好奉承的特性或品质。

Examples

  • His unctuousness made everyone feel uncomfortable during the meeting.
  • The salesperson's unctuousness was evident as he tried to flatter the customers.
  • Her unctuousness in conversation often came off as insincere.
  • Despite his unctuousness, people found it hard to trust him.
  • The politician's unctuousness did not sit well with the voters.

Cultural Notes

In English, the term often has a negative connotation, implying insincerity.
