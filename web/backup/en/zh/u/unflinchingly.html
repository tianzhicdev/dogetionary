Unflinchingly

EN→ZH

What Does Unflinchingly Mean?

  • 毫不退缩地
  • 坚定地
  • 毫无畏惧地

Unflinchingly Pronunciation

/ʌnˈflɪn.tʃɪŋ.li/

Unflinchingly Definition

adverb

Definition

In a way that shows no fear or hesitation; resolutely.

In ZH

以毫不畏惧的方式；坚定地。

How to Use Unflinchingly in a Sentence

  • She unflinchingly faced the challenges ahead.
  • He spoke unflinchingly about his beliefs.
  • The soldier acted unflinchingly in the face of danger.
  • They unflinchingly pursued their goals, despite the obstacles.
  • The team performed unflinchingly under pressure.

Synonyms for Unflinchingly

  • Unfathomable
  • Unfazed
  • Unfettered
  • Unforgettable
  • Unfurl
