Unabated

EN→ZH

What Does Unabated Mean?

  • 不减弱的
  • 不屈服的

Unabated Pronunciation

/ˌʌn.əˈbeɪ.tɪd/

Unabated Definition

adjective

Definition

Continuing at full strength or intensity without any reduction in power or force.

In ZH

持续全力或强度，没有任何减弱。

How to Use Unabated in a Sentence

  • The storm raged on unabated throughout the night.
  • Her enthusiasm for the project remained unabated.
  • The protests continued unabated despite the government's warnings.
  • His interest in the subject was unabated over the years.
  • The team's efforts were unabated as they pushed towards their goal.

Synonyms for Unabated

  • Unabashed
  • Unambiguity
