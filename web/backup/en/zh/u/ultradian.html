Ultradian

Pronunciation:/ʌlˈtreɪdiən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 超日节律
  • 超日周波
  • 超日节律周期

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Referring to biological rhythms that occur with a frequency greater than one cycle in 24 hours, such as sleep cycles.

In ZH

指生物节律，其频率超过24小时内的一个周期，例如睡眠周期。

Examples

  • The ultradian rhythm of the human body can significantly affect our sleep patterns.
  • Research shows that ultradian cycles can influence cognitive performance throughout the day.
  • Some scientists study ultradian rhythms to understand how they impact mood and energy levels.
  • Understanding ultradian rhythms can help optimize work and rest schedules.
  • Yoga and meditation can help align the body with its natural ultradian rhythms.
