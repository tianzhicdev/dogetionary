Unobtrusive

EN→ZH

What Does Unobtrusive Mean?

  • 不显眼的
  • 低调的
  • 不打扰的

Unobtrusive Pronunciation

/ˌʌn.əbˈtruː.sɪv/

Unobtrusive Definition

adjective

Definition

not attracting attention in a way that is disturbing or unwanted; subtle

In ZH

不引人注意，以不干扰或不想要的方式吸引注意；微妙

How to Use Unobtrusive in a Sentence

  • The design of the building is unobtrusive, blending in with the surroundings.
  • She wore an unobtrusive dress that did not draw attention to herself.
  • The security measures were unobtrusive, ensuring guests felt comfortable.
  • His comments were helpful yet unobtrusive, allowing others to express their opinions.
  • They opted for unobtrusive lighting that enhanced the atmosphere without overwhelming it.
