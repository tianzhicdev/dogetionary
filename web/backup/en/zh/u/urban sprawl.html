Urban Sprawl

EN→ZH

What Does Urban Sprawl Mean?

  • 城市蔓延
  • 城市扩展
  • 城市散布

Urban Sprawl Pronunciation

ˈɜːr.bən sprɔːl

Urban Sprawl Definition

noun

Definition

The uncontrolled expansion of urban areas into the surrounding rural land.

In ZH

城市地区向周围乡村土地的无序扩展。

How to Use Urban Sprawl in a Sentence

  • Urban sprawl has led to a loss of agricultural land.
  • Many cities struggle with urban sprawl as they grow larger.
  • Urban sprawl can negatively affect the environment.

Cultural Notes

Urban sprawl is often discussed in the context of environmental issues, city planning, and sustainable development.

verb

Definition

To spread into or occupy more space in an uncontrolled or disorganized way.

In ZH

以无序或不组织的方式扩展或占用更多空间。

How to Use Urban Sprawl in a Sentence

  • The neighborhood started to sprawl into the nearby forest.
  • As the population increases, cities tend to sprawl further into suburban areas.
  • Without careful planning, the town may sprawl across the river valley.

Cultural Notes

While 'sprawl' can refer to physical space, it also suggests a lack of organization or planning, often seen as a challenge in urban development.

Synonyms for Urban Sprawl

  • Urbane
