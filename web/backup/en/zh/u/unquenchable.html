Unquenchable

EN→ZH

What Does Unquenchable Mean?

  • 永不熄灭的
  • 无法满足的
  • 无法抑制的

Unquenchable Pronunciation

/ʌnˈkwɛnʧəbl/

Unquenchable Definition

adjective

Definition

Not able to be satisfied or quenched; insatiable.

In ZH

无法满足或熄灭的；贪得无厌的。

How to Use Unquenchable in a Sentence

  • Her unquenchable thirst for knowledge drove her to study late into the night.
  • The fire was unquenchable, spreading rapidly through the dry forest.
  • He had an unquenchable desire to explore the world and meet new people.
  • The team's unquenchable spirit helped them to overcome every obstacle in their path.
  • Despite numerous setbacks, his unquenchable optimism kept him moving forward.
