Uplift

EN→ZH

What Does Uplift Mean?

  • 提升
  • 振奋
  • 鼓舞

Uplift Pronunciation

/ˈʌp.lɪft/

Uplift Definition

verb

Definition

to raise something to a higher position

In ZH

把某物提升到更高的位置

How to Use Uplift in a Sentence

  • The charity aims to uplift the lives of underprivileged children.
  • Music can uplift your mood when you're feeling down.
  • She always tries to uplift her friends with positive words.
  • The new policy is designed to uplift the local economy.
  • Art has the power to uplift the human spirit.
noun

Definition

an instance of raising something or someone

In ZH

提升或鼓舞的例子

How to Use Uplift in a Sentence

  • The uplift of the community spirit was evident after the event.
  • His speech was an uplift for everyone listening.
  • The organization focuses on the uplift of marginalized voices.
  • Environmental initiatives can lead to significant uplift in awareness.
  • The uplift experienced during the festival was contagious.

Synonyms for Uplift

  • Uplevel
