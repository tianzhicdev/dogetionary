Undeviating

Pronunciation:/ʌnˈdɛviˌeɪtɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不偏离的
  • 坚持不懈的
  • 始终如一的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Not deviating; consistent or unchanging in direction or purpose.

In ZH

不偏离的；在方向或目的上始终如一的

Examples

  • Her undeviating focus on her goals led to her success.
  • The team showed undeviating commitment to their project.
  • He followed an undeviating path through the forest.
  • Their undeviating morals guided their decisions.
  • She has an undeviating routine that she follows daily.
