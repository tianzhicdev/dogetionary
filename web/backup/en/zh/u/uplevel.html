Uplevel

Pronunciation:/ˈʌpˌlɛvəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 提升
  • 升级
  • 提高

Definitions

verb

Definition

To increase the level or status of something, often in a professional or informal context.

In ZH

提升某事物的级别或地位，通常在专业或非正式语境中。

Examples

  • The company decided to uplevel its training programs to enhance employee skills.
  • She worked hard to uplevel her expertise in digital marketing.
  • The software was upleveled to include more advanced features.
  • He wants to uplevel his career by taking on more challenging projects.
  • The team needs to uplevel their performance to meet the new targets.
