Ubiquity

Pronunciation:/juːˈbɪkwɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 普遍性
  • 无处不在
  • 遍在

Definitions

noun

Definition

The fact of appearing everywhere or of being very common.

In ZH

无处不在或非常普遍的事实。

Examples

  • The ubiquity of smartphones has changed the way we communicate.
  • In modern society, the ubiquity of the internet is undeniable.
  • The ubiquity of fast food restaurants means that they are accessible almost anywhere.
  • With the ubiquity of social media, everyone can share their opinions instantly.
  • The scientific concept of ubiquity helps explain how certain species thrive in diverse environments.

  • Ubiquitous
  • Ubiquitousness
