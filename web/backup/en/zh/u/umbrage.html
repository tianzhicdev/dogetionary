Umbrage

Pronunciation:/ˈʌm.brɪdʒ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不快
  • 生气
  • 愤怒

Definitions

noun

Definition

Offense or annoyance.

In ZH

冒犯或烦恼。

Examples

  • She took umbrage at his rude remarks.
  • He expressed umbrage over the unfair treatment.
  • Taking umbrage can sometimes escalate conflict.
  • I meant no offense, but she seemed to take umbrage anyway.
  • His umbrage was clear when he left the meeting abruptly.
