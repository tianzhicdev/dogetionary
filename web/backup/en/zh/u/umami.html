Umami

Pronunciation:/uːˈmɑːmi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 鲜味
  • 第五种味

Definitions

noun

Definition

A basic taste sensation that is fundamentally savory or meaty.

In ZH

一种基本的味觉感觉，主要是鲜美或肉味的。

Examples

  • Umami is often described as the taste of broth.
  • The addition of mushrooms enhances the umami flavor in dishes.
  • Cheese is a great source of umami.
  • Tomatoes are rich in umami compounds.
  • Many people enjoy the umami flavor in soy sauce.

Cultural Notes

Umami is recognized as the fifth basic taste alongside sweet, sour, bitter, and salty, and is especially important in Japanese cuisine.
