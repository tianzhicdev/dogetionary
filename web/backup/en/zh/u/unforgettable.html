Unforgettable

Pronunciation:/ʌn.fəˈɡɛt.ə.bəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 难以忘记的
  • 令人难忘的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Something that cannot be forgotten; memorable or remarkable.

In ZH

无法被遗忘的；值得记住或引人注目的。

Examples

  • The concert last night was unforgettable.
  • She gave an unforgettable speech that inspired everyone.
  • That trip to Paris was an unforgettable experience.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, the word 'unforgettable' is often used to describe experiences, people, or events that leave a lasting impression. It can be used in both positive and negative contexts.
