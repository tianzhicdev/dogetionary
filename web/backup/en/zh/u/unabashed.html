Unabashed

Pronunciation:/ˌʌn.əˈbæʃt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 毫不掩饰的
  • 不害羞的
  • 坦率的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

not embarrassed, disconcerted, or ashamed.

In ZH

毫不尴尬、不拘束或羞愧的。

Examples

  • She was unabashed about her opinions on the matter.
  • His unabashed enthusiasm for the project inspired the team.
  • The comedian made unabashed jokes about sensitive topics.
  • They remained unabashed in their support for the candidate.
  • Unabashed, she walked onto the stage with confidence.
