Urbane

Pronunciation:/ɜːrˈbeɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 文雅的
  • 彬彬有礼的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Suave, courteous, and refined in manner.

In ZH

举止优雅、彬彬有礼和精致的。

Examples

  • He is an urbane gentleman who knows how to charm a crowd.
  • Her urbane demeanor made her the center of attention at the gala.
  • The restaurant's urbane decor attracted a sophisticated clientele.
  • Despite his wealth, he remained urbane and down-to-earth.
  • The urbane dialogue in the film reflects the characters' high social status.
