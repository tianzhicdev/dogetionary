Uebermensch

EN→ZH

What Does Uebermensch Mean?

  • 超人
  • 超越者
  • 人类的理想形象

Uebermensch Pronunciation

/ˈyːbɚˌmɛnʃ/

Uebermensch Definition

noun

Definition

a concept in philosophy referring to an individual who transcends traditional human limitations and moral values, creating their own values and purpose.

In ZH

在哲学中，指一个超越传统人类局限和道德价值观的个体，创造自己的价值和目的。

How to Use Uebermensch in a Sentence

  • The idea of the 'uebermensch' was popularized by Nietzsche.
  • In his writings, the 'uebermensch' represents an ideal human being.
  • The concept challenges people to rise above mediocrity.
  • Many artists and thinkers have been inspired by the idea of the 'uebermensch'.
  • In modern culture, the term is often misused or misunderstood.

Cultural Notes

The term 'uebermensch' is often associated with Friedrich Nietzsche's philosophy and has been interpreted in various ways, including controversial ones.
