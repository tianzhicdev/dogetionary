Ululate

Pronunciation:/ˈjuː.ljʊ.leɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 呜呜叫
  • 哀号

Definitions

verb

Definition

To howl or wail as an expression of strong emotion, especially grief or lamentation.

In ZH

作为强烈情感的表达，尤其是悲伤或哀悼而嚎叫或哀鸣。

Examples

  • The mourners began to ululate at the funeral.
  • In some cultures, it is customary to ululate during times of mourning.
  • She could hear the distant ululation of the crowd celebrating the festival.
  • The ululating call of the wolf echoed through the night.
  • As the news spread, a collective ululation filled the air.

Cultural Notes

Ululation is often used in various cultures to express grief or celebrate joyous occasions.
