Zeroth

Pronunciation:/ˈziːrəθ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 零的
  • 第零的
  • 零次的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Denoting a position before the first in a sequence, often used in mathematics and computer science.

In ZH

在序列中表示第一位之前的位置，常用于数学和计算机科学。

Examples

  • The zeroth law of thermodynamics states that if two systems are in thermal equilibrium with a third system, they are in thermal equilibrium with each other.
  • In programming, the zeroth index is often used to refer to the first element of an array.
  • The concept of zeroth-order reactions is important in chemical kinetics.
  • When sorting items, the zeroth element is the starting point of the list.
  • In statistics, the zeroth moment is a measure of total probability.
