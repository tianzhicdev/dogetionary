Zeal

EN→ZH

What Does Zeal Mean?

  • 热情
  • 热心
  • 激情

Zeal Pronunciation

/ziːl/

Zeal Definition

noun

Definition

Great energy or enthusiasm in pursuit of a cause or an objective.

In ZH

在追求某个事业或目标时表现出的巨大能量或热情。

How to Use Zeal in a Sentence

  • She approached her studies with zeal.
  • His zeal for the project inspired the whole team.
  • They worked with zeal to complete the task ahead of schedule.
  • The volunteers showed great zeal in helping the community.
  • Her zeal for environmental causes drove her to start a campaign.

Synonyms for Zeal

  • Zealot
  • Zealotry
  • Zealous
