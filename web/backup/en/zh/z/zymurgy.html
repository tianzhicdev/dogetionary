Zymurgy

Pronunciation:/ˈzaɪ.mɜːr.dʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 酿酒学
  • 发酵学

Definitions

noun

Definition

the branch of chemistry that deals with fermentation processes, especially in brewing beer and making wine.

In ZH

研究发酵过程的化学分支，尤其是在酿造啤酒和制作葡萄酒时。

  • He took a course in zymurgy to learn about yeast and fermentation.
