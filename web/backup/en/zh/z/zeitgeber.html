Zeitgeber

Pronunciation:/ˈzaɪtˌɡeɪbər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 时钟
  • 生物钟信号
  • 时间给予者

Definitions

noun

Definition

An external cue that helps to regulate biological rhythms, particularly the circadian rhythm.

In ZH

一种外部线索，帮助调节生物节律，特别是昼夜节律。

Examples

  • Light is a primary zeitgeber for regulating sleep-wake cycles.
  • Temperature changes can act as a zeitgeber for animals in hibernation.
  • Social interactions can serve as a zeitgeber in human beings.
  • The changing seasons are important zeitgebers for many species.
  • Meal times can act as a zeitgeber that influences eating habits.
