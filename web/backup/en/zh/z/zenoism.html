What Does Zenoism Mean?

  • 芝诺主义
  • 芝诺论

Zenoism Pronunciation

/ˈziːnoʊɪzəm/

Zenoism Definition

noun

Definition

A philosophical doctrine attributed to Zeno of Elea, emphasizing paradoxes and concepts of motion that challenge common sense notions of space and time.

In ZH

一种归因于埃莱亚的芝诺的哲学学说，强调悖论和运动的概念，挑战对空间和时间的常识。

How to Use Zenoism in a Sentence

  • Zenoism presents a fascinating perspective on the nature of reality.
  • Many philosophers have studied Zenoism to understand its implications on metaphysics.
  • Zenoism challenges our understanding of motion and change.
  • In discussions of ancient philosophy, Zenoism is often mentioned alongside other schools of thought.
  • The paradoxes of Zenoism continue to intrigue modern philosophers.
