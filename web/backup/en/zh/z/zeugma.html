Zeugma

EN→ZH

What Does Zeugma Mean?

  • 同言复义
  • 结合修辞
  • 连用修辞

Zeugma Pronunciation

/ˈzɔɪɡmə/

Zeugma Definition

noun

Definition

A figure of speech in which a word applies to multiple parts of the sentence.

In ZH

一种修辞手法，其中一个词适用于句子的多个部分。

How to Use Zeugma in a Sentence

  • He took his hat and his leave.
  • She broke his car and his heart.
  • The runner lost his race and his pride.
  • She opened her mind and her wallet.
  • He fished for compliments and for trout.

Cultural Notes

Zeugma is often used in literature and rhetoric to create a humorous or ironic effect.

Synonyms for Zeugma

  • Zeugmatic
