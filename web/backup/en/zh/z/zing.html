Zing

EN→ZH

What Does Zing Mean?

  • 滋味
  • 活力
  • 尖锐的言辞

Zing Pronunciation

/zɪŋ/

Zing Definition

noun

Definition

A sharp or witty remark.

In ZH

尖锐或机智的评论。

How to Use Zing in a Sentence

  • Her comment had a real zing to it.
  • He always adds a bit of zing to his speeches.
  • The critic's review was filled with zing.
  • The conversation lacked any zing last night.
  • They need to inject some zing into their marketing strategy.
verb

Definition

To make a high-pitched or vibrant sound; to move quickly and energetically.

In ZH

发出尖锐或活力的声音；快速且有活力地移动。

How to Use Zing in a Sentence

  • The arrow zinged past my ear.
  • He zinged around the track in record time.
  • She loves to zing through her projects.
  • The children zinged up and down the playground slides.
  • The car zinged away from the starting line.
