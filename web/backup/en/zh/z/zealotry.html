Zealotry

Pronunciation:/ˈziːlətri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 狂热主义
  • 热心
  • 热诚

Definitions

noun

Definition

Excessive zeal or fervor for a particular cause, often leading to fanaticism.

In ZH

对某一特定事业的过度热情或狂热，常常导致盲目主义。

Examples

  • His zealotry for the cause blinded him to the potential consequences.
  • The group's zealotry was evident in their vehement protests.
  • She admired his zealotry, but worried about its implications.
  • Political zealotry can sometimes lead to violence.
  • Their zealotry was both inspiring and terrifying to outsiders.
