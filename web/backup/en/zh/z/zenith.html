Zenith

EN→ZH

What Does Zenith Mean?

  • 顶点
  • 巅峰
  • 最高潮

Zenith Pronunciation

/ˈzɛnɪθ/

Zenith Definition

noun

Definition

The highest point reached by a celestial or other object.

In ZH

天体或其他物体所达到的最高点。

How to Use Zenith in a Sentence

  • The sun was at its zenith during noon.
  • The satellite reached its zenith over the Pacific Ocean.
  • At its zenith, the empire ruled vast territories.
  • The mountain peak is considered the zenith of the trek.
  • The artist reached the zenith of his career with that masterpiece.
noun

Definition

The time at which something is most powerful or successful.

In ZH

某事物最强大或成功的时间。

How to Use Zenith in a Sentence

  • Her career was at its zenith after the release of her bestselling book.
  • The company has seen its zenith in the last few years.
  • At the zenith of the festival, the crowd was electrified.
  • The team's performance reached its zenith in the championship game.
  • He felt he was at the zenith of his powers as a negotiator.

Synonyms for Zenith

  • Zenithal
  • Zenoism
  • Zenophobic
