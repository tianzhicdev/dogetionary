Empathy

Pronunciation:/ˈɛmpəθi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 同理心
  • 共情
  • 感同身受

Definitions

noun

Definition

The ability to understand and share the feelings of another.

In ZH

理解和分享他人感受的能力。

Examples

  • She showed great empathy towards the victims of the disaster.
  • It is important for teachers to have empathy for their students' struggles.
  • Empathy allows us to connect deeply with others.
  • He felt a strong sense of empathy for his friend going through tough times.
  • Developing empathy can lead to better interpersonal relationships.
