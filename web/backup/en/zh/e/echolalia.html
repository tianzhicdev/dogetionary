Echolalia

EN→ZH

What Does Echolalia Mean?

  • 回声言语
  • 模仿言语

Echolalia Pronunciation

/ˌɛkəˈleɪliə/

Echolalia Definition

noun

Definition

The unsolicited repetition of vocalizations made by another person, often observed in individuals with autism or certain language disorders.

In ZH

在没有请求的情况下重复他人发出的声音，常见于自闭症或某些语言障碍的个体。

How to Use Echolalia in a Sentence

  • The child exhibited echolalia by repeating phrases from his favorite cartoon.
  • In therapy, we focus on reducing echolalia to improve communication skills.
  • Echolalia can sometimes be a sign of developmental disorders.
  • Some individuals use echolalia as a way to process information.
  • Understanding echolalia can help caregivers communicate better with children.
