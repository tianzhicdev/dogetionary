Ebulliently

Pronunciation:/ɪˈbʌl.jənt.li/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 热情洋溢地
  • 兴高采烈地

Definitions

adverb

Definition

In an enthusiastic and lively manner.

In ZH

以热情洋溢和生动的方式。

Examples

  • She danced ebulliently at the party.
  • The children laughed ebulliently as they played outside.
  • He spoke ebulliently about his new job.
  • They celebrated ebulliently after winning the championship.
  • The audience responded ebulliently to the performer on stage.
