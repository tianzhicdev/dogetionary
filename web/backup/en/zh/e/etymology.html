Etymology

Pronunciation:/ˌɛtɪˈmɒlədʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 词源
  • 词源学

Definitions

noun

Definition

the study of the origin of words and their changes in structure and significance over time

In ZH

研究词语的起源及其结构和意义随时间变化的学科

Examples

  • Etymology can reveal fascinating insights into the history of a language.
  • Studying etymology helps us understand how words evolve.
  • The etymology of 'salary' comes from the Latin word for salt.
  • Many people are surprised by the etymology of common words.
  • Etymology is a useful tool in understanding literary texts.
