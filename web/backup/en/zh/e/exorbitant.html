Exorbitant

EN→ZH

What Does Exorbitant Mean?

  • 过高的
  • 过分的
  • 离谱的

Exorbitant Pronunciation

/ɪɡˈzɔːrbɪtənt/

Exorbitant Definition

adjective

Definition

Unreasonably high or excessive, especially in terms of price or amount.

In ZH

不合理的高或过多，特别是在价格或数量上。

How to Use Exorbitant in a Sentence

  • The cost of living in the city is exorbitant.
  • She was shocked by the exorbitant prices at the luxury boutique.
  • Many students struggle with exorbitant tuition fees.
  • The hotel charged exorbitant rates for a single night.
  • He received an exorbitant bill after the emergency room visit.

Synonyms for Exorbitant

  • Exonerate
  • Exorbitance
  • Exordium
