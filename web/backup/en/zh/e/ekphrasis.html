Ekphrasis

Pronunciation:/ɛkˈfræsɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 描写
  • 表述
  • 栩栩如生的描述

Definitions

noun

Definition

A literary description of or commentary on a visual work of art.

In ZH

对视觉艺术作品的文学描写或评论。

Examples

  • The poem serves as an ekphrasis of the painting.
  • In her essay, she provides an ekphrasis of the sculpture.
  • The ekphrasis vividly brings the artwork to life through words.
  • He wrote an ekphrasis that transformed the painting into a narrative.
  • Ekphrasis often explores the emotional impact of the visual art.
