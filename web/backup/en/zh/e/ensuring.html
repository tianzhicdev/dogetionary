Ensuring

EN→ZH

What Does Ensuring Mean?

  • 确保
  • 保证
  • 使...成为确定

Ensuring Pronunciation

ɪnˈʃʊərɪŋ

Ensuring Definition

verb

Definition

To make certain that something will occur or be the case.

In ZH

确保某事会发生或是这样的情况。

How to Use Ensuring in a Sentence

  • The manager is ensuring that all deadlines are met.
  • By checking the weather forecast, she is ensuring a safe trip for her family.
  • The company is ensuring quality by performing regular inspections.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, ensuring something is often associated with being responsible and thorough.

gerund

Definition

The act of making sure or guaranteeing that specific conditions are fulfilled.

In ZH

确保特定条件得到满足的行为。

How to Use Ensuring in a Sentence

  • Ensuring customer satisfaction is vital for business success.
  • They are focusing on ensuring compliance with regulations.
  • Ensuring a healthy diet contributes to overall well-being.

Cultural Notes

In professional contexts, ensuring is often linked to accountability and can be seen as a key component of effective management.
