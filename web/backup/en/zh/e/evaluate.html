Evaluate

Pronunciation:/ɪˈvælueɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 评估
  • 评价
  • 估计

Definitions

verb

Definition

to assess or appraise the value, quality, or importance of something

In ZH

评估某物的价值、质量或重要性

Examples

  • It is important to evaluate the risks before making a decision.
  • Teachers need to evaluate students' performance fairly.
  • The committee will evaluate the proposals submitted.
  • I try to evaluate my progress at the end of each month.
  • They will evaluate the effectiveness of the new policy.
