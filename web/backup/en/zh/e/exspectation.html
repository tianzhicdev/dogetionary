Exspectation

What Does Exspectation Mean?

Exspectation Pronunciation

/ˌɛkspɛkˈteɪʃən/

Exspectation Definition

noun

Definition

the state of looking forward to something or believing that something will happen

In ZH

期待某事或相信某事将要发生的状态

How to Use Exspectation in a Sentence

  • He waited in exspectation of her arrival.
  • Their exspectation for the exam results was very high.
  • She felt a sense of exspectation before the big game.
  • There was a palpable exspectation in the crowd before the concert began.
  • His exspectation turned out to be correct when she won the award.
