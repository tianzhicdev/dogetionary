Exasperate

Pronunciation:/ɪɡˈzæspəˌreɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 使恼怒
  • 激怒
  • 使烦恼

Definitions

verb

Definition

To irritate intensely; infuriate.

In ZH

使极度恼怒；激怒。

Examples

  • The persistent delays in the project began to exasperate the team members.
  • She was exasperated by his constant interruptions during the meeting.
  • His lack of respect for others can exasperate even the calmest individuals.
  • I tried to explain the situation, but it only seemed to exasperate him further.
  • Trying to find a parking spot in the crowded city can exasperate even the most patient drivers.
