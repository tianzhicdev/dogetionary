Evidense

EN→ZH

What Does Evidense Mean?

  • 证据
  • 根据
  • 证明

Evidense Pronunciation

ˈɛvɪdəns

Evidense Definition

noun

Definition

Information or facts that help to prove something is true or false.

In ZH

帮助证明某事是真还是假的信息或事实。

How to Use Evidense in a Sentence

  • The evidence showed that he was at the scene of the crime.
  • We need more evidence to support our claims.
  • She provided evidence that contradicted his story.

Cultural Notes

In legal contexts, evidence is critical for proving cases in court. Different types of evidence include physical, testimonial, and circumstantial evidence.

verb

Definition

To show or prove something by providing evidence.

In ZH

通过提供证据展示或证明某事。

How to Use Evidense in a Sentence

  • The scientist evidenced his theory through numerous experiments.
  • Her actions evidenced her commitment to the project.
  • The data collected from the survey evidenced the need for change.

Cultural Notes

This usage is less common and often appears in formal contexts, such as academic writing or legal documents.

Synonyms for Evidense

  • Evident
  • Eviscerate
