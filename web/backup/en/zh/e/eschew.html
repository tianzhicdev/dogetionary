Eschew

Pronunciation:/ɪsˈtʃuː/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 避开
  • 避免
  • 回避

Definitions

verb

Definition

To deliberately avoid using, engaging in, or adopting something.

In ZH

故意避免使用、参与或采用某物。

Examples

  • He decided to eschew processed foods for a healthier diet.
  • They eschew social media, preferring face-to-face communication.
  • The organization encourages members to eschew any form of discrimination.
  • She eschewed luxury and chose to live a minimalist lifestyle.
  • In his speeches, he often eschews technical jargon to communicate effectively.
