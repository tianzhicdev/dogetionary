Enhance

Pronunciation:/ɪnˈhæns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 增强
  • 提高
  • 改善

Definitions

verb

Definition

to improve the quality, value, or extent of something

In ZH

提高某物的质量、价值或范围

Examples

  • The new software will enhance productivity in the workplace.
  • Her skills were enhanced by additional training.
  • We need to enhance the user experience on our website.
  • The program aims to enhance community engagement.
  • He took a course to enhance his knowledge of the subject.
