Exemplar

EN→ZH

What Does Exemplar Mean?

  • 典范
  • 例子
  • 榜样

Exemplar Pronunciation

/ɪɡˈzɛm.plər/

Exemplar Definition

noun

Definition

a person or thing serving as a typical example or excellent model.

In ZH

作为典型例子或优秀模范的人或事物。

How to Use Exemplar in a Sentence

  • She is an exemplar of dedication and hard work.
  • The book is an exemplar of fine literature.
  • This model serves as an exemplar for future designs.
  • The student was chosen as an exemplar for her outstanding performance.
  • He is often cited as an exemplar of good leadership.

Synonyms for Exemplar

  • Execrable
  • Exegesis
  • Exegetical
  • Exemplary
  • Exempt
