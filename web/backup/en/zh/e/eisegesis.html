Eisegesis

EN→ZH

What Does Eisegesis Mean?

  • 解释偏见
  • 自我解读

Eisegesis Pronunciation

/ˌaɪsɪˈdʒiːsɪs/

Eisegesis Definition

noun

Definition

The process of interpreting a text by reading one's own biases or preconceived notions into it.

In ZH

通过将个人的偏见或先入之见投射到文本中来解释文本的过程。

How to Use Eisegesis in a Sentence

  • His eisegesis of the scripture led to a distorted understanding.
  • Many critics argue that eisegesis undermines true interpretation of literature.
  • The professor warned against eisegesis in academic writing.
  • Eisegesis can often lead to subjective conclusions that lack textual support.
  • In debates, eisegesis can be a major flaw in argumentation.
