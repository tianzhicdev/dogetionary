Erudite

Pronunciation:/ˈɛr.ʊˌdaɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 博学的
  • 有学问的
  • 学识渊博的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having or showing great knowledge or learning; scholarly

In ZH

拥有或展示出伟大的知识或学问；学术性的

Examples

  • The professor was known for his erudite lectures that captivated the students.
  • Her erudite understanding of the subject made her a valuable member of the research team.
  • He read extensively and became an erudite critic of modern literature.
  • The discussion ranged from philosophy to science, showcasing the erudite nature of the participants.
  • Despite his erudite demeanor, he was approachable and eager to share his knowledge.
