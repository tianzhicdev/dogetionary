Execrable

EN→ZH

What Does Execrable Mean?

  • 可恶的
  • 极坏的
  • 令人厌恶的

Execrable Pronunciation

/ˈɛksɪkrəbl/

Execrable Definition

adjective

Definition

deserving to be hated; very bad or unpleasant

In ZH

值得被仇恨的；非常糟糕或令人不快的

How to Use Execrable in a Sentence

  • The movie was so execrable that I couldn't finish watching it.
  • His execrable behavior at the party shocked everyone.
  • The report contained execrable grammar and spelling mistakes.
  • They served an execrable meal that I wouldn't recommend to anyone.
  • The team's performance was execrable, leading to their defeat.
