Escarpment

Pronunciation:/ɪsˈkɑːrp.mənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 陡坡
  • 悬崖
  • 断崖

Definitions

noun

Definition

A steep slope or cliff that separates two relatively level areas of differing elevations.

In ZH

一个陡峭的坡或悬崖，将两个相对水平的不同高度区域分开。

Examples

  • The escarpment provided a breathtaking view of the valley below.
  • Many animals make their homes along the escarpment.
  • The ancient ruins were built on top of an escarpment.
  • Hiking along the escarpment is a popular activity for tourists.
  • The town was situated at the edge of a great escarpment.
