Eloquence

EN→ZH

What Does Eloquence Mean?

  • 口才
  • 雄辩
  • 流畅

Eloquence Pronunciation

/ˈɛləkwəns/

Eloquence Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being persuasive and articulate in speaking or writing.

In ZH

在口语或写作中具有说服力和表达力的特质。

How to Use Eloquence in a Sentence

  • Her eloquence moved the audience to tears.
  • He spoke with such eloquence that everyone listened intently.
  • The politician was known for his eloquence and ability to sway public opinion.
  • In her essay, she demonstrated great eloquence and clarity of thought.
  • The speaker's eloquence was matched only by the passion of his message.

Synonyms for Eloquence

  • Eloquent
