Eldritch

Pronunciation:/ˈɛldrɪtʃ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 怪异的
  • 阴森的
  • 诡异的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Weird or sinister; eerie.

In ZH

奇怪的或不吉利的；毛骨悚然的。

Examples

  • The forest had an eldritch appearance under the pale moonlight.
  • He spoke in an eldritch tone that sent shivers down my spine.
  • The eldritch creatures of the night haunted my dreams.
  • Her eldritch laughter echoed in the dark alley.
  • They discovered an eldritch artifact that defied explanation.

Cultural Notes

The term 'eldritch' is often associated with horror literature, particularly the works of H.P. Lovecraft, where it is used to describe otherworldly and unsettling phenomena.
