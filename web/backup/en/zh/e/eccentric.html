Eccentric

EN→ZH

What Does Eccentric Mean?

  • 古怪的
  • 偏心的
  • 离心的

Eccentric Pronunciation

/ɪkˈsɛntrɪk/

Eccentric Definition

adjective

Definition

Unconventional and slightly strange

In ZH

不传统且稍微奇怪

How to Use Eccentric in a Sentence

  • Her eccentric behavior often drew attention at parties.
  • He wore an eccentric outfit that made him stand out.
  • The artist had an eccentric style that was unlike anyone else's.
  • Living in an eccentric house made her feel unique.
  • They loved to host eccentric gatherings with unusual themes.
noun

Definition

A person who has unconventional and strange views or behaviors

In ZH

持有不传统且奇怪观点或行为的人

How to Use Eccentric in a Sentence

  • He is considered an eccentric in the world of science.
  • The town is famous for its eccentric residents.
  • As an eccentric, she often challenged societal norms.
  • Many inventors are seen as eccentrics because of their unconventional ideas.
  • Eccentrics often have a unique perspective on life.

Synonyms for Eccentric

  • Eccentricity
