Emphysema

EN→ZH

What Does Emphysema Mean?

  • 肺气肿
  • 气肿

Emphysema Pronunciation

ˌem-fə-ˈzē-mə

Emphysema Definition

noun

Definition

A lung condition characterized by the destruction of the air sacs (alveoli), leading to breathing difficulties.

In ZH

一种肺部疾病，特征是肺泡（气囊）被破坏，导致呼吸困难。

How to Use Emphysema in a Sentence

  • Emphysema makes it difficult for patients to exhale air from their lungs.
  • Many smokers develop emphysema as a result of long-term smoking.
  • Doctors often use imaging tests to diagnose emphysema.

Cultural Notes

Emphysema is commonly associated with smoking; understanding this condition can raise awareness about lung health.

adjective

Definition

Relating to or having emphysema; describing a person suffering from this condition.

In ZH

与肺气肿相关的；描述患有这种疾病的人。

How to Use Emphysema in a Sentence

  • The emphysema patient required supplemental oxygen while resting.
  • Emphysematous changes in the lungs can be seen on a chest X-ray.
  • It’s crucial for emphysematous individuals to avoid pollutants.

Cultural Notes

This adjective is used in medical contexts to describe symptoms or conditions related to emphysema.
