Euphony

Pronunciation:/ˈjuːfəni/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 和谐的声音
  • 悦耳的音调

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being pleasing to the ear, especially through a harmonious combination of words.

In ZH

悦耳的声音质量，特别是通过和谐的词语组合来体现的。

Examples

  • The poem was noted for its euphony.
  • She spoke in a tone that was pure euphony.
  • The composer aimed to create a piece filled with euphony.
  • Euphony can enhance the beauty of a literary work.
  • The euphony of the song captivated the audience.
