Efface

EN→ZH

What Does Efface Mean?

  • 抹去
  • 消除
  • 除去

Efface Pronunciation

/ɪˈfeɪs/

Efface Definition

verb

Definition

to wipe out or erase something

In ZH

抹去或削除某物

How to Use Efface in a Sentence

  • She tried to efface the memories of her past mistakes.
  • The heavy rain will efface the footprints on the ground.
  • He used a cloth to efface the dirt from the surface.
  • The company aims to efface any doubts about its product quality.
  • Time will efface all the bad experiences we went through.

Synonyms for Efface

  • Effacement
  • Effervescence
  • Effervescent
  • Effete
  • Efficacious
