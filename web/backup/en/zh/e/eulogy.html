Eulogy

EN→ZH

What Does Eulogy Mean?

  • 悼词
  • 颂词
  • 追悼辞

Eulogy Pronunciation

/ˈjuː.lə.dʒi/

Eulogy Definition

noun

Definition

A speech or piece of writing that praises someone highly, typically someone who has just died.

In ZH

一种演讲或写作作品，通常赞美某人，尤其是刚去世的人。

How to Use Eulogy in a Sentence

  • At the funeral, the priest delivered a heartfelt eulogy for the deceased.
  • She wrote a touching eulogy that captured his spirit and accomplishments.
  • During the memorial service, friends shared eulogies filled with fond memories.
  • The eulogy was a tribute to her life and the impact she had on others.
  • Many attendees were moved to tears by the emotional eulogy.
