Ethnocentrism

Pronunciation:/ˌɛθ.noʊˈsɛn.trɪ.zəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 民族中心主义
  • 民族中心主义

Definitions

noun

Definition

The belief in the superiority of one's own ethnic group, leading to a tendency to view other cultures from the perspective of one's own culture.

In ZH

一种认为自己民族优越的信念，导致从自身文化的角度看待其他文化的倾向。

Examples

  • Ethnocentrism can lead to misunderstandings between different cultures.
  • Many conflicts arise from ethnocentrism and the inability to appreciate diverse perspectives.
  • In our globalized world, ethnocentrism poses challenges for international relations.
  • Ethnocentrism often manifests in the way people judge foreign customs as inferior.
  • Education can play a crucial role in reducing ethnocentrism among students.

Cultural Notes

Ethnocentrism is often discussed in sociology and cultural studies, highlighting the importance of cultural relativism.
