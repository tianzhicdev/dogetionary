Exsanguinate

Pronunciation:/ˌɛks.sæŋ.ɡwɪˈneɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 放血
  • 失血
  • 抽血

Definitions

verb

Definition

to drain of blood; to cause to lose blood, especially to an extreme extent.

In ZH

放血；使失去大量血液，特别是在极端程度上。

Examples

  • The surgeon had to exsanguinate the patient during the operation.
  • During ancient times, doctors would exsanguinate patients as a treatment for various ailments.
  • The team worked quickly to exsanguinate the animal before processing its meat.
  • In severe cases of trauma, a person may exsanguinate and require immediate medical attention.
  • The vampire folklore often features characters who exsanguinate their victims.
