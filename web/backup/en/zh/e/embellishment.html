Embellishment

EN→ZH

What Does Embellishment Mean?

  • 装饰
  • 修饰
  • 润饰

Embellishment Pronunciation

/ɪmˈbɛlɪʃmənt/

Embellishment Definition

noun

Definition

a decorative detail or feature added to something to make it more attractive.

In ZH

增加到某物上的装饰性细节或特征，使其更具吸引力。

How to Use Embellishment in a Sentence

  • The dress was beautiful, but the embellishment on it made it truly exceptional.
  • He described the event with such embellishment that it became hard to believe.
  • The artist added embellishments to the painting to enhance its beauty.
  • In literature, an embellishment can provide depth to the narrative.
  • The cake had an embellishment of flowers that made it look stunning.

Synonyms for Embellishment

  • Embellish
  • Embellishing
  • Embiggen
  • Embrace
