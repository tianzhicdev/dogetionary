Ethnocentric

EN→ZH

What Does Ethnocentric Mean?

  • 民族中心主义的
  • 以民族为中心的
  • 种族中心的

Ethnocentric Pronunciation

/ˌɛθ.noʊˈsɛn.trɪk/

Ethnocentric Definition

adjective

Definition

Evaluating other cultures according to the standards of one's own culture.

In ZH

根据自己文化的标准来评估其他文化。

How to Use Ethnocentric in a Sentence

  • Many historians criticize ethnocentric views of history.
  • His ethnocentric attitude made it difficult for him to appreciate different cultures.
  • Ethnocentric beliefs can lead to misunderstandings between groups.
  • In a globalized world, ethnocentric perspectives can be limiting.
  • Schools should promote multicultural education to counteract ethnocentric thinking.

Synonyms for Ethnocentric

  • Ethereal
  • Etherealism
  • Etherial
  • Ethicality
