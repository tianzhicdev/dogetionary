Effervescence

EN→ZH

What Does Effervescence Mean?

  • 气泡
  • 冒泡
  • 活力

Effervescence Pronunciation

/ˌɛf.ərˈvɛs.əns/

Effervescence Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being bubbly or fizzy; the escape of gas from a liquid, resulting in bubbles.

In ZH

泡沫的特性；气体从液体中逸出，形成气泡的现象。

How to Use Effervescence in a Sentence

  • The effervescence of the soda fascinated the children.
  • When the tablet dissolved in water, it produced a delightful effervescence.
  • The effervescence of the champagne added to the celebration.
  • She loved the effervescence of the bath bombs in her tub.
  • Effervescence is often a sign of freshness in sparkling wines.

Synonyms for Effervescence

  • Effacement
  • Effervescent
  • Effete
  • Efficacious
