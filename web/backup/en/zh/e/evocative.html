Evocative

EN→ZH

What Does Evocative Mean?

  • 唤起的
  • 引起回忆的
  • 令人回忆的

Evocative Pronunciation

/ɪˈvɒkətɪv/

Evocative Definition

adjective

Definition

bringing strong images, memories, or feelings to mind

In ZH

唤起强烈的形象、记忆或情感的

How to Use Evocative in a Sentence

  • The movie had an evocative soundtrack that enhanced the emotional experience.
  • Her poetry is known for its evocative imagery.
  • The painting was so evocative that it brought tears to my eyes.
  • The fragrance was evocative of my childhood home.
  • He delivered an evocative speech that inspired everyone present.
