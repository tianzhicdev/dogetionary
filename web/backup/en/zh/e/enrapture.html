Enrapture

EN→ZH

What Does Enrapture Mean?

  • 使狂喜
  • 使陶醉

Enrapture Pronunciation

/ɪnˈræp.tʃər/

Enrapture Definition

verb

Definition

to give intense pleasure or joy to; to fill with delight

In ZH

使极度愉快或快乐；使陶醉

How to Use Enrapture in a Sentence

  • The beautiful music seemed to enrapture the entire audience.
  • She was enraptured by the stunning scenery during her hike.
  • His performance enraptured the judges and earned him first place.
  • The novel enraptured readers with its captivating story.
  • They were enraptured by the artist's talent at the gallery.
