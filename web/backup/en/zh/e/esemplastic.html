Esemplastic

Pronunciation:/ɪˈzɛm.plæs.tɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 综合的
  • 合成的
  • 塑造的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having the power to shape various elements into a coherent whole.

In ZH

具有将各种元素塑造为一致整体的能力。

Examples

  • The thinker viewed nature as esemplastic, creating unity from diversity.
  • In literature, an esemplastic approach can forge connections among disparate themes.
  • The architect's esemplastic vision merged functionality with beauty.
