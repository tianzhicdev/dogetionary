Extraneous

Pronunciation:/ɪkˈstreɪniəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 外来的
  • 多余的
  • 无关的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

irrelevant or unrelated to the subject being dealt with

In ZH

与正在处理的主题无关或无关的

Examples

  • The report was filled with extraneous information that distracted from the main points.
  • She decided to remove extraneous details from her presentation to keep it concise.
  • His extraneous comments during the meeting were unhelpful.
  • The extraneous noise made it difficult to concentrate on the lecture.
  • They filtered out the extraneous data to find the useful insights.
