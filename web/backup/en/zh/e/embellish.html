Embellish

Pronunciation:/ɪmˈbɛl.ɪʃ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 装饰
  • 美化
  • 修饰

Definitions

verb

Definition

To make (something) more attractive by the addition of decorative details or features.

In ZH

通过添加装饰细节或特征来使（某物）更具吸引力。

Examples

  • She decided to embellish her dress with sequins.
  • The author chose to embellish the story with vivid descriptions.
  • He tends to embellish his achievements when talking about his career.
  • They embellished the event with beautiful decorations.
  • The report was embellished with colorful graphs and charts.
