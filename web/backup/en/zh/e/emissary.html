Emissary

EN→ZH

What Does Emissary Mean?

  • 使者
  • 代表
  • 外交使节

Emissary Pronunciation

/ˈɛmɪˌsɛri/

Emissary Definition

noun

Definition

A person sent on a special mission, often as a diplomatic representative.

In ZH

被派遣进行特殊任务的人，通常作为外交代表。

How to Use Emissary in a Sentence

  • The king sent an emissary to negotiate peace with the opposing side.
  • She acted as an emissary for the organization during the international conference.
  • The emissary delivered a message from the president to the leaders of the other countries.
  • In ancient times, emissaries were often tasked with securing alliances between tribes.
  • The emissary's role was crucial in maintaining diplomatic relations.
