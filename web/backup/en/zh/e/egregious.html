Egregious

EN→ZH

What Does Egregious Mean?

  • 极坏的
  • 过分的
  • 极端的

Egregious Pronunciation

/ɪˈɡriːdʒəs/

Egregious Definition

adjective

Definition

Outstandingly bad; shocking.

In ZH

极坏的；令人震惊的。

How to Use Egregious in a Sentence

  • The company's egregious disregard for the environment led to severe penalties.
  • His egregious behavior at the party embarrassed everyone.
  • We cannot ignore the egregious errors in the report.
  • The politician's egregious lies were exposed by the media.
  • It was egregious that they failed to address the safety concerns.

Synonyms for Egregious

  • Egregiously
  • Egregiousness
  • Egregor
  • Egrigious
