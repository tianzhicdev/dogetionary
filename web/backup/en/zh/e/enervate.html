Enervate

Pronunciation:/ˈɛn.ɚ.veɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 使无力
  • 使疲惫
  • 削弱

Definitions

verb

Definition

to weaken or drain of energy

In ZH

使虚弱或消耗能量

Examples

  • The long meeting will enervate everyone.
  • She felt enervated after a day of work.
  • The summer heat seemed to enervate the entire town.
  • His constant complaints began to enervate his friends.
  • The exhausting hike enervated the group.
  • Lack of sleep can enervate a person significantly.
