Ecliptic

Pronunciation:/ɪˈklɪptɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 黄道
  • 黄道带

Definitions

noun

Definition

The great circle on the celestial sphere that is the apparent path of the sun among the stars.

In ZH

在天球上的大圆，太阳在星星之间的表观路径。

Examples

  • The ecliptic is tilted about 23.5 degrees from the celestial equator.
  • During the equinoxes, the sun crosses the ecliptic.
  • Astrologers often use the ecliptic as a reference for celestial bodies.
  • The zodiac signs are based on the divisions of the ecliptic.
  • Understanding the ecliptic is important for astronomy.
