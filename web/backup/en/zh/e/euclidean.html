Euclidean

EN→ZH

What Does Euclidean Mean?

  • 欧几里得的
  • 欧几里得几何的

Euclidean Pronunciation

/juːˈklɪdiən/

Euclidean Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or denoting the system of geometry based on the work of Euclid, which is characterized by the acceptance of certain axioms and postulates.

In ZH

与或表示基于欧几里得的几何系统，特征是接受某些公理和假设。

How to Use Euclidean in a Sentence

  • Euclidean geometry deals with flat surfaces.
  • In Euclidean space, the shortest distance between two points is a straight line.
  • The principles of Euclidean geometry were established in ancient Greece.
  • Many mathematical concepts are rooted in Euclidean geometry.
  • The classroom taught Euclidean principles for constructing shapes.
