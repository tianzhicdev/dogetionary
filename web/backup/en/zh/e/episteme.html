Episteme

What Does Episteme Mean?

  • 知识体系
  • 认识论
  • 知识】

Episteme Pronunciation

/ˌɛpɪˈstɛmɪ/

Episteme Definition

noun

Definition

A term used in philosophy to describe knowledge or justified belief, especially in relation to the sciences and systems of thought.

In ZH

在哲学中用于描述知识或正当信念，特别是与科学和思维体系相关的术语。

How to Use Episteme in a Sentence

  • The shift in episteme marks a significant change in societal views of truth.
  • In different historical contexts, various episteme can emerge.
  • Philosophers often debate the nature of episteme and its implications for knowledge.
