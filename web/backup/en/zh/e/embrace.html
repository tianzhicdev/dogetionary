Embrace

EN→ZH

What Does Embrace Mean?

  • 拥抱
  • 抱住

Embrace Pronunciation

ɪmˈbreɪs

Embrace Definition

verb

Definition

To hold someone closely in your arms, especially as a sign of affection.

In ZH

用手臂紧紧抱住某人，尤其是作为一种亲密的标志。

How to Use Embrace in a Sentence

  • When I saw my friend after a year, I couldn't help but embrace her tightly, like a bear who just woke up from hibernation!
  • After the long debate, the politicians finally decided to embrace their differences—by agreeing to disagree over lunch.
  • The cat embraced its new bed, rolling around like it had just won the lottery!

Cultural Notes

Embracing is a common gesture in Western cultures, often signifying warmth, comfort, or love.

noun

Definition

An act of holding someone close in your arms, or a gesture of acceptance.

In ZH

紧紧抱住某人的行为，或接受的一种姿态。

How to Use Embrace in a Sentence

  • During the festival, the embrace of friends felt as warm as a freshly baked pie—deliciously satisfying!
  • His embrace of the new technology was like a grandma using a smartphone—cautious but eventually adorable!
  • In the middle of the crowded room, their embrace was as necessary as a slice of cake at a birthday party.

Cultural Notes

In some cultures, an embrace may be more formal or reserved, while in others it’s a common display of affection.
