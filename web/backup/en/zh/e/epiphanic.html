Epiphanic

EN→ZH

What Does Epiphanic Mean?

  • 显现的
  • 领悟的

Epiphanic Pronunciation

/ˌɛpɪˈfænɪk/

Epiphanic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or denoting an epiphany, often marked by sudden realization or insight.

In ZH

与显现相关，常常标志着突然的领悟或洞察。

How to Use Epiphanic in a Sentence

  • After years of searching, she had an epiphanic moment that changed her perspective.
  • His epiphanic insight into the meaning of life inspired his new book.
  • The film culminates in an epiphanic scene that reveals the protagonist's true self.
  • Many artists describe their creative process as one filled with epiphanic experiences.
  • The workshop was designed to lead participants to epiphanic breakthroughs in their thinking.

Synonyms for Epiphanic

  • Epicene
  • Epicurean
  • Epicureanism
  • Epigraph
  • Epiphany
