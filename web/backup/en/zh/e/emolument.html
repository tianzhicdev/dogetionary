Emolument

EN→ZH

What Does Emolument Mean?

  • 报酬
  • 津贴
  • 补贴

Emolument Pronunciation

/ɪˈmɒljʊmənt/

Emolument Definition

noun

Definition

A salary, fee, or profit derived from employment or office.

In ZH

从就业或公职中获得的薪水、费用或利润。

How to Use Emolument in a Sentence

  • The president receives a considerable emolument for his services.
  • City officials earn emoluments that are often scrutinized by the public.
  • Emoluments from his consulting work made him quite wealthy.
  • The emolument attached to the position was very attractive to candidates.
  • She was concerned about the legality of the emolument she would receive.

Synonyms for Emolument

  • Emollient
