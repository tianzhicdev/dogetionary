Elliptical

EN→ZH

What Does Elliptical Mean?

  • 椭圆的
  • 椭圆形的

Elliptical Pronunciation

/ɪˈlɪptɪkəl/

Elliptical Definition

adjective

Definition

Having the shape of an ellipse; oval.

In ZH

呈椭圆形的；椭圆的。

How to Use Elliptical in a Sentence

  • The garden was designed in an elliptical shape.
  • The elliptical path around the park is popular for jogging.
  • She drew an elliptical figure on the board.
  • The satellite's orbit is elliptical rather than circular.
  • They sat on an elliptical bench in the center of the plaza.
noun

Definition

A type of exercise machine that simulates walking or running without causing excessive strain on joints.

In ZH

一种模拟行走或跑步的健身器械，不会对关节造成过大压力。

How to Use Elliptical in a Sentence

  • I prefer using the elliptical at the gym for my cardio workout.
  • The new elliptical machine has various resistance levels.
  • She enjoys watching TV while using the elliptical.
  • Using an elliptical is great for a low-impact workout.
  • He added an elliptical to his home gym for convenience.

Synonyms for Elliptical
