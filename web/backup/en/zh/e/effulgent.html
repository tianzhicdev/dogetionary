Effulgent

EN→ZH

What Does Effulgent Mean?

  • 光辉的
  • 灿烂的
  • 辉煌的

Effulgent Pronunciation

/ɪˈfʌldʒənt/

Effulgent Definition

adjective

Definition

shining brightly; radiant.

In ZH

辉煌的；发光的。

How to Use Effulgent in a Sentence

  • The sun was effulgent, bathing the entire valley in golden light.
  • Her effulgent smile lit up the room and made everyone feel welcome.
  • The effulgent beauty of the starry night sky took my breath away.
  • He wore an effulgent suit that drew everyone's attention at the party.
  • The garden was effulgent with blossoms, attracting many bees and butterflies.

Synonyms for Effulgent

  • Efface
  • Effacement
  • Effervescence
  • Effervescent
  • Effete
