Elucidation

EN→ZH

What Does Elucidation Mean?

  • 阐明
  • 解释
  • 说明

Elucidation Pronunciation

/ɪˌluːsɪˈdeɪʃən/

Elucidation Definition

noun

Definition

The act of making something clear or easy to understand.

In ZH

使某事变得清晰或容易理解的行为。

How to Use Elucidation in a Sentence

  • The elucidation of the theory was essential for the students.
  • He provided an elucidation of the complex topic.
  • The author's elucidation of the characters was profound.
  • In the meeting, we required an elucidation of the proposed changes.
  • The scientist's elucidation helped the public understand the research.

Synonyms for Elucidation

  • Elucidate
