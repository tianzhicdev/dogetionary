Expiation

EN→ZH

What Does Expiation Mean?

  • 赎罪
  • 补偿

Expiation Pronunciation

/ɛkˌspɪˈeɪʃən/

Expiation Definition

noun

Definition

The act of making amends or reparation for guilt or wrongdoing.

In ZH

弥补或赔偿罪行或错误的行为。

How to Use Expiation in a Sentence

  • His expiation for his crime involved community service.
  • The ancient ritual served as an expiation for the sins of the people.
  • She sought expiation through charitable donations.
  • Many believe that prayer can be an act of expiation.
  • He felt that confessing was a necessary expiation for his past mistakes.

Synonyms for Expiation

  • Expedite
  • Expeditious
  • Exploit
  • Exploration
