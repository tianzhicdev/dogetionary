Exemplary

EN→ZH

What Does Exemplary Mean?

  • 典范的
  • 模范的
  • 楷模的

Exemplary Pronunciation

/ɪɡˈzɛm.plə.ri/

Exemplary Definition

adjective

Definition

Serving as a desirable model; representing the best of its kind.

In ZH

作为一个理想的榜样；代表其种类中的最佳。

How to Use Exemplary in a Sentence

  • Her exemplary behavior has inspired many of her peers.
  • The teacher provided an exemplary project that all students could follow.
  • His exemplary service in the community earned him a prestigious award.
  • The company is known for its exemplary customer service.
  • They set an exemplary example for future generations.
