Ephemeralness

EN→ZH

What Does Ephemeralness Mean?

  • 短暂性
  • 瞬息万变
  • 无常性

Ephemeralness Pronunciation

/ɪˈfɛm.ər.əl.nəs/

Ephemeralness Definition

noun

Definition

the quality of lasting for a very short time

In ZH

持续时间极短的特性

How to Use Ephemeralness in a Sentence

  • The ephemeralness of beauty can be a source of inspiration for many artists.
  • They appreciated the ephemeralness of the sunset, knowing it would soon be gone.
  • In today's world, the ephemeralness of social media trends is astonishing.
  • She captured the ephemeralness of youth in her photography.
  • The flowers, known for their ephemeralness, bloom for only a few days every spring.

Synonyms for Ephemeralness

  • Ephemeral
  • Ephemeralization
