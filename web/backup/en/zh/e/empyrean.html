Empyrean

Pronunciation:/ɛmˈpɪəriən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 天上的
  • 极乐世界
  • 天界

Definitions

noun

Definition

the highest heaven or celestial sphere in ancient cosmology, often considered a place of pure light and divine presence.

In ZH

古代宇宙学中，最高的天国或天体，通常被认为是纯光和神圣存在的地方。

Examples

  • The poets described the empyrean as a realm of eternal bliss.
  • In ancient texts, the empyrean is depicted as a place where the souls of the virtuous reside.
  • She often dreams of reaching the empyrean, far beyond earthly concerns.
  • The artist painted a scene of the empyrean filled with radiant light.
  • According to the philosophy of Plato, the empyrean represents the ultimate truth.
