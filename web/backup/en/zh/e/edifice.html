Edifice

EN→ZH

What Does Edifice Mean?

  • 建筑物
  • 大厦
  • 大建筑

Edifice Pronunciation

/ˈɛdɪfɪs/

Edifice Definition

noun

Definition

A large, imposing building.

In ZH

一座大型而宏伟的建筑物。

How to Use Edifice in a Sentence

  • The ancient edifice stood the test of time.
  • They visited the historic edifice during their trip.
  • The city is known for its grand edifice overlooking the river.
  • Renovations of the old edifice are set to begin next year.
  • The edifice was constructed in the 19th century.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, the term 'edifice' often implies not just size, but also historical significance.

Synonyms for Edifice

  • Edification
