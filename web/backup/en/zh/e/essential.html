Essential

EN→ZH

What Does Essential Mean?

  • 必要的
  • 本质的
  • 精华的

Essential Pronunciation

/ɪˈsɛnʃəl/

Essential Definition

adjective

Definition

absolutely necessary; extremely important

In ZH

绝对必要的；极为重要的

How to Use Essential in a Sentence

  • Water is essential for life.
  • It is essential to have a good education.
  • Vitamins are essential for maintaining health.
  • Clear communication is essential in a teamwork environment.
  • Understanding the basics is essential before moving on to advanced topics.
noun

Definition

a thing that is absolutely necessary

In ZH

绝对必要的东西

How to Use Essential in a Sentence

  • Food and water are essentials for survival.
  • A good pair of shoes is one of the essentials for hiking.
  • In winter, warm clothing is an essential.
  • First aid kits are essentials for outdoor adventures.
  • A reliable internet connection is an essential in today's work environment.
