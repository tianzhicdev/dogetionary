Egregiously

Pronunciation:/ɪˈɡriːdʒəsli/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 极其地
  • 异乎寻常地
  • 显著地

Definitions

adverb

Definition

In an outstandingly bad or shocking manner.

In ZH

以极其糟糕或令人震惊的方式。

Examples

  • The company egregiously mismanaged its funds.
  • He egregiously violated the terms of the agreement.
  • Her behavior was egregiously rude at the dinner.
  • The report egregiously underestimated the project's costs.
  • They egregiously ignored the safety regulations.
