Emollient

EN→ZH

What Does Emollient Mean?

  • 润肤剂
  • 软化剂
  • 护肤剂

Emollient Pronunciation

/ɪˈmɒl.i.ənt/

Emollient Definition

noun

Definition

A substance that has a soothing and softening effect, often used in skin care products.

In ZH

具有舒缓和柔软效果的物质，通常用于护肤产品中。

How to Use Emollient in a Sentence

  • The lotion contains an emollient to help hydrate dry skin.
  • She applied an emollient cream after her shower to keep her skin soft.
  • Emollient products are often recommended for people with eczema.
  • The dermatologist suggested an emollient for the patient's dry patches.
  • Many lip balms include emollient ingredients to prevent chapped lips.
adjective

Definition

Having the quality of softening or soothing.

In ZH

具有软化或舒缓特性的。

How to Use Emollient in a Sentence

  • The emollient properties of the ointment made it great for sensitive skin.
  • Her emollient words calmed the worried crowd.
  • The emollient texture of the cream made it easy to apply.
  • He preferred emollient oils for his massage therapy.
  • Using an emollient soap can help minimize irritation.
