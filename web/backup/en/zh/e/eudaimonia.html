Eudaimonia

Pronunciation:/ˌjuːdaɪˈmoʊniə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幸福
  • 美德
  • 福祉

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of being happy, healthy, and prosperous; often equated with human flourishing or living well.

In ZH

一种快乐、健康和繁荣的状态；通常等同于人类的繁荣或良好的生活。

Examples

  • Eudaimonia is often considered the highest good in Aristotelian ethics.
  • Many philosophers debate the best ways to achieve eudaimonia in modern life.
  • Eudaimonia can be seen as the ultimate goal of human existence.
  • Through virtue and moral strength, one can attain eudaimonia.
  • In the pursuit of eudaimonia, balance in life is essential.

Cultural Notes

Eudaimonia is a central concept in Aristotelian ethics, emphasizing the importance of virtues and moral character for true happiness.
