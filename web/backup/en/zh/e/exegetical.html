Exegetical

EN→ZH

What Does Exegetical Mean?

  • 释经的
  • 注释的
  • 解释的

Exegetical Pronunciation

/ˌɛksɪˈdʒɛtɪkəl/

Exegetical Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to the interpretation of texts, especially religious texts.

In ZH

与对文本（尤其是宗教文本）的解释有关。

How to Use Exegetical in a Sentence

  • The exegetical approach provides deeper insight into the scriptures.
  • Her exegetical analysis of the poem revealed hidden meanings.
  • Exegetical studies are vital for understanding ancient manuscripts.
  • Many scholars engage in exegetical work to clarify ambiguous passages.
  • He published an exegetical commentary on the biblical text.

Synonyms for Exegetical

  • Execrable
  • Exegesis
  • Exemplar
  • Exemplary
  • Exempt
