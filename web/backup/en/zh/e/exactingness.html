Exactingness

EN→ZH

What Does Exactingness Mean?

  • 严格性
  • 苛求
  • 精确度

Exactingness Pronunciation

/ɪɡˈzæktɪŋnəs/

Exactingness Definition

noun

Definition

the quality of being demanding or requiring precise standards

In ZH

对要求或精确标准的高度要求的质量

How to Use Exactingness in a Sentence

  • Her exactingness as a teacher pushed her students to perform at their best.
  • The exactingness of the project made it challenging for the entire team.
  • His exactingness in his work earned him a reputation for excellence.
  • The chef's exactingness with ingredients ensured every dish was perfect.
  • Their exactingness regarding data accuracy created a reliable system.
