Equivocation

Pronunciation:/ˌɛkwɪvəˈkeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 模糊语
  • 含糊其辞

Definitions

noun

Definition

The use of ambiguous language to conceal the truth or to avoid committing oneself.

In ZH

使用模棱两可的语言来隐藏真相或避免承诺。

Examples

  • His equivocation during the interview raised suspicions.
  • The politician's equivocation on the issue frustrated voters.
  • In her essay, she criticized the equivocation found in corporate communications.
  • He was skilled at equivocation, often dodging direct questions.
  • Equivocation can sometimes lead to misunderstandings among friends.
