Expostulate

Pronunciation:/ɪkˈspɒstjʊleɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 抗议
  • 指责
  • 劝诫

Definitions

verb

Definition

To express strong disapproval or disagreement.

In ZH

表达强烈的不赞成或不同意见。

Examples

  • He expostulated against the decision to cut the arts budget.
  • She expostulated her concerns during the meeting.
  • They expostulated with their parents about the unfair rules.
  • The lawyer expostulated the judge’s ruling vehemently.
  • After much debate, he finally decided to expostulate to his boss.
