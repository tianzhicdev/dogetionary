Exordium

EN→ZH

What Does Exordium Mean?

  • 序言
  • 前言
  • 开端
  • 引言

Exordium Pronunciation

/ɛkˈzɔːr.di.əm/

Exordium Definition

noun

Definition

The beginning or introductory part of a discourse, or a formal speech.

In ZH

演讲或正式演说的开始或引言部分。

How to Use Exordium in a Sentence

  • The exordium of the speech set the tone for the entire presentation.
  • In literature, the exordium often introduces the main themes.
  • The author carefully crafted the exordium to engage the reader immediately.
  • During the meeting, the exordium highlighted the key objectives of the project.
  • An effective exordium can capture the audience's attention right away.

Synonyms for Exordium

  • Exonerate
  • Exorbitance
  • Exorbitant
