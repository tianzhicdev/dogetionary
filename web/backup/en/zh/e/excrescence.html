Excrescence

Pronunciation:/ɪkˈskrɛsəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 赘生物
  • 外生物
  • 突出物

Definitions

noun

Definition

An abnormal outgrowth or enlargement, typically referring to a growth on the body or an unusual part on a structure.

In ZH

一种异常的生长或肿胀，通常指身体上的生长或结构上的异常部分。

Examples

  • The doctor removed the excrescence from the patient's skin.
  • An excrescence on the tree bark indicated a disease.
  • During the examination, an excrescence was found on the animal's paw.
  • The scientist studied the excrescence to understand its formation.
  • An excrescence is often just a benign growth but can sometimes indicate a health issue.
