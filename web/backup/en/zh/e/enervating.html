Enervating

EN→ZH

What Does Enervating Mean?

  • 削弱的
  • 使疲惫的
  • 无力的
  • 耗尽
  • 虚弱的

Enervating Pronunciation

/ˈɛnərˌveɪtɪŋ/

Enervating Definition

adjective

Definition

causing one to feel drained of energy or vitality

In ZH

使人感到精疲力竭或生命力衰竭的

How to Use Enervating in a Sentence

  • The heat was enervating, making it hard to concentrate on anything.
  • After the long hike, I felt completely enervating.
  • The meeting was so long and boring that it left everyone feeling enervated.
  • She found the constant noise from the construction site enervating.
  • The enervating routine of daily life can sometimes feel overwhelming.
