What Does Epiphonema Mean?

Epiphonema Pronunciation

/ˌɛpɪˈfɒnɪmə/

Epiphonema Definition

noun

Definition

A brief exclamation or observation often made to reinforce an argument, typically found at the end of a speech or passage.

In ZH

一种简短的感叹或观察，常用于增强论点，通常出现在演讲或段落的末尾。

  • Her use of an epiphonema highlighted the urgency of the issue at hand.
  • The epiphonema at the conclusion of the debate resonated with the audience.

In classical rhetoric, the use of an epiphonema was common in oratory to add emphasis and engage the audience.
