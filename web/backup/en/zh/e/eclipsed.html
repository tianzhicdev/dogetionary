Eclipsed

EN→ZH

What Does Eclipsed Mean?

  • 掩盖
  • 遮住
  • 月食

Eclipsed Pronunciation

/ɪˈklɪpst/

Eclipsed Definition

verb

Definition

Past tense of eclipse; to obscure or block out light from (a celestial body) or to surpass in significance or importance.

In ZH

动词的过去式，指遮住或阻挡（天体）光线，或者在重要性上超过。

How to Use Eclipsed in a Sentence

  • The sun was eclipsed by the moon during the solar event.
  • Her achievements eclipsed those of her peers.
  • The new technology has eclipsed the old models.
  • His performance eclipsed all expectations.
  • The scandal eclipsed her successful career.

Synonyms for Eclipsed

  • Eclat
  • Eclectic
  • Eclecticism
  • Ecliptic
  • Eclogue
