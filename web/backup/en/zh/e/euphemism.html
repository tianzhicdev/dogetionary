Euphemism

Pronunciation:/ˈjuːfəˌmɪz(ə)m/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 委婉语
  • 婉辞

Definitions

noun

Definition

A mild or indirect word or expression used in place of one that may be considered to be too harsh or blunt.

In ZH

一种温和或间接的词语或表达，用于代替可能被认为过于严厉或直白的词语。

Examples

  • He used a euphemism to soften the impact of his criticism.
  • Instead of saying she was fired, he used a euphemism and said she was 'let go.'
  • Many people prefer euphemisms when talking about sensitive subjects such as death.
  • The government often uses euphemisms to make policies sound more appealing.
  • In literature, euphemisms can be used to address taboo topics delicately.
