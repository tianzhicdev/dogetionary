Elation

EN→ZH

What Does Elation Mean?

  • 愉悦
  • 兴高采烈
  • 欣喜

Elation Pronunciation

/ɪˈleɪ.ʃən/

Elation Definition

noun

Definition

A feeling of great joy or delight.

In ZH

一种极大的喜悦或快乐的感觉。

How to Use Elation in a Sentence

  • She felt a sense of elation after receiving the good news.
  • The elation in the crowd was palpable when the team won the championship.
  • His elation was evident as he celebrated his birthday with friends.
  • The artist expressed her elation at the opening of her first gallery.
  • Finding the missing cat brought a wave of elation to the family.
