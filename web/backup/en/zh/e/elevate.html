Elevate

Pronunciation:ˈel.ɪ.veɪt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 提升
  • 提高
  • 抬高
  • 升高

Definitions

verb

Definition

to raise or lift something to a higher position

In ZH

把某物提升到更高的位置

Examples

  • The architect designed the building to elevate the skyline.
  • They plan to elevate the platform for better visibility.
  • He worked hard to elevate his game to a professional level.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, elevating one's status or position is often associated with personal or professional growth.

verb

Definition

to improve or enhance something, often in terms of quality or status

In ZH

改善或提升某事，通常是指质量或地位

Examples

  • The new policy aims to elevate the standards of education.
  • She took a course to elevate her skills in digital marketing.
  • His promotion will elevate the team's morale significantly.

Cultural Notes

Elevating one's skills or education is highly valued in competitive environments, reflecting the importance of continuous self-improvement.

verb

Definition

to promote someone to a higher rank or position

In ZH

将某人提升到更高的等级或职位

Examples

  • He was elevated to the position of senior manager after years of hard work.
  • The committee decided to elevate her to a leadership role due to her dedication.
  • They elevate their best performers to ensure the company's success.

Cultural Notes

Promotions can carry significant cultural weight, often symbolizing recognition of hard work and loyalty.
