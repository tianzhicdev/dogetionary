Eviscerate

Pronunciation:/ɪˈvɪs.ə.reɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 取出内脏
  • 剖腹
  • 除去内脏

Definitions

verb

Definition

To remove the internal organs of a body, particularly in reference to animals as part of butchery.

In ZH

指去除身体内部器官，特别是在屠宰动物时。

Examples

  • The chef will eviscerate the fish before cooking it.
  • During the biology class, we learned how to eviscerate a frog.
  • The farmers eviscerated the livestock to prepare them for market.
  • He watched as the hunter eviscerated the deer in the field.
  • In the cooking show, they demonstrated how to properly eviscerate poultry.

Cultural Notes

Evisceration is a common practice in both culinary and medical fields, often seen in butchery or animal dissections.
