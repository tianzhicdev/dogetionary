Evident

Pronunciation:ˈɛvɪdənt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 明显的
  • 显然的
  • 清楚的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

clearly seen or understood; obvious.

In ZH

清楚可见或明白的；显而易见的。

Examples

  • It was evident that she was upset after hearing the news.
  • His talent for music was evident from a young age.
  • The evidence was evident to everyone in the room.

Cultural Notes

The word 'evident' is often used in academic or formal contexts to indicate something that is clear or unmistakable. In everyday conversation, it can also express an opinion or observation that is generally accepted.

noun

Definition

the quality of being clear and obvious; clarity of perception.

In ZH

明显性，清晰性；感知的清晰程度。

Examples

  • The evident of his skills impressed the judges.
  • She spoke with such evident that everyone listened carefully.
  • There was an evident of joy on their faces during the celebration.

Cultural Notes

As a noun, 'evident' is less commonly used compared to its adjective form. It emphasizes clarity in understanding or observation, and can be found in more philosophical or reflective contexts.
