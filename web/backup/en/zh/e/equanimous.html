Equanimous

EN→ZH

What Does Equanimous Mean?

  • 平静的
  • 镇定的
  • 沉着的

Equanimous Pronunciation

/ˌiːkwəˈnɪməs/

Equanimous Definition

adjective

Definition

having mental calmness, composure, and evenness of temper, especially in a difficult situation.

In ZH

在困难情况下具有心理平静、镇定和情绪均衡。

How to Use Equanimous in a Sentence

  • She remained equanimous even during the heated debate.
  • His equanimous demeanor impressed everyone in the room.
  • In the face of adversity, she remained equanimous and composed.
  • Equanimous individuals are often sought after in crisis situations.
  • Practicing meditation can help one become more equanimous.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, equanimity is valued as a sign of emotional maturity and wisdom.

Synonyms for Equanimous

  • Equanimity
  • Equinox
  • Equivocal
  • Equivocate
  • Equivocation
