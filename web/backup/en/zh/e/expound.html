Expound

Pronunciation:/ɪkˈspaʊnd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 阐述
  • 解释
  • 详细说明

Definitions

verb

Definition

To explain something in detail.

In ZH

详细解释某事。

Examples

  • The professor will expound on the theory during the lecture.
  • She took the time to expound her views on the matter.
  • Can you expound your reasoning behind this decision?
  • He expounded the principles of democracy to the students.
  • In his book, he expounds the importance of critical thinking.
