Esoteric

Pronunciation:ˌɛsəˈtɛrɪk
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 深奥的
  • 秘传的
  • 小众的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Intended for or likely to be understood by only a small number of people with a specialized knowledge or interest.

In ZH

仅供少数有专业知识或兴趣的人理解的；深奥的。

Examples

  • The philosopher's theories are quite esoteric, attracting only a niche audience.
  • Many esoteric texts require years of study to fully comprehend.
  • She enjoys reading esoteric literature that challenges conventional thinking.

Cultural Notes

The term 'esoteric' often implies that the subject matter is obscure or not widely understood. It can pertain to philosophical, religious, or artistic contexts where deep knowledge or insider understanding is necessary.

noun

Definition

(In plural form) Objects, texts, or concepts that are esoteric in nature, known to only a select group.

In ZH

（复数形式）仅为特定群体所知的深奥事物，文本或概念。

Examples

  • The esoterica of ancient texts often require expert interpretation.
  • He has a collection of esoterica that few outside his circle have seen.
  • Studying esoterica can be rewarding but also very challenging.

Cultural Notes

This usage emphasizes a collection of knowledge or items that are not easily accessible. Esoterica often refer to niche interests that can be obscure or arcane.
