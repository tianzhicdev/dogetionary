Exigent

Pronunciation:/ˈɛk.sɪ.dʒənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 紧急的
  • 苛求的
  • 急迫的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Urgently requiring attention or action; demanding.

In ZH

急需注意或行动的；要求很高的。

Examples

  • The exigent circumstances required immediate intervention.
  • The exigent demands of the project left no room for delays.
  • She faced exigent deadlines that stressed her out.
  • His exigent personality made teamwork challenging.
  • The exigent requests of clients can be overwhelming.
