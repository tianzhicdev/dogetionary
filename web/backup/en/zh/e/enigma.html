Enigma

EN→ZH

What Does Enigma Mean?

  • 谜
  • 谜团
  • 难题

Enigma Pronunciation

/ɪˈnɪɡ.mə/

Enigma Definition

noun

Definition

A person or thing that is mysterious or difficult to understand.

In ZH

一个神秘或难以理解的人或事物。

How to Use Enigma in a Sentence

  • The disappearance of the ancient civilization remains an enigma.
  • She is an enigma, often hiding her true feelings.
  • The enigma of the universe puzzles scientists.
  • Solving the enigma may lead to great discoveries.
  • His motivations are an enigma, leaving everyone guessing.
