Effluence

EN→ZH

What Does Effluence Mean?

  • 排放
  • 流出物
  • 流出

Effluence Pronunciation

/ˈɛfluəns/

Effluence Definition

noun

Definition

the act of flowing out or the flow of liquid or gas from a source

In ZH

流出或从源头流出液体或气体的行为

How to Use Effluence in a Sentence

  • The effluence from the industrial plant was monitored closely.
  • Effluence can contaminate local water supplies if not treated.
  • The city established regulations to manage effluence into the river.
  • Farmers must be aware of the effluence from their fields into nearby streams.
  • Effluence from sewage treatment plants must meet safety standards.

Synonyms for Effluence

  • Efface
  • Effacement
  • Effervescence
  • Effervescent
  • Effete
