Ekphrastic

Pronunciation:/ɛkˈfræstɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 写生的
  • 描写艺术作品的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to a vivid, often dramatic, verbal description of a visual work of art.

In ZH

与对视觉艺术作品的生动、往往戏剧性的文字描述有关。

Examples

  • The poet wrote an ekphrastic piece inspired by the painting.
  • I love reading ekphrastic poetry that brings art to life.
  • Her ekphrastic writing made the sculpture feel almost alive.
  • He attended a workshop focused on creating ekphrastic descriptions of photographs.
  • Ekphrastic art can deepen our understanding of the visual work it describes.

Cultural Notes

Ekphrastic poetry and prose often serve to enhance the viewer's experience of art, bridging the gap between visual and literary art forms.
