Etherealism

Pronunciation:/ɪˈθɪəriəlɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 虚幻主义
  • 空灵主义

Definitions

noun

Definition

The belief or artistic style emphasizing the ethereal, delicate, and otherworldly qualities of art or literature.

In ZH

一种强调艺术或文学中虚幻、细腻和超凡特质的信仰或艺术风格。

Examples

  • The artist's work is a brilliant example of etherealism, capturing the light in a way that feels otherworldly.
  • Etherealism in literature often portrays characters who exist beyond the physical realm.
  • The etherealism of the music created an atmosphere that felt almost dreamlike.
  • Many poets embrace etherealism to express emotions that are difficult to define.
  • Her dance performance embodied etherealism, with every movement appearing weightless and fluid.

Cultural Notes

Etherealism is often associated with Romantic and Impressionist movements in art, focusing on beauty and emotional depth.
