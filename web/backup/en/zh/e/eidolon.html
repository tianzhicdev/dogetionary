Eidolon Pronunciation

/aɪˈdoʊlən/

Eidolon Definition

noun

Definition

A phantom or idealized figure; an image or representation of something.

In ZH

幻影或理想化的形象；某物的图像或表现。

How to Use Eidolon in a Sentence

  • The eidolon of beauty can take many forms.
  • In literature, an eidolon often symbolizes an unattainable ideal.
  • His dreams were filled with the eidolon of his lost love.
  • The artist painted an eidolon that captured the essence of nature.
  • In mythology, the eidolon serves as a reflection of the soul.
