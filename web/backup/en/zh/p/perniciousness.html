Perniciousness

EN→ZH

What Does Perniciousness Mean?

  • 恶性
  • 有害性
  • 致命性
  • 有毒性

Perniciousness Pronunciation

/pərˈnɪʃəsnəs/

Perniciousness Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being pernicious; having a harmful effect, especially in a gradual or subtle way.

In ZH

有害的性质；尤其是以逐渐或微妙的方式产生的有害影响。

How to Use Perniciousness in a Sentence

  • The perniciousness of the disease went unnoticed for years.
  • Their perniciousness was masked by their charming personalities.
  • The perniciousness of misinformation can undermine public trust.
  • His perniciousness in the workplace led to a toxic environment.
  • We must confront the perniciousness of systemic inequality.

Synonyms for Perniciousness

  • Perceived
  • Perchance
  • Percolate
  • Perdition
