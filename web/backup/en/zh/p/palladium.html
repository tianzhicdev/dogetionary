Palladium

EN→ZH

What Does Palladium Mean?

  • 钯
  • 钯金

Palladium Pronunciation

/pəˈleɪdiəm/

Palladium Definition

noun

Definition

A rare, silvery-white metal that is resistant to corrosion and is used in various industrial applications, including electronics and automotive catalytic converters.

In ZH

一种稀有的银白色金属，耐腐蚀，用于各种工业应用，包括电子产品和汽车催化转换器。

How to Use Palladium in a Sentence

  • Palladium is often used in the automotive industry for catalytic converters.
  • The jewelry industry favors palladium for its durability.
  • Scientists are researching the use of palladium in hydrogen storage.
  • Palladium can be used as a catalyst in chemical reactions.
  • Due to its rarity, palladium is more expensive than gold.
