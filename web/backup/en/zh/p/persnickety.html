Persnickety

Pronunciation:/pərˈsnɪk.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 挑剔的
  • 小气的
  • 过分讲究的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

placing too much emphasis on trivial or minor details; fussy.

In ZH

对琐碎或微小细节过于强调；挑剔的。

Examples

  • He is quite persnickety about his food.
  • Her persnickety nature makes her hard to please.
  • The persnickety customer returned the item because it had a tiny scratch.
  • I find it hard to work with someone so persnickety.
  • His persnickety approach to the project caused delays.
