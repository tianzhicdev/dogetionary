Process

Pronunciation:/ˈprəʊ.ses/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 过程
  • 处理
  • 工序

Definitions

noun

Definition

A series of actions or steps taken to achieve a particular end.

In ZH

一系列为实现特定目标而采取的行动或步骤。

Examples

  • The process of photosynthesis is crucial for life on Earth.
  • We need to streamline the process to improve efficiency.
  • The hiring process can be quite lengthy.
  • Cooking is a process that requires patience and skill.
  • The process of elimination helped me choose the best option.
verb

Definition

To perform a series of operations on (data or a signal) to produce a desired result.

In ZH

对（数据或信号）进行一系列操作以产生所需的结果。

Examples

  • The software will process the data automatically.
  • You need to process the images before posting them online.
  • The factory processes raw materials into finished products.
  • We will process your request as soon as possible.
  • It is important to process feedback effectively.
