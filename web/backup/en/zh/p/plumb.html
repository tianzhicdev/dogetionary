Plumb

EN→ZH

What Does Plumb Mean?

  • 铅锤
  • 垂直
  • 深入

Plumb Pronunciation

/plʌm/

Plumb Definition

verb

Definition

To measure the depth of something, typically water.

In ZH

测量某物的深度，通常是水。

How to Use Plumb in a Sentence

  • The diver plumbed the depths of the ocean.
  • They plumbed the well to see how deep it was.
  • The engineer plumbed the hole to assess its depth.
  • He decided to plumb the river to find the best fishing spots.
  • They plumbed the foundations before starting construction.
verb

Definition

To explore or examine deeply; to investigate thoroughly.

In ZH

深入探索或检查；彻底调查。

How to Use Plumb in a Sentence

  • The researchers plumbed the complexities of the issue.
  • She plumbed the depths of his thoughts during the interview.
  • They plumbed the topic of climate change extensively in their report.
  • He plumbed the reasons behind their decision.
  • We need to plumb this problem further before making a conclusion.
adjective

Definition

Exactly vertical; straight up and down.

In ZH

完全垂直；上下直立。

How to Use Plumb in a Sentence

  • The carpenter ensured that the frame was plumb before nailing it down.
  • Using a level, she checked that the wall was plumb.
  • He adjusted the pole until it was plumb with the ground.
  • The building's structure must remain plumb to be safe.
  • She used a plumb line to ensure the tower stood upright.
noun

Definition

A weight, usually of lead, attached to a line, used to determine vertical alignment.

In ZH

通常用铅制成的重物，附着在一条线上，用于确定垂直对齐。

How to Use Plumb in a Sentence

  • He dropped the plumb into the water to check the depth.
  • The plumb swung freely, indicating the wall was not straight.
  • We used a plumb to make sure the posts were aligned.
  • The plumb helps in ensuring the accuracy of the construction.
  • She checked the plumb before building the shelf.

Synonyms for Plumb

  • Pluck
  • Plutocracy
  • Plutocratic
