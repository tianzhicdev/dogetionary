Perspire

Pronunciation:/pərˈspaɪər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 出汗
  • 流汗

Definitions

verb

Definition

to excrete moisture through the skin, usually in the form of sweat

In ZH

通过皮肤排出水分，通常以汗水的形式

Examples

  • I tend to perspire a lot when I exercise.
  • She began to perspire under the hot sun.
  • He perspired nervously before his presentation.
  • The humidity made everyone perspire more than usual.
  • After the workout, I was completely drenched from perspiring.
