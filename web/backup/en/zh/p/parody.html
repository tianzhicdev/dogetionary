Parody

EN→ZH

What Does Parody Mean?

  • 模仿
  • 讽刺作品
  • 滑稽

Parody Pronunciation

/ˈpær.ə.di/

Parody Definition

noun

Definition

A humorous or satirical imitation of a particular piece of literature, music, or art.

In ZH

对特定文学、音乐或艺术作品的幽默或讽刺的模仿。

How to Use Parody in a Sentence

  • The movie is a parody of classic horror films.
  • She wrote a parody of a famous song.
  • His painting is a parody of the Mona Lisa.
  • The book is a clever parody of high society.
  • They performed a parody that made everyone laugh.
verb

Definition

To create a parody of; to imitate for comic effect.

In ZH

创作模仿作品；为了搞笑的效果进行模仿。

How to Use Parody in a Sentence

  • The comedian parodied several celebrities in his act.
  • He loves to parody famous songs at karaoke.
  • They parodied the news to highlight its absurdity.
  • She parodies her teacher's lectures for fun.
  • The show often parodies popular culture.

Synonyms for Parody

  • Paradigm
  • Paradigm Shift
  • Paradigmatic
  • Paradox
  • Paradoxical
