Peculation

EN→ZH

What Does Peculation Mean?

  • 挪用
  • 侵占
  • 贪污

Peculation Pronunciation

/ˌpɛkjʊˈleɪʃən/

Peculation Definition

noun

Definition

the act of embezzling or misappropriating funds or property entrusted to one's care; embezzlement.

In ZH

挪用或侵占交给自己保管的资金或财产的行为；贪污。

How to Use Peculation in a Sentence

  • The accountant was charged with peculation after the missing funds were discovered.
  • He was found guilty of peculation and sentenced to five years in prison.
  • Peculation often occurs in organizations where oversight is minimal.
  • The scandal involved significant peculation by several high-ranking officials.
  • They implemented stricter controls to prevent peculation in the department.

Synonyms for Peculation

  • Pecadillo
  • Peccadillo
  • Peculiar
  • Pecuniary
