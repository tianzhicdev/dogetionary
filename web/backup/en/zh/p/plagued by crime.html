Plagued By Crime

Pronunciation:pleɪgd baɪ kraɪm
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 被犯罪困扰
  • 受到犯罪影响
  • 遭受犯罪困扰

Definitions

phrase

Definition

Affected or troubled by a high incidence of crime.

In ZH

受到高发犯罪的影响或困扰。

Examples

  • The neighborhood has been plagued by crime for years.
  • Local businesses are suffering as the area is plagued by crime.
  • Residents are calling for action as their community is plagued by crime.

Cultural Notes

In many urban areas, phrases like 'plagued by crime' reflect social concerns about safety and can often lead to community activism or governmental intervention.
