Polymath

Pronunciation:/ˈpɒl.ɪ.mæθ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 博学者
  • 通才
  • 多才多艺的人

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person of wide-ranging knowledge or learning.

In ZH

广泛知识或学习的人。

Examples

  • Leonardo da Vinci is often considered a true polymath.
  • In today's world, being a polymath can give you a competitive edge.
  • She is a polymath who excels in both science and the arts.
  • Many historical figures were polymaths, contributing to various fields.
  • The university encourages students to become polymaths rather than specialists.
