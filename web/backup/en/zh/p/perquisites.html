Perquisites

EN→ZH

What Does Perquisites Mean?

  • 附加津贴
  • 特权
  • 额外收益

Perquisites Pronunciation

/ˈpɜːrk.wɪ.zɪts/

Perquisites Definition

noun

Definition

A benefit or perk received in addition to one's regular salary or wages.

In ZH

除了常规工资或薪水之外获得的福利或额外津贴。

How to Use Perquisites in a Sentence

  • Many jobs offer perquisites like a company car or health insurance.
  • The CEO enjoys several perquisites that come with the position.
  • Employees should be aware of their perquisites when negotiating salaries.
  • Some people see perquisites as essential for attracting talent.
  • The perquisites of the job include a generous retirement plan.
