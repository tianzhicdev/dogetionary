Philoprogenitive

EN→ZH

What Does Philoprogenitive Mean?

  • 亲生的
  • 繁殖的
  • 生育的

Philoprogenitive Pronunciation

/ˌfɪləʊprəʊˈdʒɛnɪtɪv/

Philoprogenitive Definition

adjective

Definition

producing offspring; having a strong inclination to foster or produce children.

In ZH

产生后代的；有强烈倾向去培育或生产儿童的。

How to Use Philoprogenitive in a Sentence

  • The philoprogenitive nature of the species is evident in their large litters.
  • Many cultures celebrate philoprogenitive values, emphasizing family and children.
  • Her philoprogenitive instincts drove her to become a dedicated parent.
  • The philoprogenitive policies encouraged families to have more children.
  • Philoprogenitive behavior can be seen in various animal species.
