Proximate

Pronunciation:/ˈprɒk.sɪ.mət/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 邻近的
  • 接近的
  • 近似的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

close to; nearby; immediate; not distant.

In ZH

靠近的；邻近的；不远的。

Examples

  • The proximate cause of the accident was determined to be driver error.
  • In biology, proximate explanations focus on immediate causes.
  • The team studied the proximate effects of climate change on the ecosystem.
  • His proximate goal is to finish the project by the end of the month.
  • In the context of philosophy, understanding proximate truths can help clarify complex theories.
