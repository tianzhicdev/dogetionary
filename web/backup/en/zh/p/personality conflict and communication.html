What Does Personality Conflict And Communication Mean?

Personality Conflict And Communication Pronunciation

/ˌpɜːrsəˈnælɪti ˈkɒnflɪkt/

Personality Conflict And Communication Definition

noun

Definition

A situation in which two or more people have opposing personality traits that lead to disagreement or tension.

In ZH

两个人或更多人性格特征相反，导致不合或紧张的情况。

How to Use Personality Conflict And Communication in a Sentence

  • The team's productivity suffered due to ongoing personality conflicts among its members.
  • Resolving a personality conflict can often require effective communication and understanding.
  • It's important to address personality conflicts in the workplace before they escalate.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, especially Asian cultures, direct confrontation is often avoided. Thus, personality conflicts might be understated rather than openly discussed.
