Pathological

EN→ZH

What Does Pathological Mean?

  • 病理的
  • 病态的
  • 病症的

Pathological Pronunciation

/ˌpæθəˈlɒdʒɪkəl/

Pathological Definition

adjective

Definition

Of or relating to pathology; involving or caused by disease.

In ZH

与病理学有关的；涉及或由疾病引起的。

How to Use Pathological in a Sentence

  • The pathological findings were consistent with a severe infection.
  • He had a pathological fear of heights that affected his daily life.
  • The study focused on pathological behaviors in various animal species.
  • Pathological conditions can often lead to serious health issues.
  • She displayed a pathological need for approval from her peers.

Synonyms for Pathological

  • Pathos
  • Patina
  • Patting
