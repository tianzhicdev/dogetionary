Pragmatic

Pronunciation:/præɡˈmætɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 务实的
  • 实际的
  • 实用的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

dealing with things sensibly and realistically in a way that is based on practical rather than theoretical considerations

In ZH

以切合实际和现实的方式处理事务，基于实际而非理论的考虑

Examples

  • She took a pragmatic approach to solving the problem.
  • The committee made pragmatic decisions to ensure the project's success.
  • His pragmatic mindset helped him navigate complex situations.
  • They prefer pragmatic solutions over idealistic theories.
  • We need a pragmatic plan to address these challenges.
