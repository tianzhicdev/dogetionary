Paradox

Pronunciation:/ˈpær.ə.dɒks/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 矛盾
  • 悖论
  • 自相矛盾的事

Definitions

noun

Definition

A statement or proposition that seems self-contradictory or absurd but in reality expresses a possible truth.

In ZH

一个似乎自相矛盾或荒谬的陈述或命题，但实际上表达了一种可能的真理。

Examples

  • The paradox of choice suggests that having too many options can lead to anxiety.
  • It is a paradox that in such a rich country, there are still people living in poverty.
  • His life was a paradox; he was both a successful businessman and a dedicated artist.
  • In science, a paradox can lead to new theories and discoveries.
  • The statement 'less is more' is a classic example of a paradox.
