Polyphony

Pronunciation:/pəˈlɪfəni/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 复调
  • 多声音

Definitions

noun

Definition

a type of musical texture consisting of two or more independent melodies that are sung or played simultaneously.

In ZH

一种音乐纹理，由两个或多个独立的旋律同时演唱或演奏。

Examples

  • The choir performed a beautiful piece of polyphony.
  • In polyphony, each voice has equal importance.
  • The complexity of polyphony adds depth to the composition.
  • Early music often features rich polyphony.
  • Contemporary composers are exploring new forms of polyphony.
