Peripatetic

Pronunciation:/ˌpɛrɪpəˈtɛtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 巡游的
  • 徘徊的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Walking or traveling about; itinerant.

In ZH

走动或四处旅行的；巡回的。

Examples

  • The peripatetic nature of her job means she is constantly on the move.
  • Peripatetic philosophers traveled from place to place to teach.
  • Many peripatetic workers struggle to maintain a stable home life.
  • His peripatetic lifestyle makes him hard to pin down for meetings.
  • The festival welcomed peripatetic artists from different parts of the world.
noun

Definition

A person who travels from place to place, especially for work or as a way of life.

In ZH

一个从一个地方旅行到另一个地方的人，尤其是为了工作或生活的方式。

Examples

  • As a peripatetic, he had few belongings and a strong sense of adventure.
  • The peripatetic spent most of his time exploring new cities and cultures.
  • She admired the peripatetic for his ability to adapt to new environments.
  • Being a peripatetic can be exhausting but also rewarding.
  • The challenge of being peripatetic is finding a community wherever you go.
