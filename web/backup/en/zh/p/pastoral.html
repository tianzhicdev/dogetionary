Pastoral

EN→ZH

What Does Pastoral Mean?

  • 田园的
  • 牧师的
  • 乡村的

Pastoral Pronunciation

/ˈpæs.tɚ.əl/

Pastoral Definition

adjective

Definition

relating to the countryside or rural life, often in a peaceful or idyllic way

In ZH

与乡村或田园生活有关，通常以宁静或理想化的方式

How to Use Pastoral in a Sentence

  • The pastoral scenery was breathtaking.
  • He preferred the pastoral lifestyle over the chaos of the city.
  • Many poets have written about pastoral themes.
  • The pastoral care provided by the church was invaluable.
  • She painted a pastoral landscape full of rolling hills.
noun

Definition

a work of literature or art that depicts rural life or shepherding

In ZH

描绘乡村生活或牧羊的文学或艺术作品

How to Use Pastoral in a Sentence

  • The novel is a classic pastoral that explores rural relationships.
  • His latest painting is a beautiful pastoral of rural England.
  • Pastoral literature often idealizes the simplicity of country life.
  • The festival celebrated pastoral traditions through music and dance.
  • She studied the pastoral genre in her literature class.

Synonyms for Pastoral

  • Pastiche
