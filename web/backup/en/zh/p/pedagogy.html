What Does Pedagogy Mean?

Pedagogy Pronunciation

/ˈpɛdəˌɡɒdʒi/

Pedagogy Definition

noun

Definition

The method and practice of teaching, especially as an academic subject or theoretical concept.

In ZH

教学的方法和实践，特别作为一个学术科目或理论概念。

How to Use Pedagogy in a Sentence

  • Pedagogy is essential for effective learning.
  • Many universities offer courses in pedagogy.
  • The study of pedagogy includes various teaching techniques.
  • Innovative pedagogy can enhance student engagement.
  • Understanding pedagogy helps teachers adapt to different learning styles.
