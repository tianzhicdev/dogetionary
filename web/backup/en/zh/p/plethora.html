Plethora

EN→ZH

What Does Plethora Mean?

  • 过多
  • 丰富
  • 大量

Plethora Pronunciation

/ˈplɛθ.ər.ə/

Plethora Definition

noun

Definition

A large or excessive amount of something.

In ZH

大量或过量的东西。

How to Use Plethora in a Sentence

  • There is a plethora of options available to choose from.
  • She has a plethora of hats in her collection.
  • The city offers a plethora of activities for tourists.
  • He faced a plethora of challenges during the project.
  • The report highlighted a plethora of data that supports the findings.

Synonyms for Plethora

  • Please
  • Plenary
  • Plentitude
