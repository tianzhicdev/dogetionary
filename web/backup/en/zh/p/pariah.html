Pariah

EN→ZH

What Does Pariah Mean?

  • 贱民
  • 被社会遗弃的人
  • 孤立的人

Pariah Pronunciation

/pəˈraɪə/

Pariah Definition

noun

Definition

a person who is rejected or despised by society

In ZH

被社会拒绝或鄙视的人

How to Use Pariah in a Sentence

  • After his scandal, he became a social pariah.
  • She felt like a pariah in her own community after the incident.
  • The once popular athlete is now regarded as a pariah.
  • Many people turned him into a pariah due to his controversial opinions.
  • In some cultures, those who break traditions can become pariahs.

Cultural Notes

The term 'pariah' originates from the Tamil word 'paraiyar', referring to a marginalized community in India.
