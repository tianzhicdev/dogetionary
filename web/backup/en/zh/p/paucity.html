Paucity

EN→ZH

What Does Paucity Mean?

  • 稀缺性
  • 不足

Paucity Pronunciation

/ˈpɔːsɪti/

Paucity Definition

noun

Definition

the presence of something in insufficient quantities; scarcity.

In ZH

某种东西数量不足的状态；稀缺。

How to Use Paucity in a Sentence

  • There is a paucity of evidence to support the claim.
  • The project failed due to a paucity of resources.
  • The run-down area suffers from a paucity of basic services.
  • A paucity of information can lead to misunderstandings.
  • The community has faced a long-standing paucity of funding.

Synonyms for Paucity

  • Pauciloquent
