Periphery

EN→ZH

What Does Periphery Mean?

  • 边缘
  • 周边
  • 外围

Periphery Pronunciation

/pəˈrɪf.ər.i/

Periphery Definition

noun

Definition

the outer limits or edge of an area or object

In ZH

某个区域或物体的外部边界或边缘

How to Use Periphery in a Sentence

  • The city has grown beyond its original periphery.
  • In the periphery of the forest, we found a small stream.
  • The periphery of the country was less developed than the capital.
  • She drew the outline of the periphery of the shape.
  • His ideas were often overlooked as they were considered outside the periphery of mainstream thought.

Synonyms for Periphery

  • Perceived
