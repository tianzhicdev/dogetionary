Pendulous

Pronunciation:/ˈpɛndjʊləs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 垂挂的
  • 悬垂的
  • 摇摆的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

hanging down loosely; swinging freely.

In ZH

松弛下垂的，随意悬挂的。

Examples

  • The pendulous branches of the willow tree swayed in the breeze.
  • She wore a necklace with a pendulous pendant that swayed as she walked.
  • The pendulous belly of the pregnant woman was visible.
  • The pendulous lamps in the restaurant created a cozy atmosphere.
  • The pendulous flower buds drooped under the weight of water.
  • The cat enjoyed playing with the pendulous toy that dangled from the shelf.
