Pulchritude

EN→ZH

What Does Pulchritude Mean?

  • 美丽
  • 优雅
  • 俊美

Pulchritude Pronunciation

/ˈpʌl.krɪ.tjuːd/

Pulchritude Definition

noun

Definition

Beauty; physical beauty, especially of a woman.

In ZH

美丽；尤其是女性的外貌美。

How to Use Pulchritude in a Sentence

  • Her pulchritude was evident at the gala.
  • The pulchritude of the landscape took my breath away.
  • Many poets have written about the pulchritude of nature.
  • Despite her intelligence, she was often reduced to her pulchritude in discussions.
  • Pulchritude can be found in both people and places.

Synonyms for Pulchritude

  • Pulchritudinous
  • Pull
  • Pulsar
  • Pulverize
