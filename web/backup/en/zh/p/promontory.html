Promontory

Pronunciation:/ˈprɒmənˌtɔːri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 岬
  • 突出部
  • 海岬

Definitions

noun

Definition

A high point of land or rock projecting into a body of water.

In ZH

向水体突出的高地或岩石点。

Examples

  • The cliff was a stunning promontory overlooking the ocean.
  • We hiked to the promontory for a better view of the sunset.
  • The ancient castle was built on a promontory to provide a strategic advantage.
  • Many birds nest on the rocky promontory during the summer months.
  • The geology of the region includes several prominent promontories.
