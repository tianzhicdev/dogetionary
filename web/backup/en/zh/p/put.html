Put

Pronunciation:pʊt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 放
  • 置
  • 放置
  • 把
  • 投入

Definitions

verb

Definition

To move something into a specified place or position.

In ZH

把某物移动到指定的地方或位置。

Examples

  • Please put the book on the table.
  • He put his keys in his pocket.
  • She put the flowers in a vase.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'put' can be used in many contexts, including physical placement of objects as well as more abstract uses.

verb

Definition

To state or express in a certain way.

In ZH

以某种方式说出或表达。

Examples

  • Can you put that in simpler terms?
  • She put her thoughts into words beautifully.
  • He put forth a compelling argument.

Cultural Notes

This usage of 'put' is often found in formal or academic contexts.

verb

Definition

To cause (something) to have a specified condition or be in a specified state.

In ZH

使某物处于特定的状态或条件。

Examples

  • The news put him in a good mood.
  • This information puts everything into perspective.
  • Her words put me at ease.

Cultural Notes

This meaning emphasizes the effects that actions or words can have on a person's feelings or state of mind.
