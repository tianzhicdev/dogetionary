Polymorphic

EN→ZH

What Does Polymorphic Mean?

  • 多态的
  • 多形的
  • 多样形态的

Polymorphic Pronunciation

/ˌpɒl.ɪˈmɔː.fɪk/

Polymorphic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or characterized by polymorphism; able to exist in different forms or varieties.

In ZH

与多态性有关的，能够以不同的形式或品种存在的。

How to Use Polymorphic in a Sentence

  • The software is polymorphic, adapting its structure based on the input it receives.
  • Polymorphic species can change their physical appearance based on environmental conditions.
  • In programming, polymorphic functions can operate on objects of different types.
  • The concept of polymorphic behavior is fundamental in object-oriented programming.
  • Some viruses are polymorphic, altering their code to evade detection.
