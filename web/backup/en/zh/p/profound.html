Profound

Pronunciation:/prəˈfaʊnd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 深刻的
  • 深奥的
  • 深沉的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having or showing great knowledge or understanding

In ZH

具有或展示出丰富的知识或理解能力

Examples

  • Her profound insights changed the way we think about the issue.
  • The book offers profound lessons about life and love.
  • He has a profound respect for his teachers.
  • The movie leaves a profound impact on its audience.
  • They shared a profound bond that lasted a lifetime.
adjective

Definition

very deep; intense

In ZH

非常深；强烈

Examples

  • The ocean is known for its profound depths.
  • She felt a profound sadness after the loss.
  • His words had a profound effect on the audience.
  • The painting evokes profound emotions.
  • There was a profound sense of peace in the quiet garden.
