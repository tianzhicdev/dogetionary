Puerile

Pronunciation:/ˈpjʊəraɪl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幼稚的
  • 孩子气的
  • 天真的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Childishly silly or trivial.

In ZH

幼稚的或微不足道的。

Examples

  • His puerile behavior at the party was embarrassing.
  • She rolled her eyes at his puerile jokes.
  • The discussion turned puerile as they started arguing over trivial matters.
  • He wrote a puerile response to a serious question.
  • It's difficult to engage him in mature conversation due to his puerile attitude.
