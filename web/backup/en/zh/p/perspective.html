Perspective

Pronunciation:/pərˈspɛktɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 观点
  • 透视
  • 看法
  • 视角

Definitions

noun

Definition

A particular attitude toward or way of regarding something; a point of view.

In ZH

对某事的特定态度或看法；一种观点。

Examples

  • Her perspective on the issue was very refreshing.
  • From my perspective, we need to find a compromise.
  • Different cultures may have different perspectives on life.
  • It's important to consider multiple perspectives before making a decision.
  • His perspective as an artist helps him see things differently.
