Perturbation

Pronunciation:/ˌpɜːr.tɜːrˈbeɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 扰动
  • 干扰
  • 波动

Definitions

noun

Definition

A disturbance or deviation from the normal or expected state.

In ZH

一种干扰或偏离正常或预期状态的现象。

Examples

  • The perturbation in the data led to unexpected results.
  • Scientists studied the perturbation of the system over time.
  • He felt a perturbation in his equilibrium after the news.
  • The perturbation caused by the earthquake was felt miles away.
  • In quantum mechanics, a small perturbation can lead to significant changes.
