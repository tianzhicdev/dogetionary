Proclivity

Pronunciation:/prəˈklɪv.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 倾向
  • 嗜好
  • 偏好

Definitions

noun

Definition

A tendency to choose or do something regularly; an inclination or predisposition towards a particular thing.

In ZH

一种倾向，通常定期选择或做某事；对某事物的倾向或偏好。

Examples

  • She has a proclivity for classical music.
  • His proclivity for adventure led him to travel the world.
  • The teacher noted her students' proclivity for creative writing.
  • There is a proclivity among teenagers to seek independence.
  • His proclivity for technology made him a great asset to the team.
