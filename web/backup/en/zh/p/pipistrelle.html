Pipistrelle

Pronunciation:/ˈpɪpɪstrɛl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 小蝙蝠
  • 微小的蝙蝠

Definitions

noun

Definition

a small bat of the genus Pipistrellus, known for its agile flight and high-pitched echolocation calls.

In ZH

一种属于小蝙蝠属（Pipistrellus）的蝙蝠，因其灵活的飞行和高频的回声定位声而闻名。

Examples

  • The pipistrelle flitted through the twilight, hunting for insects.
  • Conservation efforts have been made to protect the pipistrelle population in urban areas.
  • I heard a pipistrelle squeak as it flew past my head.
  • The pipistrelle is one of the most common bats in Europe.
  • During summer nights, pipistrelles can often be seen skimming over ponds and fields.
