Plant

Pronunciation:plænt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 植物
  • 工厂
  • 种植

Definitions

noun

Definition

A living organism that typically grows in soil, has leaves, stems, and roots, and is capable of photosynthesis.

In ZH

一种通常生长在土壤中，具有叶子、茎和根并能够进行光合作用的活生物体。

Examples

  • The plant in the corner is a beautiful fern.
  • Many plants require sunlight to grow.
  • She has a collection of indoor plants that she takes care of.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, plants are symbols of life and growth. Indoor gardening is becoming increasingly popular, with many people finding joy in caring for plants.

verb

Definition

To place a seed or young plant in the ground so it can grow.

In ZH

将种子或幼苗放在土壤中以使其生长。

Examples

  • He plans to plant tomatoes in his garden this spring.
  • They will plant new trees along the street.
  • She decided to plant flowers in the backyard.

Cultural Notes

Planting is often associated with seasonal changes and agricultural practices. In many cultures, gardening is seen not only as a way to grow food but also as a therapeutic hobby.

noun

Definition

A place where an industrial or manufacturing process takes place.

In ZH

进行工业或制造过程的地方。

Examples

  • The factory is a large plant that produces electronics.
  • They built a new plant to manufacture solar panels.
  • The plant employs hundreds of workers from the local community.

Cultural Notes

In industrial contexts, the word 'plant' can refer to facilities where goods are produced. This usage reflects the importance of manufacturing in many economies.
