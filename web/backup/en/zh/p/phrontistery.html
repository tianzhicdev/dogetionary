Phrontistery

Pronunciation:/frɒnˈtɪstəri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 思考之处
  • 思维中心

Definitions

noun

Definition

A place or space for thinking or contemplating; a chamber of thought.

In ZH

思考或沉思的地方；思考的房间。

Examples

  • The old library served as a phrontistery for the philosophers of the town.
  • In the midst of chaos, she found her phrontistery in the quiet corner of the park.
  • Every artist needs a phrontistery to inspire their creativity.
  • The university's study room became a phrontistery for many students during finals week.
  • He converted his basement into a phrontistery filled with books and art supplies.
