Paradoxical

Pronunciation:/ˌpær.əˈdɒk.sɪ.kəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 矛盾的
  • 自相矛盾的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

seeming to contradict itself but may nonetheless be true

In ZH

看似自相矛盾但可能是真实的

Examples

  • It is paradoxical that some of the world's richest people are unhappy.
  • The paradoxical nature of the situation left everyone confused.
  • Despite his success, he felt a paradoxical sense of failure.
  • Her paradoxical behavior made it hard for her friends to understand her.
  • The theory appears paradoxical at first glance, but it makes sense upon deeper analysis.
