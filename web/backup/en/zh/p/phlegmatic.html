Phlegmatic

EN→ZH

What Does Phlegmatic Mean?

  • 冷静的
  • 迟缓的
  • 沉着的

Phlegmatic Pronunciation

/flɛɡˈmætɪk/

Phlegmatic Definition

adjective

Definition

having an unemotional and stolidly calm disposition

In ZH

有一种冷静和沉着的性格

How to Use Phlegmatic in a Sentence

  • He remained phlegmatic in the face of the crisis.
  • Her phlegmatic demeanor often helped her in stressful situations.
  • Despite the chaos around him, he was remarkably phlegmatic.
  • It was hard to tell what he was feeling; he always had a phlegmatic expression.
  • Phlegmatic individuals often excel in high-pressure jobs.

Synonyms for Phlegmatic

  • Phlegm
