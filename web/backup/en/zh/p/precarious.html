Precarious

EN→ZH

What Does Precarious Mean?

  • 不稳定的
  • 危险的
  • 可怜的

Precarious Pronunciation

/prɪˈkɛəriəs/

Precarious Definition

adjective

Definition

not securely held or in position; dangerously likely to fall or collapse.

In ZH

不牢靠或不稳固；危险地容易倾倒或坍塌。

How to Use Precarious in a Sentence

  • The precarious ladder swayed as he climbed it.
  • Their financial situation is still precarious after the market crash.
  • She held onto the precarious ledge, hoping to find her balance.
  • His job security feels precarious without a formal contract.
  • The precarious peace between the two countries could be broken at any time.

Synonyms for Precarious

  • Precariousness
  • Precipitate
  • Precocious
  • Predilection
  • Preponderance
