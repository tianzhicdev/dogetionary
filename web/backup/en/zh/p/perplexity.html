Perplexity

Pronunciation:/pəˈplɛksɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 困惑
  • 复杂性
  • 迷惑

Definitions

noun

Definition

the state of being perplexed; uncertainty of meaning or intention.

In ZH

困惑的状态；意义或意图的不确定性。

Examples

  • The perplexity of the puzzle left him puzzled for hours.
  • In times of uncertainty, perplexity can weigh heavily on decision-making.
  • Her expression reflected a deep perplexity about the situation.
  • Despite the perplexity of the instructions, he managed to complete the task.
  • The perplexity of the problem made it difficult to find a solution.
