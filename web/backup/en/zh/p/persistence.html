Persistence

Pronunciation:/pərˈsɪstəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 坚持
  • 持久性
  • 恒心

Definitions

noun

Definition

The continued or obstinate pursuit of something despite difficulties or opposition.

In ZH

在困难或反对的情况下，继续或固执地追求某事。

Examples

  • Her persistence in studying paid off when she passed the exam.
  • The persistence of the plant allowed it to survive in harsh conditions.
  • He showed remarkable persistence in pursuing his dreams.
  • Their persistence led to significant improvements in the community.
  • The athlete's persistence was admirable as she trained through injuries.
