Pathos

EN→ZH

What Does Pathos Mean?

  • 情感
  • 悲悯
  • 感伤

Pathos Pronunciation

/ˈpeɪθɒs/

Pathos Definition

noun

Definition

A quality that evokes pity or sadness.

In ZH

一种引起同情或悲伤的特质。

How to Use Pathos in a Sentence

  • The movie was full of pathos and made everyone cry.
  • Her speech was powerful, filled with pathos and emotion.
  • The artist captured the pathos of the scene beautifully.
  • In literature, pathos is often used to engage the audience's emotions.
  • The character's struggles were depicted with great pathos.

Synonyms for Pathos

  • Pathological
  • Patina
  • Patting
