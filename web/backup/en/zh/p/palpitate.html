Palpitate

Pronunciation:/ˈpæl.pɪ.teɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 心悸
  • 跳动
  • 搏动

Definitions

verb

Definition

to beat rapidly and strongly; to throb

In ZH

快速而强有力地跳动；跳动

Examples

  • Her heart began to palpitate with excitement.
  • He felt his heart palpitate during the intense moment.
  • The athlete's heart will palpitate after an exhausting race.
  • I could feel my pulse palpitate as I stood on stage.
  • When he received the news, his heart started to palpitate.
