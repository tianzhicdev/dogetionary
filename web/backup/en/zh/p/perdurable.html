Perdurable

EN→ZH

What Does Perdurable Mean?

  • 持久的
  • 耐用的
  • 永恒的

Perdurable Pronunciation

/pərˈdʊr.ə.bəl/

Perdurable Definition

adjective

Definition

Lasting for a very long time; enduring.

In ZH

持久的；耐用的；永恒的

How to Use Perdurable in a Sentence

  • The perdurable nature of ancient monuments is a testament to human ingenuity.
  • He believed in the perdurable value of art throughout history.
  • Some friendships are perdurable, even when faced with challenges.
  • The novelist's themes are perdurable and resonate with generations.
  • Environmental conservation efforts aim for perdurable changes in society.
