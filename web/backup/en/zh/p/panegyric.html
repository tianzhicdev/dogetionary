Panegyric

EN→ZH

What Does Panegyric Mean?

  • 颂辞
  • 赞美诗
  • 颂扬
  • 赞辞

Panegyric Pronunciation

/ˌpænəˈdʒɪrɪk/

Panegyric Definition

noun

Definition

a public speech or published text in praise of someone or something.

In ZH

对某人或某事的赞美的公开演讲或出版文本。

How to Use Panegyric in a Sentence

  • The president delivered a panegyric in honor of the retiring general.
  • Her speech was a moving panegyric to the contributions of the volunteers.
  • The article was a panegyric celebrating the success of the local artist.
  • In ancient Greece, a panegyric was often delivered at public festivals.
  • The book is a panegyric of modern science and its achievements.
