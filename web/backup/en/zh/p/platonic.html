Platonic

Pronunciation:/pləˈtɒnɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 柏拉图的
  • 理想的友谊

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Of or relating to Plato or his philosophy; characterized by a close relationship but without sexual intimacy.

In ZH

与柏拉图或其哲学有关的；特指没有性亲密关系的密切关系。

Examples

  • Their relationship was purely platonic, based on mutual respect and understanding.
  • He had a platonic friendship with his childhood best friend.
  • The love story was more platonic than romantic in nature.
  • She explained that their connection was platonic and nothing more.
  • Many people seek platonic relationships that provide emotional support without the complications of romance.
noun

Definition

A person who is involved in a platonic relationship.

In ZH

参与柏拉图式关系的人。

Examples

  • He considered her a platonic, rather than romantic, companion.
  • In modern times, platonics play significant roles in personal friendships.
  • The group consisted of three platonics who enjoyed each other's company without any romantic interest.
  • She often referred to him as her platonic soulmate.
  • The importance of platonics in social dynamics cannot be overstated.
