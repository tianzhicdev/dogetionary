Psychiatry

Pronunciation:saɪˈkaɪətri
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 精神病学
  • 精神医学

Definitions

noun

Definition

The branch of medicine that deals with the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of mental disorders.

In ZH

精神病学是医学的一个分支，涉及精神障碍的诊断、治疗和预防。

Examples

  • Psychiatry plays a crucial role in understanding mental health issues.
  • Many people seek help through psychiatry when they experience severe emotional distress.
  • Psychiatry combines medical knowledge with psychological theories.
noun

Definition

The study of mental illness, including its symptoms, causes, and treatment.

In ZH

精神病学是研究心理疾病的学科，包括其症状、原因和治疗。

Examples

  • Research in psychiatry has led to new therapies for depression.
  • Understanding the causes of mental illness is a key focus of psychiatry.
  • Training in psychiatry involves extensive education in both biology and psychology.
noun

Definition

A medical specialty concerned with the diagnosis and treatment of mental health issues, using psychotherapy and medication.

In ZH

精神病学是一种医学专业，专注于心里健康问题的诊断和治疗，使用心理治疗和药物治疗。

Examples

  • Psychiatrists often collaborate with psychologists to provide comprehensive care.
  • Medication prescribed in psychiatry can help manage symptoms of anxiety disorders.
  • Psychiatry is essential for addressing the needs of patients with severe mental illnesses.
