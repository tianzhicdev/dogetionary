Promulgate

Pronunciation:/ˈprɒm.əl.ɡeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 传播
  • 发布
  • 宣告

Definitions

verb

Definition

To make known or public, especially by formal announcement or decree.

In ZH

特别是通过正式公告或法令使之为人知晓或公共可得。

Examples

  • The government decided to promulgate the new law next month.
  • They aim to promulgate the findings of their research widely.
  • It is essential to promulgate the rules for the safety of the community.
  • The organization will promulgate a series of guidelines for best practice.
  • After much debate, the committee voted to promulgate the changes immediately.
