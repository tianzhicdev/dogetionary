Palpable

Pronunciation:/ˈpæl.pə.bəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 明显的
  • 可感知的
  • 容易察觉的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

able to be touched or felt; clear to the mind or plain to see

In ZH

可触摸或感知的；清晰可见或显而易见的

Examples

  • The tension in the room was palpable.
  • Her excitement was palpable as she opened the gift.
  • There was a palpable sense of disappointment after the game.
  • The improvements in his performance were palpable.
  • You could feel the palpable enthusiasm of the crowd.
