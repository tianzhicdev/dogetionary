Personality Conflict And Communicstion

Pronunciation:/ˌpɜːrsəˈnælɪti ˈkɒnflɪkt ənd kəˌmjuːnɪˈkeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 个性冲突
  • 沟通
  • 人际沟通

Definitions

noun

Definition

A situation in which two or more individuals have differing personal traits or behaviors that lead to disagreement or tension between them.

In ZH

两个或更多人有不同的个性特征或行为，导致他们之间的分歧或紧张局势的情况。

Examples

  • The personality conflict between the two team leaders made collaboration difficult.
  • She tried to resolve the personality conflict with her coworker, but they couldn't see eye to eye.
  • Personality conflicts can arise in any workplace, especially when individuals have strong opinions.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, particularly in the West, addressing personality conflicts directly is often encouraged to promote teamwork and communication.

noun

Definition

The way in which individuals share information, thoughts, and feelings; an essential aspect of interpersonal relationships.

In ZH

个人分享信息、思想和情感的方式；人际关系的重要方面。

Examples

  • Effective communication is key to resolving any personality conflict.
  • He believes that open communication can help prevent misunderstandings.
  • Improving communication skills can lead to better relationships in both personal and professional settings.

Cultural Notes

Communication styles vary widely across cultures; in some cultures, indirect communication is preferred, while in others, directness is valued.
