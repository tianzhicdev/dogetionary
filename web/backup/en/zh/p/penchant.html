Penchant

Pronunciation:/ˈpɛn.ʃɒ̃/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 倾向
  • 偏好
  • 爱好

Definitions

noun

Definition

a strong or habitual liking for something or a tendency to do something.

In ZH

对某事物的强烈或习惯性喜好，或倾向于做某事的趋势。

Examples

  • She has a penchant for classical music.
  • He showed a penchant for adventure in his youth.
  • The artist has a penchant for using vibrant colors.
  • They developed a penchant for healthy eating habits.
  • Her penchant for reading novels is well-known.
