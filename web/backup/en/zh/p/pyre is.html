Pyre Is

Pronunciation:/paɪər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 火葬堆
  • 焚烧堆
  • 火堆

Definitions

noun

Definition

A stack of wood or other combustible material for burning a dead body, traditionally used in ceremonies.

In ZH

用于焚烧尸体的一堆木材或其他可燃材料，传统上在仪式中使用。

Examples

  • In ancient cultures, a pyre was used to honor the deceased.
  • The funeral pyre burned brightly as family and friends gathered to say their final goodbyes.
  • They prepared a pyre for the ceremonial burial, following the customs of their ancestors.

Cultural Notes

The use of pyres in burial ceremonies is common in various cultures, especially in Hinduism where cremation is a significant ritual. Pyres symbolize the release of the soul from the physical body.

noun

Definition

A large pile or heap of combustible material, especially one used for burning or as part of a ritual.

In ZH

一堆大堆的可燃材料，尤其是用于燃烧或作为仪式的一部分。

Examples

  • The villagers collected wood to build a pyre for the festival.
  • On the beach, they created a pyre for the bonfire party.
  • The artist created a massive pyre as a statement about environmental issues.

Cultural Notes

In modern contexts, the term 'pyre' can also refer to large bonfires used in festivals or celebrations, highlighting communal activities and rituals.
