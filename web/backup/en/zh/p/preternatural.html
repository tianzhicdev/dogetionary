Preternatural

EN→ZH

What Does Preternatural Mean?

  • 超自然的
  • 非凡的
  • 异常的

Preternatural Pronunciation

/ˌpriː.tərˈnætʃ.ər.əl/

Preternatural Definition

adjective

Definition

Beyond what is normal or natural; supernatural.

In ZH

超出正常或自然范围的；超自然的。

How to Use Preternatural in a Sentence

  • The preternatural abilities of the protagonist made him a legendary figure.
  • She spoke with a preternatural calmness during the crisis.
  • The forest was filled with preternatural sounds that sent chills down my spine.
  • His preternatural insight into human behavior astounded his peers.
  • The artist had a preternatural talent that set her apart from her contemporaries.

Synonyms for Preternatural

  • Precarious
  • Precariousness
  • Precipitate
  • Precocious
  • Predilection
