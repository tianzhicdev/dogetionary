Perfunctory

EN→ZH

What Does Perfunctory Mean?

  • 敷衍的
  • 草率的
  • 马虎的

Perfunctory Pronunciation

/pərˈfʌŋk.tər.i/

Perfunctory Definition

adjective

Definition

carried out with a minimum of effort or reflection; superficial.

In ZH

马虎的; 草率的，做事没有认真思考的样子。

How to Use Perfunctory in a Sentence

  • He gave a perfunctory nod in agreement.
  • The report was written in a perfunctory manner.
  • Her apology was perfunctory and lacked sincerity.
  • They conducted a perfunctory inspection of the house before buying it.
  • His perfunctory greeting made it clear he wasn't interested.

Synonyms for Perfunctory

  • Perambulate
  • Perceived
  • Perchance
  • Percolate
  • Perdition
