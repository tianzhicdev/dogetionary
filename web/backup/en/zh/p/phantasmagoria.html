Phantasmagoria

Pronunciation:/ˌfæn.tæz.məˈɡɔː.ri.ə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幻影画像
  • 变幻莫测的景象
  • 梦幻

Definitions

noun

Definition

A sequence of real or imaginary images like those seen in a dream.

In ZH

一系列真实或虚构的图像，就像在梦中看到的那样。

Examples

  • The film was a phantasmagoria of colors and sounds.
  • She described her childhood as a phantasmagoria of emotions.
  • The art exhibit was a phantasmagoria that left everyone in awe.
  • His performance was a phantasmagoria of creativity and expression.
  • The novel unfolds as a phantasmagoria of memories and dreams.
