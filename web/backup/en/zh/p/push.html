Push

Pronunciation:pʊʃ
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 推
  • 推动
  • 推挤

Definitions

verb

Definition

To exert force on someone or something to move it away from oneself.

In ZH

施加力气在某人或某物上使其远离自己

Examples

  • Please push the door to open it.
  • She pushed the stroller up the hill.
  • He pushed the limits of his endurance.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, 'push' can also refer metaphorically to encouraging someone to do something or to try harder.

noun

Definition

An act of pushing; a forceful movement in a specific direction.

In ZH

推动的动作；朝着特定方向的力量移动

Examples

  • The push from the crowd made him lose his balance.
  • She gave the bike a gentle push to start it.
  • With one final push, they moved the car out of the mud.

Cultural Notes

The term 'push' can also symbolize motivation or encouragement, such as in 'a push to succeed'.
