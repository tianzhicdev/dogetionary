Pal

Pronunciation:pæl
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 朋友
  • 兄弟
  • 伙伴

Definitions

noun

Definition

A close friend or companion.

In ZH

一个亲密的朋友或伙伴。

Examples

  • I've known him since childhood; he's my best pal.
  • Let's invite our pals to the party this weekend.
  • She considers her dog her pal.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, the term 'pal' is often used informally to refer to a friend, suggesting a friendly or casual relationship.

verb

Definition

To be friendly or to form a close friendship with someone.

In ZH

与某人友好相处或形成密切的友谊。

Examples

  • He palled around with his colleagues after work.
  • They have palled together since they met at university.
  • She likes to pall with her neighbors on weekends.

Cultural Notes

As a verb, 'to pal' is less commonly used than the noun form but still conveys a sense of forming friendships.
