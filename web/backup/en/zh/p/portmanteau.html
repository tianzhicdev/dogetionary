Portmanteau

EN→ZH

What Does Portmanteau Mean?

  • 行李箱
  • 合成词

Portmanteau Pronunciation

/pɔːrtˈmæntoʊ/

Portmanteau Definition

noun

Definition

A large suitcase or a word that combines the sounds and meanings of two or more words.

In ZH

一个大型行李箱或一个结合两个或多个单词的声音和意义的词。

How to Use Portmanteau in a Sentence

  • A portmanteau is often used by travelers to carry their belongings.
  • The word 'brunch' is a portmanteau of 'breakfast' and 'lunch'.
  • In literature, a portmanteau can refer to a blend of two genres.
  • He bought a stylish portmanteau for his trip to Europe.
  • The term 'smog' is a portmanteau combining 'smoke' and 'fog'.

Synonyms for Portmanteau

  • Portentous
