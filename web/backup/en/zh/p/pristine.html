Pristine

EN→ZH

What Does Pristine Mean?

  • 原始的
  • 未受污染的
  • 纯净的

Pristine Pronunciation

/ˈprɪstiːn/

Pristine Definition

adjective

Definition

in its original condition; unspoiled.

In ZH

处于原始状态；未被破坏的。

How to Use Pristine in a Sentence

  • The pristine forest was untouched by human activity.
  • She kept her car in pristine condition.
  • The beach was known for its pristine waters.
  • Pristine landscapes are often sought after by photographers.
  • The museum displays pristine artifacts from ancient civilizations.

Synonyms for Pristine

  • Prime
  • Primeval
