Penultimate

Pronunciation:/pəˈnʌltɪmət/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 倒数第二的
  • 次于最后的
  • 次终的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

last but one in a series of things; second to last.

In ZH

在一系列事物中倒数第二的；第二个到最后的。

Examples

  • The penultimate chapter of the book was very thrilling.
  • In the penultimate game of the season, the team secured their playoff spot.
  • She finished in penultimate position in the race, just before the last runner.
  • We watched the penultimate episode of the series last night.
  • Finding penultimate solutions can often lead to better outcomes.
