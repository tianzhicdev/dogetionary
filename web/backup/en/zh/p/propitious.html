Propitious

EN→ZH

What Does Propitious Mean?

  • 吉利的
  • 有利的
  • 适合的

Propitious Pronunciation

/prəˈpɪʃ.əs/

Propitious Definition

adjective

Definition

giving or indicating a good chance of success; favorable.

In ZH

表示成功的好机会；有利的。

How to Use Propitious in a Sentence

  • The weather conditions are propitious for sailing.
  • Her timely advice proved propitious for my career.
  • It's a propitious time to start a new business venture.
  • The negotiations took place at a propitious moment.
  • They believe that the propitious signs will lead to victory.

Synonyms for Propitious

  • Probity
  • Process
  • Proclivity
  • Procrastinate
  • Procrastination
