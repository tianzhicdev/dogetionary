Prohibited

Pronunciation:prəˈhɪbɪtɪd
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 禁止的
  • 被禁止的
  • 不允许的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Formally forbidden or not allowed by law, rules, or authority.

In ZH

正式禁止或不被法律、规则或权威允许的。

Examples

  • In a shocking twist, the mayor announced that eating donuts while walking is now prohibited in the city. Who knew 'walking and munching' could be illegal?
  • They found out that bringing a pet llama to the office was prohibited after the creature made a new friend with the coffee machine!
  • Just when Tim thought he could sneak out for ice cream during a meeting, he remembered that eating during presentations was prohibited. He'll just have to daydream about sundaes instead.

Cultural Notes

The word is often used in legal contexts, but it can also be used humorously or exaggeratedly in everyday situations to emphasize rules.
