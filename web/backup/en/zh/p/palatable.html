Palatable

Pronunciation:/ˈpæl.ə.tə.bəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 可口的
  • 美味的
  • 合意的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Pleasant to taste; acceptable or satisfactory.

In ZH

可口的；令人满意的。

Examples

  • The chef prepared a palatable dish that everyone enjoyed.
  • Although it was healthy, the salad was not very palatable to the children.
  • They managed to make the medicine more palatable by adding flavoring.
  • The restaurant is known for its palatable vegan options.
  • The wine was surprisingly palatable for its low price.
