Prestidigitation

EN→ZH

What Does Prestidigitation Mean?

  • 魔术表演
  • 手法技艺
  • 变戏法

Prestidigitation Pronunciation

/ˌprɛstɪˌdɪdʒɪˈteɪʃən/

Prestidigitation Definition

noun

Definition

The art of performing magic or tricks, especially with the hands; sleight of hand.

In ZH

表演魔术或杂技的艺术，特别是利用手法；手法技艺。

How to Use Prestidigitation in a Sentence

  • His prestidigitation skills amazed the audience.
  • She learned prestidigitation to enhance her magic performances.
  • The magician's prestidigitation was flawless and left everyone in awe.
  • At the party, he showcased his prestidigitation by pulling coins from behind people's ears.
  • Prestidigitation involves a lot of practice to perfect the tricks.

Synonyms for Prestidigitation

  • Precarious
  • Precariousness
  • Precipitate
  • Precocious
  • Predilection
