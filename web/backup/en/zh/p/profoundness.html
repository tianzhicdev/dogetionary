Profoundness

EN→ZH

What Does Profoundness Mean?

  • 深度
  • 深刻性
  • 深厚感

Profoundness Pronunciation

/prəˈfaʊnd.nəs/

Profoundness Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of having deep insight or understanding; the state of being profound.

In ZH

具有深刻洞察力或理解力的特质；深邃的状态。

How to Use Profoundness in a Sentence

  • Her profoundness in philosophy made her a revered teacher.
  • The profoundness of his thoughts left a lasting impression on the audience.
  • The novel explores the profoundness of human emotions.
  • She spoke with a profoundness that resonated with everyone present.
  • In moments of silence, we often reflect on the profoundness of life.

Synonyms for Profoundness

  • Process
  • Proclivity

  • Procrastination
