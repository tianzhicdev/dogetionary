Persevere

EN→ZH

What Does Persevere Mean?

  • 坚持
  • 不屈不挠
  • 努力不懈

Persevere Pronunciation

/ˌpɜːrsəˈvɪr/

Persevere Definition

verb

Definition

to continue in a course of action even in the face of difficulty or with little or no prospect of success

In ZH

在面对困难或几乎没有成功可能性的情况下，坚持采取行动

How to Use Persevere in a Sentence

  • She decided to persevere with her studies despite the challenges.
  • To achieve your goals, you must persevere through the tough times.
  • Many inventors had to persevere before finding success with their ideas.
  • He persevered in his efforts to improve his health after the diagnosis.
  • If you persevere long enough, you will eventually find a solution.

Synonyms for Persevere

  • Perambulate
  • Perceived
  • Perchance
  • Percolate
  • Perdition
