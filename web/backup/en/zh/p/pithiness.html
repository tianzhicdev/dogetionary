Pithiness

Pronunciation:/ˈpɪθ.i.nəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 简洁
  • 精炼
  • 简明扼要

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being concise and expressive.

In ZH

简洁和富有表现力的特性。

Examples

  • His speeches are known for their pithiness.
  • The article's pithiness made it an enjoyable read.
  • She appreciated the pithiness of his critiques.
  • The pithiness of the poem captured complex emotions in few words.
  • In a world filled with noise, pithiness is a valued skill.
