Palliate

EN→ZH

What Does Palliate Mean?

  • 缓和
  • 减轻
  • 辩解

Palliate Pronunciation

/ˈpæl.i.eɪt/

Palliate Definition

verb

Definition

To lessen the severity of a disease or condition without curing it.

In ZH

减轻疾病或病症的严重性而不治愈它。

How to Use Palliate in a Sentence

  • The doctor prescribed medication to palliate the patient's pain.
  • They are trying to palliate the symptoms of the illness.
  • Palliative care focuses on providing relief from the symptoms of serious illnesses.
  • The goal of treatment was to palliate rather than to cure the condition.
  • Palliate the effects of the storm by providing emergency supplies.

Synonyms for Palliate

  • Pal
  • Palatable
  • Palatial
  • Palaver
  • Paleolithic
