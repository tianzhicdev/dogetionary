Paragon

Pronunciation:/ˈpærəˌɡɒn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 典范
  • 模范
  • 榜样

Definitions

noun

Definition

a model of excellence or perfection of a kind; a perfect example of a particular quality.

In ZH

一种卓越或完美的典范；某种品质的完美例子。

Examples

  • She is a paragon of virtue.
  • The scientist is often regarded as a paragon in his field.
  • He is a paragon of diligence and hard work.
  • The new policy is a paragon of efficiency.
  • Their relationship is seen as a paragon of love and respect.
