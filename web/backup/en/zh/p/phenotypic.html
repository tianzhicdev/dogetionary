Phenotypic

Pronunciation:/ˌfiːnəʊˈtɪpɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 表型的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to the observable characteristics or traits of an organism, which result from the interaction of its genotype with the environment.

In ZH

与生物的可观察特征或性状有关，这些特征是其基因型与环境相互作用的结果。

Examples

  • Phenotypic expressions can vary significantly among individuals in the same species.
  • The study focused on the phenotypic traits of various plant species.
  • Phenotypic plasticity allows organisms to adapt to different environmental conditions.
  • Research in phenotypic variation helps understand evolutionary processes.
  • Genetic studies often correlate with phenotypic outcomes in breeding programs.
