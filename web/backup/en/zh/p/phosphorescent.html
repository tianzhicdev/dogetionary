Phosphorescent

Pronunciation:/ˌfɒsfəˈrɛsənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 磷光的
  • 发光的
  • 荧光的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Exhibiting phosphorescence; emitting light without appreciable heat after exposure to radiation.

In ZH

表现出磷光的特性；在放射性辐射后发出光而几乎没有可感知的热量。

Examples

  • The phosphorescent paint glowed in the dark after being exposed to light.
  • Stars on the ceiling are made from a phosphorescent material that shines at night.
  • Phosphorescent organisms can be found in the ocean, creating beautiful light displays.
  • When the lights went out, the phosphorescent stickers on the walls became visible.
  • Researchers are studying phosphorescent materials for use in energy-efficient lighting.
