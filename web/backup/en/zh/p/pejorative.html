Pejorative

Pronunciation:/pɪˈdʒɒr.ə.tɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 贬义词
  • 贬损的

Definitions

noun

Definition

A word or phrase that has negative connotations or expresses contempt.

In ZH

具有消极内涵或表达轻蔑的单词或短语。

Examples

  • Calling someone a 'loser' is using a pejorative term.
  • The term 'nerd' can be considered a pejorative in some contexts.
  • She referred to the outdated laws as pejorative and harmful.
  • In our discussion, we should avoid using pejorative language.
  • Using pejorative terms can create a hostile environment.
adjective

Definition

Expressing contempt or disapproval; derogatory.

In ZH

表达轻蔑或不赞同的；贬义的。

Examples

  • His pejorative comments about the policy were noted.
  • Using pejorative language can alienate people.
  • The speaker used a pejorative tone throughout his speech.
  • She dislikes pejorative descriptions of her profession.
  • It's important to recognize when language becomes pejorative.
