Paleolithic

Pronunciation:/ˌpeɪl.i.oʊˈlɪθ.ɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 旧石器时代

Definitions

noun

Definition

The earliest period of the Stone Age, characterized by the use of rudimentary stone tools and the development of early human culture and society.

In ZH

旧石器时代是石器时代的最早时期，其特征是使用简单的石器工具和早期人类文化与社会的发展。

Examples

  • The Paleolithic era saw the emergence of the first Homo sapiens.
  • Artifacts from the Paleolithic period provide insights into early human life.
  • During the Paleolithic, humans lived a nomadic lifestyle, following herds of animals.
  • Cave paintings from the Paleolithic are some of the earliest forms of human expression.
  • The invention of stone tools marked a significant advancement during the Paleolithic period.
