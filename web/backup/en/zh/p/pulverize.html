Pulverize

EN→ZH

What Does Pulverize Mean?

  • 粉碎
  • 磨成粉
  • 压碎

Pulverize Pronunciation

/ˈpʌl.vəˌraɪz/

Pulverize Definition

verb

Definition

To reduce to fine particles or powder by crushing or grinding.

In ZH

通过压碎或研磨将其减少为细小颗粒或粉末。

How to Use Pulverize in a Sentence

  • You need to pulverize the spices before adding them to the dish.
  • The machine can pulverize the materials to a fine dust.
  • They decided to pulverize the rocks for easier transportation.
  • She will pulverize the ingredients to make a smoother texture in her recipe.
  • It's important to pulverize the herbs to release their full flavor.

Synonyms for Pulverize

  • Pulchritude
  • Pulchritudinous
  • Pull
  • Pulsar
