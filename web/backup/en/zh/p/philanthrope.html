Philanthrope

Pronunciation:/fɪˈlænθroʊp/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 慈善家

Definitions

noun

Definition

a person who seeks to promote the welfare of others, especially by the donation of money to good causes.

In ZH

一个寻求促进他人福利的人，特别是通过向慈善事业捐赠钱款。

Examples

  • The philanthropist donated millions to improve education.
  • She was recognized as a philanthropist for her work in the community.
  • Many philanthropes support health initiatives around the world.
  • He became a philanthropist after his business success.
  • Philanthropists often establish foundations to manage their charitable activities.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, philanthropy is associated with social responsibility and community development.
