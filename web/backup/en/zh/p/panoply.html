Panoply

EN→ZH

What Does Panoply Mean?

  • 全副武装
  • 盛大的展示
  • 一系列

Panoply Pronunciation

/ˈpæn.ə.pli/

Panoply Definition

noun

Definition

A complete or impressive collection of things; a full display of something.

In ZH

完整或令人印象深刻的物品集合；某物的完整展示。

How to Use Panoply in a Sentence

  • The museum displayed a panoply of ancient artifacts.
  • She showed a panoply of skills during the performance.
  • The panoply of colors in the sunset was breathtaking.
  • There was a panoply of choices available at the festival.
  • His research presented a panoply of data supporting the theory.

Synonyms for Panoply

  • Panacea
  • Pandemonium
  • Panegyric
  • Panopticism
  • Panopticon
