Plagiarize

Pronunciation:/ˈpleɪdʒəˌraɪz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 剽窃
  • 抄袭
  • 冒用

Definitions

verb

Definition

To copy someone else's work, ideas, or expressions and present them as one's own without proper acknowledgment.

In ZH

未经适当确认，抄袭他人的作品、思想或表达，并将其作为自己的呈现。

Examples

  • He was accused of plagiarizing several passages from academic papers.
  • It's important to paraphrase correctly to avoid plagiarizing.
  • The student learned about the consequences of plagiarizing during a writing workshop.
  • Plagiarizing can lead to severe academic penalties.
  • Always cite your sources to prevent any plagiarism issues.
