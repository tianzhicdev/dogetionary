Perspicacious

Pronunciation:/ˌpɜːrspɪˈkeɪʃəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 有洞察力的
  • 聪明的
  • 敏锐的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having a ready insight into and understanding of things

In ZH

有敏锐的洞察力和对事物的理解能力

Examples

  • Her perspicacious mind allowed her to see the solution before anyone else.
  • Despite the complexity of the issue, his perspicacious analysis was appreciated by all.
  • The detective was known for his perspicacious observations that often led to solving the case.
  • Being perspicacious, she quickly grasped the nuances of the new policy.
  • His perspicacious questions during the meeting revealed a deep understanding of the topic.
