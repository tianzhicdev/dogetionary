Please

EN→ZH

What Does Please Mean?

  • 请
  • 拜托
  • 请您

Please Pronunciation

/pliːz/

Please Definition

interjection

Definition

Used as a polite addition to requests or commands.

In ZH

用作请求或命令的礼貌附加语。

How to Use Please in a Sentence

  • Could you please pass the salt?
  • Please, take a seat.
  • Would you kindly help me with this?
  • I would appreciate it if you could please listen carefully.
  • Please remember to turn off the lights before you leave.
verb

Definition

To cause to feel happy and satisfied; to give pleasure to.

In ZH

使感到快乐和满足；使愉悦。

How to Use Please in a Sentence

  • I just want to please my parents.
  • He always tries to please everyone around him.
  • What can I do to please you?
  • This meal should please even the pickiest eaters.
  • She aims to please her audience with her performances.

Synonyms for Please

  • Plenary
  • Plentitude
  • Plethora
