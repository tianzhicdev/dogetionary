Plutocracy

Pronunciation:/pluːˈtɒkrəsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 富豪政治
  • 金权政治
  • 富人统治

Definitions

noun

Definition

A system of government in which the wealthy class holds power and influence over political decisions.

In ZH

一种由富裕阶层掌握权力和影响政治决策的政府体系。

Examples

  • In a plutocracy, the interests of the wealthy often outweigh those of the general population.
  • Many argue that the country has become a plutocracy, where money dictates policy.
  • The election was heavily influenced by plutocracy, with major donors swaying the outcome.
  • Critics say that plutocracy leads to inequality and social unrest.
  • Historically, ancient Athens was considered a form of a plutocracy during certain periods.
