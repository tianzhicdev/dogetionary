Parenthetic

Pronunciation:/pærənˈθɛtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 插入的
  • 附加的
  • 括弧的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to or inserted as a parenthesis.

In ZH

与括号相关或作为插入的部分。

Examples

  • She included a parenthetic remark in her essay.
  • The parenthetic information adds context to the main idea.
  • Please check the parenthetic phrases for clarity.
  • In his speech, he made a parenthetic comment about the audience.
  • The writer used parenthetic statements to elaborate on key points.
