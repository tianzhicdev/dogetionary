Pedantry

Pronunciation:/ˈpɛdəntri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 学究气
  • 卖弄知识
  • 忽视细节

Definitions

noun

Definition

Excessive concern with minor details and rules or with displaying academic learning.

In ZH

过度关注小细节和规则，或炫耀学术知识。

Examples

  • His pedantry made discussions tedious.
  • She was criticized for her pedantry during the debate.
  • The professor's pedantry frustrated the students.
  • In his pedantry, he missed the main point of the argument.
  • Pedantry can often overshadow genuine intellectual discourse.
