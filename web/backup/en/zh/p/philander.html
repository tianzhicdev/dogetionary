Philander

Pronunciation:fɪˈlændər
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 调情
  • 花心
  • 玩弄感情

Definitions

verb

Definition

To engage in casual or uncommitted sexual relationships; to flirt with many partners without serious intentions.

In ZH

进行随意或不认真的性关系；与许多伴侣调情而没有认真意图。

Examples

  • He tends to philander, making it difficult for him to maintain a long-term relationship.
  • She discovered that he had a reputation to philander around during their dating.
  • In the story, the character philandered throughout his youth, causing havoc in his romantic relationships.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, 'philander' implies a lack of commitment and can carry a negative connotation regarding fidelity in relationships.

noun

Definition

A person who engages in casual sexual relationships; a flirt.

In ZH

进行随意性关系的人；调情者。

Examples

  • He was known as a philanderer, never staying with one woman for too long.
  • Her friend warned her that dating a philander could lead to heartbreak.
  • In literature, the philanderer is often portrayed as charming yet unreliable.

Cultural Notes

The term often describes men, but it can be used for women as well. In modern contexts, it highlights the contrast between romantic ideals and real-life behaviors.
