Persecuted

Pronunciation:ˈpɜːrsɪkjuːtɪd
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 被迫害的
  • 受害的
  • 遭受迫害的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Subjected to hostility and ill-treatment, especially because of race, political, or religious beliefs.

In ZH

因为种族、政治或宗教信仰而遭受敌意和虐待的。

Examples

  • Many individuals have been persecuted throughout history for their beliefs.
  • The group's members were persecuted due to their political stance.
  • He spoke out against the regime, knowing that he might be persecuted for his views.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in discussions about human rights, and is significant in various historical contexts, such as the persecution of certain religious groups or political dissidents.

verb

Definition

Past tense of persecute; to subject someone to hostility or ill-treatment.

In ZH

迫害某人，指对某人实施敌意或虐待。

Examples

  • They have persecuted those who oppose their regime.
  • He was persecuted by his peers for being different.
  • The activists were persecuted for their efforts to promote civil rights.

Cultural Notes

While 'persecute' usually appears in discussions about severe forms of oppression, it can also apply to less extreme social bullying or ostracism.
