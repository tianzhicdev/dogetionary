Paraglide

Pronunciation:/ˈpærəˌɡlaɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 滑翔伞
  • 降落伞
  • 滑翔

Definitions

verb

Definition

to glide through the air using a parachute-like wing that is designed to be flown by an individual.

In ZH

通过使用类似降落伞的翅膀在空中滑行，该翅膀设计为由个人飞行。

Examples

  • He decided to paraglide off the mountain for the thrill of it.
  • They learned how to paraglide during their vacation.
  • Paragliding offers a unique perspective of the landscape below.
  • She was thrilled to paraglide for the first time.
  • He watched as the paragliders gracefully descended from the sky.
noun

Definition

a recreational activity that involves flying a lightweight, free-flying aircraft called a paraglider.

In ZH

一种休闲活动，涉及飞行一种轻型、自由飞行的飞行器，称为滑翔伞。

Examples

  • Paragliding is growing in popularity among adventure seekers.
  • The paraglider soared high above the valley, enjoying the view.
  • He took up paragliding as a hobby last summer.
  • Many paragliders gather at the launch site to share tips.
  • The view while paragliding can be breathtaking.
