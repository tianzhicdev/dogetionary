Pseudopregnant

EN→ZH

What Does Pseudopregnant Mean?

  • 假孕的
  • 伪孕的

Pseudopregnant Pronunciation

/ˌsjuː.doʊˈprɛɡ.nənt/

Pseudopregnant Definition

adjective

Definition

Describing a state where a female exhibits signs of pregnancy but is not actually pregnant.

In ZH

描述一种状态，女性表现出怀孕的迹象，但实际上并没有怀孕。

How to Use Pseudopregnant in a Sentence

  • The dog appears pseudopregnant after her heat cycle.
  • Some species of animals can become pseudopregnant as a natural response.
  • Veterinarians can identify pseudopregnant conditions through specific tests.
  • Pseudopregnant women may experience symptoms similar to those of actual pregnancy.
  • Pseudopregnancy can be common in certain breeds of dogs.

Synonyms for Pseudopregnant

  • Pseudepigrapha
  • Pseudonym
  • Pseudonymity
  • Pseudopregnancy
