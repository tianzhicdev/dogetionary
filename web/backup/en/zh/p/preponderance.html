Preponderance

Pronunciation:/prɪˈpɒndərəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 优势
  • 主要成分
  • 多数
  • 重心

Definitions

noun

Definition

The state of being greater in number, quantity, or importance.

In ZH

在数量、程度或重要性上占优势的状态。

Examples

  • The preponderance of evidence suggests that the theory is correct.
  • In a democracy, the preponderance of public opinion can influence policy decisions.
  • There is a preponderance of male students in engineering programs.
  • The preponderance of the team's victories came from their strong defense.
  • She argued that the preponderance of wealth in society leads to inequality.
