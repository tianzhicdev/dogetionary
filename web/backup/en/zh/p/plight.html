Plight

EN→ZH

What Does Plight Mean?

  • 困境
  • 境遇
  • 苦境

Plight Pronunciation

/plaɪt/

Plight Definition

noun

Definition

A dangerous, difficult, or otherwise unfortunate situation.

In ZH

一个危险、困难或其他不幸的处境。

How to Use Plight in a Sentence

  • She is in a plight that she cannot escape.
  • They shared stories of their plight during the war.
  • The plight of the refugees moved many to donate.
  • In light of their plight, the community came together to help.
  • The documentary highlighted the plight of the homeless.

Synonyms for Plight
