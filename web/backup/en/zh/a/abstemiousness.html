Abstemiousness

EN→ZH

What Does Abstemiousness Mean?

  • 节制
  • 饮食有节
  • 克制

Abstemiousness Pronunciation

/əbˈstiː.mi.əs.nəs/

Abstemiousness Definition

noun

Definition

the quality of being abstemious; restraint in the consumption of food and drink

In ZH

节制的品质；在食物和饮料消费中的克制

How to Use Abstemiousness in a Sentence

  • His abstemiousness at the dinner was noted by everyone.
  • The monk lived a life of abstemiousness, avoiding all luxuries.
  • They admired her abstemiousness in the face of temptation.
  • Abstemiousness is often encouraged in many religious practices.
  • Eating with abstemiousness can contribute to better health.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, particularly in religious contexts, abstemiousness is seen as a virtue associated with self-control and moderation.

Synonyms for Abstemiousness

  • Absolve
  • Absorb
  • Absquatulate
  • Abstemious
