Anemone

Pronunciation:/əˈnɛməni/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 海葵
  • 风铃草
  • 何首乌

Definitions

noun

Definition

A flowering plant or a type of sea creature known for its tentacles and vibrant colors.

In ZH

一种开花植物或一种以触手和鲜艳色彩著称的海洋生物。

Examples

  • The anemone swayed gently in the ocean current.
  • She planted anemones in her garden for their beautiful blooms.
  • Anemones are often found in coral reefs.
  • The clownfish lives among the tentacles of the anemone for protection.
  • In mythology, anemones are often associated with love and loss.
