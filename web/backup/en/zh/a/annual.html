Annual

Pronunciation:ˈæn.ju.əl
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 年度的
  • 每年的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Occurring once a year; happening every year.

In ZH

每年发生一次的；每年的。

Examples

  • The annual potato sack race has become a town favorite, especially since the mayor tripped last year and gave everyone a good laugh!
  • I just received my annual review at work; apparently, my 'watching cat videos' skills are on par!
  • With the annual alien invasion parade coming up, the residents are preparing their best extraterrestrial costumes.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, annual events such as festivals, holidays, and meetings are important for community bonding and celebration.

noun

Definition

A book or publication that is published once a year.

In ZH

一年出版一次的书籍或出版物。

Examples

  • The library just received the latest edition of the annual sports review, which features last year's underdogs winning the championship and giving the entire city a heart attack!
  • I love reading the annual report of our local zoo; last year, the penguin population grew so much they had to start an 'aquatic reality show'!
  • Each year, my grandmother gifts me an annual of her favorite jokes, which are mostly about how she survives family gatherings.

Cultural Notes

Annual publications often provide a recap of the year's events and are popular in fields like sports, education, and entertainment.
