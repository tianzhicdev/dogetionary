Abstrusity

EN→ZH

What Does Abstrusity Mean?

  • 深奥性
  • 难懂性

Abstrusity Pronunciation

/əbˈstrjuːsɪti/

Abstrusity Definition

noun

Definition

the quality or state of being abstruse; difficult to understand.

In ZH

深奥的特性或状态；难以理解的状态。

How to Use Abstrusity in a Sentence

  • The abstrusity of his theories discouraged many potential followers.
  • Despite its abstrusity, the book gained a loyal readership.
  • The abstrusity of the subject matter makes it challenging for beginners.
  • She found the abstrusity of his writing style to be off-putting.
  • The lecture on quantum physics was full of abstrusity that left the audience puzzled.

Synonyms for Abstrusity

  • Abscond
  • Absolve
  • Absorb
  • Absquatulate
  • Abstemious
