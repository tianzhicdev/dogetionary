Acute

Pronunciation:/əˈkjuːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 急性
  • 敏锐
  • 尖锐

Definitions

adjective

Definition

sharp or severe in effect; intense

In ZH

剧烈的；强烈的

Examples

  • She felt an acute pain in her side.
  • The situation required acute attention to detail.
  • He has an acute sense of hearing.
  • The doctor diagnosed her with acute appendicitis.
  • There was an acute awareness of the risks involved.
adjective

Definition

having or showing a perceptive understanding or insight; shrewd

In ZH

有敏锐的理解或洞察力；聪明的

Examples

  • His acute mind led him to discover new theories.
  • She has an acute ability to analyze data.
  • An acute observer can easily notice the subtleties of the conversation.
  • His acute insights helped the team make better decisions.
  • The critic had an acute understanding of the artist's intentions.
noun

Definition

a term used in music to indicate a high note or pitch

In ZH

在音乐中用于表示高音或音调的术语

Examples

  • The piece begins with an acute that sets the tone for the rest.
  • Composers often use acute notes to create tension in their music.
  • The acute melody was difficult for the musicians to perform.
  • She hit an acute note that resonated throughout the hall.
  • The song features a range of acute pitches.
