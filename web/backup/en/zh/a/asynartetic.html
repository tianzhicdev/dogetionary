Asynartetic

Pronunciation:/ˌeɪsɪnˈɑːrtɪtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 非连结的
  • 分离的
  • 非连接的
  • 解构的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Describing a type of structural arrangement in which the components are not connected or coupled in a traditional manner, often used in linguistic or philosophical contexts.

In ZH

描述一种结构安排，其组成部分没有以传统方式连接或耦合，通常用于语言学或哲学上下文。
