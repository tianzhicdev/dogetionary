Ascendant

Pronunciation:/əˈsɛndənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 上升的
  • 占主导地位的
  • 祖先

Definitions

noun

Definition

a person who is dominant or influential in a certain context; an ancestor.

In ZH

在某种背景下占主导或有影响力的人；祖先。

Examples

  • The ascendant leader rallied the team to victory.
  • In astrology, your ascendant sign can affect your personality.
  • Many cultures honor their ascendants in various ceremonies.
  • The family's ascendant status in society gave them many privileges.
  • Our ascendant interests are reflected in our choices.
adjective

Definition

rising in power or influence; rising.

In ZH

在权力或影响力上上升；上升的。

Examples

  • The ascendant power of the new technology reshaped the market.
  • She felt ascendant after receiving recognition for her work.
  • The ascendant artist is quickly becoming a household name.
  • As sustainability becomes important, green companies are ascendant.
  • The ascendant trend in fashion is towards eco-friendly materials.
