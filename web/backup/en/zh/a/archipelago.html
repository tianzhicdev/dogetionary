What Does Archipelago Mean?

Archipelago Pronunciation

/ˌɑːrkɪˈpɛlɪɡoʊ/

Archipelago Definition

noun

Definition

A group or chain of islands

In ZH

一组或一系列岛屿

How to Use Archipelago in a Sentence

  • The Philippines is an archipelago consisting of more than 7,000 islands.
  • We traveled to an archipelago in the Caribbean for our vacation.
  • The archipelago is home to many unique species.
  • Navigating between the islands of the archipelago can be challenging.
  • I have always dreamed of exploring an archipelago by boat.
