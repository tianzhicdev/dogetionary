Apropos

EN→ZH

What Does Apropos Mean?

  • 关于
  • 针对
  • 适合

Apropos Pronunciation

/ˌæprəˈpoʊ/

Apropos Definition

adverb

Definition

with reference to; concerning.

In ZH

关于；涉及；就...而言

How to Use Apropos in a Sentence

  • The discussion was apropos of recent events.
  • Her comments were apropos to the main topic of the meeting.
  • He made an apropos remark that caught everyone's attention.
  • The book is apropos of current social issues.
  • His insights were apropos to our ongoing project.
adjective

Definition

fitting; well-suited; relevant.

In ZH

合适的；相关的

How to Use Apropos in a Sentence

  • Her advice was apropos given the circumstances.
  • The film's theme was apropos to the challenges of our time.
  • His choice of words was apropos for the audience.
  • An apropos example can illustrate this point well.
  • Their conversation touched on an apropos subject.

Synonyms for Apropos

  • Apraxia
  • Apricity
