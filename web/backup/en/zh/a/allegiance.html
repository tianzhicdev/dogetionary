Allegiance

EN→ZH

What Does Allegiance Mean?

  • 忠诚
  • 效忠
  • allegiance

Allegiance Pronunciation

/əˈliːdʒəns/

Allegiance Definition

noun

Definition

loyalty or commitment to a superior or to a group or cause.

In ZH

对上级或某个团体或事业的忠诚或承诺。

How to Use Allegiance in a Sentence

  • Citizens are expected to show allegiance to their country.
  • She pledged her allegiance to the new political party.
  • His allegiance to the team was unwavering through the tough times.
  • They demanded allegiance from all members of the organization.
  • The soldiers took an oath of allegiance to defend their nation.

Synonyms for Allegiance

  • Allegory
  • Allele
  • Alleles
  • Allergies
