Apsidal

EN→ZH

What Does Apsidal Mean?

  • 椭圆形的
  • 与椭圆有关的

Apsidal Pronunciation

/əpˈsɪdəl/

Apsidal Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to the apsis or apsides; having the shape of an apsis, especially in reference to an ellipse or an orbit.

In ZH

与天体的椭圆体的极值点相关的；具有椭圆形状，特别是指椭圆或轨道。

How to Use Apsidal in a Sentence

  • The apsidal shape of the planet's orbit is crucial to its climate.
  • In architecture, the apsidal design enhances the acoustics of the space.
  • Astronomers calculated the apsidal precession of the orbit.
  • The church featured an apsidal end, characteristic of Romanesque architecture.
  • Studying apsidal motion helps in understanding binary star systems.
