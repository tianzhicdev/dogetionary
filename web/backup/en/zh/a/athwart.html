Athwart

Pronunciation:/əˈθwɔːrt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 横过
  • 横向
  • 对立地

Definitions

preposition

Definition

In a position across something; from side to side.

In ZH

在某物的横穿位置；从一侧到另一侧。

Examples

  • The bridge was built athwart the river.
  • They placed the fence athwart the garden.
  • He lay athwart the path, blocking the way.
  • The ship sailed athwart the waves.
  • The lines of text run athwart the page.
adverb

Definition

Crosswise; in a direction or position that is not parallel.

In ZH

横向；以非平行的方向或位置。

Examples

  • The roads intersect athwart.
  • She threw the ribbon athwart the table.
  • Athwart the sky, the birds flew.
  • The team went athwart their opponents’ plans.
  • The colors flowed athwart the canvas.
