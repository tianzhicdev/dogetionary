Accolade

EN→ZH

What Does Accolade Mean?

  • 赞扬
  • 荣誉
  • 奖赏

Accolade Pronunciation

/ˈækəleɪd/

Accolade Definition

noun

Definition

An award or privilege granted as a special honor or acknowledgment.

In ZH

作为特别荣誉或认可而授予的奖项或特权。

How to Use Accolade in a Sentence

  • She received accolades for her outstanding performance.
  • The film won several accolades at the international festival.
  • His work has earned him accolades from his peers.
  • The teacher bestowed accolades upon the students for their hard work.
  • Accolades are often given during award ceremonies.

Synonyms for Accolade

  • Access
  • Accoutrement
