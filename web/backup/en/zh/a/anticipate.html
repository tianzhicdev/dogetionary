Anticipate

EN→ZH

What Does Anticipate Mean?

  • 预期
  • 期待
  • 预料

Anticipate Pronunciation

/ænˈtɪsɪpeɪt/

Anticipate Definition

verb

Definition

to expect or predict something that will happen in the future

In ZH

预期或预测未来将要发生的事情

How to Use Anticipate in a Sentence

  • I anticipate that it will rain tomorrow.
  • She anticipates a promotion at work this year.
  • We should anticipate the needs of our customers.
  • He anticipates a lot of questions during the presentation.
  • They anticipate completing the project by next month.

Synonyms for Anticipate

  • Antagonistic
  • Antebellum
  • Antediluvian
  • Antepenultimate
  • Anthropocene
