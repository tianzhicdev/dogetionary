Advocate

Pronunciation:ˈæd.və.kɪt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 提倡者
  • 拥护者
  • 辩護人

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who publicly supports or recommends a particular cause or policy.

In ZH

一个公开支持或推荐特定事业或政策的人。

Examples

  • She is a strong advocate for environmental protection.
  • The organization has many advocates for mental health awareness.
  • He became an advocate for human rights after his experiences abroad.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, advocates often play a key role in social movements, and advocacy can involve activism, public speaking, and community organizing.

verb

Definition

To publicly support a particular cause or policy.

In ZH

公开支持某一特定事业或政策。

Examples

  • They advocate for increased funding for education.
  • The group advocates animal rights and welfare.
  • He advocates for a more inclusive workplace.

Cultural Notes

The verb form is commonly used in political contexts, where individuals or groups advocate for changes in laws or policies.
