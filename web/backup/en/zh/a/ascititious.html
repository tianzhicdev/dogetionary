Ascititious

EN→ZH

What Does Ascititious Mean?

  • 附加的
  • 外来的
  • 非自然的

Ascititious Pronunciation

/ˌæs.ɪˈtɪʃ.əs/

Ascititious Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or denoting something that is added or not inherent; supplementary.

In ZH

与附加或非固有的东西有关；补充的。

How to Use Ascititious in a Sentence

  • The ascititious features of the building were added in the last renovation.
  • His wealth was not ascititious; it was built from hard work.
  • She questioned the ascititious nature of the proposed benefits.
  • The medication had ascititious effects that were not listed in the brochure.
  • In literature, ascititious elements can enhance the narrative.

Cultural Notes

The term 'ascititious' is often used in academic or formal contexts, particularly in philosophy and literature, to discuss concepts of addition versus inherent qualities.

Synonyms for Ascititious
