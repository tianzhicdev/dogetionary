Argle-Bargle

EN→ZH

What Does Argle-Bargle Mean?

  • 争论
  • 无谓的争辩
  • 喋喋不休

Argle-Bargle Pronunciation

/ˈɑːrɡəl ˈbɑːrɡəl/

Argle-Bargle Definition

noun

Definition

A lengthy or complicated dispute or argument

In ZH

冗长或复杂的争论或争辩

How to Use Argle-Bargle in a Sentence

  • The meeting descended into an argle-bargle about minor details.
  • There was a lot of argle-bargle over the new policy changes.
  • Their argument turned into an argle-bargle that lasted for hours.
  • After much argle-bargle, they finally reached a consensus.
  • The newspaper article was filled with argle-bargle on the topic.

Cultural Notes

Often used in a humorous context to describe unnecessary or trivial arguments.

Synonyms for Argle-Bargle

  • Argosy
  • Argot
  • Argue
  • Argy-Bargy
