Airway

EN→ZH

What Does Airway Mean?

  • 气道
  • 航空道

Airway Pronunciation

ˈɛərˌweɪ

Airway Definition

noun

Definition

The passage by which air enters and leaves the lungs, consisting of the trachea and bronchi.

In ZH

空气进出肺部的通道，包括气管和支气管。

How to Use Airway in a Sentence

  • After spending two hours in a crowded elevator, I realized that the only airway I needed was a very strong air freshener!
  • If only my airway could magically deliver pizza instead of air, I’d have the best of both worlds during my Netflix binge!
  • Trying to explain COVID-19 to my dog was tough, but I told him that the airway is our path for breathing and barking - very important!

Cultural Notes

Understanding the airway is essential in both medical and everyday contexts, especially when discussing health issues or emergencies.
