Audacity

EN→ZH

What Does Audacity Mean?

  • 大胆
  • 无畏
  • 厚颜无耻

Audacity Pronunciation

/ɔːˈdæs.ɪ.ti/

Audacity Definition

noun

Definition

the willingness to take bold risks; rude or disrespectful behavior; impudence.

In ZH

愿意冒大胆风险的状态；粗鲁或不尊重的行为；无礼。

How to Use Audacity in a Sentence

  • She had the audacity to challenge the CEO’s decision.
  • His audacity in asking for a raise during a recession surprised everyone.
  • It takes audacity to stand up for your beliefs in front of a crowd.
  • The audacity of the prank left the teachers in shock.
  • With audacity, she defied all expectations and succeeded.

Synonyms for Audacity

  • Audacious
