Abjuration

Pronunciation:/ˌæb.dʒʊˈreɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 放弃
  • 撤回
  • 弃绝

Definitions

noun

Definition

a solemn renunciation or rejection of a belief, cause, or claim

In ZH

对一种信仰、事业或主张的庄严拒绝或放弃

Examples

  • His abjuration of the faith shocked his family.
  • The politician's abjuration of his previous statements was controversial.
  • She made an abjuration of her past mistakes in her speech.
  • The cult required members to make an abjuration of their former lives.
  • After much thought, he announced his abjuration of violence.
