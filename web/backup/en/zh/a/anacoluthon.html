Anacoluthon

EN→ZH

What Does Anacoluthon Mean?

  • 不连贯句
  • 不协调
  • 不成文句
  • 断句
  • 句法不顺

Anacoluthon Pronunciation

/ˌænəˈkəluːθɒn/

Anacoluthon Definition

noun

Definition

A syntactical inconsistency in a sentence, where the expected grammatical structure is not followed.

In ZH

一种句法不连贯的情况，句子中未遵循预期的语法结构。

How to Use Anacoluthon in a Sentence

  • The use of anacoluthon can add a unique rhythm to poetic writing.
  • He began his thought clearly but ended with an anacoluthon that confused everyone.
  • Anacoluthon can be found in casual speech when ideas are spoken in a disjointed manner.
  • It’s common in literature for authors to use anacoluthon to create a stream-of-consciousness effect.
  • In conversation, many people unknowingly employ anacoluthon when articulating complex thoughts.
