Aestheticism

Pronunciation:/iˈsθɛtɪsɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 审美主义
  • 美学主义

Definitions

noun

Definition

A philosophical theory which holds that the perception of beauty is the primary aim of art and that beauty is its own justification.

In ZH

一种哲学理论，认为对美的感知是艺术的主要目的，且美本身就是其合理性。

Examples

  • Aestheticism emphasizes the importance of beauty in art.
  • Many renowned artists were influenced by the principles of aestheticism.
  • The movement of aestheticism rejected the idea that art should serve a moral or political purpose.
  • In literature, aestheticism often focuses on sensory experiences.
  • Aestheticism in the 19th century celebrated the role of beauty in everyday life.
