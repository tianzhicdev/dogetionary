Access

Pronunciation:/ˈæksɛs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 接入
  • 访问
  • 进入
  • 获取

Definitions

noun

Definition

The means of approaching or entering a place.

In ZH

接近或进入某个地方的方式。

Examples

  • You need a key to gain access to the building.
  • The access to the park is restricted at night.
  • We have access to a variety of resources.
  • She has easy access to the internet at home.
  • Limited access to clean water is a major issue in many areas.
verb

Definition

To obtain or retrieve (data or information).

In ZH

获取或检索（数据或信息）。

Examples

  • You can access your emails from any device.
  • He accessed the database to find the information.
  • They were able to access the files remotely.
  • Please access the website for more details.
  • She accessed the report and found useful data.
