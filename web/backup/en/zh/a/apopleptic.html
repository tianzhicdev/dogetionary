Apopleptic

Pronunciation:/ˌæp.əˈlɛp.tɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 中风的
  • 愤怒的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

overcome with anger; extremely indignant.

In ZH

极度愤怒；非常愤慨的。

Examples

  • He was apopleptic when he found out about the betrayal.
  • The apopleptic response from the audience was palpable after the announcement.
  • She became apopleptic at the news of the unfair decision.
  • His face turned red and apopleptic when he realized he was being mocked.
  • The boss was apopleptic during the meeting after seeing the poor performance report.
adjective

Definition

relating to apoplexy, which is a sudden loss of consciousness or voluntary motion due to the rupture or blockage of a blood vessel in the brain.

In ZH

与中风相关联，即由于大脑内血管破裂或堵塞而导致的突然失去意识或自愿运动的状态。

Examples

  • The symptoms of an apopleptic event can be life-threatening.
  • He suffered an apopleptic fit that required immediate medical attention.
  • They discussed the risks of apopleptic strokes in older patients.
  • The doctor explained that apopleptic episodes require prompt treatment.
  • After his apopleptic attack, he made significant lifestyle changes.
