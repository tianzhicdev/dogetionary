Aggle

Pronunciation:/ˈæɡəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 群
  • 鹅群
  • 一群

Definitions

noun

Definition

A group of geese on the ground or a cluster of something loosely formed.

In ZH

一种在地面上的鹅群或松散成群的事物的集群。

Examples

  • The aggle of geese waddled across the field.
  • We stumbled upon an aggle of ducks by the pond.
  • An aggle of children gathered around the ice cream truck.
  • The aggle of rabbits in the garden was a delightful sight.
  • There was an unexpected aggle of tourists at the viewpoint.
