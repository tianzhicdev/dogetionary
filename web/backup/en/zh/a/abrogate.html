Abrogate

Pronunciation:/ˈæb.rəˌɡeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 废除
  • 撤销
  • 取消

Definitions

verb

Definition

To abolish or annul by authoritative action; to do away with.

In ZH

通过权威行为废除或撤销；取消。

Examples

  • The government decided to abrogate the outdated law.
  • After the scandal, the university chose to abrogate the contract with the consulting firm.
  • The new policy will abrogate the previous regulations.
  • Some judges have the power to abrogate unjust laws.
  • In certain countries, treaties can be abrogated by one party under specific conditions.
