Ascetic

Pronunciation:/əˈsɛtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 苦行者
  • 禁欲者
  • 禁欲主义者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who practices severe self-discipline and abstention from all forms of indulgence, typically for religious reasons.

In ZH

一个为了宗教原因而实践严格自律和戒绝各种享乐的人。

Examples

  • He lived as an ascetic in the mountains, away from worldly pleasures.
  • The ascetic lifestyle appealed to those seeking spiritual enlightenment.
  • Many religions have ascetic traditions that emphasize self-denial.
  • She admired the monk's ascetic dedication to his faith.
  • Ascetics often believe that detachment from material goods leads to spiritual growth.
adjective

Definition

Characterized by or suggesting the practice of severe self-discipline and abstention.

In ZH

以严厉自律和戒绝为特征或暗示的。

Examples

  • His ascetic approach to life inspired many students.
  • The diet was ascetic, focusing only on the most basic necessities.
  • She adopted an ascetic lifestyle after her pilgrimage.
  • The ascetic practices of the monks were respected by the community.
  • Ascetic values can lead to a deeper understanding of one's purpose.
