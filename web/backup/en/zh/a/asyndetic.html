Asyndetic

Pronunciation:/əˈsɪn.dɛ.tɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无连词的
  • 省略连词的
  • 非连词的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by the absence of conjunctions between parts of a sentence or between sentences.

In ZH

特征是句子部分之间或句子之间缺少连词的。

Examples

  • In her speech, she used asyndetic structure to create a powerful impact.
  • The poem had an asyndetic style that emphasized its emotional tone.
  • He listed the ingredients asyndetically: flour, sugar, eggs, butter.
  • The use of asyndetic phrases can enhance the rhythm of writing.
  • Asyndetic constructions are often found in poetry and literary prose.
