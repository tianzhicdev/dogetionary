Astrophysics

EN→ZH

What Does Astrophysics Mean?

  • 天体物理学
  • 天文学

Astrophysics Pronunciation

/ˌæstrəˈfɪzɪks/

Astrophysics Definition

noun

Definition

The branch of astronomy that deals with the physical properties and behavior of celestial bodies and the universe as a whole.

In ZH

研究天体及宇宙整体的物理特性和行为的天文学分支。

How to Use Astrophysics in a Sentence

  • Astrophysics helps us understand the life cycle of stars.
  • Many universities offer degrees in astrophysics.
  • Astrophysics has revealed the presence of black holes in our galaxy.
  • The study of astrophysics involves complex mathematics and physics.
  • Astrophysics seeks to explain phenomena such as dark matter and dark energy.
