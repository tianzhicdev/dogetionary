Apothegm

Pronunciation:/ˈæpəθɛm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 箴言
  • 警句
  • 格言

Definitions

noun

Definition

A pithy observation that contains a general truth.

In ZH

一种含有普遍真理的简练观察。

Examples

  • She shared an apothegm that resonated with everyone in the room.
  • The philosopher was known for his clever apothegms about life.
  • He delivered an apothegm that perfectly captured the essence of wisdom.
  • Her favorite apothegm was about the importance of perseverance.
  • In the speech, he quoted an ancient apothegm that inspired the audience.
