Averring

Pronunciation:/əˈvɜːrɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 断言
  • 声称
  • 坚称

Definitions

verb

Definition

to assert or affirm with confidence

In ZH

坚定地断言或确认

Examples

  • She is averring that she saw the accident happen.
  • The lawyer was averring his client's innocence.
  • In his speech, he was averring the importance of education.
  • They are averring their rights to the property.
  • The witness is averring that he heard the conversation.
