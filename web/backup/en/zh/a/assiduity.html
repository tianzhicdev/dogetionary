Assiduity

Pronunciation:/ˌæsɪˈdjuːɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 勤勉
  • 专心致志
  • 坚持不懈

Definitions

noun

Definition

Constant or close attention to what one is doing.

In ZH

对自己所做之事的持续或密切关注。

Examples

  • Her assiduity in studying for the exam paid off with high marks.
  • The team showed great assiduity in completing the project on time.
  • Assiduity in practice is necessary for mastering any skill.
  • He is known for his assiduity and dedication to his work.
  • Their assiduity in meeting deadlines impressed the clients.
