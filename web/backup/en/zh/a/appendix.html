Appendix

Pronunciation:/əˈpɛndɪks/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 附录
  • 附加物
  • 盲肠

Definitions

noun

Definition

A supplementary material at the end of a book or document, providing additional information or resources.

In ZH

书籍或文档末尾的补充材料，提供额外的信息或资源。

Examples

  • The research paper includes an appendix with detailed data.
  • You should check the appendix for more information on the topic.
  • In the appendix, there are several charts and graphs.
  • The author added an appendix to clarify his findings.
  • Don't forget to review the appendix before the conference.
noun

Definition

A part of the body in humans and some animals, typically a tube-like structure attached to the large intestine.

In ZH

人体和某些动物体内的一部分，通常是一个附着在大肠上的管状结构。

Examples

  • The appendix can become inflamed in a condition called appendicitis.
  • Doctors often remove the appendix if it is infected.
  • Not everyone has their appendix removed after it becomes inflamed.
  • The function of the appendix is still not completely understood.
  • An inflamed appendix may require surgery.
