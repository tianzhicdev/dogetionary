Asynchronous

EN→ZH

What Does Asynchronous Mean?

  • 异步的
  • 非同步的

Asynchronous Pronunciation

/eɪˈsɪŋ.krə.nəs/

Asynchronous Definition

adjective

Definition

Not occurring at the same time; not synchronized in time.

In ZH

不同步的；不同时发生的。

How to Use Asynchronous in a Sentence

  • Asynchronous communication allows people to respond at their convenience.
  • The software can handle asynchronous calls to improve efficiency.
  • Asynchronous learning means students can study at their own pace.
  • In programming, asynchronous operations can prevent blocking.
  • Email is a form of asynchronous communication.
