Abeyance

Pronunciation:/əˈbeɪəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 暂缓
  • 悬而未决
  • 停滞

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of temporary disuse or suspension.

In ZH

暂时不使用或中止的状态。

Examples

  • The project is currently in abeyance due to budget cuts.
  • The legal proceedings were held in abeyance until further notice.
  • Her plans for travel were put in abeyance after the pandemic.
  • The committee's decisions are in abeyance until the board convenes next week.
  • Several proposals are in abeyance until the new regulations are established.
