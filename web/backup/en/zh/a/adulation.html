Adulation

EN→ZH

What Does Adulation Mean?

  • 崇拜
  • 奉承
  • 过分的赞美

Adulation Pronunciation

/ˌædʒ.əˈleɪ.ʃən/

Adulation Definition

noun

Definition

Excessive admiration or praise.

In ZH

过分的崇拜或赞美。

How to Use Adulation in a Sentence

  • The actor received much adulation after his performance.
  • His speech was met with adulation from the audience.
  • She enjoyed the adulation of her fans.
  • The coach's adulation of his star player was evident.
  • Adulation can often distort reality and lead to disappointment.

Synonyms for Adulation

  • Adulate
  • Adumbrate
  • Adumbration
  • Adumbrative
