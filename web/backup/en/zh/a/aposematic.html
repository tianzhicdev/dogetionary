Aposematic

Pronunciation:/ˌæpəʊsəˈmætɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 警告色
  • 警戒色

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Referring to coloration or markings that serve as a warning to potential predators, often indicating toxicity or unpalatability.

In ZH

指用于警告潜在捕食者的颜色或标记，通常表示毒性或不可口性。

Examples

  • The bright colors of the poison dart frog are aposematic, signaling danger to predators.
  • Many insects use aposematic signals to deter birds from eating them.
  • Aposematic species often have vibrant patterns that attract attention.
  • The butterfly's wings display aposematic colors, warning of its unpalatable taste.
  • Understanding aposematic traits helps ecologists study predator-prey interactions.
