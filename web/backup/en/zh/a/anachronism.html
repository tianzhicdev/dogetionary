Anachronism

Pronunciation:/əˈnækrəˌnɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 时代错误
  • 不合时宜的事物
  • 时空错位

Definitions

noun

Definition

A thing belonging or appropriate to a period other than that in which it exists, especially a thing that is conspicuously old-fashioned.

In ZH

一种属于或适合于某个时期但存在于其他时期的事物，特别是一个明显过时的事物。

Examples

  • The use of a smartphone in a medieval movie is a clear anachronism.
  • He made an anachronism by wearing a watch to a historical reenactment.
  • Her fashion choices are often seen as anachronisms from the 1980s.
  • In literature, anachronism can be used intentionally to create humor or critique.
  • The painting contains several anachronisms that blend different historical periods.
