Apogee

EN→ZH

What Does Apogee Mean?

  • 远地点
  • 顶点
  • 最高点

Apogee Pronunciation

/ˈæp.əˌdʒi/

Apogee Definition

noun

Definition

The highest point in the development of something; a climax or culmination.

In ZH

事物发展的最高点；高潮或巅峰。

How to Use Apogee in a Sentence

  • The project reached its apogee when it garnered international recognition.
  • At the apogee of her career, she was one of the most famous actresses in the world.
  • The apogee of the artist's work was marked by an exhibition in a prestigious gallery.
  • Technological advancements have brought humanity to its apogee in space exploration.
  • The apogee of the season's storyline captivated the audience completely.
noun

Definition

The point in the orbit of the moon or a satellite at which it is farthest from the earth.

In ZH

月球或卫星轨道中距离地球最远的点。

How to Use Apogee in a Sentence

  • The satellite's apogee allows it to cover a larger area of the Earth's surface.
  • Astronomers calculated the apogee of the moon for tonight's lunar event.
  • Understanding the apogee and perigee of satellites is crucial for successful launches.
  • The apogee of the orbit is where the satellite experiences the least gravitational pull.
  • During the apogee, the satellite's speed decreases significantly.
