Amphibology

Pronunciation:/æmˌfɪbəˈlɔɪdʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 两义论
  • 模棱两可的说法
  • 含糊不清

Definitions

noun

Definition

A phrase or sentence that can be interpreted in more than one way, often leading to ambiguity.

In ZH

一个可以用多种方式解读的短语或句子，常常导致模棱两可。

Examples

  • The term 'old men and women' is an example of amphibology since it can refer to either old men and women or young men and old women.
  • Amphibology often arises in legal documents, where misinterpretation can lead to disputes.
  • In literature, amphibology can create double meanings that enrich the text.
  • The phrase 'I saw the man with the telescope' is an example of amphibology.
  • Politicians sometimes use amphibology to avoid committing to a clear position.
