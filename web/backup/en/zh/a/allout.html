Allout

Pronunciation:/ˈɔːl aʊt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 全力以赴
  • 彻底
  • 全盘
  • 全力

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Fully committed; without reservation or limitation.

In ZH

完全投入；没有保留或限制。

Examples

  • He gave an allout effort in the marathon.
  • The team launched an allout campaign to win the championship.
  • They went allout for the party decorations.
  • She put in an allout performance during her audition.
  • The company is making an allout attempt to improve customer service.
noun

Definition

A situation where everything is done with maximum effort or intensity.

In ZH

一种情况，所有事情都以最大努力或强度进行。

Examples

  • It was an allout push to meet the deadline.
  • The war turned into an allout conflict among nations.
  • In sports, an allout strategy is sometimes necessary to win.
  • The presentation was an allout success, exceeding all expectations.
  • They treated the event as an allout celebration.
