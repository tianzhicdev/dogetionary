Ascribe

EN→ZH

What Does Ascribe Mean?

  • 归因于
  • 归咎于
  • 视为
  • 认为

Ascribe Pronunciation

/əˈskraɪb/

Ascribe Definition

verb

Definition

To attribute something to a particular cause, source, or author.

In ZH

将某事归因于特定的原因、来源或作者。

How to Use Ascribe in a Sentence

  • Many historians ascribe the success of the movement to grassroots activism.
  • He ascribes his good health to regular exercise and a balanced diet.
  • The critics ascribe the novel's popularity to its relatable characters.
  • Some scientists ascribe the changes in climate to human activity.
  • She ascribes her artistic talent to her family background.

Synonyms for Ascribe

  • Ascendant
  • Ascent
  • Ascertain
  • Ascertaining
