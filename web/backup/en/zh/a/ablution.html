Ablution

EN→ZH

What Does Ablution Mean?

  • 洗礼
  • 沐浴
  • 洗手

Ablution Pronunciation

/əˈbluːʃən/

Ablution Definition

noun

Definition

the act of washing oneself, often used in a religious context.

In ZH

洗净自我的行为，通常用于宗教背景。

How to Use Ablution in a Sentence

  • Before entering the temple, they performed their ablutions.
  • The ritual ablution is an important part of the ceremony.
  • He felt refreshed after his morning ablution.
  • Ablution is often practiced before prayer in many religions.
  • She included a period of ablution in her daily routine.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, particularly in religious practices, ablutions are considered essential for purification before engaging in sacred activities.
