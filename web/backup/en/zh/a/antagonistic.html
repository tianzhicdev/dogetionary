Antagonistic

EN→ZH

What Does Antagonistic Mean?

  • 对抗的
  • 敌对的

Antagonistic Pronunciation

/ænˌtæɡəˈnɪstɪk/

Antagonistic Definition

adjective

Definition

showing or feeling active opposition or hostility toward someone or something

In ZH

对某人或某事表现出或感到积极的对立或敌意

How to Use Antagonistic in a Sentence

  • His antagonistic attitude towards his teammates was detrimental to team morale.
  • The antagonistic relationship between the two rivals escalated into a fierce competition.
  • Antagonistic forces often lead to conflict and division within a community.
  • She maintained an antagonistic stance during the negotiation, making it difficult to reach an agreement.
  • The antagonistic nature of the debate polarized the audience.
