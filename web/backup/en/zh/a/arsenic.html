Arsenic

EN→ZH

What Does Arsenic Mean?

  • 砷

Arsenic Pronunciation

/ˈɑːrsənɪk/

Arsenic Definition

noun

Definition

a chemical element with symbol As and atomic number 33, known for its toxicity and used in various compounds and alloys.

In ZH

一种化学元素，符号为 As，原子序数为 33，以其毒性而闻名，并用于各种化合物和合金。

How to Use Arsenic in a Sentence

  • Arsenic is often found in groundwater in some regions.
  • The use of arsenic in pesticides has raised health concerns.
  • He studied the effects of arsenic exposure on human health.
  • Arsenic can be found in some traditional medicines.
  • The cleanup of arsenic-contaminated sites can be very expensive.
