Ambiguity

EN→ZH

What Does Ambiguity Mean?

  • 模棱两可
  • 不明确性
  • 含糊不清

Ambiguity Pronunciation

/æmˈbɪɡ.juː.ɪ.ti/

Ambiguity Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being open to more than one interpretation; inexactness.

In ZH

开放于多种解释的特性；不精确性。

How to Use Ambiguity in a Sentence

  • The ambiguity in the contract led to several misunderstandings.
  • His statement was filled with ambiguity, leaving everyone confused.
  • There is a certain ambiguity in the way she answered the question.
  • The plot of the movie is full of ambiguity, making it intriguing.
  • Ambiguity can sometimes lead to creative interpretations in literature.

Synonyms for Ambiguity

  • Ambagious
  • Ambivalence
  • Ambivalent
  • Amboceptor
  • Ambrosial
