Arcadia

EN→ZH

What Does Arcadia Mean?

  • 阿卡迪亚
  • 乌托邦
  • 田园诗境

Arcadia Pronunciation

/ɑːrˈkeɪdiə/

Arcadia Definition

noun

Definition

A place or state of simple pleasure and quiet, typically associated with rural or pastoral life.

In ZH

一个简单快乐与宁静的地方或状态，通常与乡村或田园生活相关联。

How to Use Arcadia in a Sentence

  • The idea of Arcadia has influenced many works of art and literature.
  • They sought refuge in an Arcadia away from the chaos of the city.
  • The garden was designed to evoke a sense of Arcadia.
  • In her paintings, she captures the beauty of Arcadia.
  • The couple dreamed of retiring to an Arcadia by the sea.

Cultural Notes

Arcadia is often referenced in literature and art as a symbol of idealized rural life and harmony with nature.
