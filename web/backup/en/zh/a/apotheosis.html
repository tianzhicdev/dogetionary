Apotheosis

EN→ZH

What Does Apotheosis Mean?

  • 升华
  • 神化
  • 典范

Apotheosis Pronunciation

/əˌpɒθ.iˈoʊ.sɪs/

Apotheosis Definition

noun

Definition

the highest point in the development of something; a culmination or climax.

In ZH

某事物发展的最高点；顶点或高潮。

How to Use Apotheosis in a Sentence

  • The apotheosis of the artist's career came with the release of their final album.
  • In classical literature, the apotheosis of a hero often signifies their transformation into a god.
  • The film was seen as the apotheosis of the genre, raising the standard for all subsequent works.
  • His speech marked the apotheosis of his political career, bringing him national acclaim.
  • The apotheosis of the project occurred when it received multiple awards at the festival.

Synonyms for Apotheosis
