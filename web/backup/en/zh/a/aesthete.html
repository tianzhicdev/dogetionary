Aesthete

Pronunciation:/ˈiːsθiːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 审美家
  • 美学家
  • 艺术爱好者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who has a special appreciation of art and beauty.

In ZH

对艺术和美有特殊欣赏的人。

Examples

  • The aesthete walked through the gallery, admiring each piece of art.
  • As an aesthete, she had a keen eye for beautiful designs in everyday objects.
  • He considered himself an aesthete, always seeking beauty in nature.
  • The film was a visual feast, appealing to the aesthete within everyone.
  • Her home was a reflection of her personality as an aesthete, filled with art and elegant decor.
