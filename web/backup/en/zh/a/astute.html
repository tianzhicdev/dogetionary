Astute

Pronunciation:/əˈstjuːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 精明的
  • 敏锐的
  • 聪明的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having or showing an ability to accurately assess situations or people and turn this to one's advantage

In ZH

具有或展示准确评估情况或人并将其利用于自己利益的能力

Examples

  • She is an astute businesswoman who always knows how to negotiate better deals.
  • His astute observation about the market trends helped the company thrive.
  • The detective was astute in noticing the smallest details at the crime scene.
  • An astute judge of character, she quickly realized the man was not trustworthy.
  • Astute investors are able to predict market shifts before they happen.
