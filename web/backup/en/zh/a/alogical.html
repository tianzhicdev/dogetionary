Alogical

EN→ZH

What Does Alogical Mean?

  • 不合逻辑的
  • 无逻辑的

Alogical Pronunciation

/əˈlɒdʒɪkl/

Alogical Definition

adjective

Definition

Not adhering to the rules of logic; lacking reason or sense.

In ZH

不遵循逻辑规则；缺乏理由或意义。

How to Use Alogical in a Sentence

  • The argument presented was alogical and hard to follow.
  • His alogical reasoning led to a series of mistakes.
  • In a debate, alogical statements can undermine your credibility.
  • They proposed an alogical solution to a complex problem.
  • The film was criticized for its alogical plot twists.
