Avis

EN→ZH

What Does Avis Mean?

  • 通知
  • 意见
  • 建议
  • 看法
  • 评论

Avis Pronunciation

/ˈeɪ.vɪs/

Avis Definition

noun

Definition

a formal notice or announcement, especially regarding a requirement or advice.

In ZH

正式通知或公告，尤其是关于要求或建议的内容。

How to Use Avis in a Sentence

  • The avis was sent out to all employees.
  • Please read the avis regarding the new company policy.
  • I received an avis about the upcoming meeting.
  • The avis clearly stated the deadline for submissions.
  • An avis will be published next week to inform the public.
noun

Definition

a legal or official notice, often relating to public affairs.

In ZH

法律或官方通知，通常与公共事务有关。

How to Use Avis in a Sentence

  • He posted an avis about the town hall meeting.
  • The avis was required by law to be in the local newspaper.
  • An avis regarding the new regulations was issued last month.
  • Citizens are advised to pay attention to any official avis.
  • This avis is crucial for understanding your rights.
