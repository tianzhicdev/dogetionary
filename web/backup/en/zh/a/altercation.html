Altercation

EN→ZH

What Does Altercation Mean?

  • 争执
  • 争吵
  • 口角

Altercation Pronunciation

/ˌɔːltərˈkeɪʃən/

Altercation Definition

noun

Definition

A noisy argument or disagreement, especially in public.

In ZH

一种嘈杂的争论或分歧，尤其是在公共场合。

How to Use Altercation in a Sentence

  • The altercation between the two neighbors escalated quickly.
  • There was a loud altercation outside the bar last night.
  • He avoided getting involved in the altercation.
  • The police were called after the altercation turned violent.
  • Witnesses reported a heated altercation in the parking lot.
