Airways

EN→ZH

What Does Airways Mean?

  • 气道
  • 航空公司
  • 气路

Airways Pronunciation

ˈɛrweɪz

Airways Definition

noun

Definition

The passages through which air moves in the lungs; also, a term used to describe the routes used by aircraft for travel.

In ZH

气道指的是空气在肺部流动的通道；同时，这个词也用于描述飞机飞行的航线。

How to Use Airways in a Sentence

  • When I heard that they were adding more airways for direct flights, I thought, 'Great! Now I can avoid layovers, just like avoiding my ex at a party!'
  • After the airways were cleared, pilots could finally see the sunshine peeking through the clouds, making them feel like they were flying into a postcard!
  • I love how airlines say they have the 'best airways,' but the only thing I want is to not be seated next to someone with a giant inflatable neck pillow!

Cultural Notes

In aviation, 'airways' refer not only to the navigational routes in the sky but also to the notion of accessibility and connectivity in global travel. Recently, during the pandemic, the term has also been humorously juxtaposed with how our 'airways' are affected by wearing masks.

verb

Definition

To provide an air route for an aircraft; to facilitate air travel or communication between locations.

In ZH

为飞机提供空中航线；促进地点之间的空中旅行或交流。

How to Use Airways in a Sentence

  • The new government initiative aims to airway more cities, but the only thing we can think of is air-fried snacks making their way into our homes!
  • As they were about to airway the emergency supplies, the pilot joked that this was the fastest way to deliver pizza!
  • The tech company announced its plan to airway their latest drone delivery service, because who doesn't want to order food and have it hover to their door?

Cultural Notes

In modern discussions, 'airway' as a verb has not been used frequently but can joke about the processes involved in enhancing air travel efficiency. Tech advancements and companies often claim they 'airway' routes to better service.
