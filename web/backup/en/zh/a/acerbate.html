Acerbate

Pronunciation:/əˈsɜːr.beɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 加剧
  • 恶化
  • 刺激

Definitions

verb

Definition

To make a problem, situation, or feeling worse; to aggravate.

In ZH

使问题、情况或感受更糟糕；加重。

Examples

  • The new policy will only acerbate the existing issues.
  • His comments seemed to acerbate the already tense situation.
  • Ignoring the problem will acerbate the conflict.
  • The lack of communication can acerbate misunderstandings.
  • Raising taxes during a recession may acerbate public dissatisfaction.
