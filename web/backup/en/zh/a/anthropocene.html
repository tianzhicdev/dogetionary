Anthropocene

EN→ZH

What Does Anthropocene Mean?

  • 人类世
  • 人类纪
  • 人类时代

Anthropocene Pronunciation

/ænθrəˈpoʊsiːn/

Anthropocene Definition

noun

Definition

A proposed geological epoch that is marked by the significant impact of human activity on the Earth's geology and ecosystems.

In ZH

一个提议的地质年代，以人类活动对地球的地质和生态系统的重大影响为标志。

How to Use Anthropocene in a Sentence

  • The Anthropocene is characterized by climate change and biodiversity loss.
  • Many scientists argue that we are currently living in the Anthropocene epoch.
  • Urbanization is a hallmark of the Anthropocene era.
  • The Anthropocene highlights the need for sustainable practices.
  • Literature from the Anthropocene often explores human-nature relationships.

Synonyms for Anthropocene

  • Antebellum
  • Antediluvian
  • Antepenultimate
  • Anthropocentric
