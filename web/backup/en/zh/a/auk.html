Auk

Pronunciation:/ɔːk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 海雀
  • 鸥

Definitions

noun

Definition

A type of seabird belonging to the family Alcidae, characterized by its compact body, short wings, and often colorful plumage.

In ZH

一种属于海雀科的海鸟，特征是体型紧凑，翅膀短，通常羽毛色彩鲜艳。

Examples

  • The auk is known for its ability to dive deep in search of food.
  • Auks are often found in cold coastal regions.
  • The extinct great auk was once abundant in the North Atlantic.
  • Researchers study auks to understand changes in marine ecosystems.
  • The population of some auk species is declining due to climate change.
