Agog

EN→ZH

What Does Agog Mean?

  • 渴望的
  • 兴奋的
  • 急切的

Agog Pronunciation

/əˈɡɒɡ/

Agog Definition

adjective

Definition

Very eager or excited to see or do something.

In ZH

非常渴望或兴奋去看或做某事。

How to Use Agog in a Sentence

  • She was agog at the thought of meeting her favorite author.
  • The children were agog with excitement as the circus came to town.
  • He was agog to hear about the latest developments in the project.
  • They stood agog in front of the grand palace, awestruck by its beauty.
  • The audience was agog with anticipation before the show started.

Cultural Notes

Often used in a context where someone is eagerly awaiting something thrilling or enjoyable.

Synonyms for Agog

  • Agogness
