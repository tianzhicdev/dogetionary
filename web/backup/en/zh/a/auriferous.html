Auriferous

Pronunciation:/ɔːˈrɪfərəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 含金的
  • 金矿的
  • 富含黄金的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

containing gold; bearing gold.

In ZH

含金的；富含黄金的。

Examples

  • The auriferous rocks in the region are highly sought after by miners.
  • Geologists discovered auriferous veins in the mountains.
  • Many rivers in the area are known for their auriferous sands.
  • The mining company specializes in extracting auriferous materials.
  • The auriferous landscape has attracted prospectors for centuries.
