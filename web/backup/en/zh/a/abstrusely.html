Abstrusely

EN→ZH

What Does Abstrusely Mean?

  • 深奥地
  • 难解地
  • 晦涩地

Abstrusely Pronunciation

/əbˈstruːsli/

Abstrusely Definition

adverb

Definition

in a manner that is difficult to understand or obscure

In ZH

以难以理解或晦涩的方式

How to Use Abstrusely in a Sentence

  • He explained the theory abstrusely, leaving most of the audience confused.
  • The text was written abstrusely, making it difficult for students to follow.
  • Philosophers often express their ideas abstrusely, challenging common understanding.
  • The lecture was delivered abstrusely, which frustrated many attendees.
  • She tends to write abstrusely, which can alienate her readers.

Synonyms for Abstrusely

  • Abscond
  • Absolve
  • Absorb
  • Absquatulate
  • Abstemious
