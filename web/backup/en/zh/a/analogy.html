Analogy

EN→ZH

What Does Analogy Mean?

  • 类比
  • 比喻
  • 类推

Analogy Pronunciation

/əˈnælədʒi/

Analogy Definition

noun

Definition

A comparison between two things, typically for the purpose of explanation or clarification.

In ZH

一种比较两个事物的方法，通常用于解释或澄清。

How to Use Analogy in a Sentence

  • The teacher used an analogy to help the students understand the complex concept.
  • He drew an analogy between the human brain and a computer.
  • In her speech, she made an analogy to illustrate the importance of teamwork.
  • The book contains several analogies that simplify difficult ideas.
  • An effective analogy can make an argument much stronger.

Synonyms for Analogy

  • Anachronism
  • Anachronistic
  • Anacoluthon
  • Anagnorisis
  • Analogous
