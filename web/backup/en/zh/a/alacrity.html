Alacrity

Pronunciation:/əˈlækrɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 敏捷
  • 乐意
  • 欣然

Definitions

noun

Definition

Brisk and cheerful readiness

In ZH

迅速而愉快的准备

Examples

  • She accepted the invitation with alacrity.
  • The team responded to the challenge with alacrity.
  • He approached his duties with alacrity and enthusiasm.
  • They completed the project with alacrity, impressing their supervisor.
  • Alacrity in the face of changes is essential for success.
