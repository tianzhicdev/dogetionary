Acanthus

Pronunciation:/əˈkænθəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 牛膝草
  • 角蕨

Definitions

noun

Definition

A plant of the genus Acanthus, often used in ornamental gardens and as a motif in architectural decoration.

In ZH

刺蓟属植物，常用于观赏花园和建筑装饰的图案。

Examples

  • The acanthus plant is known for its large, attractive leaves.
  • Many classical sculptures feature acanthus leaves in their design.
  • In ancient Rome, acanthus motifs were prevalent in architecture.
  • Gardening enthusiasts often cultivate acanthus for its ornamental value.
  • The acanthus flower blooms in summer, adding beauty to gardens.
