Alembic

EN→ZH

What Does Alembic Mean?

  • 蒸馏器
  • 炼药器
  • 酒精蒸馏器

Alembic Pronunciation

/əˈlɛmbɪk/

Alembic Definition

noun

Definition

An apparatus used in distillation, typically consisting of two vessels connected by a tube.

In ZH

一种用于蒸馏的设备，通常由两个通过管道连接的容器组成。

How to Use Alembic in a Sentence

  • The chemist used an alembic to purify the liquid.
  • In medieval times, alchemists relied on the alembic for their experiments.
  • The design of the alembic has evolved over centuries.
  • An alembic is essential for producing high-quality essential oils.
  • Many ancient texts describe the use of an alembic in the pursuit of gold.

Cultural Notes

The alembic has historical significance in both alchemy and the development of modern chemistry.

Synonyms for Alembic

  • Aleatory
  • Alembicated
