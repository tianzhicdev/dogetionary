Apostasy

EN→ZH

What Does Apostasy Mean?

  • 叛教
  • 脱离信仰
  • 背教

Apostasy Pronunciation

/əˈpɒstəsi/

Apostasy Definition

noun

Definition

The abandonment or renunciation of a religious or political belief.

In ZH

放弃或否认宗教或政治信仰。

How to Use Apostasy in a Sentence

  • His apostasy was shocking to his family.
  • Many consider apostasy a serious crime in their faith.
  • The leaders warned against apostasy during the church meeting.
  • After years of devotion, she announced her apostasy publicly.
  • He faced persecution after his apostasy was revealed.

Synonyms for Apostasy

  • Apocalyptic
  • Apochryphal
  • Apocrypha
  • Apocryphal
  • Apodictic
