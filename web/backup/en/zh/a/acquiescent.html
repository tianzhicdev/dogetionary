Acquiescent

Pronunciation:/ˌakwēˈesənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 默许的
  • 顺从的
  • 听任的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

accepting something reluctantly but without protest.

In ZH

勉强接受某事但不表示反对。

Examples

  • She was acquiescent, agreeing to the terms without any hesitation.
  • Despite her initial doubts, she remained acquiescent throughout the discussion.
  • The acquiescent attitude of the committee allowed the proposal to pass without objection.
  • He took an acquiescent stance when faced with criticism from his peers.
  • Her acquiescent nature made it difficult for her to voice her own opinions.
