Antipodean

EN→ZH

What Does Antipodean Mean?

  • 对跖的
  • 南半球的
  • 澳洲人
  • 新西兰人

Antipodean Pronunciation

/ˌæntɪˈpoʊdiən/

Antipodean Definition

adjective

Definition

Of or relating to the antipodes; situated on the opposite side of the world.

In ZH

与对跖（地球另一侧）相关的；位于地球的另一侧。

How to Use Antipodean in a Sentence

  • I have always wanted to visit antipodean countries like Australia or New Zealand.
  • The antipodean lifestyle is vastly different from that of the Northern Hemisphere.
  • The antipodean perspective on climate change is crucial for global discussions.
  • He enjoyed the antipodean sun while vacationing in Sydney.
  • They made friends with some antipodean travelers during their trip.
noun

Definition

A person from Australia or New Zealand.

In ZH

来自澳大利亚或新西兰的人。

How to Use Antipodean in a Sentence

  • Antipodeans are known for their friendly demeanor.
