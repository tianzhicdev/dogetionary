Apis

EN→ZH

What Does Apis Mean?

  • 蜜蜂
  • 瑞士蜂
  • 蜜蜂的一种

Apis Pronunciation

/ˈeɪ.pɪs/

Apis Definition

noun

Definition

A genus of social insects that includes the honeybee and bumblebee, known for producing honey and wax, and for their role in pollination.

In ZH

一种社会性昆虫的属，包括蜜蜂和大黄蜂，以生产蜜和蜡而闻名，并在授粉中发挥重要作用。

How to Use Apis in a Sentence

  • Apis are essential for the pollination of many crops.
  • Honey produced by Apis is used in various cuisines around the world.
  • The Apis genus comprises several species, including the well-known honeybee.
  • Apis mellifera is the scientific name for the Western honeybee.
  • Conservation of Apis populations is critical for maintaining biodiversity.
