Autobiography

EN→ZH

What Does Autobiography Mean?

  • 自传
  • 自传体
  • 自述

Autobiography Pronunciation

ˌɔːtəʊbaɪˈɒɡrəfi

Autobiography Definition

noun

Definition

A written account of a person's life, written by that person.

In ZH

一个人撰写的关于自己生活的书面记录。

How to Use Autobiography in a Sentence

  • She wrote an autobiography detailing her rise to fame.
  • The autobiography offers a unique perspective on historical events.
  • Many people find inspiration in reading autobiographies of famous figures.

Cultural Notes

Autobiographies are often seen as a way for individuals to share their personal experiences and insights, providing readers with a deeper understanding of their lives and contexts.

Definition

The genre of literature that consists of autobiographical works.

In ZH

由自传作品构成的文学体裁。

How to Use Autobiography in a Sentence

  • Classic autobiographies often reveal the struggles of the author.
  • The autobiography genre has gained popularity in recent years.
  • In literature classes, we often analyze autobiographies to understand the author's perspective.

Cultural Notes

The genre of autobiography is important in literature as it allows readers to connect with the author's personal journey, often reflecting broader social and historical themes.
