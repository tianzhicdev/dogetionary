Absorb

Pronunciation:əbˈzɔːrb
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 吸收
  • 理解
  • 吸引

Definitions

verb

Definition

To take in a liquid, gas, or other substance from the surface or mass of something.

In ZH

从某个物体的表面或质量中吸取液体、气体或其他物质。

Examples

  • The sponge can absorb a lot of water, making it a useful cleaning tool, unless you're thinking of using it for coffee, then it's just a soggy sponge!
  • In a shocking turn of events, scientists revealed that plants can absorb sound waves, which explains why my cactus has been singing pop songs lately!
  • After binge-watching that new series, I think my brain has absorbed all the plot twists like a sponge, but sadly, it's still confused!

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, absorbing knowledge is seen as a vital part of education, often emphasized in schools and universities.

verb

Definition

To fully understand information or ideas.

In ZH

充分理解信息或想法。

Examples

  • After reading the economics book, I felt like I had absorbed enough information to perhaps start my own bank... or just plan my next grocery shopping!
  • She tried to absorb his advice about investing, but all she could think about was how to absorb the upcoming pizza delivery instead.
  • When my friend told me about his latest conspiracy theory, I tried to absorb it all, but it left me wondering if I should wear a tinfoil hat!

Cultural Notes

This use of 'absorb' often relates to education and learning techniques, stressing the importance of understanding rather than just memorizing.

verb

Definition

To take in or soak up energy, such as heat or light.

In ZH

吸收或吸取能量，例如热或光。

Examples

  • I learned that dark colors can absorb heat, which is why my black car feels like an oven in the summer!
  • The new solar panels absorb sunlight and turn it into energy; I just wish they could absorb my student loans too!
  • When I accidentally wore a black shirt to the beach, I realized it was the ultimate way to absorb sunburn instead of sun rays!

Cultural Notes

This definition is commonly used in science and technology discussions, particularly in topics of energy efficiency.
