Amorphous

EN→ZH

What Does Amorphous Mean?

  • 无定形的
  • 模糊的
  • 不确定的

Amorphous Pronunciation

/əˈmɔːrfəs/

Amorphous Definition

adjective

Definition

lacking a clear shape or form; not organized or definite

In ZH

缺乏清晰的形状或形式；未组织或明确的

How to Use Amorphous in a Sentence

  • The artist prefers to work with amorphous shapes.
  • Many gases are amorphous and lack a defined structure.
  • His thoughts were amorphous and difficult to articulate.
  • The amorphous nature of the substance made it hard to analyze.
  • The clouds in the sky appeared amorphous and constantly changing.
