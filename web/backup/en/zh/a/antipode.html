Antipode

EN→ZH

What Does Antipode Mean?

  • 对蹠点
  • 对立面

Antipode Pronunciation

/ˈæntɪpoʊd/

Antipode Definition

noun

Definition

A direct or exact opposite.

In ZH

一个直接或完全相反的事物。

How to Use Antipode in a Sentence

  • The antipode of love is hate.
  • In a philosophical debate, happiness can sometimes be seen as the antipode of suffering.
  • Their views on the topic were antipodes; they couldn't agree on anything.
  • Geographically, New Zealand is often considered the antipode of Spain.
  • He felt that their lifestyles were antipodes, reflecting their different values.

Synonyms for Antipode

  • Antagonistic
  • Antebellum
  • Antediluvian
  • Antepenultimate
  • Anthropocene
