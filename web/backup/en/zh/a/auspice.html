Auspice

EN→ZH

What Does Auspice Mean?

  • 庇护
  • 赞助
  • 保护
  • 预兆

Auspice Pronunciation

/ˈɔː.spɪs/

Auspice Definition

noun

Definition

A favorable sign or prophetic omen; support or protection.

In ZH

一种有利的迹象或预兆；支持或保护。

How to Use Auspice in a Sentence

  • The project was undertaken under the auspices of a well-known charity.
  • We hosted the conference with the auspices of the local government.
  • The exhibition was held under the auspices of an esteemed art foundation.
  • They acted with the auspices of international law.
  • He sought the auspices of a mentor before starting his business.

Synonyms for Auspice

  • Auspicate
  • Auspicious
  • Austerity
