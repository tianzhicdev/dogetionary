Agogness

EN→ZH

What Does Agogness Mean?

  • 渴望
  • 兴奋

Agogness Pronunciation

/əˈɡɒɡ.nəs/

Agogness Definition

noun

Definition

The state of being agog; eager or excited anticipation.

In ZH

渴望的状态；急切或兴奋的期待。

How to Use Agogness in a Sentence

  • She was in a state of agogness waiting for the concert to start.
  • The children’s agogness for the holiday season was palpable.
  • His agogness for the new video game release was evident in his constant checking of the release date.
  • There was a sense of agogness in the air as the guests awaited the announcement.
  • Her agogness about the upcoming trip made her hard to concentrate.
