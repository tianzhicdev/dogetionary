Acedia

EN→ZH

What Does Acedia Mean?

  • 懒惰
  • 无动于衷
  • 忧郁

Acedia Pronunciation

/əˈsiːdiə/

Acedia Definition

noun

Definition

A state of listlessness or torpor; apathy; a lack of caring or motivation.

In ZH

一种无精打采或疲惫的状态；冷漠；缺乏关心或动机。

How to Use Acedia in a Sentence

  • He experienced acedia, finding it difficult to engage in any meaningful activities.
  • The long winter often brings feelings of acedia to those who dislike the cold.
  • In times of hardship, many fall into a state of acedia, losing the will to strive.
  • Acedia is often described as a spiritual sloth that hinders personal growth.
  • She combat acedia by setting small, achievable goals each day.

Synonyms for Acedia

  • Acerbate
  • Acerbic
  • Acerbity
