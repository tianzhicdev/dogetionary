Attenuate

Pronunciation:/əˈtɛn.ju.eɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 减弱
  • 降低
  • 稀释

Definitions

verb

Definition

to reduce in force, effect, or value; to weaken.

In ZH

减少力量、效果或价值；削弱。

Examples

  • The medication helped to attenuate the symptoms of the disease.
  • The scientist aimed to attenuate the effects of the radiation exposure.
  • We need to attenuate our approach to avoid overwhelming the audience.
  • The new policy will attenuate the impact of the economic downturn.
  • His arguments did little to attenuate her concerns about the project.
