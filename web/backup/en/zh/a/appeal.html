Appeal

Pronunciation:/əˈpiːl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 呼吁
  • 上诉
  • 吸引力

Definitions

noun

Definition

a serious or urgent request, typically to the public.

In ZH

一种严肃或紧急的请求，通常针对公众。

Examples

  • The charity made an appeal for donations.
  • Her emotional appeal moved the audience.
  • The lawyer filed an appeal to the higher court.
  • There's an appeal to this new design that attracts many buyers.
  • They launched a public appeal to find the missing child.
verb

Definition

to make a serious or urgent request.

In ZH

做出严肃或紧急的请求。

Examples

  • He decided to appeal to the judge for leniency.
  • The government plans to appeal the decision.
  • They are appealing for more support from the community.
  • She appealed to her friends for help.
  • The organization appeals for volunteers every year.
noun

Definition

the power or ability to attract or interest.

In ZH

吸引或引起兴趣的能力。

Examples

  • The appeal of the show lies in its narrative style.
  • There is a strong appeal in her artistic work.
  • The appeal of the new smartphone is its affordability.
  • His charm added to his appeal as a speaker.
  • The appeal of the city is its rich history.
