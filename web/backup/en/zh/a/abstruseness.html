Abstruseness

EN→ZH

What Does Abstruseness Mean?

  • 深奥
  • 难懂
  • 抽象
  • 复杂

Abstruseness Pronunciation

/əbˈstrus.nəs/

Abstruseness Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being difficult to understand or comprehend.

In ZH

难以理解或掌握的特性。

How to Use Abstruseness in a Sentence

  • The abstruseness of the philosophical text frustrated many students.
  • Her writing was notable for its abstruseness, often leaving readers confused.
  • Despite its abstruseness, the theory offered profound insights into human behavior.
  • The abstruseness of the topic made it a challenge for even the experts.
  • Many avoid the abstruseness of legal language in contracts.

Synonyms for Abstruseness

  • Absolve
  • Absorb
  • Absquatulate
  • Abstemious
