Ascendance

EN→ZH

What Does Ascendance Mean?

  • 上升
  • 优越
  • 统治地位

Ascendance Pronunciation

/əˈsɛndəns/

Ascendance Definition

noun

Definition

the state of rising to higher levels, often used in a context of influence or power

In ZH

上升到更高水平的状态，通常用于影响力或权力的语境

How to Use Ascendance in a Sentence

  • The ascendance of new technologies has changed the way we communicate.
  • His ascendance to the position of CEO was a surprise to many.
  • Cultural ascendance often leads to shifts in societal norms.
  • The ascendance of democratic ideals revolutionized governance in many countries.
  • With her ascendance in the art world, she quickly became a sought-after artist.

Synonyms for Ascendance

  • Ascent
  • Ascertain
  • Ascertaining
  • Ascetic
