Aggrandize

Pronunciation:/əˈɡræn.daɪz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 夸大
  • 增强
  • 提升

Definitions

verb

Definition

To increase in power, status, or wealth; to make something seem greater than it is.

In ZH

增加权力、地位或财富；使某事似乎比实际更伟大。

Examples

  • He sought to aggrandize his position within the company.
  • The film aggrandizes the hero's achievements beyond reality.
  • Her remarks were intended to aggrandize her own contributions.
  • They used propaganda to aggrandize the nation's military prowess.
  • It's easy to aggrandize past accomplishments in hindsight.
