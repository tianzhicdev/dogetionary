Administration

Pronunciation:/ədˌmɪnɪˈstreɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 管理
  • 行政
  • 行政管理

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of managing an organization or government.

In ZH

管理一个组织或政府的行为。

Examples

  • The administration is responsible for implementing new policies.
  • She works in the administration department of the university.
  • The administration of the company has changed hands.
  • We need to consult with the administration before making any decisions.
  • The local administration provided support during the crisis.
noun

Definition

The group of people responsible for running a business or government.

In ZH

负责经营企业或政府的一群人。

Examples

  • The administration is meeting to discuss the budget.
  • The school's administration sent out an email to all parents.
  • Many people criticize the current administration's policies.
  • The administration made several key appointments last week.
  • Changes in the administration can affect local schools.
