Attend

EN→ZH

What Does Attend Mean?

  • 参加
  • 出席
  • 照顾

Attend Pronunciation

/əˈtɛnd/

Attend Definition

verb

Definition

to be present at an event, such as a meeting or class

In ZH

出席某个事件，例如会议或课程

How to Use Attend in a Sentence

  • I will attend the conference next week.
  • She attends yoga classes every Saturday.
  • They attended the wedding together.
  • He usually attends the morning meetings.
  • Students are required to attend all lectures.

Synonyms for Attend
