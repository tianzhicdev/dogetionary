Alto

Pronunciation:/ˈæl.toʊ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 高音
  • 女高音
  • 高音部

Definitions

noun

Definition

A voice or instrument that is lower than a soprano but higher than a tenor.

In ZH

一种音调或乐器，音调比女高音低，但比男高音高。

Examples

  • The choir needed an alto to balance the sound.
  • She sang the alto part beautifully during the performance.
  • He has a rich alto voice that captivates the audience.
  • The music score showed the alto line clearly marked.
  • In this piece, the alto harmonizes with the soprano.
adjective

Definition

Referring to a register of music or a type of voice that is lower than soprano but higher than tenor.

In ZH

与乐器或声音的音域相关，音调低于女高音，高于男高音。

Examples

  • She prefers singing alto in the choir.
  • The arrangement featured a beautiful alto solo.
  • An alto saxophone is often used in jazz.
  • They chose an alto range for the song to suit her voice.
  • The composition includes sections for both soprano and alto parts.
