Adverse

EN→ZH

What Does Adverse Mean?

  • 不利的
  • 相反的
  • 有害的

Adverse Pronunciation

/ˈæd.vɜːrs/

Adverse Definition

adjective

Definition

preventing success or development; harmful; unfavorable.

In ZH

阻止成功或发展的；有害的；不利的。

How to Use Adverse in a Sentence

  • The adverse weather conditions halted our plans.
  • She faced many adverse reactions to her proposal.
  • Adverse effects of the medication include dizziness and nausea.
  • The company reported adverse financial results this quarter.
  • Adverse circumstances forced them to change their strategy.
