Agrarian

EN→ZH

What Does Agrarian Mean?

  • 农业的
  • 农田的
  • 耕作的

Agrarian Pronunciation

/əˈɡrɛəriən/

Agrarian Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to land, agriculture, or rural matters.

In ZH

与土地、农业或乡村事务有关的。

How to Use Agrarian in a Sentence

  • The agrarian society depended heavily on farming.
  • Agrarian reforms are essential for rural development.
  • He wrote a book about agrarian practices in the 19th century.
  • The agrarian lifestyle offers peace and tranquility.
  • She is studying agrarian economics to understand farming subsidies.
