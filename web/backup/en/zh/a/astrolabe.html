Astrolabe

EN→ZH

What Does Astrolabe Mean?

  • 天文仪
  • 星盘
  • 天体仪

Astrolabe Pronunciation

/ˈæstrəˌleɪb/

Astrolabe Definition

noun

Definition

An ancient instrument used for solving problems related to time and the position of the stars.

In ZH

一种古老的仪器，用于解决与时间和星星位置相关的问题。

How to Use Astrolabe in a Sentence

  • The astrolabe was widely used by astronomers in the Middle Ages.
  • With the astrolabe, sailors could determine their latitude at sea.
  • An astrolabe can measure the altitude of celestial bodies.
  • Astrolabes played a key role in the development of astronomy.
  • Using an astrolabe requires a good understanding of geometry.

Cultural Notes

The astrolabe is often associated with Islamic astronomy during the Golden Age.
