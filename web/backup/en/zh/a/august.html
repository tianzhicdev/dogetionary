August

Pronunciation:/ɔːˈɡʌst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 八月
  • 威严
  • 令人敬畏的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Respected and impressive; dignified.

In ZH

受尊敬且令人印象深刻的；庄严的。

Examples

  • The august institution has served the community for over a century.
  • They made an august entry into the hall, commanding everyone's attention.
  • Her august demeanor made her a natural leader in the group.
  • The awards ceremony was an august occasion, filled with dignitaries.
  • The conference featured several august speakers from different fields.
noun

Definition

The eighth month of the year in the Gregorian calendar.

In ZH

公历一年中的第八个月。

Examples

  • August is typically the hottest month in many regions.
  • Many schools start their academic year in August.
  • The festival is celebrated every year in August.
  • In August, the days begin to shorten as summer ends.
  • August brings with it a sense of anticipation for the fall.
