Asunder

Pronunciation:/əˈsʌndər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 分离
  • 分开
  • 隔开

Definitions

adverb

Definition

Into pieces; apart.

In ZH

成碎片；分开。

Examples

  • The storm tore the ship asunder.
  • Their paths were thrown asunder by circumstances beyond their control.
  • The vase shattered and fell asunder.
  • He spoke words that split the group asunder.
  • She felt her heart break asunder at the news.
adjective

Definition

Separated; divided.

In ZH

分开的；被割裂的。

Examples

  • Their lives had been asunder for many years before they met again.
  • The bridge lay asunder after the earthquake.
  • Traditionally, the term described communities that were asunder due to conflicts.
  • His thoughts were asunder, conflicted by doubt and hope.
  • In the play, the characters' lives are shown asunder by tragic events.
