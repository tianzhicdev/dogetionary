Anachronistic

Pronunciation:/əˌnækrəˈnɪstɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 时代错误的
  • 不合时代的
  • 落后于时代的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Belonging to a period other than that being portrayed.

In ZH

属于与所描绘的时期不同的时期。

Examples

  • The film was criticized for its anachronistic costumes.
  • Using a smartphone in a medieval setting is clearly anachronistic.
  • Her views on technology seem anachronistic in today's world.
  • The book contains an anachronistic reference to modern politics.
  • The architect's design was deemed anachronistic for the historic district.
