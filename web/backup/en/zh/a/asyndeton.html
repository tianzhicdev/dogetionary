Asyndeton

Pronunciation:/əˌsɪndɪˈtɒn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无连词法
  • 省略连词法

Definitions

noun

Definition

A stylistic device in which conjunctions are deliberately omitted from a series of related clauses.

In ZH

一种修辞手法，在一系列相关从句中故意省略连词。

Examples

  • He came, he saw, he conquered.
  • I came, I saw, I left.
  • No more running, no more hiding, no more regrets.
  • Friends, Romans, countrymen, lend me your ears.
  • I wanted pizza, I wanted burgers, I wanted fries.
