Antipathy

Pronunciation:/ænˈtɪpəθi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 反感
  • 敌意
  • 厌恶

Definitions

noun

Definition

a deep-seated feeling of aversion or dislike.

In ZH

一种根深蒂固的厌恶或反感的感觉。

Examples

  • She felt a growing antipathy towards the unfair policies.
  • His antipathy for the project made him reluctant to participate.
  • There was a strong antipathy between the rival teams.
  • Despite their antipathy, they had to work together for the project.
  • Her antipathy for loud music is well known.
