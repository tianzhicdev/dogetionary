Antithetical

Pronunciation:/ˌæntɪˈθɛtɪkəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 对立的
  • 相对的
  • 矛盾的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

directly opposed or contrasted; mutually incompatible.

In ZH

直接相反或对比；互相不相容。

Examples

  • The two theories are antithetical to each other.
  • His views are antithetical to the party's principles.
  • The antithetical nature of their beliefs led to constant arguments.
  • They found themselves in an antithetical position on the issue.
  • Her actions are often antithetical to her stated beliefs.
