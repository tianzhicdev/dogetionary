Antidisestablishmentarianism

Pronunciation:/ˌæn.tiˌdaɪ.sɛs.təˌblɪʃ.mənˈtɛə.ri.ən.ɪ.zəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 反对政教分离主义
  • 反建立主义

Definitions

noun

Definition

Opposition to the disestablishment of the Church of England.

In ZH

反对英格兰国教的解散

Examples

  • Antidisestablishmentarianism was a significant political movement in 19th century England.
  • The debate around antidisestablishmentarianism reflected deep societal divides.
  • He often quoted antidisestablishmentarianism in discussions about church and state.
  • Antidisestablishmentarianism can be hard to explain to those unfamiliar with British history.
  • In modern discussions, antidisestablishmentarianism is often referenced humorously.

Cultural Notes

The term is often cited as one of the longest words in the English language and is sometimes used to illustrate the complexity of political and religious discussions.
