Abracadabra

Pronunciation:/ˌæbrəˈkædəbə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 神奇的咒语
  • 阿布拉卡达布拉

Definitions

noun

Definition

A magical phrase used to conjure or create a magical effect.

In ZH

用于召唤或创造魔法效果的魔法短语。

Examples

  • The magician waved his wand and said 'abracadabra'.
  • Children often say 'abracadabra' when pretending to do magic.
  • She whispered 'abracadabra' under her breath as she made a wish.
  • In the show, the rabbit disappeared with a simple 'abracadabra'.
  • He felt a surge of excitement every time he heard 'abracadabra'.

Cultural Notes

Often associated with stage magic and illusion, 'abracadabra' has roots in ancient practices and is commonly used in pop culture.
