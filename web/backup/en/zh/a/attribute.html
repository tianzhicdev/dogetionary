Attribute

Pronunciation:/əˈtrɪbjuːt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 属性
  • 特徵
  • 特性

Definitions

noun

Definition

A quality or feature regarded as a characteristic or inherent part of someone or something.

In ZH

被视为某人或某事的特征或固有部分的质量或特征。

Examples

  • Honesty is a valuable attribute.
  • A good leader should possess several key attributes.
  • The attributes of this model include speed and efficiency.
  • Courage is an important attribute for soldiers.
  • The software has many helpful attributes.
verb

Definition

To regard something as being caused by someone or something.

In ZH

将某事视为由某人或某事引起。

Examples

  • She attributes her success to hard work.
  • Many scientists attribute climate change to human activity.
  • He attributed his failure to lack of preparation.
  • They attribute the discovery to a team of researchers.
  • The artist attributes her inspiration to nature.
