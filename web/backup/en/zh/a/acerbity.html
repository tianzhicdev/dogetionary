Acerbity

Pronunciation:/əˈsɜːrbɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 尖酸刻薄
  • 酸涩
  • 辛辣

Definitions

noun

Definition

Bitterness or sharpness of taste or manner.

In ZH

味道或态度的苦涩或尖刻。

Examples

  • The acerbity of his remarks shocked everyone at the meeting.
  • She spoke with such acerbity that it left the audience in silence.
  • His acerbity in criticizing the project made him unpopular among his peers.
  • The acerbity of her tone indicated her displeasure with the situation.
  • Despite the acerbity of the conversation, they reached a compromise.
