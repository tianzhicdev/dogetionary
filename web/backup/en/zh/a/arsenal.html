Arsenal

EN→ZH

What Does Arsenal Mean?

  • 军火库
  • 武器库
  • 存储
  • 兵工厂

Arsenal Pronunciation

/ˈɑːrsənəl/

Arsenal Definition

noun

Definition

A place where weapons and military equipment are stored or made.

In ZH

存放或制造武器和军事装备的地方。

How to Use Arsenal in a Sentence

  • The military base has a large arsenal of weapons.
  • The government decided to build an arsenal for national defense.
  • During the war, they relied heavily on their arsenal to supply troops.
  • An arsenal can also refer to a collection of tools or resources.
  • The city’s arsenal was crucial during times of conflict.

Synonyms for Arsenal

  • Arsenic
