Apperception

EN→ZH

What Does Apperception Mean?

  • 感知
  • 知觉
  • 理解

Apperception Pronunciation

/ˌæpərˈsɛpʃən/

Apperception Definition

noun

Definition

The process of understanding something by interpreting it in the context of what one already knows.

In ZH

通过将某种事物与已知内容结合，理解其过程。

How to Use Apperception in a Sentence

  • Apperception is essential for learning new concepts.
  • In psychology, apperception helps explain how we understand complex information.
  • Children use apperception to relate new experiences to what they already know.
  • The teacher encouraged apperception in her students to enhance their learning.
  • He applied the concept of apperception to analyze the character's motivations in the story.
