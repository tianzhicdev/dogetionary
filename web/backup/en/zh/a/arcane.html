Arcane

Pronunciation:/ɑːrˈkeɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 神秘的
  • 晦涩的
  • 隐秘的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Understood by few; mysterious or secret.

In ZH

鲜为人知的；神秘或秘密的。

Examples

  • The arcane rituals of the ancient society fascinated historians.
  • He had an arcane knowledge of the art that few could appreciate.
  • Many found the professor's lectures on arcane philosophy to be challenging.
  • The book delves into arcane topics that are rarely discussed in mainstream science.
  • Her arcane references to classical literature went over the heads of most in the audience.
