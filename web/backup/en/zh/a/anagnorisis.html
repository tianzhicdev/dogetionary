Anagnorisis

Pronunciation:/ˌænəɡˈnɔːrɪsɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 识别
  • 认知
  • 认知时刻

Definitions

noun

Definition

A moment in a story where a character makes a critical discovery, especially regarding their true identity or the true nature of their situation.

In ZH

故事中角色发现关键真相的时刻，尤其是关于他们真实身份或其情况的真实性质。

Examples

  • In the play, the anagnorisis occurs when Oedipus realizes he has killed his father.
  • The protagonist's anagnorisis leads to a tragic outcome for the entire family.
  • Anagnorisis is a common theme in Shakespeare's works, showcasing characters' realizations.
  • In modern cinema, anagnorisis often serves as a turning point for the hero.
  • The concept of anagnorisis can be seen in many classic novels, highlighting character development.
