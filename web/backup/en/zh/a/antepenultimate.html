Antepenultimate

Pronunciation:/ˌæn.tə.penˈʌl.tɪ.mət/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 倒数第三的
  • 倒数第三位的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Third from the end

In ZH

倒数第三

Examples

  • In the word 'alphabet', 'l' is the antepenultimate letter.
  • The antepenultimate syllable of the word determines its stress in many languages.
  • When counting down, the antepenultimate number is the second number before one.
  • In a race with ten runners, the antepenultimate place is eighth.
  • The antepenultimate chapter of the book reveals the main character's secret.
