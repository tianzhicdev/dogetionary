Avant-Garde

Pronunciation:/ˌævɒ̃ˈɡɑːrd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 前卫
  • 先锋
  • 前卫派

Definitions

noun

Definition

A movement or the leaders of a movement in the arts or other fields, characterized by innovative and experimental ideas.

In ZH

一种运动或运动的领导者，艺术或其他领域，以创新和实验的思想为特征。

Examples

  • The avant-garde of modern art has challenged traditional aesthetics.
  • Many musicians have drawn inspiration from the avant-garde movement.
  • The film festival showcased a variety of avant-garde films.
  • Avant-garde literature often breaks conventional narrative forms.
  • Artists in the avant-garde seek to redefine cultural norms.
