Area

EN→ZH

What Does Area Mean?

  • 区域
  • 地区
  • 领域

Area Pronunciation

/ˈɛəriə/

Area Definition

noun

Definition

A region or part of a town, a city, or country.

In ZH

一个城镇、城市或国家的区域或部分。

How to Use Area in a Sentence

  • The park is a lovely area to relax in.
  • She lives in a quiet area of the city.
  • This area is known for its beautiful landscapes.
  • What area do you live in?
  • We are expanding our business into the surrounding areas.
noun

Definition

The extent or measurement of a surface.

In ZH

一个表面的程度或测量。

How to Use Area in a Sentence

  • The area of the rectangle is calculated by multiplying its length and width.
  • Do you know the area of a circle formula?
  • He needs to find the area of his garden for planting.
  • The area of this room is 150 square feet.
  • She measured the area before buying the carpet.
