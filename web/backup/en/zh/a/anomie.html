Anomie

Pronunciation:/ˈæn.ə.mi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无序
  • 失范
  • 无常

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of normlessness or breakdown of social norms.

In ZH

一种规范缺失或社会规范崩溃的状态。

Examples

  • In times of crisis, society can experience a sense of anomie.
  • Anomie often leads to feelings of isolation and hopelessness.
  • Sociologists study the effects of anomie on mental health.
  • The rise of individualism can contribute to anomie in communities.
  • Anomie can sometimes result in increased deviant behavior.
