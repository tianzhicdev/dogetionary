Aestheticization

EN→ZH

What Does Aestheticization Mean?

  • 美学化
  • 审美化
  • 审美化过程

Aestheticization Pronunciation

/iˌsθɛtɪsɪˈzeɪʃən/

Aestheticization Definition

noun

Definition

The process of making something aesthetically pleasing or emphasizing its aesthetic qualities.

In ZH

使某物具有美学特性的过程，或强调其美学品质。

How to Use Aestheticization in a Sentence

  • The aestheticization of everyday objects has become a popular trend among designers.
  • In art, aestheticization can transform mundane subjects into visually appealing works.
  • The aestheticization of architecture often reflects cultural values.
  • Critics argue that the aestheticization of violence in media can desensitize audiences.
  • Social media platforms contribute to the aestheticization of personal branding.

Synonyms for Aestheticization

  • Aesthesia
  • Aesthete
  • Aesthetic
  • Aestheticism
