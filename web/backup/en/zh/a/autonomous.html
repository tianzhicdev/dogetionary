Autonomous

EN→ZH

What Does Autonomous Mean?

  • 自主的
  • 自治的
  • 独立的

Autonomous Pronunciation

/ɔːˈtɒnəməs/

Autonomous Definition

adjective

Definition

Having the freedom to govern itself or control its own affairs.

In ZH

拥有自主权，能够管理自己或控制自己事务的。

How to Use Autonomous in a Sentence

  • The country became autonomous after years of colonial rule.
  • Many regions are seeking to become more autonomous within their national governments.
  • Autonomous vehicles are designed to operate without human intervention.
  • She is an autonomous individual who makes her own decisions.
  • The autonomous zone was established to promote self-governance.
