Aeonian

Pronunciation:/iːˈoʊ.ni.ən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 永恒的
  • 无尽的
  • 时代的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

lasting for an immeasurably or indefinitely long period of time; eternal.

In ZH

持续的时间不可测量或无限；永恒的。

Examples

  • The aeonian beauty of the landscape captivated everyone.
  • They spoke of aeonian principles that guide their philosophy.
  • His love for her felt aeonian, as if it would never fade.
  • The aeonian cycle of nature is a reminder of our place in the universe.
  • Many cultures have myths that reflect aeonian themes of creation and destruction.
