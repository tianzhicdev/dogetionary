Asphodel

Pronunciation:/ˈæsfəˌdɛl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 葱兰
  • 亚斯佛德

Definitions

noun

Definition

A type of flowering plant belonging to the family Asphodelaceae, often found in Mediterranean regions, characterized by long, narrow leaves and spikes of white or yellow flowers.

In ZH

一种属于葱兰科的开花植物，通常生长在地中海地区，具有长而狭的叶子和白色或黄色花朵的穗。

Examples

  • Asphodel can often be found in arid regions of the Mediterranean.
  • In ancient Greece, asphodel was associated with the afterlife.
  • Many poets have mentioned asphodel in their works as a symbol of death and rebirth.
  • The garden featured an array of asphodel plants blooming in the summer.
  • The roots of the asphodel are sometimes used for medicinal purposes.

Cultural Notes

In Greek mythology, asphodel fields are a place for the souls of the dead.
