Ascent

Pronunciation:/əˈsɛnt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 上升
  • 登高
  • 升高

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of rising or climbing up; an upward movement.

In ZH

上升或攀登的行为；向上的移动。

Examples

  • The ascent to the mountain summit was challenging but rewarding.
  • His ascent to fame came quickly after the successful movie release.
  • She took a deep breath before the ascent up the steep hill.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, 'ascent' can symbolize personal growth or progress, not just physical climbing.

noun

Definition

A slope or incline; a gradual rise in height.

In ZH

倾斜或坡度；逐渐升高的斜面。

Examples

  • The ascent of the road made the drive more exciting.
  • The trail had a steep ascent that tested our endurance.
  • We enjoyed the scenic views during the ascent of the hill.

Cultural Notes

Ascent is often used in terms of geography or topography, indicating physical changes in elevation.
