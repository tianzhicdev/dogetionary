Adventitious

Pronunciation:/ˌæd.vənˈtɪʃ.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 偶然的
  • 外来的
  • 附带的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Happening by chance rather than design; accidental or incidental.

In ZH

偶然发生的，而非设计的；意外的或附带的。

Examples

  • The adventitious nature of their meeting was surprising.
  • His success seemed to be purely adventitious, not the result of hard work.
  • Plant species can be adventitious, appearing in unexpected locations.
  • Adventitious mutations can lead to new traits in organisms.
  • The adventitious noise from the street distracted the students.
