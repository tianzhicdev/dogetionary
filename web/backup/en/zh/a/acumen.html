Acumen

Pronunciation:/əˈkjuːmən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 敏锐观察力
  • 聪明才智
  • 洞察力

Definitions

noun

Definition

the ability to make good judgments and quick decisions, typically in a particular domain

In ZH

做出良好判断和快速决策的能力，通常在特定领域内

Examples

  • Her business acumen is exceptional, making her a successful entrepreneur.
  • He demonstrated great acumen in financial matters during the meeting.
  • With his political acumen, he navigated the complex negotiations effectively.
  • The lawyer’s acumen in the courtroom impressed everyone present.
  • She used her marketing acumen to boost sales significantly.
