Amanuensis

Pronunciation:/əˌmæn.juˈɛn.sɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 书信员
  • 助手
  • 记录员

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person employed to write what another person dictates or to copy what has been written by another.

In ZH

被雇用来写下他人所说内容，或抄写他人已写内容的人。

Examples

  • The famous author had an amanuensis to help with his correspondence.
  • In the past, many scholars relied on an amanuensis to record their lectures.
  • An amanuensis often plays a crucial role in preserving historical documents.
  • The poet dictated his new verses to his amanuensis.
  • During the meeting, I acted as an amanuensis to take detailed notes.
