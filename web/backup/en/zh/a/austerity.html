Austerity

Pronunciation:/ɔːˈstɛr.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 紧缩
  • 严厉
  • 节俭

Definitions

noun

Definition

A policy or set of policies aimed at reducing government deficits through spending cuts and increased taxes.

In ZH

旨在通过削减政府赤字支出和提高税收的一套政策或政策。这通常发生在经济危机或不景气时期。

Examples

  • The government implemented austerity measures to tackle the rising national debt.
  • Many citizens protested against the austerity policies introduced by the administration.
  • Austerity can lead to a decline in public services and social welfare programs.
  • The economic recovery was slow due to ongoing austerity measures.
  • In times of austerity, families often struggle to make ends meet.

Cultural Notes

Austerity measures are often controversial as they can lead to public backlash, especially when they affect essential services.
