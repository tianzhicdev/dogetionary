Adulate

Pronunciation:/ˈædʒ.ə.leɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 谄媚
  • 奉承
  • 过分称赞

Definitions

verb

Definition

To praise someone excessively or obsequiously.

In ZH

过分称赞某人或谄媚某人。

Examples

  • The critics began to adulate the new film as a masterpiece.
  • She felt uncomfortable with how her colleagues adulated her after the presentation.
  • It's easy to adulate celebrities without knowing their true character.
  • Teachers should not adulate students for just doing the bare minimum.
  • He was adulated by his fans after winning the championship.
