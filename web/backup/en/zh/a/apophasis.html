Apophasis

Pronunciation:/əˈpɒfəsɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 提喻法
  • 隐言法

Definitions

noun

Definition

a rhetorical device involving the mention of something in the act of denying it.

In ZH

提喻法是一种修辞手法，在否认某事的同时提到它。

Examples

  • The politician used apophasis to criticize his opponent while pretending not to.
  • In her speech, she employed apophasis to subtly bring attention to the scandal.
  • He mentioned the issue through apophasis, claiming he wouldn’t talk about it.
  • Apophasis can be an effective tool in persuasion and debate.
  • The author cleverly used apophasis to highlight the flaws in the argument.

Cultural Notes

Apophasis is often used in political rhetoric and literature to create an ironic effect.
