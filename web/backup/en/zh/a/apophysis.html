Apophysis

Pronunciation:/əˈpɒfɪsɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 附突
  • 棘突
  • 突起

Definitions

noun

Definition

A projection or outgrowth from a bone or other structure, often serving as a site for muscle attachment.

In ZH

骨头或其他结构的突起或生长，通常作为肌肉附着的部位。

Examples

  • The apophysis of the femur is crucial for muscle attachment.
  • During the growth phase, the apophysis can be a common site for injuries.
  • Doctors often examine the apophysis for signs of stress fractures in athletes.
  • The apophysis may become inflamed due to overuse in young athletes.
  • In certain species, the apophysis can vary significantly in size.
