Abstemious

Pronunciation:/æbˈstiːmiəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 节制的
  • 克己的
  • 饮食有度的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

not allowing oneself much in the way of food or drink; moderate in consumption

In ZH

不对饮食或饮料过度放纵；适度消费

Examples

  • She leads an abstemious lifestyle, avoiding excess in all aspects of life.
  • His abstemious habits helped him maintain a healthy weight.
  • While celebratory feasts abound, most of us are abstemious during the week.
  • The monk lived an abstemious life, focusing on meditation rather than indulgence.
  • Choosing an abstemious diet can contribute to longevity.
