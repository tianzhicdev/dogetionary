Apraxia

EN→ZH

What Does Apraxia Mean?

  • 失用症
  • 行动障碍

Apraxia Pronunciation

əˈpræk.si.ə

Apraxia Definition

noun

Definition

A neurological condition characterized by the inability to perform movements or tasks despite having the physical ability and desire to do so.

In ZH

一种神经系统疾病，表现为尽管具备身体能力和动机，但无法执行动作或任务。

How to Use Apraxia in a Sentence

  • The patient was diagnosed with apraxia, which made simple tasks difficult.
  • Apraxia can affect a person's ability to speak or use their hands effectively.
  • Therapists work with individuals who have apraxia to develop new strategies for performing tasks.

Cultural Notes

In the medical community, understanding apraxia is crucial for developing effective rehabilitation strategies. It is often associated with other neurological disorders, such as Alzheimer’s disease or stroke.
