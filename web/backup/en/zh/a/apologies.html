Apologies

Pronunciation:əˈpɑːlədʒiz
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 道歉
  • 歉意

Definitions

noun

Definition

An expression of regret for having done something wrong or causing harm.

In ZH

对于犯错或造成伤害的遗憾表示。

Examples

  • He offered his apologies for being late.
  • She wrote a letter of apologies to her friend.
  • The manager issued formal apologies to the customers.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, offering apologies is an important way to mend relationships and acknowledge mistakes. In English-speaking contexts, an apology can often be seen as a sign of maturity and responsibility.

noun

Definition

A reason or justification given for a fault or offense; an explanation.

In ZH

对于过失或冒犯所提供的理由或解释；一个说明。

Examples

  • His apologies for missing the meeting were accepted.
  • She made apologies for her earlier comments during the discussion.
  • An apology was made for the inconvenience caused.

Cultural Notes

In this context, an apology can also function somewhat like an excuse, though it is more about acknowledging a fault rather than justifying it. It's important to differentiate between a genuine apology and simply making excuses.
