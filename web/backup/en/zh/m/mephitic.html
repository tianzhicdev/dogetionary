Mephitic

EN→ZH

What Does Mephitic Mean?

  • 有毒的
  • 恶臭的

Mephitic Pronunciation

/mɛˈfɪtɪk/

Mephitic Definition

adjective

Definition

Having a foul or noxious smell; offensive to the smell.

In ZH

有恶臭或有毒的气味；刺鼻的。

How to Use Mephitic in a Sentence

  • The mephitic air in the abandoned factory was unbearable.
  • He waved his hand in front of his nose to ward off the mephitic odor.
  • The mephitic vapors from the swamp made it difficult to breathe.
  • Even a short walk near the mephitic lagoon was enough to make them gag.
  • They avoided the mephitic fumes that emanated from the old, decaying pile of garbage.
