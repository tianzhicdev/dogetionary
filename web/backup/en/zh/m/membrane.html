Membrane

EN→ZH

What Does Membrane Mean?

  • 膜
  • 薄膜

Membrane Pronunciation

ˈmɛmˌbreɪn

Membrane Definition

noun

Definition

A thin layer of tissue that covers a surface, lines a cavity, or divides a space or organ.

In ZH

覆盖表面、衬里腔体或分隔空间或器官的薄层组织。

How to Use Membrane in a Sentence

  • The cell membrane protects the interior of the cell.
  • In biology class, we learned about the different types of membranes in animal cells.
  • The structure of the plasma membrane is crucial for cell function.

Cultural Notes

In biology and medicine, the term 'membrane' is frequently used to discuss cellular structures and physiological functions.
