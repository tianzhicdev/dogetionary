Monitor

Pronunciation:/ˈmɒn.ɪ.tər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 监视器
  • 监控器
  • 监测器

Definitions

noun

Definition

A device used to display video output from a computer or other electronic device.

In ZH

用于显示计算机或其他电子设备视频输出的设备。

Examples

  • I need to buy a new monitor for my computer.
  • The monitor displays the graphics clearly.
  • He checked the video feed on the security monitor.
  • Make sure to adjust the brightness on your monitor.
  • Her dual-monitor setup improved her productivity.
verb

Definition

To observe and check the progress or quality of something over a period of time.

In ZH

在一段时间内观察和检查某事的进展或质量。

Examples

  • The teacher will monitor the students' progress.
  • She needs to monitor her blood pressure regularly.
  • They hired a company to monitor the environmental impact.
  • It's important to monitor your expenses each month.
  • We will monitor the system for any issues.
