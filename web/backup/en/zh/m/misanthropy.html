Misanthropy

Pronunciation:/ˈmɪsənˌθrɒpi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 厌世主义
  • 人类厌恶
  • 对人类的冷漠

Definitions

noun

Definition

a general dislike, distrust, or disdain for the human species or human nature

In ZH

对人类或人性的一般不喜欢、不信任或蔑视

Examples

  • His misanthropy made it difficult for him to form lasting friendships.
  • Misanthropy can lead to a very lonely existence.
  • She expressed her misanthropy through her writings about the failures of society.
  • Despite his misanthropy, he often sought solace in nature and animals instead of people.
  • His misanthropy was evident in the way he avoided social gatherings.
