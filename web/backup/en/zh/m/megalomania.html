Megalomania

EN→ZH

What Does Megalomania Mean?

  • 自大狂
  • 夸大狂
  • 狂妄症

Megalomania Pronunciation

/ˌmɛɡəloʊˈmeɪniə/

Megalomania Definition

noun

Definition

a psychological condition characterized by delusional fantasies of power, relevance, or omnipotence.

In ZH

一种心理状态，以对权力、重要性或全能的妄想幻想为特征。

How to Use Megalomania in a Sentence

  • His megalomania led him to believe he could control everything.
  • She was diagnosed with megalomania after exhibiting extreme narcissism.
  • The character's megalomania drove the plot of the novel.
  • Megalomania can often lead to disastrous decisions in leadership roles.
  • Many dictators are said to suffer from megalomania.
