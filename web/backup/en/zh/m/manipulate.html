Manipulate

EN→ZH

What Does Manipulate Mean?

  • 操纵
  • 操作
  • 影响

Manipulate Pronunciation

/məˈnɪpjʊleɪt/

Manipulate Definition

verb

Definition

To control or influence (a person or situation) cleverly or unscrupulously.

In ZH

巧妙或不择手段地控制或影响（一个人或情况）。

How to Use Manipulate in a Sentence

  • She used her charm to manipulate the decision-makers.
  • He learned how to manipulate the stock market for profit.
  • The therapist helped her manipulate her feelings in a constructive way.
  • They accused the politician of trying to manipulate public opinion.
  • It's unethical to manipulate people for personal gain.
