Meliorate

EN→ZH

What Does Meliorate Mean?

  • 改善
  • 提高
  • 改良

Meliorate Pronunciation

/mɛlˈɪəreɪt/

Meliorate Definition

verb

Definition

to make something better or improve it

In ZH

使某事物更好，或改善它

How to Use Meliorate in a Sentence

  • We need to meliorate our approach to customer service.
  • Efforts to meliorate the living conditions in the area have been successful.
  • The new policies aim to meliorate the educational system.
  • Community programs have been established to meliorate the impact of poverty.
  • They are working hard to meliorate the relationships between different cultures.
