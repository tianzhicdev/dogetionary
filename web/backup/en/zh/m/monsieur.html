Monsieur

EN→ZH

What Does Monsieur Mean?

  • 先生
  • 阁下
  • 男士

Monsieur Pronunciation

/mɒn.sjœ/

Monsieur Definition

noun

Definition

A title of respect used for a man, equivalent to 'mister'.

In ZH

用于对男性的尊称，相当于“先生”。

How to Use Monsieur in a Sentence

  • Monsieur Dupont is our new neighbor.
  • Please tell Monsieur that dinner is ready.
  • I would like to introduce you to Monsieur Moreau.
  • Monsieur, may I take your order?
  • The term 'monsieur' is often used in formal settings.
