Magnum Opus

Pronunciation:/ˈmæɡ.nəm ˈoʊ.pəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 杰作
  • 重要作品
  • 大作

Definitions

noun

Definition

A great work, especially a literary or artistic masterpiece.

In ZH

杰作，尤其是文学或艺术的杰作。

Examples

  • Her latest novel is considered her magnum opus.
  • The painting is regarded as the artist's magnum opus.
  • His symphony is a magnum opus that took years to complete.
  • In terms of architecture, the cathedral is his magnum opus.
  • The film is widely viewed as the director's magnum opus.
