Malignant

EN→ZH

What Does Malignant Mean?

  • 恶性
  • 有害的
  • 危害的

Malignant Pronunciation

/məˈlɪɡnənt/

Malignant Definition

adjective

Definition

malevolent; intentionally harmful; (in medicine) denoting a cancerous growth that is invasive and likely to cause death.

In ZH

有恶意的；故意有害的；（在医学上）指侵入性强且可能导致死亡的癌性肿瘤。

How to Use Malignant in a Sentence

  • The tumor was found to be malignant after the biopsy.
  • Her malignant behavior towards her colleagues was concerning.
  • They discovered a malignant growth on the patient's liver.
  • Malignant cells can spread rapidly through the body.
  • The doctors explained that malignant tumors require immediate treatment.

Synonyms for Malignant

  • Maladroit
  • Malagasy
  • Malaise
  • Malapropism
  • Malediction
