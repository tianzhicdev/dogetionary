Mondegreen

Pronunciation:/ˈmɒndəɡriːn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 误听成词
  • 朦胧绿

Definitions

noun

Definition

A misheard phrase or lyric, often resulting in a nonsensical interpretation.

In ZH

误听的短语或歌词，通常导致无意义的解释。

Examples

  • I always thought the song said 'excuse me while I kiss this guy' instead of 'excuse me while I kiss the sky.'
  • The term 'mondegreen' was coined to describe such humorous misunderstandings.
  • When children mishear lyrics, it often leads to funny mondegreens.
  • I couldn't stop laughing at the mondegreen my friend made while singing along.
  • She shared her favorite mondegreen from a classic rock song.

Cultural Notes

The term originates from a misinterpretation of a line from a ballad where the phrase 'Lady Mondegreen' was misunderstood from 'laid him on the green'.
