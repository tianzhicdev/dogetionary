Maudlin

Pronunciation:/ˈmɔːdlɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 感伤的
  • 多愁善感的
  • 醉酒后呜咽的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Self-pityingly or tearfully sentimental, often through drunkenness.

In ZH

因自怜或醉酒而显得敏感或多愁善感的

Examples

  • His maudlin speech moved some to tears.
  • After a few drinks, she became maudlin and started reminiscing about her childhood.
  • The movie was filled with maudlin moments that felt forced.
  • He was in a maudlin mood after the breakup, constantly listening to sad songs.
  • She watched the maudlin romantic film and ended up crying.
  • Maudlin displays of affection in public can sometimes be uncomfortable.

Cultural Notes

The term 'maudlin' often carries a negative connotation, suggesting that the sentimentality is excessive or insincere.
