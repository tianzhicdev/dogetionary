What Does Metanoetic Mean?

  • 变思的
  • 思维变迁的

Metanoetic Pronunciation

/ˌmɛtəˈnoʊɛtɪk/

Metanoetic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to a higher level of cognitive or reflective thought, especially in the context of understanding consciousness or mental processes.

In ZH

与更高层次的认知或反思思维有关，特别是在理解意识或心理过程时。

How to Use Metanoetic in a Sentence

  • In philosophy, metanoetic reflection can lead to deeper insights about the self.
  • Metanoetic awareness is crucial for personal growth and self-improvement.
  • The author explores metanoetic themes in his latest book.
  • Understanding metanoetic processes can enhance our approach to learning.
