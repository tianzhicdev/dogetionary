Multifarious

Pronunciation:/ˌmʌl.tɪˈfɛr.i.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 多种多样的
  • 各种各样的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having many different parts, elements, or forms.

In ZH

具有许多不同的部分、元素或形式。

Examples

  • The conference attracted a multifarious group of speakers and attendees.
  • Her multifarious interests include art, science, and philosophy.
  • The city is known for its multifarious cultural influences.
  • They offer a multifarious selection of products to meet various customer needs.
  • His multifarious talents make him a valuable team member.
