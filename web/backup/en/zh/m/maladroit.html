Maladroit

Pronunciation:/ˌmæl.əˈdrɔɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 笨拙的
  • 不灵巧的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

clumsy; awkward in movement or manner.

In ZH

笨拙的；在动作或方式上显示出笨拙。

Examples

  • He was so maladroit that he always tripped over his own feet.
  • Her maladroit attempts at flirting made everyone uncomfortable.
  • The artist's maladroit handling of the paint resulted in a messy canvas.
  • Despite his intelligence, his maladroit social skills kept him from making friends easily.
  • She found his maladroit compliments off-putting rather than charming.
