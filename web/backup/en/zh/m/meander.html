Meander

Pronunciation:/miˈændər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 蜿蜒
  • 曲折
  • 漫游
  • 闲逛

Definitions

verb

Definition

to follow a winding course

In ZH

蜿蜒而行

Examples

  • The river meanders through the valley.
  • We decided to meander through the park instead of taking the main road.
  • As we meandered along the coastline, we discovered hidden coves.
  • The path meanders up the hill, offering beautiful views.
  • He likes to meander in his thoughts when he walks.
noun

Definition

a winding path or course

In ZH

蜿蜒的道路

Examples

  • The meander of the river created a beautiful landscape.
  • Explorers often sought out the meanders of the mountain trails.
  • The intricate meanders of the maze puzzled the visitors.
  • Artists love to depict the meanders of nature in their works.
  • During our hike, we encountered several meanders that challenged our navigation.
