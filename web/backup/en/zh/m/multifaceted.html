Multifaceted

Pronunciation:/ˌmʌltɪˈfæsɪtɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 多面性的
  • 多方面的
  • 多元化的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having many different aspects or features

In ZH

具有多种不同的方面或特征

Examples

  • Her expertise is multifaceted, ranging from engineering to art.
  • The problem is multifaceted and requires a comprehensive approach.
  • He has a multifaceted personality that intrigues everyone.
  • This multifaceted project involves several departments.
  • The city's culture is multifaceted, influenced by various traditions.
