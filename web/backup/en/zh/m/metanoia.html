Metanoia

Pronunciation:/ˌmɛtəˈnɔɪə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 悔改
  • 精神的转变
  • 心灵的改变

Definitions

noun

Definition

a transformative change of heart; a spiritual conversion

In ZH

一种心灵的转变；一种精神的转换

Examples

  • His metanoia led him to a life of service.
  • The book discusses the metanoia experienced by the protagonist.
  • After years of struggle, she underwent a metanoia that changed her perspective.
  • Metanoia is often seen as the first step in one's spiritual journey.
  • The concept of metanoia is central to many philosophical discussions.

Cultural Notes

In Christian theology, metanoia is viewed as a vital step towards repentance and renewal.
