Malediction

EN→ZH

What Does Malediction Mean?

  • 诅咒
  • 咒语
  • 恶言

Malediction Pronunciation

/ˌmæl.əˈdɪk.ʃən/

Malediction Definition

noun

Definition

A curse or the utterance of a curse; the calling down of evil upon someone or something.

In ZH

诅咒或诅咒的口述；对某人或某事的恶魔降临。

How to Use Malediction in a Sentence

  • The witch spoke a malediction that haunted him for years.
  • In folklore, a malediction can bring misfortune to its target.
  • She believed the malediction would ruin her chances of success.
  • The ancient text contained a powerful malediction.
  • He felt the weight of the malediction as soon as it was spoken.

Synonyms for Malediction

  • Malagasy
  • Malaise
  • Malapropism
  • Malevolent
