Meraki

Pronunciation:/mɛˈrɑːki/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 独特的热情
  • 投入的精神
  • 创作时的专注

Definitions

noun

Definition

The soul, creativity, or love put into something; the essence of oneself that is left in your work.

In ZH

投入到某事中的灵魂、创造力或爱；留在你工作中的自我本质。

Examples

  • She paints with such meraki that each piece feels alive.
  • When he cooks, there's a sense of meraki in every dish he serves.
  • The author infused her novel with meraki, making it a true reflection of her passions.
  • You can see the meraki in the craftsmanship of the handmade furniture.
  • He approached his gardening with meraki, treating it as a labor of love.

Cultural Notes

In Greek culture, meraki reflects a deep connection to one's work and the joy derived from creating or doing something with love.
