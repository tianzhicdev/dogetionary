Mediate

EN→ZH

What Does Mediate Mean?

  • 调解
  • 中介
  • 调停

Mediate Pronunciation

/ˈmiːdieɪt/

Mediate Definition

verb

Definition

To intervene between parties in order to bring about an agreement or reconciliation.

In ZH

在各方之间进行干预，以达成协议或调和。

How to Use Mediate in a Sentence

  • She was asked to mediate the dispute between the two groups.
  • The lawyer will mediate the negotiations to ensure a fair outcome.
  • He often mediates conflicts in his community.
  • They hired a professional to mediate their divorce settlement.
  • In such cases, it is beneficial to mediate rather than escalate the conflict.

Synonyms for Mediate

  • Meditate
