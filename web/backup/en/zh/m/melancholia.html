Melancholia

Pronunciation:/ˌmɛlənˈkoʊliə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 忧郁症
  • 忧伤
  • 忧郁

Definitions

noun

Definition

A deep, persistent sadness or gloom that is often associated with depression.

In ZH

一种持久的深沉悲伤或忧郁，通常与抑郁症相关。

Examples

  • After the breakup, she fell into a state of melancholia.
  • He wrote poems about his melancholia and the beauty of sadness.
  • The painting perfectly captures the essence of melancholia.
  • Melancholia can sometimes inspire great art and literature.
  • She felt a sense of melancholia as autumn leaves began to fall.
