Magnitude

Pronunciation:/ˈmæɡ.nɪ.tjuːd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 大小
  • 重要性
  • 程度

Definitions

noun

Definition

The great size or extent of something.

In ZH

某物的巨大规模或范围。

Examples

  • The magnitude of the universe is difficult to comprehend.
  • They measured the magnitude of the earthquake.
  • Her contributions to the project were of great magnitude.
  • The magnitude of his achievements is impressive.
  • We need to assess the magnitude of the risks involved.
noun

Definition

The importance or significance of something.

In ZH

某事的重要性或意义。

Examples

  • The study highlights the magnitude of climate change.
  • Understanding the magnitude of this issue is crucial.
  • The magnitude of their friendship cannot be overstated.
  • We must address the magnitude of poverty in our society.
  • She underestimated the magnitude of the challenge ahead.
