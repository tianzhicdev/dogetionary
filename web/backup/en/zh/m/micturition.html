Micturition

Pronunciation:/ˌmɪk.tʃəˈrɪʃ.ən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 排尿
  • 尿尿

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of urinating; the process of discharging urine from the urinary bladder through the urethra to the outside of the body.

In ZH

排尿的行为；通过尿道将尿液从膀胱排出体外的过程。

Examples

  • After drinking three liters of water, I found myself in a hilarious race to the bathroom for some micturition.
  • In a recent news report, a man accidentally confused a micturition station for a coffee shop, resulting in quite the bizarre scene.
  • The cat seems to have the best micturition technique, always finding the most inconvenient spot to showcase it!

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, discussing micturition is often considered humorous or awkward, especially among children or in lighthearted conversations. In China, it is usually referred to more casually as '尿尿' (niào niào), which is more child-friendly.
