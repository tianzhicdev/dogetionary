Memento

EN→ZH

What Does Memento Mean?

  • 纪念品
  • 遗物
  • 备忘录

Memento Pronunciation

/məˈmɛn.toʊ/

Memento Definition

noun

Definition

An object kept as a reminder of a person, place, or event.

In ZH

作为对某人、某地或某事件的提醒而保存的物品。

How to Use Memento in a Sentence

  • I kept the ticket as a memento of the concert.
  • The old photograph serves as a memento of my childhood.
  • She gave me a small memento from her trip to Paris.
  • Every time I see that gift, it brings back memories as a memento of our friendship.
  • He collected mementos from every city he visited.

Cultural Notes

The term 'memento' is often used in contexts involving souvenirs or keepsakes that hold personal significance.

Synonyms for Memento

  • Membrane
  • Memento Mori
