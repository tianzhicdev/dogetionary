Myriad

Pronunciation:/ˈmɪr.i.əd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 无数
  • 大量
  • 众多

Definitions

noun

Definition

a countless or extremely great number.

In ZH

无数或极大的数量。

Examples

  • There are a myriad of stars in the night sky.
  • The museum showcases a myriad of artifacts from different cultures.
  • She has a myriad of ideas for her new project.
  • The book outlines a myriad of strategies for improving productivity.
  • He faced a myriad of challenges during his journey.
adjective

Definition

countless or innumerable; having a great number.

In ZH

无数的；数量巨大的。

Examples

  • The garden was filled with myriad flowers in bloom.
  • Myriad opportunities await those who are willing to explore.
  • The city boasts a myriad of restaurants to choose from.
  • There are myriad ways to approach this problem.
  • She has a myriad of interests and hobbies.
