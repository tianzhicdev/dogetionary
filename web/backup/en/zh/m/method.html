Method

EN→ZH

What Does Method Mean?

  • 方法
  • 办法
  • 措施

Method Pronunciation

ˈmɛθəd

Method Definition

noun

Definition

A particular procedure for accomplishing or approaching something, especially a systematic or established one.

In ZH

完成或处理某事的特定程序，尤其是系统性或既定的程序。

How to Use Method in a Sentence

  • Scientists have developed a new method to teach squirrels how to use smartphones. Apparently, they are more tech-savvy than some humans!
  • In an unprecedented method of protest, a group of pigeons formed a coalition to demand better breadcrumbs. The city was baffled!
  • The chef's secret method for making soufflé involves singing to the eggs. It seems the eggs really respond to a good classic rock ballad!

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, the term 'method' is often associated with both formal and informal processes. In academia, a method is recognized as a critical aspect of research, often debated and scrutinized. The phrase 'method to the madness' implies that there is a logical process behind what appears to be chaotic.

verb

Definition

To arrange or plan something systematically; although this usage is archaic or less common, it still exists in some contexts.

In ZH

以系统的方式安排或计划某事；虽然这种用法是古老的或不太常见，但在某些上下文中仍然存在。

How to Use Method in a Sentence

  • She method-ed her way through her messy room, creating paths out of the clothes like an archaeologist uncovering ancient ruins.
  • The dog method-ed its own escape plan, meticulously plotting the best time to dig under the fence when no one was looking.
  • In a surprising twist, he method-ed his speech by adding a rap verse about the benefits of broccoli. It was unforgettable!

Cultural Notes

While 'method' as a verb isn’t commonly used today, it can still be found in literary or humorous contexts. Such creative usage adds flair to storytelling or character dialogue.

Synonyms for Method
