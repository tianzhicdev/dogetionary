Meta

EN→ZH

What Does Meta Mean?

  • 元
  • 元数据
  • 元信息

Meta Pronunciation

/ˈmɛtə/

Meta Definition

adjective

Definition

Referring to itself or to the conventions of its genre; self-referential.

In ZH

指其自身或其类型的约定；自指的。

How to Use Meta in a Sentence

  • The film is a meta-commentary on the nature of fame.
  • She wrote a meta-analysis of existing studies on climate change.
  • His novel includes meta-fiction elements that challenge traditional storytelling.
  • They created a meta-joke that makes fun of typical punchlines.
  • In our class, we often discuss meta-ethics and moral philosophy.
noun

Definition

A prefix used to indicate a concept that is an abstraction from another concept.

In ZH

一个用于指示从另一个概念抽象出来的概念的前缀。

How to Use Meta in a Sentence

  • The term 'metadata' refers to data about data.
  • In linguistics, 'metaphor' is a type of figurative language.
  • The conference focused on metacognition and learning strategies.
  • He is interested in metatheory and its implications for science.
  • The publisher released a meta-book discussing the influence of digital media.

Synonyms for Meta

  • Metacognition
  • Metacognitive
  • Metamorphose
  • Metamorphosis
  • Metanarrative
