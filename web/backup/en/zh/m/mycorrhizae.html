Mycorrhizae

Pronunciation:/ˌmaɪ.kəˈraɪ.ziː/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 菌根
  • 菌根植物
  • 真菌根系

Definitions

noun

Definition

A symbiotic association between fungi and the roots of a plant, enabling enhanced nutrient absorption.

In ZH

真菌与植物根部之间的共生关系，能增强营养吸收。

Examples

  • Mycorrhizae are crucial for the growth of many plants.
  • Plants with mycorrhizae can absorb more water and nutrients from the soil.
  • The presence of mycorrhizae can improve soil health.
  • Farmers often inoculate seeds with mycorrhizal fungi to boost crop yields.
  • Research shows that mycorrhizae can help plants tolerate drought conditions.
