Mordant

Pronunciation:/ˈmɔːrdənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 蚀刻剂
  • 尖锐的
  • 讽刺的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having or showing a sharp or critical quality; biting

In ZH

具有尖锐或批判特质；讽刺的

Examples

  • The critic wrote a mordant review of the play.
  • Her mordant wit often left her friends in stitches.
  • He had a mordant way of discussing politics that many found refreshing.
  • The article was notable for its mordant commentary on social issues.
  • She delivered a mordant speech that resonated with the audience.
noun

Definition

a substance used to treat materials, particularly in dyeing processes, to help fix colors

In ZH

一种用于处理材料的物质，特别是在染色过程中，以帮助固定颜色

Examples

  • The artist used a mordant to ensure the colors would hold.
  • In textile manufacturing, a mordant is crucial for bright and lasting dyes.
  • Different mordants can produce various shades in dyed fabrics.
  • The use of mordants dates back centuries in traditional dyeing techniques.
  • Understanding mordants is key for any serious dyer.
