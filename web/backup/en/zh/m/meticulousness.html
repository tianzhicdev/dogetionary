Meticulousness

Pronunciation:/məˈtɪkjələsnəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 细致
  • 一丝不苟
  • 周密

Definitions

noun

Definition

the quality of being very careful and precise about details

In ZH

对细节非常小心和准确的特性

Examples

  • Her meticulousness in organizing the event was greatly appreciated.
  • He approached his work with a level of meticulousness that was unmatched.
  • The artist’s meticulousness was evident in every brush stroke.
  • Their meticulousness in planning ensured a successful project.
  • Meticulousness in documentation is crucial for legal cases.
