Metacognition

Pronunciation:/ˌmɛtəkoʊɡˈnɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 元认知
  • 自我认知

Definitions

noun

Definition

Awareness and understanding of one's own thought processes.

In ZH

对自身思维过程的意识和理解。

Examples

  • She developed her metacognition skills to improve her learning efficiency.
  • Metacognition involves thinking about one's own thinking patterns.
  • Teachers can foster metacognition by encouraging students to reflect on their learning strategies.
  • He realized that his metacognition was lacking, which affected his problem-solving abilities.
  • Metacognition can help individuals regulate their own learning.
