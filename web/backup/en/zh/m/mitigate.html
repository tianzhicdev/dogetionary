Mitigate

EN→ZH

What Does Mitigate Mean?

  • 缓和
  • 减轻
  • 减缓

Mitigate Pronunciation

/ˈmɪtɪˌɡeɪt/

Mitigate Definition

verb

Definition

To make less severe, serious, or painful.

In ZH

减少严重性、严峻性或疼痛感。

How to Use Mitigate in a Sentence

  • The government is trying to mitigate the impact of the recession.
  • We took steps to mitigate the risks involved in the project.
  • Planting trees can help mitigate air pollution.
  • The new policies are designed to mitigate the effects of climate change.
  • She used a cold compress to mitigate the swelling on her ankle.
