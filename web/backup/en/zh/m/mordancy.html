Mordancy

EN→ZH

What Does Mordancy Mean?

  • 讽刺
  • 尖刻
  • 刻薄

Mordancy Pronunciation

/ˈmɔːrdənsi/

Mordancy Definition

noun

Definition

a biting or caustic quality, especially in speech or writing; sarcasm or sharp wit

In ZH

尖刻或腐蚀的特点，尤其是在语言或写作中；讽刺或尖锐的机智

How to Use Mordancy in a Sentence

  • Her mordancy made her comments unforgettable.
  • The mordancy of his critique left the audience in shock.
  • With a touch of mordancy, the playwright addressed serious issues with humor.
  • The author's use of mordancy in the dialogue added depth to the characters.
  • Despite its mordancy, the book was well received by critics.
