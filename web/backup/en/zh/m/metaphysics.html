Metaphysics

Pronunciation:ˌmɛtəˈfɪzɪks
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 形而上学
  • 超越论

Definitions

noun

Definition

A branch of philosophy that deals with the fundamental nature of reality, including concepts such as being, existence, and the universe.

In ZH

哲学的一个分支，研究现实的基本性质，包括存在、存在方式和宇宙等概念。

Examples

  • Metaphysics attempts to answer questions that go beyond the physical sciences.
  • In studying metaphysics, we explore what it means for something to exist.
  • Many ancient philosophers contributed to the field of metaphysics, such as Aristotle and Plato.

Cultural Notes

Metaphysics is an important area in Western philosophy, often regarded as one of the oldest disciplines in philosophy, confronting issues that are seen as abstract or beyond empirical observation.

adjective

Definition

Relating to metaphysics; concerning the fundamental nature of reality.

In ZH

与形而上学相关的；涉及现实的基本特性。

Examples

  • Her metaphysical thoughts often challenge conventional beliefs.
  • The novel includes metaphysical themes that explore the nature of existence.
  • He has a unique metaphysical perspective on life that intrigues others.

Cultural Notes

The term 'metaphysical' can also be used in literature or art to describe works that explore themes beyond the physical world or tangible realities.
