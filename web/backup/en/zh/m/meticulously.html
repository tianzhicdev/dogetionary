Meticulously

Pronunciation:məˈtɪkjuləsli
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 小心翼翼地
  • 一丝不苟地
  • 详细地

Definitions

adverb

Definition

In a way that shows great attention to detail; very thoroughly.

In ZH

以极大的关注细节的方式；非常彻底地。

Examples

  • The artist meticulously painted every detail of the landscape.
  • She prepares her presentations meticulously, ensuring nothing is overlooked.
  • They meticulously planned the event to make sure it was perfect.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, especially in China, attention to detail and thoroughness are highly valued traits, especially in professional settings. This adverb is often used to commend someone for their dedication to perfection.

adverb

Definition

With great care and precision; done with careful consideration.

In ZH

以极大的关心和精确度；经过仔细考虑后进行的。

Examples

  • He meticulously checked every figure in the report to avoid errors.
  • The scientist meticulously documented all of her findings during the experiment.
  • When baking, it's important to measure ingredients meticulously to ensure the best results.

Cultural Notes

Meticulousness can be crucial in fields like science and arts. In Western contexts, it is often associated with professionalism and quality, making it a positive attribute in workplace environments.
