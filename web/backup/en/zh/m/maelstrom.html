Maelstrom

Pronunciation:/ˈmeɪlstrəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 漩涡
  • 大混乱
  • 动荡

Definitions

noun

Definition

A powerful whirlpool in the sea or a river.

In ZH

海洋或河流中的强大漩涡。

Examples

  • The ship was drawn into the maelstrom and capsized.
  • He felt caught in a maelstrom of emotions after hearing the news.
  • The maelstrom of the storm made navigation nearly impossible.
  • They watched the maelstrom from a safe distance on the shore.
  • The author describes the protagonist's life as a maelstrom of chaos.
noun

Definition

A situation or state of confused movement or violent turmoil.

In ZH

混乱或暴力动荡的情况或状态。

Examples

  • The city was in a maelstrom of protests and demonstrations.
  • She found herself in a maelstrom of conflicting opinions.
  • The political maelstrom led to a change in leadership.
  • Their relationship was a maelstrom of love and conflict.
  • He navigated through the maelstrom of public opinion with skill.
