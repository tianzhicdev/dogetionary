Mademoiselle

Pronunciation:/ˌmæ.də.mwɑːˈzɛl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 小姐
  • 未婚女子

Definitions

noun

Definition

a French term used to address or refer to an unmarried woman or girl.

In ZH

法语术语，用于称呼或指代未婚女性或女孩。

Examples

  • Mademoiselle Dupont is my French teacher.
  • She received a letter addressed to Mademoiselle.
  • I spoke with a mademoiselle at the café.
  • Mademoiselle, can I offer you a drink?
  • The invitation was sent to Mademoiselle Claire.

Cultural Notes

In contemporary usage, 'mademoiselle' is less common, as many prefer 'madame' regardless of marital status to promote gender equality.
