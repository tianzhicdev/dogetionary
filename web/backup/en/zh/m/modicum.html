Modicum

EN→ZH

What Does Modicum Mean?

  • 一点
  • 少量

Modicum Pronunciation

/ˈmɒdɪkəm/

Modicum Definition

noun

Definition

A small quantity of a particular thing, especially something considered desirable or valuable.

In ZH

被认为是可取或有价值的特定事物的小数量。

How to Use Modicum in a Sentence

  • She showed a modicum of respect for her elders.
  • There was a modicum of truth in his statement.
  • I have only a modicum of experience in this field.
  • To succeed, you need at least a modicum of confidence.
  • The recipe calls for a modicum of sugar.

Synonyms for Modicum

  • Moderate
