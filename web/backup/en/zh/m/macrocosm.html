Macrocosm

Pronunciation:/ˈmækroʊˌkɑːzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 宏观世界
  • 大宇宙
  • 整体

Definitions

noun

Definition

The whole of a complex structure, especially the world or universe as a whole.

In ZH

一个复杂结构的整体，特别是世界或宇宙的整体。

Examples

  • The macrocosm of society reflects the myriad interactions of its individual members.
  • In philosophy, the macrocosm often symbolizes the greater reality that encompasses all smaller realities.
  • Astronomy helps us understand the macrocosm, from galaxies to cosmic events.
  • The concept of the macrocosm is used to explain the relationship between the universe and the individual.
  • Artists often explore the macrocosm in their work, representing universal themes.
