More

Pronunciation:/mɔːr/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 更多
  • 更大
  • 更
  • 更加

Definitions

adjective

Definition

a greater or additional amount or degree.

In ZH

更大或额外的数量或程度。

Examples

  • I need more time to finish my project.
  • Can you bring me more water?
  • She has more experience than anyone else here.
  • We want more choices on the menu.
  • He scored more points in the game.
adverb

Definition

to a greater extent or degree.

In ZH

到更大程度或程度。

Examples

  • This task is more complicated than I thought.
  • She runs more quickly than her brother.
  • He needs to study more thoroughly before the exam.
  • The weather is more unpredictable these days.
  • They are more likely to succeed with proper planning.
noun

Definition

a greater or additional amount or degree.

In ZH

更大或额外的数量或程度。

Examples

  • The team needs more to improve their performance.
  • There is always more to learn in life.
  • They asked for more after the first round of snacks.
  • More is not always better when it comes to food.
  • We could use a little more help on this project.
