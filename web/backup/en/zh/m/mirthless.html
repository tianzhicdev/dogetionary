Mirthless

EN→ZH

What Does Mirthless Mean?

  • 无欢乐的
  • 不快乐的
  • 无趣的

Mirthless Pronunciation

/ˈmɜːrθləs/

Mirthless Definition

adjective

Definition

Lacking joy, amusement, or happiness; characterized by sadness or seriousness.

In ZH

缺乏欢乐、娱乐或幸福；以悲伤或严肃为特征。

How to Use Mirthless in a Sentence

  • His mirthless demeanor made everyone uncomfortable.
  • She gave a mirthless laugh that echoed in the empty room.
  • The movie was filled with mirthless scenes that left the audience in silence.
  • Despite the festivities, there was a mirthless atmosphere in the hall.
  • He approached the task with a mirthless seriousness that was unusual for him.

Synonyms for Mirthless

  • Mire
  • Mirth
  • Mirthful
