Melismatic

EN→ZH

What Does Melismatic Mean?

  • 旋律丰富的
  • 音节丰富的
  • 多旋律的

Melismatic Pronunciation

/mɛlɪzˈmætɪk/

Melismatic Definition

adjective

Definition

Pertaining to the technique of singing several notes on a single syllable of text, often found in various musical styles.

In ZH

指歌唱的技术，在一个音节的文本上唱出多个音符，通常出现在各种音乐风格中。

How to Use Melismatic in a Sentence

  • The singer's melismatic style captivated the audience.
  • In classical music, melismatic phrases can be quite expressive.
  • Many contemporary vocalists use melismatic techniques in their performances.
  • The melismatic ornamentation in the aria added to its emotional depth.
  • She practiced her melismatic runs to improve her vocal agility.

Synonyms for Melismatic

  • Melancholia
  • Melancholic
  • Melancholy
  • Melange
  • Melifluous
