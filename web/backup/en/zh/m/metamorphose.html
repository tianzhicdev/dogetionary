Metamorphose

Pronunciation:/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəʊz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 变形
  • 蜕变
  • 转变

Definitions

verb

Definition

to change or cause to change completely in form or appearance

In ZH

完全改变形状或外观或导致其改变

Examples

  • The caterpillar will metamorphose into a butterfly.
  • His ideas metamorphosed over the years.
  • The city is set to metamorphose after the new development plans are approved.
  • She felt as if she was about to metamorphose into something greater.
  • Many species of frogs will metamorphose from tadpoles into adult frogs.
