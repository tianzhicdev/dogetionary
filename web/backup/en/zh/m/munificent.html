Munificent

Pronunciation:/mjuːˈnɪfɪsənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 慷慨的
  • 丰厚的
  • 大方的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

very generous; giving more than is necessary or expected

In ZH

非常慷慨；给予超过必要或预期的东西

Examples

  • The munificent donor contributed a large sum to the charity.
  • Her munificent nature made her popular among her colleagues.
  • He was known for his munificent gifts to the local community.
  • The munificent support from the government helped the project succeed.
  • Thanks to the munificent bequest, the museum could expand its collection.
