Moribund

Pronunciation:/ˈmɒrɪˌbʌnd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 垂死的
  • 濒临死亡的
  • 即将过时的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

in a state of decline; near death or termination

In ZH

处于衰退状态；接近死亡或终止

Examples

  • The moribund economy needs a boost from the government.
  • He felt moribund after the long illness.
  • The once thriving business is now moribund due to lack of innovation.
  • Many traditional practices are becoming moribund as society modernizes.
  • The moribund village was a shadow of its former self.
