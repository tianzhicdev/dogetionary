Misanthrope

Pronunciation:/ˈmɪs.ən.θroʊp/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 厌世者
  • 反人类者
  • 反社会者

Definitions

noun

Definition

a person who dislikes humankind and avoids human society

In ZH

一个厌恶人类并避免与人类社会接触的人

Examples

  • He became a misanthrope after years of disappointment.
  • Many consider him a misanthrope for his disdain of social gatherings.
  • The novel's protagonist is a misanthrope who isolates himself from society.
  • Her misanthrope tendencies made it difficult for her to maintain friendships.
  • In a world full of betrayal, he turned into a complete misanthrope.
