Metamorphosis

EN→ZH

What Does Metamorphosis Mean?

  • 蜕变
  • 变形
  • 彻底改变

Metamorphosis Pronunciation

/ˌmɛtəˈmɔːrfəsɪs/

Metamorphosis Definition

noun

Definition

A profound change in form, structure, or substance, especially a striking or rapid change.

In ZH

一种形式、结构或物质的深刻变化，尤其是一种显著或快速的变化。

How to Use Metamorphosis in a Sentence

  • The caterpillar undergoes a metamorphosis to become a butterfly.
  • His metamorphosis from a shy boy to a confident speaker inspired everyone.
  • The novel explores the metamorphosis of society during the revolution.
  • Metamorphosis can refer to changes in both the physical and emotional realms.
  • The concept of metamorphosis is often used in literature to symbolize personal growth.

Synonyms for Metamorphosis

  • Meta
  • Metacognition
  • Metacognitive
  • Metamorphose
  • Metanarrative
