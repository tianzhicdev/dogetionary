Mission

Pronunciation:ˈmɪʃ.ən
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 使命
  • 任务
  • 任务
  • 使命

Definitions

noun

Definition

A specific task or duty that a person or group is assigned to complete.

In ZH

一个人或团队被分配去完成的特定任务或职责。

Examples

  • The mission of our team is to find the best pizza in town, a quest more difficult than finding a needle in a haystack!
  • I thought my mission was to prepare for the exam, but Netflix had other plans!
  • A cat's mission is to nap on every piece of furniture in the house just to make sure it's all comfortable.

Cultural Notes

In a business context, a company may have a mission statement that defines its purpose and values.

noun

Definition

An important goal that is worth pursuing; a calling or purpose in life.

In ZH

一个值得追求的重要目标；生活中的召唤或目的。

Examples

  • His mission in life is to eat ice cream in every country, which sounds like the sweetest job ever!
  • I realized my mission is to discover the best coffee spots, because my productivity depends on caffeine!
  • Some believe their mission is to save the environment, while others think it's just to save that last slice of cake.

Cultural Notes

This definition often reflects a person's values and aspirations, and it can be used in various contexts, such as personal development or activism.

noun

Definition

A military operation or a specific engagement in warfare.

In ZH

军事行动或战争中的特定交战。

Examples

  • The mission was to sneak into the office party and steal the secret recipe for the world's best cookie!
  • In the latest action movie, the mission was absurdly complex, involving ninjas, a talking dog, and a quest for the holy grail of snacks!
  • The soldiers' mission was to rescue the pizza delivery guy who accidentally delivered to the enemy base.

Cultural Notes

In military terms, a mission may involve strategic objectives and can vary greatly in complexity.
