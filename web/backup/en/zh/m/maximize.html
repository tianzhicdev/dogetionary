Maximize

Pronunciation:/ˈmæksɪmaɪz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 最大化
  • 使最大化
  • 增大

Definitions

verb

Definition

To make as large or great as possible.

In ZH

使尽可能大或伟大。

Examples

  • We need to maximize our profits this quarter.
  • She aims to maximize her potential by taking on new challenges.
  • The software can help you maximize your productivity.
  • To maximize the effectiveness of your workout, focus on your form.
  • They are working hard to maximize the use of available resources.
