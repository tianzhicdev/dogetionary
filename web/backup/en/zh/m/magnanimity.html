Magnanimity

EN→ZH

What Does Magnanimity Mean?

  • 宽宏大量
  • 高尚
  • 大度

Magnanimity Pronunciation

/ˌmæɡ.nəˈnɪm.ɪ.ti/

Magnanimity Definition

noun

Definition

the quality of being generous and forgiving, especially towards a rival or someone less powerful than oneself.

In ZH

宽容和慷慨的品质，尤其是对竞争对手或地位低于自己的人表现出来的宽宏大量。

How to Use Magnanimity in a Sentence

  • Her magnanimity towards her opponents earned her great respect.
  • He showed magnanimity by forgiving those who had wronged him.
  • The charity event was a display of magnanimity from the wealthy donors.
  • In times of conflict, magnanimity can lead to reconciliation.
  • Their magnanimity in victory inspired others to emulate their actions.

Synonyms for Magnanimity

  • Magnanimous
  • Magniloquent
  • Magnitude
  • Magnum Opus
