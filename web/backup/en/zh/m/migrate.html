Migrate

Pronunciation:/ˈmaɪɡreɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 迁徙
  • 移民
  • 迁移

Definitions

verb

Definition

to move from one place to another, especially to a different country or region, often to settle there permanently or temporarily.

In ZH

从一个地方移到另一个地方，特别是到不同的国家或地区，通常是为了永久或暂时定居。

Examples

  • Birds migrate south for the winter.
  • Many families migrate to the city in search of better job opportunities.
  • The company plans to migrate its data to the cloud.
  • During the summer, students often migrate to cooler areas.
  • Some species of fish migrate upstream to spawn.
