Mellifluent

EN→ZH

What Does Mellifluent Mean?

  • 甜美的
  • 流畅的
  • 蜜甜的
  • 悦耳的
  • 动听的

Mellifluent Pronunciation

/məˈlɪf.lu.ənt/

Mellifluent Definition

adjective

Definition

Having a smooth, rich flow of sound; pleasing to the ear due to a sweet or melodic quality.

In ZH

声音流畅且富有音乐感；由于甜美或旋律的品质而令人愉悦。

How to Use Mellifluent in a Sentence

  • The singer's mellifluent voice captivated the audience.
  • He spoke in a mellifluent tone that soothed everyone around him.
  • The mellifluent sounds of the stream made for a relaxing afternoon.
  • Her mellifluent laughter filled the room with joy.
  • The mellifluent melodies of the orchestra transported the listeners to another world.

Synonyms for Mellifluent

  • Melancholia
  • Melancholic
  • Melancholy
  • Melange
  • Melifluous
