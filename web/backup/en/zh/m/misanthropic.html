Misanthropic

Pronunciation:/ˌmɪsənˈθrɒpɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 厌世的
  • 人性悲观的
  • 对人类持有负面态度的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by a dislike of humankind or human society

In ZH

以厌恶人类或人类社会为特征的

Examples

  • His misanthropic tendencies made it difficult for him to form close relationships.
  • She preferred the company of books to people due to her misanthropic views.
  • The misanthropic author wrote about the flaws of society.
  • After years of disappointment, he became increasingly misanthropic.
  • Misanthropic characters are often found in literature to explore themes of isolation.

Cultural Notes

The term 'misanthropic' is often used in literature to describe characters who are reclusive or critical of society, reflecting deeper themes of existentialism.
