Vignette

Pronunciation:/vɪnˈjɛt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 小插曲
  • 短篇
  • 花絮

Definitions

noun

Definition

A brief evocative description, account, or episode.

In ZH

简短而富有感染力的描述、叙述或插曲。

Examples

  • The novel opens with a vignette of the protagonist's childhood.
  • She wrote a vignette about her travels in Europe.
  • His speech included several poignant vignettes from his life.
  • The film consists of several vignettes illustrating different aspects of urban life.
  • The artist created a series of vignettes that captured the essence of the city.
