Venerated

Pronunciation:/ˈvɛn.ə.reɪ.tɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 崇敬的
  • 受尊敬的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Regarded with great respect and reverence.

In ZH

受到极大尊敬和崇敬的。

Examples

  • The venerated leader gave an inspirational speech.
  • Many venerated artists have left a lasting impact on culture.
  • She was venerated for her contributions to science.
  • The temple is a place where venerated figures are honored.
  • In many cultures, ancestors are venerated by their descendants.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in religious or historical contexts, where figures such as saints or icons are venerated.
