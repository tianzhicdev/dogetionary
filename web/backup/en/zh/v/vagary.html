Vagary

EN→ZH

What Does Vagary Mean?

  • 奇想
  • 变幻
  • 异想
  • 奇特行为
  • 幻想

Vagary Pronunciation

/ˈveɪɡəri/

Vagary Definition

noun

Definition

an unexpected and inexplicable change in a situation or in someone's behavior.

In ZH

情况或某人行为中意外且难以解释的变化。

How to Use Vagary in a Sentence

  • The vagaries of weather can disrupt travel plans.
  • He was subject to the vagaries of fortune throughout his life.
  • The vagaries of human emotions can be hard to predict.
  • Her decision was influenced by the vagaries of the market.
  • We must adapt to the vagaries of our circumstances.

Synonyms for Vagary

  • Vagabond
  • Vagabondage
