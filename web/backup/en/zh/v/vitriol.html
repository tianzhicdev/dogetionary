Vitriol

Pronunciation:/ˈvɪtriəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 硫酸盐
  • 尖刻的言辞
  • 毒舌

Definitions

noun

Definition

Cruel and bitter criticism; also, sulfuric acid (historically known as vitriol).

In ZH

尖刻而苦涩的批评；也指硫酸（历史上被称为硫酸盐）。

Examples

  • The politician faced vitriol for his controversial remarks.
  • Her review of the movie was filled with vitriol and disdain.
  • In the debate, both sides exchanged vitriol that surprised the audience.
  • He wrote a letter filled with vitriol aimed at his former employer.
  • The journalist's article was a vitriol against corruption in politics.
