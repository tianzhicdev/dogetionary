Varries

Pronunciation:ˈver-ēz
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 变化
  • 不同
  • 多样化

Definitions

verb

Definition

to change or alter in form, appearance, or nature; to differ.

In ZH

改变或变更形式、外观或性质；有所不同。

Examples

  • The weather in April varies so much that I never know whether to carry an umbrella or sunglasses – sometimes I need both!
  • The prices of avocado toast vary from one café to another, but they always seem to cost as much as a small mortgage!
  • In recent news, how the party platforms vary could lead to a very entertaining election season, especially with all the campaign memes circulating online.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'varies' is often used to describe differences and changes, especially in contexts such as weather, prices, and opinions. It's a useful word for discussions about comparisons.

noun

Definition

the quality of being different or diverse; a lack of uniformity.

In ZH

不同或多样性的特质；缺乏统一性。

Examples

  • The varies in opinions about pineapple on pizza could spark a culinary war – it's a real divisive topic!
  • At the festival, the varies of street food offerings made it hard to choose; should I have tacos or fish and chips this time?
  • The new study shows that the varies in sleep patterns among teenagers could explain why they are always tired, especially after a binge-watch.

Cultural Notes

As a noun, 'varies' is less common but emphasizes diversity. It’s often used in discussions about culture, food, and lifestyles.
