Vigil

Pronunciation:/ˈvɪdʒ.ɪl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 守夜
  • 警戒
  • 清醒的夜晚

Definitions

noun

Definition

A period of keeping awake during the time usually spent asleep, especially to keep watch or pray.

In ZH

通常在晚上保持清醒以监视或祈祷的时间段。

Examples

  • The community held a candlelight vigil for the victims.
  • They kept a vigil outside the hospital for hours.
  • During the vigil, people shared stories and memories.
  • A vigil was organized to raise awareness about climate change.
  • She attended the vigil to show support for the missing person.
