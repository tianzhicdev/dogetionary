Vivisection

EN→ZH

What Does Vivisection Mean?

  • 活体解剖
  • 生理解剖
  • 生物解剖

Vivisection Pronunciation

/ˌvɪvɪˈsɛkʃən/

Vivisection Definition

noun

Definition

The practice of performing operations on live animals for the purpose of experimentation or scientific research.

In ZH

在活体动物身上进行手术以进行实验或科学研究的做法。

How to Use Vivisection in a Sentence

  • Vivisection has sparked significant ethical debates in the scientific community.
  • Many people oppose vivisection due to concerns about animal welfare.
  • Researchers argue that vivisection is necessary for advancing medical science.
  • The laws regulating vivisection vary by country and region.
  • Alternatives to vivisection are being explored in many laboratories.

Synonyms for Vivisection

  • Vivacious
  • Vivify
