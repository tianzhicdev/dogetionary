Vestige

EN→ZH

What Does Vestige Mean?

  • 遗迹
  • 残留物
  • 痕迹

Vestige Pronunciation

/ˈvɛstɪdʒ/

Vestige Definition

noun

Definition

a trace, mark, or visible sign left by something that is no longer present or in existence

In ZH

某事物不再存在时留下的痕迹、标记或可见迹象

How to Use Vestige in a Sentence

  • The ruins are the last vestige of a once-great civilization.
  • She could see the vestige of her former self in the mirror.
  • Fossils are vestiges of ancient life.
  • There was only a vestige of hope left after the tragedy.
  • The once vibrant colors were now just a vestige of their former glory.
