Viridescent

Pronunciation:/ˌvɪrɪˈdɛs.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 翠绿色的
  • 青翠的
  • 生机盎然的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having a greenish tint; becoming green or greenish.

In ZH

具有绿色调；变得绿色或青绿色。

Examples

  • The walls of the room were painted a viridescent shade that reminded her of spring.
  • The viridescent leaves shimmered in the sunlight.
  • He wore a viridescent tie that complemented his suit perfectly.
  • In the garden, the viridescent plants thrived under the sun.
  • She admired the viridescent hues of the forest during her hike.
