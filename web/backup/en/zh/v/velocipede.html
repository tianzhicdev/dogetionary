Velocipede

EN→ZH

What Does Velocipede Mean?

  • 快速脚踏车
  • 古代自行车

Velocipede Pronunciation

/ˈvɛl.ə.sɪ.piːd/

Velocipede Definition

noun

Definition

a human-powered vehicle with two or three wheels, typically propelled by pedaling.

In ZH

一种由人力驱动的交通工具，通常有两个或三个轮子，通常通过踏板驱动。

How to Use Velocipede in a Sentence

  • The velocipede was a popular mode of transportation in the 19th century.
  • Many inventors experimented with designs for the velocipede.
  • Children rode their velocipedes around the park on sunny days.
  • The museum features a velocipede from the late 1800s.
  • Learning to ride a velocipede can be a fun challenge.

Cultural Notes

The velocipede is an early precursor to the modern bicycle, representing a significant development in transportation technology.

Synonyms for Velocipede
