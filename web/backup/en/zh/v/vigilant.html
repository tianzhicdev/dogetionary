Vigilant

EN→ZH

What Does Vigilant Mean?

  • 警惕的
  • 警觉的
  • 警醒的

Vigilant Pronunciation

/ˈvɪdʒ.ɪ.lənt/

Vigilant Definition

adjective

Definition

keeping careful watch for possible danger or difficulties

In ZH

保持警惕，密切注意潜在的危险或困难

How to Use Vigilant in a Sentence

  • The security guards remained vigilant throughout the night.
  • She was vigilant in monitoring her surroundings.
  • It's important to stay vigilant while driving at night.
  • The community is vigilant against any signs of crime.
  • Vigilant citizens can help prevent accidents in the neighborhood.

Synonyms for Vigilant

  • Vignette
  • Vigorous
