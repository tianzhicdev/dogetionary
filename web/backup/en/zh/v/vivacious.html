Vivacious

EN→ZH

What Does Vivacious Mean?

  • 活泼的
  • 生动的
  • 快活的

Vivacious Pronunciation

/vɪˈveɪʃəs/

Vivacious Definition

adjective

Definition

Attractively lively and animated.

In ZH

吸引人地生动和活泼的。

How to Use Vivacious in a Sentence

  • She has a vivacious personality that lights up the room.
  • The vivacious host kept the guests entertained all night.
  • His vivacious spirit makes him a joy to be around.
  • The vivacious colors of the flowers brightened the garden.
  • She is known for her vivacious style and bold fashion choices.

Synonyms for Vivacious

  • Vivify
  • Vivisection
