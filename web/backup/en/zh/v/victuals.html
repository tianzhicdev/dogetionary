Victuals

EN→ZH

What Does Victuals Mean?

  • 食品
  • 食物
  • 粮食

Victuals Pronunciation

/ˈvɪtʃʊəlz/

Victuals Definition

noun

Definition

Food and provisions, especially when prepared for consumption.

In ZH

食品和供应，尤其是准备供食用的食物。

How to Use Victuals in a Sentence

  • They packed enough victuals for the long journey ahead.
  • The pantry was stocked with various victuals for the winter.
  • He served a feast of victuals that delighted the guests.
  • During the camping trip, we relied on simple victuals like canned goods and snacks.
  • Victuals were scarce in the small village, making meals a challenge.
