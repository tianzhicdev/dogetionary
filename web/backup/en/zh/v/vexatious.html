Vexatious

EN→ZH

What Does Vexatious Mean?

  • 烦扰的
  • 诉讼繁琐的
  • 令人恼怒的

Vexatious Pronunciation

/vɛkˈseɪ.ʃəs/

Vexatious Definition

adjective

Definition

causing or intended to cause annoyance, frustration, or worry

In ZH

造成或意图造成烦恼、挫折或担忧的

How to Use Vexatious in a Sentence

  • The repeated phone calls from the telemarketer were vexatious.
  • Her vexatious behavior made it difficult for everyone to concentrate.
  • He filed a vexatious lawsuit against his neighbors.
  • It was a vexatious problem that required immediate attention.
  • The vexatious nature of the task led to much frustration among the team.

Synonyms for Vexatious

  • Vexation
  • Vexillology
  • Vexing
