Vorfreude

Pronunciation:/ˈfɔːrˌfrɔɪdə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 期待，盼望

Definitions

noun

Definition

a German word for the joyful, intense anticipation that comes from imagining future pleasures.

In ZH

一种德语单词，指的是想象未来快乐时产生的愉悦和强烈期待。

Examples

  • She felt a sense of vorfreude as the holiday approached.
  • The children were filled with vorfreude before their birthday party.
  • He couldn't contain his vorfreude for the concert next week.
  • The vorfreude of starting a new job can be just as exciting as the job itself.
  • Travelers experience vorfreude when planning their next adventure.

Cultural Notes

Vorfreude is a culturally significant concept in German-speaking countries, often associated with the enjoyment of looking forward to something.
