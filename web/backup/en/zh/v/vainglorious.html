Vainglorious

Pronunciation:/ˌveɪnˈɡlɔːr.i.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 自负的
  • 虚荣的
  • 自夸的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

excessively proud of oneself or one's achievements; boastful

In ZH

过分自豪于自己或自己的成就；自夸的

Examples

  • His vainglorious behavior turned many people away.
  • She was too vainglorious to accept criticism.
  • In his vainglorious speech, he exaggerated his accomplishments.
  • The vainglorious prince demanded admiration from all his subjects.
  • Vainglorious individuals often struggle with genuine relationships.
