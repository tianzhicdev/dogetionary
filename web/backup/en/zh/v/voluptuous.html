Voluptuous

EN→ZH

What Does Voluptuous Mean?

  • 丰盈的
  • 性感的
  • 奢华的

Voluptuous Pronunciation

/vəˈlʌp.tʃu.əs/

Voluptuous Definition

adjective

Definition

Characterized by luxury, sensual pleasure, or curvaceous body shape.

In ZH

以奢华、感官愉悦或曲线身材为特征的。

How to Use Voluptuous in a Sentence

  • The artist portrayed a voluptuous figure in her painting.
  • She wore a voluptuous dress that accentuated her curves.
  • The voluptuous dessert was a delight to the senses.
  • He found the landscape to be voluptuous in its beauty.
  • Her voice had a voluptuous quality that captivated the audience.

Synonyms for Voluptuous

  • Volitional
  • Voluble
  • Volume
  • Voluminous
