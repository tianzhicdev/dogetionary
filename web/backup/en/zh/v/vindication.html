Vindication

Pronunciation:/ˌvɪndɪˈkeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 辩护
  • 维护
  • 证明

Definitions

noun

Definition

the action of clearing someone of blame or suspicion

In ZH

清除某人的指责或怀疑的行为

Examples

  • Her successful appeal was a vindication of her innocence.
  • The evidence provided a vindication for those who had remained loyal to her.
  • He sought vindication after being wrongfully accused of the crime.
  • The court's ruling was a vindication of his claims.
  • Public opinion shifted in his favor, leading to a feeling of vindication.
