Valetudinarian

Pronunciation:/ˌvælɪˌtjuːdɪˈnɛəriən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 虚弱者
  • 身体虚弱的人
  • 久病的人

Definitions

noun

Definition

a person who is weak or sickly, often someone who is chronically ill

In ZH

一个虚弱或多病的人，通常是指长期生病的人

Examples

  • The valetudinarian spent most of his time resting in his armchair.
  • Despite being a valetudinarian, she maintained a positive outlook on life.
  • The community rallied around the valetudinarian to provide support.
  • He was often seen as a valetudinarian due to his frequent illnesses.
  • Valetudinarian habits can lead to a sense of helplessness.
