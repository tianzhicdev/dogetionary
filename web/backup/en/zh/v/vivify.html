Vivify

EN→ZH

What Does Vivify Mean?

  • 使生动
  • 使复活
  • 使有活力

Vivify Pronunciation

/ˈvɪvɪfaɪ/

Vivify Definition

verb

Definition

to endow with life or energy; to animate

In ZH

赋予生命或活力；使生动

How to Use Vivify in a Sentence

  • The artist's vibrant colors vivify the landscape in her painting.
  • Reading can vivify the imagination and inspire creativity.
  • The speech was so passionate that it seemed to vivify the audience.
  • Ancient stories vivify our understanding of past cultures.
  • The new policies are designed to vivify the economy.

Synonyms for Vivify

  • Vivacious
  • Vivisection
