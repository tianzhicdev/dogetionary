Veneration

Pronunciation:/ˌvɛnəˈreɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 崇敬
  • 尊敬
  • 敬仰

Definitions

noun

Definition

Great respect or reverence for someone or something.

In ZH

对某人或某事的极大尊敬或崇敬。

Examples

  • The veneration of historical figures is common in many cultures.
  • She approached the shrine with a sense of veneration.
  • Veneration for the elderly is a strong tradition in many societies.
  • The veneration of nature is evident in their artwork.
  • He held a deep veneration for his mentors.
