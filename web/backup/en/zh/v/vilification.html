Vilification

EN→ZH

What Does Vilification Mean?

  • 抹黑
  • 诽谤
  • 贬低

Vilification Pronunciation

/ˌvɪlɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/

Vilification Definition

noun

Definition

the act of criticizing someone or something very harshly

In ZH

对某人或某事进行非常严厉的批评的行为

How to Use Vilification in a Sentence

  • The vilification of public figures is often rampant in social media.
  • His vilification by the press was unwarranted and cruel.
  • She faced vilification after speaking out against the injustices.
  • The vilification of certain groups can lead to dangerous stereotypes.
  • He wrote a book about the vilification of political dissenters.

Synonyms for Vilification

  • Vile
  • Vilify
  • Vilipend
  • Villein
