Variegation

Pronunciation:/ˌvɛrɪˈɡeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 变色
  • 斑驳
  • 杂色

Definitions

noun

Definition

The appearance of different colors in the leaves or flowers of plants due to the presence of different pigments.

In ZH

植物叶子或花朵因含有不同色素而呈现不同颜色的外观。

Examples

  • The variegation on the leaves of the plant makes it particularly attractive.
  • Many gardeners appreciate the variegation found in certain species of houseplants.
  • The artist used variegation in her paintings to represent the diversity of nature.
  • Some plants exhibit variegation as a natural mutation.
  • The variegation of the flower petals adds depth to the garden design.
