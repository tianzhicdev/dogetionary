Verdant

EN→ZH

What Does Verdant Mean?

  • 青翠的
  • 嫩绿的
  • 葱郁的

Verdant Pronunciation

/ˈvɜːrdənt/

Verdant Definition

adjective

Definition

Green with grass or other rich vegetation.

In ZH

绿草如茵或其他丰富植被的。

How to Use Verdant in a Sentence

  • The verdant hills were a sight to behold.
  • After the rain, the garden looked especially verdant.
  • They hiked through the verdant forest, enjoying the lush surroundings.
  • The artist painted a verdant landscape full of life.
  • Spring brings a verdant renewal to the countryside.
