Venturous

EN→ZH

What Does Venturous Mean?

  • 冒险的
  • 大胆的
  • 有风险的

Venturous Pronunciation

/ˈvɛn.tʃər.əs/

Venturous Definition

adjective

Definition

willing to take risks or try out new methods, ideas, or experiences; inclined to adventure.

In ZH

愿意冒险或尝试新方法、想法或体验的；倾向于冒险的。

How to Use Venturous in a Sentence

  • She has a venturous spirit and loves to travel to unknown places.
  • His venturous approach to business led to many successful ventures.
  • Many venturous people seek thrill in extreme sports.
  • The venturous students organized a trip to climb the highest mountain.
  • Being venturous can sometimes lead to unexpected opportunities.

Synonyms for Venturous

  • Veneer
  • Venerable
  • Venerate
  • Venerated
