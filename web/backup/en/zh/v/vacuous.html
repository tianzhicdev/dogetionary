Vacuous

EN→ZH

What Does Vacuous Mean?

  • 空洞的
  • 无内容的
  • 愚蠢的

Vacuous Pronunciation

/ˈvæk.ju.əs/

Vacuous Definition

adjective

Definition

Having or showing a lack of thought or intelligence; mindless.

In ZH

表现出缺乏思考或智慧；无思想的。

How to Use Vacuous in a Sentence

  • His vacuous remarks during the meeting left everyone confused.
  • She gave a vacuous smile, clearly not understanding the joke.
  • The novel was criticized for its vacuous characters and predictable plot.
  • Despite his title, his arguments were vacuous and unconvincing.
  • The vacuous look in his eyes suggested he was lost in thought.

Synonyms for Vacuous

  • Vacillate
  • Vacillating
  • Vacillation
  • Vacuity
