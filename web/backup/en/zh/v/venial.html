Venial

Pronunciation:/ˈviː.ni.əl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 可宽恕的
  • 轻罪的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Able to be forgiven; a minor sin or offense.

In ZH

可以被宽恕的；一种轻微的罪或过错。

Examples

  • His mistakes were venial, not deserving of harsh punishment.
  • The teacher considered the late homework a venial offense.
  • In the eyes of the law, this was a venial crime.
  • She committed a venial sin by telling a little white lie.
  • The church teaches that venial sins can be forgiven easily.
