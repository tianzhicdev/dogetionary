Visceral

EN→ZH

What Does Visceral Mean?

  • 内脏的
  • 发自内心的
  • 感情深厚的

Visceral Pronunciation

/ˈvɪs.ər.əl/

Visceral Definition

adjective

Definition

relating to deep inward feelings rather than to the intellect

In ZH

与深刻的内心感受而非智力相关

How to Use Visceral in a Sentence

  • Her visceral reaction to the news was unmistakable.
  • The film's visceral horror stayed with me long after it ended.
  • He wrote with a visceral intensity that captivated readers.
  • Many people have a visceral dislike of public speaking.
  • The artist's work evokes visceral responses from the audience.

Synonyms for Visceral
