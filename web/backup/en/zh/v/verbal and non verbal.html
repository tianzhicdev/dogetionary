Verbal And Non Verbal

Pronunciation:ˈvɜːr.bəl ənd ˈnɒn ˈvɜːr.bəl
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 口头的和非口头的
  • 语言和非语言
  • 言语和非言语

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to or consisting of words; spoken or written communication.

In ZH

与词语相关或由词语组成的；口头或书面交流。

Examples

  • Verbal communication is essential in a workplace setting.
  • He expressed his feelings more through verbal communication than through actions.
  • During the presentation, she used verbal cues to engage the audience.

Cultural Notes

Verbal communication is often valued in cultures that prioritize directness and clarity in conversation.

adjective

Definition

Relating to or expressed by non-linguistic means; involving body language, gestures, and facial expressions.

In ZH

与非语言手段相关或通过这些手段表达的；包括肢体语言、手势和面部表情。

Examples

  • Non-verbal cues can often convey more than words.
  • She communicated her approval through non-verbal gestures.
  • In many cultures, non-verbal communication plays a crucial role in social interactions.

Cultural Notes

Non-verbal communication may differ significantly across cultures; what is acceptable in one culture may be inappropriate in another.
