Vitiate

Pronunciation:/ˈvɪʃ.i.eɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 损害
  • 使恶化
  • 破坏

Definitions

verb

Definition

to impair the quality or efficiency of something; to spoil or ruin.

In ZH

损害某物的质量或效率；破坏或毁坏。

Examples

  • The scandal served to vitiate the politician's reputation.
  • These errors will vitiate the results of the experiment.
  • If you vitiate the contract, it will no longer be valid.
  • The lack of proper care can vitiate the benefits of the treatment.
  • His comments did nothing but vitiate the discussion.
