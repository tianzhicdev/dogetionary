Vexillology

EN→ZH

What Does Vexillology Mean?

  • 旗帜学
  • 旗帜研究
  • 研究旗帜的学科

Vexillology Pronunciation

/vɛkˈsɪləˌlɒdʒi/

Vexillology Definition

noun

Definition

the study of flags and their symbolism, history, and design

In ZH

对旗帜及其象征意义、历史和设计的研究

How to Use Vexillology in a Sentence

  • Vexillology helps us understand the significance of national flags.
  • She decided to pursue a degree in vexillology after her passion for flags grew.
  • The vexillology conference attracted experts from around the world.
  • He published a paper on the vexillology of medieval banners.
  • Many nations have unique vexillology that reflects their culture.
