Veracity

Pronunciation:/vəˈræs.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 真实性
  • 准确性
  • 诚实

Definitions

noun

Definition

Conformity to facts; accuracy or truthfulness.

In ZH

与事实相符；准确性或诚实性。

Examples

  • The veracity of his claims has been called into question.
  • We must ensure the veracity of the data before publishing the report.
  • Her veracity is what makes her a reliable witness.
  • Veracity is essential in journalism to maintain trust with the audience.
  • The veracity of the historical documents is widely accepted by scholars.
