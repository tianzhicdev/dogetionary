Vitriolic

Pronunciation:/ˌvɪtrɪˈɒlɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 尖刻的
  • 毒舌的
  • 愤怒的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

filled with bitter criticism or malice

In ZH

充满尖锐批评或恶意的

Examples

  • His vitriolic remarks damaged their friendship.
  • The article was criticized for its vitriolic tone.
  • She responded with a vitriolic letter.
  • The debate turned vitriolic as the candidates attacked each other.
  • His vitriolic sarcasm often made others uncomfortable.
