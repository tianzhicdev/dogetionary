Voluble

Pronunciation:/ˈvɒljʊbəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 多话的
  • 健谈的
  • 流利的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by a ready and continuous flow of words; fluent; talkative.

In ZH

以单词的顺畅和持续的流动为特征；流利；健谈的。

Examples

  • She is a voluble speaker who can captivate an audience.
  • The voluble discussion in the meeting made it hard to follow.
  • He became voluble after a few drinks at the party.
  • Her voluble nature made her the life of the gathering.
  • The voluble commentary provided by the host kept viewers entertained.
