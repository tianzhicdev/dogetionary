Vanquish

EN→ZH

What Does Vanquish Mean?

  • 征服
  • 战胜
  • 击败

Vanquish Pronunciation

/ˈvæŋkwɪʃ/

Vanquish Definition

verb

Definition

to defeat thoroughly; to overcome in a contest or conflict.

In ZH

彻底击败; 在比赛或冲突中胜过。

How to Use Vanquish in a Sentence

  • The warriors sought to vanquish their enemies in battle.
  • She vowed to vanquish her fears and pursue her dreams.
  • The new strategy helped the company vanquish its competition.
  • He managed to vanquish his opponent in a surprising turn of events.
  • The hero was determined to vanquish the evil villain.

Synonyms for Vanquish

  • Vantage
