Vortex

EN→ZH

What Does Vortex Mean?

  • 漩涡
  • 旋涡

Vortex Pronunciation

/ˈvɔːr.tɛks/

Vortex Definition

noun

Definition

A mass of whirling fluid or air, especially a whirlpool or whirlwind.

In ZH

一种旋转的液体或空气团，尤其是漩涡或旋风。

How to Use Vortex in a Sentence

  • The boat was caught in a dangerous vortex.
  • You could see the leaves swirling around in the vortex of wind.
  • The scientist studied the vortex created by the moving water.
  • In the storm, a funnel-shaped vortex descended from the clouds.
  • The tornado formed a powerful vortex that damaged many buildings.

Synonyms for Vortex

  • Voracious
  • Voracity
  • Vorfreude
  • Vorpal
