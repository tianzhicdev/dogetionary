Vexing

Pronunciation:/ˈvɛksɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 烦恼的
  • 令人困惑的
  • 恼人的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

causing annoyance, frustration, or worry

In ZH

引起烦恼、挫折或担忧的

Examples

  • His attitude is vexing to everyone in the office.
  • The delay in the project has been quite vexing.
  • It's vexing that we can't find a solution to this problem.
  • I found her constant questions to be rather vexing.
  • Dealing with the bureaucracy can be a vexing experience.
