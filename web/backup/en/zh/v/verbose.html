Verbose

Pronunciation:/vɜːrˈboʊs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 冗长的
  • 啰嗦的
  • 累赘的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

using more words than are needed; wordy

In ZH

使用比必要的更多的词；冗长的

Examples

  • His verbose explanation confused the audience.
  • The report was so verbose that it lost its main point.
  • She prefers concise writing over verbose descriptions.
  • The verbose lecturer droned on for hours.
  • In a business setting, being too verbose can be a disadvantage.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, conciseness is often valued, particularly in professional and academic writing.
