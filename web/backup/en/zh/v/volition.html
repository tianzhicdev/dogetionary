Volition

Pronunciation:/voʊˈlɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 意志
  • 选择
  • 决心

Definitions

noun

Definition

The faculty or power of using one's will.

In ZH

使用自己意志的能力或权力。

Examples

  • She made the decision of her own volition.
  • The act was done without coercion, purely out of volition.
  • Understanding one's volition can lead to better self-control.
  • He acted with volition, choosing to pursue his dreams.
  • Volition plays a key role in moral responsibility.
