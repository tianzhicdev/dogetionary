Vilipend

EN→ZH

What Does Vilipend Mean?

  • 贬低
  • 轻视
  • 鄙视

Vilipend Pronunciation

/ˈvɪlɪˌpɛnd/

Vilipend Definition

verb

Definition

to regard or treat as of little worth; to belittle or disparage.

In ZH

把...视为微不足道; 贬低或轻视。

How to Use Vilipend in a Sentence

  • The critics vilipend the author's new novel, claiming it lacks originality.
  • She felt vilipended by her colleagues after her proposal was rejected.
  • The politician attempted to vilipend his opponent's character during the debate.
  • It is unfair to vilipend someone based on rumors alone.
  • Many artists have been vilipended in their time, only to be celebrated later.

Synonyms for Vilipend

  • Vile
  • Vilification
  • Vilify
  • Villein
