Vociferous

EN→ZH

What Does Vociferous Mean?

  • 喧哗的
  • 大声叫的
  • 吵闹的

Vociferous Pronunciation

/vəˈsɪf.ə.rəs/

Vociferous Definition

adjective

Definition

Vehement or clamorous; loud and noisy.

In ZH

激烈或喧哗的；大声而吵闹的。

How to Use Vociferous in a Sentence

  • The vociferous crowd demanded justice.
  • His vociferous arguments made it hard to ignore his point of view.
  • During the debate, she was particularly vociferous in her opinions.
  • The children became vociferous when they realized they were going to the amusement park.
  • He was a vociferous critic of the government's new policy.

Synonyms for Vociferous

  • Vociferate
