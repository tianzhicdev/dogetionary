Doppelganger

EN→ZH

What Does Doppelganger Mean?

  • 幽灵影像
  • 分身
  • 双胞胎

Doppelganger Pronunciation

/ˈdɒpəlˌɡæŋər/

Doppelganger Definition

noun

Definition

a non-biologically related look-alike or double of a living person.

In ZH

一个与生物学上无关的相似者或一个人的复制品。

How to Use Doppelganger in a Sentence

  • I was surprised to see my doppelganger at the event last night.
  • Some people believe that everyone has a doppelganger somewhere in the world.
  • In folklore, a doppelganger is often seen as an omen or a bad sign.
  • The movie featured a character who meets his own doppelganger in an alternate universe.
  • She jokingly referred to her friend as her doppelganger because they looked so similar.
