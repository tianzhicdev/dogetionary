Discrete

Pronunciation:dɪˈskrit
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 离散的
  • 不连续的
  • 分离的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Distinct; separate; non-continuous.

In ZH

不同的；单独的；不连续的。

Examples

  • Did you hear about the scientist who studied discrete particles? He said it was all a matter of finding the right 'separating' factor!
  • In statistics, discrete data is like the difference between popcorn kernels (separate) and a movie theater floor (one big continuous mess)!
  • I tried to count my discrete steps on a treadmill, but it turned into a continuous 'run for my life' situation when I forgot how to get off!

Cultural Notes

The word 'discrete' is commonly used in mathematics and computer science, emphasizing clarity and division. Mispronouncing it as 'discreet' can lead to humorous misunderstandings since 'discreet' relates to being careful or unobtrusive.
