Derivative

EN→ZH

What Does Derivative Mean?

  • 衍生物
  • 导数

Derivative Pronunciation

/dɪˈrɪv.ɪ.tɪv/

Derivative Definition

noun

Definition

Something that is based on another source; a result or consequence derived from something else.

In ZH

基于其他来源的事物；从其他事物中得出的结果或后果。

How to Use Derivative in a Sentence

  • The financial product was a derivative based on underlying assets.
  • In mathematics, a derivative represents the rate of change of a function.
  • Many modern art styles are derivative of earlier movements.
  • The film is considered derivative of a classic novel.
  • She created a derivative work inspired by the original painting.

Cultural Notes

In finance, derivatives are often used for hedging or speculation. In art and literature, the term can carry a negative connotation, implying a lack of originality.
