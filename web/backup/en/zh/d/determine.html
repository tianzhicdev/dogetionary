Determine

Pronunciation:dɪˈtɜr.mɪn
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 确定
  • 决定
  • 测定
  • 判定

Definitions

verb

Definition

To cause something to occur in a particular way; to be the decisive factor in something.

In ZH

使某事以特定方式发生；在某事中起决定性作用。

Examples

  • The weather will determine our travel plans.
  • The results of the survey will determine the next steps of the project.
  • Personal interests often determine career choices.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'determine' often implies a sense of finality or control over outcomes, which can vary in usage based on context.

verb

Definition

To officially decide or conclude something after careful consideration.

In ZH

在仔细考虑后正式决定或结束某事。

Examples

  • The committee will determine the winner of the competition next week.
  • She is still trying to determine the best course of action.
  • They need to determine the eligibility requirements for applicants.

Cultural Notes

This usage emphasizes the decision-making process, often found in formal or legal contexts.

verb

Definition

To ascertain or calculate something based on evidence or reasoning.

In ZH

根据证据或推理确定或计算某事。

Examples

  • Scientists determine the age of fossils using various methods.
  • You should determine your budget before starting the project.
  • They were able to determine the cause of the problem after a thorough investigation.

Cultural Notes

This sense is common in scientific or analytical settings where precise data is crucial.
