Dendrophilous

Pronunciation:/ˌdɛn.drəˈfɪl.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 树木栖息的
  • 喜树的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

referring to organisms that love or are dependent on trees for their habitat or sustenance

In ZH

指依赖树木生存或栖息的生物

Examples

  • The dendrophilous species thrive in forested areas.
  • Many dendrophilous insects are critical for pollination.
  • Dendrophilous birds often build their nests high in the branches.
  • Researchers study dendrophilous plants to understand their ecology.
  • Protecting dendrophilous organisms is essential for forest conservation.
