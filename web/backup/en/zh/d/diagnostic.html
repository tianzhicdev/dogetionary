Diagnostic

EN→ZH

What Does Diagnostic Mean?

  • 诊断的
  • 判别的
  • 诊断工具

Diagnostic Pronunciation

ˌdaɪəgˈnɒstɪk

Diagnostic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to the identification of the nature of an illness or other problem by examination of the symptoms.

In ZH

与通过检查症状来识别疾病或其他问题的性质相关的。

How to Use Diagnostic in a Sentence

  • The doctor performed a diagnostic test to determine the cause of the patient's symptoms.
  • Our team used diagnostic tools to troubleshoot the software issue effectively.
  • She provided a diagnostic analysis of the economic trends in the report.

Cultural Notes

In medical contexts, 'diagnostic' often relates to procedures or tests like X-rays or blood tests that help in understanding a patient's health status.

noun

Definition

A test, examination, or procedure used to identify a disease or condition.

In ZH

用于识别疾病或状况的测试、检查或程序。

How to Use Diagnostic in a Sentence

  • The diagnostic revealed that he had a vitamin deficiency.
  • After conducting several diagnostics, the technician repaired the network issues quickly.
  • The diagnostic included a series of questions about the patient's medical history.

Cultural Notes

In many healthcare systems, diagnostic tests are crucial for ensuring proper treatment and are often the first step in the healthcare process.

Synonyms for Diagnostic
