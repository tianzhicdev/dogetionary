Development

Pronunciation:dɪˈvɛl.əp.mənt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 发展
  • 开发
  • 进展
  • 成长

Definitions

noun

Definition

The process of developing or being developed, such as growth, progress, or improvement in a particular field or area.

In ZH

发展或被发展的过程，例如在特定领域或领域中的增长、进展或改进。

Examples

  • His development as an artist has been impressive.
  • The development of new technologies is essential for future growth.
  • They are focused on the development of the community's infrastructure.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, the concept of development not only relates to economic and technological growth but also to social and educational progress.

noun

Definition

A specific instance of developing or being developed, often referring to a new project or a change in a particular area.

In ZH

发展或被发展的特定实例，通常指的是一个新项目或某一领域的变化。

Examples

  • The development of the new shopping mall will create hundreds of jobs.
  • We are excited about the development of the new park downtown.
  • The latest development in the project will be unveiled next week.

Cultural Notes

In urban contexts, developments often signify not just physical structures but also potential changes in the social fabric of neighborhoods.

noun

Definition

The state of being developed; maturity or progress in various contexts, including personal growth, business, or technology.

In ZH

被发展的状态；在个人成长、商业或技术等各种背景下的成熟或进展。

Examples

  • Personal development is important for career success.
  • The development of his skills has made him a valuable employee.
  • Continuous development is necessary to keep up with industry standards.

Cultural Notes

Personal and professional development are commonly emphasized in Western education and business practices, often involving self-improvement and lifelong learning.
