Damask

EN→ZH

What Does Damask Mean?

  • 大马士革布
  • 花纹织物
  • 大马士革

Damask Pronunciation

/ˈdæm.əsk/

Damask Definition

noun

Definition

A rich patterned fabric of silk or linen or a similar fabric made from artificial fibers.

In ZH

一种由丝绸、亚麻或类似的人工纤维制成的丰富图案织物。

How to Use Damask in a Sentence

  • The elegant tablecloth was made of damask.
  • She wore a stunning dress crafted from damask.
  • The curtains were made of a luxurious damask fabric.
  • Damask roses are known for their sweet fragrance.
  • He admired the intricate patterns on the damask upholstery.
noun

Definition

A type of ornamental pattern or design used in weaving, characterized by its reversible pattern.

In ZH

一种用于织造的装饰性图案或设计，具有可逆的图案特征。

How to Use Damask in a Sentence

  • The artist designed a damask pattern for the wallpaper.
  • She chose a damask motif for the quilt she was making.
  • The damask patterns were inspired by ancient designs.
  • They produced textiles featuring elaborate damask designs.
  • The wedding invitations had a damask print that added elegance.
