Dissemination

EN→ZH

What Does Dissemination Mean?

  • 传播
  • 散布
  • 发布

Dissemination Pronunciation

/dɪˌsɛmɪˈneɪʃən/

Dissemination Definition

noun

Definition

The act of spreading something, especially information, widely.

In ZH

广泛传播某事物的行为，特别是信息。

How to Use Dissemination in a Sentence

  • The dissemination of information is crucial for public awareness.
  • Effective dissemination of knowledge can change lives.
  • The organization focuses on the dissemination of scientific research.
  • Digital media has changed the way we approach dissemination.
  • Timely dissemination of news is important for a democratic society.

Synonyms for Dissemination

  • Disambiguation
  • Discern
  • Discernment
  • Disclosing
