Decision

Pronunciation:dɪˈsɪʒ.ən
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 决定
  • 决策
  • 选择

Definitions

noun

Definition

A conclusion or resolution reached after consideration.

In ZH

经过考虑后达成的结论或决议。

Examples

  • After much deliberation, my cat decided that the best place to lay down was right on my laptop keyboard, effectively making a 'purr-suasive' case for cat naps!
  • In the latest news, a town voted to replace traffic lights with disco balls—talk about a 'groovy' decision!
  • Why did the scarecrow win an award? Because he was outstanding in his field, making a decision to stand tall!

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, a 'decision' often implies a process of weighing options, reflecting values of individualism and critical thinking.

verb

Definition

To make a choice or come to a conclusion about something.

In ZH

对某事做出选择或得出结论。

Examples

  • I decided to wear my sandwich costume to the meeting today—because sometimes, you just have to relish the moment!
  • My friend decided to start running marathons, but her only training was running late to brunch every Sunday—priorities!
  • She decided to open a bakery that only sells flatbread, as she believes in only 'kneading' the essentials!

Cultural Notes

The verb form is less common but emphasizes the personal agency in making choices. It's often used in discussions about accountability and responsibilities.
