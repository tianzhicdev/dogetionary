Destitute

EN→ZH

What Does Destitute Mean?

  • 贫困的
  • 缺乏的
  • 穷困的

Destitute Pronunciation

/ˈdɛstəˌtjut/

Destitute Definition

adjective

Definition

Lacking the necessities of life; extremely poor.

In ZH

缺乏生活必需品的；极其贫穷的。

How to Use Destitute in a Sentence

  • The charity aims to help destitute families in the community.
  • She was destitute after losing her job and home.
  • Many destitute individuals rely on shelters for food and safety.
  • He felt a deep compassion for the destitute living on the streets.
  • The destitute population in the city has been increasing over the years.
noun

Definition

A person who is destitute; someone who is extremely poor.

In ZH

一个贫困的人；极其贫穷的人。

How to Use Destitute in a Sentence

  • The destitutes often struggle to find basic necessities.
  • She dedicated her life to helping the destitutes in her area.
  • Governments should invest more in programs for the destitutes.
  • He spoke out on behalf of the destitutes.
  • The city organized a fundraiser to assist the destitutes.

Synonyms for Destitute

  • Descant
  • Descent
  • Descry
  • Desideratum
  • Desperate
