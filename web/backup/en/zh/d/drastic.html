Drastic

Pronunciation:/ˈdræstɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 激烈的
  • 急剧的
  • 极端的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

likely to have a strong or far-reaching effect; extreme in nature.

In ZH

可能对结果有强烈或深远影响；性质极端的。

Examples

  • The government implemented drastic measures to control inflation.
  • After the accident, she made drastic changes to her lifestyle.
  • He took drastic steps to improve his health.
  • The company faced drastic losses due to the market crash.
  • Drastic weather changes can affect agriculture significantly.
