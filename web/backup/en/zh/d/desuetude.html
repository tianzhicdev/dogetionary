Desuetude

Pronunciation:/ˈdɛs.wɪ.tjuːd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 废弃
  • 不再使用
  • 不再流行

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of disuse or inactivity.

In ZH

废弃或不活动的状态。

Examples

  • The old railway station fell into desuetude after the new line was built.
  • Many traditions fall into desuetude as society evolves.
  • Once a popular venue, the theater now exists in desuetude.
  • The practice of writing letters has fallen into desuetude in the digital age.
  • This law has been largely in desuetude for several decades.
