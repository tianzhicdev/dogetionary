Decadent

Pronunciation:/ˈdɛk.ə.dənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 颓废的
  • 奢华的
  • 堕落的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by or reflecting a state of moral or cultural decline, often due to excessive indulgence or luxury.

In ZH

以堕落或文化衰退的状态为特征，通常由于过度放纵或奢华。

Examples

  • The decadent lifestyle of the rich can sometimes seem unattractive.
  • Many critics argue that the film's decadent themes detract from its overall quality.
  • She enjoyed a decadent dessert that was rich with chocolate and cream.
  • The party was filled with decadent decorations and lavish food.
  • His writing often explores the decadent aspects of society.
