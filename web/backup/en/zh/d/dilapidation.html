Dilapidation

EN→ZH

What Does Dilapidation Mean?

  • 倒塌
  • 破旧
  • 废弃

Dilapidation Pronunciation

/dɪˌlæpɪˈdeɪʃən/

Dilapidation Definition

noun

Definition

the state or process of falling into decay or destruction; ruin.

In ZH

坠入衰退或破坏状态的状态或过程；毁灭。

How to Use Dilapidation in a Sentence

  • The dilapidation of the old church was a sad sight.
  • They are raising funds to prevent the dilapidation of historic buildings.
  • The once grand mansion fell into dilapidation after years of neglect.
  • We need to address the dilapidation of public parks in the area.
  • The city's dilapidation problem requires immediate attention.
