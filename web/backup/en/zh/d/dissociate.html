Dissociate

Pronunciation:/dɪsəˈsoʊʃiːeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 分裂
  • 脱离
  • 解除

Definitions

verb

Definition

to separate or detach oneself from a connection or association

In ZH

将自己从某种联系或关联中分开或脱离

Examples

  • I need to dissociate myself from negative influences.
  • It is important to dissociate facts from opinions.
  • They often dissociate from their past to start fresh.
  • She learned to dissociate her emotions during stressful situations.
  • It's hard to dissociate the smells of childhood from memories.
verb

Definition

to cause to become separated or detached

In ZH

使分开或分离

Examples

  • The medication can dissociate the molecules in the solution.
  • He tried to dissociate his work life from his personal life.
  • The group was dissociated from the main organization after a scandal.
  • The artist aims to dissociate art from commercialism.
  • The scientists wanted to dissociate the two chemicals to observe their reactions.
