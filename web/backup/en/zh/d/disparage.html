Disparage

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈpærɪdʒ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 贬低
  • 轻视
  • 抨击

Definitions

verb

Definition

To regard or represent as being of little worth.

In ZH

把...视为没有价值；贬低。

Examples

  • It's unwise to disparage someone's efforts.
  • She disparaged his achievements during the meeting.
  • The article seemed to disparage his contributions to the project.
  • Disparaging remarks can hurt someone's feelings.
  • They tried to disparage the competitor's reputation.
