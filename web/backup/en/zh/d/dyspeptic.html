Dyspeptic

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈpɛptɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 消化不良的
  • 胃痛的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having difficulty in digesting food; feeling ill from indigestion.

In ZH

消化不良的；由于消化不良而感到不适的。

Examples

  • After the heavy meal, he felt quite dyspeptic.
  • The dyspeptic patient complained of stomach pain.
  • She always becomes dyspeptic when she eats too much fried food.
  • His dyspeptic mood made it hard for him to enjoy the party.
  • The doctor recommended a bland diet for her dyspeptic symptoms.
noun

Definition

a person who suffers from dyspepsia or has a gloomy disposition.

In ZH

一个患有消化不良或心情阴郁的人。

Examples

  • He is known as a dyspeptic, always complaining about something.
  • In literature, the dyspeptic character often adds comic relief.
  • Her friends teased her for being such a dyspeptic.
  • The dyspeptics of society often see the glass as half empty.
  • Dyspeptics tend to have a pessimistic outlook on life.
