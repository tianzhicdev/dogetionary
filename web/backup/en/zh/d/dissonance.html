Dissonance

EN→ZH

What Does Dissonance Mean?

  • 不和谐
  • 不一致

Dissonance Pronunciation

/ˈdɪsənəns/

Dissonance Definition

noun

Definition

a lack of harmony among musical notes or a disagreement or conflict between ideas or beliefs

In ZH

音符之间缺乏和谐或思想、信念之间的分歧或冲突

How to Use Dissonance in a Sentence

  • The dissonance in the music created a sense of unease.
  • There was a dissonance between her words and her actions.
  • The team experienced dissonance as they struggled to agree on a common strategy.
  • Philosophers often discuss the dissonance between perception and reality.
  • The dissonance in the community led to open debates about cultural values.
