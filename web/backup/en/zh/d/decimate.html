Decimate

EN→ZH

What Does Decimate Mean?

  • 大规模毁灭
  • 严重削弱
  • 严重破坏

Decimate Pronunciation

/ˈdɛs.ɪ.meɪt/

Decimate Definition

verb

Definition

To destroy a large part of something; to severely reduce the strength or effectiveness of something.

In ZH

摧毁一部分事物；严重削弱某事物的力量或效果。

How to Use Decimate in a Sentence

  • The population was decimated by the plague.
  • They decimated the enemy forces in battle.
  • Their revenues were decimated by the economic downturn.
  • The environmental changes could decimate local wildlife.
  • The new policies could decimate small businesses.

Synonyms for Decimate

  • Decadent
  • Decades
  • Decamp
  • Decant
  • Decay
