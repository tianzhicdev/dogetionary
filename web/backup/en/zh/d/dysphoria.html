Dysphoria

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈfɔːr.i.ə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不快感
  • 情绪不安
  • 烦躁

Definitions

noun

Definition

a state of general dissatisfaction or unease, often associated with a feeling of discomfort in one's identity or sense of self.

In ZH

一种普遍的不满或不安状态，通常与对自我身份或自我感觉的不适感相关联。

Examples

  • She felt a deep sense of dysphoria that she couldn't quite explain.
  • Many people experience dysphoria during times of significant life changes.
  • The feeling of dysphoria can lead to depression if not addressed.
  • He struggled with gender dysphoria for many years before transitioning.
  • Dysphoria often affects mental health and well-being.

Cultural Notes

In contemporary discussions of mental health, dysphoria, particularly gender dysphoria, has gained recognition and understanding, leading to significant social conversations around identity.
