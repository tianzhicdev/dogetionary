Disparate

Pronunciation:/ˈdɪspərət/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 截然不同的
  • 不相干的
  • 异类的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

essentially different in kind; not allowing comparison

In ZH

本质上种类不同的；不允许比较的

Examples

  • The two cultures are so disparate that they can't seem to communicate.
  • Despite their disparate backgrounds, they became the best of friends.
  • The disparate opinions on the issue made it difficult to reach a consensus.
  • In a disparate society, individuals often have radically different life experiences.
  • Their disparate approaches to problem-solving led to innovative solutions.
