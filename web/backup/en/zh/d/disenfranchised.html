Disenfranchised

EN→ZH

What Does Disenfranchised Mean?

  • 被剥夺权利的
  • 失去选举权的
  • 无权的

Disenfranchised Pronunciation

/ˌdɪs.ɛnˈfræn.tʃaɪzd/

Disenfranchised Definition

adjective

Definition

Deprived of the rights of citizenship, especially the right to vote.

In ZH

剥夺公民权利，特别是投票权的。

How to Use Disenfranchised in a Sentence

  • Many groups in society have historically been disenfranchised.
  • The new law aims to protect the rights of disenfranchised citizens.
  • After the reform, thousands of disenfranchised voters were able to cast their ballots.
  • Activists worked tirelessly to help the disenfranchised gain access to voting.
  • Disenfranchised communities often lack representation in government.
