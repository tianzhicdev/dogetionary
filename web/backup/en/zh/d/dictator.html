Dictator

EN→ZH

What Does Dictator Mean?

  • 独裁者
  • 专制统治者

Dictator Pronunciation

dɪkˈteɪtər

Dictator Definition

noun

Definition

A ruler with total power over a country, typically one who has obtained control by force.

In ZH

一个对国家拥有绝对权力的统治者，通常是通过武力获得控制权的。

How to Use Dictator in a Sentence

  • The dictator ruled the country with an iron fist.
  • Many people protested against the dictator's oppressive regime.
  • After the revolution, the dictator was overthrown.

Cultural Notes

In various historical contexts, dictators are often seen as authoritarian leaders who suppress political opposition and limit personal freedoms. The term can evoke strong reactions based on the associated historical events.

noun

Definition

A person who expresses their opinions in an overbearing or domineering manner, similar to how a ruler would enforce laws without regard for others.

In ZH

以一种专横或主导的方式表达自己意见的人，类似于统治者在没有考虑他人的情况下强制执行法律。

How to Use Dictator in a Sentence

  • He acted like a dictator, making all the decisions without consulting anyone.
  • She was a dictator in the group project, refusing to consider other ideas.
  • Nobody wanted to challenge the dictator of the team.

Cultural Notes

This usage is often applied in informal settings, such as in workplaces or group projects, to describe someone who takes charge without allowing others to contribute.

Synonyms for Dictator

  • Dichotomy
