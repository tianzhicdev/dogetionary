Dodecahedron

Pronunciation:/doʊˈdɛkəˌhiːdrən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 十二面体
  • 十二面体形状
  • 十二面体几何

Definitions

noun

Definition

A three-dimensional shape with twelve flat faces, typically with regular pentagonal faces.

In ZH

一种具有十二个平面面的三维形状，通常具有规则的五边形面。

Examples

  • The dodecahedron is one of the five Platonic solids.
  • In geometry class, we studied the properties of the dodecahedron.
  • The artist created a sculpture inspired by the shape of a dodecahedron.
  • Dodecahedrons can be found in various board games as dice.
  • Mathematicians often explore the symmetrical properties of a dodecahedron.
