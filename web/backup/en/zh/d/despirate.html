Despirate

Pronunciation:dɪˈspaɪərɪt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 绝望的
  • 绝望
  • 失去希望的

Definitions

verb

Definition

To lose hope or confidence.

In ZH

失去希望或信心。

Examples

  • After trying so many times and failing, she began to despirate about her chances of success.
  • He despirated when he couldn't find a solution to the problem.

Cultural Notes

The term is rarely used in contemporary English and may be considered archaic or poetic. It reflects a deep sense of hopelessness, often found in literary contexts.

Definition

Characterized by a loss of hope; hopeless.

In ZH

以失去希望为特征；绝望的。
