Dendrology

Pronunciation:/ˌdɛnˈdrɒlədʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 树木学
  • 树木学科
  • 树木论

Definitions

noun

Definition

the branch of biology concerned with the study of trees and woody plants.

In ZH

研究树木和木本植物的生物学分支。

Examples

  • Dendrology is essential for understanding forest ecosystems.
  • He studied dendrology to learn more about tree species.
  • Many universities offer courses in dendrology for biology majors.
  • The dendrology field trip allowed students to identify various tree species.
  • Research in dendrology can help in conservation efforts.
