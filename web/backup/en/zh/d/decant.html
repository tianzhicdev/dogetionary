Decant

EN→ZH

What Does Decant Mean?

  • 倒出
  • 分离
  • 转移

Decant Pronunciation

/dɪˈkænt/

Decant Definition

verb

Definition

To pour a liquid, especially wine, from one container into another, typically to separate it from sediment.

In ZH

将液体（特别是酒）从一个容器倒入另一个容器，通常是为了将其与沉淀物分开。

How to Use Decant in a Sentence

  • I decided to decant the wine to enhance its flavor.
  • After letting it breathe, I will decant the whisky.
  • She slowly decanted the juice into a glass.
  • It is advisable to decant aged wines before serving.
  • He forgot to decant the new batch of beer before the tasting.

Cultural Notes

Decanting is a common practice in wine tasting to improve the taste and aroma of the wine.
