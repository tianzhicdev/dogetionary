Dichotomous

Pronunciation:/daɪˈkɒtə.məs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 对立的
  • 二分的
  • 两分法的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by the division into two mutually exclusive or contradictory groups

In ZH

特征是分为两个互相排斥或矛盾的组

Examples

  • In scientific studies, data can often be categorized into dichotomous variables.
  • The dichotomous nature of the debate left no room for middle ground.
  • He presented a dichotomous view of the political landscape.
  • The concept of good versus evil is often seen as dichotomous in literature.
  • Dichotomous thinking can lead to oversimplified conclusions.
