Deprecate

Pronunciation:/ˈdɛprɪkeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 贬低
  • 反对
  • 不赞成

Definitions

verb

Definition

To express disapproval of or criticize something, especially something that one considers to be undesirable or not worthy of respect.

In ZH

对某事表示不赞成或批评，尤其是对人们认为不理想或不值得尊重的事物。

Examples

  • Many experts deprecate the use of outdated technology in classrooms.
  • She deprecated the idea of having a party during the pandemic.
  • The teacher deprecated the students’ late submission of assignments.
  • His comments were meant to deprecate the team's efforts, despite their hard work.
  • They deprecated the proposal for a new policy that would raise taxes.
