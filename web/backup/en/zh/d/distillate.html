Distillate

EN→ZH

What Does Distillate Mean?

  • 提炼物
  • 蒸馏物

Distillate Pronunciation

/ˈdɪstɪleɪt/

Distillate Definition

noun

Definition

A purified liquid or substance obtained by distillation, especially a concentrated extract.

In ZH

通过蒸馏获得的纯化液体或物质，尤指浓缩提取物。

How to Use Distillate in a Sentence

  • The distillate from the process was clear and free of impurities.
  • He used the distillate to create a more potent solution.
  • After distillation, the distillate was stored in glass containers.
  • The essential oils are often the main distillate in perfumery.
  • In chemistry, distillates are frequently analyzed for quality control.
