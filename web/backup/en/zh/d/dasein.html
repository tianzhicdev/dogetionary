Dasein

Pronunciation:/ˈdɑːzeɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 存在
  • 在世
  • 此在

Definitions

noun

Definition

A concept in existential philosophy, primarily associated with Martin Heidegger, referring to the experience of being that is unique to human beings.

In ZH

存在主义哲学中的一个概念，主要与马丁·海德格尔关联，指的是独特于人类的存在体验。

Examples

  • Dasein is a central concept in Heidegger's philosophy.
  • According to Heidegger, understanding Dasein is crucial for grasping the nature of existence.
  • The study of Dasein involves exploring the relationship between being and time.
  • In existentialist thought, Dasein represents the individual's experience of their own existence.
  • Heidegger argues that Dasein is fundamentally characterized by its ability to reflect on itself.

Cultural Notes

In philosophy, Dasein is often discussed in relation to concepts such as 'Being-toward-death' and the significance of authenticity.
