Decades

EN→ZH

What Does Decades Mean?

  • 十年
  • 十年期

Decades Pronunciation

ˈdɛkeɪdz

Decades Definition

noun

Definition

A period of ten years, often referred to in terms of a particular time frame (e.g., the 1990s).

In ZH

一个十年的时期，通常是指特定时间段（例如，1990年代）。

How to Use Decades in a Sentence

  • If I had a dollar for every time a decade went by without me learning how to cook, I'd be a millionaire by now!
  • During the last decade, scientists discovered that 90s fashion is making a comeback—who knew we’d be sporting flannel shirts again?
  • I thought my New Year's resolution would take a decade to achieve, but surprise—I'm still on the couch!

Cultural Notes

Decades are often referenced in terms of cultural trends, music styles, and significant historical events. For example, people may say they love the '80s for its music and fashion, which is a way to connect with others who share similar interests.

verb

Definition

The act of marking or celebrating the passage of ten years since a significant event.

In ZH

标记或庆祝自重大事件过去十年的行为。

How to Use Decades in a Sentence

  • Every decade, my family holds a reunion, which is just an excuse for my uncle to bring out his terrible karaoke skills!
  • Our city is celebrating its centennial with a decade of festivals—if only the weather cooperated!
  • I thought a decade party would be great, but everyone just ended up reminiscing about their youth—talk about a time warp!

Cultural Notes

Celebrating decades often involves looking back at achievements and changes over time, typically accompanied by parties or events that reflect on the past.

Synonyms for Decades
