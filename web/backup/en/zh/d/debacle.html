Debacle

EN→ZH

What Does Debacle Mean?

  • 灾难
  • 惨败
  • 崩溃

Debacle Pronunciation

/dəˈbɑː.kəl/

Debacle Definition

noun

Definition

A sudden and ignominious failure; a fiasco.

In ZH

突然且耻辱的失败；一场惨败。

How to Use Debacle in a Sentence

  • The event was a complete debacle from start to finish.
  • Their plans ended in debacle when the CEO resigned unexpectedly.
  • The company's financial debacle was felt across the entire industry.
  • What started as a promising project turned into a debacle.
  • The political debate turned into a debacle, with both candidates losing credibility.

Synonyms for Debacle

  • Debauch
  • Debauchery
  • Debilitate
  • Debilitating
  • Debonair
