Dastardly

Pronunciation:/ˈdæstərdli/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 卑鄙的
  • 胆小的
  • 恶劣的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

wickedly cowardly; treacherous

In ZH

卑鄙的; 胆小的; 背信弃义的

Examples

  • His dastardly acts shocked everyone in the community.
  • The villain made a dastardly escape from the scene of the crime.
  • They planned a dastardly betrayal that would alter the course of history.
  • She described his dastardly behavior as completely unacceptable.
  • The dastardly scheme was uncovered by the police before it could be executed.
