Dendritic

EN→ZH

What Does Dendritic Mean?

  • 树枝状的
  • 树突的

Dendritic Pronunciation

/dɛnˈdritɪk/

Dendritic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or resembling a dendrite, which is a branching structure.

In ZH

与树突有关或类似的一种分支结构。

How to Use Dendritic in a Sentence

  • The neuron has many dendritic branches that receive signals from other cells.
  • In the study of crystals, dendritic patterns can be observed under certain conditions.
  • The dendritic architecture of the immune system is crucial for the response to pathogens.
  • Researchers found that dendritic cells play a vital role in initiating the immune response.
  • Dendritic growth is a significant feature in the context of various biological processes.
