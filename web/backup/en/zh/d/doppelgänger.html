Doppelgänger

EN→ZH

What Does Doppelgänger Mean?

  • 替身
  • 影分身
  • 双胞胎
  • 幽灵
  • 魔鬼

Doppelgänger Pronunciation

/ˈdɒpəlˌɡɛŋər/

Doppelgänger Definition

noun

Definition

A non-biologically related look-alike or double of a living person.

In ZH

一个与某人看起来非常相似但不是生物学上相关的替身或双胞胎。

How to Use Doppelgänger in a Sentence

  • I met my doppelgänger at a coffee shop yesterday.
  • Many people believe that everyone has a doppelgänger somewhere in the world.
  • In folklore, encountering your doppelgänger is considered an omen of death.
  • The film's plot revolves around the protagonist meeting her doppelgänger.
  • He often jokes that he has a doppelgänger living in another country.
