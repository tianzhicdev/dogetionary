Dandy

Pronunciation:/ˈdændi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 花哨的人
  • 极好的
  • 男士服饰

Definitions

noun

Definition

A man who is excessively concerned with his clothes and appearance; a fop.

In ZH

一个过于关注自己衣着和外貌的男子；花花公子。

Examples

  • He dresses like a dandy from the 19th century.
  • The party was full of dandies showing off their fashion.
  • In his youth, he was known as a dandy in the city.
  • Dandies often pay a lot of attention to their grooming.
  • Many artists of the time were considered dandies.
adjective

Definition

Excellent; splendid.

In ZH

极好的；辉煌的。

Examples

  • That was a dandy performance by the musician.
  • The weather is just dandy for a picnic today.
  • She gave a dandy speech at the conference.
  • He has a dandy sense of humor.
  • Everything turned out dandy after all.
