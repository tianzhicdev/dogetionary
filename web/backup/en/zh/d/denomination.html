Denomination

Pronunciation:dɪˌnɑːmɪˈneɪʃən
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 面额
  • 宗派
  • 名称

Definitions

noun

Definition

A category or class of something, especially a group of religious congregations unified under a common name, doctrine, and organization.

In ZH

一种类别或类，尤其是指一群宗教教堂，根据共同的名称、教义和组织而统一的。

Examples

  • The Baptist denomination is one of the largest in the United States.
  • Many people find comfort in their denomination's teachings.
  • She decided to join a denomination that supported her views on social justice.

Cultural Notes

In the context of religion, denominations often reflect differing beliefs and practices among groups within the same faith.

noun

Definition

The value or size of a specific unit of money, often used in reference to different values of currency notes or coins.

In ZH

特定货币单位的价值或大小，通常用于参考不同面值的纸币或硬币。

Examples

  • I need a five-dollar bill in a smaller denomination for the vending machine.
  • Banks often have a variety of denominations available for exchange.
  • When traveling, it's wise to carry small denominations of local currency.

Cultural Notes

Understanding the denominations of currency is key in financial transactions, especially in international travel.
