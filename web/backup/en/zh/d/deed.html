Deed

EN→ZH

What Does Deed Mean?

  • 行为
  • 契约
  • 事迹

Deed Pronunciation

diːd

Deed Definition

noun

Definition

An action that is performed intentionally or deliberately.

In ZH

一个故意或有意执行的行为。

How to Use Deed in a Sentence

  • He performed a good deed by helping the elderly neighbor.
  • The police officer described her heroic deeds during the rescue.
  • Every deed has its consequences, whether good or bad.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, performing good deeds is often associated with moral values and is encouraged in community interactions.

  • He signed the deed to transfer ownership of the house.
  • The lawyer reviewed the deed before the closing of the sale.
  • A deed must be recorded at the county office for it to be effective.

In real estate practices in English-speaking countries, deeds are crucial as they legally document property transactions.
