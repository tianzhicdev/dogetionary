Descry

Pronunciation:/dɪˈskraɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 看见
  • 发现
  • 识别
  • 察觉

Definitions

verb

Definition

to catch sight of or to discover something that is difficult to see or is far away

In ZH

看见或发现难以看见或距离较远的事物

Examples

  • From the top of the hill, I could descry the village below.
  • He managed to descry a small boat in the distance.
  • As we walked through the fog, we could descry the outline of the lighthouse.
  • The sailor's keen eyes allowed him to descry the approaching storm.
  • With great effort, she was able to descry the hidden path in the thick woods.
