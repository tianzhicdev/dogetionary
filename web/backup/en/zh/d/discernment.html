Discernment

EN→ZH

What Does Discernment Mean?

  • 识别能力
  • 洞察力
  • 判断力

Discernment Pronunciation

/dɪsˈɜrn.mənt/

Discernment Definition

noun

Definition

The ability to judge well; insight.

In ZH

良好判断的能力；洞察力。

How to Use Discernment in a Sentence

  • Her discernment in choosing the right candidate impressed everyone.
  • He showed great discernment in his assessment of the situation.
  • With discernment, you can tell the difference between truth and lies.
  • The artist's discernment of colors created stunning visuals.
  • Discernment is key to making wise decisions.
