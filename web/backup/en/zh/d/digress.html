Digress Pronunciation

/daɪˈɡrɛs/

Digress Definition

verb

Definition

to deviate from the main subject in speaking or writing

In ZH

在说话或写作中偏离主要主题

How to Use Digress in a Sentence

  • I tend to digress when I get excited about a topic.
  • During the lecture, the professor often digressed into personal anecdotes.
  • She started to digress from the main theme of her essay.
  • While explaining the project, he digressed to talk about his vacation.
  • It's easy to digress in a long conversation.
