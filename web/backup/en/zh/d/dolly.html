Dolly

EN→ZH

What Does Dolly Mean?

  • 多莉
  • 玩具车
  • 小女孩

Dolly Pronunciation

/ˈdɒli/

Dolly Definition

noun

Definition

A small wheeled cart or platform used for moving heavy objects, often made of wood or metal.

In ZH

一种用于搬运重物的小型有轮手推车，通常由木材或金属制成。

How to Use Dolly in a Sentence

  • He used a dolly to transport the boxes to the truck.
  • The construction workers employed a dolly to move the heavy equipment around the site.
  • We rented a dolly to help with our move.
  • The dolly was essential for moving the furniture without damaging it.
  • She loaded the dolly with several bags of cement for the project.
noun

Definition

A child's toy in the form of a small doll.

In ZH

一种儿童玩具，呈现为小型娃娃。

How to Use Dolly in a Sentence

  • The little girl played with her favorite dolly all day.
  • For her birthday, she received a beautiful new dolly.
  • The dollhouse came with a matching dolly.
  • He dressed his dolly in colorful clothes.
  • She took her dolly on all of their adventures.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, dolls are considered important for children's play and learning.

verb

Definition

To move or push (something) using a dolly.

In ZH

使用手推车移动或推动（某物）。

How to Use Dolly in a Sentence

  • They dolly the heavy crates to the loading dock.
  • Please dolly the equipment over to the other room.
  • He dollyed the supplies to make the process easier.
  • We need to dolly the furniture before the carpets are laid.
  • She dollyed the bags across the parking lot.
