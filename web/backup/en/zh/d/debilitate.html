Debilitate

EN→ZH

What Does Debilitate Mean?

  • 削弱
  • 使虚弱

Debilitate Pronunciation

/dɪˈbɪlɪˌteɪt/

Debilitate Definition

verb

Definition

to make (someone or something) weak; to impair strength or effectiveness.

In ZH

使（某人或某物）虚弱；削弱力量或效能。

How to Use Debilitate in a Sentence

  • The illness can debilitate even the strongest individuals.
  • In extreme cases, the disease may debilitate the patient's immune system.
  • The constant stress can debilitate one's mental health.
  • Lack of proper nutrition can debilitate children's growth and development.
  • The economic downturn may debilitate many small businesses.

Synonyms for Debilitate

  • Debacle
  • Debauch
  • Debauchery
  • Debilitating
  • Debonair
