Diaphanous

Pronunciation:/daɪˈæfənəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 半透明的
  • 透明的
  • 模糊的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Light, delicate, and translucent; allowing light to pass through but not detailed shapes.

In ZH

轻巧、精致、透明；允许光线透过但不显示细节形状。

Examples

  • The diaphanous fabric billowed in the breeze.
  • She wore a diaphanous gown that shimmered under the lights.
  • The artist used diaphanous layers of paint to create depth.
  • In the early morning, the diaphanous mist hung over the lake.
  • The curtains were made of a diaphanous material that let in soft sunlight.
