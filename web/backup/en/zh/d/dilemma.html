Dilemma

Pronunciation:/dɪˈlɛmə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 困境
  • 两难
  • 难题

Definitions

noun

Definition

a situation in which a difficult choice has to be made between two or more alternatives, especially equally undesirable ones.

In ZH

一种情况，在这种情况下必须在两个或多个选择之间做出困难的选择，尤其是同样不令人愉快的选择。

Examples

  • I faced a dilemma when choosing between two job offers.
  • The ethical dilemma made her question her morals.
  • He was caught in a dilemma over whether to tell the truth or protect his friend.
  • Toying with a dilemma, she couldn't decide on which college to attend.
  • The committee is in a dilemma over funding the new project.
