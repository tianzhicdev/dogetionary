Deracinated

Pronunciation:/ˌdɛrəˈsɪneɪtɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 根除的
  • 移植的
  • 离土的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having been ripped from one's roots or origins; displaced or uprooted.

In ZH

脱离根源或起源的；被驱逐或连根拔起的。

Examples

  • The deracinated communities struggled to maintain their cultural identity.
  • Many deracinated refugees have lost touch with their homeland.
  • Deracinated individuals often feel a profound sense of alienation.
  • The novel explores the lives of deracinated characters in a foreign land.
  • In a globalized world, people become increasingly deracinated.
