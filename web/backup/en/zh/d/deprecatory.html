Deprecatory

EN→ZH

What Does Deprecatory Mean?

  • 贬低的
  • 不赞同的
  • 轻视的

Deprecatory Pronunciation

/ˈdɛprɪˌkeɪtəri/

Deprecatory Definition

adjective

Definition

Expressing disapproval; disapproving or belittling.

In ZH

表示不赞成；持否定态度或轻视。

How to Use Deprecatory in a Sentence

  • His deprecatory remarks about the project discouraged others.
  • She gave a deprecatory smile when he suggested the team could finish early.
  • The manager's deprecatory attitude towards the interns was surprising.
  • He made a deprecatory comment about the movie's plot.
  • Her deprecatory tone was evident when discussing her rival's achievements.

Synonyms for Deprecatory

  • Deplete
  • Depravity
  • Deprecate
  • Deprecation
