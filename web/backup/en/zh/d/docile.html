Docile

Pronunciation:/ˈdɒs.aɪl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 温顺的
  • 听话的
  • 驯服的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

ready to accept control or instruction; submissive.

In ZH

愿意接受控制或指导；顺从的。

Examples

  • The dog was surprisingly docile, following every command.
  • In class, the students were docile and attentive.
  • Although he was known to be aggressive, he was actually quite docile at home.
  • She preferred docile pets over those that were more energetic.
  • The docile nature of the horse made it a favorite among beginners.
