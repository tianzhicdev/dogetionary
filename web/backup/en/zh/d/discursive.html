Discursive

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈkɜːrsɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 离题的
  • 散漫的
  • 论述的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by a disorganized or rambling style of discourse; tending to digress from the main subject.

In ZH

特征为一种无组织或冗长的论述风格；倾向于偏离主题。

Examples

  • The professor's discursive lectures often left students confused.
  • She had a discursive style that sometimes made her points hard to follow.
  • His discursive writing reflects a broad range of interests.
  • The debate became discursive, with participants straying from the topic.
  • The author's discursive approach allows for multiple interpretations.
