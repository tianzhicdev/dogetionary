Dilettante

Pronunciation:/ˌdɪl.əˈtɑːn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 业余爱好者
  • 文艺爱好者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who cultivates an area of interest, such as the arts, without real commitment or knowledge.

In ZH

一个人对某个兴趣领域，如艺术，进行培养，但没有真正的承诺或知识。

Examples

  • Many consider him a dilettante in the art world.
  • She dabbled in painting but was just a dilettante.
  • The exhibition was filled with works by dilettantes and professionals.
  • He was labeled a dilettante because he jumps from one hobby to another.
  • Dilettantes often lack the depth of knowledge found in experts.
