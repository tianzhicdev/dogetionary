Disseminate

Pronunciation:/dɪˈsɛmɪˌneɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 传播
  • 散布
  • Disseminate

Definitions

verb

Definition

to spread or disperse something, especially information or knowledge, widely.

In ZH

传播信息或知识，尤其是广泛地散布或传播某事物。

Examples

  • The organization aims to disseminate educational materials to schools.
  • Scientists need to disseminate their findings to the public.
  • Social media can be a powerful tool to disseminate information quickly.
  • The government has launched a campaign to disseminate health advice.
  • We should disseminate our research through academic journals.
