Devote

EN→ZH

What Does Devote Mean?

  • 奉献
  • 投入
  • 致力于
  • 专心于

Devote Pronunciation

/dɪˈvoʊt/

Devote Definition

verb

Definition

To give a large part of one's time or resources to a particular activity, person, or cause.

In ZH

将大量时间或资源投入到特定活动、人物或事业中。

How to Use Devote in a Sentence

  • She decided to devote her life to helping others.
  • He devotes hours each week to volunteer work.
  • They devoted their attention to the project to ensure its success.
  • The author devoted several years to researching the topic.
  • He has devoted a significant amount of time to his studies.

Synonyms for Devote

  • Develop
  • Development
  • Deviate
  • Devoted
