Defunct

EN→ZH

What Does Defunct Mean?

  • 失效的
  • 不存在的
  • 无效的
  • 已停止使用的

Defunct Pronunciation

/dɪˈfʌŋkt/

Defunct Definition

adjective

Definition

no longer existing, operating, or functioning

In ZH

不再存在、运行或发挥作用的

How to Use Defunct in a Sentence

  • The factory has been defunct since it closed down in 2010.
  • Many species of animals have become defunct due to climate change.
  • The app is now defunct and no longer available for download.
  • The organization was declared defunct after failing to meet its goals.
  • He found the defunct company listed in the old directory.
