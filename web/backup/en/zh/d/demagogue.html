Demagogue

EN→ZH

What Does Demagogue Mean?

  • 煽动者
  • 政治骗子
  • 民粹主义者

Demagogue Pronunciation

/ˈdɛm.ə.ɡɒɡ/

Demagogue Definition

noun

Definition

A political leader who seeks support by appealing to popular desires and prejudices rather than by using rational argument.

In ZH

一个通过迎合民众的欲望和偏见，而不是通过理性论点来寻求支持的政治领导者。

How to Use Demagogue in a Sentence

  • The demagogue rallied the crowd with promises of change.
  • During the election, several demagogues emerged, manipulating the fears of citizens.
  • His demagogue tactics were evident as he blamed others for the country's problems.
  • Many people were drawn to the demagogue's fiery speeches.
  • Critics argued that the demagogue was spreading misinformation to gain followers.

  • Demarcation
  • Demesne
  • Demolished
