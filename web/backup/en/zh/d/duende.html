Duende

Pronunciation:/dwenˈdeɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 妖灵
  • 精灵
  • 鬼魅

Definitions

noun

Definition

A heightened state of emotion, expression, and authenticity, often associated with art, particularly in Spanish culture.

In ZH

一种高度的情感、表达和真实性状态，常与艺术相关，尤其是在西班牙文化中。

Examples

  • The singer performed with a sense of duende that captivated the audience.
  • To experience duende is to feel a deep connection with the art.
  • Many flamenco dancers strive to invoke duende during their performances.
  • The artist's latest work was filled with duende, moving viewers to tears.
  • In the world of poetry, duende is often sought after for its profound impact.

Cultural Notes

In Spanish folklore, duende is often described as a spirit that inspires creativity and passionate expression.
