Dysmenorrhea

EN→ZH

What Does Dysmenorrhea Mean?

  • 痛经
  • 月经痛

Dysmenorrhea Pronunciation

dɪsˌmɛnəˈriːə

Dysmenorrhea Definition

noun

Definition

Painful menstruation that can include cramps in the abdomen and lower back.

In ZH

痛经是指月经期间可能伴随腹部和下背部疼痛的情况。

How to Use Dysmenorrhea in a Sentence

  • I once joked that if I could harness the power of my dysmenorrhea during finals, I would have enough energy to power the entire university!
  • Breaking news: Scientists have discovered that the cure for dysmenorrhea is actually a good Netflix binge-watching session. Who needs medicine when you have a cozy blanket?
  • They say laughter is the best medicine, but during dysmenorrhea, I'm starting to think chocolate is a close second!

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, dysmenorrhea is often viewed as a taboo topic, making it hard for people to discuss their symptoms or seek help. In some places, traditional remedies are preferred over medical treatments.
