Distillation

Pronunciation:/ˌdɪstəˈleɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 蒸馏
  • 提炼
  • 蒸馏法

Definitions

noun

Definition

The process of separating the components of a liquid mixture by selective boiling and condensation.

In ZH

通过选择性蒸发和冷凝分离液体混合物成分的过程。

Examples

  • Distillation is commonly used to purify water.
  • The distillation process removes impurities from the liquid.
  • She learned how to perform distillation in her chemistry class.
  • The company specializes in the distillation of essential oils.
  • After distillation, the alcohol content increased significantly.
