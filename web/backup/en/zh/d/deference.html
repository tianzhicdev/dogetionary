Deference

Pronunciation:/ˈdɛfərəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 尊重
  • 敬意
  • 顺从

Definitions

noun

Definition

Respect and esteem due to the opinions or judgments of others.

In ZH

对他人意见或判断的尊重和敬意。

Examples

  • He showed great deference to his elders in the community.
  • In deference to her wishes, we decided to take a different route.
  • The students listened with deference to the professor's advice.
  • They acted in deference to the local customs during their visit.
  • In deference to her privacy, we chose not to discuss the matter further.
