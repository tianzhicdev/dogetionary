Deliquescent

Pronunciation:/ˌdɛlɪˈkwɛsənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 溶解的
  • 吸湿性溶解的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

becoming liquid, especially through the absorption of moisture from the air.

In ZH

通过从空气中吸收水分而变成液体的，尤其指物质。

Examples

  • The deliquescent salt gradually dissolved in the humid air.
  • In a deliquescent state, the substance becomes liquid.
  • Deliquescent compounds are often used in humidifying processes.
  • The scientists observed the deliquescent crystals melting on the lab bench.
  • When left exposed to the environment, the deliquescent material transformed into a solution.
