Discuss The Technologies Used In Communication

Pronunciation:dɪsˈkʌs ðə tɛkˈnɑlədʒiz juːzd ɪn kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 讨论通信中使用的技术
  • 讨论沟通中使用的技术
  • 讨论交流中使用的技术

Definitions

verb

Definition

To talk about something with someone in order to exchange ideas or information.

In ZH

与某人讨论某事，以便交换想法或信息。

Examples

  • Let's discuss the new communication technologies in our meeting.
  • We need to discuss the technologies used in communication for our project.
  • Can we discuss the best tools for online communication?

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, discussing topics openly is often encouraged and seen as a way to foster collaboration and innovation.

noun

Definition

The act of talking about something, particularly to reach a conclusion or share understanding.

In ZH

讨论某事的行为，特别是为了达成结论或分享理解。

Examples

  • The discussion on communication technologies was very enlightening.
  • We had a fruitful discussion about the new apps used for communication.
  • Discussion is key in any team project.

Cultural Notes

Group discussions are common in educational settings in English-speaking countries, promoting critical thinking and teamwork.

noun

Definition

Technologies that facilitate communication, such as the Internet, smartphones, and messaging apps.

In ZH

促进交流的技术，如互联网、智能手机和消息应用程序。

Examples

  • The technologies used in communication have evolved significantly over the past decade.
  • I read an article about the latest communication technologies.
  • Understanding the technologies used in communication is essential in today's digital age.

Cultural Notes

Awareness of current communication technologies is crucial in many fields, including business, education, and social interactions.
