Dystopian

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈtoʊpiən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 反乌托邦的
  • 乌托邦式的
  • 反乌托邦

Definitions

adjective

Definition

relating to or denoting an imagined state or society where there is great suffering or injustice, typically one that is totalitarian or post-apocalyptic

In ZH

与想象中的国家或社会相关或表示，通常是极大的痛苦或不公正的地方，通常是极权主义或后启示录的。

Examples

  • The novel portrays a dystopian future ravaged by war.
  • Many films explore dystopian themes where survival is a constant struggle.
  • Dystopian literature often reflects society's fears and critiques of current events.
  • In a dystopian world, individual freedoms are often suppressed.
  • The story is set in a dystopian society governed by strict rules.
