Discriminate

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈkrɪm.ɪ.neɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 歧视
  • 辨别
  • 区分

Definitions

verb

Definition

to recognize a distinction; differentiate

In ZH

辨别；区分

Examples

  • It is important to discriminate between fact and opinion.
  • She can discriminate various shades of color with ease.
  • The law prohibits companies from discriminating based on gender.
  • He was trained to discriminate the subtle differences in flavors.
  • Animals can discriminate between different sounds.
