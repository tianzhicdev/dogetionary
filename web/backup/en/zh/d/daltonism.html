Daltonism

EN→ZH

What Does Daltonism Mean?

  • 色盲
  • 达尔顿症

Daltonism Pronunciation

/ˈdɔːltəˌnɪzəm/

Daltonism Definition

noun

Definition

A condition characterized by the inability to distinguish certain colors, especially red and green.

In ZH

一种以无法区分特定颜色（尤其是红色和绿色）为特征的疾病。

How to Use Daltonism in a Sentence

  • Many people with daltonism have trouble telling red from green.
  • Daltonism can affect daily activities such as choosing clothing.
  • Artists may need to consider daltonism when creating color palettes.
  • Educational programs can help those with daltonism learn color recognition techniques.
  • Daltonism is often inherited and can vary in severity.
