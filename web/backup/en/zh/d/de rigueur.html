De Rigueur

EN→ZH

What Does De Rigueur Mean?

  • 必需的
  • 必然的
  • 流行的
  • 规范的

De Rigueur Pronunciation

/də rɪˈɡɜːr/

De Rigueur Definition

adjective

Definition

required by etiquette or current fashion; necessary or obligatory

In ZH

根据礼仪或当前时尚所要求的；必要的或强制性的

How to Use De Rigueur in a Sentence

  • Wearing a tuxedo to the gala was considered de rigueur.
  • In this industry, knowing how to network is de rigueur.
  • It's de rigueur to arrive on time at formal events.
  • A good education is de rigueur for success in this field.
  • For a successful presentation, preparation is de rigueur.

Cultural Notes

The phrase 'de rigueur' is often used in the context of fashion, etiquette, and social conventions in Western culture.
