Deus Ex Machina

EN→ZH

What Does Deus Ex Machina Mean?

  • 神明降临
  • 机械神
  • 意外的解决办法

Deus Ex Machina Pronunciation

/ˌdeɪ.əs ɛks ˈmɑː.kɪ.nə/

Deus Ex Machina Definition

noun

Definition

A plot device whereby a seemingly unsolvable problem is suddenly and abruptly resolved by an unexpected and unlikely occurrence.

In ZH

一种情节装置，通过一个看似无法解决的问题，突然而迅速地通过一个意外和不太可能的事件得到解决。

How to Use Deus Ex Machina in a Sentence

  • The story ended with a deus ex machina that left the audience bewildered.
  • In the final act, the hero escapes danger thanks to a deus ex machina.
  • The movie was criticized for its reliance on a deus ex machina to resolve the conflict.
  • The author used a deus ex machina to ensure a happy ending for all the characters.
  • Many classic plays feature a deus ex machina to tie up loose ends.

Cultural Notes

The term originates from ancient Greek drama, where a god was lowered onto the stage to resolve complex plot issues.
