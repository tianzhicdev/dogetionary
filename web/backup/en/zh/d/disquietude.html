Disquietude

Pronunciation:/dɪsˈkwaɪ.ɪ.tjuːd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不安
  • 焦虑
  • 忧虑

Definitions

noun

Definition

a state of uneasiness or anxiety; a feeling of disquiet

In ZH

不安或焦虑的状态；一种不安的感觉

Examples

  • The disquietude in the room was palpable as the deadline approached.
  • She felt a sense of disquietude when she heard the news.
  • His disquietude about the exam was evident in his restless behavior.
  • The disquietude among the staff caused a decline in productivity.
  • In times of uncertainty, disquietude can lead to poor decision-making.
