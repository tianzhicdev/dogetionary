Dehiscence

EN→ZH

What Does Dehiscence Mean?

  • 裂开
  • 分裂
  • 开裂

Dehiscence Pronunciation

/dɪˈhɪsəns/

Dehiscence Definition

noun

Definition

The splitting or bursting open of a part of a plant, such as a seed pod, to release seeds.

In ZH

植物某部分的劈裂或破裂，例如种子荚，以释放种子。

How to Use Dehiscence in a Sentence

  • The dehiscence of the seed pod allows the seeds to disperse.
  • During dehiscence, the fruit splits open to reveal the seeds inside.
  • Certain plants exhibit rapid dehiscence to ensure seed dispersal at the right time.
  • Dehiscence is an important process for the reproductive success of many flowering plants.
  • Farmers may observe dehiscence in their crops as they reach maturity.
