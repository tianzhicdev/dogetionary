Détente

EN→ZH

What Does Détente Mean?

  • 缓和
  • 放松

Détente Pronunciation

/deɪˈtɑːnt/

Détente Definition

noun

Definition

the easing of hostility or strained relations, especially between countries.

In ZH

减轻敌意或紧张关系，尤其是指国家之间的关系。

How to Use Détente in a Sentence

  • The two countries entered a period of détente after years of conflict.
  • Détente allowed for more diplomatic communication between the opposing nations.
  • During the Cold War, détente aimed to reduce nuclear tensions between the superpowers.
  • The détente policy helped foster trade agreements.
  • Many historians debate the effectiveness of détente in achieving lasting peace.

Cultural Notes

The term 'détente' is often associated with the Cold War era, particularly in relation to U.S.-Soviet relations.
