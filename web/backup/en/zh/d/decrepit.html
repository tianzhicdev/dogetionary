Decrepit

EN→ZH

What Does Decrepit Mean?

  • 破旧的
  • 衰老的
  • 年久失修的

Decrepit Pronunciation

/dɪˈkrɛpɪt/

Decrepit Definition

adjective

Definition

worn out or ruined because of age or neglect

In ZH

因为年龄或忽视而磨损或毁坏的

How to Use Decrepit in a Sentence

  • The old man lived in a decrepit house.
  • His decrepit car broke down on the highway.
  • She walked with a decrepit cane.
  • The building was deemed hazardous due to its decrepit state.
  • They had to replace the decrepit furniture in the library.

Synonyms for Decrepit

  • Decadent
  • Decades
  • Decamp
  • Decant
  • Decay
