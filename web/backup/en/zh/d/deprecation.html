Deprecation

EN→ZH

What Does Deprecation Mean?

  • 贬值
  • 反对
  • 谴责

Deprecation Pronunciation

/ˌdɛprɪˈkeɪʃən/

Deprecation Definition

noun

Definition

The action of expressing disapproval or a lowering in value.

In ZH

表示不赞成的行为或价值的降低。

How to Use Deprecation in a Sentence

  • There was a sense of deprecation in her tone when she spoke about the old methods.
  • The deprecation of the software led many users to seek alternatives.
  • His comments were filled with deprecation regarding modern art.
  • The company announced the deprecation of the old API in favor of a new version.
  • Many developers felt deprecation was necessary to improve the overall quality of code.

Synonyms for Deprecation

  • Deplete
  • Depravity
  • Deprecate
  • Deprecatory
