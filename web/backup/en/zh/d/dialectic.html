Dialectic

Pronunciation:/daɪəˈlɛktɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 辩证法
  • 辩证论

Definitions

noun

Definition

The art of investigating or discussing the truth of opinions; a method of argument for resolving disagreement.

In ZH

研究或讨论意见真理的艺术；一种解决分歧的论证方法。

Examples

  • In philosophy, dialectic plays a crucial role in reasoning.
  • The dialectic method was used by Socrates in his teachings.
  • Many political theories have evolved through dialectic discussions.
  • Dialectic is essential in debate for exposing contradictions.
  • The process of dialectic involves thesis, antithesis, and synthesis.
