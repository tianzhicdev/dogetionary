Develop

Pronunciation:/dɪˈvɛl.əp/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 发展
  • 开发
  • 形成

Definitions

verb

Definition

to grow or cause to grow and become more mature, advanced, or elaborate

In ZH

使成长或导致成长，变得更加成熟、先进或复杂

Examples

  • She wants to develop her skills in photography.
  • The city plans to develop the waterfront area.
  • He is working hard to develop a new software program.
  • They aim to develop a better understanding of the issues at hand.
  • The team needs to develop a strategy for the upcoming competition.
