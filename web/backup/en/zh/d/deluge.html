Deluge

Pronunciation:/ˈdɛljuːdʒ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 洪水
  • 大洪水
  • 暴雨

Definitions

noun

Definition

a severe flood or an overwhelming amount of something

In ZH

严重的洪水或大量的东西

Examples

  • The deluge caused widespread damage to homes and infrastructure.
  • After the deluge, the town was unrecognizable under the floodwaters.
  • The report outlined a deluge of complaints from unhappy customers.
  • He received a deluge of emails after the announcement was made.
  • The sudden deluge overwhelmed the storm drainage system.
verb

Definition

to flood or overwhelm with a great quantity

In ZH

用大量东西淹没或压倒

Examples

  • The city was deluged with rain last night.
  • She was deluged with work after her colleague quit.
  • The website was deluged by visitors following the viral video.
  • They deluged their neighbors with invitations to the party.
  • The school was deluged with applications this year.
