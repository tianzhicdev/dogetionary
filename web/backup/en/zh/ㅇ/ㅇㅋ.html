ㅇㅋ

Pronunciation:o-kei
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 好的
  • 可以
  • 行

Definitions

interjection

Definition

An informal way of expressing agreement or acknowledgment, similar to saying 'okay' in English.

In ZH

一种非正式的表达同意或认可的方式，类似于英语中的'okay'。

Examples

  • I'll meet you at 5? ㅇㅋ!
  • Do you want to go to the movies tonight? ㅇㅋ!
  • Can you help me with this task? ㅇㅋ, no problem.

Cultural Notes

Commonly used in casual conversations, especially in texting or online chat, 'ㅇㅋ' reflects a relaxed form of communication among friends and peers.

abbreviation

Definition

A shortened form of the word 'okay' used primarily in electronic communication.

In ZH

在电子通讯中主要使用的'okay'的缩写形式。

Examples

  • Just send me the details when you can, ㅇㅋ?
  • If you're tired, we can reschedule, ㅇㅋ?
  • I'm ready to start whenever you are, ㅇㅋ.

Cultural Notes

'ㅇㅋ' is particularly prevalent in South Korean texting culture, where brevity is valued in fast-paced communications, similar to the usage of acronyms in English.
