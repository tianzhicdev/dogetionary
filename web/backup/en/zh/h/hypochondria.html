Hypochondria

EN→ZH

What Does Hypochondria Mean?

  • 疑病症
  • 忧郁病
  • 疑病

Hypochondria Pronunciation

/ˌhaɪ.pəˈkɒn.dri.ə/

Hypochondria Definition

noun

Definition

an excessive preoccupation with one's health, often leading to misinterpretation of normal bodily functions as signs of serious illness.

In ZH

对自身健康的过度关注，常常导致对正常身体功能的误解，认为是严重疾病的迹象。

How to Use Hypochondria in a Sentence

  • She was diagnosed with hypochondria after constantly worrying about her health.
  • His hypochondria made it difficult for him to enjoy social gatherings without fearing illness.
  • Hypochondria can lead to unnecessary medical tests and treatments.
  • Many patients with hypochondria genuinely believe they are sick despite medical reassurance.
  • Cognitive-behavioral therapy may help those suffering from hypochondria.

Synonyms for Hypochondria

  • Hyperbaton
  • Hyperbole
  • Hyperbolic
  • Hyperborean
  • Hyphae
