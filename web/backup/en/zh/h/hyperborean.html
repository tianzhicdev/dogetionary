Hyperborean

EN→ZH

What Does Hyperborean Mean?

  • 北极的
  • 超北极的

Hyperborean Pronunciation

/haɪˈpɜːrbɔːriən/

Hyperborean Definition

noun

Definition

A mythical person from the far northern region of the world, often associated with extreme cold and darkness.

In ZH

来自世界极北地区的神话人物，通常与极端寒冷和黑暗相关联。

How to Use Hyperborean in a Sentence

  • In ancient lore, the hyperboreans were said to live in eternal sunshine.
  • The story of the hyperborean people fascinated many explorers of the Arctic.
  • Some believe that the hyperborean myth reflects ancient peoples' fascination with the unknown north.
  • The hyperborean lands were described as places of peace and plenty.
  • Legends of hyperboreans can be found in various mythologies, often linked to the Greeks.
adjective

Definition

Relating to or characteristic of the extremely cold regions in the far north.

In ZH

与远北极端寒冷地区相关或特征的。

How to Use Hyperborean in a Sentence

  • The hyperborean winds swept through the valley, chilling everything in their path.
  • Many hyperborean creatures adapt to survive in frigid temperatures.
  • Hyperborean landscapes are often depicted in literature as stark and beautiful.
  • Scientists study hyperborean climates to understand global weather patterns.
  • The term 'hyperborean' is sometimes used in literature to evoke a sense of isolation.
