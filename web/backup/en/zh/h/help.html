Help

Pronunciation:/hɛlp/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 帮助
  • 援助
  • 助手

Definitions

verb

Definition

to provide assistance or support to someone; to make it easier for someone to do something.

In ZH

提供帮助或支持; 使某人更容易做到某事。

Examples

  • Can you help me with my homework?
  • I need someone to help me carry these boxes.
  • She always tries to help her friends when they are in trouble.
  • He called to ask if I could help him fix his car.
  • They are looking for volunteers to help at the shelter.
noun

Definition

the action of helping someone; assistance.

In ZH

帮助某人的行为; 协助。

Examples

  • I could use a little help with this project.
  • Thank you for your help today.
  • The teacher provided help to struggling students.
  • Help is on the way!
  • They offered their help to the community.
