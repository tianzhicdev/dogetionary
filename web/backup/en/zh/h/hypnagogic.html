Hypnagogic

EN→ZH

What Does Hypnagogic Mean?

  • 催眠的
  • 入睡的
  • 入睡时的

Hypnagogic Pronunciation

/hɪpnəˈɡɒdʒɪk/

Hypnagogic Definition

adjective

Definition

Of or relating to the state immediately before falling asleep.

In ZH

与入睡前的状态有关。

How to Use Hypnagogic in a Sentence

  • She experienced hypnagogic hallucinations as she drifted off to sleep.
  • The hypnagogic state can produce vivid imagery and sounds.
  • Many artists have drawn inspiration from their hypnagogic visions.
  • He often felt a sense of weightlessness during his hypnagogic moments.
  • Understanding hypnagogic sensations can help improve sleep quality.

Synonyms for Hypnagogic

  • Hyperbaton
  • Hyperbole
  • Hyperbolic
  • Hyperborean
  • Hyphae
