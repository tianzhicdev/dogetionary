Hiraeth

Pronunciation:/ˈhɪraɪθ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 怀旧
  • 思乡
  • 乡愁

Definitions

noun

Definition

A deep, nostalgic sense of longing for one's home or homeland, often associated with feelings that cannot fully be expressed in words.

In ZH

一种对家或故乡的深刻怀旧感，常常伴随着无法完全用言语表达的情感。

Examples

  • After years living abroad, she felt a strong hiraeth for her childhood home.
  • His hiraeth was evident when he spoke about the landscapes of his youth.
  • The songs they sang brought a sense of hiraeth to the group, reminding them of their past.
  • In moments of silence, he often succumbed to hiraeth, missing the familiar sights of his village.
  • Hiraeth is a feeling that many immigrants experience as they navigate their new lives.

Cultural Notes

Hiraeth is a Welsh word that embodies a complicated mix of nostalgia, yearning, and homesickness, often reflecting a deep emotional connection to one’s culture and homeland.
