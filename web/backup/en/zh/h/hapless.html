Hapless

EN→ZH

What Does Hapless Mean?

  • 倒霉的
  • 不幸的
  • 碰运气的

Hapless Pronunciation

/ˈhæp.ləs/

Hapless Definition

adjective

Definition

unfortunate or unlucky; having bad luck

In ZH

不幸的；运气不佳的

How to Use Hapless in a Sentence

  • The hapless victim was caught in the middle of the storm.
  • She was a hapless traveler, always missing her flights.
  • His hapless attempts to fix the car only made things worse.
  • The hapless children were left without any food during the holiday.
  • Despite his hapless nature, he retained a cheerful disposition.

Synonyms for Hapless
