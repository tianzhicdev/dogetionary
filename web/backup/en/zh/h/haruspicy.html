Haruspicy

Pronunciation:/həˈrʌspɪsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 占卜
  • 通过观察动物内脏来进行的占卜

Definitions

noun

Definition

the practice of divination by inspecting the entrails of sacrificed animals, especially in ancient Rome and Etruscan culture.

In ZH

通过检查被牺牲动物的内脏进行的占卜，尤其是在古罗马和伊特鲁里亚文化中。

Examples

  • Haruspicy was an important ritual in ancient Roman religion.
  • The haruspex examined the liver of the sacrificed animal for omens.
  • Many leaders consulted haruspices before making significant decisions.
  • Haruspicy fell out of favor as Christianity spread through the Roman Empire.
  • Understanding haruspicy provides insights into ancient belief systems.

Cultural Notes

Haruspicy was central to Roman religious practices and was often performed by specialized priests known as haruspices.
