Hermetically

Pronunciation:/hɜrˈmɛtɪkli/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 密封地
  • 完全地
  • 绝对地

Definitions

adverb

Definition

In a way that is airtight; to prevent air from entering or escaping.

In ZH

以气密的方式；阻止空气进出。

Examples

  • The jar must be sealed hermetically to preserve its contents.
  • They packaged the food hermetically to ensure freshness.
  • The laboratory was hermetically sealed to prevent contamination.
  • He closed the envelope hermetically to secure the documents inside.
  • The bunker was designed to be hermetically safe from outside elements.
