What Does Hesperian Mean?

Hesperian Pronunciation

/hɛˈspɛr.i.ən/

Hesperian Definition

adjective

Definition

relating to the western part or regions of the world; often associated with the evening or twilight time

In ZH

与世界的西部或区域相关；通常与傍晚或黄昏时间有关

  • In literature, themes of Hesperian beauty evoke feelings of nostalgia.
