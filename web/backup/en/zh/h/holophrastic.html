Holophrastic

EN→ZH

What Does Holophrastic Mean?

  • 单词句的
  • 单词句法的

Holophrastic Pronunciation

/ˌhoʊloʊˈfræstɪk/

Holophrastic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to a holophrase, which is a single word that conveys a complete idea or sentence.

In ZH

与单词句相关的，单词句是指一个单独的单词表达一个完整的想法或句子。

How to Use Holophrastic in a Sentence

  • The term 'go' can be holophrastic when used to express the action of leaving.
  • In early language development, children often use holophrastic speech.
  • A child's use of 'juice' can be holophrastic if it means 'I want juice.'
  • Holophrastic expressions can convey complex ideas succinctly.
  • Understanding holophrastic language can aid in language acquisition.

Synonyms for Holophrastic

  • Holistic
  • Holluschick
