Hemophiliacs

Pronunciation:hiːməˈfɪliˌæks
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 血友病患者
  • 血友病人

Definitions

noun

Definition

Individuals affected by hemophilia, a genetic disorder that impairs the body's ability to make blood clots, a process needed to stop bleeding.

In ZH

受血友病影响的人，这是一种遗传性疾病，影响身体形成血块的能力，而血块是止血所必需的过程。

Examples

  • Hemophiliacs must always carry a medical alert card to inform others of their condition.
  • Without proper treatment, hemophiliacs can experience severe bleeding after minor injuries.
  • Advancements in medicine have greatly improved the quality of life for hemophiliacs.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, awareness about genetic disorders like hemophilia is growing, leading to better support systems for those affected. It's important in discussions about genetic health.
