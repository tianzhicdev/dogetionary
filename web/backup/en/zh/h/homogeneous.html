Homogeneous

Pronunciation:/həˈmɒdʒənəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 均匀的
  • 同类的
  • 同质的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

of the same or similar kind or nature; uniform in composition throughout.

In ZH

同类或相似的性质；在组成上是均匀的。

Examples

  • The mixture was homogeneous, with all components evenly distributed.
  • In a homogeneous society, cultural differences are minimal.
  • The researcher preferred to use a homogeneous sample for the study.
  • Homogeneous materials are often easier to analyze than heterogeneous ones.
  • Solar energy is homogeneous energy because it is uniform and consistent.
