Helix

Pronunciation:/ˈhiːlɪks/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 螺旋
  • 螺旋体
  • 螺旋形

Definitions

noun

Definition

A three-dimensional geometric shape that is a curve that lies on a cylinder or cone and whose distance from a reference point increases or decreases at a constant rate as it revolves around the reference point.

In ZH

一种三维几何形状，是一种曲线，位于圆柱或圆锥上，其距离参考点以恒定速率增加或减少，随着其围绕参考点旋转。

Examples

  • The staircase was designed in a helix structure.
  • The DNA molecule is shaped like a double helix.
  • The architect used a helix design for the tower.
  • Helixes can be found in nature, such as in snail shells.
  • In mathematics, a helix is often studied in relation to parametric equations.
