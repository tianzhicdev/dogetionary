Hyphae

EN→ZH

What Does Hyphae Mean?

  • 菌丝
  • 菌丝体

Hyphae Pronunciation

/ˈhaɪfeɪ/

Hyphae Definition

noun

Definition

The long, branching filamentous structures of a fungus that contain cytoplasm and are surrounded by a cell wall.

In ZH

真菌的长而分支的丝状结构，带有细胞质并被细胞壁包围。

How to Use Hyphae in a Sentence

  • Hyphae are essential for the growth of most fungi.
  • The hyphae spread out in search of nutrients.
  • Under the microscope, the hyphae appear thread-like.
  • Mycelium is formed by a network of hyphae.
  • Hyphae can break down complex organic materials.
