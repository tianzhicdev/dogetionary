Haphazard

Pronunciation:/hæpˈhæz.ərd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 偶然的
  • 无秩序的
  • 随机的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

lacking any obvious principle of organization; done in a random or chaotic manner.

In ZH

缺乏任何明显的组织原则；以随机或混乱的方式进行的。

Examples

  • The books were piled in a haphazard manner on the shelves.
  • His haphazard approach to the project led to many errors.
  • They made haphazard decisions without any planning.
  • The garden was filled with haphazard arrangements of different plants.
  • She cleaned her room in a haphazard way, missing many spots.
noun

Definition

a state of things that is not arranged in a systematic way; an occurrence in a random manner.

In ZH

事物未以系统方式安排的状态；以随机方式发生的事件。

Examples

  • The haphazard of the layout made it difficult to navigate the space.
  • There was a haphazard of information that confused the students.
  • He was known for his haphazard of ideas during meetings.
  • The kitchen was in a haphazard after the cooking session.
  • Their haphazard led to several misunderstandings.
