Hugbox

Pronunciation:/ˈhʌɡbɒks/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 拥抱盒子
  • 温暖的空间
  • 安全区

Definitions

noun

Definition

A metaphorical or literal safe space where individuals feel emotionally supported and accepted, often used in contexts relating to online communities or social dynamics.

In ZH

一个隐喻或字面上的安全空间，在这个空间中，个人感到情感上的支持和接纳，通常用于与在线社区或社交动态相关的语境。

Examples

  • Online forums often serve as hugboxes for those seeking support.
  • She created a hugbox for her friends to share their feelings.
  • In the hugbox, everyone felt free to express their emotions.
  • This community acts like a hugbox, providing comfort to its members.
  • Hugboxes can sometimes lead to echo chambers where only similar opinions are shared.

Cultural Notes

The term 'hugbox' is often used in discussions about online communities and social media, where safe spaces are created for expressive support.
