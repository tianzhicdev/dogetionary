Haecceity

Pronunciation:/ˈhɛk.sɪ.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 这个性
  • 唯一性
  • 个体性

Definitions

noun

Definition

The individual characteristic of a thing that makes it uniquely itself, as opposed to its qualities or properties.

In ZH

一个事物独特的个性，使其与其他事物区别开来的个体特征，而不是其特性或属性。

Examples

  • The haecceity of each person ensures that everyone's experience is unique.
  • Philosophers often debate the concept of haecceity in the context of personal identity.
  • To understand haecceity is to grasp the essence of individuality.
  • In metaphysics, haecceity is crucial for discussions about existence.
  • The artist sought to capture the haecceity of his subjects in his paintings.
