Hegemony

Pronunciation:/hɪˈdʒɛməni/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 霸权
  • 领导权
  • 支配权

Definitions

noun

Definition

Leadership or dominance, especially by one country or social group over others.

In ZH

一种领导或主导地位，尤其是一个国家或社会群体对其他国家或群体的主导。

Examples

  • The country's hegemony in the region has led to significant political influence.
  • Many argue that economic hegemony leads to cultural imperialism.
  • The decline of American hegemony raises questions about global stability.
  • He spoke about the hegemony of the tech industry in modern society.
  • Efforts to counteract the hegemony of major corporations are gaining traction.
