Harp On

Pronunciation:/hɑːrp ɒn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不断地重复
  • 唠叨
  • 强调

Definitions

verb

Definition

To talk or write persistently and tediously about something.

In ZH

不断且乏味地谈论或写作某事。

Examples

  • She always harps on about the importance of saving money.
  • He tends to harp on the same issues during meetings.
  • Don't harp on that mistake; we need to move forward.
  • My teacher harped on the need for good study habits.
  • They harp on environmental issues at every conference.
