Harangue

Pronunciation:/həˈræŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 长篇大论
  • 激昂训斥
  • 高谈阔论

Definitions

noun

Definition

a lengthy and aggressive speech

In ZH

冗长而激烈的演讲

Examples

  • After the meeting, he went on a harangue about the need for change.
  • The politician delivered a harangue that inspired many.
  • She launched into a harangue against the proposed legislation.
  • We were subjected to a harangue that lasted over an hour.
  • His harangue was filled with passionate arguments.
verb

Definition

to lecture someone at length in an aggressive and critical manner

In ZH

以激烈和批评的方式长篇大论地训斥某人

Examples

  • The coach harangued the players for their poor performance.
  • She harangued them about their responsibilities.
  • He tends to harangue his friends when they disagree with him.
  • They were harangued by protesters outside the event.
  • The professor harangued the students for coming late.
