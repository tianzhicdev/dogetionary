Habitude

Pronunciation:/ˈhæbɪtjuːd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 习惯
  • 习性
  • 惯例

Definitions

noun

Definition

a habitual practice; a tendency or custom.

In ZH

一种习惯性的做法；一种倾向或习俗。

Examples

  • He developed a habitude of reading every night before bed.
  • In many cultures, social habitude dictates how people greet each other.
  • Her habitude of exercising every morning kept her healthy.
  • The habitude of punctuality is valued in many workplaces.
  • His habitude of journaling helped him process his thoughts.

Cultural Notes

In English, the term 'habitude' is less commonly used than 'habit.' However, it suggests a deeper, more ingrained practice.
