Hermeneutic

EN→ZH

What Does Hermeneutic Mean?

  • 解释学
  • 释义
  • 阐释学

Hermeneutic Pronunciation

/ˌhɜːrməˈnjuːtɪk/

Hermeneutic Definition

noun

Definition

The theory and methodology of interpretation, especially of scriptural texts.

In ZH

解释的理论和方法，尤其是对经文的解释。

How to Use Hermeneutic in a Sentence

  • Hermeneutics is essential for understanding historical documents.
  • The hermeneutic approach provides a framework for interpreting literature.
  • Many philosophers have contributed to the field of hermeneutics.
  • In theology, hermeneutics helps clarify religious texts.
  • She studied hermeneutics to better analyze ancient manuscripts.
adjective

Definition

Relating to or concerned with interpretation or explanation, especially of texts.

In ZH

与解释或说明有关，尤其是文本的解释。

How to Use Hermeneutic in a Sentence

  • The hermeneutic perspective is vital in literary criticism.
  • When reading philosophy, a hermeneutic approach is often required.
  • Hermeneutic analysis can reveal deeper meanings in art.
  • His hermeneutic skills helped unlock the meaning of the poem.
  • They adopted a hermeneutic method to study cultural artifacts.

Synonyms for Hermeneutic

  • Herculean
  • Heresy
  • Heretical
  • Heretofore
  • Hermeneutics
