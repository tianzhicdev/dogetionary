Holluschick

Pronunciation:/həˈluːʃɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 海狮
  • 小海狮
  • 幼年海狮

Definitions

noun

Definition

A term used to refer to a young male harbor seal, especially one that is not yet fully matured.

In ZH

指年轻的雄性海狮，尤其是尚未完全成熟的海狮。

Examples

  • The holluschick was playing on the beach.
  • We spotted a holluschick among the adult seals.
  • The holluschick showed off its swimming skills.
  • Researchers are studying the holluschick's behavior.
  • During the summer, holluschick can be seen basking in the sun.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in marine biology and can be important in discussions about seal populations and their behaviors.
