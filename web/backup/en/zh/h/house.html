House

EN→ZH

What Does House Mean?

  • 房子
  • 住宅
  • 屋子

House Pronunciation

/haʊs/

House Definition

noun

Definition

A building for human habitation, typically consisting of a ground floor and one or more upper storeys.

In ZH

供人类居住的建筑，通常由一层和一个或多个上层构成。

How to Use House in a Sentence

  • She bought a new house in the suburbs.
  • The old house was filled with memories.
  • They painted the house a bright color.
  • He invited his friends over to his house for dinner.
  • The house was built in the 19th century.
