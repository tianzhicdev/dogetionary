Hypothetical

EN→ZH

What Does Hypothetical Mean?

  • 假设的
  • 假想的
  • 假定的

Hypothetical Pronunciation

/ˌhaɪpəˈθɛtɪkəl/

Hypothetical Definition

adjective

Definition

Based on or involving a suggested idea or theory; not real or actual.

In ZH

基于或涉及建议的想法或理论；不是现实或实际的。

How to Use Hypothetical in a Sentence

  • In a hypothetical situation, what would you do?
  • The researchers created a hypothetical model to test their theory.
  • Let's consider a hypothetical scenario where all resources are unlimited.
  • They proposed a hypothetical project to address climate change.
  • His argument was purely hypothetical and lacked real-world evidence.

Synonyms for Hypothetical

  • Hyperbaton
  • Hyperbole
  • Hyperbolic
  • Hyperborean
  • Hyphae
