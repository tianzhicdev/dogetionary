Heterogeneous

Pronunciation:/ˌhɛt.ər.əʊˈdʒiː.ni.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 异质的
  • 多样的
  • 不均匀的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

composed of different or diverse elements; not uniform

In ZH

由不同或多样元素组成；不均匀

Examples

  • The population of the city is heterogeneous, consisting of people from various backgrounds.
  • She has a heterogeneous collection of books, ranging from fiction to nonfiction and everything in between.
  • The team was heterogeneous, bringing together members with different skills and experiences.
  • Heterogeneous mixtures are common in nature, like soil or salad.
  • In a heterogeneous society, cultural diversity is often celebrated.
