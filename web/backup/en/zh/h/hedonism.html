Hedonism

EN→ZH

What Does Hedonism Mean?

  • 享乐主义
  • 快乐主义
  • 极乐主义

Hedonism Pronunciation

/ˈhɛdəˌnɪzəm/

Hedonism Definition

noun

Definition

The belief that pleasure or happiness is the highest good and primary motivation for human actions.

In ZH

享乐或幸福是最高善和人类行为的主要动机的信念。

How to Use Hedonism in a Sentence

  • Hedonism often leads to a focus on personal gratification.
  • Many philosophers have debated the merits of hedonism as a lifestyle.
  • The hedonism of the party culture can sometimes overshadow deeper values.
  • In literature, hedonism is frequently depicted as a double-edged sword.
  • Modern hedonism can be seen in the rise of wellness trends that prioritize pleasure.

Synonyms for Hedonism

  • Hedgerow
  • Hedonistic
