Hedonistic

EN→ZH

What Does Hedonistic Mean?

  • 享乐主义的
  • 享乐主义者的

Hedonistic Pronunciation

/ˌhɛdəˈnɪstɪk/

Hedonistic Definition

adjective

Definition

Characterized by the pursuit of pleasure and self-gratification.

In ZH

以追求快乐和自我满足为特征的。

How to Use Hedonistic in a Sentence

  • The hedonistic lifestyle often prioritizes immediate gratification.
  • Many hedonistic philosophers argue that pleasure is the highest good.
  • She made hedonistic choices that focused on her personal enjoyment.
  • His hedonistic tendencies sometimes led to irresponsible decisions.
  • A hedonistic individual might seek out extravagant experiences.

Synonyms for Hedonistic

  • Hedgerow
  • Hedonism
