Heresy

EN→ZH

What Does Heresy Mean?

  • 异端
  • 邪说
  • 叛教

Heresy Pronunciation

/ˈhɛrəsi/

Heresy Definition

noun

Definition

A belief or opinion that goes against established religious doctrine, especially in Christianity.

In ZH

与既定宗教教义相悖的信仰或观点，尤其是在基督教中。

How to Use Heresy in a Sentence

  • The church condemned his views as heresy.
  • In the Middle Ages, many were executed for heresy.
  • Heresy often led to excommunication from the church.
  • He studied various heresies to understand their origins.
  • The concept of heresy has shifted over time.

Cultural Notes

In historical contexts, heresy was often met with severe penalties, including imprisonment and execution.
