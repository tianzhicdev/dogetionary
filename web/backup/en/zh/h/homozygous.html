Homozygous

Pronunciation:/ˌhoʊ.moʊˈzaɪ.ɡəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 同型合子
  • 纯合子

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having identical alleles for a particular gene on both homologous chromosomes.

In ZH

在两个同源染色体上对某一特定基因具有相同等位基因的情况。

Examples

  • The plant is homozygous for flower color, resulting in all red flowers.
  • In genetic studies, homozygous individuals can be important for understanding inheritance patterns.
  • The homozygous genotype was used in the experiment to ensure consistency.
  • Individuals who are homozygous for this trait often show a stronger expression of the phenotype.
  • Researchers often prefer homozygous lines for creating genetically uniform populations.
