Hapax Legomenon

Pronunciation:/ˈhæpæks lɪˈɡɛmənən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 单次出现的词
  • 独立词

Definitions

noun

Definition

A word that occurs only once within a context, such as in a single text or the entire body of work of an author.

In ZH

在某个上下文中，仅出现一次的词，如在单个文本或作者的全部作品中。

Examples

  • The term 'hapax legomenon' itself is a hapax legomenon in most discussions.
  • In ancient Greek literature, 'hapax legomena' are particularly intriguing to linguists.
  • The word 'floccinaucinihilipilification' is a hapax legomenon in many English writings.
  • Studying hapax legomena can reveal unique insights about an author's style.
  • He used a hapax legomenon to create an air of mystery in his poetry.

Cultural Notes

Often discussed in linguistic and literary analyses, hapax legomena can influence interpretations of a text due to their rarity.
