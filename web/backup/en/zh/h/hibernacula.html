Hibernacula

EN→ZH

What Does Hibernacula Mean?

  • 冬眠窝
  • 栖息地
  • 隐蔽处

Hibernacula Pronunciation

/haɪˈbɜrnəˌkjuːlə/

Hibernacula Definition

noun

Definition

A place where an animal hibernates, typically a hidden or secluded den or burrow.

In ZH

动物冬眠的地方，通常是一个隐蔽或隐秘的洞穴或地道。

How to Use Hibernacula in a Sentence

  • Bears often use hibernacula during the cold winter months.
  • Some species of frogs find hibernacula in mud or under leaves.
  • The research focused on the hibernacula of various small mammals.
  • Hibernate in hibernacula allows these animals to survive harsh conditions.
  • In the fall, many animals begin to search for suitable hibernacula.
