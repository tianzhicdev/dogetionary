Hoarding

Pronunciation:ˈhɔːrdɪŋ
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 囤积
  • 贮藏
  • 储存

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of accumulating and storing items, often in excessive amounts, without a clear purpose or the intention to use them.

In ZH

积累和存储物品的行为，通常以过量的方式进行，没有明确的目的或使用意图。

Examples

  • His hoarding of newspapers made the room feel cramped.
  • She was known for her hoarding tendencies, filling every corner of her house with items she might need someday.
  • The documentary highlighted the psychological aspects of hoarding and its impact on relationships.

Cultural Notes

In some cultures, hoarding can be seen as a sign of resourcefulness, while in others it may indicate psychological issues. Understanding the reasons behind hoarding behavior can vary globally.

verb

Definition

To accumulate items for future use, often to the point where it becomes problematic.

In ZH

为了将来使用而积累物品，通常积累到问题的程度。

Examples

  • He tends to hoard unnecessary items instead of donating them.
  • The fear of running out of supplies caused her to hoard food during the pandemic.
  • It's important to recognize when hoarding is becoming a health issue.

Cultural Notes

Hoarding behavior may be interpreted differently across cultures. In some societies, saving and preserving items is celebrated, while in others it can be stigmatized.
