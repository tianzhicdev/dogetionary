What Does Hyperbolic Mean?

Hyperbolic Pronunciation

/haɪˈpɜːrbəlɪk/

Hyperbolic Definition

adjective

Definition

Of or relating to hyperbola; characterized by hyperbole; exaggerated

In ZH

与双曲线相关的；特征是夸张的；夸张的

How to Use Hyperbolic in a Sentence

  • His claims about his experience seemed hyperbolic to me.
  • The hyperbolic shape of the bridge made it visually striking.
  • In literature, hyperbolic expressions are often used for comedic effect.
  • She used hyperbolic language to emphasize her point during the debate.
  • Many mathematicians study hyperbolic geometry as part of their curriculum.

Cultural Notes

In mathematics, hyperbolic refers to a specific type of geometry that differs fundamentally from Euclidean geometry. In everyday language, hyperbolic often describes exaggerated statements that are not meant to be taken literally.
