Hullabaloo

Pronunciation:/ˌhʌl.ə.bəˈluː/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 喧闹
  • 骚动
  • 嘈杂

Definitions

noun

Definition

A commotion or fuss, especially one caused by conflicting views.

In ZH

一场骚动或纷扰，尤其是由相互对立的观点引起的。

Examples

  • There was a hullabaloo outside the school when the fire alarm went off.
  • The hullabaloo over the election results lasted for weeks.
  • She created quite a hullabaloo with her controversial remarks.
  • The children made such a hullabaloo that the parents had to intervene.
  • We heard a hullabaloo coming from the party next door.
