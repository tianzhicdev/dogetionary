Hyphenate

EN→ZH

What Does Hyphenate Mean?

  • 连字符
  • 连写
  • 连音符

Hyphenate Pronunciation

/ˈhaɪ.fə.neɪt/

Hyphenate Definition

verb

Definition

To add a hyphen to a word or phrase, often to join two or more components into a single term.

In ZH

在单词或短语中添加连字符，通常将两个或多个组成部分结合成一个术语。

How to Use Hyphenate in a Sentence

  • Please hyphenate the words where necessary.
  • The teacher asked us to hyphenate compound adjectives.
  • In formal writing, it's important to hyphenate certain phrases.
  • Make sure to hyphenate the names correctly in the document.
  • It's common to hyphenate terms in technical writing.
