Sacrosanct

EN→ZH

What Does Sacrosanct Mean?

  • 神圣的
  • 至高无上的
  • 不可侵犯的

Sacrosanct Pronunciation

/ˈsækrəˌsæŋkt/

Sacrosanct Definition

adjective

Definition

Regarded as too important or valuable to be interfered with.

In ZH

被认为是太重要或有价值而不能被干预的。

How to Use Sacrosanct in a Sentence

  • Many view freedom of speech as a sacrosanct right.
  • The sacred texts are often considered sacrosanct by their followers.
  • In some societies, certain traditions are seen as sacrosanct and must not be altered.
  • The principle of justice is often regarded as sacrosanct in legal systems.
  • While some policies may be debated, the constitution is viewed as sacrosanct.

Synonyms for Sacrosanct

  • Saccharine
  • Sacrilege
  • Sacrilegious
