Spatiotemporal

Pronunciation:/ˌspeɪʃiəʊˈtɛmpərəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 时空的
  • 时空相关的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to both space and time, especially in the context of physics or other sciences.

In ZH

与空间和时间相关的，尤其是在物理或其他科学的背景下。

  • In philosophy, spatiotemporal concepts are essential to understanding motion.
