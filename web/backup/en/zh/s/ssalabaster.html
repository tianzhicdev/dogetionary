Ssalabaster

Pronunciation:/ˈsæl.əˌbæ.stər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 石膏
  • 白石
  • 滑石

Definitions

noun

Definition

A fine-grained, translucent form of gypsum, often used for carving.

In ZH

一种细粒、半透明的石膏，常用于雕刻。

Examples

  • The artist chose alabaster for his latest sculpture.
  • Alabaster is known for its smooth texture and ease of carving.
  • She bought an alabaster vase at the art fair.
  • Many ancient statues were made from alabaster due to its beauty.
  • The intricate details of the alabaster relief amazed everyone.
