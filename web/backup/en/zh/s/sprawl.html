Sprawl

Pronunciation:sprɔːl
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 蔓延
  • 扩展
  • 杂乱无章的状态
  • 城市蔓延

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of spreading out over a large area in a messy or untidy way.

In ZH

在一个大区域中杂乱无章地扩展的行为。

Examples

  • The urban sprawl has led to traffic congestion in the city.
  • The sprawl of the city into the countryside raises environmental concerns.
  • They tried to control the sprawl of the new housing developments.

Cultural Notes

In urban studies, 'sprawl' often refers to the uncontrolled and unplanned expansion of cities into surrounding areas, causing issues such as increased commute times and loss of farmland.

verb

Definition

To spread out or stretch out in a disordered or awkward manner.

In ZH

以杂乱或笨拙的方式扩展或伸展。

Examples

  • The cat sprawled across the sunny spot on the floor.
  • After the long hike, we sprawled out on the grass to rest.
  • He sprawled in his chair, looking very comfortable.

Cultural Notes

The verb form can describe both a literal action, such as lying down in a relaxed position, as well as a figurative sense, like the spread of population across a geographic area.
