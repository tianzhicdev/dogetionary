Scherzando

EN→ZH

What Does Scherzando Mean?

  • 嬉戏的
  • 玩笑的
  • 滑稽的

Scherzando Pronunciation

/sˈkɛr.zɑːn.doʊ/

Scherzando Definition

adjective

Definition

In a playful or jesting manner, typically used in music to indicate a lively or humorous style of performance.

In ZH

以玩耍或开玩笑的方式，通常用于音乐中，指示一种活泼或幽默的演奏风格。

How to Use Scherzando in a Sentence

  • The performer played the piece in a scherzando style, bringing smiles to the audience.
  • The scherzando section of the symphony added a playful contrast to the more serious themes.
  • She interpreted the scherzando marking with a light-hearted touch that delighted everyone.
  • In classical music, scherzando often appears to lighten the mood between darker movements.
  • The conductor urged the orchestra to embrace the scherzando feeling, infusing the performance with joy.
