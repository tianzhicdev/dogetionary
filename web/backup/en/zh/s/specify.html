Specify

Pronunciation:/ˈspɛsɪfaɪ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 指定
  • 明确说明
  • 特别说明

Definitions

verb

Definition

To state or identify something clearly and precisely.

In ZH

清楚而准确地陈述或识别某事。

Examples

  • Please specify your requirements for the project.
  • She specified the date and location for the meeting.
  • Can you specify which version of the software you need?
  • The instructions didn't specify how to handle errors.
  • It's important to specify the rules before starting the game.
