Scintillating

EN→ZH

What Does Scintillating Mean?

  • 闪烁的
  • 发光的
  • 令人兴奋的

Scintillating Pronunciation

/ˈsɪn.tɪ.leɪ.tɪŋ/

Scintillating Definition

adjective

Definition

sparkling or shining brightly; brilliantly and excitingly clever or skillful

In ZH

闪烁或明亮地发光；聪明或技巧上非常精彩和激动人心的

How to Use Scintillating in a Sentence

  • The scintillating performance by the orchestra captivated the audience.
  • Her scintillating wit made the conversation lively.
  • The scintillating stars in the night sky were breathtaking.
  • He delivered a scintillating lecture that kept everyone engaged.
  • The novel is full of scintillating ideas and profound insights.

Synonyms for Scintillating

  • Scintilla
  • Scintillate
  • Scintillation
