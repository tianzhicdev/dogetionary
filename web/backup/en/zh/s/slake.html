Slake

EN→ZH

What Does Slake Mean?

  • 解渴
  • 消除
  • 减轻

Slake Pronunciation

/sleɪk/

Slake Definition

verb

Definition

to quench (thirst), to satisfy, or to relieve

In ZH

解渴，满足，或缓和

How to Use Slake in a Sentence

  • After a long hike, I needed to slake my thirst.
  • The rain helped to slake the dryness of the earth.
  • He tried to slake his hunger with a quick snack.
  • She slaked her curiosity by reading the entire book.
  • The team hoped to slake their fans' excitement with a big win.

Synonyms for Slake

  • Slander
  • Slavish
