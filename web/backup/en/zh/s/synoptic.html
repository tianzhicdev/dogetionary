Synoptic

Pronunciation:/sɪˈnɒptɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 概要的
  • 天候的
  • 摘要的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to a general summary or synopsis; providing a broad view of something.

In ZH

与概括或摘要有关的；提供某事物的广泛视图。

Examples

  • The synoptic gospels provide a summary of Jesus's life and teachings.
  • The synoptic view of the project allowed the team to identify key areas of improvement.
  • During the meeting, a synoptic analysis of the data was presented.
  • She provided a synoptic report of the conference proceedings.
  • The synoptic weather chart showed patterns of atmospheric pressure across the region.
