Sedulous

Pronunciation:/ˈsɛdʒ.ə.ləs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 勤勉的
  • 孜孜不倦的
  • 专心致志的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

showing dedication and diligence in one's work or tasks

In ZH

在工作或任务中表现出专注和勤奋的

Examples

  • She was sedulous in her studies, earning top marks in every subject.
  • The sedulous efforts of the volunteers led to the success of the charity event.
  • He was known for his sedulous attention to detail in his work.
  • Their sedulous planning paid off when they launched the product successfully.
  • The artist was sedulous in perfecting her craft, practicing for hours every day.
