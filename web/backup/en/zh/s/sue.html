Sue

Pronunciation:suː
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 起诉
  • 控告
  • 诉讼

Definitions

verb

Definition

To initiate legal proceedings against someone in a court of law.

In ZH

在法庭上对某人提起法律诉讼。

Examples

  • She decided to sue the company for wrongful termination.
  • If you feel wronged, you might consider suing for damages.
  • He sued his neighbor for damaging his property.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, suing someone is often seen as a last resort after other forms of conflict resolution have failed.

verb

Definition

To seek compensation or remedy from someone or something in a legal context.

In ZH

在法律背景下向某人或某事寻求赔偿或补救。

Examples

  • The couple sued the hospital for medical malpractice.
  • They are suing the landlord for failing to fix the heating.
  • You can sue for emotional distress caused by the accident.

Cultural Notes

In the United States, high-profile lawsuits are common and can often be highly publicized, affecting public perception.

noun

Definition

The act of taking legal action against someone; a legal claim.

In ZH

对某人采取法律行动的行为；法律索赔。

Examples

  • The lawsuit was filed in federal court.
  • He won the lawsuit against his former employer.
  • They are in the midst of a lengthy lawsuit over the property dispute.

Cultural Notes

Lawsuits can be lengthy and costly, and it's common for parties to settle out of court to avoid the time and expense.
