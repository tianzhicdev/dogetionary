Slumberous

Pronunciation:/ˈslʌmbərəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 昏昏欲睡的
  • 沉睡的
  • 催眠的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having a quality of inducing sleep; drowsy or lethargic.

In ZH

有催眠效果的；昏昏欲睡的

Examples

  • The slumberous atmosphere of the library made it hard to focus.
  • After a long day, he felt slumberous and ready for bed.
  • The slumberous heat of the afternoon made everyone sleepy.
  • Her slumberous gaze suggested she was not paying attention.
  • The slumberous melody lulled the children to sleep.
