Sesquipedalian

Pronunciation:/ˌsɛskwɪpɪˈdeɪliən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 长词的
  • 多音节的
  • 轮廓庞大的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

characterized by long words; long-winded.

In ZH

以长词为特征；冗长的。

Examples

  • The sesquipedalian nature of his writing made it difficult to understand.
  • She delivered a sesquipedalian speech that left most of the audience confused.
  • Using sesquipedalian terms can sometimes alienate your readers.
  • His sesquipedalian style is both impressive and pretentious.
  • In academic circles, sesquipedalian language is often seen as a sign of intelligence.
noun

Definition

a person who uses long words.

In ZH

一个使用长词的人。

Examples

  • The professor was a true sesquipedalian, often using words that few could define.
  • As a sesquipedalian, he enjoyed showcasing his extensive vocabulary.
  • In discussions, he acted like a sesquipedalian to impress his peers.
  • Being a sesquipedalian isn't always helpful in casual conversation.
  • She was often teased for being a sesquipedalian at the family gatherings.
