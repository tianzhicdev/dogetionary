Sanctity

Pronunciation:/ˈsæŋk.tɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 神圣性
  • 圣洁
  • 尊严

Definitions

noun

Definition

The state or quality of being holy, sacred, or saintly.

In ZH

神圣、圣洁或圣人的状态或品质。

Examples

  • The sanctity of life is a fundamental principle in many cultures.
  • He spoke about the sanctity of marriage during his speech.
  • The community gathered to protect the sanctity of their sacred site.
  • She believed in the sanctity of her personal beliefs.
  • Many religions emphasize the sanctity of their scriptures.
