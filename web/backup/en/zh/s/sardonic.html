Sardonic

EN→ZH

What Does Sardonic Mean?

  • 讽刺的
  • 嘲讽的
  • 冷嘲热讽的

Sardonic Pronunciation

/sɑːrˈdɒnɪk/

Sardonic Definition

adjective

Definition

characterized by bitter or scornful derision; mocking.

In ZH

以苦涩或轻蔑的嘲讽为特征；讽刺的。

How to Use Sardonic in a Sentence

  • His sardonic smile made it clear he was not impressed.
  • She had a sardonic wit that often surprised her friends.
  • The movie's sardonic humor was a refreshing change from typical comedies.
  • His sardonic comments during the meeting made the atmosphere tense.
  • She often used sardonic remarks to cope with difficult situations.

Synonyms for Sardonic

  • Sardonically
  • Sardonicism
  • Sartor
  • Sartorial
