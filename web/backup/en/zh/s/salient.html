Salient

Pronunciation:/ˈseɪ.li.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 显著的
  • 突出的
  • 重要的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Most noticeable or important.

In ZH

最显著或最重要的。

Examples

  • The salient features of the product make it stand out in the market.
  • Her salient points during the presentation highlighted the main issues.
  • The report summarizes the salient findings of the research.
  • In the debate, he focused on the salient arguments.
  • The salient aspects of the landscape were captured beautifully in the painting.
