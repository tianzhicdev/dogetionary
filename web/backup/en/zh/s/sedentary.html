Sedentary

Pronunciation:/ˈsɛd.ənˌtɛr.i/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 久坐的
  • 静态的
  • 不动的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by a sitting position; not active or mobile.

In ZH

以坐姿为特征；不活跃或不移动。

Examples

  • Many office jobs are sedentary and require long hours of sitting.
  • A sedentary lifestyle can lead to various health issues.
  • He prefers a sedentary hobby like reading over more physical activities.
  • Sedentary animals are often less prone to predation.
  • Sedentary behavior is linked to increased risk of obesity.
