Singularity

Pronunciation:/sɪŋˈɡjʊləti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 奇点
  • 特殊性
  • 独特性

Definitions

noun

Definition

The state of being singular or unique; a point in which something is unusual or unique, particularly in mathematics or physics.

In ZH

唯一性；独特性，特别是数学或物理学中的一个点。

Examples

  • The concept of singularity is often discussed in the context of black holes.
  • The technological singularity refers to a hypothetical future point when artificial intelligence surpasses human intelligence.
  • In mathematics, a singularity can refer to a point where a function does not behave normally.
  • Many scientists believe that the universe began with a singularity.
  • The singularity in the fabric of spacetime creates extreme gravitational forces.
