Semblance

Pronunciation:/ˈsɛmbləns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 相似
  • 外表
  • 假象

Definitions

noun

Definition

the outward appearance or apparent form of something, especially when the reality is different.

In ZH

事物的外观或表面形式，尤其是当其真实情况不同的时候。

Examples

  • His calm demeanor was merely a semblance of confidence.
  • The two buildings share a semblance in design.
  • She tried to maintain a semblance of normalcy during the chaos.
  • There is a semblance of truth in his explanation.
  • They put on a semblance of happiness for the guests.
