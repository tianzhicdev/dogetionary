Sybaritic

EN→ZH

What Does Sybaritic Mean?

  • 奢侈的
  • 享乐的
  • 贪图享乐的

Sybaritic Pronunciation

/ˌsɪb.əˈrɪt.ɪk/

Sybaritic Definition

adjective

Definition

characterized by or loving luxury and the pleasures of life

In ZH

以奢华和享受生活的乐趣为特征或热爱的

How to Use Sybaritic in a Sentence

  • Living in a sybaritic lifestyle, she surrounded herself with fine wines and gourmet food.
  • The sybaritic hotel offered every luxury imaginable to its guests.
  • He was known for his sybaritic tendencies, often indulging in lavish parties.
  • Their sybaritic habits led them to spend more than they could afford.
  • In contrast to their austere upbringing, their new life was utterly sybaritic.

Synonyms for Sybaritic
