Seek

EN→ZH

What Does Seek Mean?

  • 寻找
  • 寻求
  • 追求

Seek Pronunciation

/siːk/

Seek Definition

verb

Definition

to attempt to find or obtain (something)

In ZH

努力寻找或获得（某物）

How to Use Seek in a Sentence

  • I seek knowledge wherever I go.
  • They are seeking a solution to the problem.
  • She seeks the truth in her research.
  • We often seek comfort in difficult times.
  • He seeks approval from his peers.
