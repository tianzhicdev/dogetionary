Scope Of Microbiology

What Does Scope Of Microbiology Mean?

  • 微生物学的范围
  • 微生物学的领域

Scope Of Microbiology Pronunciation

skəʊp əv maɪkroʊbaɪɒlədʒi

Scope Of Microbiology Definition

noun

Definition

The extent of the area or subject matter that is covered by microbiology, including its applications in health, industry, and environmental science.

In ZH

微生物学所涵盖的领域或主题的范围，包括其在健康、工业和环境科学中的应用。

How to Use Scope Of Microbiology in a Sentence

  • The scope of microbiology includes studying bacteria, viruses, fungi, and protozoa.
  • Understanding the scope of microbiology is essential for developing new antibiotics.
  • The scope of microbiology affects various fields such as medicine, agriculture, and biotechnology.

Cultural Notes

In many countries, microbiology is a critical area of study that has significant implications for public health, food safety, and environmental protection.

adjective

Definition

Relating to the various branches and studies within microbiology that contribute to its understanding and applications.

In ZH

与微生物学内部的各个分支和研究相关，帮助理解和应用微生物学。

How to Use Scope Of Microbiology in a Sentence

  • The diverse scope of microbiology allows for a wide range of research opportunities.
  • Innovations in technology expand the scope of microbiology every day.
  • Students are encouraged to explore the expansive scope of microbiology during their studies.

Cultural Notes

As a discipline, microbiology is known for its rapid growth and evolution, particularly during health crises such as the COVID-19 pandemic.
