Soothe

Pronunciation:suːð
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 安慰
  • 抚慰
  • 镇静

Definitions

verb

Definition

To gently calm or comfort someone or something, often by reducing pain or distress.

In ZH

温和地平静或安慰某人或某物，通常是通过减轻疼痛或痛苦。

Examples

  • She sang a lullaby to soothe the baby to sleep.
  • I tried to soothe my friend after her difficult day.
  • The herbal tea helped to soothe my stomach.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, soothing actions can involve physical touch, such as hugging or patting someone on the back. Additionally, traditional practices like singing or using gentle words play a significant role in providing comfort.

verb

Definition

To relieve or mitigate feelings of anxiety, anger, or sadness.

In ZH

减轻或缓和焦虑、愤怒或悲伤的情绪。

Examples

  • He spoke softly to soothe her fears about the future.
  • Taking deep breaths can help soothe your nerves before a big presentation.
  • The therapist gave her advice to soothe her racing thoughts.

Cultural Notes

The notion of soothing can differ between cultures; for instance, some cultures might prefer direct emotional expression while others value restraint. Understanding how to soothe someone can depend on these cultural nuances.
