Swagger

EN→ZH

What Does Swagger Mean?

  • 潇洒
  • 气派
  • 神气

Swagger Pronunciation

/ˈswæɡər/

Swagger Definition

noun

Definition

a manner of walking or moving that is confident and arrogant; swagger can also refer to a sense of style or confidence.

In ZH

一种自信且傲慢的走路或移动方式；swagger 也可以指一种风格或自信的感觉。

How to Use Swagger in a Sentence

  • He walked into the room with a lot of swagger.
  • Her swagger made her stand out in the crowd.
  • The athlete is known for his confident swagger on the field.
  • With a swagger in his step, he approached the podium.
  • That new jacket gives him an air of swagger.
verb

Definition

to walk or behave in a very confident and arrogant way.

In ZH

以非常自信和傲慢的方式走路或行为。

How to Use Swagger in a Sentence

  • They swaggered around the party like they owned the place.
  • She swags through the hallway, drawing everyone's attention.
  • The band swaggered onto the stage, ready to perform.
  • He tends to swagger when he talks about his accomplishments.
  • The kids swaggered into the school, full of enthusiasm.

Synonyms for Swagger

  • Swayed
