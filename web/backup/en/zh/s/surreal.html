Surreal

Pronunciation:/səˈriːəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 超现实的
  • 超现实主义的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having the qualities of surrealism; bizarre or dreamlike.

In ZH

具有超现实主义的特征；奇异或梦幻般的。

Examples

  • The painting had a surreal quality that left everyone amazed.
  • His dream was filled with surreal images that defied logic.
  • The movie's surreal scenes challenged viewers' perceptions of reality.
  • Walking through the fog felt surreal, as if in another world.
  • The artist's work is often described as surreal, blending reality with fantasy.

Cultural Notes

Surrealism originated in the early 20th century and is closely associated with the art and literary movement that sought to release the creative potential of the unconscious mind.
