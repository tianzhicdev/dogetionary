Susurration

Pronunciation:/sʌsəˈreɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 低语
  • 耳语
  • 细语

Definitions

noun

Definition

A soft, whispering, or rustling sound.

In ZH

柔和的耳语或沙沙声。

Examples

  • The susurration of the leaves in the breeze was soothing.
  • She heard the susurration of the ocean waves from her porch.
  • In the quiet library, the only sound was the susurration of pages turning.
  • The susurration of the crowd filled the hall as people waited for the show to start.
  • As the wind picked up, there was a comforting susurration through the trees.
