Since

Pronunciation:/sɪns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 自从
  • 因为
  • 自...以来

Definitions

conjunction

Definition

Indicates a starting point in time, often used to denote that something has been the case from a specific moment until now.

In ZH

表示时间的起点，通常用于说明从某一特定时刻以来某事一直如此。

Examples

  • I have been living here since 2010.
  • She hasn’t called me since last week.
  • They have evolved since their initial concept.
  • We’ve known each other since childhood.
  • It has been raining since morning.
preposition

Definition

Used with a specific time reference to indicate when an action or state began.

In ZH

与特定时间参考一起使用，以指示动作或状态开始的时间。

Examples

  • I haven't seen him since the party.
  • He has been unhappy since his dog passed away.
  • She has worked here since the company started.
  • They haven’t visited us since their last trip.
  • I’ve eaten this dish since I was a child.
