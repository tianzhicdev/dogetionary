Solipsism

Pronunciation:/ˈsɒlɪpsɪzəm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 唯我主义
  • 自我主义
  • 唯心主义

Definitions

noun

Definition

The philosophical idea that only one's own mind is sure to exist, leading to the conclusion that knowledge outside one's own mind is unsure.

In ZH

一种哲学思想，认为只有自己的意识是确定存在的，进而得出外界的知识是不确定的结论。

Examples

  • Solipsism raises questions about the nature of reality.
  • In a solipsistic view, the existence of others is questionable.
  • He wrote a paper arguing against solipsism.
  • Many philosophers have debated the implications of solipsism.
  • Solipsism can be seen as a challenge to interpersonal relationships.
