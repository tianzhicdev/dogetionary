Smock

EN→ZH

What Does Smock Mean?

  • 工作服
  • 罩衫
  • 围裙

Smock Pronunciation

/smɒk/

Smock Definition

noun

Definition

A loose, lightweight garment worn over the clothes to protect them while working, especially in artistic or craft contexts.

In ZH

一种宽松、轻便的服装，穿在衣服上以保护它们，尤其是在艺术或手工制作的场合。

How to Use Smock in a Sentence

  • She wore a smock while painting to keep her clothes clean.
  • The children put on their smocks before starting the messy craft project.
  • A smock is essential for anyone working with clay or paint.
  • He bought a new smock for his pottery class.
  • The artist always dons her smock before getting to work.
