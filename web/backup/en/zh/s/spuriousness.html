Spuriousness

EN→ZH

What Does Spuriousness Mean?

  • 虚假性
  • 伪造性
  • 假冒性

Spuriousness Pronunciation

/ˈspjʊəriəsnəs/

Spuriousness Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being spurious; not being what it purports to be; false or deceptive in nature.

In ZH

虚假性；不是真实的；本质上虚假或欺骗性的质量。

How to Use Spuriousness in a Sentence

  • The spuriousness of the claim was exposed after further investigation.
  • Many products on the market are criticized for their spuriousness.
  • The researcher was careful to address the spuriousness of the data before drawing conclusions.
  • Debates on social media often highlight the spuriousness of information being shared.
  • To avoid spuriousness, it's important to verify facts before accepting them.

Synonyms for Spuriousness

  • Spurious
  • Spurn
