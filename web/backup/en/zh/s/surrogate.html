Surrogate

Surrogate Pronunciation

/ˈsɜːrəɡət/

Surrogate Definition

noun

Definition

A person or thing that takes the place of another.

In ZH

一个取代另一个的人或事物。

How to Use Surrogate in a Sentence

  • She served as a surrogate for her sister during the pregnancy.
  • The surrogate mother carried the baby for the couple.
  • They found a surrogate to help them start a family.
  • In some cases, a surrogate can also refer to a placeholder in discussions.
  • The role of a surrogate can sometimes involve legal complexities.
