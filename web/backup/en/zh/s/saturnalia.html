Saturnalia

EN→ZH

What Does Saturnalia Mean?

  • 农神节
  • 土星节
  • 世俗狂欢节

Saturnalia Pronunciation

/ˌsætərˈneɪliə/

Saturnalia Definition

noun

Definition

an ancient Roman festival in December in honor of Saturn, characterized by feasting, role reversals, and revelry

In ZH

古罗马的节日，庆祝土星，特征是盛宴、角色颠倒和狂欢

How to Use Saturnalia in a Sentence

  • During Saturnalia, social norms were often upended.
  • The Saturnalia festival allowed slaves to partake in the festivities like free men.
  • In ancient Rome, people exchanged gifts during Saturnalia.
  • Saturnalia is often regarded as a precursor to modern Christmas celebrations.
  • People would decorate their homes and celebrate with parties during Saturnalia.
