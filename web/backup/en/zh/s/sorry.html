Sorry

What Does Sorry Mean?

  • 对不起
  • 抱歉
  • 遗憾

Sorry Pronunciation

/ˈsɔːri/

Sorry Definition

adjective

Definition

feeling regret or penitence; feeling sorry for something that has happened or for a mistake that has been made

In ZH

感到遗憾或悔恨；为发生的事情或所犯的错误感到抱歉

How to Use Sorry in a Sentence

  • I'm sorry for being late.
  • She felt sorry for breaking his favorite vase.
  • He's sorry that he forgot your birthday.
  • I'm sorry to hear about your loss.
  • Please forgive me; I am truly sorry.
interjection

Definition

used to express apology

In ZH

用于表示歉意

How to Use Sorry in a Sentence

  • Sorry! I didn’t mean to bump into you.
  • Sorry, can you repeat that?
  • Sorry, but I can’t help you right now.
  • I’m sorry, but we’re out of stock.
  • Sorry for the confusion.
