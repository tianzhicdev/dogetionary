Supercilious

EN→ZH

What Does Supercilious Mean?

  • 高傲的
  • 傲慢的

Supercilious Pronunciation

/ˌsuːpəˈsɪliəs/

Supercilious Definition

adjective

Definition

behaving or looking as though one thinks one is superior to others

In ZH

表现或看起来像是认为自己优于他人的

How to Use Supercilious in a Sentence

  • His supercilious attitude made him unpopular at work.
  • She gave him a supercilious smile as if she was better than everyone else.
  • The supercilious waiter looked down on us because we were not dressed up.
  • He spoke in a supercilious tone that irritated everyone in the room.
  • They were criticized for their supercilious disregard for the opinions of the local community.

Synonyms for Supercilious

  • Superfluous
  • Supersession
  • Supine
  • Suppersession
