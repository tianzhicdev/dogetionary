Spontaneity

Pronunciation:/spɒntəˈniːɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 自发性
  • 自发
  • 自然性

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being spontaneous; the condition of occurring without premeditation or external cause.

In ZH

自发的特质；在没有预先考虑或外部原因的情况下发生的状态。

Examples

  • Her spontaneity often leads to exciting adventures.
  • He admired her spontaneity and willingness to try new things.
  • Spontaneity can be refreshing in a world of rigid schedules.
  • The spontaneity of youth can sometimes result in unplanned actions.
  • In relationships, spontaneity can keep the spark alive.
