Suburbs

EN→ZH

What Does Suburbs Mean?

  • 郊区
  • 市郊

Suburbs Pronunciation

/ˈsʌbɜrbz/

Suburbs Definition

noun

Definition

Residential areas located on the outskirts of a city, typically characterized by lower population density and more green spaces than urban centers.

In ZH

位于城市边缘的住宅区，通常其人口密度较低，绿地比城市中心多。

How to Use Suburbs in a Sentence

  • Many families prefer to live in the suburbs to have larger homes and yards.
  • The suburbs offer a peaceful living environment while still being close to the city.
  • Public transport connects the suburbs to the downtown area, making commuting easier for residents.

Cultural Notes

In many countries, especially in the United States, suburbs are where many families choose to live for more space and a quieter lifestyle compared to the hectic city life.

noun

Definition

An area that is primarily residential, often with shopping centers and parks, serving as a transition zone between urban and rural areas.

In ZH

主要为住宅区的区域，通常有购物中心和公园，作为城市和农村区域之间的过渡区。

How to Use Suburbs in a Sentence

  • Suburbs usually have schools and parks that are more accessible to families with children.
  • Local events in the suburbs help bring the community together, such as farmers' markets and festivals.
  • Many people drive to the suburbs to enjoy the larger spaces and outdoor activities available there.

Cultural Notes

Suburbs often have a blend of residential homes, schools, and commercial areas, which creates a community feel and may host local events.
