Shibboleth

EN→ZH

What Does Shibboleth Mean?

  • 口令
  • 信号
  • 特征

Shibboleth Pronunciation

/ˈʃɪb.ə.lɛθ/

Shibboleth Definition

noun

Definition

A custom, principle, or belief distinguishing a particular group of people.

In ZH

一种区分特定人群的习俗、原则或信念。

How to Use Shibboleth in a Sentence

  • The word 'shibboleth' has long been a symbol of social identity.
  • In some cultures, certain phrases serve as shibboleths for group belonging.
  • The use of outdated technology became a shibboleth of the company's decline.
  • In the debate, the candidate repeatedly used shibboleths to appeal to their base.
  • The pronunciation of the word was a shibboleth that identified many as outsiders.
