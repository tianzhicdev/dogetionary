Sententious

Pronunciation:/sɛnˈtɛnʃəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 好发表意见的
  • 说教的
  • 简洁的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Given to excessive moralizing; self-righteously lofty in tone or style; pompous or pretentious in expression.

In ZH

喜欢过度道德化；自以为是的高调或风格；在表达上浮夸或自命不凡。

Examples

  • His sententious remarks often alienate his friends.
  • The author's sententious style makes the book hard to read.
  • She delivered a sententious lecture that left everyone bored.
  • His sententious warnings about the future were largely ignored.
  • They found his sententious attitude off-putting during the discussion.
