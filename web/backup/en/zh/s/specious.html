Specious

Pronunciation:/ˈspiːʃəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 似是而非的
  • 虚假的
  • 华而不实的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

superficially plausible, but actually wrong or misleading

In ZH

表面上似乎合理，但实际上是错误或具有误导性的

Examples

  • His argument was specious and did not hold up under scrutiny.
  • She gave a specious explanation that failed to convince anyone.
  • The specious reasoning in the article led to widespread misconceptions.
  • They made specious claims about the benefits of the product.
  • Don't be fooled by their specious promises.
