Sale

EN→ZH

What Does Sale Mean?

  • 出售
  • 销售信
  • 特卖
  • 折扣销售
  • 促销

Sale Pronunciation

/seɪl/

Sale Definition

noun

Definition

An event during which goods are sold at reduced prices.

In ZH

一种商品以降低价格出售的事件。

How to Use Sale in a Sentence

  • The store is having a big sale this weekend.
  • I bought a new jacket on sale.
  • There was a clearance sale at the mall.
  • They announced a flash sale on their website.
  • I love finding great deals during the end-of-season sale.
noun

Definition

The exchange of goods or services for money.

In ZH

用钱交换商品或服务。

How to Use Sale in a Sentence

  • The sale of the house was finalized last week.
  • He made a sale and earned a commission.
  • Sales have increased since the advertising campaign started.
  • The sale of tickets for the concert begins tomorrow.
  • I specialize in the sale of vintage clothing.

Synonyms for Sale

  • Salience
  • Salient
  • Salir
  • Saltation
