Sondering

Pronunciation:/ˈsɒndərɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 思绪涌动
  • 探索

Definitions

noun

Definition

The feeling of realizing that each random passerby is living a life as vivid and complex as your own.

In ZH

意识到每一个随意经过的人都过着和你一样生动复杂的生活的感觉。
