Sanctuary

Pronunciation:/ˈsæŋk.tʃuː.er.i/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 避难所
  • 圣所
  • 保护区

Definitions

noun

Definition

A place of refuge or safety.

In ZH

一个避难或安全的地方。

Examples

  • The wildlife sanctuary is home to many endangered species.
  • She found sanctuary in her grandmother's house during the storm.
  • The church provided sanctuary for refugees.
  • They built a sanctuary for the rescued animals.
  • The monks live in a sanctuary dedicated to peace.
