Surfeit

Pronunciation:/ˈsɜːrfɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 过量
  • 过剩
  • 饮食过度

Definitions

noun

Definition

An excessive amount of something, especially food or drink.

In ZH

过量，尤其是食物或饮料的过剩。

Examples

  • After the feast, we were all suffering from a surfeit of food.
  • The surfeit of options made it difficult to choose a restaurant.
  • He experienced a surfeit of praise after completing the project.
  • A surfeit of sweets can lead to health issues.
  • The festival was marked by a surfeit of joy and celebration.
verb

Definition

To supply with an excessive amount of something.

In ZH

使某物过量供应。

Examples

  • The holiday season tends to surfeit us with gifts and treats.
  • They surfeited the guests with an endless flow of wine.
  • Be careful not to surfeit your audience with too much information too quickly.
  • The company was surfeited with applications after the job posting went live.
  • Surfeiting the children with sweets is not advisable.
