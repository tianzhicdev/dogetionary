Serendipity

EN→ZH

What Does Serendipity Mean?

  • 机缘
  • 意外的好运
  • 偶然发现的事物

Serendipity Pronunciation

/ˌsɛrənˈdɪpɪti/

Serendipity Definition

noun

Definition

The occurrence of events by chance in a happy or beneficial way.

In ZH

偶然发生的愉快或有益的事件的发生。

How to Use Serendipity in a Sentence

  • Finding that old photograph was pure serendipity.
  • Their meeting was a case of serendipity.
  • Serendipity led them to discover a hidden gem of a restaurant.
  • Sometimes serendipity can lead to the best experiences in life.
  • She believed that serendipity plays a role in love.

Synonyms for Serendipity

  • Seraphic
  • Seraphim
  • Sere
  • Serendipitous
  • Serpentine
