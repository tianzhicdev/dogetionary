Stultify

EN→ZH

What Does Stultify Mean?

  • 使变得无效
  • 使无用
  • 使愚蠢

Stultify Pronunciation

/ˈstʌl.tɪ.faɪ/

Stultify Definition

verb

Definition

to cause to lose enthusiasm and initiative, especially as a result of a tedious or restrictive routine

In ZH

使失去热情和主动性，尤其是由于单调或限制性的日常生活

How to Use Stultify in a Sentence

  • The tedious meetings stultify creativity.
  • She felt the strict regulations stultified her ability to innovate.
  • His monotonous job began to stultify his passion for learning.
  • The educational system can sometimes stultify students' independent thinking.
  • Too many rules can stultify the development of new ideas.

Synonyms for Stultify

  • Stupefy
  • Stuttering
