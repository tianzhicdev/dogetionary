Supplies

Pronunciation:sə-ˈplīz
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 供应品
  • 物资
  • 补给品

Definitions

noun

Definition

Items or materials that are used to fulfill a specific need or purpose.

In ZH

用于满足特定需求或目的的物品或材料。

Examples

  • The school provided the students with all the necessary supplies for their projects.
  • We need to order more office supplies because we are running low on paper and ink.
  • During the emergency, volunteers gathered supplies to help those affected.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, especially in educational or work settings, having the right supplies is crucial for success. Schools often provide students with lists of required supplies before the start of a new term.

verb

Definition

To provide someone with necessary items or materials.

In ZH

向某人提供必要的物品或材料。

Examples

  • They will supply the food and drinks for the party.
  • Our company aims to supply quality products to our customers.
  • The organization aims to supply aid to communities in need.

Cultural Notes

This verb usage often reflects a philanthropic or commercial context where assistance or resources are provided to others.
