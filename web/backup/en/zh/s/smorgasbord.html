Smorgasbord

EN→ZH

What Does Smorgasbord Mean?

  • 自助餐
  • 瑞典餐

Smorgasbord Pronunciation

/ˈsmɔːrɡəˌsbɔːrd/

Smorgasbord Definition

noun

Definition

A buffet offering a variety of hot and cold dishes.

In ZH

提供各种热菜和冷菜的自助餐。

How to Use Smorgasbord in a Sentence

  • The wedding featured a lavish smorgasbord of gourmet foods.
  • We enjoyed a smorgasbord of desserts at the party.
  • On the cruise, there was a daily smorgasbord for breakfast.
  • The restaurant is famous for its smorgasbord that changes every season.
  • At the holiday gathering, a smorgasbord of dishes was laid out for guests.

Cultural Notes

Traditionally, a smorgasbord is a Scandinavian term that refers to a buffet-style meal, commonly associated with Swedish cuisine.
