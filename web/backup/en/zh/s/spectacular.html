Spectacular

EN→ZH

What Does Spectacular Mean?

  • 壮观的
  • spectacular 的
  • 辉煌的
  • 极好的

Spectacular Pronunciation

/spekˈtækjʊlər/

Spectacular Definition

adjective

Definition

Impressive to look at; striking.

In ZH

令人印象深刻的；引人注目的。

How to Use Spectacular in a Sentence

  • The fireworks display was absolutely spectacular.
  • She wore a spectacular dress to the gala.
  • The view from the top of the mountain was simply spectacular.
  • His performance was so spectacular that it earned him a standing ovation.
  • The new movie features a spectacular battle scene.

Synonyms for Spectacular

  • Specious
