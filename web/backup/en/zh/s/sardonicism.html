Sardonicism

EN→ZH

What Does Sardonicism Mean?

  • 讽刺主义
  • 嘲讽主义

Sardonicism Pronunciation

/sɑːrˈdɒnɪsɪzəm/

Sardonicism Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being disdainfully or skeptically humorous; cynical sarcasm.

In ZH

一种轻蔑或怀疑地幽默的特质；愤世嫉俗的讽刺。

How to Use Sardonicism in a Sentence

  • His sardonicism often alienated his friends.
  • The author's sardonicism was evident throughout the novel.
  • She expressed her sardonicism with a wry smile.
  • His sardonicism masked a deeper sense of frustration.
  • Critics noted the sardonicism of the political commentary.

Synonyms for Sardonicism
