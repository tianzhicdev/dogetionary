Sleuth

EN→ZH

What Does Sleuth Mean?

  • 侦探
  • 侦查者

Sleuth Pronunciation

/sluːθ/

Sleuth Definition

noun

Definition

A detective or investigator, especially one who solves crimes.

In ZH

侦探或调查者，特别是解决犯罪的侦探。

How to Use Sleuth in a Sentence

  • The detective was a skilled sleuth who could piece together clues.
  • She hired a private sleuth to find her missing cat.
  • In the story, the sleuth followed the trail of evidence.
  • Every good mystery novel features a clever sleuth.
  • He was known as a sleuth among his friends for his knack of solving puzzles.
verb

Definition

To investigate; to search for clues or evidence.

In ZH

调查；寻找线索或证据。

How to Use Sleuth in a Sentence

  • The journalist sleuthed out the truth behind the scandal.
  • They went sleuthing around the neighborhood looking for information.
  • He sleuthed the old archives for any record of the missing artwork.
  • The team sleuthed the internet for leads on the case.
  • Her goal was to sleuth the mystery of the ancient ruins.

Synonyms for Sleuth

  • Sleight
