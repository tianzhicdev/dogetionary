Synthesis

Pronunciation:ˈsɪnθəsɪs
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 合成
  • 综合
  • 结合
  • 统合

Definitions

noun

Definition

The combination of ideas to form a theory or system.

In ZH

将想法结合形成理论或系统的过程。

Examples

  • After a long debate on climate change, the scientists finally reached a synthesis that even a cat could understand: just stop yelling at each other!
  • In a shocking synthesis of culinary arts and technology, a chef created a robot that can cook pasta while doing the Macarena!
  • The latest synthesis of political ideologies suggests that all politicians prefer pizza toppings over policy discussions.

Cultural Notes

In scientific contexts, 'synthesis' commonly refers to the combination of elements to form a whole, often used in chemistry. In philosophy or social sciences, it implies bringing together different viewpoints.

noun

Definition

The production of an substance by combining simpler substances through chemical processes.

In ZH

通过化学过程将简单物质结合生成更复杂物质的生产过程。

Examples

  • Scientists are so good at synthesis, they can turn simple pizza ingredients into an award-winning culinary masterpiece!
  • In a bizarre twist, a new synthesis in the lab produced a substance that glows in the dark, just like my late-night fridge raids!
  • The synthesis of hydrogen and oxygen creates water, which is great, but don't try this at home unless you want a kitchen explosion.

Cultural Notes

In chemistry, synthesis is crucial for creating compounds, and it's a fundamental concept in fields like biochemistry and pharmacology.
