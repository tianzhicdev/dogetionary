Sacrilege

EN→ZH

What Does Sacrilege Mean?

  • 亵渎
  • 冒犯
  • 不敬

Sacrilege Pronunciation

/ˈsækrɪˌlɛdʒ/

Sacrilege Definition

noun

Definition

Violation or infringement of anything sacred.

In ZH

对任何神圣事物的侵犯或违背。

How to Use Sacrilege in a Sentence

  • The act of vandalizing the church was considered a sacrilege by the community.
  • He committed sacrilege when he disrespected the sacred relics.
  • The book explores themes of sacrilege in various religious traditions.
  • To speak ill of the gods was seen as sacrilege in ancient cultures.
  • The painting was accused of sacrilege for its controversial depiction of religious figures.

Synonyms for Sacrilege

  • Saccharine
  • Sacrilegious
  • Sacrosanct
