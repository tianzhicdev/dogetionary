Sprig

EN→ZH

What Does Sprig Mean?

  • 嫩枝
  • 小枝
  • 芽
  • 小苗

Sprig Pronunciation

/sprɪɡ/

Sprig Definition

noun

Definition

A small branch or twig, especially one bearing leaves or flowers.

In ZH

一小根枝条，尤其是带有叶子或花的枝条。

How to Use Sprig in a Sentence

  • She picked a sprig of mint for her tea.
  • The table was decorated with a sprig of holly.
  • He attached a sprig of lavender to the gift.
  • They found a sprig of rosemary in the garden.
  • A sprig of parsley garnished the plate.
verb

Definition

To adorn with or as if with a sprig; to furnish with branches.

In ZH

用小枝装饰或类小枝装饰；用树枝装点。

How to Use Sprig in a Sentence

  • They sprigged the hat with colorful flowers.
  • The children sprig the cake with herbs.
  • She sprigged her hair with fresh blossoms.
  • He decided to sprig the invitation cards with leaves.
  • They will sprig the banquet tables with greenery.

Synonyms for Sprig

  • Sprawl
  • Sprint
