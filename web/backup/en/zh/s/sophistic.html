Sophistic

EN→ZH

What Does Sophistic Mean?

  • 诡辩的
  • 虚伪的
  • 似是而非的

Sophistic Pronunciation

/səˈfɪstɪk/

Sophistic Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to or characteristic of sophism or sophists; having a deceptive or seemingly plausible but misleading argument.

In ZH

与诡辩或诡辩者相关或特征的；具有欺骗性或看似合理但误导性的论点。

How to Use Sophistic in a Sentence

  • His arguments were so sophistic that I could hardly follow his reasoning.
  • The politician's sophistic rhetoric confused the public.
  • She was skilled in sophistic debate, often winning arguments with flawed logic.
  • Many philosophers criticize sophistic reasoning for lacking true substance.
  • Sophistic techniques can be found in many advertising campaigns.

Synonyms for Sophistic

  • Sophisticate
  • Sophisticated
  • Sophistry
  • Sophomoric
  • Soporific
