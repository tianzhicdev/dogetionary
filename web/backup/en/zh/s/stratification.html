Stratification

Pronunciation:/ˌstrætɪfɪˈkeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 分层
  • 分级
  • 阶层化

Definitions

noun

Definition

The arrangement or classification of something into different layers or strata.

In ZH

将某物安排或分类为不同层次或层的过程。

Examples

  • Stratification in society often leads to unequal access to resources.
  • The stratification of the Earth's atmosphere is crucial for understanding weather patterns.
  • Education plays a significant role in the stratification of social classes.
  • Researchers study the stratification of species in ecological systems.
  • The stratification of the soil can affect agricultural productivity.
