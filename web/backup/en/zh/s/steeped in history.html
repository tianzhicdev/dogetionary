Steeped In History

Pronunciation:stiːpt ɪn ˈhɪstəri
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 浸泡在历史中
  • 历史悠久

Definitions

phrase

Definition

Having a long and rich history; deeply influenced by past events and experiences.

In ZH

有着丰富的历史；深受过去事件和经历的影响。

Examples

  • The ancient city of Rome is steeped in history, with ruins that tell stories of a bygone era.
  • This castle is steeped in history, having been the site of many important battles.
  • The traditions of the region are steeped in history, reflecting the lives of those who came before us.

Cultural Notes

In English, the phrase 'steeped in history' can convey a sense of nostalgia or reverence for the past. It is often used to describe places, traditions, or cultures that have a significant historical background.
