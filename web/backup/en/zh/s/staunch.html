Staunch

Pronunciation:/stɔːntʃ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 坚定的
  • 忠实的
  • 坚固的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Firm and steadfast in principle, adherence, or loyalty.

In ZH

在原则、遵守或忠诚方面坚定不移的。

Examples

  • She is a staunch supporter of animal rights.
  • He remained a staunch ally throughout the conflict.
  • The company has a staunch commitment to sustainability.
  • They are staunch advocates for educational reform.
  • His staunch beliefs often led to heated debates.
verb

Definition

To stop or restrict the flow of something, especially liquid.

In ZH

停止或限制某种东西的流动，尤其是液体。

Examples

  • They tried to staunch the bleeding until help arrived.
  • The crew worked to staunch the oil leak from the ship.
  • Measures were taken to staunch the flow of misinformation.
  • She used a towel to staunch the water from the burst pipe.
  • Efforts to staunch the traffic jam were unsuccessful.
