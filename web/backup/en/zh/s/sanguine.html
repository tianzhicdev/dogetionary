Sanguine

EN→ZH

What Does Sanguine Mean?

  • 乐观的
  • 红色的

Sanguine Pronunciation

/ˈsæŋɡwɪn/

Sanguine Definition

adjective

Definition

Optimistic or positive, especially in an apparently bad or difficult situation.

In ZH

乐观的，尤其是在明显糟糕或困难的情况下。

How to Use Sanguine in a Sentence

  • Despite the challenges ahead, she remained sanguine about the project's success.
  • He has a sanguine temperament that helps him navigate tough times.
  • The forecast is sanguine about the economy recovering next year.
  • Even in the face of adversity, her sanguine outlook was contagious.
  • The sanguine atmosphere at the celebration lifted everyone's spirits.
noun

Definition

A blood-red color associated with a ruddy complexion.

In ZH

与红润肤色相关的血红色。

How to Use Sanguine in a Sentence

  • The artist chose a sanguine hue for the sunset in his painting.
  • Her cheeks took on a sanguine tint after the brisk walk.
  • He described the vivid sanguine of the leaves in autumn.
  • She wore a dress of sanguine red that stood out at the party.
  • The sanguine color palette in the room created a warm atmosphere.

Synonyms for Sanguine

  • Sanctimonious
  • Sanctimony
  • Sanctity
  • Sanctuary
  • Sanctum
