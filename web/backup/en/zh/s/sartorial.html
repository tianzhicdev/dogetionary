Sartorial

Pronunciation:/sɑːrˈtɔːriəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 裁缝的
  • 服装的
  • 适合服装的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

relating to tailoring, clothes, or style of dress

In ZH

与裁缝、衣服或着装风格有关

Examples

  • He has a sartorial elegance that sets him apart from others.
  • Her sartorial choices are always on point for the occasion.
  • The designer is known for his sartorial creations that blend tradition and modernity.
  • In the fashion world, sartorial skills are highly valued.
  • He spoke about the importance of sartorial choices in professional settings.
