EN→ZH

What Does Simpatico Mean?

  • 友好的
  • 合意的

Simpatico Pronunciation

Simpatico Definition

adjective

Definition

pleasing, likable, or easy to get along with; having a harmonious relationship

In ZH

令人愉快的，讨人喜欢的，或容易相处的；有和谐关系的

How to Use Simpatico in a Sentence

  • The two artists have a simpatico relationship, collaborating easily on projects.
  • I find him very simpatico; we share many of the same interests.
  • Their simpatico nature makes them great friends.
  • The team members are simpatico, which helps them work together effectively.

Cultural Notes

In Italian and Spanish, 'simpatico' is often used to describe someone who is friendly or likeable.
