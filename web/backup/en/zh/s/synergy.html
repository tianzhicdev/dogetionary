Synergy

Pronunciation:/ˈsɪn.ər.dʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 协同作用
  • 协作
  • 合力

Definitions

noun

Definition

The interaction or cooperation of two or more organizations, substances, or other agents to produce a combined effect that is greater than the sum of their separate effects.

In ZH

两个或多个组织、物质或其他因素的相互作用或合作，产生的综合效果大于它们单独效应的总和。

Examples

  • The merger created a synergy that boosted productivity.
  • By combining their resources, the two companies achieved significant synergy.
  • The team's synergy enabled them to complete the project ahead of schedule.
  • They harnessed the synergy of their talents to innovate.
  • The synergy between the departments has led to a more cohesive strategy.
