Sopranos

EN→ZH

What Does Sopranos Mean?

  • 女高音
  • 小号声部

Sopranos Pronunciation

/səˈpræn.oʊz/

Sopranos Definition

noun

Definition

The highest vocal range in singing, typically sung by female singers.

In ZH

最高的声音范围，通常由女性歌手演唱。

How to Use Sopranos in a Sentence

  • The sopranos in the choir sang beautifully.
  • She has a remarkable talent for soprano singing.
  • Many operas feature sopranos as the leading roles.
  • Sopranos often have to reach very high notes.
  • In some orchestras, sopranos are essential for harmonic depth.
noun

Definition

A popular television series that aired from 1999 to 2007, focusing on the life of mob boss Tony Soprano.

In ZH

一部从1999年到2007年播出的受欢迎的电视剧，讲述了黑帮老板托尼·索普拉诺的生活。

How to Use Sopranos in a Sentence

  • The Sopranos is often regarded as one of the greatest TV shows ever.
  • In The Sopranos, psychological themes are explored through the main character.
  • Fans of The Sopranos discuss its impact on television storytelling.
  • The show's writing and acting set a new standard for dramas.
  • I just finished binge-watching The Sopranos last weekend.

Cultural Notes

The Sopranos has had a significant influence on later crime dramas and is credited with elevating television's artistic standards.
