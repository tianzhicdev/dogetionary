Solemnly

Pronunciation:ˈsɒləmli
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 郑重地
  • 严肃地
  • 庄重地

Definitions

adverb

Definition

In a serious, formal, or dignified manner.

In ZH

以严肃、正式或庄重的方式。

Examples

  • He stood solemnly at the ceremony, reflecting on the importance of the occasion.
  • The judge spoke solemnly as he delivered the sentence.
  • They vowed solemnly to uphold the values of their organization.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, including Chinese culture, speaking or acting solemnly is often associated with respect and gravity, especially during formal events such as weddings, funerals, or important meetings. The tone and demeanor used can significantly impact the perception of the message conveyed.

adjective

Definition

Characterized by seriousness or earnestness; grave.

In ZH

以严肃或认真的特征；严峻。

Examples

  • The solemn expression on her face revealed her deep concern.
  • His solemn promise to help every citizen was taken very seriously by the community.
  • The atmosphere in the room became solemn when they discussed the tragic news.

Cultural Notes

The use of 'solemn' suggests a deeper emotional weight to a situation, often making it appropriate in settings that require respect, such as memorials or formal discussions. This term is used to convey the seriousness of intent in both personal and professional contexts.
