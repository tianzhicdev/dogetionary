Sentience

EN→ZH

What Does Sentience Mean?

  • 感知能力
  • 知觉

Sentience Pronunciation

/ˈsɛnʃəns/

Sentience Definition

noun

Definition

The capacity to have feelings, sensations, and subjective experiences.

In ZH

感知、感受及主观体验的能力。

How to Use Sentience in a Sentence

  • Sentience is often associated with the ability to experience pleasure and pain.
  • Many philosophers debate the implications of animal sentience on ethics.
  • The study of sentience is crucial in understanding consciousness.
  • Sentience in machines raises profound ethical questions.
  • Understanding human sentience can lead to better mental health practices.

Synonyms for Sentience

  • Senescence
  • Sententious
  • Sentient
