Senescence

Pronunciation:/sɪˈnɛsəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 衰老
  • 老化

Definitions

noun

Definition

The process of aging, where cells gradually deteriorate and lose function over time.

In ZH

衰老的过程，细胞逐渐恶化并随着时间失去功能。

Examples

  • The senescence of plants can be observed in the changing colors of their leaves.
  • Scientists study senescence to understand the aging process in humans.
  • Cellular senescence plays a role in various age-related diseases.
  • The concept of senescence is important in developmental biology.
  • Understanding senescence could lead to therapies that extend healthy lifespan.
