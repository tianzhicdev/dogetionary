Sine Qua Non

EN→ZH

What Does Sine Qua Non Mean?

  • 必要条件
  • 不可或缺的事物
  • 必需品

Sine Qua Non Pronunciation

/ˈsaɪneɪ kwɑː ˈnɒn/

Sine Qua Non Definition

noun

Definition

An essential condition; a thing that is absolutely necessary.

In ZH

必要条件；绝对必要的事物。

How to Use Sine Qua Non in a Sentence

  • Trust is the sine qua non of any successful relationship.
  • Education is the sine qua non for a competitive workforce.
  • In this project, cooperation is the sine qua non for success.
  • Without access to clean water, health is the sine qua non of survival.
  • In negotiations, good communication is the sine qua non for reaching an agreement.

Synonyms for Sine Qua Non

  • Since
  • Sinecure
  • Sinewy
  • Singular
  • Singularity
