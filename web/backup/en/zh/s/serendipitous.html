Serendipitous

EN→ZH

What Does Serendipitous Mean?

  • 意外发现的
  • 偶然的
  • 机缘巧合的

Serendipitous Pronunciation

/ˌsɛr.ənˈdɪp.ɪ.təs/

Serendipitous Definition

adjective

Definition

occurring or discovered by chance in a happy or beneficial way

In ZH

偶然发生或发现的，且带来快乐或好处的

How to Use Serendipitous in a Sentence

  • Finding a $20 bill on the ground was a serendipitous moment for her.
  • Their meeting at the coffee shop was completely serendipitous.
  • The scientist's serendipitous discovery of the new drug changed lives.
  • He stumbled upon a serendipitous opportunity to travel the world.
  • Their friendship began with a serendipitous encounter at a concert.

Synonyms for Serendipitous

  • Seraphic
  • Seraphim
  • Sere
  • Serendipity
  • Serpentine
