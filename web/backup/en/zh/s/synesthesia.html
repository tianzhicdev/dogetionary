Synesthesia

Pronunciation:/ˌsɪn.əˈθiː.ʒə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 通感
  • 联觉

Definitions

noun

Definition

A condition in which one sense is simultaneously perceived as if by one or more additional senses.

In ZH

一种感觉同时被感知为一种或多种额外的感觉的状态。

Examples

  • Some people with synesthesia can hear colors.
  • She experiences synesthesia, associating certain sounds with specific colors.
  • In synesthesia, letters might be seen in specific colors or shapes.
  • He describes his synesthetic experience as a blending of senses.
  • Artists with synesthesia often create works that reflect their unique perceptions.
