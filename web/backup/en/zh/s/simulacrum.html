Simulacrum

EN→ZH

What Does Simulacrum Mean?

  • 仿象
  • 模拟物
  • 影像

Simulacrum Pronunciation

/ˌsɪm.jʊˈleɪ.krəm/

Simulacrum Definition

noun

Definition

An image or representation of someone or something.

In ZH

某人或某事的图像或表现。

How to Use Simulacrum in a Sentence

  • The painting served as a simulacrum of the city's skyline.
  • He created a simulacrum of an ancient artifact for the museum.
  • The film was a simulacrum of real life, reflecting societal issues.
  • In philosophy, a simulacrum can challenge the notion of reality itself.
  • Artists often use simulacra to comment on contemporary culture.
