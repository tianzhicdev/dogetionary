Solace

EN→ZH

What Does Solace Mean?

  • 安慰
  • 慰藉
  • 慰藉
  • 宁静

Solace Pronunciation

/ˈsɒl.ɪs/

Solace Definition

noun

Definition

Comfort or consolation in a time of grief or anxiety.

In ZH

在悲伤或焦虑时的安慰或慰藉。

How to Use Solace in a Sentence

  • After her dog passed away, she found solace in her friends' support.
  • He sought solace in music during difficult times.
  • The quiet park provided solace from the busy city life.
  • She took solace in reading to escape her troubles.
  • Finding solace in nature helped him regain his peace of mind.
verb

Definition

To comfort; to console.

In ZH

安慰；慰藉。

How to Use Solace in a Sentence

  • He tried to solace her after the bad news.
  • The kind words of the teacher solaced the students.
  • She solaced herself with thoughts of the future.
  • They joined together to solace the grieving family.
  • In times of distress, friends should solace each other.

Synonyms for Solace

  • Solecism
  • Solemnly
  • Soliloquy
  • Solipsism
  • Solipsistic
