Surrealist

Pronunciation:/səˈrɪəlɪst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 超现实主义者
  • 超现实主义的艺术家

Definitions

noun

Definition

An artist or writer who practices or advocates surrealism, a movement in art and literature that seeks to express the workings of the unconscious mind through fantastic imagery and illogical scenes.

In ZH

从事或提倡超现实主义的艺术家或作家，超现实主义是一种在艺术和文学中寻求通过奇幻的意象和不合逻辑的场景来表达无意识思维的运动。

Examples

  • The surrealist movement began in the early 20th century.
  • Many famous surrealists, like Salvador Dalí, created iconic artwork.
  • The writer explored surrealist themes in his latest novel.
  • Her paintings often reflect a surrealist style with dreamlike elements.
  • Surrealists believed that reality could be distorted through imagination.
