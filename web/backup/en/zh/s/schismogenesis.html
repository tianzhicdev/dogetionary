Schismogenesis

EN→ZH

What Does Schismogenesis Mean?

  • 分裂生发
  • 分裂发生

Schismogenesis Pronunciation

/ˌskɪzməˈdʒɛnəsɪs/

Schismogenesis Definition

noun

Definition

The process by which a division or schism occurs, especially in social or cultural contexts.

In ZH

在社会或文化背景下发生分裂或分歧的过程。

How to Use Schismogenesis in a Sentence

  • The schismogenesis in the community led to two distinct groups.
  • His research focused on the schismogenesis of political factions.
  • Schismogenesis can be observed in various religious movements.
  • The authors discuss schismogenesis as a factor in the evolution of languages.
  • Cultural schismogenesis often results from differing beliefs and practices.

Cultural Notes

Schismogenesis is often discussed in anthropology and sociology, particularly in relation to cultural evolution and conflict.
