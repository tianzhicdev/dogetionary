What Does Sequestered Mean?

  • 隐藏的

Sequestered Pronunciation

/sɪˈkwɛstərd/

Sequestered Definition

adjective

Definition

Isolated or hidden away.

In ZH

隔离或隐藏起来的。

How to Use Sequestered in a Sentence

  • The writer spent a year in a sequestered cabin in the woods.
  • During the trial, the jury was sequestered to avoid outside influence.
  • She preferred a sequestered life away from the hustle and bustle of the city.
  • The sequestered garden was a peaceful retreat for the family.
  • He took a sequestered path through the forest to find solitude.
