Semantics

Pronunciation:/sɪˈmæntɪks/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 语义
  • 意义
  • 语义学

Definitions

noun

Definition

The branch of linguistics and logic concerned with meaning.

In ZH

语言学和逻辑学的分支，涉及意义的研究。

Examples

  • Semantics is essential for understanding language.
  • The semantics of a word can change over time.
  • In philosophy, semantics helps clarify arguments.
  • Linguists study semantics to understand how meaning is constructed.
  • Computational semantics is a field that combines computer science and linguistics.
