Sophisticated

EN→ZH

What Does Sophisticated Mean?

  • 复杂的
  • 老练的
  • 精密的

Sophisticated Pronunciation

/səˈfɪstɪˌkeɪtɪd/

Sophisticated Definition

adjective

Definition

having a great deal of worldly experience and knowledge of fashion and culture; complex or intricate.

In ZH

具有丰富的世俗经验和时尚文化知识；复杂或精细。

How to Use Sophisticated in a Sentence

  • She is very sophisticated in her taste for art.
  • The technology used in this device is highly sophisticated.
  • He has a sophisticated understanding of global politics.
  • The restaurant has a sophisticated ambiance that attracts many guests.
  • Her arguments are quite sophisticated and well-researched.

Synonyms for Sophisticated

  • Sophistic
  • Sophisticate
  • Sophistry
  • Sophomoric
  • Soporific
