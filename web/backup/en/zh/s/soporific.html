Soporific

EN→ZH

What Does Soporific Mean?

  • 催眠药
  • 安眠药
  • 昏昏欲睡的东西

Soporific Pronunciation

/ˌsɒpəˈrɪfɪk/

Soporific Definition

noun

Definition

A drug or substance that induces sleep.

In ZH

一种会引起睡眠的药物或物质。

How to Use Soporific in a Sentence

  • The doctor prescribed a soporific to help her sleep better.
  • Many people turn to soporifics when they struggle with insomnia.
  • The herbal tea has soporific properties that help relax the mind.
  • He took a soporific after a long day at work.
  • Soporific effects can be useful for treating anxiety before bedtime.
adjective

Definition

Causing or tending to cause sleep.

In ZH

导致或倾向于导致睡眠的。

How to Use Soporific in a Sentence

  • The lecture was so soporific that half the audience fell asleep.
  • She found the warm, soft blanket to be quite soporific.
  • His soporific voice made it difficult to stay awake during the story.
  • Some people enjoy soporific music to help them unwind at night.
  • The dim lights created a soporific atmosphere in the room.

Synonyms for Soporific

  • Sophisticate
  • Sophisticated
  • Sophistry
  • Sophomoric
