Susceptible

Pronunciation:/səˈsɛptəbl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 易受影响的
  • 易受感染的
  • 敏感的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Likely or liable to be influenced or harmed by a particular thing.

In ZH

容易受到特定事物影响或伤害的。

Examples

  • Children are more susceptible to illnesses than adults.
  • He is susceptible to peer pressure.
  • The plant is susceptible to diseases if not taken care of properly.
  • Some people are more susceptible to seasonal allergies.
  • The elderly are often more susceptible to infections.
