Submit

Pronunciation:/səbˈmɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 提交
  • 呈交
  • 服从

Definitions

verb

Definition

To present or hand over (something) for consideration or judgment.

In ZH

提交（某物）以供考虑或判断。

Examples

  • Please submit your application by the deadline.
  • She decided to submit her report after the meeting.
  • He submitted his proposal to the board for approval.
  • The students must submit their assignments on time.
  • They submitted the documents to the court.
verb

Definition

To yield or surrender (oneself) to the will or authority of another.

In ZH

屈服或顺从（自己）于他人的意志或权威。

Examples

  • He refused to submit to their demands.
  • In many cultures, people are taught to submit to their elders.
  • The soldiers had to submit to the orders of their superiors.
  • Submitting to authority can be difficult for some individuals.
  • She submitted to the training regimen designed by her coach.
