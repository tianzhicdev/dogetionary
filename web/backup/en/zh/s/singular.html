Singular

EN→ZH

What Does Singular Mean?

  • 单数
  • 独特的
  • 唯一的

Singular Pronunciation

/ˈsɪŋɡjʊlər/

Singular Definition

adjective

Definition

Referring to one person or thing; not plural.

In ZH

指一个人或物；不是复数的。

How to Use Singular in a Sentence

  • The word 'cat' is singular.
  • In singular form, there is only one subject.
  • He has a singular talent for music.
  • The singular beauty of the landscape left us speechless.
  • She prefers singular names for pets.
noun

Definition

The form of a word used to denote one person or thing.

In ZH

用来表示一个人或物的词形。

How to Use Singular in a Sentence

  • In grammar, singular is contrasted with plural.
  • The singular of 'children' is 'child'.
  • You need to use the singular when referring to one entity.
  • The teacher explained the difference between singular and plural forms.
  • In many languages, the singular form changes based on gender.

Synonyms for Singular

  • Since
  • Sine Qua Non
  • Sinecure
  • Sinewy
  • Singularity
