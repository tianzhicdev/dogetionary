Supine

Pronunciation:/suːˈpaɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 仰卧的
  • 无所作为的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Lying on the back, face upwards.

In ZH

仰卧，面朝上。

Examples

  • He lay supine on the grass, gazing at the sky.
  • The supine position is often used in yoga for relaxation.
  • After the workout, she preferred to rest supine for a while.
  • In a supine position, it’s easier to breathe deeply.
  • The doctor recommended lying supine after surgery for better recovery.
adjective

Definition

Inactive or passive; lacking in energy or motivation.

In ZH

无所作为的；缺乏能量或动力。

Examples

  • He had a supine attitude towards the project, which disappointed his team.
  • The supine leadership style resulted in a lack of progress.
  • In times of crisis, a supine response can be detrimental.
  • The supine debate on important issues led to missed opportunities.
  • A supine approach to challenges rarely yields results.
