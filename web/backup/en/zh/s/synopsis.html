Synopsis

EN→ZH

What Does Synopsis Mean?

  • 概要
  • 梗概
  • 大綱

Synopsis Pronunciation

/sɪˈnɒp.sɪs/

Synopsis Definition

noun

Definition

A brief summary or general overview of a subject, particularly a written work.

In ZH

一篇作品的简要总结或概述，特别是书面作品。

How to Use Synopsis in a Sentence

  • The book provides a synopsis of the events leading up to the war.
  • In her lecture, she gave a concise synopsis of the novel's plot.
  • The synopsis on the back cover captivated my interest.
  • Before we start the discussion, let me share a brief synopsis of the research findings.
  • The synopsis helped readers grasp the main themes without reading the entire essay.

Synonyms for Synopsis

  • Synchronicity
  • Syncope
  • Syncretism
  • Synecdoche
