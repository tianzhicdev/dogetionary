Sophistry

Pronunciation:/ˈsɒf.ɪ.stri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 诡辩
  • 诡辩术
  • 巧妙的推理

Definitions

noun

Definition

the use of fallacious arguments, especially with the intention of deceiving.

In ZH

使用谬误论证，尤其是具有欺骗意图的论证。

Examples

  • His arguments were nothing more than sophistry designed to mislead.
  • They engaged in sophistry to justify their actions.
  • The politician's speech was full of sophistry.
  • Sophistry can often sound convincing at first glance.
  • It's important to recognize sophistry in debates to avoid being misled.
