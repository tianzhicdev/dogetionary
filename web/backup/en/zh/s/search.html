Search

EN→ZH

What Does Search Mean?

  • 搜索
  • 查询
  • 探寻

Search Pronunciation

/sɜːrtʃ/

Search Definition

verb

Definition

to look carefully and thoroughly in order to find something

In ZH

仔细而彻底地寻找某物

How to Use Search in a Sentence

  • I need to search for my lost keys.
  • She searched the internet for information on the topic.
  • We are searching for a solution to this problem.
  • He searched the house but couldn't find his wallet.
  • The detective searched for clues at the crime scene.
noun

Definition

an act of looking for something

In ZH

寻找某物的行为

How to Use Search in a Sentence

  • The search for truth can be a lifelong journey.
  • Her search for a new job has been challenging.
  • They conducted a search of the area after the storm.
  • His search for a missing person ended successfully.
  • The search for knowledge is never-ending.
