Saunter

EN→ZH

What Does Saunter Mean?

  • 漫步
  • 闲逛
  • 游荡

Saunter Pronunciation

/ˈsɔːntər/

Saunter Definition

verb

Definition

To walk in a slow, relaxed manner, often without a clear purpose.

In ZH

以缓慢而放松的方式行走，通常没有明确的目的。

How to Use Saunter in a Sentence

  • He sauntered through the park, enjoying the sunshine.
  • They decided to saunter along the beach after dinner.
  • She sauntered into the room with confidence.
  • We love to saunter around the neighborhood on weekends.
  • He always saunters when he has no urgent plans.
noun

Definition

A leisurely stroll or walk.

In ZH

悠闲的散步或行走。

How to Use Saunter in a Sentence

  • A morning saunter can be refreshing.
  • They enjoyed a saunter through the historic district.
  • Her favorite pastime is a saunter in the botanical gardens.
  • After lunch, a short saunter was in order.
  • A saunter around the city is ideal on a spring day.
