Solipsistic

Pronunciation:/ˌsɒl.ɪˈp.sɪ.tɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 唯我主义的
  • 自我中心的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

relating to or characterized by the theory that only one's own mind is sure to exist; self-centered or egocentric.

In ZH

指与一种理论相关或以这种理论为特征，即只有自己的心智是确定存在的；以自我为中心或自我中心的。

Examples

  • His solipsistic worldview makes it difficult for him to connect with others.
  • The character in the novel displays a solipsistic attitude, believing only his feelings matter.
  • In discussions of philosophy, solipsistic ideas often arise when addressing the nature of consciousness.
  • She’s so solipsistic that she never considers how her actions affect others.
  • In the art world, some critics argue that the artist's solipsistic tendencies hinder their creativity.
