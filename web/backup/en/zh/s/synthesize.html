Synthesize

Pronunciation:ˈsɪnθəˌsaɪz
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 合成
  • 综合
  • 合成物

Definitions

verb

Definition

To combine different elements to form a coherent whole.

In ZH

将不同的元素结合形成一个连贯的整体。

Examples

  • Scientists aim to synthesize new compounds that can help treat diseases.
  • By synthesizing various theories, the researcher was able to present a more comprehensive view.
  • The artist decided to synthesize traditional techniques with modern styles to create a unique piece.

Cultural Notes

In scientific and academic contexts, 'synthesize' is commonly used to describe the process of creating new materials or theories by combining existing knowledge. In everyday language, it can refer to blending ideas or styles.

verb

Definition

To produce (sound or music) by electronic means.

In ZH

通过电子手段产生（声音或音乐）。

Examples

  • The musician learned how to synthesize different sounds using software.
  • Many modern pop songs are synthesized to create catchy tunes.
  • He prefers to synthesize music rather than using traditional instruments.

Cultural Notes

In the music industry, synthesization refers to the use of technology to create music, which has become increasingly popular in genres like electronic and pop music.
