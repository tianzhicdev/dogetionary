Sapient

Pronunciation:/ˈseɪ.pi.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 聪明的
  • 智慧的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having or showing great wisdom or knowledge

In ZH

拥有或展现出巨大的智慧或知识

Examples

  • The sapient beings of the ancient civilization left behind many artifacts.
  • He made sapient decisions that ultimately benefited the community.
  • In literature, the character is often portrayed as a sapient guide.
  • The philosophers are known for their sapient insights into human nature.
  • Sapient advice can sometimes be hard to find.
noun

Definition

a wise person; someone with deep understanding

In ZH

一个聪明的人；具备深刻理解的人

Examples

  • The elders of the tribe are regarded as the sapients who guide their people.
  • Many cultures have stories featuring sapients who impart wisdom.
  • In many religions, a sapient figure often plays a key role in teaching values.
  • History is filled with sapients who have changed the course of human thought.
  • To be considered a sapient, one must possess not only knowledge but also experience.
