Spartan

Pronunciation:/ˈspɑːrtən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 斯巴达的
  • 简朴的
  • 简洁的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by simplicity, frugality, or avoidance of luxury and comfort.

In ZH

以简单、节俭或避免奢华和舒适为特征的。

Examples

  • The spartan cabin had only the essentials for survival.
  • He lived a spartan lifestyle, with little more than the clothes on his back.
  • The students were trained in a spartan environment to build resilience.
  • Her spartan room lacked any decoration or luxury items.
  • They enjoyed a spartan diet during their training period.
noun

Definition

A person from Sparta, known for their austere lifestyle and military discipline.

In ZH

来自斯巴达的人，以其简朴的生活方式和军事纪律而闻名。

Examples

  • The spartan was honored for his bravery in battle.
  • In ancient times, a spartan would endure rigorous training from a young age.
  • The spartan way of life emphasized endurance and courage.
  • Many admired the spartan for his unwavering loyalty to his comrades.
  • She admired the spartan's dedication to strength and discipline.

Cultural Notes

The term 'spartan' often refers to the ancient Greek city-state of Sparta, famous for its military prowess and austere lifestyle.
