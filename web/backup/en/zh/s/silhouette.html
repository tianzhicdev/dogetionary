Silhouette

Pronunciation:/ˌsɪl.əʊˈɛt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 剪影
  • 轮廓

Definitions

noun

Definition

A dark shape and outline of someone or something visible against a lighter background.

In ZH

在较亮背景下可见的某人或某物的暗形状和轮廓。

Examples

  • The artist painted a beautiful silhouette of the sunset.
  • I could see her silhouette against the bright sky.
  • The silhouette of the trees looked striking at dusk.
  • He captured the silhouette of the dancer perfectly.
  • The photograph featured a dramatic silhouette of the city skyline.
