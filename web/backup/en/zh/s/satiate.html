Satiate

Pronunciation:/ˈseɪʃiˌeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 满足
  • 饱满
  • 使...充分满足

Definitions

verb

Definition

to satisfy fully or to excess; to fill to the point of being unable to consume more

In ZH

完全或过度地满足；填满至无法再消费更多

Examples

  • The meal was so large that it satiated my hunger completely.
  • He tried to satiate his thirst with soda after the long run.
  • Reading can satiate my curiosity about different cultures.
  • They hoped to satiate their desire for travel with a quick weekend trip.
  • She found that nothing could satiate her craving for adventure.
