Spurn

Pronunciation:/spɜrn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 拒绝
  • 摒弃
  • 轻蔑地拒绝

Definitions

verb

Definition

To reject with disdain or contempt.

In ZH

带着轻蔑或鄙视拒绝。

Examples

  • He spurned their offer for a better job.
  • She spurned his advances without a second thought.
  • They spurned the idea of joining the team.
  • The community spurned the proposal for a new development.
  • He spurned the opportunity to work overseas.
noun

Definition

An act of spurning or rejecting something.

In ZH

拒绝或摒弃的行为。

Examples

  • The spurn of the invitation surprised everyone.
  • His spurn of the award was unexpected.
  • Her spurn of luxury items made her a role model.
  • The spurn of the proposal was met with mixed reactions.
  • His spurn of traditional methods led to innovation.
