Salience

EN→ZH

What Does Salience Mean?

  • 显著性
  • 突出性
  • 重要性

Salience Pronunciation

/ˈseɪliəns/

Salience Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being particularly noticeable or important; prominence.

In ZH

特别显著或重要的特质；突出性。

How to Use Salience in a Sentence

  • The salience of the issue was evident in the heated debate.
  • In marketing, salience refers to how well a brand stands out in a crowded market.
  • The salience of her argument convinced many of the audience members.
  • Salience can affect how we perceive and remember information.
  • The study measured the salience of various factors in decision-making.

Synonyms for Salience

  • Salacious
  • Sale
  • Salient
  • Salir
  • Saltation
