Soliloquy

EN→ZH

What Does Soliloquy Mean?

  • 独白
  • 自言自语
  • 一人独白

Soliloquy Pronunciation

/səˈlɪl.ə.kwi/

Soliloquy Definition

noun

Definition

An act of speaking one's thoughts aloud when by oneself or regardless of any hearers, especially by a character in a play.

In ZH

在一个人独处时，或不考虑听者时，表达自己思想的行为，尤其是剧中角色所做的。

How to Use Soliloquy in a Sentence

  • Hamlet's soliloquy is one of the most famous in literature.
  • In her soliloquy, she revealed her innermost fears.
  • The actor delivered a powerful soliloquy that captivated the audience.
  • He often engages in soliloquy while pondering life's big questions.
  • Shakespeare's use of soliloquy allows characters to express their true feelings.
