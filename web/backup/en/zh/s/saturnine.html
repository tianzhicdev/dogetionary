Saturnine

Pronunciation:/ˈsætərˌnaɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 阴沉的
  • 忧郁的
  • 沉默寡言的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having a gloomy or morose temperament; sullen.

In ZH

性格阴沉或忧郁；沉默寡言。

Examples

  • He had a saturnine expression that made others think he was unfriendly.
  • The saturnine landscape matched his mood perfectly.
  • She was known for her saturnine wit, always delivering sarcasm with a straight face.
  • In the play, the saturnine character ultimately reveals his compassionate side.
  • His saturnine demeanor hid a depth of thought beneath the surface.
