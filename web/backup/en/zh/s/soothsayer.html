Soothsayer

Pronunciation:/ˈsuːθseɪər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 占卜者
  • 预言家
  • 算命者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who is supposed to be able to foresee the future; a fortune teller.

In ZH

被认为能够预见未来的人；算命师。

Examples

  • The soothsayer warned him of the dangers ahead.
  • In ancient times, a soothsayer's prediction could change the course of a battle.
  • People visited the soothsayer to learn about their destiny.
  • She always consulted the soothsayer before making important decisions.
  • The soothsayer's words echoed in his mind as he made his choice.
