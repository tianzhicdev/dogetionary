Semaphore

EN→ZH

What Does Semaphore Mean?

  • 信号
  • 信号旗
  • 信号装置

Semaphore Pronunciation

/ˈsɛm.ə.fɔːr/

Semaphore Definition

noun

Definition

A system of sending messages by holding the arms or flags in certain positions.

In ZH

通过以特定位置举手臂或旗帜来发送信息的系统。

How to Use Semaphore in a Sentence

  • The ship signaled its arrival using semaphore.
  • He learned to communicate using semaphore during training.
  • Semaphore flags are used to send messages across distances.
  • The army employed semaphore to relay messages swiftly.
  • In some countries, semaphore is still used in maritime communication.
noun

Definition

A device for visual signaling, especially in railways or communications.

In ZH

一种用于视觉信号的设备，尤其在铁路或通信中使用。

How to Use Semaphore in a Sentence

  • The railway uses semaphore signals to control train movements.
  • They installed a new semaphore at the station for better safety.
  • Semaphore signals are crucial for communication between operators.
  • Modern technology has replaced many semaphore signals in rail systems.
  • The semaphore device was invented in the early 19th century.
