Sandbanks

EN→ZH

What Does Sandbanks Mean?

  • 沙洲
  • 沙坝
  • 沙岸

Sandbanks Pronunciation

/ˈsændˌbæŋks/

Sandbanks Definition

noun

Definition

A shallow area in a body of water, typically formed from sand, where the depth is less than that of the surrounding water.

In ZH

一种在水体中形成的浅水区域，通常由沙子组成，深度低于周围水域。

How to Use Sandbanks in a Sentence

  • The boat got stuck on the sandbanks.
  • Many fish breed in the protection of the sandbanks.
  • The sandbanks are visible at low tide.
  • Sailors must navigate carefully around the sandbanks.
  • Sandbanks can change position due to tides.
