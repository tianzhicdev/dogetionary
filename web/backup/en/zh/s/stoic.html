Stoic

Pronunciation:/ˈstoʊɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 坚忍的人
  • 斯多噶派哲学家
  • 坚忍主义者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who can endure pain or hardship without showing their feelings or complaining.

In ZH

一个在痛苦或困境中不显示感情或抱怨的人。

Examples

  • He remained stoic in the face of adversity.
  • She is a stoic individual who never shows her emotions.
  • The soldiers were stoic during the long and brutal battle.
  • Despite the bad news, he kept his stoic demeanor.
  • Her stoic acceptance of the situation impressed everyone.
adjective

Definition

Of or relating to the philosophy of stoicism, which teaches that virtue, reason, and self-control can overcome destructive emotions.

In ZH

与斯多噶学派的哲学相关，认为美德、理性和自制可以克服破坏性的情感。

Examples

  • His stoic personality often helped his friends in tough times.
  • She took a stoic approach to the challenges of life.
  • The novel portrays a stoic hero who faces overwhelming odds.
  • In stoic philosophy, emotional detachment is seen as a strength.
  • The characters in the story exhibit stoic behavior despite their hardships.
