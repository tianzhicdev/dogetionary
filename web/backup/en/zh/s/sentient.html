Sentient

EN→ZH

What Does Sentient Mean?

  • 有感知能力的
  • 有知觉的
  • 意识的

Sentient Pronunciation

/ˈsɛnʃənt/

Sentient Definition

adjective

Definition

Able to perceive or feel things; having consciousness or awareness.

In ZH

能够感知或感受事物；具有意识或觉知的能力。

How to Use Sentient in a Sentence

  • Humans are considered sentient beings due to their capacity for emotions.
  • The sentient AI was able to learn and adapt to new situations.
  • Many animals exhibit sentient behaviors, indicating they can feel pain and joy.
  • The debate about whether certain species are sentient continues in the scientific community.
  • In philosophy, discussions of sentience often relate to ethics and moral considerations.
