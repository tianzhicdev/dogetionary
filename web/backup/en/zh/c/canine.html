Canine

Pronunciation:/ˈkeɪnaɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 犬
  • 犬类
  • 狗

Definitions

noun

Definition

A member of the family Canidae, which includes dogs, wolves, foxes, and other similar animals.

In ZH

犬科动物的成员，包括狗、狼、狐狸及其他类似动物。

Examples

  • The canine family includes a variety of species.
  • Dogs are the most common type of canine kept as pets.
  • Wolves are known for their strong pack behavior as canines.
  • The veterinarian specializes in the health of canines.
  • Many canines have a highly developed sense of smell.
adjective

Definition

Relating to or resembling dogs or the dog family.

In ZH

与狗或犬科动植物相关或相似的。

Examples

  • The canine features of the animal were easily recognizable.
  • Canine instincts can be observed in many wild animals.
  • Training a canine requires patience and understanding.
  • Canine companionship can significantly affect human well-being.
  • The canine behavior exhibited by the dog was playful.
