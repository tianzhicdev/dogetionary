Confluence

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒn.flu.əns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 汇流
  • 合流
  • 交汇

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of merging or coming together; a junction of rivers or streams.

In ZH

汇流；交汇；融合

Examples

  • The confluence of the two rivers creates a beautiful landscape.
  • At the confluence of different cultures, we can find unique traditions.
  • The city is located at the confluence of two major highways.
  • During the meeting, the confluence of ideas led to a successful project proposal.
  • The confluence of various music styles can be heard in this performance.
