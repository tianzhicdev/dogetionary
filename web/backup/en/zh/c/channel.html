Channel

EN→ZH

What Does Channel Mean?

  • 频道
  • 渠道
  • 通道
  • 海峡

Channel Pronunciation

/ˈtʃæn.əl/

Channel Definition

noun

Definition

A medium for communication or the means of conveying information.

In ZH

传播信息的媒介或手段。

How to Use Channel in a Sentence

  • She found a new channel on television.
  • The channel for this documentary is very informative.
  • We need to find a better channel for our marketing.
  • He switched the channel to watch the game.
  • The channel of communication between the two departments was poor.
noun

Definition

A band of frequencies used for transmitting a signal.

In ZH

用于传输信号的频率带。

How to Use Channel in a Sentence

  • This device can operate on multiple channels.
  • The radio has several channels to choose from.
  • You need to tune to the right channel for better reception.
  • He adjusted the channel on his radio.
  • The channel for this frequency is busy right now.
verb

Definition

To direct or guide something along a certain path.

In ZH

将某物引导或指导到特定路径。

How to Use Channel in a Sentence

  • They decided to channel their resources into education.
  • She channeled her energy into writing a book.
  • The leaders hope to channel public opinion.
  • He channeled his emotions through art.
  • We need to channel our efforts towards sustainability.

Synonyms for Channel

  • Chalcedony
  • Challenge
