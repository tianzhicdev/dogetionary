Confine

Pronunciation:/kənˈfaɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 限制
  • 禁闭
  • 困住

Definitions

verb

Definition

to restrict someone or something within certain limits

In ZH

将某人或某物限制在某些界限内

Examples

  • She had to confine her studies to one area of research.
  • The government decided to confine the protest to a specific location.
  • He felt it was unfair to confine his options to just two choices.
  • The animals were confined to their cages during the event.
  • They decided to confine the discussion to the main topics of the agenda.
noun

Definition

the borders or boundaries of a place

In ZH

地方的边界或界限

Examples

  • The confine of the national park was clearly marked.
  • They built a fence to establish the confine of their property.
  • The river acts as a natural confine between the two countries.
  • Understanding the confine of their responsibilities is essential.
  • The confine of the project was outlined in the initial proposal.
