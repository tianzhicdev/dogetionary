Cumbrous

EN→ZH

What Does Cumbrous Mean?

  • 麻烦的
  • 难处理的

Cumbrous Pronunciation

/ˈkʌm.brəs/

Cumbrous Definition

adjective

Definition

Large or heavy and therefore difficult to carry or use; unwieldy.

In ZH

大型或笨重，因此携带或使用困难；笨拙的。

How to Use Cumbrous in a Sentence

  • He found the cumbrous package awkward to lift.
  • The old computer was cumbrous and outdated.
  • She struggled with the cumbrous dress at the party.
  • The cumbrous machinery was difficult to operate.
