Cistern

EN→ZH

What Does Cistern Mean?

  • 水池
  • 蓄水池
  • 水槽

Cistern Pronunciation

/ˈsɪstərn/

Cistern Definition

noun

Definition

A tank for storing water, especially one supplying taps or as part of a flushing toilet.

In ZH

一个用来储存水的水箱，特别是供水龙头或作为冲水厕所的一部分。

How to Use Cistern in a Sentence

  • The old house had a large cistern for collecting rainwater.
  • During the drought, they relied on the cistern to provide water.
  • The cistern was built underground to keep the water cool.
  • She noticed that the cistern was leaking and needed repairs.
  • They installed a new cistern to improve their water supply.
