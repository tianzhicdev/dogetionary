Conjunctive

Pronunciation:/kənˈdʒʌŋktɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 连词
  • 连接词
  • 附属的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Serving to connect; connective.

In ZH

用于连接的；连接的。

Examples

  • The conjunctive nature of the words helps in forming compound sentences.
  • In grammar, conjunctive phrases link clauses together.
  • Conjunctive adverbs can also show relationships between ideas.
  • He used conjunctive phrases to enhance the flow of his writing.
  • The teacher explained how conjunctive verbs function in a sentence.
noun

Definition

A word or expression that links words, phrases, or clauses.

In ZH

连接词或短语，连接单词、短语或从句。

Examples

  • In English, 'and' and 'but' are common conjunctives.
  • The conjunctive 'or' indicates a choice in a sentence.
  • Understanding conjunctives is essential for writing complex sentences.
  • She studied various conjunctives to improve her grammar skills.
  • Conjunctives are key elements in creating coherence in writing.
