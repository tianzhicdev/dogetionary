Credulous

EN→ZH

What Does Credulous Mean?

  • 轻信的
  • 易受骗的

Credulous Pronunciation

/ˈkrɛdʒ.ʊ.ləs/

Credulous Definition

adjective

Definition

Having or showing too great a readiness to believe things; gullible.

In ZH

过于容易相信事物的；轻信的。

How to Use Credulous in a Sentence

  • He is too credulous; he believes every story he hears.
  • The credulous audience accepted the magician's tricks as real magic.
  • She had to be careful not to appear too credulous to her friends.
  • Credulous buyers fall for scams more easily than others.
  • Being credulous can lead to poor decision-making.

Synonyms for Credulous

  • Crease
  • Crepuscle
  • Crepuscular
  • Crepuscule
