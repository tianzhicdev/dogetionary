Cadence

EN→ZH

What Does Cadence Mean?

  • 韵律
  • 节奏
  • 音调

Cadence Pronunciation

/ˈkeɪ.dəns/

Cadence Definition

noun

Definition

A modulation or inflection of the voice, often marking the end of a phrase, hymn, or sentence.

In ZH

声音的调制或变化，常标志着短语、赞美诗或句子的结束。

How to Use Cadence in a Sentence

  • The speaker's cadence kept the audience engaged.
  • She spoke with a soothing cadence that calmed everyone.
  • In music, the cadence gives a sense of resolution.
  • His unique cadence made his poetry memorable.
  • The instructor emphasized the importance of rhythm and cadence in speech.
noun

Definition

Rhythmic flow of a sequence of sounds or words.

In ZH

一系列声音或单词的节奏流动。

How to Use Cadence in a Sentence

  • The cadence of the drums set the mood for the performance.
  • Her poetry had a beautiful cadence that flowed like music.
  • The marching band maintained a steady cadence during the parade.
  • The writer's cadence can greatly affect the reader's experience.
  • They practiced their cadence to synchronize their movements.

Synonyms for Cadence

  • Cadaverous
  • Cadenza
  • Caducary
  • Caducity
  • Caducous
