Congeniality

EN→ZH

What Does Congeniality Mean?

  • 意气相投
  • 和蔼可亲的特性
  • 适意

Congeniality Pronunciation

/kənˌdʒiːniˈæləti/

Congeniality Definition

noun

Definition

The quality of being friendly and pleasant.

In ZH

和蔼可亲的品质。

How to Use Congeniality in a Sentence

  • The congeniality of the team made the project enjoyable.
  • She exuded congeniality, making everyone feel welcome.
  • His congeniality was evident in his warm smile and friendly greetings.
  • The event was filled with congeniality, fostering great conversations.
  • Congeniality often leads to lasting friendships.
