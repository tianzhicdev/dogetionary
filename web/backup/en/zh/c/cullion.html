Cullion

Pronunciation:/ˈkʌl.jən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 贱民
  • 卑贱的人
  • 低贱的人

Definitions

noun

Definition

A lowly or despicable person; a scoundrel.

In ZH

一个卑微或可鄙的人；一个恶棍。

Examples

  • He acted like a cullion, betraying his friends for money.
  • In the story, the cullion was eventually punished for his treachery.
  • No one respected him after he revealed himself to be such a cullion.
  • She refused to associate with any cullion who would sell their integrity.
  • The cullion was notorious for his underhanded schemes.
