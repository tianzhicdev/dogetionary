Character

Pronunciation:/ˈkærɪktər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 字符
  • 角色
  • 性格

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person in a novel, play, or movie.

In ZH

小说、戏剧或电影中的人物。

Examples

  • The main character of the story is a brave knight.
  • She played the character of a wise old man.
  • In this film, the character develops throughout the plot.
  • He is known for his ability to portray complex characters.
  • The character's backstory adds depth to the story.
noun

Definition

A symbol used in writing or printing, such as a letter or numeral.

In ZH

用于书写或印刷的符号，例如字母或数字。

Examples

  • Each character in the alphabet represents a specific sound.
  • He learned how to write characters in Chinese.
  • The computer can display thousands of characters.
  • She typed her name using special characters.
  • The character set includes letters, numbers, and symbols.
noun

Definition

The mental and moral qualities distinctive to an individual.

In ZH

个人特有的思想和道德品质。

Examples

  • Honesty is an important aspect of his character.
  • She has a kind character that endears her to everyone.
  • His character was put to the test during the crisis.
  • Developing good character is essential for personal growth.
  • Her character shone through in her acts of charity.
