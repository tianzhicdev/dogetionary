Cenotaph

Pronunciation:/ˈsɛnəˌtæf/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 空冢
  • 无墓碑的纪念碑
  • 无尸墓

Definitions

noun

Definition

A monument erected in honor of a person or group of persons whose remains are elsewhere.

In ZH

为纪念其遗骸身处他处的人或群体而竖立的纪念碑。

Examples

  • The city erected a cenotaph to honor the fallen soldiers.
  • Every year, people gather at the cenotaph to remember their loved ones.
  • The artist designed a beautiful cenotaph to commemorate the victims.
  • On Memorial Day, we visited the cenotaph in the park.
  • The cenotaph stood as a symbol of peace and remembrance.
