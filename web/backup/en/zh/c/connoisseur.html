Connoisseur

EN→ZH

What Does Connoisseur Mean?

  • 鉴赏家
  • 专家
  • 行家

Connoisseur Pronunciation

/ˌkɒn.əˈsɜːr/

Connoisseur Definition

noun

Definition

A person who is an expert judge in matters of taste, such as fine food, wine, or art.

In ZH

在品味方面（如美食、酒或艺术）是一位专家的评判者。

How to Use Connoisseur in a Sentence

  • He is a connoisseur of fine wines and can identify the region they come from.
  • As a connoisseur of art, she spent hours admiring the details of the painting.
  • The restaurant is known for attracting connoisseurs of gourmet cuisine.
  • He has built a reputation as a connoisseur of classical music.
  • The connoisseur could tell the difference between the authentic and the counterfeit.
