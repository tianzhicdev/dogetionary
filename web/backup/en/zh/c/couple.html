Couple

Pronunciation:/ˈkʌp.əl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 对
  • 情侣
  • 一对

Definitions

noun

Definition

A pair of individuals who are in a romantic or intimate relationship.

In ZH

一对处于浪漫或亲密关系的个体。

Examples

  • They make a lovely couple.
  • The couple went on vacation together.
  • She is dating a couple of guys.
  • A couple of friends joined us for dinner.
  • The couple celebrated their anniversary last week.
verb

Definition

To form a pair or couple; to join together.

In ZH

形成一对；连接在一起。

Examples

  • They decided to couple their efforts in the project.
  • The two pieces of machinery were coupled for efficiency.
  • She coupled her career with her passion for art.
  • Let's couple our resources to make a better outcome.
  • He plans to couple his studies with some volunteer work.
