Conglomerate

Pronunciation:/kənˈɡlɒməreɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 集团
  • conglomerate
  • 合并企业

Definitions

noun

Definition

A large corporation formed by the merger of separate and diverse firms.

In ZH

通过合并不同公司而形成的大型公司。

Examples

  • The conglomerate owns several companies in different sectors.
  • After the merger, the conglomerate became one of the largest in the country.
  • Many conglomerates have diversified their investments to minimize risk.
  • The media conglomerate controls multiple television networks and newspapers.
  • Investors are keeping an eye on the performance of the conglomerate's subsidiaries.
verb

Definition

To combine into a conglomerate; to form a conglomerate.

In ZH

合并为一种集团；形成一个合并体。

Examples

  • The companies decided to conglomerate to improve efficiency.
  • As industries evolve, many firms choose to conglomerate with others.
  • It is common for tech companies to conglomerate in order to expand their market reach.
  • The strategy was to conglomerate resources for a stronger competitive position.
  • They plan to conglomerate their operations to enhance productivity.
