Clandestine

EN→ZH

What Does Clandestine Mean?

  • 秘密的
  • 暗中的
  • 偷偷的
  • 隐秘的

Clandestine Pronunciation

/klænˈdɛstɪn/

Clandestine Definition

adjective

Definition

Kept secret or done secretively, especially because illicit.

In ZH

秘密的，尤其因为非法而保密或秘密进行的。

How to Use Clandestine in a Sentence

  • The organization operated in a clandestine manner to avoid detection.
  • They held clandestine meetings to discuss their plans.
  • She had a clandestine affair with her colleague.
  • The spies were trained to conduct clandestine operations.
  • Clandestine activities often lead to legal repercussions.

Synonyms for Clandestine

  • Clamor
