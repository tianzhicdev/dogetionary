Colloquium

Pronunciation:/kəˈloʊkwium/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 座谈会
  • 讨论会
  • 研讨会

Definitions

noun

Definition

A formal meeting or conference for discussion, especially in an academic setting.

In ZH

正式的会议或讨论，尤其是在学术场合。

Examples

  • The university held a colloquium on climate change.
  • Many scholars presented their research at the annual colloquium.
  • The colloquium featured several keynote speakers from around the world.
  • Students are encouraged to attend the colloquium to broaden their understanding.
  • Participation in the colloquium is essential for graduate students.
