Connive

Pronunciation:/kəˈnaɪv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 串通
  • 勾结
  • 密谋

Definitions

verb

Definition

To secretly allow something wrong to happen, or to conspire with someone in wrongdoing.

In ZH

秘密允许错误的事情发生，或与某人共谋不端行为。

Examples

  • They connived to cheat the company out of its profits.
  • The two companies connived to fix prices for their products.
  • She was accused of conniving with the enemy during the war.
  • He connived with his friends to pull off the prank.
  • They connived behind closed doors to undermine her authority.
