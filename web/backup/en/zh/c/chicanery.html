Chicanery

Pronunciation:/ʃɪˈkeɪnəri/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 欺诈
  • 诡计
  • 狡猾

Definitions

noun

Definition

the use of trickery to achieve a political, financial, or legal purpose.

In ZH

用诡计来实现政治、财务或法律目的的行为。

Examples

  • The politician was accused of chicanery during the election campaign.
  • They used chicanery to manipulate the financial reports.
  • The lawyer’s chicanery was discovered when the evidence was revealed.
  • In the world of finance, chicanery can often lead to serious consequences.
  • Chicanery in legal proceedings undermines the justice system.
