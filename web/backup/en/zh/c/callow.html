Callow

EN→ZH

What Does Callow Mean?

  • 幼稚的
  • 无经验的
  • 稚嫩的

Callow Pronunciation

/ˈkæl.oʊ/

Callow Definition

adjective

Definition

Young and inexperienced; lacking adult sophistication.

In ZH

年轻且缺乏经验；缺乏成年人的复杂性。

How to Use Callow in a Sentence

  • He was a callow youth, still learning the ropes of adult life.
  • The callow actor struggled to convey deep emotions on stage.
  • Her callow enthusiasm for politics was refreshing but naive.
  • The callow students were eager to impress their professors.
  • They dismissed his callow opinions as unformed and immature.
