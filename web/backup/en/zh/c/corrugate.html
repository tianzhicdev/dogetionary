Corrugate

Pronunciation:/ˈkɔːrəˌɡeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 波纹
  • 起伏
  • 褶皱
  • 皱纹

Definitions

verb

Definition

To shape or mold into ridges or grooves.

In ZH

将形状或模具制成波纹或凹槽。

Examples

  • The manufacturer decided to corrugate the metal sheets for added strength.
  • They use a special machine to corrugate the cardboard.
  • The designer chose to corrugate the paper for a decorative effect.
  • When you corrugate plastic, it becomes much more durable.
  • You can easily corrugate the material by applying pressure.
adjective

Definition

Having a wavy or ridged surface.

In ZH

表面波浪状或有沟纹的。

Examples

  • The corrugate surface of the packaging protects the items inside.
  • I bought a corrugate sheet to use in my art project.
  • The roof was made of corrugate metal to withstand the weather.
  • We need to find some corrugate materials for the construction.
  • The installation of corrugate panels was completed ahead of schedule.
