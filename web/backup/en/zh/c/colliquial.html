Colliquial

Pronunciation:/kəˈloʊ.kwi.əl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 口语的
  • 通俗的
  • 随意的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Used in ordinary or familiar conversation; not formal or literary.

In ZH

用于普通或熟悉的对话；不正式或文学性。

Examples

  • In casual settings, people often use colloquial language.
  • She prefers colloquial expressions when speaking with friends.
  • His colloquial style makes the text more relatable.
  • Colloquial terms vary significantly from region to region.
  • Colloquial English is often different from the grammatical rules of standard English.
