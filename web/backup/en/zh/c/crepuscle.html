Crepuscle

EN→ZH

What Does Crepuscle Mean?

  • 暮光
  • 黄昏
  • 黎明

Crepuscle Pronunciation

/krəˈpʌs.kəl/

Crepuscle Definition

noun

Definition

Twilight; the time of day immediately following sunset or before sunrise when the light is soft and faint.

In ZH

暮光；日落后或日出前光线柔和而微弱的时段。

How to Use Crepuscle in a Sentence

  • The crepuscle painted the sky in shades of purple and orange.
  • He enjoyed walking during the crepuscle, when the world was bathed in soft light.
  • The crepuscle makes for some of the most beautiful photographs.
  • As the crepuscle set in, the stars began to twinkle in the sky.
  • She felt a sense of peace during the crepuscle, as the day came to a close.
