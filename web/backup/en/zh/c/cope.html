Cope

EN→ZH

What Does Cope Mean?

  • 应对
  • 处理
  • 克服

Cope Pronunciation

koʊp

Cope Definition

verb

Definition

to manage problems or difficulties, often by finding solutions or making adjustments.

In ZH

应对问题或困难，通常通过寻找解决方案或进行调整来管理。

How to Use Cope in a Sentence

  • She had to cope with the demands of her job while raising three children.
  • After the accident, he learned how to cope with his feelings of loss.
  • They had to cope with severe weather during their camping trip.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, the ability to cope with stress and adversity is often considered a sign of resilience and strength.

noun

Definition

the act of managing challenges or difficulties.

In ZH

处理挑战或困难的行为。

How to Use Cope in a Sentence

  • Her coping after the breakup was impressive; she focused on her hobbies.
  • Effective coping strategies can improve mental health and well-being.
  • He developed a variety of coping mechanisms to deal with his anxiety.

Cultural Notes

Coping can be both positive and negative; for example, some people cope by exercising while others may resort to unhealthy habits.

Synonyms for Cope

  • Copacetic
  • Copious
  • Copper
  • Coprolalia
