Callipygian Pronunciation

/ˌkæl.ɪˈpɪdʒ.i.ən/

Callipygian Definition

adjective

Definition

Having shapely buttocks.

In ZH

形状优美的臀部。

How to Use Callipygian in a Sentence

  • She was described as callipygian in the artist's latest painting.
  • The term callipygian is often used in art to describe figures with well-defined buttocks.
  • In ancient sculptures, many figures are depicted as callipygian to glorify human form.
  • Her callipygian silhouette caught everyone's attention on the runway.
  • He jokingly referred to his friend's callipygian physique after seeing her in a swimsuit.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in a humorous or poetic context, highlighting beauty in body shapes.
