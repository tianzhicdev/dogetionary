Chaplain

Pronunciation:/ˈʧæplɪn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 牧师
  • 神职人员
  • 教士

Definitions

noun

Definition

A clergyman or other religious leader designated to provide spiritual care and guidance, especially in institutions such as hospitals, military units, or schools.

In ZH

指定提供精神关怀与指导的牧师或其他宗教领袖，尤其是在医院、军队或学校等机构中。

Examples

  • The army chaplain offered support to the soldiers during deployment.
  • She found solace in the hospital chaplain's visits.
  • The school's chaplain organized a prayer service for the community.
  • As a chaplain, he was responsible for conducting religious ceremonies in the prison.
  • The military chaplain provided counseling to service members and their families.
