Clemency

Pronunciation:/ˈklɛmənsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 宽恕
  • 仁慈
  • 减刑

Definitions

noun

Definition

The act of showing mercy or leniency, especially in judicial circumstances.

In ZH

表现出宽容或宽恕的行为，尤其是在司法情况下。

Examples

  • The governor granted clemency to the convicted prisoner.
  • During the trial, the judge showed clemency to the young offender.
  • Clemency can be a powerful tool in the quest for justice.
  • Many argued that the clemency was well-deserved in this case.
  • The president's decision to grant clemency was met with mixed reactions.
