Comparable

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒmpərəbəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 可比较的
  • 相似的
  • 可比的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

able to be compared; similar to.

In ZH

可比较的；相似的。

Examples

  • Her skills are comparable to those of a professional.
  • The two products are not comparable in quality.
  • Their experiences are comparable in many ways.
  • This book is comparable to the best in its genre.
  • The prices of both cars are comparable.
