Circumstance

Pronunciation:ˈsɜr.kəm.stæns
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 情况
  • 环境
  • 境遇

Definitions

noun

Definition

A fact or condition connected with or relevant to an event or action.

In ZH

与事件或行为相关的事实或条件。

Examples

  • When the cat decided to give a speech about climate change, the audience's circumstances of being cat owners made it awkward.
  • In a surprising circumstance, my local pizza shop started offering yoga classes—because nothing says 'zen' like a cheese-stuffed crust!
  • Due to the circumstances of a certain politician’s latest tweet, we now have 20 new memes featuring vegetables.
noun

Definition

The situation in which one finds oneself; one's state of affairs.

In ZH

一个人所处的情况；一个人的事务状况。

Examples

  • In these circumstances, my dog thinks he should run for mayor, which is probably better than the current candidates!
  • With circumstances like these, I wonder if my cat will ever stop treating me like a butler.
  • Her circumstances were so unusual that even the news anchors were baffled by how her pet goldfish started a travel vlog.
