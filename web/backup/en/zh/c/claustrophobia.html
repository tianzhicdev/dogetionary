Claustrophobia

Pronunciation:/ˌklɔːstrəˈfoʊbiə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幽闭恐惧症

Definitions

noun

Definition

An intense fear of being in closed or confined spaces.

In ZH

对闭合或封闭空间的强烈恐惧。

Examples

  • She has claustrophobia and avoids elevators.
  • His claustrophobia made it difficult for him to stay in small rooms.
  • During the MRI scan, her claustrophobia kicked in and she felt anxious.
  • He was diagnosed with claustrophobia after being trapped in an elevator for hours.
  • Understanding her claustrophobia helped her family support her during travels.
