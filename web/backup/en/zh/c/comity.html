Comity

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒm.ɪ.ti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 礼让
  • 和睦
  • 友好关系

Definitions

noun

Definition

The mutual recognition and respect between political entities, often used in the context of international law.

In ZH

政治实体间的相互承认和尊重，常用于国际法的语境中。

Examples

  • The countries agreed to maintain comity in their diplomatic relations.
  • In matters concerning international law, comity can lead to smoother negotiations.
  • The principle of comity fosters goodwill among nations.
  • Comity is essential in maintaining peaceful coexistence.
  • Judicial comity is respected across different states.
