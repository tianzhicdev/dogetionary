Cogitate

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒdʒɪteɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 深思熟虑
  • 考虑
  • 思索

Definitions

verb

Definition

to think deeply about something; to contemplate or meditate.

In ZH

深思熟虑；考虑；思索

Examples

  • I need to cogitate on this matter before making a decision.
  • She sat quietly, cogitating her next move in the game.
  • The philosopher cogitated about the nature of reality for years.
  • He tends to cogitate over every detail before presenting his ideas.
  • They are cogitating on how to improve the current situation.
