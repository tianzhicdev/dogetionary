Cantankerous

Pronunciation:/kənˈtæŋ.kə.rəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 脾气坏的
  • 爱争吵的
  • 不和谐的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

bad-tempered, argumentative, and uncooperative

In ZH

脾气坏，有争议和不合作的

Examples

  • The cantankerous old man yelled at the kids playing in the yard.
  • She has a cantankerous attitude that often puts people off.
  • The cantankerous cat swatted at anyone who came too close.
  • His cantankerous nature made it difficult to work as a team.
  • My cantankerous neighbor always complains about the noise.
