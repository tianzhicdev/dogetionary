Content

EN→ZH

What Does Content Mean?

  • 内容
  • 内容物
  • 主题

Content Pronunciation

/ˈkɒn.tent/

Content Definition

noun

Definition

The information or material contained within something, such as a book, document, or website.

In ZH

某物中包含的信息或材料，例如书籍、文件或网站。

How to Use Content in a Sentence

  • The content of the book was fascinating.
  • She published the content online for others to view.
  • The content of the report was very detailed.
  • They need to improve the content on their website.
  • He was responsible for creating content for the marketing campaign.
adjective

Definition

In a state of satisfaction or happiness, often used to describe a person's feelings.

In ZH

一种满意或快乐的状态，通常用来形容一个人的感受。

How to Use Content in a Sentence

  • She felt content after completing her project.
  • He was content with his life choices.
  • They sat in content silence, enjoying each other's company.
  • A content mind is essential for happiness.
  • After the meal, he felt pleasantly content.

Cultural Notes

In Western cultures, being 'content' often implies a deeper sense of peace and satisfaction compared to simply being 'happy'.

Synonyms for Content

  • Concatenation
