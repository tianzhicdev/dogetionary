Congruous

EN→ZH

What Does Congruous Mean?

  • 一致的
  • 适合的
  • 相符的

Congruous Pronunciation

/ˈkɒŋɡruəs/

Congruous Definition

adjective

Definition

In agreement or harmony; compatible.

In ZH

一致或和谐；兼容的。

How to Use Congruous in a Sentence

  • The plans for the new building are congruous with the city’s historical architecture.
  • Her actions were congruous with her words, showing her integrity.
  • The findings of the study are congruous with existing theories.
  • His personality is congruous with the nature of the job he holds.
  • We need to ensure that our marketing strategy is congruous with our brand values.

Synonyms for Congruous

  • Concatenate
  • Concatenation
  • Concept
  • Concomitant
  • Concordant
