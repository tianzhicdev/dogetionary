Cathartic

EN→ZH

What Does Cathartic Mean?

  • 宣泄的
  • 通便的
  • 排泄的

Cathartic Pronunciation

/kəˈθɑːrtɪk/

Cathartic Definition

adjective

Definition

Providing psychological relief through the expression of strong emotions.

In ZH

通过强烈情感的表达提供心理上的解脱。

How to Use Cathartic in a Sentence

  • The movie was a cathartic experience for many viewers.
  • Writing in her journal has a cathartic effect on her emotions.
  • He found that talking to friends can be cathartic during tough times.
  • The play was a cathartic release for the audience.
  • She felt a cathartic sense of relief after crying for hours.
noun

Definition

A substance that induces a cathartic effect, often used in medicine.

In ZH

一种引发通便效应的物质，通常用于医学。

How to Use Cathartic in a Sentence

  • The doctor prescribed a cathartic to help address her constipation.
  • Some herbal remedies are known for their cathartic properties.
  • In ancient medicine, cathartics were often used to cleanse the body.
  • They used a cathartic to treat the patient's digestive issues.
  • The pharmacist recommended a mild cathartic for better gut health.

Synonyms for Cathartic

  • Catachresis
  • Cataclysm
  • Cataclysmic
  • Catafalque
  • Catalyptic
