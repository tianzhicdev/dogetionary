Conflagration

Pronunciation:/ˌkɒn.fləˈɡreɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 大火
  • 火灾
  • 烈火

Definitions

noun

Definition

A large and destructive fire.

In ZH

一场大而破坏性的火灾。

Examples

  • The conflagration consumed the entire forest within hours.
  • Authorities declared a state of emergency due to the massive conflagration.
  • Local firefighters battled the conflagration for several days.
  • Witnesses reported seeing flames from the conflagration for miles around.
  • The conflagration started in the industrial district and spread rapidly.
