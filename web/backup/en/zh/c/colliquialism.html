Colliquialism

EN→ZH

What Does Colliquialism Mean?

  • 口语化
  • 方言
  • 俗语

Colliquialism Pronunciation

/kəˈloʊkwɪəˌlɪzəm/

Colliquialism Definition

noun

Definition

A word or phrase that is used in informal, everyday language rather than in formal speech or writing.

In ZH

在非正式的日常语言中使用的单词或短语，而不是在正式的言语或写作中使用。

How to Use Colliquialism in a Sentence

  • Many colloquialisms can be heard in casual conversations.
  • The use of colloquialisms can make writing feel more personal and relatable.
  • In some regions, colloquialisms can vary widely from one community to another.
  • Colloquialisms often reflect the culture and identity of a group.
  • Learning colloquialisms is essential for understanding native speakers.
