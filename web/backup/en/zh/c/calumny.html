Calumny

Pronunciation:/ˈkælʌmni/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 诽谤
  • 中伤
  • 造谣

Definitions

noun

Definition

The making of false and defamatory statements about someone in order to damage their reputation.

In ZH

对某人进行虚假和诽谤的陈述，以损害其名声。

Examples

  • His reputation was ruined by the calumny spread by his rivals.
  • The newspaper published a calumny that led to a public outcry.
  • She faced calumny for daring to speak out against the powerful.
  • Calumny can have devastating effects on a person's life.
  • In politics, calumny is often used as a weapon against opponents.
