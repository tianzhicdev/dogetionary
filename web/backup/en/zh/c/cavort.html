Cavort

EN→ZH

What Does Cavort Mean?

  • 嬉戏
  • 欢跃
  • 蹦跳

Cavort Pronunciation

/kəˈvɔːrt/

Cavort Definition

verb

Definition

To leap or dance in a lively manner; to frolic.

In ZH

以生动的方式跳跃或舞蹈；嬉戏。

How to Use Cavort in a Sentence

  • The children cavorted in the park, laughing and playing.
  • The puppies cavorted around their mother, full of energy.
  • During the festival, people cavorted joyfully in the streets.
  • He cavorted with delight when he heard the good news.
  • The dancers cavorted on stage, captivating the audience.

Synonyms for Cavort

  • Cavalcade
  • Caveat
  • Caviar
  • Cavil
  • Cavorting
