Caducity

Pronunciation:/kəˈdjuːsɪti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 失效
  • 过期
  • 衰老

Definitions

noun

Definition

the quality of being caducous; the state of being temporary or transitory.

In ZH

易逝性；短暂性

Examples

  • The caducity of youth is often lamented in literature.
  • In the realm of fashion, caducity is a constant theme as trends come and go.
  • The scientist spoke about the caducity of life in his lecture on aging.
  • We must remember the caducity of material wealth in our pursuit of happiness.
  • Her reflections on love highlighted the caducity of romantic relationships.
