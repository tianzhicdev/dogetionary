Caboodle

EN→ZH

What Does Caboodle Mean?

  • 全体
  • 一切
  • 所有的一切

Caboodle Pronunciation

/kəˈbuːdəl/

Caboodle Definition

noun

Definition

A collection or group of things; often used in the phrase 'the whole kit and caboodle'.

In ZH

一组事物的集合；通常用在短语“整个套件和所有的一切”中。

How to Use Caboodle in a Sentence

  • I've packed the whole kit and caboodle for our trip.
  • He brought along the caboodle of tools needed for the job.
  • She has the whole caboodle of toys in her room.
  • After the party, we cleaned up the caboodle of decorations.
  • Don't forget to take the caboodle when you leave.

Cultural Notes

The phrase 'the whole kit and caboodle' is often used in informal English to refer to everything involved in a situation.

Synonyms for Caboodle

  • Cabal
  • Cabalistic
