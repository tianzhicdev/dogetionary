Circumspect

Pronunciation:/ˈsɜːrkəmˌspɛkt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 谨慎的
  • 周到的
  • 小心的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Careful to consider all circumstances and possible consequences; prudent.

In ZH

小心翼翼地考虑所有情况和可能的后果；谨慎的。

Examples

  • She was circumspect in her speech, choosing her words carefully.
  • The circumspect investor always researches before making a decision.
  • He took a circumspect approach to dealing with the new situation.
  • Being circumspect can help avoid unnecessary conflicts.
  • The manager's circumspect nature kept the project on track.
