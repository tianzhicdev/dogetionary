Camarilla

EN→ZH

What Does Camarilla Mean?

  • 小圈子
  • 派系
  • 内阁

Camarilla Pronunciation

/ˌkæməˈrɪlə/

Camarilla Definition

noun

Definition

a secret or exclusive group of advisors or followers who wield power behind the scenes

In ZH

一个在幕后掌权的秘密或独占顾问或追随者团体

How to Use Camarilla in a Sentence

  • The president surrounded himself with a camarilla of loyal advisors.
  • The king's decisions were influenced by a hidden camarilla.
  • In the corporate world, a camarilla can dictate the direction of the company.
  • Historical accounts often speak of a camarilla that manipulated royal decisions.
  • To gain influence, one must navigate the intricacies of the camarilla.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in political contexts to refer to groups that operate behind the scenes, particularly in relation to governance and decision-making.

Synonyms for Camarilla

  • Camaraderie
  • Camouflaged
  • Campestral
