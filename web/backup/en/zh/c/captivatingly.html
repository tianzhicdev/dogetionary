Captivatingly

EN→ZH

What Does Captivatingly Mean?

  • 迷人地
  • 吸引人地
  • 迷住地

Captivatingly Pronunciation

/ˌkæp.tɪˈveɪ.tɪŋ.li/

Captivatingly Definition

adverb

Definition

In a manner that attracts and holds interest or attention.

In ZH

以吸引并保持兴趣或注意力的方式。

How to Use Captivatingly in a Sentence

  • She spoke captivatingly, drawing everyone into her story.
  • The performance was captivatingly beautiful and left the audience in awe.
  • He captivates the crowd captivatingly with his charm and charisma.
  • They presented their ideas captivatingly during the seminar.
  • Her captivatingly funny anecdotes made the lecture enjoyable.
