Courteous

EN→ZH

What Does Courteous Mean?

  • 有礼貌的
  • 客气的
  • 谦恭的

Courteous Pronunciation

ˈkɜːrt̬iəs

Courteous Definition

adjective

Definition

Polite, respectful, or considerate in manner.

In ZH

礼貌的、尊重的或体贴的举止。

How to Use Courteous in a Sentence

  • He was always courteous to his colleagues.
  • A courteous host makes guests feel welcome.
  • It's courteous to thank someone when they help you.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, being courteous is an important social norm and is often expected in both personal and professional interactions. For example, in Chinese culture, showing courtesy involves not just polite language but also gestures such as bowing or offering gifts.

noun

Definition

The quality of being courteous; politeness.

In ZH

礼貌的品质；礼节。

How to Use Courteous in a Sentence

  • Her courteous made her stand out in the group.
  • Being courteous is essential in customer service.
  • His courteous behavior earned him many friends.

Cultural Notes

In the context of western cultures, a courteous attitude is often appreciated in various settings, such as business meetings and social gatherings. It is seen as a reflection of one’s upbringing and personality.
