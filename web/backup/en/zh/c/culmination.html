Culmination

EN→ZH

What Does Culmination Mean?

  • 顶点
  • 高潮
  • 结局

Culmination Pronunciation

/ˌkʌl.mɪˈneɪ.ʃən/

Culmination Definition

noun

Definition

the highest point or climax of something, especially after a long period of development or effort

In ZH

某事物的最高点或高潮，尤其是在经过长时间发展或努力之后

How to Use Culmination in a Sentence

  • The culmination of her studies was her graduation ceremony.
  • The project reached its culmination after two years of hard work.
  • His achievements are the culmination of a lifetime of effort.
  • The culmination of the festival was the spectacular fireworks display.
  • They celebrated the culmination of their journey with a big party.

Synonyms for Culmination

  • Cullion
  • Culminate
  • Culpability
  • Culpable
  • Cultural
