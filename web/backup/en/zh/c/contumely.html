Contumely Pronunciation

/kənˈtjuːməli/

Contumely Definition

noun

Definition

insulting or contemptuous behavior; scornful treatment

In ZH

侮辱或轻蔑的行为；蔑视的对待

How to Use Contumely in a Sentence

  • His contumely was evident in the way he spoke to her.
  • The contumely directed at the speaker was palpable during the debate.
  • She endured a life filled with contumely and derision.
  • Contumely can often escalate into more serious forms of abuse.
  • He faced contumely from his peers for his unconventional ideas.
