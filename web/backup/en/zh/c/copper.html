Copper

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒp.ər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 铜

Definitions

noun

Definition

A reddish-brown metal used for electrical wiring, plumbing, and various alloys.

In ZH

一种用于电气布线、管道和各种合金的红棕色金属。

Examples

  • Copper is an excellent conductor of electricity.
  • The statue was made of pure copper.
  • She wore a beautiful copper bracelet.
  • Copper pipes are commonly used in plumbing.
  • The coin is made of copper and nickel.
