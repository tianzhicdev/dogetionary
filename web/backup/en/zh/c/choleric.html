Choleric

EN→ZH

What Does Choleric Mean?

  • 易怒的
  • 胆汁质的
  • 脾气暴躁的

Choleric Pronunciation

/ˈkɒlərɪk/

Choleric Definition

adjective

Definition

easily angered; bad-tempered

In ZH

容易生气的；脾气暴躁的

How to Use Choleric in a Sentence

  • His choleric response surprised everyone at the meeting.
  • She has a choleric temperament that makes her hard to work with.
  • Choleric individuals often find it difficult to control their anger.
  • Despite his choleric nature, he is a good leader.
  • The choleric coach often yelled at the players during practice.
