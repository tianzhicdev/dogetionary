Consonance

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒnsənəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 和谐
  • 一致
  • 协调

Definitions

noun

Definition

The occurrence of the same letter or sound at the beginning of adjacent or closely connected words.

In ZH

相邻或紧密相关词的开头出现相同的字母或声音的情况。

Examples

  • The poem exhibited great consonance in its use of alliteration.
  • In music, consonance refers to a harmonious combination of sounds.
  • The consonance of their ideas led to a fruitful collaboration.
  • There was a consonance between the themes of the two novels.
  • The consonance of their voices created a beautiful harmony.
noun

Definition

Agreement or compatibility between opinions or actions.

In ZH

意见或行为之间的符合或兼容性。

Examples

  • There was a consonance among the committee members regarding the new policy.
  • The consonance of their beliefs made it easier to work together.
  • We found consonance in our visions for the future.
  • Her actions showed consonance with her stated values.
  • The organization's goals reflect a consonance with the community's needs.
