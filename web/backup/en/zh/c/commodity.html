Commodity

Pronunciation:/kəˈmɒdəti/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 商品
  • 物品
  • 货物
  • 日用品
  • 原料

Definitions

noun

Definition

A basic good used in commerce that is interchangeable with other goods of the same type.

In ZH

在商业中使用的基本商品，可与同类商品互换。

Examples

  • Gold is considered a valuable commodity.
  • Coffee is one of the most traded commodities in the world.
  • Farmers often grow crops that are classified as commodities.
  • Oil is a crucial commodity that affects global economies.
  • The price of agricultural commodities can fluctuate due to weather conditions.
