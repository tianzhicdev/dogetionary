Cachophony Pronunciation

/kæˈkɒfəni/

Cachophony Definition

noun

Definition

A harsh, discordant mixture of sounds.

In ZH

刺耳的声音或杂音的混合。

How to Use Cachophony in a Sentence

  • The cachophony of the city made it hard to concentrate.
  • In the orchestra, the cacophony was a sign of disorganization.
  • As the children played, their laughter turned into a cachophony of noise.
  • The protest created a cachophony that echoed through the streets.
  • When the band started playing, the initial cachophony startled everyone.

Cultural Notes

The word 'cacophony' is often used in musical contexts to describe harsh or jarring sounds.
