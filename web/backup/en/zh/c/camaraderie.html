Camaraderie

EN→ZH

What Does Camaraderie Mean?

  • 同志情谊
  • 友情
  • 团结感

Camaraderie Pronunciation

/ˌkæməˈrɑːdəri/

Camaraderie Definition

noun

Definition

Mutual trust and friendship among people who spend a lot of time together.

In ZH

在一起花很多时间的人之间的相互信任和友谊。

How to Use Camaraderie in a Sentence

  • The camaraderie among the team members was evident during the project.
  • We developed a strong sense of camaraderie during our hiking trip.
  • There is a special camaraderie in military units that is hard to describe.
  • The camaraderie in the office makes it a pleasant place to work.
  • Building camaraderie is essential for team success.

Synonyms for Camaraderie

  • Camarilla
  • Camouflaged
  • Campestral
