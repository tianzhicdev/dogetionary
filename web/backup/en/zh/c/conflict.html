Conflict

EN→ZH

What Does Conflict Mean?

  • 冲突
  • 矛盾
  • 战斗

Conflict Pronunciation

ˈkɒnflɪkt

Conflict Definition

noun

Definition

A serious disagreement or argument, typically a protracted one.

In ZH

一种严重的分歧或争论，通常是长时间的。

How to Use Conflict in a Sentence

  • The recent debate over avocado toast prices turned into a fierce conflict between brunch lovers and economists.
  • The cat and the dog had a conflict over who would get the best spot on the couch, resulting in a very loud disagreement.
  • In a shocking twist, the local council's meeting on traffic lights ended in a conflict over whether to ban bicycles altogether.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, conflicts can be seen as a natural part of relationships, but in Western culture, they are often addressed directly.

verb

Definition

To come into collision or disagreement; to be contradictory or inconsistent.

In ZH

发生冲突或争论；矛盾或不一致。

How to Use Conflict in a Sentence

  • When I tried to wear two different socks on a Monday, my outfit choices conflicted with my sense of style.
  • The weather forecast and my plans for a barbecue conflict, leaving me with a conundrum: grill in the rain or order pizza.
  • When you say you love running but then spend every Saturday binge-watching shows, you are seriously conflicting with your fitness goals.

Cultural Notes

Using 'conflict' as a verb is common when discussing schedules or ideas that do not match, especially in professional settings.
