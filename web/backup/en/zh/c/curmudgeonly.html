Curmudgeonly

EN→ZH

What Does Curmudgeonly Mean?

  • 脾气坏的
  • 爱抱怨的
  • 易怒的

Curmudgeonly Pronunciation

/kərˈmʌdʒ.ən.li/

Curmudgeonly Definition

adjective

Definition

bad-tempered, difficult, and grumpy

In ZH

脾气坏的，难以相处的，易怒的

How to Use Curmudgeonly in a Sentence

  • He had a curmudgeonly attitude towards the new policies.
  • Her curmudgeonly demeanor made it hard for her to make friends.
  • The curmudgeonly old man yelled at the kids playing in the park.
  • Despite his curmudgeonly exterior, he had a soft spot for his grandchildren.
  • She often complained about curmudgeonly customers at the store.

Synonyms for Curmudgeonly

  • Curmudgeon
  • Cursory
