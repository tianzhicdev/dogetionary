Copious

Pronunciation:/ˈkoʊ.pi.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 丰富的
  • 大量的
  • 广泛的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

abundant in supply or quantity

In ZH

充足的，数量众多的

Examples

  • She took copious notes during the lecture.
  • The garden produced copious amounts of vegetables this summer.
  • He had a copious amount of data to support his theory.
  • They provided copious evidence to back up their claims.
  • The author included copious references in his book.
