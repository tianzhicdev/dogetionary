Convalesce

EN→ZH

What Does Convalesce Mean?

  • 恢复
  • 康复
  • 痊愈

Convalesce Pronunciation

/ˌkɒn.vəˈlɛs/

Convalesce Definition

verb

Definition

to recover health and strength gradually after sickness or weakness

In ZH

在生病或虚弱后渐渐恢复健康和力量

How to Use Convalesce in a Sentence

  • After the surgery, it took her several weeks to convalesce.
  • He is convalescing at home after his hospitalization.
  • The doctor advised him to take time off to convalesce properly.
  • During the summer, many people convalesce in the countryside.
  • Convalescing patients often need support from family and friends.
