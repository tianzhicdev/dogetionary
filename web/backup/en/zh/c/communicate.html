Communicate

Pronunciation:/kəˈmjuːnɪkeɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 交流
  • 传达
  • 沟通

Definitions

verb

Definition

to share or exchange information, ideas, or feelings with someone

In ZH

与某人分享或交流信息、想法或感情

Examples

  • I need to communicate my thoughts clearly.
  • They communicate frequently through emails.
  • It's essential to communicate in a relationship.
  • The teacher tried to communicate the lesson effectively.
  • She used body language to communicate her feelings.
