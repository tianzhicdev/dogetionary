Commiserate

Pronunciation:/kəˈmɪzəˌreɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 同情
  • 怜悯
  • 哀悼

Definitions

verb

Definition

To express sympathy or share in someone's sorrow or discomfort.

In ZH

表达同情或分享某人的悲伤或不适。

Examples

  • I went to the funeral to commiserate with the grieving family.
  • She commiserated with him over his recent loss.
  • They commiserated about the difficulties of job hunting.
  • Friends gathered to commiserate after the team's defeat.
  • It's important to commiserate with others in tough times.
