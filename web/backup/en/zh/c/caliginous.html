Caliginous

Pronunciation:/kəˈlɪdʒɪnəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 昏暗的
  • 黑暗的
  • 模糊的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

having or appearing to have a dark, dim, or gloomy quality; obscure.

In ZH

具有或看起来具有黑暗、阴暗或阴郁的特征；模糊的。

Examples

  • The caliginous forest was filled with eerie sounds.
  • She felt a caliginous presence in the abandoned house.
  • The sky turned caliginous as the storm approached.
  • His caliginous thoughts weighed heavily on his mind.
  • Under the caliginous veil of night, secrets were revealed.
