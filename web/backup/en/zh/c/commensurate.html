Commensurate

Pronunciation:/kəˈmɛn.sər.ət/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 相称的
  • 成比例的
  • 相应的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

matching or corresponding in degree or size; appropriate or equal in measure.

In ZH

在程度或大小上相匹配或相应的；适当或等量的。

Examples

  • His salary is commensurate with his experience.
  • The punishment should be commensurate with the crime.
  • They expect a commensurate increase in productivity.
  • Her responsibilities are commensurate to her position in the company.
  • The benefits offered are commensurate with those of other firms.
