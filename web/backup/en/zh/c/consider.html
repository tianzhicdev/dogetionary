Consider

Pronunciation:/kənˈsɪdər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 考虑
  • 认为
  • 顾及

Definitions

verb

Definition

to think about carefully, especially in making decisions

In ZH

仔细考虑，特别是在做决定时

Examples

  • I will consider all the options before making a decision.
  • Please consider his feelings when you talk to him.
  • She is considering moving to a different city.
  • We need to consider the potential risks involved.
  • He considered going back to school for a master's degree.
