Cultural

Pronunciation:/ˈkʌltʃərəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 文化的
  • 文化上的
  • 文化性

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Relating to the ideas, customs, and social behavior of a society.

In ZH

与一个社会的思想、习俗和社会行为有关。

Examples

  • Cultural diversity enriches society.
  • The cultural heritage of the region is preserved in its museums.
  • She studied cultural anthropology to understand different societies better.
  • Cultural awareness is important in a globalized world.
  • They organized a cultural festival to celebrate their traditions.
