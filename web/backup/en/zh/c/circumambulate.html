Circumambulate

EN→ZH

What Does Circumambulate Mean?

  • 环绕
  • 绕行
  • 顺绕

Circumambulate Pronunciation

/ˌsɜː.kəmˈæm.bjʊ.leɪt/

Circumambulate Definition

verb

Definition

To walk all the way around something, especially as part of a ritual or ceremony.

In ZH

环绕某物走一圈，特别是作为仪式或典礼的一部分。

How to Use Circumambulate in a Sentence

  • They will circumambulate the sacred temple as a form of worship.
  • In ancient times, pilgrims would circumambulate sacred sites.
  • During the festival, participants circumambulate the city in colorful costumes.
  • He decided to circumambulate the mountain as part of his hike.
  • The monks circumambulated the stupa in reverence.

Synonyms for Circumambulate

  • Circuitous
  • Circumambient
  • Circumlocution
  • Circumscribe
  • Circumspect
