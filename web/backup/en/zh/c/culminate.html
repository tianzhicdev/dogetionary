Culminate

Pronunciation:/ˈkʌlmɪneɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 达到顶点
  • 告终
  • 结束

Definitions

verb

Definition

to reach a climax or point of highest development

In ZH

达到顶点或最高发展的点

Examples

  • The project will culminate in a major presentation next month.
  • Their efforts culminated in a successful event.
  • Her studies culminated in a prestigious award.
  • The negotiations culminated in a comprehensive agreement.
  • The season culminates with a grand finale.
