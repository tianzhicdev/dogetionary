Conflated

EN→ZH

What Does Conflated Mean?

  • 合并的
  • 混淆的
  • 合成的

Conflated Pronunciation

/kənˈfleɪtɪd/

Conflated Definition

adjective

Definition

Combined or merged together, often in a way that can lead to confusion or a lack of clarity.

In ZH

合并在一起，通常以可能导致混淆或缺乏清晰度的方式。

How to Use Conflated in a Sentence

  • The two concepts were conflated in the author's argument.
  • It's easy to conflate personal beliefs with scientific facts.
  • In his speech, he conflated several historical events into one narrative.
  • The terms are often conflated, leading to misunderstandings.
  • Don't conflate the issue of funding with the issue of access.

Synonyms for Conflated

  • Concatenate
  • Concatenation
  • Concept
  • Concomitant
  • Concordant
