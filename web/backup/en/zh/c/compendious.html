Compendious

Pronunciation:/kəmˈpɛn.di.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 简明的
  • 概括的
  • 简要的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Containing or presenting the essential facts in a comprehensive but concise way.

In ZH

包含或呈现基本事实，全面而简洁。

Examples

  • The report was compendious, summarizing the key findings in just a few pages.
  • She wrote a compendious guide to the complex subject of quantum physics.
  • His compendious summary was appreciated by all those who attended the briefing.
  • In a compendious manner, the teacher covered all the necessary topics for the exam.
  • The book provides a compendious overview of the history of art in just under 200 pages.
