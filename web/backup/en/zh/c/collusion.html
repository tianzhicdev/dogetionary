Collusion

Pronunciation:/kəˈluːʒən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 勾结
  • 串通
  • 合谋

Definitions

noun

Definition

Secret or illegal cooperation or conspiracy in order to deceive others.

In ZH

秘密或非法合作或阴谋，以欺骗他人。

Examples

  • The two companies were accused of collusion to fix prices.
  • There was evidence of collusion between the politicians and the lobbyists.
  • The investigation revealed a collusion that undermined the integrity of the election.
  • The firm faced serious consequences for its collusion with a foreign government.
  • They denied any collusion, but the evidence was overwhelming.
