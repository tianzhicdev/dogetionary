Caprice

Pronunciation:/kəˈpriːs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 奇思妙想
  • 任性
  • 变化无常

Definitions

noun

Definition

A sudden and unaccountable change of mood or behavior.

In ZH

一种突然而不可理喻的情绪或行为的变化。

Examples

  • Her caprice often left her friends confused.
  • He made a caprice decision to travel abroad on a whim.
  • The weather in this region is known for its caprice.
  • His caprice made it difficult for others to plan around him.
  • She followed her caprice and decided to paint the living room bright red.
