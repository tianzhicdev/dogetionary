Confluent

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒn.flu.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 汇合的
  • 交融的
  • 相通的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Flowing together; merging.

In ZH

汇合的; 融合的。

Examples

  • The confluent rivers created a large lake.
  • Their opinions are confluent on this matter.
  • The confluent streams formed a beautiful landscape.
  • In physics, confluent waves can create interference patterns.
  • The confluent cultures enriched the local traditions.

Definition

A tributary stream that flows into a larger river.

In ZH

Examples

  • The confluent is essential for the ecosystem of the river.
  • Many fish species use confluent streams for spawning.

  • Researchers measured the pollution levels at the confluent.
