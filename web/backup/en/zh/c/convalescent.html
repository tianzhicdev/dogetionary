Convalescent

EN→ZH

What Does Convalescent Mean?

  • 恢复期的
  • 康复期的

Convalescent Pronunciation

/ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsənt/

Convalescent Definition

noun

Definition

A person who is recovering after illness or medical treatment.

In ZH

一个在疾病或医疗治疗后正在恢复的人。

How to Use Convalescent in a Sentence

  • The hospital has a special wing for convalescents.
  • She became a convalescent after her surgery.
  • The convalescent center offers therapy sessions.
  • Doctors recommend rest for convalescents.
  • He was a convalescent for several weeks before returning to work.
adjective

Definition

Relating to the period of recovery after illness.

In ZH

与疾病后的恢复期有关。

How to Use Convalescent in a Sentence

  • Convalescent care is essential for recovery.
  • The convalescent phase can take weeks to months.
  • They provided convalescent support to those in need.
  • Convalescent foods are often easy to digest.
  • During the convalescent period, it is important to maintain a healthy diet.

Synonyms for Convalescent

  • Concatenation
