Culpable

EN→ZH

What Does Culpable Mean?

  • 有罪的
  • 应受责备的

Culpable Pronunciation

/ˈkʌlpəbl/

Culpable Definition

adjective

Definition

Deserving blame or censure; guilty.

In ZH

应受责备的；有罪的

How to Use Culpable in a Sentence

  • The jury found the defendant culpable of the crime.
  • He felt culpable for not helping his friend in need.
  • In many cases, the company is culpable for environmental damage.
  • She was deemed culpable after the investigation revealed her negligence.
  • The report concluded that the management was culpable for the unsafe working conditions.
