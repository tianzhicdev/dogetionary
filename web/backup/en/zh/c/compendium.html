Compendium

Pronunciation:/kəmˈpɛn.di.əm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 汇编
  • 纲要
  • 概述
  • 简介

Definitions

noun

Definition

A collection of concise but detailed information about a particular subject, especially in a book.

In ZH

关于特定主题的简明而详细的信息集合，尤其是在一本书中。

Examples

  • The library has a compendium of scientific research papers.
  • He wrote a compendium of folklore from various cultures.
  • The teacher provided a compendium of resources for the students.
  • The compendium covers all aspects of the topic.
  • I found a useful compendium of tips for new parents.
