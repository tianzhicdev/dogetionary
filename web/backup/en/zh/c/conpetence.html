Conpetence Pronunciation

ˈkɒmpɪtəns

Conpetence Definition

noun

Definition

The ability to do something successfully or efficiently.

In ZH

成功或有效地做某事的能力。

How to Use Conpetence in a Sentence

  • She showed great competence in her job.
  • His competence in multiple languages impressed the hiring manager.
  • The team's competence was evident during the project.

Cultural Notes

Competence is highly valued in professional environments, and being viewed as competent can lead to opportunities for advancement.

Definition

  • The job requires a high level of competence in technical skills.

In many cultures, especially in Western countries, having a specific set of competencies is crucial for career development and is often assessed during hiring processes.
