Congregation

Pronunciation:/ˌkɒŋɡrɪˈɡeɪʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 会众
  • 集会
  • 宗教会众

Definitions

noun

Definition

A group of people assembled for religious worship.

In ZH

为宗教崇拜而聚集的一群人。

Examples

  • The congregation gathered to celebrate the holiday together.
  • The pastor addressed the congregation with a message of hope.
  • After the service, the congregation enjoyed refreshments and socializing.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, a congregation is often associated with church services, where members come together to participate in collective worship, prayer, and community activities.

noun

Definition

A group of people brought together for a common purpose or activity.

In ZH

为了共同的目的或活动而聚集的一群人。

Examples

  • The congregation of environmental activists met to discuss their strategies.
  • A congregation of fans awaited the arrival of their favorite band.
  • The university has a congregation of students dedicated to service projects.

Cultural Notes

Beyond religious contexts, the term can apply to any assembly for a common interest, including community meetings or events.
