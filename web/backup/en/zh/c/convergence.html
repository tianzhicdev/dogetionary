Convergence

EN→ZH

What Does Convergence Mean?

  • 汇聚
  • 收敛
  • 趋同

Convergence Pronunciation

/kənˈvɜːrdʒəns/

Convergence Definition

noun

Definition

the process or state of converging; the tendency of separate entities to come together or unite in some way

In ZH

汇聚的过程或状态；多个独立实体趋向某种方式聚集或联合的倾向

How to Use Convergence in a Sentence

  • The convergence of cultures can lead to a rich exchange of ideas.
  • Mathematics often studies the convergence of sequences and functions.
  • The convergence of technology and education has transformed learning.
  • There was a convergence of opinions on the need for environmental protection.
  • The convergence of different technologies has given rise to new innovations.
