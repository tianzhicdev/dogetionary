Cajole

Pronunciation:/kəˈdʒoʊl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 哄骗
  • 劝诱
  • 诱导

Definitions

verb

Definition

to persuade someone to do something by coaxing or flattery

In ZH

通过劝诱或奉承说服某人做某事

Examples

  • She tried to cajole her parents into letting her go to the party.
  • He managed to cajole his friend into helping him with his project.
  • They used charm to cajole the audience into buying their product.
  • The salesman tried to cajole her into signing the contract immediately.
  • I don't want to cajole you, but it would be great if you could join us.
