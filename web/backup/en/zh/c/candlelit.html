Candlelit

EN→ZH

What Does Candlelit Mean?

  • 烛光的
  • 烛光照明的

Candlelit Pronunciation

/ˈkændəlˌlɪt/

Candlelit Definition

adjective

Definition

Illuminated by candles; having light from candles.

In ZH

由蜡烛照明的；有蜡烛光的。

How to Use Candlelit in a Sentence

  • The restaurant had a beautiful candlelit atmosphere.
  • They celebrated their anniversary with a candlelit dinner.
  • The room was filled with a warm, candlelit glow.
  • She read her book by the candlelit fireplace.
  • A candlelit path led to the garden.

Synonyms for Candlelit
