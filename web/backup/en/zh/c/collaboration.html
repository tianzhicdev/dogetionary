Collaboration

EN→ZH

What Does Collaboration Mean?

  • 合作
  • 协作
  • 协力

Collaboration Pronunciation

/kəˌlæbəˈreɪʃən/

Collaboration Definition

noun

Definition

The action of working with someone to produce or create something.

In ZH

与某人一起工作以产生或创造某物的行为。

How to Use Collaboration in a Sentence

  • The collaboration between the two companies led to innovative products.
  • Her collaboration with local artists helped revitalize the community.
  • They announced their collaboration on the new project at the conference.
  • It was a fruitful collaboration that lasted for several years.
  • His collaboration with the renowned author was highly anticipated.

Synonyms for Collaboration

  • Collaborate
  • Collate
  • Collating
  • Colliquial
  • Colliquialism
