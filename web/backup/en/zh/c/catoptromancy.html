Catoptromancy

Pronunciation:/ˌkæ.tɒp.trəˈmæn.si/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 镜占术
  • 镜像占卜

Definitions

noun

Definition

A form of divination using mirrors or reflective surfaces to gain insight or predict the future.

In ZH

一种使用镜子或反射表面进行的占卜形式，用以获得洞察或预测未来。

Examples

  • The ancient Greeks practiced catoptromancy to seek guidance from their deities.
  • In medieval times, catoptromancy was considered a legitimate form of divination among certain cultures.
  • She turned to catoptromancy, gazing into the mirror for answers about her future.
  • Catoptromancy involves interpreting the images seen in a reflective surface for prophetic messages.
  • Many believe that catoptromancy can reveal hidden truths about oneself.

Cultural Notes

Catoptromancy has historical significance in various cultures, particularly in ancient Greece and medieval Europe, where it was used in rituals and ceremonies.
