Casualist

EN→ZH

What Does Casualist Mean?

  • 随意者
  • 漫不经心者
  • 轻松主义者

Casualist Pronunciation

/ˈkæʒ.u.əl.ɪst/

Casualist Definition

noun

Definition

A person who adheres to or advocates for a casual approach to situations, often avoiding seriousness or formality.

In ZH

一个坚持或倡导对情况采取随意态度的人，通常避免严肃或正式。

How to Use Casualist in a Sentence

  • He is a casualist who believes that life should be enjoyed without too much pressure.
  • Many casualists attend events without dressing up, preferring comfort over formality.
  • Her casualist approach to work allows her to create a relaxed atmosphere for her team.

Cultural Notes

In modern contexts, 'casualist' can often refer to individuals who prioritize comfort and leisure over professionalism, particularly in creative industries.

adjective

Definition

Relating to or characterized by a casual or informal manner.

In ZH

与随意或非正式的方式相关或具有特征的。

How to Use Casualist in a Sentence

  • His casualist attitude helped to ease the tension in the room.
  • She prefers a casualist style in her clothing, often opting for jeans and t-shirts.
  • The casualist decor of the café makes it a favorite spot for young people.

Cultural Notes

The adjective form is less commonly used in formal writing but can be seen in discussions about lifestyle and fashion.

Synonyms for Casualist
