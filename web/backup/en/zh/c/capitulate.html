Capitulate

EN→ZH

What Does Capitulate Mean?

  • 投降
  • 屈服
  • 让步

Capitulate Pronunciation

/kəˈpɪtʃʊleɪt/

Capitulate Definition

verb

Definition

to cease to resist an opponent or an unwelcome demand; surrender.

In ZH

停止抵抗对手或不受欢迎的要求；投降。

How to Use Capitulate in a Sentence

  • The army was forced to capitulate after months of fighting.
  • After a lengthy negotiation, the company decided to capitulate to the union's demands.
  • He refused to capitulate, even under immense pressure.
  • Many countries capitulated to the invading forces during the war.
  • She will not capitulate to social pressure and will stand by her beliefs.

Synonyms for Capitulate

  • Capable
  • Caper
  • Capitulation
  • Caprice
  • Capricious
