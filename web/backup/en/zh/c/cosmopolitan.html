Cosmopolitan

Pronunciation:/ˌkɒz.məˈpɒl.ɪ.tən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 国际化的
  • 世界主义者
  • 都市人

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Familiar with and at ease in many different countries and cultures; worldly.

In ZH

熟悉并且能在许多不同国家和文化中自如生活的；世俗的。

Examples

  • She has a cosmopolitan outlook and enjoys traveling to different countries.
  • New York is one of the most cosmopolitan cities in the world.
  • The restaurant offers cosmopolitan cuisine that reflects various cultures.
  • He is a cosmopolitan individual who speaks multiple languages.
  • The fashion show featured cosmopolitan styles from around the globe.
noun

Definition

A person who is at home in all countries; a citizen of the world.

In ZH

在所有国家都有家的人；世界公民。

Examples

  • As a cosmopolitan, he feels comfortable in any city around the globe.
  • Many cosmopolitans choose to live in urban areas for the cultural diversity.
  • The cosmopolitan community values inclusivity and acceptance of different backgrounds.
  • She considers herself a cosmopolitan, having lived in five different countries.
  • Cosmopolitans often embrace international perspectives in their personal and professional lives.
