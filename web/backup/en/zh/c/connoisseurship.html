Connoisseurship

Pronunciation:/ˌkɒn.əˈsɜːr.ʃɪp/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 鉴赏家地位
  • 鉴赏能力
  • 鉴赏类

Definitions

noun

Definition

The skill or knowledge of a connoisseur; the ability to appreciate the details and nuances of a particular field, especially in art, food, or wine.

In ZH

鉴赏家特有的技能或知识；尤其在艺术、食物或葡萄酒领域中，欣赏细节和微妙之处的能力。

Examples

  • His connoisseurship of fine wines is unparalleled.
  • She has developed a connoisseurship of modern art over the years.
  • The chef's expertise reflects her connoisseurship of gourmet cuisine.
  • His connoisseurship was evident as he described the subtle flavors of the dish.
  • In the world of fashion, connoisseurship can elevate one's style significantly.

Cultural Notes

Connoisseurship is often associated with luxury and the elite, as it implies an extensive knowledge and appreciation that goes beyond mere consumption.
