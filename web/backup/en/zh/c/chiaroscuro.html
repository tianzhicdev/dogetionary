Chiaroscuro

EN→ZH

What Does Chiaroscuro Mean?

  • 明暗对照
  • 明暗法

Chiaroscuro Pronunciation

/ˌkiː.ə.rəˈskjʊə.roʊ/

Chiaroscuro Definition

noun

Definition

The treatment of light and shade in drawing and painting.

In ZH

绘画和素描中对光和阴影的处理。

How to Use Chiaroscuro in a Sentence

  • The artist used chiaroscuro to create dramatic contrasts in his painting.
  • In photography, chiaroscuro can enhance the mood of the image.
  • The chiaroscuro technique is evident in the works of Caravaggio.
  • Understanding chiaroscuro is essential for mastering portrait drawing.
  • Chiaroscuro emphasizes the three-dimensionality of figures.
noun

Definition

A method of representing light and shadow in visual arts, often highlighting the volume and depth of subjects.

In ZH

在视觉艺术中表现光与影的方法，通常突出主题的体积和深度。

How to Use Chiaroscuro in a Sentence

  • Chiaroscuro helped the artist convey emotion in his work.
  • Many Renaissance painters employed chiaroscuro to bring their subjects to life.
  • The chiaroscuro effect can be powerful in creating visual drama.
  • Experimenting with chiaroscuro can lead to innovative artistic expressions.
  • Chiaroscuro plays a significant role in enhancing realism in artworks.
