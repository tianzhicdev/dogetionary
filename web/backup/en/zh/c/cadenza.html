Cadenza

Pronunciation:/kəˈdɛn.zə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 坚琴独奏
  • 华彩
  • 独奏音段

Definitions

noun

Definition

a flourish or embellishment played by a soloist, often at the end of a movement in a concerto

In ZH

由独奏者演奏的华彩，通常在协奏曲的乐章末尾出现

Examples

  • The violinist played a stunning cadenza that captivated the audience.
  • In classical concertos, the cadenza allows the soloist to showcase their skill.
  • She improvised a beautiful cadenza during the performance.
  • The composer wrote a challenging cadenza for the piano concerto.
  • His cadenza received a standing ovation from the crowd.
