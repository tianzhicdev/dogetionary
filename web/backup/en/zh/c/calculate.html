Calculate

Pronunciation:ˈkæl.kjə.leɪt
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 计算
  • 估算
  • 算出

Definitions

verb

Definition

To determine the amount or number of something mathematically.

In ZH

用数学方法确定某事物的数量或数值。

Examples

  • If I calculate correctly, I’ll need 27 pizzas for the party, and that's assuming no one eats the crusts!
  • I tried to calculate how much coffee I need to stay awake during my Zoom meeting, but it ended up being a brew-tiful disaster!
  • The politician calculated that his approval rating would rise if he wore a clown nose during his speeches... it didn't work out as he planned.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, including Chinese culture, mathematics is considered an important skill. Calculating correctly is often associated with intelligence and capability. It’s common to joke about calculating everyday things like how much to tip, or the number of snacks needed for a movie night.

noun

Definition

The process of using mathematics to find a number or amount.

In ZH

使用数学来找到一个数字或数量的过程。

Examples

  • After hours of calculation, I realized I only needed 5 beans for my 'magical' potion instead of 50!
  • The calculation of how many cupcakes I can eat before I feel sick is a delicate balance... I'm still working on it!
  • The latest calculation showed that if you put a billion cats in a room, at least one will find a way to knock over a glass of water.

Cultural Notes

The concept of calculation is deeply integrated into how people approach problem-solving in various fields, from science to finance. Making calculations often involves critical thinking and creativity!
