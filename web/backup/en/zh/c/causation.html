Causation

EN→ZH

What Does Causation Mean?

  • 因果关系
  • 造成
  • 引起

Causation Pronunciation

/kɔːˈzeɪʃən/

Causation Definition

noun

Definition

The action of causing something; the relationship between cause and effect.

In ZH

造成某事的动作；因果关系。

How to Use Causation in a Sentence

  • Causation is a key concept in philosophy.
  • In science, causation helps us understand the relationship between variables.
  • The court must establish causation to find a defendant liable.
  • Many diseases have complex patterns of causation.
  • Understanding causation can help in preventing accidents.

Synonyms for Causation

  • Caustic
