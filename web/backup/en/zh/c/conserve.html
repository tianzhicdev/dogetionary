Conserve

Pronunciation:/kənˈsɜːrv/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 保护
  • 节约
  • 保存

Definitions

verb

Definition

To protect from loss or harm; to keep safe.

In ZH

保护免受损失或伤害；保持安全。

Examples

  • We must conserve water during the drought.
  • She works to conserve wildlife habitats.
  • The organization aims to conserve endangered species.
  • It's important to conserve energy by turning off lights when not in use.
  • We need to find ways to conserve our natural resources.
verb

Definition

To use a resource sparingly; to save.

In ZH

节省资源；存储。

Examples

  • To conserve resources, we should recycle more.
  • He is trying to conserve money for a new car.
  • They implemented measures to conserve energy.
  • Conserving fuel is essential for the environment.
  • We should all work together to conserve our forests.
