Collaborate

Pronunciation:/kəˈlæbəˌreɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 合作
  • 协作
  • 协同工作

Definitions

verb

Definition

to work jointly with others or together especially in an intellectual endeavor

In ZH

与他人共同工作，尤其是在智力活动中

Examples

  • Two scientists will collaborate on the research project.
  • The schools in the district collaborate to improve educational resources.
  • Artists often collaborate to create unique pieces of work.
  • The company plans to collaborate with international partners.
  • In order to solve the problem, we must collaborate with the community.
