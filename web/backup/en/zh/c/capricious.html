Capricious

EN→ZH

What Does Capricious Mean?

  • 变幻无常的
  • 任性的
  • 多变的

Capricious Pronunciation

/kəˈprɪʃəs/

Capricious Definition

adjective

Definition

Given to sudden and unaccountable changes of mood or behavior.

In ZH

情绪或行为突然且不可预测的变化。

How to Use Capricious in a Sentence

  • Her capricious nature made it difficult to predict her reactions.
  • The weather in spring can be quite capricious, changing from sunny to rainy in moments.
  • He was known for his capricious decisions that often surprised his colleagues.
  • The artist's capricious style appealed to many, as it was always fresh and unexpected.
  • Investments in the stock market can be capricious, making them risky for some investors.

Synonyms for Capricious

  • Capable
  • Caper
  • Capitulate
  • Capitulation
  • Caprice
