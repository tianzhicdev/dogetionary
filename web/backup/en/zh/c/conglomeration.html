Conglomeration

EN→ZH

What Does Conglomeration Mean?

  • 聚集体
  • 集合体
  • 混合体

Conglomeration Pronunciation

/kənˌɡlɒməˈreɪʃən/

Conglomeration Definition

noun

Definition

A collection or mixture of different things.

In ZH

不同事物的集合或混合。

How to Use Conglomeration in a Sentence

  • The city is a conglomeration of cultures and traditions.
  • She owns a conglomeration of businesses in various industries.
  • The museum features a conglomeration of art from around the world.
  • His backpack contained a conglomeration of items he thought he might need.
  • The novel is a conglomeration of different stories woven together.

Synonyms for Conglomeration

  • Concatenate
  • Concatenation
  • Concept
  • Concomitant
  • Concordant
