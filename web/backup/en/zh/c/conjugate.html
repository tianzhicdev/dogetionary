Conjugate

EN→ZH

What Does Conjugate Mean?

  • 结合
  • 动词变位
  • 合并

Conjugate Pronunciation

/ˈkɒn.dʒʊ.ɡeɪt/

Conjugate Definition

verb

Definition

To change a verb from its base form to reflect tense, mood, person, or number.

In ZH

将动词从其基本形式改变以反映时态、语气、人称或数。

How to Use Conjugate in a Sentence

  • You need to conjugate the verb 'to be' correctly.
  • He has learned to conjugate regular and irregular verbs.
  • In Spanish, students often struggle to conjugate verbs in the past tense.
  • Can you conjugate the verb 'to run' in the present simple?
  • They are teaching us how to conjugate verbs in our language class.

Cultural Notes

In language learning, conjugation is a fundamental aspect that helps students understand how to use verbs in different contexts.

noun

Definition

A form of a verb that is conjugated for a specific subject or tense.

In ZH

为特定主语或时态而变位的动词形式。

How to Use Conjugate in a Sentence

  • The conjugate of 'run' for he is 'runs'.
  • In English, the conjugates of 'to have' include 'has' and 'had'.
  • Every conjugate of a verb gives information about the subject.
  • Learning the conjugates can improve your grammar skills.
  • The teacher explained the importance of knowing the conjugates.

Synonyms for Conjugate

  • Concatenate
  • Concatenation
  • Concept
  • Concomitant
  • Concordant
