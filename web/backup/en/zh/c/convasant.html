Convasant

EN→ZH

What Does Convasant Mean?

  • 恢复期
  • 康复的
  • 复原者

Convasant Pronunciation

ˌkɒnvəˈlɛsənt

Convasant Definition

adjective

Definition

Relating to the period of recovery after illness or medical treatment.

In ZH

与生病或医疗治疗后的恢复期相关的。

How to Use Convasant in a Sentence

  • After surgery, the patient was placed in a convalescent home to aid in their recovery.
  • Convalescent care is essential for someone who has undergone a major operation.
  • The doctor advised a convalescent period of at least two weeks before returning to work.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking countries, 'convalescent homes' are facilities designed to help individuals recover from illness. This term is particularly relevant in health care settings.

noun

Definition

A person who is recovering from illness or medical treatment.

In ZH

正在从疾病或医疗治疗中恢复的人。

How to Use Convasant in a Sentence

  • The convalescent needed special care and support from family and friends.
  • She visited her convalescent friend every week to brighten his spirits.
  • A convalescent can often benefit from therapy to regain strength.

Cultural Notes

The term 'convalescent' implies a time of healing, and it often signifies a supportive environment. In some cultures, family plays a significant role in aiding convalescents.
