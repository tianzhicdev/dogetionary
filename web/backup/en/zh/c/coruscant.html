Coruscant

EN→ZH

What Does Coruscant Mean?

  • 闪闪发光的
  • 明亮的
  • 辉耀的

Coruscant Pronunciation

/ˈkɒr.ʊ.sænt/

Coruscant Definition

adjective

Definition

shining, sparkling, or flashing; emitting or reflecting light.

In ZH

闪耀、闪光或闪烁；发出或反射光。

How to Use Coruscant in a Sentence

  • The coruscant stars lit up the night sky.
  • The coruscant jewels in her necklace caught everyone's attention.
  • He described the coruscant lights of the city from his apartment.
  • The coruscant waves of the ocean shimmered under the moonlight.
  • She wore a coruscant gown that dazzled everyone at the party.
