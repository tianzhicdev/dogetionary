Chimerical

Pronunciation:/kaɪˈmɛrɪkəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幻想的
  • 空想的
  • 虚构的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Existing only as the product of unchecked imagination; fantastically visionary or improbable.

In ZH

仅作为不受控制的想象的产物而存在；幻想般的异想天开或不太可能的。

Examples

  • The chimerical project failed to gain any support.
  • His chimerical ideas about flying cars captivated the audience.
  • They embarked on a chimerical quest to find a mythical creature.
  • Many consider her chimerical art to be ahead of its time.
  • The chimerical plans for the theme park were quickly abandoned.
