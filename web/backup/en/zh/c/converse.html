Converse

Pronunciation:/kənˈvɜrs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 交谈
  • 对话
  • 谈话

Definitions

verb

Definition

To engage in conversation.

In ZH

进行交谈。

Examples

  • They like to converse during their lunch breaks.
  • It's nice to converse with someone who shares your interests.
  • She found it difficult to converse with strangers.
  • We often converse about our favorite books and movies.
  • The teacher encouraged the students to converse in English.
noun

Definition

A conversation or discourse.

In ZH

对话或谈话。

Examples

  • The two friends had a long converse about their dreams.
  • Their converse inspired new ideas for the project.
  • The converse of the discussion was insightful and enlightening.
  • After a brief converse, they reached an agreement.
  • He recalled a humorous converse from last summer.
