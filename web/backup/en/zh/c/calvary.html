Calvary

EN→ZH

What Does Calvary Mean?

  • 十字架
  • 苦难
  • 耶稣被钉十字架的地方

Calvary Pronunciation

ˈkæl.və.ri

Calvary Definition

noun

Definition

The location outside of Jerusalem where Jesus was crucified.

In ZH

耶路撒冷外的一个地方，耶稣被钉十字架的地方。

How to Use Calvary in a Sentence

  • When I heard they were filming a new biblical epic at Calvary, I thought, 'Great, now we’ll have a lot more ancient blog posts about crucifixions.'
  • If you ever find yourself visiting Calvary, just remember to pack a picnic — it’s a real 'cross' between history and lunch!
  • The historian said the reenactment at Calvary was so realistic, you'd think they had just discovered the original recipe for crucifixion.

Cultural Notes

Calvary is significant in Christianity as it represents the sacrifice of Jesus. It's often depicted in art and literature, and is central to the Easter narrative.
