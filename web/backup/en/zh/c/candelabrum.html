Candelabrum

EN→ZH

What Does Candelabrum Mean?

  • 烛台
  • 烛笼
  • 蜡烛架

Candelabrum Pronunciation

/ˌkændəˈleɪbrəm/

Candelabrum Definition

noun

Definition

A large branched candlestick or holder for multiple candles.

In ZH

一个大型的分支蜡烛台或多个蜡烛的支架。

How to Use Candelabrum in a Sentence

  • The ornate candelabrum in the dining room added an air of elegance.
  • During the ceremony, they lit the candelabrum to symbolize hope and light.
  • She found a vintage candelabrum at the flea market.
  • The candelabrum was made of brass and decorated with intricate patterns.
  • In the dark, the candelabrum cast beautiful shadows on the wall.

  • Cantankerous
