Corporeal

EN→ZH

What Does Corporeal Mean?

  • 肉体的
  • 物质的
  • 有形的

Corporeal Pronunciation

/kɔːrˈpɔːr.i.əl/

Corporeal Definition

adjective

Definition

relating to a person's body, especially as opposed to their spirit

In ZH

与人的身体有关，尤其是与灵魂相对

How to Use Corporeal in a Sentence

  • The corporeal form of the artist was captured beautifully in the sculpture.
  • Many philosophies discuss the distinction between the corporeal and the spiritual.
  • She believed in both corporeal and incorporeal beings.
  • The corporeal punishment in schools has been a controversial topic.
  • He felt a strong corporeal connection to the earth.
