Conniption

EN→ZH

What Does Conniption Mean?

  • 发怒
  • 狂怒
  • 暴怒

Conniption Pronunciation

/kəˈnɪp.ʃən/

Conniption Definition

noun

Definition

a fit of rage or hysterics; an extreme reaction of anger or agitation.

In ZH

愤怒或歇斯底里的发作；极端的愤怒或激动反应。

How to Use Conniption in a Sentence

  • When she heard the news, she had a conniption fit.
  • His unexpected arrival caused her to have a conniption.
  • I thought he was going to have a conniption over the broken vase.
  • They laughed at her conniption when the car refused to start.
  • The teacher had a conniption when she discovered the students had cheated.
