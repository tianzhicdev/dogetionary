Congruent

EN→ZH

What Does Congruent Mean?

  • 一致的
  • 相符的
  • 同形的

Congruent Pronunciation

/kənˈɡruːənt/

Congruent Definition

adjective

Definition

In agreement or harmony; corresponding in character or kind.

In ZH

一致或和谐；在特征或类型上相符。

How to Use Congruent in a Sentence

  • The two triangles are congruent in size and shape.
  • Her opinions are congruent with mine on this matter.
  • The designs were made congruent to each other for the project.
  • In mathematics, congruent shapes can be overlapped perfectly.
  • The team's goals are congruent with the company's mission.

Synonyms for Congruent

  • Concatenate
  • Concatenation
  • Concept
  • Concomitant
  • Concordant
