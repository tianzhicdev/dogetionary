Colloquy

Pronunciation:/ˈkɒləkwi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 对话
  • 谈话
  • 交谈

Definitions

noun

Definition

A formal conversation or dialogue.

In ZH

正式的对话或谈话。

Examples

  • The colloquy between the two leaders helped to ease tensions.
  • During the colloquy, they reached a mutual understanding.
  • A colloquy on ethics was held at the university.
  • The authors participated in a colloquy about their latest book.
  • Her last colloquy with her friend was very enlightening.
