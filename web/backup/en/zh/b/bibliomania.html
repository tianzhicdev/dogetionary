Bibliomania

Pronunciation:/ˌbɪbliˈmeɪniə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 书籍狂热
  • 藏书癖

Definitions

noun

Definition

An extreme passion for collecting books.

In ZH

对收集书籍的极度热情。

Examples

  • He was diagnosed with bibliomania after accumulating thousands of books.
  • Her bibliomania led her to spend all her savings on rare first editions.
  • The library hosted a seminar on bibliomania and its impact on collectors.
  • Bibliomania can sometimes result in cluttered living spaces filled with books.
  • Many famous authors have expressed their own struggles with bibliomania.
