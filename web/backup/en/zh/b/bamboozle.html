Bamboozle

Pronunciation:/bæmˈbuː.zəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 欺骗
  • 愚弄
  • 诈骗

Definitions

verb

Definition

To trick or deceive someone, often in a playful or humorous way.

In ZH

欺骗或愚弄某人，通常带有玩笑或幽默的意味。

Examples

  • He tried to bamboozle me into paying for his lunch.
  • The magician bamboozled the audience with his clever tricks.
  • They bamboozled the investors with false claims about their product.
  • Don't let him bamboozle you; he's just trying to sell you a scam.
  • She was bamboozled by her friends into believing it was her birthday.
