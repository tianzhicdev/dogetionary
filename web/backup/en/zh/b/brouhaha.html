Brouhaha

Pronunciation:/ˈbruːhɑːhɑː/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 喧闹
  • 骚动
  • 嘈杂

Definitions

noun

Definition

A noisy and overexcited reaction or response to something, often involving conflict or debate.

In ZH

对某事的嘈杂且过于兴奋的反应或回应，通常涉及冲突或辩论。

Examples

  • The announcement of the new policy caused quite a brouhaha among the employees.
  • After the game, there was a brouhaha about the referee's controversial decision.
  • The celebrity's tweet resulted in a social media brouhaha that lasted for days.
  • There was a brouhaha at the festival when a famous band unexpectedly showed up.
  • The proposal resulted in a brouhaha at the town hall meeting.
