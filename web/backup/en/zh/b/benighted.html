Benighted

EN→ZH

What Does Benighted Mean?

  • 无知的
  • 被夜色笼罩的

Benighted Pronunciation

/bɪˈnaɪtɪd/

Benighted Definition

adjective

Definition

In a state of moral or intellectual darkness; ignorant.

In ZH

处于道德或智力的黑暗状态；无知的。

How to Use Benighted in a Sentence

  • He was benighted in his understanding of the world.
  • The citizens were benighted and lacked education.
  • Don't let your beliefs remain benighted; seek knowledge.
  • She felt benighted in a society that valued ignorance.
  • The benighted villagers had little access to modern technology.

Synonyms for Benighted

  • Benediction
  • Benign
  • Benignly
  • Benison
