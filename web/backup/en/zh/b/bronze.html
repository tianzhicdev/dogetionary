Bronze

Pronunciation:/brɒnz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 铜
  • 青铜

Definitions

noun

Definition

A metal alloy consisting mainly of copper, commonly used for making statues, coins, and various industrial applications.

In ZH

一种主要由铜组成的金属合金，常用于制作雕像、硬币和各种工业应用。

Examples

  • The statue was made of bronze and stood in the town square.
  • Bronze is often used for coins due to its durability.
  • He won a bronze medal in the Olympics.
  • The artist preferred to work with bronze for its unique finish.
  • Bronze was historically significant in many ancient cultures.
adjective

Definition

Of a brownish-gold color, resembling bronze.

In ZH

一种棕褐色的金色，类似于铜。

Examples

  • She chose a bronze dress for the gala event.
  • The walls were painted in a warm bronze hue.
  • Bronze finishes are popular in modern home decor.
  • The sunrise cast a beautiful bronze light over the landscape.
  • The awards were given in bronze, silver, and gold.
