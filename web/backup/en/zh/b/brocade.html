Brocade

Pronunciation:/brəˈkeɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 锦缎
  • 提花织物
  • 金丝织物

Definitions

noun

Definition

A rich fabric woven with a raised pattern, typically in gold or silver thread.

In ZH

一种用金线或银线织成的，有浮雕图案的华丽织物。

Examples

  • The dress was made of luxurious brocade.
  • She chose a brocade fabric for the upholstery.
  • The royal robes were embroidered with intricate brocade patterns.
  • He gifted her a brocade handbag for her birthday.
  • The brocade curtains added elegance to the room.

Cultural Notes

Brocade is often associated with luxury and is commonly used in ceremonial garments or decorative items in various cultures.
