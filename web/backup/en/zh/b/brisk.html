Brisk

Pronunciation:brɪsk
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 轻快的
  • 活泼的
  • 迅速的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Quick and energetic; lively and brisk in manner.

In ZH

快速而充满活力的；举止活泼的。

Examples

  • She took a brisk walk in the morning to start her day.
  • The brisk pace of the music made everyone want to dance.
  • He gave a brisk nod to acknowledge her greeting.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, describing someone as 'brisk' often implies an efficient and enthusiastic approach to activities, whether it's walking, working, or even speaking.

noun

Definition

A brisk pace or manner; a quick and lively movement.

In ZH

轻快的步伐或举止；快速而生动的动作。

Examples

  • The brisk of his walk indicated he was in a hurry.
  • There was a brisk in her voice as she called for attention.
  • The brisk of the conversation kept everyone engaged.

Cultural Notes

Using 'brisk' as a noun is less common, but can describe an atmosphere or a way of moving that conveys urgency and activity, often in business or social contexts.
