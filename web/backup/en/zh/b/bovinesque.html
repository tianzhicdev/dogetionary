Bovinesque

Pronunciation:/ˌboʊ.vɪˈnɛsk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 牛的
  • 像牛的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Resembling or characteristic of a bovine; having qualities associated with cattle.

In ZH

像牛一样的；具有与牛相关的特征。

Examples

  • The artist's latest work has a bovinesque quality that evokes the serenity of nature.
  • His slow movements had a bovinesque grace that seemed almost hypnotic.
  • She wore a bovinesque expression as she contemplated her next meal.
  • The character in the play had a bovinesque demeanor, which made him relatable to the audience.
  • The landscape was dotted with bovinesque figures, grazing peacefully in the fields.
