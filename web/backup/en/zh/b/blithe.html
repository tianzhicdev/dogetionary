Blithe

EN→ZH

What Does Blithe Mean?

  • 快乐的
  • 无忧的
  • 轻松的

Blithe Pronunciation

/blaɪð/

Blithe Definition

adjective

Definition

Showing a casual and cheerful indifference considered to be callous or improper.

In ZH

表现出一种轻松愉快的漠不关心，常被认为是冷漠或不恰当的。

How to Use Blithe in a Sentence

  • She has a blithe spirit that makes everyone around her feel happy.
  • His blithe attitude towards the serious situation annoyed his friends.
  • They approached the task with a blithe disregard for the rules.
  • The blithe couple danced through the streets without a care in the world.
  • Despite the challenges ahead, she remained blithe and optimistic.

Synonyms for Blithe

  • Blithering
  • Blithesome
