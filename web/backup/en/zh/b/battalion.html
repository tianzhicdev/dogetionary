Battalion

Pronunciation:/bəˈtæljən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 营
  • 大队
  • 军营

Definitions

noun

Definition

A large body of troops organized to act together.

In ZH

一大群组织在一起行动的军队。

Examples

  • The battalion was deployed to the conflict zone.
  • Each battalion consists of several companies.
  • They formed a new battalion to strengthen the defenses.
  • The battalion marched in formation during the parade.
  • He served in a battalion known for its bravery.
