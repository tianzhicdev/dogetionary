Bears

EN→ZH

What Does Bears Mean?

  • 熊
  • 承担者
  • 忍耐者

Bears Pronunciation

bɛrz

Bears Definition

verb

Definition

To carry, support, or endure something.

In ZH

承载、支撑或忍受某物。

How to Use Bears in a Sentence

  • She bears the weight of her responsibilities with grace.
  • He cannot bear the thought of losing his friends.
  • They bear the burden of their family's expectations.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, the idea of 'bearing' often carries a sense of responsibility and endurance, suggesting strength in difficult situations.

noun

Definition

Large, heavy mammals known for their thick fur and short tails, typically found in forests and mountains.

In ZH

大型、笨重的哺乳动物，以浓密的毛发和短尾巴为特征，通常生活在森林和山区。

How to Use Bears in a Sentence

  • Bears often hibernate during the winter months.
  • The grizzly bear is known for its strength and size.
  • Many children love stories about bears like Winnie the Pooh.

Cultural Notes

Bears are significant in many cultures, often symbolizing strength and bravery. They appear in folklore and children's stories across the world.
