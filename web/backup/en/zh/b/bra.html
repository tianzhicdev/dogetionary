Bra

EN→ZH

What Does Bra Mean?

  • 胸罩
  • 文胸

Bra Pronunciation

brɑː

Bra Definition

noun

Definition

A bra is a women's undergarment designed to support the breasts.

In ZH

胸罩是为支持女性乳房而设计的一种内衣。

How to Use Bra in a Sentence

  • She wore a beautiful dress with a matching bra.
  • It's important to choose a comfortable bra for everyday wear.
  • Many women prefer wireless bras for extra comfort.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, bras are considered a standard part of women's clothing. The style and type of bra can vary widely based on fashion trends, personal preference, and the occasion.

verb

Definition

To bra means to cook food in a covered pot with a small amount of liquid, usually at low heat.

In ZH

'Bra'表示在一个封闭的锅中用少量液体以低温烹饪食物。

How to Use Bra in a Sentence

  • You can braise the meat to make it tender and flavorful.
  • They decided to bra the vegetables with spices for dinner.
  • The chef taught us how to bra the chicken properly.

Cultural Notes

The verb 'bra' is less common and primarily used in culinary contexts. It is important to note that this usage is not related to the noun 'bra'.
