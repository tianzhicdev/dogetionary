Bonjour

EN→ZH

What Does Bonjour Mean?

  • 你好
  • 早安

Bonjour Pronunciation

bɔ̃.ʒuʁ

Bonjour Definition

interjection

Definition

A greeting used in the French language, typically meaning 'good morning' or 'hello'.

In ZH

法语中使用的问候，通常意味着“早上好”或“你好”。

How to Use Bonjour in a Sentence

  • Bonjour! How are you today?
  • She greeted him with a cheerful 'bonjour'.
  • In France, it's common to say 'bonjour' when entering a shop.
  • He waved and said 'bonjour' to his neighbors.
  • Every morning, I start my day with a friendly 'bonjour'.
  • It's polite to say 'bonjour' before asking for help.

Synonyms for Bonjour

  • Bon Vivant
  • Bond
  • Bonhomie
  • Bonnet
