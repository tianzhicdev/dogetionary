Balderdash

Pronunciation:/ˈbɔːldərˌdæʃ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 胡言乱语
  • 废话
  • 无稽之谈

Definitions

noun

Definition

senseless talk or writing; nonsense.

In ZH

无意义的谈话或写作；废话。

Examples

  • What he said was pure balderdash.
  • Don't listen to her; it's all balderdash.
  • His arguments were nothing but balderdash.
  • The idea that the earth is flat is complete balderdash.
  • She referred to the rumors as balderdash.
