Behoove

EN→ZH

What Does Behoove Mean?

  • 有必要
  • 对...有责任
  • 适合

Behoove Pronunciation

/bɪˈhuːv/

Behoove Definition

verb

Definition

to be necessary or proper for someone; to be one's duty or responsibility.

In ZH

对某人来说是必要或恰当的；是某人的责任或职责。

How to Use Behoove in a Sentence

  • It would behoove you to study for the exam.
  • It does not behoove a leader to act irresponsibly.
  • It would behoove us to consider all options before making a decision.
  • It is a duty that would behoove any conscientious citizen.
  • To understand the topic well, it would behoove you to do some additional reading.

Synonyms for Behoove

  • Behemoth
  • Beholden
