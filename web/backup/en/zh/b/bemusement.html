Bemusement

Pronunciation:/bɪˈmjuːzmənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 困惑
  • 迷惑
  • 不解
  • 惊愕

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of being confused or puzzled, often with a sense of amusement.

In ZH

感到困惑或困扰的状态，常伴有一种轻松的愉悦感。

Examples

  • She looked at him in bemusement after hearing his explanation.
  • The bemusement on his face was evident when he failed to understand the joke.
  • In a moment of bemusement, I completely forgot what I was going to say.
  • The children’s bemusement at the magic trick was heartwarming.
  • His bemusement turned to laughter as the situation unfolded.
