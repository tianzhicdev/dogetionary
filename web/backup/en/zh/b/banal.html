Banal

Pronunciation:/bəˈnæl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 平凡的
  • 无聊的
  • 陈腐的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

So lacking in originality as to be obvious and boring.

In ZH

缺乏原创性，以至于显得显而易见且无聊。

Examples

  • The lecture was so banal that I struggled to stay awake.
  • Her writing is full of banal clichés that offer nothing new.
  • We need to find a way to make this topic less banal and more interesting.
  • His comments were just a banal repetition of what everyone else was saying.
  • In a world filled with banal entertainment, originality is hard to find.
