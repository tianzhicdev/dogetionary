Babeldom

Pronunciation:/ˈbeɪbəlˌdɛm/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 巴别
  • 巴别塔的城市

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state or condition of confusion and lack of understanding, often due to language barriers.

In ZH

一种混乱和缺乏理解的状态，通常由于语言障碍造成。

Examples

  • In the international conference, babeldom reigned as delegates struggled to communicate.
  • Her attempts to translate led to babeldom among the tourists.
  • The company's global expansion faced babeldom when local teams failed to align.
  • During the multilingual meeting, babeldom was inevitable without sufficient interpreters.
  • The internet sometimes resembles babeldom, where users from different cultures clash.
