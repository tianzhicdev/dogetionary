Brobdingnagian

Pronunciation:/ˌbrɒb.dɪŋˈnæɡ.i.ən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 巨人国的
  • 非常巨大的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Gigantic; enormous.

In ZH

巨大的; 庞大的。

Examples

  • The brobdingnagian statue impressed everyone who walked by.
  • She had a brobdingnagian appetite for adventure.
  • The brobdingnagian size of the building was overwhelming.
  • His brobdingnagian ego often made him difficult to work with.
  • The artist created a brobdingnagian mural on the side of the building.

Cultural Notes

The term 'brobdingnagian' originates from Jonathan Swift's 'Gulliver's Travels', referring to the land of the giants.

  • He felt like a brobdingnagian in a world of little people.
