Bravado

Pronunciation:/brəˈvæd.oʊ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 虚张声势
  • 勇敢的表现
  • 自信的态度

Definitions

noun

Definition

a show of boldness or confidence intended to impress or intimidate others; swagger.

In ZH

为了给他人留下深刻印象或威吓他人而表现出的大胆或自信；虚张声势。

Examples

  • His bravado impressed everyone at the party.
  • She walked into the room with a sense of bravado.
  • Despite his bravado, he was nervous inside.
  • The soldier showed great bravado during the battle.
  • They put on a bravado to hide their fears.
