Baksheesh

EN→ZH

What Does Baksheesh Mean?

  • 小费
  • 赈济金
  • 酬金

Baksheesh Pronunciation

/bækˈʃiːʃ/

Baksheesh Definition

noun

Definition

A sum of money given as a tip or bribe, especially in Middle Eastern and South Asian cultures.

In ZH

在中东和南亚文化中，作为小费或贿赂给出的一笔钱。

How to Use Baksheesh in a Sentence

  • The waiter expected a baksheesh for his service.
  • In some countries, giving baksheesh is a common practice.
  • She slipped the guide a baksheesh after the tour.
  • Baksheesh is sometimes necessary to get better service.
  • In many markets, vendors appreciate a little baksheesh for their efforts.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, particularly in the Middle East and parts of Asia, baksheesh is not only a tip but also a form of social interaction.
