Ballyhooed

EN→ZH

What Does Ballyhooed Mean?

  • 大肆宣传
  • 喧闹
  • 夸张宣传

Ballyhooed Pronunciation

/ˌbæliˈhuːd/

Ballyhooed Definition

verb

Definition

Publicized or promoted extravagantly or noisily.

In ZH

以夸张或喧闹的方式宣传或推广。

How to Use Ballyhooed in a Sentence

  • The movie was ballyhooed as the best of the year.
  • The new restaurant was ballyhooed by food critics before its opening.
  • She ballyhooed her charity event to attract more donors.
  • The celebrity ballyhooed the launch of her new product on social media.
  • Ballyhooed by the media, the event attracted thousands of attendees.

Synonyms for Ballyhooed

  • Baldachin
  • Balderdash
  • Baleful
  • Balkanization
  • Balletic
