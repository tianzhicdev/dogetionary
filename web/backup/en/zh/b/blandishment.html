Blandishment

EN→ZH

What Does Blandishment Mean?

  • 劝诱
  • 奉承
  • 甜言蜜语

Blandishment Pronunciation

/ˈblændɪʃmənt/

Blandishment Definition

noun

Definition

a flattering remark or action designed to persuade someone to do something

In ZH

一种旨在说服某人做某事的恭维语或行为

How to Use Blandishment in a Sentence

  • The salesman used every blandishment in the book to get the customer to buy the car.
  • Despite his blandishments, she remained firm in her decision to leave.
  • She offered blandishments to the committee in hopes of securing the grant.
  • He was not swayed by the blandishments of his colleagues to change his opinion.
  • Blandishments can sometimes be more effective than harsh arguments.

Synonyms for Blandishment

  • Bland
  • Blandishments
  • Blarney
  • Blasé
  • Blatherskite
