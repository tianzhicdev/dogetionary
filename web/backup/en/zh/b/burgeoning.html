Burgeoning

EN→ZH

What Does Burgeoning Mean?

  • 迅速发展的
  • 成长中的
  • 蓬勃的

Burgeoning Pronunciation

/ˈbɜːrdʒənɪŋ/

Burgeoning Definition

adjective

Definition

beginning to grow or increase rapidly; flourishing

In ZH

开始迅速增长或增加；繁荣的

How to Use Burgeoning in a Sentence

  • The burgeoning tech industry is attracting many investors.
  • She has a burgeoning interest in environmental issues.
  • The city is known for its burgeoning arts scene.
  • There is a burgeoning market for organic food.
  • Their burgeoning relationship has been the talk of the town.

Synonyms for Burgeoning

  • Burgeon
  • Burnish
