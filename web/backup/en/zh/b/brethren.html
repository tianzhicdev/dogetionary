Brethren

EN→ZH

What Does Brethren Mean?

  • 兄弟们
  • 弟兄们
  • 兄弟

Brethren Pronunciation

/ˈbrɛðərən/

Brethren Definition

noun

Definition

Brothers; specifically used in a religious or formal context to refer to members of a brotherhood or community.

In ZH

兄弟；特别是用于宗教或正式语境中，指兄弟关系或团体的成员。

How to Use Brethren in a Sentence

  • The brethren gathered for a meeting.
  • He referred to his brethren in faith.
  • The congregation welcomed the new brethren.
  • The organization consists of a diverse group of brethren.
  • The message was meant for all brethren.
