Be

EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 是
  • 存在
  • 成为

Definitions

Definition

To exist or live.

In ZH

存在或生活。

Examples

  • I used to think I could be a superhero, but then I realized I can't even be on time for my Zoom meetings!
  • In a world where cats can be influencers, I just want to be the person who feeds them.
  • Scientists say that to be or not to be is a question, but I think the real question is: can I be a millionaire by next week?

Definition

To indicate identity or role.

In ZH

指示身份或角色。

Examples

  • I want to be the next big thing in cooking, but my specialty is burning toast!
  • If I could be any animal, I would be a sloth, because they seem to have mastered the art of doing nothing.
  • They say you can be anything you want, but I think my true calling is to be a professional napper.

Definition

To become or change into a particular state.

In ZH

变成或转变为某种状态。

Examples

  • I thought I could be a morning person, but my bed has other plans every day!
  • After that last slice of pizza, I can officially be classified as a food coma expert.
  • If my plants could talk, they would definitely be asking for a vacation from my gardening skills.
