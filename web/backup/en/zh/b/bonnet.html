Bonnet

Pronunciation:/ˈbɒn.ɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 帽子
  • 罩子
  • 引擎盖

Definitions

noun

Definition

A type of headwear that is typically tied under the chin and worn by women and girls, or a soft cap worn by infants.

In ZH

一种通常在下巴处系着的头饰，通常由女性和女孩佩戴，或婴儿穿戴的软帽子。

Examples

  • She wore a beautiful bonnet to the garden party.
  • The baby looked adorable in her pink bonnet.
  • In the past, bonnets were a common fashion for women.
  • He lifted the bonnet of the car to check the engine.
  • The bonnet of the car was damaged in the accident.
noun

Definition

The hinged cover that rests over the engine of a motor vehicle, allowing access to the engine compartment.

In ZH

一个铰接的盖子，覆盖在机动车的引擎上，以便进入引擎舱。

Examples

  • I opened the bonnet to inspect the oil levels.
  • The mechanic found the problem under the bonnet.
  • Make sure to close the bonnet before driving.
  • She painted the bonnet of her car a bright red color.
  • The design of the bonnet can affect the aerodynamics of the vehicle.
