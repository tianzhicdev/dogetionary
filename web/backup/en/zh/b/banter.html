Banter

EN→ZH

What Does Banter Mean?

  • 开玩笑
  • 打趣
  • 戏谑

Banter Pronunciation

/ˈbæn.tər/

Banter Definition

noun

Definition

The playful and friendly exchange of teasing remarks.

In ZH

调皮友好的互相调侃的交流。

How to Use Banter in a Sentence

  • Their banter always makes me smile.
  • The friends engaged in light-hearted banter during dinner.
  • She loved the banter between her coworkers.
  • His banter often lightens the mood in the room.
  • We enjoyed some friendly banter after the meeting.
verb

Definition

To exchange teasing remarks; to engage in light-hearted conversation.

In ZH

互相调侃；进行轻松的对话。

How to Use Banter in a Sentence

  • They bantered back and forth for hours.
  • He loves to banter with his siblings.
  • She banters with her friends over coffee.
  • We often banter about our favorite TV shows.
  • The two comedians bantered on stage.

Synonyms for Banter

  • Banal
  • Banana
  • Bandersnatch
  • Bane
