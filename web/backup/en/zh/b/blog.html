Blog

EN→ZH

What Does Blog Mean?

  • 博客
  • 日志
  • 网络日志

Blog Pronunciation

blɔɡ

Blog Definition

noun

Definition

A regularly updated website or web page, typically run by an individual or small group, written in an informal or conversational style.

In ZH

一个定期更新的网站或网页，通常由个人或小组运行，采用非正式或对话的风格。

How to Use Blog in a Sentence

  • I started a blog about my cat's adventures, and now she's more famous than I am!
  • After reading too many conspiracy theories on blogs, I’m convinced that aliens are running for president!
  • My friend’s travel blog made me realize that my couch is the most traveled place in my life.

Cultural Notes

Blogs are popular platforms for sharing opinions, experiences, and hobbies. They can cover a wide range of topics, from personal stories to professional advice.

verb

Definition

To write about or maintain a blog.

In ZH

撰写或维护一个博客。

How to Use Blog in a Sentence

  • I decided to blog about my journey to becoming a professional pancake flipper!
  • He blogs every day, but I think he needs to take a break from posting pictures of his breakfast!
  • She blogs so much that her laptop is starting to grow a beard from all the crumbs!

Cultural Notes

Blogging as a verb emphasizes the activity of creating content online, often leading to communities built around shared interests.

Synonyms for Blog
