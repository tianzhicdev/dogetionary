Baroque

Pronunciation:/bəˈroʊk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 巴洛克
  • 繁复的
  • 巴洛克风格

Definitions

noun

Definition

A style of architecture, art, and music that emerged in Europe in the late 16th century and is characterized by exaggerated motion, clear detail, and dramatic intensity.

In ZH

一种在16世纪末出现在欧洲的建筑、艺术和音乐风格，其特点是夸张的动作、清晰的细节和戏剧性的强度。

Examples

  • The baroque period produced many famous artists and architects.
  • She decorated her home in a baroque style with ornate furnishings.
  • The cathedral is a fine example of baroque architecture.
  • Baroque music features complex harmonies and elaborate ornamentation.
  • The baroque paintings often depict religious themes with dramatic lighting.
adjective

Definition

Relating to the baroque style or period; characterized by ornate detail and grandeur.

In ZH

与巴洛克风格或时期相关；特征是华丽的细节和宏伟。

Examples

  • The baroque details of the sculpture were breathtaking.
  • Baroque art is known for its extravagant and theatrical style.
  • She wore a dress with baroque elements like lace and embroidery.
  • The novelist's narrative style was described as baroque due to its complexity.
  • Many baroque churches are adorned with gilded decorations.
