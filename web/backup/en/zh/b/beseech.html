Beseech

EN→ZH

What Does Beseech Mean?

  • 恳求
  • 乞求
  • 请求

Beseech Pronunciation

/bɪˈsiːtʃ/

Beseech Definition

verb

Definition

to urgently and fervently request or implore someone to do something

In ZH

急切和热切地请求或恳求某人做某事

How to Use Beseech in a Sentence

  • I beseech you to help me in this time of need.
  • The child beseeched her mother for a new toy.
  • He fell to his knees and beseeched the king for mercy.
  • She beseeched her friends to attend her recital.
  • They beseeched the officials to reconsider their decision.

Synonyms for Beseech

  • Beseeching
  • Besmirch
