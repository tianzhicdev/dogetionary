Bifrost

EN→ZH

What Does Bifrost Mean?

  • 比弗罗斯特

Bifrost Pronunciation

/ˈbaɪfrɒst/

Bifrost Definition

noun

Definition

In Norse mythology, Bifrost is the rainbow bridge that connects Midgard (the realm of humans) to Asgard (the realm of the gods).

In ZH

在北欧神话中，比弗罗斯特是连接人类世界（米德加德）和神的世界（阿斯加德）的彩虹桥。

How to Use Bifrost in a Sentence

  • Bifrost is often depicted as a shimmering bridge beckoning the gods.
  • In the stories, Bifrost is guarded by the god Heimdall.
  • When the gods need to travel to Earth, they use Bifrost.
  • The destruction of Bifrost signifies the onset of Ragnarök.
  • Many artists have imagined how Bifrost might look in their works.

Cultural Notes

Bifrost is a significant element in Norse mythology and has been popularized in modern media, particularly in comic book adaptations and films.

Synonyms for Bifrost

  • Bifid
  • Bifurcate
  • Bifurcation
