Bravura

Pronunciation:/brəˈvjʊərə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 华彩片段
  • 演技高超的表演

Definitions

noun

Definition

A show of boldness or brilliance, especially in the arts; a display of daring or skill.

In ZH

一种在艺术中表现出的大胆或辉煌，尤其是在艺术中的炫耀或技巧的展示。

Examples

  • The pianist concluded the concert with a bravura performance that left the audience breathless.
  • Her bravura in the painting showcased her exceptional talent and creativity.
  • In the opera, the soprano's bravura aria was the highlight of the evening.
  • The dancer's bravura style captivated everyone in the theater.
  • His bravura speech inspired the audience to take action.
