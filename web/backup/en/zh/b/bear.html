Bear

Pronunciation:bɛər
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 熊
  • 承担
  • 忍受

Definitions

noun

Definition

A large mammal with thick fur and a short tail, typically found in forests or icy regions.

In ZH

一种大型哺乳动物，通常生活在森林或冰冷地区，毛发厚实，尾巴短。

Examples

  • The bear roamed through the forest looking for food.
  • We saw a bear at the national park during our trip.
  • Bears can hibernate during the winter when food is scarce.

Cultural Notes

Bears are significant in many cultures and often symbolize strength and bravery.

verb

Definition

To carry or support the weight of something.

In ZH

承受或支撑某物的重量。

Examples

  • She had to bear the heavy load on her back all day.
  • He could not bear the thought of losing his best friend.
  • They bear the responsibility for the project’s success.

Cultural Notes

This usage is common in both everyday conversation and in formal contexts.

verb

Definition

To tolerate or endure something unpleasant.

In ZH

忍受或容忍某种不愉快的事情。

Examples

  • I cannot bear the noise from the construction outside.
  • He had to bear with her constant complaining.
  • She bears the pain quietly without showing any signs of discomfort.

Cultural Notes

This meaning often refers to emotional or physical hardships.
