Brazenly

EN→ZH

What Does Brazenly Mean?

  • 厚颜无耻地
  • 大剌剌地
  • 无耻地

Brazenly Pronunciation

/ˈbreɪ.zən.li/

Brazenly Definition

adverb

Definition

in a bold and shameless manner

In ZH

以大胆和厚颜无耻的方式

How to Use Brazenly in a Sentence

  • She brazenly ignored the rules set by her employer.
  • He brazenly claimed credit for someone else's work.
  • They brazenly walked into the event without tickets.
  • The student brazenly confronted the teacher about the unfair grading.
  • She brazenly expressed her opinions, regardless of the audience.
