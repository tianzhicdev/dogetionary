Brooding

EN→ZH

What Does Brooding Mean?

  • 沉思的
  • 忧郁的
  • 沉闷的

Brooding Pronunciation

/ˈbruːdɪŋ/

Brooding Definition

adjective

Definition

showing deep unhappiness of thought

In ZH

表现出深思的忧郁

How to Use Brooding in a Sentence

  • He sat in a brooding silence, lost in his thoughts.
  • Her brooding expression suggested that something was bothering her.
  • The brooding clouds overhead hinted at an approaching storm.
  • He has a brooding personality that makes him seem mysterious.
  • The film's brooding atmosphere set a serious tone.
noun

Definition

the act of thinking deeply about something, often in a melancholic way

In ZH

沉思的行为，通常带有忧郁的感觉

How to Use Brooding in a Sentence

  • His brooding over past mistakes affected his mood.
  • The artist's brooding inspired many of his works.
  • Her tendency for brooding led her to write poetry.
  • Brooding can be a form of introspection.
  • The novel explores the theme of brooding as a coping mechanism.

Synonyms for Brooding

  • Broaching
  • Brobdingnagian
  • Brocade
  • Brogue
  • Bromide
