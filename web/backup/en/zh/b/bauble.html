Bauble

EN→ZH

What Does Bauble Mean?

  • 小饰品
  • 装饰品
  • 玩物

Bauble Pronunciation

/ˈbɔː.bəl/

Bauble Definition

noun

Definition

A small, showy ornament or decoration, often of little value.

In ZH

一种小而显眼的装饰品，通常价值不高。

How to Use Bauble in a Sentence

  • She wore a necklace with a colorful bauble.
  • The Christmas tree was adorned with shiny baubles.
  • He received a bauble as a gift for his birthday.
  • The store sells various baubles for the holiday season.
  • Children love to collect baubles during festive occasions.
