Basest

Pronunciation:/ˈbeɪ.sɪst/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 最卑鄙的
  • 最低的
  • 最低级的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

the most base; lacking higher qualities of mind or spirit; morally low or vile

In ZH

最低级的；缺乏更高的心灵或精神特质；道德低下或卑鄙的

Examples

  • He showed his basest instincts during the argument.
  • The novel explores the basest desires of humanity.
  • In his basest moments, he succumbed to greed.
  • Her behavior was the basest I've ever seen.
  • They acted out of their basest fears.
