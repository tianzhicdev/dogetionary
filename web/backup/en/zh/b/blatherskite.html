Blatherskite

Pronunciation:/ˈblæðərskaɪt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 胡言乱语
  • 废话
  • 无聊的话

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who talks nonsense or foolishly; a person who engages in frivolous chatter.

In ZH

一个说胡言乱语或愚蠢的人；一个从事琐碎闲聊的人。

Examples

  • He is such a blatherskite, always talking about things that make no sense.
  • During the meeting, her blatherskite distracted everyone from the main topic.
  • Avoiding blatherskites in conversation can help keep discussions productive.
  • The politician was accused of being a blatherskite due to his empty promises.
  • It's hard to listen to a blatherskite when you just want straightforward answers.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in a humorous or derogatory context to describe someone who speaks a lot but lacks substance.
