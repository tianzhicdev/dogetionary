Belligerent

Pronunciation:/bəˈlɪdʒərənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 好斗的
  • 交战的
  • 敌对的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

hostile and aggressive; inclined to or exhibiting assertiveness in conflict.

In ZH

敌对的，侵略性的；在冲突中表现出或倾向于表现出攻击性。

Examples

  • The belligerent nations declared war on each other.
  • He became belligerent after a few drinks.
  • The belligerent tone of his voice made everyone uncomfortable.
  • The group was known for its belligerent behavior during protests.
  • Their belligerent attitude towards negotiations led to a breakdown in talks.
noun

Definition

a nation or person engaged in war or conflict, as recognized by international law.

In ZH

在国际法认可的战争或冲突中参与的国家或个人。

Examples

  • The belligerents in the conflict signed a peace treaty.
  • Each belligerent was required to respect the terms of the ceasefire.
  • The rights of belligerents are protected under the Geneva Conventions.
  • As a belligerent, the country must follow certain rules of war.
  • The involvement of neutral parties can help mediate disputes between belligerents.
