Bibelot

What Does Bibelot Mean?

  • 小玩意

Bibelot Pronunciation

/ˈbɪb.ləˌloʊ/

Bibelot Definition

noun

Definition

A small decorative object or trinket.

In ZH

小装饰物或饰品。

How to Use Bibelot in a Sentence

  • She has a collection of bibelots from her travels around the world.
  • The shelf was adorned with cute bibelots that caught the eye.
  • He gave her a bibelot as a token of his affection.
  • The store specializes in vintage bibelots from the 19th century.
  • Every corner of the room was filled with colorful bibelots.
