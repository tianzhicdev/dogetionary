Bibliophile

Pronunciation:/ˈbɪbliəˌfaɪl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 书迷
  • 藏书家

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who loves or collects books, especially as examples of fine or unusual printing, binding, or the like.

In ZH

一个热爱或收集书籍的人，尤其是作为精美或独特印刷、装帧等的例子。

Examples

  • She is a true bibliophile with a vast collection of rare books.
  • His bibliophile tendencies often lead him to spend hours in the library.
  • Every bibliophile knows the importance of a well-stocked bookshelf.
  • He inherited his grandfather's bibliophile passion for collecting first editions.
  • As a bibliophile, she enjoys attending book fairs and literary festivals.
