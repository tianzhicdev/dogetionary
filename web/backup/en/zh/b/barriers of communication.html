Barriers Of Communication

Pronunciation:ˈbɛr.i.ərz əv kəˌmjunɪˈkeɪʃən
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 交流障碍
  • 沟通障碍

Definitions

noun

Definition

Obstacles that prevent effective exchange of information between individuals or groups.

In ZH

阻碍个人或团体之间有效信息交流的障碍。

Examples

  • Language differences often create barriers of communication in international business.
  • Cultural misunderstandings can also be significant barriers of communication.
  • Using jargon can lead to barriers of communication in a diverse team.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, effective communication relies not just on language but also on understanding cultural norms and cues. Awareness of these barriers is essential for successful interactions.
