Bifid

EN→ZH

What Does Bifid Mean?

  • 分叉的
  • 二裂的

Bifid Pronunciation

/ˈbɪfɪd/

Bifid Definition

adjective

Definition

Divided into two parts or lobes.

In ZH

分为两部分或两瓣的。

How to Use Bifid in a Sentence

  • The bifid structure of the leaf helps in water collection.
  • In biology, bifid structures can be observed in certain species of plants.
  • The bifid tail of the fish aids in its swimming efficiency.
  • Some insects have bifid antennae that enhance sensory perception.
  • The bifid appearance of the muscle was noted during the examination.
