Burgeon

EN→ZH

What Does Burgeon Mean?

  • 迅速增长
  • 繁荣
  • 发展
  • 急剧增加

Burgeon Pronunciation

/ˈbɜːrdʒən/

Burgeon Definition

verb

Definition

to begin to grow or increase rapidly; to flourish

In ZH

开始迅速增长或增加；繁荣

How to Use Burgeon in a Sentence

  • The city's population began to burgeon after the new industry was established.
  • Her enthusiasm for the project continued to burgeon as more people got involved.
  • The plant will burgeon in the right conditions.
  • Interest in the subject has burgeoned in recent years.
  • The startup saw its profits burgeon after the successful launch.

Synonyms for Burgeon

  • Burgeoning
  • Burnish
