Beguilement

Pronunciation:/bɪˈɡaɪlmənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 欺骗
  • 迷惑
  • 诱惑

Definitions

noun

Definition

the act of charming or enchanting someone, often in a deceptive way

In ZH

迷惑或欺骗某人的行为，通常是通过魅力或吸引力来实现的

Examples

  • The beguilement of the city lights was overwhelming.
  • She used her beguilement to win over the crowd.
  • His beguilement was so effective that no one saw through his plan.
  • The artist's beguilement of the audience left them in awe.
  • Through her beguilement, she created a magical atmosphere.
