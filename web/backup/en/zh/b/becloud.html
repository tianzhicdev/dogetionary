Becloud

EN→ZH

What Does Becloud Mean?

  • 使模糊
  • 使朦胧
  • 使混乱

Becloud Pronunciation

/bɪˈklaʊd/

Becloud Definition

verb

Definition

to make obscure or unclear; to cloud or confuse.

In ZH

使模糊或不清楚；使混乱或困惑。

How to Use Becloud in a Sentence

  • The details of the plan were beclouded by the lack of information.
  • Her thoughts were beclouded by worry about the future.
  • The artist's work often beclouds the line between reality and imagination.
  • The fog beclouded the landscape, making it hard to see.
  • Conflicting reports beclouded the truth of the situation.
