Bond

EN→ZH

What Does Bond Mean?

  • 债券
  • 纽带
  • 结合

Bond Pronunciation

/bɒnd/

Bond Definition

noun

Definition

A bond is a financial instrument that represents a loan made by an investor to a borrower.

In ZH

债券是一种金融工具，代表投资者向借款人提供的贷款。

How to Use Bond in a Sentence

  • The company issued a bond to raise funds.
  • Investors are attracted to government bonds due to their low risk.
  • She bought a bond as a long-term investment.
  • The bond matures in ten years.
  • Interest rates on bonds fluctuate based on market conditions.
verb

Definition

To bond means to develop a close relationship or connection with someone or something.

In ZH

联结意味着与某人或某物建立密切的关系或联系。

How to Use Bond in a Sentence

  • The mother bonded with her newborn baby instantly.
  • They bonded over their shared love of music.
  • Dogs bond closely with their owners.
  • It takes time to bond with new teammates.
  • He found it easy to bond with people from different cultures.

Synonyms for Bond

  • Bon Vivant
  • Bonhomie
  • Bonjour
  • Bonnet
