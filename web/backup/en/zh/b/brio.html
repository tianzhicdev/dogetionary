Brio

EN→ZH

What Does Brio Mean?

  • 活力
  • 生气勃勃
  • 生动

Brio Pronunciation

/ˈbriːoʊ/

Brio Definition

noun

Definition

Vigor or vivacity of style or performance.

In ZH

风格或表演的活力或生气勃勃。

How to Use Brio in a Sentence

  • The performance was full of brio and enthusiasm.
  • She approached the project with great brio and determination.
  • His speech was delivered with such brio that the audience was captivated.
  • They danced with brio, showcasing their passion for the art.
  • The musician played with brio, bringing the piece to life.

Synonyms for Brio

  • Brilliant
  • Brisk
