Berserk

Pronunciation:/bərˈzɜrk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 狂暴的
  • 发狂的
  • 失去理智的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

out of control with anger or excitement; wild or frenzied

In ZH

失去控制，充满愤怒或兴奋；疯狂的

Examples

  • He went berserk when he found out someone had stolen his car.
  • The crowd went berserk when the band took the stage.
  • She gets berserk when she doesn't get enough sleep.
  • Don't push him too far; he can go berserk in an instant.
  • The dog became berserk when it saw the squirrel.
