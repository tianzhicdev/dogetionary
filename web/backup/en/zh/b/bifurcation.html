Bifurcation

EN→ZH

What Does Bifurcation Mean?

  • 分叉
  • 二分

Bifurcation Pronunciation

/ˌbaɪ.fərˈkeɪ.ʃən/

Bifurcation Definition

noun

Definition

The division of something into two branches or parts.

In ZH

将某物分为两个分支或部分的过程。

How to Use Bifurcation in a Sentence

  • The bifurcation of the river created two distinct pathways.
  • In mathematics, bifurcation theory studies the changes in the structure of solutions to equations.
  • The bifurcation of the road confused many travelers.
  • We witnessed the bifurcation of the tree trunk after the storm.
  • Bifurcation can be a critical concept in understanding complex systems.

Synonyms for Bifurcation

  • Bifid
  • Bifrost
  • Bifurcate
