Banana

Pronunciation:bəˈnænə
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 香蕉

Definitions

noun

Definition

A long, curved fruit with a thick yellow skin and soft, sweet, white flesh inside, typically eaten as a snack or used in desserts.

In ZH

一种长而弯曲的水果，外皮厚实且呈黄色，内部有柔软、甜美的白色果肉，通常作为零食或用于制作甜点。

Examples

  • I had a banana for breakfast, and it was so delicious that I almost considered starting a banana fan club.
  • Did you hear about the banana that went to the doctor? It wasn’t peeling very well!
  • In a recent poll, scientists discovered that bananas are now considered a top fruit for dancing – just look at their curves!

Cultural Notes

Bananas are one of the most popular fruits in the world and are often associated with humor due to the comedy trope of slipping on a banana peel.

verb

Definition

To decorate or flavor food with banana, or to use bananas in cooking.

In ZH

用香蕉装饰或调味食物，或在烹饪中使用香蕉。

Examples

  • I decided to banana my oatmeal this morning by adding slices of banana on top, and now I feel like a gourmet chef!
  • When life gives you lemons, just banana everything – it works wonders!
  • I tried to banana my toast, but it ended up looking like a banana Picasso painting.

Cultural Notes

Using ‘banana’ as a verb is more informal and often used humorously.
