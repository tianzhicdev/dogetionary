Benison

EN→ZH

What Does Benison Mean?

  • 祝福
  • 恩惠
  • 祈福

Benison Pronunciation

/ˈbɛnɪsən/

Benison Definition

noun

Definition

A blessing or benediction.

In ZH

祝福或祝祷。

How to Use Benison in a Sentence

  • The priest offered a benison over the gathered congregation.
  • After their marriage, they received a benison from their families.
  • The old woman spoke a benison to the children as they left.
  • Receiving a benison before dinner felt like a cherished tradition.
  • In some cultures, a benison is an important part of a wedding ceremony.

Synonyms for Benison

  • Benediction
  • Benighted
  • Benign
  • Benignly
