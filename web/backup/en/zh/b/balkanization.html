Balkanization

Pronunciation:/ˌbɔːl.kən.ɪˈzeɪ.ʃən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 巴尔干化
  • 分裂
  • 割裂

Definitions

noun

Definition

The division of a region or state into smaller, often hostile, units.

In ZH

一个地区或国家分裂成较小，通常敌对的单位的过程。

Examples

  • The balkanization of the region led to ongoing conflicts.
  • Many analysts feared the balkanization of the country would destabilize the entire area.
  • Balkanization can result in economic fragmentation and political instability.
  • The term balkanization is often used when discussing the breakup of states.
  • Historical events can lead to the balkanization of formerly cohesive nations.
