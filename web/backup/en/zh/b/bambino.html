Bambino

Pronunciation:/bæmˈbiːnoʊ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幼儿
  • 小孩
  • 婴儿

Definitions

noun

Definition

A young child, typically a baby or toddler.

In ZH

一个年轻的孩子，通常是婴儿或学步儿童。

Examples

  • The bambino slept peacefully in the crib.
  • She bought a new toy for her bambinos.
  • In Italy, a bambino is often the center of attention during family gatherings.
  • He referred to his little brother as his favorite bambino.
  • The restaurant has a special menu for bambinos.

Cultural Notes

In Italian culture, 'bambino' is often used affectionately and can indicate a sense of familial warmth.
