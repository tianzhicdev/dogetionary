Bon Vivant

Pronunciation:/bɒn viːˈvɒ̃/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 美食家
  • 享乐主义者
  • 饕餮客

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who enjoys a sociable and luxurious lifestyle, especially in relation to good food and drink.

In ZH

一个喜爱社交和奢华生活方式的人，特别是与美食和饮料相关的。

Examples

  • He was known as a bon vivant who hosted lavish dinner parties.
  • As a bon vivant, she traveled the world to sample gourmet cuisines.
  • The restaurant attracted bon vivants seeking the finest culinary experiences.
  • Bon vivants often appreciate the finer things in life, such as fine wines and gourmet meals.
  • His reputation as a bon vivant made him a popular figure in high society.
