Batten

EN→ZH

What Does Batten Mean?

  • 条木
  • 木条
  • 挡板

Batten Pronunciation

/ˈbætn/

Batten Definition

noun

Definition

A long, flat strip of wood or metal used to secure or reinforce fabric or other materials.

In ZH

用于固定或增强织物或其他材料的长平条木或金属条。

How to Use Batten in a Sentence

  • The carpenter used a batten to help secure the roof panels.
  • She attached a batten to the wall to hold the drywall in place.
  • The window frame was reinforced with wooden battens.
  • In the play, the actors used a batten to lower the backdrop.
  • Make sure to cut the batten to the correct length before installation.
verb

Definition

To fasten or secure something with battens.

In ZH

用条木固定或加固某物。

How to Use Batten in a Sentence

  • They battened down the hatches before the storm hit.
  • You need to batten the canvas to prevent it from flapping in the wind.
  • The crew battened down all loose items on deck.
  • We will batten the roof to ensure it withstands strong winds.
  • He battened the sheets together to keep them from blowing away.

Synonyms for Batten

  • Bateau
  • Bated
  • Bathos
  • Battalion
  • Battery
