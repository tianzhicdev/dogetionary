Barefoot

EN→ZH

What Does Barefoot Mean?

  • 赤脚
  • 光脚
  • 不穿鞋

Barefoot Pronunciation

ˈbɛrˌfʊt

Barefoot Definition

adjective

Definition

Wearing nothing on the feet; not wearing shoes or socks.

In ZH

赤脚；不穿鞋或袜子。

How to Use Barefoot in a Sentence

  • He walked barefoot on the hot sand.
  • I prefer to be barefoot when I'm at home.
  • The children ran barefoot in the grass.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, being barefoot is common, especially in informal settings or at home. It can symbolize comfort and relaxation.

adverb

Definition

Without shoes or socks; in a manner that involves the feet being exposed.

In ZH

赤脚；以暴露脚的方式。

How to Use Barefoot in a Sentence

  • She danced barefoot on the stage.
  • They went barefoot during their hike in the summer.
  • He prefers to live barefoot in nature.

Cultural Notes

In some cultures, going barefoot is seen as a sign of humility or connection to the earth. In formal settings, it is usually considered inappropriate.
