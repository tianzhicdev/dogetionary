Blithering

What Does Blithering Mean?

  • 喋喋不休
  • 胡言乱语
  • 说废话

Blithering Pronunciation

/ˈblɪðərɪŋ/

Blithering Definition

adjective

Definition

talking nonsense or foolishly

In ZH

胡说或愚蠢地说话

How to Use Blithering in a Sentence

  • He kept blithering on about his favorite movie.
  • Stop blithering and get to the point.
  • Her blithering made the meeting last an extra hour.
  • They were blithering about subjects that didn't matter.
  • I don't want to listen to your blithering for another minute.
noun

Definition

a state of talking nonsense

In ZH

说废话的状态

How to Use Blithering in a Sentence

  • His blithering was amusing at first, but then it became annoying.
  • The blithering from the crowd made it hard to concentrate.
  • She had enough of his blithering during the conversation.
  • His blithering could fill a book with ridiculousness.
  • I can't tolerate such blithering during serious discussions.

Synonyms for Blithering

  • Blithe
  • Blithesome
