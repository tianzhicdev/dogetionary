Barrier

EN→ZH

What Does Barrier Mean?

  • 障碍
  • 屏障
  • 隔阂

Barrier Pronunciation

ˈbæriər

Barrier Definition

noun

Definition

A physical structure that prevents passage or access.

In ZH

一种阻止通行或进入的物理结构。

How to Use Barrier in a Sentence

  • The fence served as a barrier between the two properties.
  • They built a wall as a barrier against intruders.
  • Snow created a natural barrier on the road.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, barriers can symbolize division or separation, both physically and metaphorically.

noun

Definition

An obstacle that prevents progress or achievement.

In ZH

一个阻碍进步或成就的障碍。

How to Use Barrier in a Sentence

  • Language can be a barrier to communication for many people.
  • The lack of resources was a significant barrier to their success.
  • She faced several barriers in her career but overcame them through hard work.

Cultural Notes

In the context of personal and professional growth, barriers often reflect societal challenges such as discrimination or socio-economic factors.

verb

Definition

To obstruct or hinder (often used in a figurative sense).

In ZH

阻碍或妨碍（通常用于比喻意义）。

How to Use Barrier in a Sentence

  • They tried to barrier the flow of information to the public.
  • The new policies barrier the access to certain services.
  • He wanted to barrier his feelings to avoid conflict.

Cultural Notes

When used as a verb, it is more common in formal or literary contexts than in everyday conversation.

Synonyms for Barrier

  • Barmecide
