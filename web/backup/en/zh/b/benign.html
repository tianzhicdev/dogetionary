Benign

EN→ZH

What Does Benign Mean?

  • 良性的
  • 善良的
  • 温和的

Benign Pronunciation

/bɪˈnaɪn/

Benign Definition

adjective

Definition

gentle or kindly; not harmful in effect.

In ZH

温和的；良性的；没有害处的。

How to Use Benign in a Sentence

  • Her benign smile reassured the children.
  • The tumor was found to be benign after a thorough examination.
  • He had a benign approach to leadership, encouraging his team.
  • The climate in this region is generally benign, suitable for agriculture.
  • Benign neglect can sometimes be the best way to allow innovation to thrive.

Synonyms for Benign

  • Benediction
  • Benighted
  • Benignly
  • Benison
