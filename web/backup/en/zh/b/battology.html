Battology

Pronunciation:/bæˈtɒlədʒi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 空洞无物的言辞
  • 冗长的重复
  • 废话

Definitions

noun

Definition

the repetition of the same meaningless word or phrase; a redundancy in speech or writing.

In ZH

重复同样没有意义的词或短语；言语或写作中的冗余。

Examples

  • His speech was filled with battology, making it hard to follow.
  • The article suffered from battology, repeating the same ideas without adding new insights.
  • In literature, battology can weaken the impact of the message.
  • Avoid battology in your writing to keep it concise and engaging.
  • Critics noted that the politician's address was full of battology rather than substantive content.
