Bumbershoot

EN→ZH

What Does Bumbershoot Mean?

  • 雨伞
  • 遮阳伞

Bumbershoot Pronunciation

/ˈbʌmbərˌʃuːt/

Bumbershoot Definition

noun

Definition

An umbrella.

In ZH

雨伞。

How to Use Bumbershoot in a Sentence

  • I always carry a bumbershoot in case it rains.
  • The bumbershoot kept me dry during the unexpected downpour.
  • She opened her colorful bumbershoot as the storm approached.
  • He forgot his bumbershoot and got soaked on the way home.
  • They were walking under their bumbershoots, enjoying the spring rain.

Cultural Notes

The term 'bumbershoot' is considered quite whimsical and is often associated with American English, particularly in the early 20th century.
