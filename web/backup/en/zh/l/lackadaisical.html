Lackadaisical

Pronunciation:/ˌlækəˈdeɪzɪkəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 懒散的
  • 无精打采的
  • 漫不经心的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

lacking enthusiasm and determination; carelessly lazy

In ZH

缺乏热情和决心；随意懒惰

Examples

  • She was lackadaisical about finishing her project on time.
  • His lackadaisical attitude toward his studies worried his parents.
  • The team's lackadaisical performance cost them the championship.
  • Despite the deadline, he had a lackadaisical approach to his work.
  • Her lackadaisical response made it clear she wasn't interested.
