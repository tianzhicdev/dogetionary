Luminescence

EN→ZH

What Does Luminescence Mean?

  • 发光
  • 光照
  • 光亮

Luminescence Pronunciation

/ˌluːmɪˈnɛsəns/

Luminescence Definition

noun

Definition

The emission of light by a substance that has not been heated, as in fluorescence and phosphorescence.

In ZH

一种物质未被加热而发出光的现象，如荧光和磷光。

How to Use Luminescence in a Sentence

  • The luminescence of the firefly is mesmerizing at night.
  • Scientists study luminescence to understand chemical reactions.
  • Luminescence can be seen in deep-sea creatures.
  • The luminescence of the glow sticks adds excitement to the party.
  • In art, luminescence can create a magical atmosphere.

Synonyms for Luminescence

  • Luminal
  • Luminance
  • Luminary
  • Luminescent
  • Luminous
