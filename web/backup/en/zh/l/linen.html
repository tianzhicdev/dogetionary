Linen

Pronunciation:ˈlɪn.ən
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 亚麻
  • 林恩

Definitions

noun

Definition

A type of fabric made from the fibers of the flax plant, known for its durability and breathability.

In ZH

一种由亚麻植物的纤维制成的织物，以其耐用性和透气性而闻名。

Examples

  • I thought I bought a linen shirt, but it turned out to be a very fancy tablecloth!
  • With the summer heat, my linen clothes are feeling cooler than my ex's excuses!
  • If I had a dollar for every wrinkle in my linen pants, I’d have enough to hire a professional ironer!

Cultural Notes

Linen is often associated with luxury and high-quality clothing due to its texture and breathability. It is commonly worn in summer in many cultures.

adjective

Definition

Relating to or made of linen fabric.

In ZH

与亚麻织物相关或由其制成的。

Examples

  • She wore a lovely linen dress that made her look like she just stepped off a European vacation.
  • My grandmother always said that linen sheets are the best, but I think she just wanted an excuse for her 100-thread-count sheets!
  • When it rains, my linen suit turns into a very fashionable wet noodle!

Cultural Notes

Linen is frequently used in home textiles like tablecloths and bed linens, enhancing the feel of elegance and comfort.
