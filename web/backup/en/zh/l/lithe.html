Lithe

Pronunciation:/laɪð/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 柔韧的
  • 轻盈的
  • 灵活的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Characterized by gracefulness and flexibility; able to move easily and gracefully.

In ZH

以优雅和灵活性为特征；能够轻松而优雅地移动。

Examples

  • The dancer had a lithe body that seemed to flow effortlessly.
  • She moved with a lithe grace that captivated everyone in the room.
  • His lithe movements on the basketball court made him a star player.
  • The gymnast's lithe form was a result of years of training.
  • In the forest, the lithe deer darted between the trees.
