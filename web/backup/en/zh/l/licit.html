Licit

EN→ZH

What Does Licit Mean?

  • 合法的
  • 许可的
  • 正当的

Licit Pronunciation

/ˈlɪsɪt/

Licit Definition

adjective

Definition

Permitted by law; lawful.

In ZH

法律允许的；合法的。

How to Use Licit in a Sentence

  • The contract was signed under licit circumstances.
  • Licit activities are important for economic growth.
  • He ensured that all his business transactions were licit.
  • It is crucial to distinguish between licit and illicit goods.
  • Licit use of the property is legally protected.

Synonyms for Licit
