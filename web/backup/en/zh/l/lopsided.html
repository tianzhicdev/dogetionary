Lopsided

EN→ZH

What Does Lopsided Mean?

  • 倾斜的
  • 不平衡的
  • 歪斜的

Lopsided Pronunciation

/ˈlɒpˌsaɪ.dɪd/

Lopsided Definition

adjective

Definition

having one side lower or smaller than the other

In ZH

一侧比另一侧低或小的

How to Use Lopsided in a Sentence

  • The lopsided cake looked amusing at the party.
  • He had a lopsided grin that made everyone laugh.
  • The lopsided tree leaned dangerously to one side.
  • Her lopsided approach to the project raised some eyebrows.
  • They took a lopsided photograph that showcased the fun of the moment.
