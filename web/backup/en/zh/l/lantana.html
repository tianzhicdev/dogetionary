Lantana

EN→ZH

What Does Lantana Mean?

  • 马缨丹
  • 五色梅
  • 花叶马缨丹

Lantana Pronunciation

/lænˈtænə/

Lantana Definition

noun

Definition

A genus of flowering plants in the verbena family, commonly known for their colorful clusters of flowers.

In ZH

一种开花植物的属，属于马鞭草科，以其色彩缤纷的花朵簇而闻名。

How to Use Lantana in a Sentence

  • Lantanas are often used in gardens for their vibrant flowers.
  • The lantana bush attracts many butterflies.
  • In tropical regions, lantana can become an invasive species.
  • Lantanas thrive in sunny areas and require little water.
  • Gardeners appreciate the lantana for its long blooming season.
