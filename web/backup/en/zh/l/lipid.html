Lipid

Pronunciation:/ˈlɪpɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 脂质
  • 脂肪
  • 脂肪酸

Definitions

noun

Definition

Any of a group of organic compounds that are insoluble in water but soluble in organic solvents; they include fats, oils, waxes, and steroids.

In ZH

一组在水中不溶但在有机溶剂中溶解的有机化合物，包括脂肪、油、蜡和类固醇。

Examples

  • Lipids are essential for energy storage in the body.
  • Many lipids, such as triglycerides, are important for human health.
  • Some lipids are used as signaling molecules in biological systems.
  • Dietary lipids play a crucial role in maintaining cell membrane integrity.
  • Research on lipids has increased due to their relevance in metabolic disorders.
