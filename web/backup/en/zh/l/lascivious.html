Lascivious

Pronunciation:/ləˈsɪv.i.əs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 淫荡的
  • 好色的
  • 猥亵的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Feeling or revealing an overt and often offensive sexual desire.

In ZH

表现或透露出明显且常常令人反感的性欲的。

Examples

  • His lascivious behavior made everyone uncomfortable.
  • The film was criticized for its lascivious content.
  • She received lascivious comments on her social media post.
  • His lascivious grin suggested he had ulterior motives.
  • Lascivious literature has been banned in many cultures.
