Loquacious

EN→ZH

What Does Loquacious Mean?

  • 好辩的
  • 多话的
  • 饶舌的

Loquacious Pronunciation

/ləˈkweɪʃəs/

Loquacious Definition

adjective

Definition

tending to talk a great deal; talkative

In ZH

倾向于大量交谈；多话的

How to Use Loquacious in a Sentence

  • She is so loquacious that she can hold a conversation for hours.
  • The loquacious tour guide kept everyone entertained.
  • His loquacious nature made him a favorite at parties.
  • Loquacious individuals often find it easy to make new friends.
  • Despite his loquacious reputation, he also knows when to listen.

Synonyms for Loquacious

  • Loquaciously
  • Loquaciousness
  • Loquacity
