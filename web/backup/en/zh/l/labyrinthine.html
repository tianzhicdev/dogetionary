Labyrinthine

Pronunciation:/ˌlæbəˈrɪnθin/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 迷宫的
  • 错综复杂的
  • labyrinthine

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Like a labyrinth; irregular and twisting; intricate and bewildering.

In ZH

像迷宫一样；不规则和扭曲；复杂且令人困惑。

Examples

  • The old city had labyrinthine streets that were difficult to navigate.
  • Her argument was labyrinthine, making it hard to follow her point.
  • He got lost in the labyrinthine corridors of the ancient palace.
  • The book's plot is labyrinthine, filled with unexpected twists.
  • The labyrinthine network of tunnels was a challenge for the explorers.
