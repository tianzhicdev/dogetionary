Lugubriousness

Pronunciation:/luːˈɡuːbriəsnəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 阴郁
  • 悲哀
  • 凄凉

Definitions

noun

Definition

The quality of being mournful, dismal, or gloomy; a state of deep sadness or sorrow.

In ZH

悲伤、忧郁或阴郁的性质；一种深深的悲哀或悲伤的状态。

Examples

  • The lugubriousness of the rain matched my mood perfectly.
  • Her face was marked by a lugubriousness that was hard to ignore.
  • He spoke with a lugubriousness that made everyone uncomfortable.
  • The lugubriousness of the music set a somber tone for the evening.
  • In contrast to the party's joy, his lugubriousness was evident.
