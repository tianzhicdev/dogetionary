Loquaciously

EN→ZH

What Does Loquaciously Mean?

  • 多话地
  • 饶舌地

Loquaciously Pronunciation

/ləˈkweɪʃəsli/

Loquaciously Definition

adverb

Definition

In a way that is very talkative or chatty.

In ZH

以非常健谈或话多的方式

How to Use Loquaciously in a Sentence

  • She spoke loquaciously at the party, captivating everyone.
  • He tends to explain loquaciously, adding details that most people overlook.
  • The children loquaciously shared stories during their playtime.
  • They loquaciously debated the topic until midnight.
  • She wrote her essay loquaciously, filled with elaborate descriptions.

Synonyms for Loquaciously

  • Loquacious
  • Loquaciousness
  • Loquacity
