Legal Matters

Pronunciation:ˈliːɡəl ˈmætərz
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 法律事务
  • 法律问题
  • 法律事宜

Definitions

noun

Definition

Issues or topics related to law and legal processes.

In ZH

与法律和法律程序相关的问题或主题。

Examples

  • When my dog ate my homework, I didn't realize it could lead to such legal matters—turns out my teacher thought I was trying to form a canine union!
  • After accidentally sending a text to my boss instead of my best friend, I thought we might need legal matters covered—who knew there were laws against sharing embarrassing secrets?
  • The lawsuit filed by the cat against its owner over the unfair sharing of snacks has opened up a whole new realm of legal matters in pet ownership.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, discussing legal matters can be complex and sensitive. It's often advisable to approach these topics with care and respect. In a humorous context, legal matters can add a light-hearted twist to everyday situations.
