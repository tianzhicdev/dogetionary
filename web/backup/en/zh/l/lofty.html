Lofty

EN→ZH

What Does Lofty Mean?

  • 高耸的
  • 崇高的
  • 高远的

Lofty Pronunciation

/ˈlɔːfti/

Lofty Definition

adjective

Definition

of imposing height; very tall or high

In ZH

具有令人印象深刻的高度；非常高或高的

How to Use Lofty in a Sentence

  • The lofty mountains were capped with snow.
  • She had lofty ambitions to change the world.
  • The skyscraper stood lofty against the skyline.
  • He spoke in a lofty tone that inspired the audience.
  • They admired the lofty trees in the ancient forest.
adjective

Definition

elevated in character and spirit; noble

In ZH

在性格和精神上升华；崇高的

  • The poem expressed lofty themes of love and sacrifice.
  • She maintained a lofty perspective despite the challenges.
  • The lecture was filled with lofty concepts that challenged conventional thinking.
  • They valued lofty principles over material wealth.
