Lassitude

Pronunciation:/ˈlæsɪˌtud/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 疲惫
  • 倦怠
  • 无力感

Definitions

noun

Definition

A state of physical or mental weariness; lack of energy.

In ZH

一种身体或精神的疲惫状态；缺乏能量。

Examples

  • After the long hike, I felt a deep sense of lassitude.
  • The heat of the summer afternoon induced a feeling of lassitude.
  • Lassitude can often be a sign of underlying health issues.
  • He overcame his lassitude with a strong cup of coffee.
  • The constant stress at work left her in a state of lassitude.
