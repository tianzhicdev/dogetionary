Latitudinarian

Pronunciation:/ˌlætɪˌtjuːdɪˈnɛəriən/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 宽容主义者
  • 自由主义者
  • 放宽主义者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who is tolerant of different opinions or beliefs, especially regarding religious matters.

In ZH

一个宽容不同意见或信仰的人，特别是在宗教问题上。

Examples

  • As a latitudinarian, she embraced various viewpoints on spirituality.
  • The latitudinarian approach to theology allowed for diverse interpretations of sacred texts.
  • In a latitudinarian society, people feel free to express their beliefs without fear.
  • He was often labeled a latitudinarian for his acceptance of differing ethical beliefs.
  • Latitudinarianism promotes understanding among various cultural groups.
