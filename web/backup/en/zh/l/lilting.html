Lilting

Pronunciation:/ˈlɪltɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 轻快的
  • 吟唱的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Having a light, cheerful, and rhythmic quality; characterized by a musical or lively tone.

In ZH

具有轻快、愉悦和有节奏的特征；以音乐或生动的音调为特征。

Examples

  • The singer's lilting voice captured everyone's attention.
  • As the children played, their laughter was lilting in the air.
  • She spoke with a lilting accent that reminded me of my childhood.
  • The poem had a lilting quality that made it enjoyable to read aloud.
  • The music was lilting and made everyone want to dance.
