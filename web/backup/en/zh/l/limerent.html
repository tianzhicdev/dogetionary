Limerent

Pronunciation:/ˈlɪm.ər.ənt/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 迷恋的
  • 爱慕的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

describing a state of intense romantic desire for another person, often characterized by obsessive thoughts and feelings of euphoria and anxiety.

In ZH

形容对另一个人产生强烈浪漫欲望的状态，通常伴随着强迫性思维和愉悦与焦虑的感觉。

Examples

  • She felt limerent towards her colleague, constantly daydreaming about him.
  • His limerent feelings made it hard for him to focus on anything else.
  • Limerent love can lead to an emotional rollercoaster of highs and lows.
  • After the first date, she entered a limerent phase that consumed her thoughts.
  • Understanding limerence can help people navigate the complexities of love.

Cultural Notes

Limerence is a term coined by psychologist Dorothy Tennov in the 1970s, describing a state that can sometimes be confused with actual love but is more about infatuation.
