Luminary

EN→ZH

What Does Luminary Mean?

  • 发光体
  • 杰出人物
  • 名人

Luminary Pronunciation

/ˈluː.mɪ.nəri/

Luminary Definition

noun

Definition

A person who inspires or influences others, especially one prominent in a particular sphere.

In ZH

一个激励或影响他人的人，尤其是在某一特定领域中突出的个人。

How to Use Luminary in a Sentence

  • Albert Einstein is considered a luminary in the field of physics.
  • The conference featured several luminaries from the tech industry.
  • She became a luminary in the world of fashion design.
  • The festival celebrates the luminaries of the local arts community.
  • Many young writers look up to luminaries like Maya Angelou.
