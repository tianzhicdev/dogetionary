Lachesis

Pronunciation:/ˈlæ.kɪ.sɪs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 拉刻西斯
  • 命运女神之一

Definitions

noun

Definition

One of the three Fates in Greek mythology, Lachesis is responsible for measuring the thread of life.

In ZH

在希腊神话中，拉刻西斯是三女神之一，负责测量生命的线。

Examples

  • Lachesis determines the length of each person's life.
  • In some myths, Lachesis is depicted with a measuring rod.
  • Lachesis plays a crucial role in the fate of mortals.
  • The character of Lachesis appears in various works of literature.
  • Lachesis weaves the destinies of all living beings.

Cultural Notes

In mythology, Lachesis represents fate and destiny, often depicted alongside Clotho and Atropos, the other two Fates.
