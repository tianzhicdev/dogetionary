Lampoon

Pronunciation:/læmˈpuːn/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 讽刺
  • 嘲讽
  • 讽刺作品

Definitions

verb

Definition

To publicly criticize someone or something by using ridicule, irony, or sarcasm.

In ZH

通过使用嘲笑、讽刺或反语公开批评某人或某物。

Examples

  • The article lampoons the politician's latest proposal.
  • She lampooned the celebrity's behavior on her blog.
  • The play lampoons the absurdity of modern life.
  • They used satire to lampoon the government's decisions.
  • His cartoons often lampoon various social issues.
noun

Definition

A speech or text criticizing someone or something in this way.

In ZH

以这种方式批评某人或某物的演讲或文本。

Examples

  • The writer's lampoon of the fashion industry became a bestseller.
  • He was known for his sharp lampoons of public figures.
  • A famous lampoon of that era still resonates today.
  • The lampoon appeared in a popular magazine.
  • Her satirical lampoon received mixed reviews.
