Lattice

EN→ZH

What Does Lattice Mean?

  • 格子
  • 晶格
  • 网格

Lattice Pronunciation

/ˈlæt.ɪs/

Lattice Definition

noun

Definition

A structure consisting of strips of wood or metal crossed and fastened together, typically used for fences or trellises.

In ZH

由木条或金属条交叉并固定在一起的结构，通常用于栅栏或藤架。

How to Use Lattice in a Sentence

  • The garden was surrounded by a wooden lattice.
  • She painted the lattice a bright color to enhance the appearance of the garden.
  • They used a metal lattice to support the climbing plants.
  • A lattice design can add charm to any outdoor space.
  • He climbed over the lattice to reach the roof.
noun

Definition

A grid-like structure used in mathematics or physics to represent points or spaces.

In ZH

用于数学或物理学中表示点或空间的网格状结构。

How to Use Lattice in a Sentence

  • In geometry, a lattice can help visualize complex shapes.
  • The physicist used a lattice model to explain crystal structures.
  • Mathematicians study properties of lattices in abstract algebra.
  • The data was arranged in a lattice to facilitate analysis.
  • A lattice can simplify calculations in theoretical physics.

Synonyms for Lattice

  • Latent
  • Latitudinarian
