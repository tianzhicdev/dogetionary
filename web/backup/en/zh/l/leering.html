Leering

Pronunciation:ˈlɪrɪŋ
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 盯着看
  • 淫笑
  • 流氓的目光

Definitions

verb

Definition

To look or gaze in a way that suggests a sexual interest or lust.

In ZH

用一种暗示性欲或欲望的方式看或盯着。

Examples

  • He was leering at the new employee in the break room, which made everyone uncomfortable.
  • After the latest viral video of the cat doing yoga, people started leering at their own pets, hoping for a viral moment.
  • During the movie, the villain was leering at the heroine, making the audience laugh when he tripped over his own feet.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, leering is often regarded as inappropriate or disrespectful, especially in professional settings. The word often carries a negative connotation.

adjective

Definition

Having a sly or lascivious look, often associated with inappropriate intentions.

In ZH

有狡诈或猥亵的外表，通常与不当意图相关。

Examples

  • The leering grin on his face suggested he had more than just friendly intentions at the party.
  • The advertisement featured a leering character that was meant to be humorous but ended up being a bit creepy instead.
  • Last Halloween, he wore a mask with a leering expression, which scared more neighbors than he intended.

Cultural Notes

Leering can sometimes be portrayed in media as a comedic element but can also highlight serious issues around consent and respect.
