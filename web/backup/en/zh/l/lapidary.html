Lapidary

EN→ZH

What Does Lapidary Mean?

  • 宝石商
  • 石匠
  • 雕刻者

Lapidary Pronunciation

/ˈlæp.ɪˌdɛr.i/

Lapidary Definition

noun

Definition

A person who cuts, polishes, and engraves stones and gems.

In ZH

一个切割、抛光和雕刻石头和宝石的人。

How to Use Lapidary in a Sentence

  • The lapidary skillfully polished the amethyst into a brilliant stone.
  • He became a respected lapidary after years of practice.
  • The museum featured a stunning collection of lapidary works.
  • She learned the art of lapidary from her grandfather.
  • Finding a skilled lapidary is crucial for high-quality gemstone cutting.
adjective

Definition

Relating to the art of cutting and polishing stones and gems.

In ZH

与切割和抛光石头和宝石的艺术有关。

How to Use Lapidary in a Sentence

  • Lapidary techniques have been developed for centuries.
  • The lapidary quality of the jewelry showed exceptional craftsmanship.
  • She admired the lapidary designs of ancient civilizations.
  • The workshop focused on lapidary motifs in modern art.
  • His lapidary skills are evident in the intricate details of his work.
