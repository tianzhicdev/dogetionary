Lachrymose

Pronunciation:/ˈlækrɪmoʊs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 流泪的
  • 爱哭的
  • 泪汪汪的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

tearful or given to weeping; inducing tears; sad.

In ZH

流泪的，爱哭的；引发泪水的；悲伤的。

Examples

  • The movie was so lachrymose that everyone in the theater was crying.
  • She had a lachrymose expression after hearing the bad news.
  • His lachrymose speech left the audience in tears.
  • The book's lachrymose themes resonated with many readers.
  • Despite its lachrymose nature, the story ultimately has a hopeful message.
