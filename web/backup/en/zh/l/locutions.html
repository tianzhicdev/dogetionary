Locutions Pronunciation

ləˈkjuːʃənz

Locutions Definition

noun

Definition

A way of speaking; a phrase or expression, especially a peculiar form of language in a particular region or among a particular group.

In ZH

一种说话的方式；短语或表达，尤其是特定区域或特定群体的独特语言形式。

How to Use Locutions in a Sentence

  • His locutions were often hard to understand due to their complexity.
  • In this region, certain locutions are common that might confuse outsiders.
  • The author is known for his unique locutions that reflect the local culture.

Cultural Notes

Locutions can reflect regional dialects and cultural nuances, therefore understanding them can enhance communication in specific locales.
