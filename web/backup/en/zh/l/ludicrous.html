Ludicrous

Pronunciation:/ˈluː.dɪ.krəs/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 滑稽的
  • 荒谬的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

absurd or ridiculous; deserving laughter because of being foolish or absurd

In ZH

荒谬的；可笑的；因愚蠢或荒唐而值得嘲笑的

Examples

  • The idea that we could finish the project in two days was ludicrous.
  • His ludicrous behavior at the meeting had everyone in stitches.
  • It was ludicrous to think she could run a marathon without training.
  • They made a ludicrous amount of noise during the concert.
  • The movie was filled with ludicrous plot twists that made no sense.
