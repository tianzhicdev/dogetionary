Limerence

Pronunciation:/ˈlɪm.ə.rəns/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 迷恋
  • 狂热爱情
  • 恋慕

Definitions

noun

Definition

the state of being infatuated or obsessed with another person, typically characterized by a strong desire for reciprocation of one's feelings.

In ZH

沉迷于或痴迷于另一个人的状态，通常以强烈希望对方回应自己感情的特征为特征。

Examples

  • She experienced limerence for him during their summer romance.
  • Limerence can often lead to obsessive behaviors in individuals.
  • He could not focus on anything else due to his limerence.
  • Limerence is sometimes confused with genuine love.
  • Understanding limerence can help people navigate their feelings more effectively.

Cultural Notes

Limerence is a psychological concept that refers to the intense romantic desire often seen in the early stages of a relationship; it was first introduced by psychologist Dorothy Tennov.
