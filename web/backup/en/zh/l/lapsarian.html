Lapsarian

EN→ZH

What Does Lapsarian Mean?

  • 堕落论者
  • 预定论者
  • 人类堕落论者

Lapsarian Pronunciation

/læpˈsɛəriən/

Lapsarian Definition

noun

Definition

A person who holds a particular theological doctrine concerning the origin of sin and its relation to the sovereignty of God, especially in the context of predestination.

In ZH

一个持有关于罪的起源及其与上帝主权关系的特定神学教义的人，尤其是在预定论的背景下。

How to Use Lapsarian in a Sentence

  • He is considered a lapsarian due to his views on predestination.
  • The debate among lapsarians and infralapsarians continues to shape modern theological discussions.
  • Many lapsarians argue that God's sovereignty allows for human choice.
  • In the church, the lapsarian perspective often influences sermons on salvation.
  • The historical writings of lapsarians provide insight into early Protestant thought.

Cultural Notes

The term is often used in Reformed theology, particularly in discussions about predestination and the fall of man.

Synonyms for Lapsarian

  • Lapidary
