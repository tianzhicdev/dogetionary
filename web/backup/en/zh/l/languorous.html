Languorous

EN→ZH

What Does Languorous Mean?

  • 倦怠的
  • 无精打采的
  • 懒洋洋的

Languorous Pronunciation

/ˈlæŋɡərəs/

Languorous Definition

adjective

Definition

characterized by a state of dreamy relaxation or languor; lacking energy or vitality

In ZH

以梦幻的放松或倦怠状态为特征；缺乏能量或活力

How to Use Languorous in a Sentence

  • She felt languorous after spending the entire afternoon lounging in the sun.
  • The languorous music made everyone feel drowsy and relaxed.
  • His languorous movements suggested he was not in a hurry.
  • On a languorous summer evening, the fireflies danced in the air.
  • The novel's languorous prose invites readers to lose themselves in its world.

Synonyms for Languorous

  • Languid
  • Languish
  • Languor
  • Lantana
