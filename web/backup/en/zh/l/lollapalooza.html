Lollapalooza

Pronunciation:/ˌlɒləpəˈluːzə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 洛拉帕卢扎
  • 盛大活动

Definitions

noun

Definition

a person or thing that is outstanding or excellent, often used to refer to an extraordinary event or performance.

In ZH

一个优秀或杰出的人或事物，常用来指代非凡的事件或表演。

Examples

  • The concert was a lollapalooza that everyone talked about for weeks.
  • She threw a lollapalooza of a birthday party this year.
  • His performance at the festival was a lollapalooza that left the audience in awe.
  • The new restaurant in town is a lollapalooza for food lovers.
  • That movie was a real lollapalooza, captivating everyone in the theater.

Cultural Notes

The term 'lollapalooza' originated in the late 19th century as a slang term and has been popularized in modern culture through the music festival of the same name.
