Libretto

Pronunciation:/lɪˈbrɛtoʊ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 剧本
  • 歌词
  • 歌剧剧本

Definitions

noun

Definition

the text of an opera or other long vocal work.

In ZH

歌剧或其他长篇声乐作品的文本。

Examples

  • The libretto of the opera was written by a famous poet.
  • She studied the libretto to understand the story better.
  • The composer and librettist collaborated closely on the new opera.
  • I always read the libretto while listening to the performance.
  • The libretto includes both the lyrics and the stage directions.

Cultural Notes

In opera, the libretto serves as the foundation of the performance, guiding both the music and the acting.
