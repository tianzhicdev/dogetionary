Lethologica

Pronunciation:/ˌlɛθəˈlɒdʒɪkə/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 失语症
  • 遗忘症
  • 言语失调

Definitions

noun

Definition

The inability to remember a word or name.

In ZH

无法记起一个单词或名字的能力。

Examples

  • I experienced lethologica when I was trying to introduce my friend at the party.
  • That feeling of lethologica can be frustrating when you know you know the word.
  • After reading for hours, I hit a moment of lethologica and couldn't recall the term I had just learned.
  • He struggled with lethologica during his speech, forgetting key points he wanted to make.
  • Lethologica can sometimes strike when you're under pressure to perform.
