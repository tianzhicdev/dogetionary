Laminate

EN→ZH

What Does Laminate Mean?

  • 层压材料
  • 复合板
  • 层压物

Laminate Pronunciation

/ˈlæm.ɪ.neɪt/

Laminate Definition

verb

Definition

To cover a surface with a thin layer of material, often for protection or durability.

In ZH

用薄层材料覆盖表面，通常是为了保护或增强耐用性。

How to Use Laminate in a Sentence

  • We need to laminate the poster to protect it from damage.
  • She decided to laminate her recipe cards to keep them clean.
  • The school lamination service makes it easy to preserve important documents.
  • They laminate the edges of the table for a smooth finish.
  • You can laminate your ID card for durability.
noun

Definition

A product made by laminating materials together, often used in furniture or flooring.

In ZH

通过将材料层压在一起制成的产品，通常用于家具或地板。

How to Use Laminate in a Sentence

  • This laminate is scratch-resistant and easy to clean.
  • The flooring is made of high-quality laminate that looks like wood.
  • I prefer laminate countertops because they are affordable.
  • The kitchen was upgraded with new laminate cabinets.
  • We chose a laminate material for its durability.

Synonyms for Laminate

  • Lambent
  • Lamentation
  • Lammergeier
  • Lampoon
  • Lampropeltis
