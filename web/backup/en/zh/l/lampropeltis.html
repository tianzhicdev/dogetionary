What Does Lampropeltis Mean?

  • 光滑蛇属

Lampropeltis Pronunciation

/ˌlæm.prəˈpɛl.tɪs/

Lampropeltis Definition

noun

Definition

A genus of non-venomous snakes commonly known as kingsnakes or milksnakes.

In ZH

一种无毒蛇类的属，通常称为王蛇或牛奶蛇。
