Lucrative Pronunciation

Lucrative Definition

adjective

Definition

producing a great deal of profit

In ZH

How to Use Lucrative in a Sentence

  • Many businesses find that online advertising is more lucrative than traditional methods.
  • He moved into a lucrative career in finance after graduation.
  • She has a talent for identifying lucrative investment opportunities.
  • The lucrative nature of the job attracted many applicants.
