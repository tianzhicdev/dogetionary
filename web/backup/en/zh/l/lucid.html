Lucid

Pronunciation:/ˈluː.sɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 清晰的
  • 明白的
  • 透明的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

expressed clearly; easy to understand.

In ZH

表达清楚的；易于理解的。

Examples

  • The professor gave a lucid explanation of the complex theory.
  • Her ideas were presented in a lucid manner during the debate.
  • He wrote a lucid report that everyone could understand.
  • After a good night's sleep, her thoughts were lucid and coherent.
  • The article was so lucid that even a child could grasp the concepts.
adjective

Definition

able to think clearly, especially during or after a period of confusion or illness.

In ZH

能够清晰思考，尤其是在混乱或生病后的状态。

Examples

  • During his lucid moments, he would tell fascinating stories from his youth.
  • After the medication wore off, he became lucid and was able to talk normally.
  • She has lucid dreams that she often remembers in detail.
  • The patient experienced lucid intervals amidst the confusion of his illness.
  • In 'One Flew Over the Cuckoo's Nest', characters often struggle between lucid moments and confusion.
