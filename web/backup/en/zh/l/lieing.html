Lieing

Pronunciation:ˈlaɪɪŋ
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 撒谎
  • 躺下
  • 谎言

Definitions

verb

Definition

The act of not telling the truth; to make a false statement.

In ZH

不说真话的行为；说出虚假的陈述。

Examples

  • She was lying about her whereabouts.
  • He lied to protect his friend.
  • Lying can damage trust in relationships.

Cultural Notes

Lying is generally viewed negatively in most cultures, including Chinese culture, where honesty is highly valued.

adjective

Definition

Present participle of lie; positioned in a horizontal or recumbent position.

In ZH

躺着的状态或位置。

Examples

  • The cat is lying on the sofa.
  • He was lying on the beach soaking up the sun.
  • She enjoys lying in bed on weekends.

Cultural Notes

In most cultures, including English-speaking ones, lying down can also symbolize relaxation or leisure.
