Queried

Pronunciation:ˈkwɪr.iːd
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 询问
  • 查询
  • 质疑

Definitions

verb

Definition

To ask a question or to inquire about something.

In ZH

询问或查询某事。

Examples

  • When the cat sat on my laptop, I queried if it was trying to hack into my emails.
  • After watching the news about flying cars, I queried my friend if we should start calling our taxis 'sky taxis'.
  • I queried the waiter if the 'mystery meat' was really a mystery or just a really bad joke.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, querying usually implies a desire for information or clarification, often used in formal and informal contexts.

adjective

Definition

Past tense of query, indicating that a question has been asked or raised previously.

In ZH

查询的过去时，表示问题已经被询问或提出。

Examples

  • The report that I queried last week was finally published, but it was just a picture of a cat.
  • I queried my bank about my missing money, and they replied with a reminder that I probably spent it all on coffee.
  • After I queried the teacher about the exam, she looked at me as if I had just suggested we replace math with pizza day.

Cultural Notes

In everyday conversation, people may use the term 'queried' to refer to clarifications they have made, often in a lighthearted or informal way.
