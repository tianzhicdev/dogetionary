Quidnunc

Pronunciation:/ˈkwɪd.nʌŋk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 好事之徒
  • 八卦者
  • 爱说闲话的人

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who is eager to know the latest news and gossip; a busybody.

In ZH

一个渴望了解最新新闻和闲话的人；一个爱管闲事的人。

Examples

  • The quidnunc at the coffee shop always seems to know everyone's business.
  • In small towns, it's common to encounter a quidnunc who knows the gossip about everyone.
  • She is such a quidnunc, always asking questions about my personal life.
  • They gathered in the park, a couple of quidnuncs sharing the latest rumors.
  • Being a quidnunc can sometimes lead to trouble when the gossip is not true.

Cultural Notes

The term quidnunc has a historical usage, often associated with English literature and social commentary, reflecting a playful or mocking attitude towards those who are overly interested in the affairs of others.
