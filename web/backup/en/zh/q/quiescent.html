Quiescent

EN→ZH

What Does Quiescent Mean?

  • 静止的
  • 沉默的
  • 无活动的

Quiescent Pronunciation

/kwɪˈɛs.ənt/

Quiescent Definition

adjective

Definition

In a state or period of inactivity or dormancy.

In ZH

处于不活跃或沉睡状态的；静止的。

How to Use Quiescent in a Sentence

  • The volcano has been quiescent for decades.
  • During the winter, many animals become quiescent.
  • The patient's symptoms were quiescent after treatment.
  • She remained quiescent, allowing others to take the lead.
  • The quiescent atmosphere made it a perfect time for reflection.
