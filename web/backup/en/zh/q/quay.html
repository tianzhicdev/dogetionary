Quay

EN→ZH

What Does Quay Mean?

  • 码头
  • 岸
  • 栈桥

Quay Pronunciation

/kiː/

Quay Definition

noun

Definition

A structure on the shore of a harbor where ships may dock to load and unload cargo or passengers.

In ZH

在港口岸边的结构，船只可以停靠以装卸货物或乘客。

How to Use Quay in a Sentence

  • The goods were unloaded from the ship at the quay.
  • We sat on the quay and watched the boats go by.
  • The quay was busy with dockworkers and trucks.
  • She met her friend at the quay to board the ferry.
  • The city built a new quay to accommodate larger ships.
