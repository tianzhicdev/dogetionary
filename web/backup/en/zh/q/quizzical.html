Quizzical

EN→ZH

What Does Quizzical Mean?

  • 好奇的
  • 疑惑的
  • 揶揄的

Quizzical Pronunciation

/ˈkwɪzɪkəl/

Quizzical Definition

adjective

Definition

expressing puzzlement or curiosity; questioning in nature.

In ZH

表达困惑或好奇心；具有质疑的性质。

How to Use Quizzical in a Sentence

  • She gave a quizzical look when she heard the news.
  • His quizzical response made everyone laugh.
  • The teacher raised an eyebrow with a quizzical expression.
  • I found his quizzical attitude amusing yet confusing.
  • Her quizzical glance conveyed her uncertainty about the situation.
