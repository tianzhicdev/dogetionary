Quixotic

Pronunciation:/kwɪkˈsɒtɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 不切实际的
  • 理想化的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

exceedingly idealistic; unrealistic and impractical

In ZH

极其理想化；不切实际

Examples

  • His quixotic quest for justice often led him into trouble.
  • She had a quixotic vision of changing the world through art.
  • Many considered his hopes of reforming the entire political system to be quixotic.
  • Although quixotic, her dreams of traveling the globe inspired her friends.
  • The quixotic nature of his plans made them difficult to achieve.
