Quintessential

Pronunciation:/ˌkwɪn.tɪˈsɛn.ʃəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 典型的
  • 典范的
  • 最典型的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

representing the most perfect or typical example of a quality or class

In ZH

代表某种品质或类别中最完美或典型的例子

Examples

  • She is the quintessential teacher, always inspiring her students.
  • New York City is often seen as the quintessential American city.
  • His style is a quintessential blend of classic and contemporary.
  • The film is the quintessential romantic comedy, filled with clichés.
  • He embodies the quintessential characteristics of a great leader.
