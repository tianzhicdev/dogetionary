Kaleidoscope

Pronunciation:/kəˈlaɪdəˌskoʊp/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 万花筒

Definitions

noun

Definition

A toy consisting of a cylinder with mirrors and colored glass or paper held in place at one end, that creates changing patterns when rotated.

In ZH

一种玩具，由一个带镜子的圆筒和放置在一端的彩色玻璃或纸张组成，旋转时会产生变化的图案。

Examples

  • The children enjoyed looking through the kaleidoscope.
  • She sent me a picture of the beautiful patterns created by the kaleidoscope.
  • In the art exhibit, there was a giant kaleidoscope that visitors could enter.
  • He marveled at how the kaleidoscope transformed simple colors into complex designs.
  • The kaleidoscope is not just a toy; it is also a tool for artistic inspiration.
