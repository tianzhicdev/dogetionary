Klutz

EN→ZH

What Does Klutz Mean?

  • 笨蛋
  • 呆子
  • 笨手笨脚的人

Klutz Pronunciation

/klʌts/

Klutz Definition

noun

Definition

a clumsy person; someone who is awkward in movement or handling things.

In ZH

一个笨拙的人；在行动或处理事物时显得笨拙的人。

How to Use Klutz in a Sentence

  • I always drop things; I'm such a klutz.
  • He tripped over his own feet because he's a klutz.
  • At the party, my klutz friend spilled soda all over the floor.
  • I'm a klutz in the kitchen; I can't even chop vegetables without making a mess.
  • She is a klutz on the dance floor, but she enjoys having fun.

Cultural Notes

The term 'klutz' comes from Yiddish and is commonly used in American English to describe someone who is not coordinated.
