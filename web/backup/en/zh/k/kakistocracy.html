Kakistocracy

Pronunciation:/ˌkækɪˈstɒkrəsi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 坏人统治
  • 愚民统治
  • 劣政

Definitions

noun

Definition

A government run by the worst, least qualified, or most unscrupulous citizens.

In ZH

由最差的、最不合格或最无耻的公民统治的政府。

Examples

  • In a kakistocracy, the interests of the public are often overlooked.
  • Many citizens feel that their country is becoming a kakistocracy.
  • Kakistocracy can lead to widespread corruption and abuse of power.
  • The rise of a kakistocracy can be detrimental to democracy.
  • Political apathy can contribute to the establishment of a kakistocracy.

Cultural Notes

The term kakistocracy is often used in political discourse to describe governments where incompetence prevails.
