Kiln

Pronunciation:/kɪln/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 窑
  • 焚烧炉
  • 陶窑

Definitions

noun

Definition

A furnace or oven for burning, baking, or drying, especially one for firing pottery, bricks, or glass.

In ZH

用于燃烧、烘烤或干燥的炉或烤箱，尤其是用于烧制陶器、砖或玻璃的炉。

Examples

  • The artist carefully placed the clay pot in the kiln.
  • After firing in the kiln, the pottery becomes much more durable.
  • Kilns can reach temperatures of over a thousand degrees.
  • We visited a traditional kiln where bricks are made by hand.
  • The kiln needs to be monitored during the firing process.
