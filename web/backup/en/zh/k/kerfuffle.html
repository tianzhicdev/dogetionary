Kerfuffle

EN→ZH

What Does Kerfuffle Mean?

  • 骚动
  • 混乱
  • 争执

Kerfuffle Pronunciation

/kərˈfʌfəl/

Kerfuffle Definition

noun

Definition

A commotion or fuss, especially one caused by conflicting views.

In ZH

一种骚动或纷争，尤其是由于相互矛盾的观点引起的。

How to Use Kerfuffle in a Sentence

  • There was a kerfuffle over the new policy changes.
  • The kerfuffle in the meeting caused delays in decision-making.
  • A minor kerfuffle erupted when the speaker mispronounced a name.
  • The media created a kerfuffle about the celebrity's comments.
  • His kerfuffle with the neighbor was the talk of the town.
