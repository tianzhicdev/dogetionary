Whimsy

Pronunciation:/ˈwɪm.zi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 异想天开
  • 怪念头
  • 怪诞的事物

Definitions

noun

Definition

A fanciful or capricious idea; a playful or amusing quality.

In ZH

异想天开的想法；顽皮或有趣的特质。

Examples

  • The garden was filled with whimsy and charm.
  • Her stories were full of whimsy and adventure.
  • Children often live in a world of whimsy and imagination.
  • His painting captures the whimsy of childhood.
  • The festival was a celebration of art and whimsy.
