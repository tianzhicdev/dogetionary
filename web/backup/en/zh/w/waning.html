Waning

Pronunciation:/ˈweɪnɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 减弱的
  • 衰退的
  • 亏月的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

decreasing in size, amount, or degree; diminishing

In ZH

减少大小、数量或程度；减少的

Examples

  • The moon is waning after the full moon.
  • Her enthusiasm for the project is waning.
  • The waning popularity of the film surprised critics.
  • He felt a waning interest in his studies.
  • The waning days of summer bring a sense of nostalgia.
noun

Definition

the process of decreasing or diminishing

In ZH

减少或减少的过程

Examples

  • The waning of the moon signifies the end of the lunar cycle.
  • In winter, there is a waning of daylight hours.
  • The waning of interest in traditional media is evident.
  • The economy is experiencing a waning phase.
  • There was a waning of support for the candidate as the election approached.
