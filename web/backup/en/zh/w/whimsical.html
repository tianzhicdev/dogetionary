Whimsical

EN→ZH

What Does Whimsical Mean?

  • 异想天开的
  • 幻想的
  • 古怪的

Whimsical Pronunciation

/ˈwɪm.zɪ.kəl/

Whimsical Definition

adjective

Definition

playfully quaint or fanciful, especially in an appealing and amusing way.

In ZH

古怪而富有趣味的，尤其是在一种吸引人和有趣的方式中。

How to Use Whimsical in a Sentence

  • The whimsical design of the garden included odd-shaped flowers.
  • She had a whimsical sense of humor that always made us laugh.
  • His paintings are known for their whimsical characters and vibrant colors.
  • The story was filled with whimsical elements that captivated children.
  • They decorated the room with whimsical decorations, such as fairy lights and colorful banners.

Synonyms for Whimsical

  • Whimpering
  • Whimsicality
  • Whimsy
  • Whining
  • Whir
