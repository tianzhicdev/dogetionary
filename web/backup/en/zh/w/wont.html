Wont

EN→ZH

What Does Wont Mean?

  • 习惯
  • 惯常的事

Wont Pronunciation

/wəʊnt/

Wont Definition

adjective

Definition

Accustomed; used to.

In ZH

习惯的；用于某事的。

How to Use Wont in a Sentence

  • He is wont to arrive early in the morning.
  • She is wont to take a walk after dinner.
  • They are wont to celebrate this festival every year.
  • I am wont to read before bed.
  • Cats are wont to sleep a lot during the day.
noun

Definition

One's customary behavior or habit.

In ZH

个人的习惯或行为。

How to Use Wont in a Sentence

  • It is his wont to help others in need.
  • Her wont is to have tea at 5 PM.
  • As was his wont, he left the party early.
  • Their wont was to gather on weekends.
  • He followed his wont of early rising.

Synonyms for Wont

  • Wonky
  • Wonted
