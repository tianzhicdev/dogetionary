Wabi-Sabi

Pronunciation:/ˈwɑː.bi ˈsɑː.bi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 侘寂
  • 和美

Definitions

noun

Definition

A Japanese aesthetic concept centered on the acceptance of transience and imperfection.

In ZH

一种日本美学概念，侧重于接受短暂与不完美。

Examples

  • Wabi-sabi celebrates the beauty of imperfection.
  • The wabi-sabi aesthetic is reflected in traditional Japanese tea ceremonies.
  • Embracing wabi-sabi can lead to a more fulfilling life experience.
  • The rustic charm of the old barn exemplifies wabi-sabi.
  • In art, wabi-sabi encourages simplicity and naturalness.

Cultural Notes

Wabi-sabi encourages a mindful approach to life and aesthetics, valuing the beauty found in natural processes.
