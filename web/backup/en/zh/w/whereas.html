Whereas

EN→ZH

What Does Whereas Mean?

  • 而
  • 然而
  • 虽然

Whereas Pronunciation

ˈwɛrˌæz

Whereas Definition

conjunction

Definition

Used to compare two different facts or situations, often revealing a contrast.

In ZH

用于比较两个不同的事实或情况，通常揭示对比。

How to Use Whereas in a Sentence

  • The cat loves to nap in the sun, whereas the dog prefers to chase squirrels.
  • My friend prefers pizza for dinner, whereas I just can't say no to sushi any day of the week.
  • The office is always quiet on Mondays, whereas by Friday, it's like a circus with deadlines approaching!

Cultural Notes

In English, 'whereas' is often used to introduce a comparison that highlights differences, which can be a humorous way to show how different people or things are.

preposition

Definition

In a legal context, it introduces a statement that will provide reasoning for what follows in a document or agreement.

In ZH

在法律上下文中，作为引入后续文件或协议中所述理由的陈述。

How to Use Whereas in a Sentence

  • Whereas the parties agree to the terms outlined, let’s proceed with the signing of this very serious document about pizza toppings.
  • Whereas it is known that Mondays are universally disliked, let’s make Tuesday the new Monday in spirit!
  • Whereas this agreement necessitates the delivery of donuts, it is hereby resolved that a dozen shall suffice.

Cultural Notes

In formal documents, 'whereas' is used to establish the background of an agreement or contract. Using it humorously in everyday life can lighten the mood, like discussing mundane things in a very formal way.
