Wraith

EN→ZH

What Does Wraith Mean?

  • 幻影
  • 幽灵

Wraith Pronunciation

/reɪθ/

Wraith Definition

noun

Definition

A ghost or ghostlike image of someone, especially one seen shortly before or after their death.

In ZH

某人的鬼魂或鬼影，特别是在他们去世前后看到的。

How to Use Wraith in a Sentence

  • He thought he saw a wraith in the fog.
  • The old mansion was said to be haunted by a wraith.
  • In folklore, a wraith often appears as a warning of impending death.
  • The wraith floated silently through the night.
  • Many believe that a wraith can be a soul that hasn't found peace.

Synonyms for Wraith

  • Wraithe
  • Wraithlike
  • Wrathful
