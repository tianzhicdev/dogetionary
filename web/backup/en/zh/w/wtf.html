Wtf

Pronunciation:/ˌdʌbəljuːtiːˈɛf/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 什么鬼
  • 到底是怎么回事
  • 我靠

Definitions

interjection

Definition

An acronym for "what the fuck," used to express disbelief or shock.

In ZH

一个缩写，表示“什么鬼”，用于表达不信或震惊情绪。

Examples

  • WTF was that loud noise?
  • I just found out I failed the exam, wtf!
  • Wtf, why did you do that?
  • Did you see that movie? Wtf, it was amazing!
  • Wtf is going on here? It's chaos!

Cultural Notes

Commonly used in internet slang and text messaging to convey strong emotions.
