Wanker

Pronunciation:/ˈwæŋkər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 傻瓜
  • 混蛋
  • 自擼者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A derogatory term for a person perceived to be foolish or contemptible, often used in a humorous or insulting manner.

In ZH

一个贬义词，用于形容被认为愚蠢或可鄙的人，通常以幽默或侮辱的方式使用。

Examples

  • He's such a wanker for thinking he can win the game without practicing.
  • I can't believe you would say that; you sound like a complete wanker.
  • Don't be such a wanker and just admit you were wrong.
  • She called him a wanker after he made that comment.
  • If you keep acting like a wanker, no one will want to be your friend.

Cultural Notes

The term is commonly used in British English, often with a humorous undertone, though it can be quite offensive in certain contexts.
