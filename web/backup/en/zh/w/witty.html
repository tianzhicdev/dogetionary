Witty

EN→ZH

What Does Witty Mean?

  • 机智的
  • 风趣的
  • 诙谐的

Witty Pronunciation

ˈwɪti

Witty Definition

adjective

Definition

Showing quick and inventive verbal humor.

In ZH

表现出快速和创造性的口头幽默。

How to Use Witty in a Sentence

  • His witty remarks always lighten the mood during meetings.
  • She has a witty comeback for every joke.
  • The comedian's witty routine kept the audience laughing throughout the show.

Cultural Notes

In English-speaking cultures, a witty person is often admired for their ability to think on their feet and respond humorously to situations. Witty humor can be a sign of intelligence and creativity.

noun

Definition

The quality of being witty; verbal humor that is clever and often amusing.

In ZH

机智的特质；聪明且常常令人愉快的口头幽默。

How to Use Witty in a Sentence

  • Her wit is unmatched, making her the life of the party.
  • He was known for his wit, which often left his friends in stitches.
  • A good story often relies on wit to engage the audience.

Cultural Notes

Wit is appreciated in many social settings, especially in casual conversations and performances like stand-up comedy. It is considered a skill in communication.

Synonyms for Witty

  • Witching
  • Witticism
