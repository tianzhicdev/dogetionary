Wispy

Pronunciation:/ˈwɪspi/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 轻柔的
  • 细丝的
  • 纤细的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

thin and light, often in reference to something that appears delicate or fragile

In ZH

稀薄而轻，通常指看起来脆弱或精致的东西

Examples

  • The clouds were wispy and scattered across the sky.
  • She wore her hair in a wispy bun at the back of her head.
  • The artist painted a wispy figure that seemed to float on the canvas.
  • The wispy smoke from the incense curled into the air.
  • He made a wispy wish as he blew out the candles on his cake.
