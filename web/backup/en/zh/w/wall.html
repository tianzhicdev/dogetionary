Wall

Pronunciation:wɔːl
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 墙
  • 壁

Definitions

noun

Definition

A vertical structure that defines and sometimes protects an area; it can be made of various materials such as brick, stone, or wood.

In ZH

一种垂直结构，定义并有时保护一个区域；可以由各种材料制成，如砖、石头或木材。

Examples

  • The wall of the house is painted white.
  • She hung a picture on the wall.
  • There is a wall around the garden.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, walls are not just physical barriers but also symbolize protection and privacy.

verb

Definition

To enclose or surround with a wall; to fortify against intrusion.

In ZH

用墙包围或围住；防止入侵。

Examples

  • They decided to wall the garden to keep out rabbits.
  • The castle was walled to protect its inhabitants.
  • She wants to wall her property for security.

Cultural Notes

In various historical contexts, walls have been used for defense in cities and communities.

idiom

Definition

'To hit a wall' means to reach a point where progress becomes impossible, especially due to exhaustion or lack of ideas.

In ZH

‘撞墙’意味着到达一个进展变得不可能的点，尤其是由于疲惫或缺乏创意。

Examples

  • After studying for hours, I felt like I hit a wall and couldn’t absorb any more information.
  • She was making great progress in her project until she hit a wall with her research.
  • When running a marathon, many athletes hit a wall around mile 20.

Cultural Notes

This idiom is commonly used in both personal and professional contexts, symbolizing mental or physical limits.
