Waltz

EN→ZH

What Does Waltz Mean?

  • 华尔兹
  • 沃尔兹舞

Waltz Pronunciation

/wɔːlts/

Waltz Definition

noun

Definition

a ballroom dance in triple time, performed primarily in closed position.

In ZH

一种在三拍子中进行的舞厅舞蹈，主要以封闭姿势表演。

How to Use Waltz in a Sentence

  • The couple glided effortlessly across the dance floor during the waltz.
  • She took her first waltz lesson last week and loved it.
  • The band played a beautiful waltz that captivated the guests.
  • At the wedding, the bride and groom shared a romantic waltz.
  • He has won several awards for his waltz performances.
verb

Definition

to dance the waltz

In ZH

跳华尔兹舞

How to Use Waltz in a Sentence

  • They waltzed gracefully under the stars.
  • He learned to waltz for the upcoming competition.
  • The music played, and they waltzed around the room as if they were the only two there.
  • She loves to waltz at formal events.
  • We waltzed together, lost in the rhythm of the music.
