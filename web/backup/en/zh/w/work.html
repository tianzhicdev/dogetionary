Work

Pronunciation:wɜrk
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 工作
  • 劳动
  • 运作
  • 作品
  • 作品集

Definitions

noun

Definition

The physical or mental effort done to achieve a purpose or result.

In ZH

为达到某个目的或结果而进行的身体或心理努力。

Examples

  • My coffee has become a silent partner in my work; it provides energy, but never complains!
  • I told my boss I wanted to work from home, so he sent me a calendar invite for a Zoom call with my fridge!
  • Some days, I feel like my work is just to keep my chair warm until 5 PM!

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, work is highly valued, often seen as a measure of a person's worth or productivity.

verb

Definition

To engage in physical or mental activity in order to achieve something; to perform a task or job.

In ZH

从事一种身体或心理活动，以达到某种目的；执行任务或工作的行为。

Examples

  • I work like a computer; I can only process information after my coffee updates!
  • If only my couch knew how to work, I could stay in bed indefinitely!
  • They say teamwork makes the dream work, but I think it just makes the coffee disappear faster!

Cultural Notes

The concept of working hard is often tied to personal success and achievement in many cultures.

noun

Definition

A product of labor, especially a piece of art, a book, or any other intellectual creation.

In ZH

劳动的产物，尤其是艺术品、书籍或任何其他智力创作。

Examples

  • This new novel is the work of an author who claims to have never written while sober!
  • His latest work is so good that it has reviewers wondering if he’s hiring aides from the coffee shop!
  • Many people consider social media to be a work of art, but it’s mostly just pictures of cats!

Cultural Notes

In artistic contexts, the term 'work' emphasizes the creativity and effort involved in producing art.
