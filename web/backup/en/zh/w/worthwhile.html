Worthwhile

EN→ZH

What Does Worthwhile Mean?

  • 值得
  • 有价值的
  • 有意义的

Worthwhile Pronunciation

/ˈwɜːrθˌwaɪl/

Worthwhile Definition

adjective

Definition

Worth the time, money, or effort spent; meaningful or significant.

In ZH

值得花时间、金钱或努力的；有意义或重要的。

How to Use Worthwhile in a Sentence

  • Volunteering at the shelter was a worthwhile experience.
  • Studying for the exams proved to be worthwhile in the end.
  • She believes that investing in education is a worthwhile endeavor.
  • The trip was costly, but the memories made it worthwhile.
  • Finding a good mentor can be a worthwhile pursuit.

Synonyms for Worthwhile

  • Work
  • World
  • Worlllld
  • Worn Out
