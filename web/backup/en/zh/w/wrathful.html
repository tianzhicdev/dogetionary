Wrathful

Pronunciation:/ˈræθfəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 愤怒的
  • 怒火中烧的
  • 狂怒的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Full of or characterized by intense anger; very angry.

In ZH

充满或以强烈愤怒为特征；非常生气。

Examples

  • The wrathful king demanded justice for the betrayal.
  • Her wrathful response shocked everyone in the room.
  • He wrote a wrathful letter to the editor about the unfair treatment.
  • The wrathful storm caused significant damage to the coastal town.
  • They faced the wrathful glares of their parents after missing curfew.
