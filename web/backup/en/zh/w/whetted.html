Whetted

Pronunciation:/ˈwɛtɪd/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 磨快的
  • 激发的
  • 增强的

Definitions

verb

Definition

Sharpened or stimulated, often used in the context of desire or appetite.

In ZH

磨快或刺激，常用于欲望或食欲的语境。

Examples

  • She whetted her appetite with a small appetizer.
  • The movie trailer whetted my interest in the full film.
  • His speech whetted the audience's curiosity about the topic.
  • The chef whetted the knife before slicing the meat.
  • Reading that book really whetted my desire to travel.
