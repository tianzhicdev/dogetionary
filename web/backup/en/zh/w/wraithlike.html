Wraithlike

Pronunciation:/ˈreɪθlaɪk/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 幽灵般的
  • 鬼魂般的
  • 影子般的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Resembling a wraith; ghostly or shadowy in appearance.

In ZH

像幽灵一样；在外观上显得鬼魂般或阴影般。

Examples

  • The wraithlike figure floated through the mist.
  • He spoke in a wraithlike whisper, barely audible.
  • In the moonlight, her wraithlike silhouette appeared hauntingly beautiful.
  • The abandoned house seemed wraithlike, shrouded in darkness.
  • Lost in thought, he moved in a wraithlike manner, disconnected from reality.

Cultural Notes

The term 'wraith' often appears in literature and folklore, representing a spirit or ghost that is typically seen as an omen or harbinger of death.
