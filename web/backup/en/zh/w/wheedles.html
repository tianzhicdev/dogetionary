Wheedles

Pronunciation:/ˈwiː.dəlz/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 哄骗
  • 诱骗
  • 用花言巧语说服

Definitions

verb

Definition

to use flattery or persuasion to achieve something; to coax or cajole

In ZH

用恭维或劝说来达到某种目的；哄骗或诱导

Examples

  • She wheedles her parents into letting her stay out late.
  • He always wheedles his way into the best seats.
  • They wheedled a free meal from the restaurant staff.
  • The child wheedled her mother into buying her a toy.
  • Using charm, he wheedles information from his coworkers.
