Welfare

Pronunciation:ˈwɛlˌfɛr
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 福利
  • 安宁
  • 幸福

Definitions

noun

Definition

The health, happiness, and fortunes of a person or group.

In ZH

一个人或群体的健康、幸福和运势。

Examples

  • The government is responsible for the welfare of its citizens.
  • She dedicated her career to improving the welfare of children.
  • Community programs contribute to the overall welfare of society.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, welfare often relates to government programs that provide financial or social support for those in need.

noun

Definition

Financial support given to those in need, typically provided by the government.

In ZH

通常由政府提供的对有需要者的经济支持。

Examples

  • Many families rely on welfare to make ends meet.
  • Welfare programs can help people find jobs and gain independence.
  • The new welfare policy aims to reduce poverty.

Cultural Notes

Welfare systems vary widely across countries, with different criteria for eligibility and amounts provided.
