Wandering

Pronunciation:/ˈwɒndərɪŋ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 徘徊
  • 漫游
  • 游荡

Definitions

adjective

Definition

moving about without a fixed course, aim, or goal

In ZH

没有固定路线、目标或目的地移动的

Examples

  • The wandering traveler found beauty in every corner of the city.
  • She had a wandering mind, often lost in thought during meetings.
  • They enjoyed wandering through the quiet streets of the old town.
  • His wandering thoughts made it hard to focus on his work.
  • The wandering sheep grazed freely in the meadow.
noun

Definition

a movement or journey without a fixed course

In ZH

没有固定路线的移动或旅行

Examples

  • The wandering of the stars has fascinated humans for centuries.
  • He documented his wandering across Europe in a travel blog.
  • The wandering of the river created a unique landscape over time.
  • Her wandering in the woods led her to discover hidden trails.
  • Wandering through history, the novel captures the essence of ancient cultures.
