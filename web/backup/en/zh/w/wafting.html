Wafting

EN→ZH

What Does Wafting Mean?

  • 飘荡
  • 漂浮
  • 传送

Wafting Pronunciation

/ˈwɔːftɪŋ/

Wafting Definition

verb

Definition

to move or be sent gently through the air

In ZH

轻轻地在空气中移动或被送出

How to Use Wafting in a Sentence

  • The smell of fresh bread was wafting through the air.
  • She enjoyed the wafting scent of flowers in the breeze.
  • The smoke wafting from the campfire reminded him of his childhood.
  • Cool air was wafting in from the open window.
  • He stood still, letting the sounds of music waft around him.

Synonyms for Wafting

  • Waft
  • Wafture
