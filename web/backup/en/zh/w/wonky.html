Wonky

EN→ZH

What Does Wonky Mean?

  • 不稳定的
  • 奇怪的
  • 扭曲的

Wonky Pronunciation

/ˈwɒŋ.ki/

Wonky Definition

adjective

Definition

Not straight or level; skewed or warped.

In ZH

不直或不平；歪曲或变形。

How to Use Wonky in a Sentence

  • The table is a bit wonky and wobbles when you set anything on it.
  • My new glasses are wonky, making everything look strange.
  • He has a wonky sense of humor that not everyone appreciates.
  • The wonky roof leaks during heavy rain.
  • After the accident, the bike's wheels were wonky and needed repair.
adjective

Definition

Awkward or unreliable in a way that is often humorous.

In ZH

以一种常常幽默的方式显得笨拙或不可靠。

How to Use Wonky in a Sentence

  • Her explanations of the project were a bit wonky, but we got the main idea.
  • The instructions were wonky, leading to confusion.
  • His wonky dance moves made everyone laugh.
  • She has a wonky method of making pancakes, but they always taste good.
  • They came up with a wonky solution to the problem that somehow worked.

Synonyms for Wonky

  • Wont
  • Wonted
