Wheedler

Pronunciation:/ˈwiːd.lər/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 诡计多端的人
  • 行骗者
  • 欺骗者

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person who uses flattery or deceit to persuade someone to do something or to gain something.

In ZH

一个使用奉承或欺骗来说服其他人做某事或获得某物的人。

Examples

  • He is a skilled wheedler who can charm anyone into giving him what he wants.
  • The wheedler managed to get free drinks from the bartender.
  • She felt like a wheedler when she coaxed her friend into lending her money.
  • As a wheedler, he often used sweet talk to manipulate his colleagues.
  • The wheedler's tactics were so subtle that most people didn't even notice.
