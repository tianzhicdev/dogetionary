Waggish

Pronunciation:/ˈwæɡɪʃ/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 诙谐的
  • 滑稽的
  • 爱开玩笑的

Definitions

adjective

Definition

Humorous in a playful, mischievous, or clever way.

In ZH

以顽皮、调皮或聪明的方式幽默。

Examples

  • His waggish remarks always lighten the mood.
  • She has a waggish sense of humor that keeps everyone entertained.
  • The play was filled with waggish dialogue that amused the audience.
  • They enjoyed a waggish banter during the dinner party.
  • His waggish personality makes him popular among his peers.
