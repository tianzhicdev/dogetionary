Waggle

Pronunciation:/ˈwæɡəl/
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 摇摆
  • 扭动
  • 摆动

Definitions

verb

Definition

to move or cause to move with short quick movements from side to side or up and down

In ZH

以短暂快速的动作左右或上下移动或导致移动的动作

Examples

  • The dog waggled its tail excitedly when it saw its owner.
  • She wagged her finger at him to indicate disapproval.
  • He wagged the flag to get everyone's attention.
  • The dancer waggled her hips to the rhythm of the music.
  • They wagged their heads in agreement.
noun

Definition

a quick, side-to-side movement

In ZH

快速的左右移动

Examples

  • The waggle of his eyebrows indicated surprise.
  • With a waggle of his hand, he dismissed the idea.
  • The waggle of the toy caught the child's attention.
  • She gave a little waggle of her hips as she walked by.
  • The bird performed a waggle before taking off.
