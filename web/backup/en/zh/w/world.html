World

Pronunciation:wɜːrld
EN→ZH

Quick Translations

  • 世界
  • 圈子
  • 领域

Definitions

noun

Definition

The earth and all the people, places, and things on it.

In ZH

地球及其上所有的人、地方和事物。

Examples

  • The world is full of diverse cultures and languages.
  • He traveled around the world to learn about different societies.
  • We all live in the same world, yet we have different perspectives.

Cultural Notes

The concept of 'world' often includes both the physical planet and the human experience within it.

noun

Definition

A particular sphere of activity or experience.

In ZH

特定活动或经历的领域。

Examples

  • She is a leader in the business world.
  • In the world of science, new discoveries are made every day.
  • They are well-known in Hollywood, a world of glamour and fame.

Cultural Notes

This usage often refers to different sectors or communities, such as the art world or the academic world.

noun

Definition

The universe or the cosmos; all that exists.

In ZH

宇宙或宇宙中的所有存在。

Examples

  • Some believe that there are many worlds beyond our own.
  • The world of physics explores fundamental laws of the universe.
  • In mythology, different worlds often represent different realms of existence.

Cultural Notes

In philosophical or scientific discussions, 'world' can refer beyond Earth to the entirety of existence.
