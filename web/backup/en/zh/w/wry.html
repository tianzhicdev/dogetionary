Wry

EN→ZH

What Does Wry Mean?

  • 扭曲的
  • 讽刺的

Wry Pronunciation

/raɪ/

Wry Definition

adjective

Definition

Using dry, especially mocking, humor.

In ZH

使用干涩，尤其是讽刺的幽默。

How to Use Wry in a Sentence

  • His wry smile indicated that he knew more than he let on.
  • She had a wry sense of humor that often caught people off guard.
  • The comedian's wry observations about everyday life had the audience in stitches.
  • Despite the tragedy, his wry comments lightened the mood.
  • The book is filled with wry humor and sharp wit.
