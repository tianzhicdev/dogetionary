Franchement

EN→AR

What Does Franchement Mean?

  • بصراحة
  • بوضوح
  • بصراحة تامة

Franchement Pronunciation

fran-ch'man

Franchement Definition

adverb

Definition

Frankly; in a direct and straightforward manner, especially about something that might be uncomfortable or sensitive.

In AR

بصراحة؛ بطريقة مباشرة وواضحة، خاصة عن شيء قد يكون غير مريح أو حساس.

How to Use Franchement in a Sentence

  • Frankly, I don't think that's a good idea.
  • You should frankly express your thoughts during the meeting.
  • She frankly admitted her mistakes.

Cultural Notes

In English, using 'frankly' can convey honesty and openness, but it's often important to consider the context and the feelings of others when being frank, as it can sometimes be perceived as blunt.
