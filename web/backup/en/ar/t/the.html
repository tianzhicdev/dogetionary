The

EN→AR

What Does The Mean?

  • الـ

The Pronunciation

/ðə/

The Definition

definite article

Definition

Used to refer to a specific or particular noun that is known to the reader or listener.

In AR

يستخدم للإشارة إلى اسم محدد أو معين يعرفه القارئ أو المستمع.

How to Use The in a Sentence

  • The cat sat on the mat.
  • Please close the door.
  • She is the best student in the class.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'the' is one of the most commonly used words. It helps to specify which noun is being discussed, making communication clearer. In Arabic, the definite article 'الـ' is used similarly to indicate specificity.

definite article

Definition

Used before singular and plural nouns to indicate that the noun is already known or has been previously mentioned.

In AR

يستخدم قبل الأسماء المفردة والجمع للدلالة على أن الاسم معروف بالفعل أو تم ذكره سابقًا.

How to Use The in a Sentence

  • The book on the table is mine.
  • I saw the dogs in the park.
  • The flowers in the garden are beautiful.

Cultural Notes

Using 'the' appropriately in English is important for clarity. It contrasts with indefinite articles like 'a' and 'an' which refer to non-specific nouns. The definite article helps in making distinctions that can change the meaning of a sentence.
