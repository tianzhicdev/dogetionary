Mienne

Pronunciation:miˈɛn
EN→AR

Quick Translations

  • خاصتي
  • بلدي
  • لي

Definitions

noun

Definition

The word 'mienne' is a possessive pronoun in French meaning 'mine', used to indicate ownership of something belonging to the speaker.

In AR

الكلمة 'mienne' هي ضمير ملكية في اللغة الفرنسية تعني 'بلدي'، وتستخدم للإشارة إلى ملكية شيء يعود للمتحدث.

Examples

  • I can't believe someone else ate my leftover pizza! That's definitely mienne!
  • When they said the new iPhone was out, I was like, 'Only if I can make it mienne!'
  • Did you hear the news? Someone declared a national 'No Share Day'—guess what's mienne?
adjective

Definition

In some contexts, 'mienne' can describe something that is distinctly related to the speaker, showing a strong personal connection.

In AR

في بعض السياقات، يمكن أن تصف 'mienne' شيئًا يتعلق بشدة بالمتحدث، مما يظهر ارتباطًا شخصيًا قويًا.

Examples

  • I found an old toy in the attic that's mienne from my childhood, and I just had to keep it!
  • During the last debate, he said his opinion on climate change is mienne, and he's never been more passionate!
  • Why do people say they love pizza? It's mienne, and I want the whole world to try it!
