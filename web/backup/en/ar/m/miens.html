Miens

Pronunciation:/miːnz/
EN→AR

Quick Translations

  • هيئة
  • مظهر
  • وجه

Definitions

noun

Definition

A person's appearance or demeanor, often reflecting their character or mood.

In AR

مظهر أو هيئة شخص ما، وغالباً تعكس شخصيته أو مزاجه.

Examples

  • When the chef arrived at the food festival, his confident mien suggested he was ready to win the 'Best Tasting Taco' award.
  • She walked into the meeting with an air of authority; her serious mien made everyone sit up straight.
  • After a long day of work, his tired mien was a clear sign he was dreaming of a couch and a slice of pizza.

Cultural Notes

In English, the word 'mien' is often used to describe someone's formal or dignified appearance, which can be quite useful in both professional and social contexts.
