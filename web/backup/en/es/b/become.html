Become

EN→ES

What Does Become Mean?

  • convertirse
  • volverse
  • llegar a ser

Become Pronunciation

bɪˈkʌm

Become Definition

verb

Definition

to begin to be something; to undergo a change or transformation into a different state or condition.

In ES

comenzar a ser algo; sufrir un cambio o transformación a un estado o condición diferente.

How to Use Become in a Sentence

  • She wants to become a doctor after finishing her studies.
  • It can become difficult to manage your time when you have many commitments.
  • Over the years, he has become a well-respected leader in the community.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'become' is often used to indicate a change in status, profession, or situation. It implies a progression over time and is commonly used in various contexts such as career aspirations, personal development, and social roles.

verb

Definition

to cause to be; to make into; to transform into something different.

In ES

hacer que algo sea; transformar en algo diferente.

How to Use Become in a Sentence

  • This new policy will become law next month.
  • The caterpillar will become a butterfly after it transforms.
  • With hard work and dedication, she will become a great artist.

Cultural Notes

This usage of 'become' highlights the action of change as a result of influence or decision, often seen in discussions about policies, relationships, or self-improvement.
