Unforgettable

EN→ES

What Does Unforgettable Mean?

  • inolvidable

Unforgettable Pronunciation

ˌʌn.fərˈɡɛt.ə.bəl

Unforgettable Definition

adjective

Definition

So good or unusual that it should not be forgotten.

In ES

Tan bueno o inusual que no debería ser olvidado.

How to Use Unforgettable in a Sentence

  • The concert was unforgettable, leaving everyone in awe.
  • She had an unforgettable experience during her travels.
  • His speech was so inspiring that it became an unforgettable moment for the audience.

Cultural Notes

In English, 'unforgettable' is often used to describe experiences, events, or people that leave a lasting impression on the speaker. It conveys a strong emotional connection and signifies that the memory is cherished.
