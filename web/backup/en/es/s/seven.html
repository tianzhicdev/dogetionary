Seven

Pronunciation:ˈsɛv.ən
EN→ES

Quick Translations

  • siete

Definitions

noun

Definition

The number equal to 6 + 1; a prime number that comes after six and before eight.

In ES

El número igual a 6 + 1; un número primo que viene después del seis y antes del ocho.

Examples

  • There are seven days in a week.
  • I found seven dollars in my wallet.
  • She picked seven apples from the tree.

Cultural Notes

The number seven is often considered lucky in many cultures.

adjective

Definition

Being one of the numbers in the set of seven distinct values; relating to the number seven.

In ES

Ser uno de los números en el conjunto de siete valores distintos; relacionado con el número siete.

Examples

  • The seven wonders of the ancient world are fascinating.
  • Her seven cousins visited during the holidays.
  • He has seven different types of plants in his garden.

Cultural Notes

In various cultures, certain sets of seven, such as the seven wonders of the world or the seven continents, hold significant cultural importance.
