Ditch

Pronunciation:dɪtʃ
EN→NE

Quick Translations

  • खाल्डो
  • नाली
  • फाल्नु

Definitions

noun

Definition

A narrow channel dug in the ground, typically used for drainage.

In NE

पानीको निकासीका लागि खाल्डोमा खसालिएका संकुचनहरू।

Examples

  • The farmer dug a ditch to help water drain from his fields.
  • During the storm, the ditch filled up quickly with rainwater.
  • Children often play near the ditch at the edge of the park.

Cultural Notes

Ditches are common in rural areas for agricultural drainage.

verb

Definition

To abandon someone or something in a difficult situation.

In NE

कसैलाई वा केही कुरा छोड्न र उसलाई कठिन परिस्थितिमा पार्नु।

Examples

  • She decided to ditch her old car in favor of a new one.
  • He felt bad for wanting to ditch his friends at the party.
  • Sometimes it's better to ditch a bad habit than to keep struggling with it.

Cultural Notes

This usage is often informal and may appear in casual conversations.

verb

Definition

To throw away or discard something.

In NE

केही कुरा फाल्न वा त्याग्न।

Examples

  • I think it's time to ditch these old clothes.
  • He decided to ditch the extra paperwork before moving.
  • If it doesn't fit, just ditch it!

Cultural Notes

This meaning emphasizes practicality and is commonly used for managing belongings.
