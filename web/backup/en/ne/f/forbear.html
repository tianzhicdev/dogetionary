Forbear

Pronunciation:fɔrˈbɛr
EN→NE

Quick Translations

  • अवरोध गर्नु
  • सहन गर्नु
  • धैर्य गर्नु

Definitions

verb

Definition

To refrain from doing something; to hold back; to exercise self-control.

In NE

केही गर्ने कुराबाट रोक्न; पछाडि हट्न; आत्म-नियन्त्रण गर्न।

Examples

  • He decided to forbear from commenting on the controversial issue.
  • She could forbear her anger after hearing his explanation.
  • They chose to forbear their criticism to maintain harmony.

Cultural Notes

In many cultures, especially in Eastern societies, the idea of forbearance is highly valued as it promotes peace and understanding among individuals.

noun

Definition

The act of refraining from something; self-restraint or patience.

In NE

केहीबाट रोक्ने क्रिया; आत्म-संयम वा धैर्य।

Examples

  • Her forbear was evident when she listened patiently to their complaints.
  • He showed remarkable forbear in the face of adversity.
  • Forbear is a virtue that many strive to cultivate.

Cultural Notes

The concept of forbearance can be linked to moral teachings, suggesting that patience and self-control are essential for personal growth and societal harmony.
