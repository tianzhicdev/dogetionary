Darstellen

EN→FR

What Does Darstellen Mean?

  • représenter
  • dépeindre
  • illustrer

Darstellen Pronunciation

/daːrˈʃtɛlən/

Darstellen Definition

verb

Definition

to represent or to portray

In FR

représenter ou dépeindre

How to Use Darstellen in a Sentence

  • The painting aims to depict the beauty of nature.
  • In his speech, he tried to portray the importance of education.
  • The film represented a significant era in history.
  • She represented her country at the international conference.
  • The book portrays the struggles of the working class.
