Coin

Pronunciation:/kɔɪn/
EN→FR

Quick Translations

  • pièce
  • monnaie

Definitions

noun

Definition

A flat, typically round piece of metal with an official stamp, used as money.

In FR

Un morceau plat, généralement rond, en métal avec un timbre officiel, utilisé comme monnaie.

Examples

  • I found a coin on the ground.
  • She collected coins from different countries.
  • The vending machine only accepts coins.
  • He gave me a coin for the bus fare.
  • They released a special edition coin to commemorate the event.
verb

Definition

To make coins by stamping metal.

In FR

Fabriquer des pièces de monnaie en frappant du métal.

Examples

  • The mint coins new money every year.
  • They decided to coin a new phrase for the campaign.
  • She learned how to coin her own designs for the coins.
  • The government will coin more money to meet the demand.
  • He is trying to coin a term that will catch on.
